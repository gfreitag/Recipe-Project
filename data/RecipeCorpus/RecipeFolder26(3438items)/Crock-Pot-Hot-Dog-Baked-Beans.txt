Title: Crock-Pot Hot Dog Baked Beans
Categories: Crockpot, Hot dogs, Beans
Yield: 1 pot
3/4 c  Onion; diced
1/3 c  Light molasses
1/4 c  Open pit sauce w/onions
1 lb Hotdogs; chopped
4 lb Canned baked beans
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/4 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Sugar
Put beans, onion, onion powder, molasses, ketchup, bbq sauce, sugar
and hot dogs in crockpot. Stir gently to mix. Cool on low for about 8
hours.

If sauce is runny, lift cover for last hour or two to let moisture
evaporate.
