Title: Barbecued Country-Style Spareribs
Categories: Main dish, Pork, Sthrn/livng, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Sugar, brown
2 tb Vinegar
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
3 1/2 lb Spareribs, country-style
2 md Onions; thinly sliced
2 tb Butter (or marg.); melted
1 lg Garlic clove; minced
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
1 c  Catsup
3/4 c  Water
1 ts Mustard, dry
Saute onion in butter until golden brown; stir in next 11 ingredients.
Bring sauce to a boil; reduce heat, and simmmer 20 minutes.

Place ribs, meaty side up, on a rack in a shallow baking dish.  Bake
at 325 degrees for about 2 hours or until well done, basting with
sauce and turning every 30 minutes.
