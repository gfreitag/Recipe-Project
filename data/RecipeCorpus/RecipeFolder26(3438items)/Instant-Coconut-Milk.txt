Title: Instant Coconut Milk
Categories: Condiment, Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 cups
3 1/2 c  Water
2 c  Shredded, desiccated,
-unsweetened coconut
Put coconut in medium bowl. Pour simmering water over the coconut. Let
stand until cooled. Strain, discard the coconut, refrigerate or
freeze.
