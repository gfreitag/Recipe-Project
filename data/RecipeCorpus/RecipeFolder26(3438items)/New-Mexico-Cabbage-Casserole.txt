Title: New Mexico Cabbage Casserole
Categories: Vegetable, Casserole, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Water
2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
Buttered bread crumbs
4 c  Shredded cabbage
1 ea Can cream of potato soup
(10 3/4 oz)
Place cabbage in greased 7 x 11 casserole.  Mix potato soup and
water. Pour over cabbage.  Cover with grated cheese.  Bake at 350 F
about 30 minutes. Sprinkle with bread crumbs and bake an additional
15 minutes.

From Loren Martin, Big Cabin, Okla
