Title: Potluck Special
Categories: Ground beef, Vegetable, Casserole, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
28 oz Cn tomatoes with liquid,
-cut up
16 oz Sauerkraut, rinsed and
-drained
1 1/2 c  Cooked rice
1 md Green pepper, chopped
"It's delicious for
-sauerkraut lovers and easy
-to double
For a larger crowd." - Reta
-Christensen
1 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion, chopped
In a skillet, brown the ground beef and onion; drain.  Add remaining
ingredients; transfer to a 2-quart baking dish. Cover and bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour. Serves: 6-8 From: "Taste of Home" Magazine,
October/November 1994 Posted by: Debbie Carlson - Cooking
