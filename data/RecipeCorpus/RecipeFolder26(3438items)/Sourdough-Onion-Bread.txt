Title: Sourdough Onion Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Sourdough
Yield: 1 loaf
1/2 c  Diced onions
2/3 c  Milk
1 1/3 c  Starter
2 ts Yeast
2 tb Vital gluten, optional
4 c  Whole wheat flour
2/3 ts Salt
For DAK or Welbilt, use Sweet Bread Cycle.  Use 'double knead' method
if possible.

Per 1 oz. slice:  66.8 calories, .3 g fat, 2.9 g protein, 13/8 g
carbohydrate, .1 mg cholesterol, 51.6 mg sodium

From:  _The Bread Machine Cookbook, Vol IV_ by Donna German, MM by
Linda Shogren
