Title: Lazy Perogy Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea Onions, chopped finely
1 c  Sour cream
1 lg Egg
1 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
1/4 t  Salt
1 c  Margarine
15 ea LASAGNA NOODLES, OR
2 c  MACARONI
2 c  COTTAGE CHEESE
1/4 t  ONION SALT
2 c  Potatoes, mashed
1/8 t  Pepper
Cook noodles or macaroni; drain.  Line bottom of 9x13 pan with 3
lasagne noodles or 1/3 of macaroni. In med. bowl mix cottage cheese,
egg and onion salt.  Spoon all of mixture over noodles in pan.  Cover
with second layer of noodles.  Mix cheddar cheese with  mashed
potatoes, salt, onion salt, and pepper.  Spread over second layer of
noodles. Cover with 3rd layer. Melt margarine in fry pan and saute
onions until clear. Pour over noodles. Cover with foil and bake in
350 deg. oven for 30 mins. Let stand about 10 minutes before serving.
Serve with sour cream. Posted by: Sharon Dibble 12-31-94
