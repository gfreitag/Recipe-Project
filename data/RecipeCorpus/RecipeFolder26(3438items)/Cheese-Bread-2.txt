Title: Cheese Bread 2
Categories: Bread, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 12 servings
1 c  Cheddar cheese
-shredded
1 c  Milk
1    Egg (slightly beaten)
1 tb Butter, melted
2 1/3 c  Cup flour
3/4 c  Sugar
4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
Mix dry ingredients. Add cheese. Make a well. Add egg, butter, &
milk. Bake in a greased loaf pan @ 350 degrees for 1 hour.
