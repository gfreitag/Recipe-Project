Title: Quick Spam a la King
Categories: Spam, Waffles, Main dish
Yield: 6 spam eater
2 tb Red pepper; chopped*
4    Mushrooms; fresh chopped
-finely.
6    Waffles
1 cn Spam, cut into strips
1/4 c  Margarine; OR LESS
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup;
1/2    Soup can milk;
*Had frozen red peppers, guess green will work fine. Cut spam into
bitsize or strips.  Heat skillet and add margarine. When margarine is
hot, add strips of chicken and onion.  Cook, stirring constantly, 5
minutes. Add soup, milk, and pimiento.  Cover and simmer for 3
minutes or until thoroughly heated.  Bake waffles.  Serve over
waffles. Source: Bert's Cuisine and Nancy's Cooking. Brought to you
and yours from Him and Her and their Meal-Master.
