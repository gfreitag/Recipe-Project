Title: Raisin Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Wine vinegar
Tabasco sauce (a few drops)
Salt & Pepper
1 pn Ground Mace
1/4 c  Red Currant Jelly
1/2 c  Raisins, cut or chopped into
-small pieces
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Butter
Method:  Dissolve sugar in water, then boil steadily for 5 minutes.
Add remaining ingredients and simmer gently until the red currant
jelly has dissolved.  Serve hot or cold with ham.
