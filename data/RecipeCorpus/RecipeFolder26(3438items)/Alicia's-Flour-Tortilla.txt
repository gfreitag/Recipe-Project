Title: Alicia's Flour Tortilla
Categories: Tex/mexican, Bread
Yield: 3 servings
MMMMM----------------FROM: ALICIA CENTENO FROM AN---------------------
1/2 c  Warm milk
1 T  Baking powder
1 t  Salt
Into English)
4 c  Flour
2 T  Lard
Mix all dry ingredients and sift. Mix in lard. Add milk gradually.
Knead til smooth. Roll in pieces. roll out flat very thin. Cook on
