Title: Frozen Fruit Yogurt
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 qt Unflavored yogurt
1 tb Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
4 c  (2 pints) washed, halved
-strawberries, sliced
-bananas or peeled peaches
-or nectarines
Puree fruit in blender until smooth (makes 3 cups puree).  Combine
sugar, vanilla and salt in large bowl.  Stir in pureed fruit.  Pour
into 1 gallon freezer.  Cover.  Freeze with ice-salt mixture in
proportions recommended for freezer.  When yogurt is frozen, pour off
brine. Remove cover and scrape frozen yogurt from dasher.

Source:  Instruction & Recipe Book - Ronco Food Dehydrator
