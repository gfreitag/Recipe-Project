Title: Waffles
Categories: Waffles
Yield: 1 servings
4 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2 tb Butter; melted
2    Eggs
3 c  Milk; sour
3 3/4 c  Flour
1 tb Sugar
Note: No directions given. Combine ingredients, bake on waffle iron
until crisp and brown. Source: Mrs. F. A. Morgan, North Lima Grange,
Mahoning County, OH
