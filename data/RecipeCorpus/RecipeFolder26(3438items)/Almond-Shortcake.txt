Title: Almond Shortcake
Categories: Holiday, Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 2 pans
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
10    Almonds, whole, blanched
3/4 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
4 tb Almond paste
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 ts Almond extract
Preheat oven to 325. Cream butter, sugar and almond paste well. Add
1/2 of beaten egg and almond extract. Add dry ingredients and knead
briefly. Lightly grease 2 8" round cake pans or pie plates. Press
mixture into pans; score edges with a fork. Brush with remaining egg
and arrange almonds in centre. Bake at 325 for 15 min or until
lightly browned. Source: Meda Osborne Typed by: Sharon Verrall
