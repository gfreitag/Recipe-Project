Title: Toasty Garbanzos
Categories: Snack, Beans
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 t  Cayenne pepper (1/4 t if
-you don't like them spicy)*
1 cn Garbanzo beans, drained and
-rinsed (chick peas)
1/4 t  Ground cumin
* Instead of salt and cayenne try 1 teaspoon of Cajun seasoning salt.
Turn on the broiler. In a bowl, toss the drained beans with the
seasonings. Spread onto a cookie sheet. Place under the broiler about
4 inches away from the flame. Broil for 2 minutes and stir the beans.
Broil for 2 more minutes and stir again. Broil for at least 2 more
minutes until they are golden brown and crunchy. Watch so they don't
burn. Let cool and store in an airtight container. SOURCE:* Mother
Earth News magazine, Feb/Mar 1995 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 1/95
