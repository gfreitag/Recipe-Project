Title: Honey Mulled Cider
Categories: Beverage, Holiday, Crockpot, Apples
Yield: 10 cups
ds Nutmeg, ground
5    Inches, Stick cinnamon
1 1/2 ts Whole cloves
10 c  Apple cider,OR apple juice
1/3 c  Honey
1/8 ts Salt
ds Ginger, ground
In crokery cooker combine all above ingredients (tie cinnamon and
cloves in cheesecloth, if desired). Cover; heat on low setting 4 to 4
1/2 hours.

(To "mull" means to heat - to infuse with the flavors of the other
ingredients, which are generally spices or herb.  Both apple cider
and red wine lend themselves to be mulled with cinnamon, nutmeg,
cloves, and allspice.)
