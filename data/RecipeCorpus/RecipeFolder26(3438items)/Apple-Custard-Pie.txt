Title: Apple Custard Pie
Categories: Fruit, Pie/tart, Apples
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Apples; peeled and sliced
1 ts Vanilla
1    Pie crust  (9 inch)
2    Egg yolks
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Butter
Recipe by: Sue Klapper Preparation Time: 0:20 In top of double boiler,
combine egg yolks and sugar.  Add butter, apples a water in double
boiler, stirring constantly until thickened.
Pour into pie crust.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.  Source: Favorite
Apple
