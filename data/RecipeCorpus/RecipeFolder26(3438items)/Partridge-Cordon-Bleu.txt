Title: Partridge Cordon Bleu
Categories: Game, Cheese/eggs, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Pepper
8    Grouse breast filets
4 sl Ham
4 sl Swiss (or other) cheese
Oil for frying
1    Egg
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Cornflake crumbs
1 ts Parsley flakes
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Salt

filets sealing the edges... 3) Dip the filets in egg mixture and then
