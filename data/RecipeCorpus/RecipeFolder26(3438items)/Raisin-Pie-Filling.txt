Title: Raisin Pie Filling
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  Corn starch
1/2 t  Cinamon
1/2 t  Salt
1 T  Vinegar
2 c  Water
2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 T  Butter
Boil raisins and water 5 minutes.  Mix the rest and cook till clear.
Add vinegar and butter. Origin: My Mom, Shirley Whittaker Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
