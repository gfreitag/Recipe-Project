Title: Herman Sourdough Croutons
Categories: Bread, Sourdough
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Garlic flavored oil
2 tb Parsley; finely chopped
3 tb Butter
-1/3" cubes
1/2 ts Salt
-----lisa crawley   tspn00b-
1/2    Loaf herman bread; cut into
2 tb Garlic; minced
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Chives; minced
Melt butter w/ oils in lg. skillet. When butter stops "sputtering"
add the garlic, herbs and salt; cook 1 min. Add bread cubes, tossing
gently until all the butter is absorbed. On a baking sheet spread
bread cubes in a single layer. Bake. Serve warm or at rm. temp. on
your favorite salad. Cool completely before storing. OVEN TEMP> 350
TIME: 10-15 min:or til golden brown. Converted by MMCONV vers. 1.00
