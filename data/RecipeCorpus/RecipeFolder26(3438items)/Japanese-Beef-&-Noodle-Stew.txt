Title: Japanese Beef & Noodle Stew
Categories: Japanese, Main dish, Soup/stew, Beef, Pasta
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Cold water
1 tb Teriyaki sauce
1/4 c  Red wine
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/4 lb Round beef, cubed
1 tb Butter
1 pk Instant ramen noodles
2 tb Cornstarch
1. Brown beef in butter; set aside.

2. Prepare ramen according to package directions; drain liquid from
noodles, reserving hot liquid.

3. Add cornstarch to cold water, stirring until dissolved.

4. Carefully add hot liquid from noodles to cornstarch mixture; heat
until mixture becomes thick and glossy.

5. Add teriyaki sauce, wine and ginger to sauce.

6. Combine beef, noodles and sauce; mix well. Serve hot.
