Title: Batter-Fried Vegetables
Categories: Bread, Appetizer, Vegetable
Yield: 8 servings
5 c  Veggies to bread.
Shortening or oil to fry in
Dipping Sauce
1    Egg, slightly beaten
1 c  Ice water, cold milk or beer
1 c  Flour
For batter, in medium mixing bowl stir together egg and ice water,
milk or beer.  Add flour all at once.  Beat with a rotary mixer or
whisk until mixture is smooth.  Set aside.

Rinse vegetables under cold water, drain.  Pat dry with paper towels.
Set aside.

In a heavy saucepan or deep fryer heat 2 inches of melted shortening
or oil to 375 degrees F.  Dip veggies a few at a time into the
batter, swirling to coat.  Fry for 2 to 3 minutes or until light
golden brown. Drain vegetables on paper towels.  Keep warm in a 300
degree F oven. Repeat with remaining vegetables.

Serve with individual bowls of Dipping Sauce.  Makes 8 servings.
