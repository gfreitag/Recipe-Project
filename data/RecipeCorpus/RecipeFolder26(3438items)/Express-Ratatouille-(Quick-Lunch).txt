Title: Express Ratatouille (Quick Lunch)
Categories: Fatfree, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 1 servings
1 cn Chopped Italian style
Tomaotes
4    To 8 Zucchini, depending on
Size of zucchini and
Appetite
Cut up zucchini into about 1/2 to 1 inch pieces. Put into plastic
lunch container. Add tomatoes. Mix. At work, nuke for 3-5 minutes,
depending on amount and on how soft you like the zucchini.
