Title: Tarantula Jack's Chili
Categories: Chili, Beef, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Water
1 cn (8 oz.) tomato sauce
7 T  Chili powder
2 T  Ground cumin
2 t  Tabasco
3 lb Beef boneless top round,
Cubed
2 md Onions, grated
2 lg Cloves garlic, minced
2 cn (10 1/2 oz.@) condensed
Chicken broth
Cook beef in nonstick Dutch oven until brown; stir occasionally. Add
onions, garlic, broth and water. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Cover
and simmer 1 1/2 hours. Stir in tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin and
pepper sauce. Cover and simmer 1 hour, adding additional water for
desired consistancy.

FROM BETTY CROCKER'S INTERNATIONAL CHILI SOCIETY 1989 $25,000 WINNER.
