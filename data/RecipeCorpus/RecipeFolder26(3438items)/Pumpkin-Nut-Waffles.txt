Title: Pumpkin Nut Waffles
Categories: Breakfast, Nuts/grains, Waffles, Pumpkin
Yield: 1 batch
1 3/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Cooking Oil
1/2 c  Canned Pumpkin
3    Egg Yolks
1/2 c  Chopped Pecans
2 c  Flour
1 tb Baking Powder
3/4 ts Pumpkin Pie Spice
1/4 ts Salt
3    Egg Yolks
Thoroughly stir together flour, baking powder, spice and salt. Beat
yolks; stir in milk, oil and pumpkin. Stir into dry ingredients. Beat
egg whites to stiff peaks; fold into batter. Stir in nuts. Bake in
waffle iron according to maker directions. Top with maple syrup,
apple butter or other favorite syrup.
