Title: Chicken & Vegetable Ball Soup ^
Categories: Chicken, Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
1    Egg, beaten
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1 ts Soup
4 ts Sugar
3 c  Chicken stock
2 c  Chopped cooked chicken
2    Bamboo shoots, cut in very
-thin srips
4    Mushrooms, chopped
2 sm Carrots, finely chopped
Combine chicken, bamboo shoots, mushrooms, carrots, egg, 1 teaspoon
soy sauce and 1 teaspoon sguar. Mix well and form into balls. Bring
stock, 1/4 cup soy sauce and remaining sugar to a boil. Drop chicken
balls into boiling stock; reduce heat and simmer 8 minutes and serve.
