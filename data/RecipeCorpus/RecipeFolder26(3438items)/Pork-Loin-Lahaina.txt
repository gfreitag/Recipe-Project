Title: Pork Loin Lahaina
Categories: Pork, Fruit, Main dish
Yield: 10 servings
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Ginger
1/3 c  Soy sauce
6 lb Pork loin roast(center cut)
1 cn Pineapple slices (20 oz)
1/4 c  Honey
Have butcher loosen chine (back) bone. Place fat side up, on rack in
open roasting pan.  Insert meat thermometer with bulb in thickest
part not touching bone.  Roast in 325 degree oven 2 1/2 hours or
until 170 degrees is reached on thermometer.  drain pineapple well
reserving all syrup, blend with remaining ingredients.  Pour over
pineapple to marinate. Brush pork with marinade every 10 minutes the
last half hour of cooking. remove to serving platter, cut into 10
portions. Insert pineapple between each portion.  heat and serve
remaining marinade with pork. Submitted By STEWART HOPPER   On
01-01-95
