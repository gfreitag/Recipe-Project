Title: Two Minute Fudge
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 pound
1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
1 lb Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
2 tb Margarine
Blend together powdered sugar and cocoa in a microwave bowl. Add milk
DO NOT STIR microwave on high for 2 minutes, then stir in the vanilla
and nuts. Pour into a buttered cake dish, refrigerate until cool then
cut into squares.
