Title: Chicken & Rice Vegetable Soup - Jean Ramsay
Categories: Main dish, Soup/stew, Chicken, Rice, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
5 c  Chicken
-Broth
1 c  Sliced
-celery
1/4 c  Parsley
1/2 t  Dried
-Thyme, crushed
1    Bag Instant Rice
1 1/2 c  Chopped
-uncooked Chicken
1 c  Sliced Carrots
1/2 c  Chopped Onion
1/2 t  Pepper
1    Bay Leaf
1 T  Lime Juice
Prepare rice according to package.  Combine broth, chicken,celery,
carrots, onions, parsley, pepper, thyme, and bay leaf in a lg.
saucepan or dutch oven. Bring to a boil over med.-high heat, stirring
once or twice. Reduce heat to low; simmer 10 to 15 min. or until
chicken is no longer pink in the center. Remove bay leaf; discard.
Stir in rice and lime juice. Makes 4 servings.
