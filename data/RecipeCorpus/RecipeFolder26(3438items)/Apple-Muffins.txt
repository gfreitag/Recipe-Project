Title: Apple Muffins
Categories: Fatfree, Apples, Muffin
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/4 t  Nutmeg
1 1/2 c  Chopped apples (these need
To be small pieces or the
Muffins
Tend to fall apart).
1/4 c  Apple sauce
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 t  Vanilla
1    Egg white, beaten
1 1/4 c  Flour
1 t  BS
1/4 t  Salt
Add apples at the end and fold in gently.  Can add raisins if you
like. Bake at 350 for 20 min.  Makes 8 muffins.
