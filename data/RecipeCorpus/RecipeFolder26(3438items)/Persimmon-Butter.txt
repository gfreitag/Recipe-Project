Title: Persimmon Butter
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit
Yield: 5 pints
1 1/2 c  Honey
Grated rind of 1 orange
8 c  Persimmon puree
1 c  Orange juice
Combine all ingredients in an enameled or stainless steel kettle and
cook over medium-high heat until thick, about 10-15 minutes.  Ladle
into hot jars and seal.  Process pint jars for 15 minutes in boiling
water bath. Makes about 5 pints.  Try on toast or English muffins.
