Title: Chocolate Peanut Butter Cheesecake
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Chocolate, Nuts/grains, Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 servings
18 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Peanut butter
-GKWH72D
1 1/4 c  Choc. wafer cookie crumbs
3 tb Sugar
3 tb Butter; melted
1 c  Cho. chips; melted
CRUST: Crush cookies in food processor; combine w/3 tb sugar and
butter. Press firmly into bottom of springform pan.
FILLING: Melt choc. chips & set aside. In bowl, cream cheese and
sugar til light & fluffy. Add eggs 1 at a time. Beat well after each.
Add vanilla. Divide mixture in half. Into one half blend in melted
chocolate. In the other half, blend peanut butter. Spoon mixtures
alternately into the prepared pan. Swirl the two batters together
with a knife. Bake at 300Ã¸ for 60-65 minutes. Cool. Refrigerate.
Remove outer rim & cut into wedges.  Very rich - enjoy!
