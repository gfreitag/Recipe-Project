Title: Crab Chops
Categories: Crab/shrimp, Cajun/creol
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 t  Cayenne pepper
2 ea Dashes Tabasco
Crackermeal or bread
-crumbs for dredging
Butter and vegetable oil
-for frying
Boiled crab claws for
-garnish (optional)
3 ea Green onions - minced
3 T  Butter or margarine
2 T  Flour
1 c  Milk
1 lb Crabmeat
20 ea Saltine crackers - crumbled
1 ea Egg - beaten
1/2 t  Salt
Saute onions in butter or margarine.  Alternate adding flour and milk,
stirring constantly to make a medium-thick white sauce.  Remove from
heat and add crabmeat, cracker crumbs, egg, salt and cayenne pepper.
Gently mix together and set aside to cool thoroughly. Shape into 6
patties and dredge in cracker meal or bread crumbs.  Put about
1/2-inch of equal parts vegetable oil and butter in a skillet and fry
patties on both sides until they are golden brown.

Recipe from "Who's Your Mama, Are You Catholic and Can You Make a
Roux" by Marcelle Bienvenu.
