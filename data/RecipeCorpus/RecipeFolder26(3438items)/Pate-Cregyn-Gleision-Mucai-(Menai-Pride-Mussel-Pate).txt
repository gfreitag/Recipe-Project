Title: Pate Cregyn Gleision Mucai (Menai Pride Mussel Pate)
Categories: Appetizer, British, Clam/oyster
Yield: 4 servings
Pinch mixed herbs
Pinch dill weed
Pinch crushed garlic
Brandy & double cream to tst
Seasoning
3 oz Cooked and shelled mussels
1 oz Celery
1 oz Carrot
1 oz Breadcrumbs
4    Egg yolks
2 oz Herring roe
Finely mince mussels, vegetables and herring roe.  Add herbs,
seasoning and breadcrumbs.  Blend well with egg yolks, brandy and
cream. Cook for 1/2 hour at 350F in a bainmarie in the oven. Cook and
serve with toast fingers. Glantraeth Restaurant Bodorgan, Anglesey.
