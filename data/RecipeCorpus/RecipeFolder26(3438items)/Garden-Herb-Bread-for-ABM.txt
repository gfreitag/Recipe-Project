Title: Garden Herb Bread for ABM
Categories: Breadmaker, Bread, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 loaf
MMMMM------------------------REGULAR LOAF-----------------------------
1 ts Marjoram
1 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Basil
1 ts Yeast (fast rise)
OR
2 ts Yeast (active dry)
3/4 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
1 tb Dry milk
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
1 ts Chives
MMMMM-------------------------LARGE LOAF------------------------------
1/2 tb Marjoram
1/2 tb Thyme
1 ts Basil
2 ts Yeast (fast rise)
OR
3 ts Yeast (active dry)
1 1/4 c  Water
3 c  White bread flour
2 tb Dry milk
2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1/2 tb Chives
Load and bake according to your machine's directions.

Use dried herbs that are flaked and not ground.  If using ground,
reduce the amount by half.  If using fresh herbs, double the amount.

Any combination of spices may be substituted, according to your
tastes.

This recipe can be made with the regular, rapid, or delayed time bake
cycles.
