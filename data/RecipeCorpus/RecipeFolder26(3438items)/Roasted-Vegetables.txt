Title: Roasted Vegetables
Categories: Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
x  Garlic
x  Fresh vegetables
x  Balsamic vinegar
As a brush-on for roasted veggies, especially strips of eggplant.  I
drag strips of eggplant through balsamic vinegar, sprinkle with
garlic and roast for about 40 minutes at about 375 degrees.
