Title: Delicious Waffles
Categories: Waffles, Rice, Breakfast
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk;
1    Egg;
1/4 c  Vegetable oil;
1 c  Cooked rice;
1 c  Flour;
1 tb Sugar;
1 ts Baking powder;
1/2 ts Soda;
1/4 ts Salt;
Sift the first 5 ingredients together.  Add 1 cup of buttermilk and
egg. Mix and let stand for about 15 minutes.  Then add oil, 1/2 c of
buttermilk and cooked rice.  Cook in waffle iron.  Serves 4. Source:
Mrs. Richard M. Dunn from "The Stuffed Cougar". Brought to you and
yours via Nancy O'Brion and her Meal-Master.
