Title: Roasted Garlic and Bean Spread
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Beans, Dip
Yield: 48 servings
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Snipped fresh parsley
1/4 c  Minced red onion
1/4 c  Minced sweet green pepper
1    Whole head garlic, separated
-about 12 cloves) but not
-peeled
2 cn White beans, drained
1/4 c  Lemon juice
Bake garlic in a small baking dish at 400 degrees until soft,
but not brown (about 15 minutes). When cool, slip garlic skins off
and trim the ends.  Place half the beans, the lemon juice, oil and
garlic in a food processor.
Puree until smooth, then transfer to a medium bowl. Stir in
remaining beans, parsley, black pepper and half the onions and green
peppers. Mash with a fork. Garnish with the remaining onions and
peppers.
Yields: 3 cups.

Per tablespoon:  80 calories, 1.5 g fat (17% of calories), 25 mg
sodium, 2 g dietary fiber.
