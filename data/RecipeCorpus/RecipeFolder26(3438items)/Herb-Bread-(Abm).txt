Title: Herb Bread (Abm)
Categories: Fatfree, Breadmaker, Bread
Yield: 12 servings
1 1/2    Sugar
1 1/4    Italian seasoning
1 1/2    Dry yeast
3/4    Salt
1    Applesauce or margarine
2    Bread flour
1 1/2    Instant dry milk powder
Combine all ingredients according to manufacturer's directions.
Process in regular, delayed, or rapid bake cycle.
