Title: Curried Fruit
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Curries, Fruit, Fish
Yield: 1 batch
1/4 c  Dry white wine
1 tb Curry powder
Salt and pepper
1 ts Canola oil
1 c  Pear and/or apple; diced
1/3 c  Onion; minced
1/4 c  Raisins
Heat the oil, add the diced fruit and the onion. SautÂ‚, stirring,
until the fruit and onion begin to soften, about 5 minutes. Add the
remaining ingredients and simmer over medium heat until the raisins
are plump and the mixture begins to thicken, about 5 minutes longer.
Taste for seasoning. Spoon warm or cool over grilled or broiled
swordfish.
