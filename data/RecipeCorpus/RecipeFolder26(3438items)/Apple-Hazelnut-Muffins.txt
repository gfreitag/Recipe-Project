Title: Apple-Hazelnut Muffins
Categories: Apples, Fruit, Bread, Muffin
Yield: 1 servings
-cooled
1/2 c  Warm milk
1 t  Vanilla extract
pn Of salt
1/2 c  Chopped dried apples
Preheat oven to 350F.
1/2 c  Hazelnuts, grounded in
-blender to a coarse flour
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
2 T  Baking powder
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
2 T  Unsalted butter, melted and
Combine hazelnuts, flour, baking powder and sugar. Add egg, butter,
milk, vanilla, salt and apples. Let stand 10 minutes. Fill 4 cups of
a nonstick muffin pan (or regular muffin pan with paper liners) with
the batter. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the
center comes out clean. From the cookbook "Apples" by Robert Berkley
