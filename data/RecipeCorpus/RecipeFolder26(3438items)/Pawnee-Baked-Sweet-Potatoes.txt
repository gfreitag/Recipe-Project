Title: Pawnee Baked Sweet Potatoes
Categories: Native amer, Sweetpotato
Yield: 8 servings
-lightly toasted
Honey
4 md Sweet potatoes
2 tb Chopped fresh chives
2 tb Hulled raw sunflower seeds,
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Scrub potatoes and prick a few times
with a fork.  Roast potatoes in a baking pan in the oven for about 1
hour, until fork tender.  When done, slit potatoes on top and push
ends in to plump. Slice each potato in half.  Drizzle with honey and
sprinkle with chives and sunflower seeds.

Serving equals 1/2 potato.
