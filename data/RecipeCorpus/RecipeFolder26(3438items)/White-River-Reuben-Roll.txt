Title: White River Reuben Roll
Categories: Ground beef, Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Vegetable
Yield: 3 servings
1 T  Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Freshly ground black pepper
1 ea 14-oz can sauerkraut, well
-drained (or 14 ounces of a
1 ea Plastic bag of sauerkraut)
1 1/2 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
6 ea Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 c  Fresh caraway rye bread
-crumbs
1 ea Egg, lightly beaten
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
1 T  Sweet pickle relish
1/4 c  Thousand Island dressing
In large bowl, combine beef, bread crumbs, egg, onion, sweet pickle
relish, Thousand Island dressing, Worcestershire, salt and pepper.
Work mixture with hands until blended.  Shape mixture into 6x14-inch
rectangle on a sheet of waxed paper.  Sprinkle with sauerkraut and
Swiss cheese, leaving a 1-inch border. Starting with the narrow end,
carefully roll the mixture, using wax paper as a guide. Place roll in
shallow pan and bake uncovered at 350 F for 45 minutes. Creme de
Colorado Cookbook (1987) From the collection of Jim Vorheis
