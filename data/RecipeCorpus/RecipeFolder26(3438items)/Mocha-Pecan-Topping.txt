Title: Mocha Pecan Topping
Categories: Nuts/grains, Waffles, Pancakes, Breakfast, Syrup
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped
1 c  Chocolate syrup;
2 tb Instant coffee;
Combine and warm syrup and coffee.  When cool add nuts. Yield: 1-1/4
cups Source: Mrs. Carter J. Cain from "The Stuffed Cougar". Brought
to you and yours via Nancy O'Brion and Meal-Master.
