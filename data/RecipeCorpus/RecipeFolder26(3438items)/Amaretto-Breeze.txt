Title: Amaretto Breeze
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Whipping cream
1 pt Blackberries or raspberries
1 pt Strawberries [sliced length
-wise]
1 pk (8oz) Philadelphia Brand
Cream Cheese [softened]
1/2 c  BREAKSTONES Sour Cream
1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Almond flavored liqueur
Easy elegance... For a refreashing change, serve the sauce with melon
balls blueberries, sliced peaches, sliced pears, or any comination
that you can think of...

Beat the cream cheese and sour cream together in a small
1)
mixing bowl at med. speedwith an electric mixer, adding in the sugar,
liqueur, and cream. Mix `til well blended and then chill...

Toss the fruit together and spoon into individual serving
2)
dishes, and top with the chilled cream cheese sauce...
