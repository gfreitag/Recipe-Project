Title: Chinese-Style Pasta Salad - Country Living
Categories: Salad, Side dish, Pasta
Yield: 6 servings
-and strings removed
1/3 c  Coarsely chopped walnuts
1/4 t  Chinese five-spice powder
1 pk 8-oz rotini (spirals)
2 T  Margarine
1 1/2 t  Hot chili oil
2 c  Sugar-snap pea pods, tips
1. Prepare rotini pasta following package directions.

2. Meanwhile, in large, nonstick skillet, heat margarine and oil over
md heat. Add peas and cook, stirring, until brightly colored and
tender. Stir in walnuts and five-spice powder and cook until walnuts
are lightly toasted-about 2 minutes.

3. Toss sugar-snap-pea mixture with drained cooked pasta. Serve
immediately.
