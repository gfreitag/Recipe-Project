Title: Irish Cream Liqueur 1
Categories: Beverage, Alcohol, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Coconut extract
3 tb Chocolate syrup
13 oz Scotch Whiskey, (mickey)
3    Eggs
15 oz Canned milk or "Eagle Brand"
1/2 pt Whipping cream
Combine all ingredients in a blended for 1 minute. Let stand over
night. Bottle the next day. No waiting time. From: Lindsey Jones
