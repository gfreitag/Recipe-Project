Title: Philadelphia Ice Cream
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream
Yield: 1 servings
-grains salt
5 t  Vanilla extract
1 qt Light cream
3/4 c  Granulated sugar   Few
Mix all ingredients; stir until sugar dissolves. Then freeze in 2-qt.
crank freezer until difficult to turn, using 8 parts crushed ice to 1
part ice-cream salt. Makes about 1 qt.
