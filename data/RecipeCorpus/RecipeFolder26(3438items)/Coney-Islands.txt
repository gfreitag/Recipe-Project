Title: Coney Islands
Categories: Ground beef, Sandwich, Sthrn/livng, Hot dogs, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 10 sandwiches
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
10    Wieners
10    Wiener buns
1/2 lb Beef, ground
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 ts Chili powder
Cook ground beef and onion, stirring with fork as beef browns.  Mix in
tomato sauce, chili powder, salt, and Worcestershire sauce.  Cook 10
minutes, stirring occasionally.  Heat 10 wieners and put in heated
buns. Top with sauce.
