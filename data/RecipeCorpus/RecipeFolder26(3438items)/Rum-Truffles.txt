Title: Rum Truffles
Categories: Candy, Alcohol, Chocolate
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Dark rum
3 tb Butter
Powdered chocolate
9 oz Chocolate
1 1/2 tb Half and half
3    Eggs
Melt the chocolate and half and half in a pan over very low heat.
Remove from heat. Add eggs, butter and rum. Blend the mixture until
thick. Refrigerate. When cool, form into small balls, about 3/4" and
toss in powdered chocolate - not cocoa. They freeze well and pack
well in airtight containers.
