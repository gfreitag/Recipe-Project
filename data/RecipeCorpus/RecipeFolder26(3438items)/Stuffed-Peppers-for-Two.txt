Title: Stuffed Peppers for Two
Categories: Main dish, Ground beef, Sthrn/livng, Vegetable
Yield: 2 servings
1 tb Onion; chopped
1/2 ts Salt
ds Pepper
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Egg; beaten
2 md Green peppers
1/2 lb Beef, ground
1/4 c  Rice, instant; uncooked
8 oz Tomato sauce with cheese;
-divided
Cut off top of each green pepper; remove seeds.  Cook 5 minutes in
boiling salted water to cover.  Drain; set aside.

Combine ground beef, rice, 1/4 cup tomato sauce, and remaining
ingredients. Stuff peppers with mixture, and place in a small baking
pan.

Pour remaining tomato sauce over stuffed peppers.  Cover and bake at
350 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes or until meat is done, basting
peppers twice with drippings.
