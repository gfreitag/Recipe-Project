Title: Marvelous Fruit Mix-Up
Categories: Salad, Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
8 oz Sour Cream (1 Container)
20 oz Crushed Pineapple, Drained
-(1 Can)
11 oz Mandarin Orange Sections,
-Drained (1 Can)
1/4 c  Chopped Walnuts
1 c  Miniature Marshmallows
Here is the final old stand
-by for either desserts or
-salads.
Makes 8 Servings
3 ea Ozs Lemon-Flavored Gelatin
-(1 Package)
2 c  Ice Cubes
In a large bowl, stir 1/4 cup of boiling water into the gelatin,
stirring until completely dissolved.  Add the ice cube and stir
constantly for 3 to 5 minutes or until mixture is slightly thickened.
With a slotted spoon, remove any unmelted ice.  Stir in the sour
cream until well blended. Set aside the following: 1/2 cup pineapple,
1/4 cup of mandarin orange sections, and 1 Tbls of chopped walnuts,
for the garnish. Stir in the remaining ingredients and pour into an
8-inch square baking dish topping with the garnish mixture that you
set aside. Refrigerate at least 4 hours or overnight until firm.

From The Redbook Magazine January 1986
