Title: Frosted Orange Foamy
Categories: Beverage, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
-using all Orange Juice at
-the time.
Makes 1 serving
This fabulous frothy drink
-was something devised by
-one of the
Diners on the East Coast,
Process 1/2 Cup Ice Cubes, 1/2 Cup Freshly Squeezed Orange Juice and
1 Tbls Instant Orange Breakfast drink in a blender that will handle
crushing ice. Blend until smooth and serve by pouring into a frosted
10 Oz glass garnished with an orange slice.

From The Redbook Magazine January 1986
