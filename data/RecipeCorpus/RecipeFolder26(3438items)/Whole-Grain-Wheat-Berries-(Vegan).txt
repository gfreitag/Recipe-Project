Title: Whole-Grain Wheat Berries (Vegan)
Categories: Fatfree, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Salt, (added AFTER cooking)
2 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Whole-grain wheat, rinsed
In 2qt saucepan, boil water, stir in wheat, and return to boil.
Reduce to simmer, covered, for 2 hours, until almost all of the
liquid has been absorbed. Remove from heat, let stand for 15 minutes.
Add salt and fluff with a fork
