Title: Grape Catsup (Good with Baked Beans or Hash)
Categories: Relish, Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
1 t  Ground cloves
2 c  Sugar
1 qt Grapes
1 c  Vinegar
1 t  Cinnamon
Pick grapes from stem and measure and stew with vinegar.  Strain
through sieve, add spices and boil till slightly thickened.  Bottle.
"Just great with New England Baked Beans"
