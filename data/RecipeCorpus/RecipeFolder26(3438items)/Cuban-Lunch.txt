Title: Cuban Lunch
Categories: Candy, Chocolate
Yield: 75 servings
-chips
1 c  Chopped peanuts
12 oz Pkg pure chocolate wafers
12 oz Pkg peanut butter chips
7 oz Pkg crushed regular potato
This sweet treat is excellent for Christmas or large buffets -
anytime you want to serve a special dessert nibble. Melt chocolate
wafers and peanut butter chips. Mix in peanuts and potato chips.
Spoon or pour into small paper baking cups.  Store in the
refrigerator. Makes 75.

Origin:  Homestyles, Canadian Classics Shared by: Sharon Stevens
