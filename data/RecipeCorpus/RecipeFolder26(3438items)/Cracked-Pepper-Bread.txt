Title: Cracked Pepper Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Bread
Yield: 2 servings
2 ts Coarsely ground pepper
1 pk Yeast (2 1/4 tsp.)
1 1/4 c  Milk
2 tb Butter
1    Egg
-America's Best Recipes
4 1/4 c  A-p flour (I had to add
-5 Tbsp. flour - it all
-fit in my Regal)
2 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
Put all ingredients into pot & push "Start".  Adjust for wetness or
dryness.

From America's Best Recipes, 1989 (credited to Cooking in Clover II).
Slightly adjusted by me. <g>
