Title: Hopping Jack
Categories: Beans, Main dish, Pork, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
2 cn Black beans, rinsed and
-drained well (15 oz. ea.)
1/4 c  Tomato paste
1 t  Brown sugar
1/4 t  Allspice
1/2 t  Dried thyme
1/2 t  Dried basil
*Use black eye peas for
-Hopping John.
1 t  Olive oil
2 lg Cloves garlic, minced
1 lg Onion, finely chopped
1/2 t  Minced jalapeno pepper
1 lg Green pepper, seeded and
-finely chopped OR
2 ea Poblano peppers, seeded and
-finely chopped
1/2 lb Baked ham, cut into cubes
In a large saucepan, saute garlic and onion in the oil over medium
heat for a minute or so, then adding the hot and green peppers,
cooking for another minute. Add the remaining ingredients and simmer
for 10-15 minutes, adding a little water if it gets too thick. Serve
with rice and any topping you prefer. SOURCE:* Mother Earth News
magazine, Feb/Mar 1995 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 1/95
