Title: Belmar Fluke
Categories: Fish, Main dish, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 T  Chopped green onions
2 t  Chopped parsley
Oil for frying
2 lb Skinned fluke fillets
1/2 t  Garlic powder or salt
1/2 c  Mashed potato flakes (Heap)
1 c  Chicken broth
Fry fluke fillets in hot oil in pan for 4 or 5 minutes on each side
until golden brown.  Check with fork to see if the fillets flake
easily. Drain on paper and keep warm until sauce is made.  In a pan,
add garlic salt or powder, potato flakes and broth, and simmer for 1
to 2 minutes, or until thick, stirring constantly.  Pour sauce over
the fish and garnish with green onion and parsley.
