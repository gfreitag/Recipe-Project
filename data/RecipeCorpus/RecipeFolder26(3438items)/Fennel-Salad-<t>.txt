Title: Fennel Salad <t>
Categories: Fatfree, Vegetable, Fruit, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
2    Oranges, peeled and
Sectioned
1    Orange, juiced
2 T  Rice vinegar
3    To 4 cups fennel, thinly
Sliced or anise bulb, use
Inside part of the bulb
1/2 c  Or so watercress leaves
Toss with juice of 1 orange and 2 TBSP rice vinegar. Serve on lettuce
or more watercress.
