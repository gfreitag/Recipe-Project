Title: Sourdough Pancakes
Categories: Bread, Pancakes, Sourdough
Yield: 28 pancakes
MMMMM-------------------BH&G HERITAGE COOKBOOK------------------------
1    Beaten egg
1 c  Starter
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Cooking oil
1 c  All-purpose flour
2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking soda
In a bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and baking soda.
Combien egg, the 1 cup starter, milk, and oil; stir into flour
mixture till combined. Using 2 tablespoonfuls batter for each
pancake, bake on hit, lightly greased griddle till golden brown; turn
once.
