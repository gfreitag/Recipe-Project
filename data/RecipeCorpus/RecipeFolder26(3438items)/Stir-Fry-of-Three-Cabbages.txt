Title: Stir-Fry of Three Cabbages
Categories: Vegetable, Asian, Stir fry
Yield: 1 recipe
-finely shredded
2 c  Chinese cabbage
-finely shredded
1 tb Canola or vegetable oil
1 tb Fresh gingerroot
-finely minced
1 ts Sesame seeds
2 tb Rice vinegar
2 ts Cornstarch
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Sesame oil
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper flakes
2 c  Finely shredded red cabbage
2 c  Green cabbage
1. Whisk the vinegar into the cornstarch until dissolved; then whisk
in the soy sauce, sesame oil and pepper flakes.  Set aside.

2. Combine the cabbages in a large bowl.

3. In a large heave pan heat the oil over medium-high heat.  Add the
cabbages and ginger; stir-fry 2 minutes, until cabbage is starting to
wilt.

4. Add the sauce ingredients and stir until thickened.  Stir in the
sesame seeds and serve.

Makes 4 to 6 servings.

Data per serving (for 6 servings): Calories 68, Protein 1g, Fat 5g,
Carbohydrates 5g, Sodium 349mg, Saturated fat 1g, Monounsaturated fat
2g, Polyunsaturated fat 2g, Cholesterol 0mg.
