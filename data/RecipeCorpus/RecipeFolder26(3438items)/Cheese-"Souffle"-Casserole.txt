Title: Cheese "Souffle" Casserole
Categories: Crockpot, Cheese/eggs, Casserole
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 T  Butter or oleo
6 ea Eggs
14 sl Bread, crust removed
3 c  Grated sharp cheese
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1/2 t  Salt
Paprika
3 c  Milk, scalded
2 t  Worcestershire sauce
Tear bread into small pieces. Place half in well-greased CROCK-POT.
Add ha)f of grated cheese and half of butter. Add remaining bread,
cheese and butter. Beat eggs, milk, Worcestershire sauce and salt.
Pour over bread and cheese. Sprinkle top with paprika. Cover and cook
on Low 4 to 5 hours. Serves 4.
