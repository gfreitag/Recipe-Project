Title: Sweet Potato-Apple Casserole
Categories: Sweetpotato, Apples, Microwave, Casserole, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
-margarine, melted
1/4 c  Fresh orange juice
1 T  Freshly grated orange peel
1 t  Cinnamon
1/4 t  Mace
6 md Sweet potatoes (about 5 lbs)
4 md Apples, cored and thinly
-sliced (about 1 lb)
1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts or pecans
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter or
Pierce potatoes with fork.  Arrange on microwave-safe rack.  Cook on
High 20 min, turning halfway through cooking time.  Peel potatoes and
slice thinly.  Arrange apples in shallow glass or ceramic baking
dish. Sprinkle with sugar.  Layer potato slices evenly over apples.
Sprinkle nuts over potatoes.  Combine remaining ingredients in small
bowl.  Pour over top. Cover and cook on high until apples are tender,
5 to 7 min. Serve warm.
