Title: Holiday Pralines
Categories: Candy, Holiday, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
3 T  Butter
1 c  Pecans, chopped
1 T  Maple syrup
1 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Light cream
1/8 t  Salt
Bring brown sugar, sugar and cream to a full boil for 1 minute. Add
remaining ingredients and cook to soft-ball stage, about 234 degrees.
Quickly stir and drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper; cool. Posted
