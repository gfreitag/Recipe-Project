Title: Butterscotch Chip Chocolate Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 42 cookies
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Chopped pecans (optional)
1    Chocolate cake mix
2 c  Nestle Butterscotch
-morsels
Combine cake mix, oil, and eggs in large bowl.  Stir in morsels and
pecans. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto ungreased baking sheets.

Bake in preheated 350 oven for 8 to 10 minutes or until centers are
just set.  Let stand for 2 minutes; remove to wire racks to cool
completely. Submitted By CHERYL NELSON   On   11-08-94
