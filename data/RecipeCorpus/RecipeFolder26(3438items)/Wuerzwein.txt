Title: Wuerzwein
Categories: Beverage, Alcohol, Herbs/spice, German
Yield: 4 servings
3    Cloves, whole
2    Lemons
1    Orange
4 c  Red Wine
1 1/2 c  Water
3 oz Sugar
1    Cinnamon stick
1. Peel the lemons and the orange real fine and keep the juice
for something else. 2. Mix wine, water, cinnamon, cloves, sugar,
lemonpeel and
orange peel in a pot. 3. Heat, until very hot but DO NOT BOIL. 4.
Discard the seasons and peel and serve very hot. 5. Decorate with a
slice of lemon if desired.

Out of "1 X 1 Der guten Kueche"
