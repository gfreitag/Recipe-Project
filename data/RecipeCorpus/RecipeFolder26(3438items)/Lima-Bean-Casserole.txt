Title: Lima Bean Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Beans, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese (4
-oz.)
1/4 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
2 c  Frozen baby lima beans
1 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
-soup, undiluted (10 3/4
-oz.)
1/2 c  Chopped celery
In a bowl, combine lima beans, soup, celery and milk. Pour into a
greased 11" x 7" x 2" baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese and bread
crumbs. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes. Uncover; bake
10 minutes longer or until the bread crumbs are lightly browned.
Serves 6-8. SOURCE:*Taste of Home Magazine Dec/Jan 1994 POSTED BY:
