Title: Polish Sausage
Categories: Sausage, Pork, Beef
Yield: 1 servings
8 ea Cloves Garlic, pressed
1/4 t  Ground Allspice
3 1/2 t  Salt
1 c  Cold Water
4 lb Coarse Ground Pork Butt
1 lb Coarse Ground Beef Stew Meat
2 T  Coarsely Ground Pepper
1 T  Marjoram Leaves
Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into hog casing or make
patties. SOURCE: The Sausage Making Cookbook, by Jerry Predika
Collection of Clarence Fontish
