Title: Chocolate-Coconut Candies
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 gift
1 lb Flaked coconut
12 oz Bag chocolate chips
2 tb Shortening
1 c  Cold or warm mashed potatoes
1 lb Powdered sugar
1 ts Almond extract
Mix potatoes, powdered sugar, and almond extract in large bowl. Stir
in coconut. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper; shape into
balls. If mixture is too soft, refrigerate until able to handle. Heat
chocolate chips and shortening over low heat until melted. dip 1 ball
at a time until coated; place on waxed paper. Chill balls until firm
(why did this strike me funny?) About 4 dozen candies.

Coconut Bars: Press into an ungreased 13x9x2 pan. Spread with melted
chocolate. Chill until firm; cut into bars 2x1". About 4 dozen bars.

Source: Betty Crocker's New Christmas Cookbook ISBN: 0-671-79927-4
