Title: Hi-Speed 100% Whole Wheat Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Bread
Yield: 2 servings
3 tb Molasses
1 1/8 c  Hot tap water (fill the
-included measuring cup
-all the way to the top)
3 c  Whole wheat flour
-T-Fox5 [JOE EKAITIS]
2 ts Active dry yeast
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Powdered milk
1 1/2 tb Cooking oil
Measure the first 6 ingredients into the pan in the order listed.
Holding the pan by the handle, twist your hand back and forth to
swish the ingredients together about 10 times.

Add the flour, 1 cup at a time, and swish the pan again after each
addition.  By the time you put the pan in the machine, the yeast will
already be bubbling merrily away.

Select the BREAD RAPID setting and install the pan. Push the START
pad and come back when it's finised.
