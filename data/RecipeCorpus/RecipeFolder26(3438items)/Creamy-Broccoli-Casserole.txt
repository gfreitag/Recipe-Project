Title: Creamy Broccoli Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Vegetable, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
2 pk Frozen chopped broccoli,
-cooked and drained (10 oz.
-ea.)
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese (4
-oz.)
1 T  Butter or margarine, melted
1/4 c  Soft bread crumbs
2 ea Eggs
1 cn Condensed cream of mushroom
-soup, undiluted (10 3/4
-oz.)
1 c  Mayonnaise
3/4 c  Chopped pecans
1 md Onion, chopped
In a bowl, beat eggs; add soup, mayonnaise, pecans, and onion. Stir in
broccoli; pour into a greased 2 quart shallow baking disk. Sprinkle
with the cheese. Combine butter and bread crumbs; sprinkle on top.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Serves 8-10. SOURCE:*
Reminisce Magazine Nov./Dec. 1994 POSTED BY: Jim Bodle 12/94
