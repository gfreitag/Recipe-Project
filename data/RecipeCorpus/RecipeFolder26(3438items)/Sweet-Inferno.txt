Title: Sweet Inferno
Categories: Condiment, Spread
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Honey
2 c  Mayonnaise
4 oz Dry mustard
1/2 c  Tarragon vinegar
3/4 c  Dry white wine
2    Eggs
Combine mustard, vinegar and wine.  Stir well and let stand
overnight. In top of double boiler, mix eggs, sugar and honey. Bring
water to a boil. Reduce heat to medium, stirring constantly, until
mixture is thickened. Remove from heat.  When cool, add mustard
mixture and mayonnaise. Store in refrigerator.  Wicked on sandwiches.
