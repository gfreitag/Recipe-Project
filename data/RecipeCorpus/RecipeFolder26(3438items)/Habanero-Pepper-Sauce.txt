Title: Habanero Pepper Sauce
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce, Chilies
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Chopped carrots
1/2 c  Distilled vinegar
1/4 c  Lime juice
12 ea Habanero chiles, stems
-removed, chopped
1/2 c  Chopped onion
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
Saute the onion and garlic in the oil until soft.  Add the carrots
with a small amount of water.  Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, and
simmer until the carrots are soft. Place the mixture and the chiles
in a blender, and puree the mixture until smooth. Combine the puree
with the vinegar and lime juice and simmer for 5 minutes to combine
the flavors. Strain the mixture into sterilized bottles and seal. The
