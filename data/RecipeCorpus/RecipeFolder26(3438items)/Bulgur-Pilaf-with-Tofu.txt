Title: Bulgur Pilaf with Tofu
Categories: Vegetable, Nuts/grains, Side dish
Yield: 1 servings
ds Of pepper
9 oz Tofu
2 t  Soy sauce
1 ea Tomato, chopped
Parmesan cheese
ds Of parsley
3 T  Butter or oil
2 ea Cloves garlic
1 c  Bulgur wheat
1 sm Onion, chopped
2 c  Vegetable stock
1/4 t  Oregano
Melt butter in saucepan. Add bulgur, onion, and garlic and saute for
4 to 5 minutes. Remove garlic buds. Add stock, oregano and pepper,
cover pan, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15
minutes, or until all liquid is absorbed. Combine tofu and soy sauce
in a small bowl and mash together, then stir into the cooked grain.
Serve hot seasoned with parmesan cheese and parsley stirred in. AR/95
