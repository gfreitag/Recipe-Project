Title: Marmalade Nut Bread
Categories: Bread, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Orange marmalade
1 tb Orange rind; freshly
Grated
1 c  Pecans or walnuts; chopped
3 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs; beaten
1 c  Milk
Recipe by: Fannie Flagg's Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook Preheat
oven to 350Â° F. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a
large bowl. Combine remaining ingredients except pecans, mixing well.
Make a well in center of flour mixture; add milk mixture, and stir
just until dry ingredients are moistened. Stir in pecans. Pour into a
greased and floured 9- x 5- x 3-inch loaf pan. Bake for 1 hour and 10
to 15 mins, or until a wooden pick inserted comes out clean. Let cool
in pan 10 mins; remove from pan and let cool completely on a wire
rack. YIELD: 1 loaf

NOTES : Delicious (toasted or not) spread with softened cream cheese.

From:  Dan Klepach
