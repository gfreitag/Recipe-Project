Title: Sizzling Steaks and Salsa
Categories: Beef, Cheese/eggs, Salsa/sauce, Tex/mexican, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------FROM: RICH HARPER, COOKING E---------------------
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 4 SERVINGS--------------------------
-Chopped
1/4 t  Ground Cumin
1/2 c  Cheddar Cheese, Finely
-Shredded
Cilantro Sprigs
1 lb Boneless Beef Sirloin
-Steak, Cut 3/4-inch Thick
3/4 c  Chopped And Seeded Tomatoes
1/2 c  Salsa
2 md Green Onions With Tops,
Combine the tomatoes, salsa, onions and cumin and set aside.  Trim the
exterior fat and cut the boneless beef top sirloin steak into 4
serving sized pieces.  Place each on a flat surface, cover with waxed
paper and flatten with the bottom of a heavy saucepan, mallet, or
cleaver to 1/4-inch thick.  Heat a nonstick frying pan over medium
high heat for 2 minutes. Quickly pan broil the steaks for 1 minute.
Turn the steaks and top each with an equal amount of cheese. Cook 1
to 2 minutes, DO NOT overcook. Serve the steaks over the reserved
salsa. Garnish with cilantro. From Cow Country Cusine by Kathy G. Mc
Craine Copyright 1988
