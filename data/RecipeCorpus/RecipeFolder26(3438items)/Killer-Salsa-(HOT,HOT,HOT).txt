Title: Killer Salsa (HOT,HOT,HOT)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chilies
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Oregano
1 c  Tomato Juice(Can Use Up to
-2)
2 ea Pk Dried Chile Pequins *
1 t  Cumin (Crushed)
1/2 t  Salt (Optional)
2 ea Cloves Garlic (3 If Wanted)
*  Or other hot peppers. NOTE:  Add juice of one lemon if you are
canning.
