Title: Ham Waffles
Categories: Breakfast, Pork, Waffles
Yield: 6 waffles 8"
2    Eggs; separated
2 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Margarine; melted
1 1/2 c  Ham, ground; cooked
2 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
Combine flour, soda, salt, and sugar; set aside.  Combine egg yolks,
buttermilk, and margarine; beat well, and add to flour mixture.  Add
ham, and stir well.

Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form; carefully fold into batter.
Bake in preheated waffle iron.
