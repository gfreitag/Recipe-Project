Title: Crunchy Fudgy Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 20 squares
1 c  Whole wheat pastry flour
2 tb Soy flour
1/4 c  Bran
2 tb Milk powder
1/2 c  Carob powder
1/3 c  Chopped walnuts
1/3 c  Vegetable oil; preferably
-olive oil
1/3 c  Honey
2 tb Lecithin granules
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
-sesame seeds
Grapenuts cereal, millet and
Preheat oven to 325F.

In food processor, blender, or mixing bowl, blend the oil, honey,
lecithin, eggs, and vanilla until smooth and creamy.  Add the
combined flours, bran, and milk and carob powders, and process
briefly until ingredients are well-mixed.  Mix in the nuts.

Spoon into an 8-inch-square pan lined with parchment paper or greased
with a mixture of liquid lecithin and oil.  Top with 1 1/2-inch-wide
rows of (alternately) Grapenuts, millet, and sesame seeds.  Bake for
20 to 25 minutes.  Cut into 1 1/2-inch squares.

Approximately 100 calories each square
