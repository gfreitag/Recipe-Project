Title: Mock Butterfingers
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 36 servings
1 pk Milk chocolate chips
1 pk Wheat Thins Crackers
1 c  Creamy peanut butter
Put peanut butter on several crackers and top with another cracker to
make "sandwiches".  Melt chocolate chips in double boiler.  Dip
Cracker "sandwiches" in chocolate, then put on waxed paper to cool.
Make as many as you can with the amount of chocolate you have.
