Title: Onion Lover's Twist
Categories: Bread, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 t  Salt
1/2 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Softened butter or margarine
1 ea Egg
1 lg Or 2 small loaves
1 ea Package dry yeast
1/4 c  Warm water
4 c  Flour
1/4 c  Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 T  Sesame or poppy seed
1/4 t  Garlic powder
3/4 t  Salt
1 t  Paprika
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Finely chopped onion (or
-1/4 cup instant minced
Onions)
1 T  Grated Parmesan cheese
Grease cookie sheet.  In large bowl dissolve yeast in warm water. Add
2 cups flour, sugar, salt, water, milk, butter and egg.  With
electric mixer, blend at low speed until moistened.  Beat 2 minutes
at medium speed. By hand, stir in remaining flour to form a soft
dough. Cover and let rise in a warm place until light and doubled in
size, 45 to 60 minutes. To prepare filling, melt butter in saucepan
and add remaining ingredients. When dough has doubled in size stir
down and place on a floured board. Knead until no longer sticky.
Roll out to an 18x12-inch rectangle. Spread with filling, then cut
lengthwise into three 18x4-inch strips. Beginning with the 18-inch
side, roll up each strip. Seal edges and ends. On the prepared cookie
sheet braid the three rolls together. cover and allow to rise until
doubled, about 45 to 60 minutes.  Bake at 350 F for 30 to 35 minutes,
or until golden brown.  Serve warm or cool. Note: To make 2 small
loaves, cut the 3 filled rolls in half crosswise before braiding.
Braid each set of rolls separately on greased cookie sheet; bake as
directed. Colorado Cache Cookbook (1978) From the collection of Jim
Vorheis
