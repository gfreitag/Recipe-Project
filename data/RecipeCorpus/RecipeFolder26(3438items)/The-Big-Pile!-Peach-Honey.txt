Title: The Big Pile! Peach Honey
Categories: Can/preserv, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
12 oz Dried peaches
2 1/2 c  Water
2 c  Sugar
Combine all ingredients in a large saucepan. Bring to boiling over
high heat. Reduce heat; simmer 45 minutes or until peaches are soft.
Puree in blender or food processor; pour into hot, sterilized jars
and seal. Makes about 3 half pints. Source: The Big Pile! Typed by
Carolyn Shaw 12-94
