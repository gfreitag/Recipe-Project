Title: New England Nutmeg Sauce
Categories: Dessert, Salsa/sauce, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Butter
1 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour
1 c  Water; boiling
Recipe by: The American Heritage Cookbook - ISBN 0-8281-04-03-4 Mix
together 1 cup sugar and 1 Tbs flour. Stir in 1 cup boiling water and
cook, stirring constantly, until sauce bubbles and thickens slightly.
Add 1 Tbs butter and simmer gently for 5 mins. Remove from heat and
stir in 1 tsp nutmeg. Serve hot.

From:  Dan Klepach
