Title: Mrs. Arnold's Daughter Martha's Egg; or "A Martha Egg"
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
Pepper
Butter
1 lg Egg
1/4 c  Milk
1 pn Salt
Beat an egg with the milk, salt and pepper.  Fry in butter at a low
heat. Serve with a sweet smile and a kind word.  If serving to a kid,
pat it on the head.  This egg is pure love and heals all wounds.

From "White Trash Cooking" by Ernest Matthew Mickler. U/L by Burt
