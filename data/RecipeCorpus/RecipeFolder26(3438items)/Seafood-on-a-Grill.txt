Title: Seafood on a Grill
Categories: Seafood, Bbq/grill, Fish, Crab/shrimp, Clam/oyster
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1/2 t  Thyme
1/2 c  Lemon Juice
Salt and Pepper to taste
1 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 t  Tarragon
3 ea Cloves garlic, chopped
1/2 t  Rosemary
Simmer butter, lemon juice and all spices in pan on stove for a few
minutes. Make packages in doubled heavy duty tin foil. Put in fillet,
clams, a half a lobster tail, shrimp, scallops, anything will do but
a mix is best. Divide the Butter sauce among the packages and seal
the foil well. Cook on the grill, sealed side first for 10 min. then
turn over & when it starts steaming well it is done. Can be done in
the oven.
