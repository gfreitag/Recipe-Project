Title: Souscaille (Fresh Mango Appetizer)
Categories: Appetizer, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
Hard green unripe mangoes
MMMMM-----------------------SAUCE PIQUANTE----------------------------
1    Fresh hot green chili
-finely chopped
-more to taste
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 c  Cold water
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
1/2 ts Salt
Freshly ground pepper
Peel the mango and cut it into thin slices. Marinate it in the hot
sauce for at least 10 minutes, longer if desired. The longer you
marinate the mango, the hotter it will be. This is traditionally
eaten by hand. Submitted By DIANE LAZARUS   On   01-13-95
