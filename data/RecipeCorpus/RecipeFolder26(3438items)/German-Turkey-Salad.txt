Title: German Turkey Salad
Categories: Salad, Turkey, German, Potato, Apples
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM----------------KEYWORDS: SOUR, MUSTARD, TOM---------------------
Sugar
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 t  Duesseldorf mustard
2 t  Tomato paste
2 T  Sour cream
1 lb Turkey, thinly sliced into
-strips
3 ea Apples, peeled and cored
3 lg Gherkin pickles
10 ea Cooked, cooled potatoes
Lemon juice
Slice the apples, pickles and potatoes into strips and add turkey.
Season with lemon juice and a little sugar.  Toss to mix well.
Meanwhile, add mayonnaise, mustard, tomato paste and sour cream
together. Mix together and chill for at least 1 hour. Garnish with
tomato wedges and quartered hardboiled eggs.  Serves 6. Origin:
Sharon Stevens. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
