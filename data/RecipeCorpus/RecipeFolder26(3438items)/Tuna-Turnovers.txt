Title: Tuna Turnovers
Categories: Appetizer, Snack, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 2 servings
1 ts Flour
1 lg Clove garlic, crushed
1/4 ts Salt
2    6 1/2cn tuna fish, drained
1 tb Chopped onion
4 oz Can mushrooms, drained,
-reserving liquid
1/2 c  Chopped pitted olives
2 tb Butter
-Pastry for 2 crust pie
:

Saute onion, mushrooms, and olives in butter for 5 minutes. Stir in
flour, garlic, salt, and reserved mushroom liquid; heat thoroughly.
Add tuna and mix well; remove from heat. Preheat oven to 450. Roll
out pastry. Cut into 2 1/2" circles. Place 2 tsp tuna mixture in
cemter of each pastry circle. Fold pastry over; dampen edges to seal.
Place on baking sheet; bake for 7 minutes, or until crust is golden
brown. Remove from oven, and serve at once.
