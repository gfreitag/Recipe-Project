Title: Beef Fajitas
Categories: Tex/mexican, Beef, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
Flour tortillas, warmed
lg Tomato, chopped
Green onions, chopped
1 lb Beef top round steak
(1/2-inch thick) or
Flank or skirt steak
Juice of 2 to 3 limes
1 ts Garalic salt (or more)
Pound beef steak to 1/4-inch thickness.  Place steak in plastic bag;
sprinkle both sides of steak with lime juice, garlic salt, and pepper.
Close bag securely and marinate in refrigerator 6 to 8 hours (or
overnight, if desired).  Drain and discard marinade.  Panbroil over
medium heat 5 to 7 minutes, turning occasionally.  Carve across the
grain into thin slices. Serve steak in warmed tortillas; add tomatoes
and onions. Makes 4 servings. [ 1989 Beef Board and Beef Industry
Council ]
