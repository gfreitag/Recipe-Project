Title: Dijon Green Onion Appetizers
Categories: Appetizer, Vegetable, Bread
Yield: 40 servings
-diagonally
Into 40 pieces
Sliced ripe olives
-(optional) [I don't use
-'em]
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan
2 T  Grey Poupon Dijon mustard
2 ea Green onions - sliced
1 ea 8-oz. French bread
-baguette - sliced
Combine mayonnaise, cheese, mustard and onions.  Spread approximately
2 teaspoons mixture on each bread slice.  Place cheese side up on
baking sheet. Broil until lightly golden. Garnish with ripe olives
just before broiling, if desired.

40 appetizers (depending on how thickly you slice the bread).
