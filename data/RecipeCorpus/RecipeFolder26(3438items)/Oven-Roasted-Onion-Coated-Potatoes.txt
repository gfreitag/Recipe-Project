Title: Oven Roasted Onion-Coated Potatoes
Categories: Fatfree, Vegetable, Potato
Yield: 1 servings
6    Potatoes, cut up
2    Egg-equivalent of replacer
1 pk Lipton Onion Soup
Put all the ingredients in a plastic bag or anything else that will
hold up to the egg mix.

Lay in a single layer in a baking pan.  Bake at 350 degrees until
tender.
