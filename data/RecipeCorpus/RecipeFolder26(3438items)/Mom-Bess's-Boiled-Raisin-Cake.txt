Title: Mom Bess's Boiled Raisin Cake
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Mom's, Fruit
Yield: 1 cake
1 ts Cloves
1 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Water raisins were boiled in
1 ts Soda
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Raisins
2 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
3 tb Molasses
1 ts Cinnamon
(only directions given)

Boil the 1 Cup raisins for 20 minutes in the 2 Cups water.
