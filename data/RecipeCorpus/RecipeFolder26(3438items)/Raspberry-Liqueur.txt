Title: Raspberry Liqueur
Categories: Alcohol, Homemade, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Lemon, juiced and rind
-grated fine
1 ea Bottle vodka
4 c  Raspberries
2 c  Sugar
Mix all together.  Stir until sugar is completely dissolved.  Pour
into bottles.  Cap in sterilized glass bottles and store in cool dark
place. Make 1 26 oz and 1 13 oz bottle. Origin: My Dad, Lorne
Whittaker. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
