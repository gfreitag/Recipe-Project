Title: Southern Cream Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Bacon or sausage drippings
1/4 c  Self-rising flour
1 c  Milk
Recipe by: Fannie Flagg's Original Whistle Stop Cafe Cookbook Combine
drippings and flour in a large heavy skillet. Cook over medium heat
until color is pale brown. Stir in milk, water, salt, and pepper;
cook until thickened, stirring constantly. YIELD: 2 cups
