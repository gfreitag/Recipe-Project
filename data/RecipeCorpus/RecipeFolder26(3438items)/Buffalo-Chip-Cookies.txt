Title: Buffalo Chip Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 30 servings
2 t  Vanilla
4 c  Flour, all-purpose
2 t  Baking soda
1 c  Pecans
1 c  Coconut
2 t  Baking powder
2 c  Rice Krispies
1 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Shortening
1 lb Brown sugar
2 c  Oatmeal
1 pk 6 oz Chocolate chips
2 c  Sugar
4 ea Eggs
You'll need to start with a big bowl for this one. If this is your
first try, you might want to do a half-batch.

Cream butter and shortening. Add brown sugar, eggs, sugar and
vanilla. Sift and add the flour, baking powder, baking soda; add
oatmel, chocolate chips, pecans, Rice Krispies and coconut. Use 1/4
measured cup per cookie or ice cream scoop. Put on ungreased cookie
sheet, 6 to a sheet. Cook 15 minutes at 350 F; let cool 5 minutes
before removing from sheet. Makes 30-40 cookies.
