Title: Sugarplums
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 48 servings
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 ds Salt
6 oz Chocolate chips
2    Egg whites, stiffly beaten
2/3 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Baking powder
Preheat oven to 350 F. While beating egg whites, gradually add salt,
baking powder, sugar and vanilla. Beat until stiff. Fold in chocolate
chips. Drop by teaspoonfuls on WELL-GREASED cookie sheet. Turn off
oven, and place cookie sheet in oven overnight, or at least 5-6 hours.
