Title: Tuna Terrific
Categories: Main dish, Seafood, Fish, Rice
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Toasted slivered almonds
1/4 c  Dry sherry
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Rosemary
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/4 ts Pepper
3 c  Cooked rice
2 cn Tuna fish packed in oil,
-undrained
1 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
1/2 c  Diced green pepper
1/4 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1/4 c  Minced pimento
Drain tuna oil into frying pan; heat. Add celery, onion, and green
pepper; cook, stirring constantly, for 3-4 minutes. Add tuna fish,
mushrooms, pimento, almonds, , sherry, rosemary, marjoram, and pepper
to celery mixture. Simmer until heated through, stirring
occasionally. Add cooked rice and serve.
