Title: Ninfa's Green Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Fresh cilantro, minced OR
1/4 ts Dried cilantro
1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
2    OR
3 tb Fresh lemon juice
1    RIPE avocado
1/4 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Minced onion
1    Clove garlic, mashed
1.  Peel avocado and mash to a smooth pulp. 2. Add sour cream, milk,
onion, garlic, juice and blend with a
wire whip. 3. Let stand at least an hour before serving.
Refrigerate any
:   leftover sauce.
