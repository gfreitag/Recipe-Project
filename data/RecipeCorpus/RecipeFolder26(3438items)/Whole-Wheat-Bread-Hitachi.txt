Title: Whole Wheat Bread Hitachi
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 servings
3 T  Sugar
1 1/2 T  Butter
1 1/2 T  Dried milk
1 1/2 t  Dry yeast or
2 t  Dry yeast Bread Rapid
****Medium Loaf****
3/4 c  Tap water
1 3/4 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Whole wheat flour
1 t  Salt
2 T  Sugar
1 T  Butter
1 T  Dry milk
1 t  Dry yeast or
2 t  Dry yeast Bread Rapid
****Extra Large Loaf****
11 1/2 oz Tap water (1 7/16 cups)
3 1/2 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
2 t  Salt
4 T  Sugar
2 T  Butter
2 T  Dry milk
2 t  Dry yeast or
3 t  Dry yeast Bread Rapid
****Large Loaf****
1 1/8 c  Tap water
2 2/3 c  Bread flour
3/8 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/2 t  Salt
Pour water in bread pan, then all dry ingredients except yeast. Place
Dry yeast on top of flour away from water.  Return bread pan to
bakery. Close lid and plug into a receptacle.  Set controls per
machine instructions.

Source:  Hitachi Automatic Home Bakery Cookbook From: Wayne Long,
Tulsa, Oklahoma
