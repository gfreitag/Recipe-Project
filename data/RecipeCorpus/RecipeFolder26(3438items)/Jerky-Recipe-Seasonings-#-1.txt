Title: Jerky Recipe Seasonings # 1
Categories: Can/preserv, Marinade, Information
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/8 t  Pepper
2 T  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 t  Ground ginger
---Bob's Beef Jerky---
4 T  Soy sauce
4 T  Teriyaki sauce
1 T  Corriander
1/4 t  Pepper
1 T  Ketchup (May use hot
-ketchup if desired)
4 T  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 t  Liquid smoke
1/4 t  Garlic powder
---Mild Mexican Jerky---
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1 t  Chili powder
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1/2 t  Crushed oregano
1 t  Paprika
---Curried Jerky---
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1/8 t  Cinnamon
1/8 t  Ground cloves
1 1/2 t  Curry Powder
1/2 t  Garlic powder
1 t  Ground ginger
1/8 t  Ground cumin
---Frontier Jerky---
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
2 T  Worcestershire sauce
2 T  Liquid smoke
1 t  Garlic powder
---Great Jerky---
3/4 t  Salt
1/4 t  Cracked pepper
1 T  Brown sugar
2 T  Soy sauce
1 T  Worcestershire sauce
1 ea Clove Garlic, crushed
---Teriyaki Jerky---
You may have to alter the ingredient list depending upon the amount
of meat you intend on using.

As you can see, these are a basic ingredient list that you may use
for the different types of jerky.

Play around with it until you find something you really like. All of
it will turn out good. Just compare it to the store bought jerky
prices......You will love these recipes.
