Title: White Bread for Bread Machine
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker
Yield: 6 servings
1 ts Salt
3 c  Bread flour
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk
2 1/2 ts Yeast
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Margerine or butter
1    Egg
2 tb Sugar
Note: This is for a Large (1.5 lb) loaf, adjust # of servings to 4
for medium loaf, 3 for small loaf.

Put ingredients in your machine in the order suggested by the
manufacture and engage warp engines on regular bread cycle.

This makes a very nice (rich) white bread.

From: The Bread Machine Cookbook (vol I) by Donna Rathmell German
