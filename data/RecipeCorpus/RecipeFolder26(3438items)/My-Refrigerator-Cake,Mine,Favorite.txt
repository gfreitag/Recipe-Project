Title: My Refrigerator Cake,Mine,Favorite
Categories: Cake/cookie, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 cake
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Graham wafers, crushed
24    Marshmallows
1/2 c  Chopped nuts, optional
2    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Coconut, shredded
Directions Combine first 5 ingredients in a saucepan. Cook 10
minutes. Add to mixture graham wafers, marshmallows and nuts. Place
in buttered pan and refrigerate till hard.
