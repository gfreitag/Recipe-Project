Title: Mom Bess's Raisen Pie
Categories: Dessert, Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 1 pie
1 pn Salt
1 tb Vinegar
1 ts Vanilla
1 ea Blob of butter
2 c  Raisens
2 c  Water
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Cinnamon
Boil raisens in 1 3/4 C. water for 5 minutes.  Combine sugar,
cornstarch, cinnamon. Add 1/4 C. cold water.  Add raisens.  Bring to
boil and add vinegar, butter, and vanilla.  Bake in 350 oven in a
couble crust.

Source:  Mom Bess Becci McClain 1994
