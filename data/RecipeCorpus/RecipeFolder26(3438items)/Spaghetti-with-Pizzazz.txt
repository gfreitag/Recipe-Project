Title: Spaghetti with Pizzazz
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Sthrn/livng, Ground beef
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Hot sauce
2 oz Mushrooms, sliced; drained
Spaghetti; hot, cooked
1/2 lb Beef, ground
4 oz Tomato paste
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Green pepper; diced
1/4 c  Onion; minced
1    Bay leaf
1/2 ts Garlic salt
Cook ground beef until browned, and drain well; stir in tomato paste
and water.  Bring to a boil, and stir in next 8 ingredients.  Reduce
heat; cover and simmer 40 minutes, adding water as needed.

Stir in mushrooms, and simmer 5 additional minutes; remove bay leaf.
Serve over spaghetti.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, April 1980. Typos by Nancy Coleman.
