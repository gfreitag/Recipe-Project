Title: Creole Rice
Categories: Cajun/creol, Rice
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Green onion, sliced
1/2 c  Green pepper, diced
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1 x  Grated cheese, optional
1 c  Long-grain rice
1 c  Finely chopped onion
1 ea Clove garlic, minced
2 T  Vegetable oil
1 c  Chicken broth
1 c  Tomato sauce (8oz)
Combine rice, onion, garlic, green onion, and oil in microwave dish.
Microwave at high power for 3 minutes. Stir in chicken broth, tomato
sauce, green pepper, salt, and pepper. Cover.  Microwave at high
power for 20 minutes, stirring after 10 minutes, until rice is hot
and tender. Let sit 5 minutes, covered, before serving.
