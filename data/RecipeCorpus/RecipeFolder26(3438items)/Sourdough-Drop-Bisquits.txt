Title: Sourdough Drop Bisquits
Categories: Bread, Sourdough
Yield: 18 servings
MMMMM--------------------------COMBINE-------------------------------
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
MMMMM---------------------------CUT IN--------------------------------
1/3 c  Shortening
MMMMM------------------------MIX AND ADD-----------------------------
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 c  Starter
Drop onto greased pan.  Bake at 450 for 10 minutes.
