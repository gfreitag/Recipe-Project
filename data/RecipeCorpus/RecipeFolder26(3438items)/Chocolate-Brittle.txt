Title: Chocolate Brittle
Categories: Candy, Chocolate, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Walnuts; finely chopped
1 lb Semi-sweet chocolate
1 c  Walnuts; whole
1 lb Butter
1 lb Sugar
1 lb Almonds
In a saucepan cook butter & sugar, boiling 5 minutes. Stir in almonds
& cook 10-20 minutes or until nuts begin to pop & turn brown. Pour
into a shallow pan & let cool. Melt chocolate & pour over mixture in
pan. Sprinkle with finely chopped walnuts. After mixture hardens,
turn over and sprinkle bottom side with walnuts. Break candy into
pieces.
