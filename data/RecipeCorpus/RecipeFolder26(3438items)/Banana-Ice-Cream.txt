Title: Banana Ice Cream
Categories: Mom's, Ice cream, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Salt
2 ea Eggs, beaten
2 c  Mashed ripe
Bananas (5 or 6 bananas)
1/2 T  Fresh, frozen,   1 cup milk
1 1/2 teasp. vanilla extract 1/2 cup
2 cups heavy cream Mix together first 3
or canned lemon juice
granulated sugar
ingredients. Add salt, eggs, milk, vanilla. Stir in cream. Freeze in
2-qt. crank freezer until difficult to turn, using 8 parts crushed
ice to 1 part ice-cream salt. Makes about 2 qt.
