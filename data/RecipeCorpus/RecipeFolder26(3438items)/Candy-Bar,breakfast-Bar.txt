Title: Candy Bar/breakfast Bar
Categories: Low-cal/fat, Candy
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Raisins
2 tb Oatmeal
1 pk Alba or WW Chocolate Drink
2 tb Coffee
1 tb Peanut Butter
Mix all together; place on plastic wrap. Shape into a log. Freeze.
Cut in slices. WW exchanges: 1 milk, 1 fruit, 1 bread, 1 protein, 1
fat. Formtted for you by Joyce Burton....PDPP83A.
