Title: Lois's Rye Bread
Categories: Breadmaker, Bread
Yield: 1 loaf
MMMMM----------------------1 1/2 POUND LOAF---------------------------
4 ts Gluten
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Butter
3 tb Molasses
pn Caraway seeds *
2 1/4 ts Red star active dry yeast**
5/8 c  Milk
3/4 c  Water (for Welbilt/Dak add
3 tbs more water)
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1 1/2 c  Bread Flour
1 c  Rye flour
MMMMM------------------------1 POUND LOAF-----------------------------
1 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
2 tb Molasses
pn Caraway seeds *
2 ts Red Star active dry yeast
For all machines
1/2 c  Milk
3/8 c  Water (For Welbilt add 1 tbl
More water)
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  Bread flour
2 1/2 ts Gluten
1/2 c  Rye flour
* I used just a sprinkle of caraway seeds.  Recipe calls for 2 ts for
small loaf and 1 tbl for large loaf.

** For all machines except 1 1/2 lb. Panasonic/National machines -
use 4 1/2 ts yeast for those machines.

Place all ingredients in bread pan in order recommended by
manufacturer.

I warm up the liquids in the microwave for 60 secs.

Bake on regular setting.

Personal Note - very good, makes tall loaf.
