Title: O'Henry Slices
Categories: Candy, Nuts/grains, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Walnuts
1 c  Coconute
1/2 c  Cut cherries
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Crushed wafers
Boil the brown sugar, margarine and milk.  Take off heat and quickly
add the last four ingredients.  Pour mixture over 8x8 pan lined with
whole wafers.  Place more whole wafers of top.  Press down gently.
Frost with melted caramels if desired. Origin: My recipe file Shared
by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
