Title: Yugeno (Peruvian Brandy with Orange Juice)
Categories: So. america, Fruit, Beverage, Alcohol
Yield: 1 cocktail
4    Ice cubes
4 oz. cocktail glass, cold
1 1/2 oz Orange juice, fresh
1 1/2 oz Pisco; Peruvian brandy
Combine the orange juice, pisco and ice cubes in a mixing glass, and
place a bar shaker on top of the glass.  Grasping the glass and shaker
firmly together with both hands, shake them vigorously 9 or 10 times.
Remove the shaker, and place a strainer over the mixing glass.  Then
pour the yugeno into a chilled cocktail glass.
