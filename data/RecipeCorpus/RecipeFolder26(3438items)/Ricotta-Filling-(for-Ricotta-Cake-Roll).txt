Title: Ricotta Filling (for Ricotta Cake Roll)
Categories: Dessert, Cake/cookie, Question?
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Confectioner's sugar
1 t  Vanilla extract
1/4 t  Ground cinnamon
1 ea Square semisweet chocolate
-(1 ounce), finely chopped
8 ea Ounces light cream cheese
-(Neufchatel), softened (1
-pkge)
15 ea Ounces part-skim ricotta
-cheese (1 container)
Beat the cream cheese with a spoon in a large bowl until smooth. Beat
in the ricotta cheese, sugar, vanilla and cinnamon until smooth. Stir
in the finely chopped chocolate.  Cover and refrigerate until ready
to use as the filling for Ricotta Cake Roll.
