Title: Orange Bread Hitachi
Categories: Bread, Breadmaker, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 T  Butter
1 T  Dried milk
1 1/2 t  Dry yeast or
2 t  Dry yeast Bread Rapid
****Medium Loaf****
5 1/2 oz Orange juice
2 c  Bread flour
1 t  Salt
2 T  Sugar
1 T  Butter
1 T  Dry milk
1 t  Dry yeast or
2 t  Dry yeast Bread Rapid
****Extra Large Loaf****
11 1/2 oz Orange juice (1 7/16 cups)
4 c  Bread flour
1 1/2 t  Salt
4 T  Sugar
2 T  Butter
2 T  Dry milk
2 t  Dry yeast or
3 t  Dry yeast Bread Rapid
****Large Loaf****
9 oz Orange juice
3 c  Bread flour
1 t  Salt
3 T  Sugar
Pour liquids in bread pan, then all dry ingredients except yeast.
Place Dry yeast on top of flour away from liquid. Return bread pan to
bakery. Close lid and plug into a receptacle. Set controls per
machine instructions.

***This can not be used on Bread Rapid cycle***

Source:  Hitachi Automatic Home Bakery Cookbook From: Wayne Long,
Tulsa, Oklahoma
