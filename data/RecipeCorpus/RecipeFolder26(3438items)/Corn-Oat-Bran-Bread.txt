Title: Corn Oat-Bran Bread
Categories: Bread, Cajun/creol, Low-cal/fat
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 ea Egg white
1 t  Baking powder
1/8 t  Ground allspice
1/8 t  Freshly ground black pepper
1/2 c  Yellow cornmeal
1/2 c  Oat bran
1/2 c  Skim milk
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Chopped green bell pepper
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
Prehead oven to 350 degrees F. Blend all ingredients and pour into a
small (8X14-inch) nonstick loaf pan. Bake for 35 minutes, or until a
knife inserted in the center of the bread comes out clean.
Variations: Using 1/2 cup fresh corn kernels in place of the onion
and bell pepper is a tasty alternative. The addition of 1/4 tsp
cayenne will add a nice bit of spice to this bread.
