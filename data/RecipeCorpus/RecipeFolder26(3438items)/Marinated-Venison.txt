Title: Marinated Venison
Categories: Game, Vegetable, Main dish, Marinade
Yield: 12 servings
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Basil
2    Bay leaves
1/2 tb Parsley [chopped]
4 c  Vinegar
4 c  Water
2 c  Beer
4 lb Venison shoulder [cubed]
2 lg Carrots [sliced]
2 lg Onions [sliced]
2    Celery stalks [finely
Chopper]
1/2 c  Olive oil
2    Garlic cloves
2 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper [fresh ground]
1/2 c  Sugar
