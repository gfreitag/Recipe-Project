Title: Tomato Sauce Mix
Categories: Tomato, Salsa/sauce, Mixes, Holiday
Yield: 3 servings
-mix.
1 c  Dried tomatoes
2 T  Dried parsley
1 T  Dried basil
2 t  Dried marjoram
1/2 t  Hot pepper flakes
Dried tomatoes (not oil
-packed) make a tomato
-sauce mix with a unique
Flavor that pasta lovers
-will appreciate.  Give the
-recipe for Double
Tomato Sauce along with the
With scissors, sliver tomatoes, combine in airtight container with
parsley, basil, marjoram and pepper flakes.  Makes about 1 cup.
DOUBLE TOMATO SAUCE: In 2 tbsp olive oil, cook 1 chopped onion and 2
minced garlic cloves over medium heat until softened, about 3
minutes.  Add 1/2 cup MIX and 1 can (19oz) tomatoes, breaking up
tomatoes.  Simmer for 15 minutes or until thickened.  Season with
salt and pepper to taste.  Makes 3 cups, enough for 4 servings.
