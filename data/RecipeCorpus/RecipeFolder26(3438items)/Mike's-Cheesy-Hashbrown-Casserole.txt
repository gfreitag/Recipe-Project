Title: Mike's Cheesy Hashbrown Casserole
Categories: Main dish, Casserole, Potato, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Dried Minced Onion
1/2 ts Ground Black Pepper
1/8 ts Liquid Smoke Flavoring
1/2 c  Shredded Cheddar Cheese
2 lb Frozen Shredded Hashbrowns
1 cn Campbell Cheddar Cheese Soup
1 cn Milk
1/2 c  Butter
Preheat oven to 350. Spray a 4 quart casserole with non-stick cooking
spray. Thaw potatoes. Heat soup, milk, butter, onion, pepper, and
smoke flavoring in a medium saucepan just to boiling over medium
heat. Put potatoes in casserole and pour hot soup mixture over. Stir
to blend. Bake in covered casserole for 50 minutes. Garnish with
shredded cheese. Submitted By PAT BROWNLEE   On   01-16-95 (1545)
