Title: Sharon's Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Homemade
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Nuts
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Butter or marg
2 ea Squares chocolate
1 c  Sugar
2 md Eggs
Cream butter, add melted chocolate.  Blend in sugar.  Add beaten eggs.
Blend in flour.  Stir in nuts and raisins.  Pour into a greased 8x8
brownie pan.  Bake at 350F for 25-30 minutes. Origin: Sharon Stevens
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, Jan/95.
