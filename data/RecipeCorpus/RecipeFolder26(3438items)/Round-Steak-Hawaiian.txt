Title: Round Steak Hawaiian
Categories: Beef, Mushroom, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
-  Chow mein noodles
1 lb Mushrooms, sliced
1/4 c  Cooking oil
1 1/2 lb Round steak
1 ea Bell pepper cut into strips
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
1 ea Jar homestyle beef gravy
1/2 t  Salt
1 cn Sliced water chestnuts,
-drained
Cut steak into 1/4" strips.  Heat oil over medium-high heat.  Add
steak, onion, green pepper, mushrooms and salt.  Cook until meat is
brown, stirring constantly.  Drain and add water chestnuts and gravy.
Cover and simmer 1-1/2 to 2 hours, stirring occasionally. Serve over
rice and sprinkle with chow mein noodles.
