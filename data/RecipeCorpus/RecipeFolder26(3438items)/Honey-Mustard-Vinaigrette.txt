Title: Honey Mustard Vinaigrette
Categories: Dressing, Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
1 t  Prepared stoneground
-mustard
1/4 c  Apple cider vinegar
1/2 t  Honey
Put all ingredients in a small bowl.  Cream together until well
blended. R. S. Koss <rskoss@bronze.coil.com> Tanya Heikkinen
theikkinen@marvin.ag.uidaho.edu
