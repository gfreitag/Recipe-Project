Title: Baked Cinnamon Apples
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Sthrn/livng, Apples
Yield: 6 servings
6 md Apples;peeled, cored,
-quartered
1 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Red cinnamon candies
Combine water and sugar in a saucepan; bring to a boil.  Add candies,
stirring constantly until melted.

Place apples in a 7-1/2x11-3/4" baking dish; pour candy syrup over
apples. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.  Turn apples over; bake
20 additional minutes, basting frequently with syrup.

NOTE: Whole baking apples may be substituted for wedges.  Bake at 400
degrees for 40 minutes, basting frequently.
