Title: Rip & Dip
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Water chestnuts, diced
1/2 c  Cheese; cheddar, grated
1/4 pk Knorr swiss vegetable soup m
1 ea Onion; small, diced
1 ea Bread; round, hollowed out,
1 pk Spinach; frozen, chopped
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Mayonnaise
Mix all ingredients except bread together and refridgerate until ready to
serve.  When ready to serve pour into hollowed out bread and serve with
inside of bread ripped up and crackers. (Auntie Kay's Recipe)
