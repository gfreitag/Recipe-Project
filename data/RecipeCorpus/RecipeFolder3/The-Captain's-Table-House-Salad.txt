Title: The Captain's Table House Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1    Carrot, shredded
1    Tomato, sliced into eighths
1 c  Sliced thinly
House vinaigrette dressing
1    Head chicory, cleaned and
Broken into bite-size pieces
1    Head romaine, cleaned and
Broken into bite-size pieces
Serve with House Vinaigrette Dressing recipe.  Toss ingredients together
and sprinkle with dressing.
