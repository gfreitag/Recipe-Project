Title: Chutney Peanut Butter Canapes
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Major Grey's chutney,
-finely chopped
Toasted rye bread slices,
-crusts removed
3/4 c  Chunky-style peanut butter
1 pk Cream cheese (3oz)
1/8 ts Seasoned salt
1/4 c  Dry red wine
1/4 ts Worcestershire
Blend well the peanut butter, cream cheese, salt, wine worcestershire and
chutney. Spread on toast. Cut into individual triangles or fingers. Makes 2
cups of spread.
