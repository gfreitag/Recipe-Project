Title: Bacon Corn Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Sugar
1    Egg, well beaten
1 c  Sweet milk
1/4 c  Diced bacon
1 c  Corn-meal
1 c  White flour
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder and salt.  Add corn-meal.
Combine egg, milk, and bacon which has been crisped. Add dry ingredients.
Fill well-oiled muffin tins 2/3 full.  Bake in hot oven (450Ã¸ F) 15
minutes.  10 minutes. Mrs. Fred Snell, Erie, PA.
