Title: Salsa Suprema
Categories: Mexican, Sauces, Relishes, Salsa
Yield: 16 servings
1 ea Medium onion, chopped
1 ea Or 4 oz can green chili
1/2 ts Monosodium glutamate(option)
1 ea Large tomato, chopped
2 ea Fresh green chilies, chopped
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 x  Salt to taste
Combine all ingredients and chill, covered, in refrigerator at least one
hour.
