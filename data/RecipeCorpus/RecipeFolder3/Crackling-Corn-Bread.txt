Title: Crackling Corn Bread
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 servings
1    Egg, well beaten
3 ts Baking powder
1 1/4 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Cracklings
1 1/2 c  Corn-meal
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Flour
2 ts Sugar
Add flour, corn-meal, sugar, salt, baking powder, egg, and milk. Add
cracklings.  Beat thoroughly.  Bake in well-oiled shallow pan in hot oven
(450Ã¸ F) about 20 minutes, or drop by teaspoonfuls on a hot well-oiled
griddle.  Fry until golden brown.  6 servings. Virginia Cooper, New
Orleans, LA.
