Title: QUICK NUT FUDGE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Confectioners sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Chopped pecans
6 tb Butter
4 tb Milk
1 tb Vanilla
Combine all ingredients except nuts in top of double boiler. Place
over simmering water and stir until smooth, add nuts and mix. Spread
candy quickly in buttered 9 x 5 loaf pan. Cool, cut in squares.
