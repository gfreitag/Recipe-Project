Title: RED HOT SALAD
Categories: Salads, Candies, Fruits/nuts
Yield: 6 Servings
6 oz Cherry Jell-O; (2 Pkgs)
4 oz Red Hots Candy
3 c  ;Boiling Water
20 oz Pineapple;Crushed, Undrained
2 c  Applesauce
Dissolve Jell-O and red hots (also known as Cinnamon Imperials) in
boiling water.  When cooled to room temperature, add pineapple and
applesauce. Pour into oiled 8-cup mold. Chill before serving.
