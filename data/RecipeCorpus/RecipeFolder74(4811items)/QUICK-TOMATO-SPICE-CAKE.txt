Title: QUICK TOMATO SPICE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
JOYCE BURTON-PDPP83A
1 pk Spice cake mix
10 1/2 oz Can condensed tomato soup
1/4 c  Water
Mix cake as directed on package, substituting soup and water for
liquid. (Add eggs if called for.) Bake as directed on packages.
Frost, if desired. Source: Cooking with Soup (a Campbell cookbook).
