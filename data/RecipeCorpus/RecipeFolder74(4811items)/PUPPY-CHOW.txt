Title: PUPPY CHOW
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
6 oz Chocolate chips
10 c  Corn Chex cereal
2 c  Powdered sugar
Melt peanut butter, butter or margarine, and chocolate chips in a
saucepan over medium heat.  Pour over Corn Chex, being sure that all
cereal is coated.  Put 2 cups powdered sugar in a large paper bag.
Put cereal in bag and shake gently until all cereal is coated. Pour
out on wax paper to cool.
