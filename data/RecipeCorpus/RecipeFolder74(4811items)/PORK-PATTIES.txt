Title: PORK PATTIES
Categories: Pork
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Ground Pork -- lean
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried Sage
1/4 ts Ground Nutmeg
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Dried Thyme
pn Ground Ginger
2 tb Water
In a bowl combine all ingredients; mix well. Shape into six patties. =
Fry=20 in a skillet until meat is browned and cooked throughout.
Yield: 6=20 servings.
