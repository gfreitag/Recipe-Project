Title: REUNION CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Flour
3/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/4 ts Savory
6    Whole chicken breasts, boned
-and skinned
5 tb Butter
1 lb Mushrooms, sliced
2    Onions, chopped
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 tb Chopped parsley
Additional salt, pepper, and
-savory to taste
1/2 c  Each chicken broth, white
-wine, and orange juice
2 ts Brown sugar
1    10 oz. pkg. each frozen snow
-peas, green peas, and
-artichoke hearts
Combine flour, 3/4 tsp. salt, pepper, and savory and coat chicken
breasts. Brown chicken in 3 tbsp. butter and transfer to a 2 qt.
casserole.

Melt remaining 2 tbsp. butter and saute mushrooms, onions, garlic, and
parsley.  Add additional salt, pepper, and savory. Stir in the broth,
wine, juice, and brown sugar. Pour sauce over the chicken.

Cover the casserole with foil and bake at 350Ã¸F for 45 minutes.
Remove from oven and add frozen snow peas, green peas, and
artichokes. Return to oven and bake uncovered for 20 minutes. Yield:
