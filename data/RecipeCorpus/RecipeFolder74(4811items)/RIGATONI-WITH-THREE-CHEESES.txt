Title: RIGATONI WITH THREE CHEESES
Categories: Pasta, Italian
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Salt
1 lb Rigatoni
3 1/2 tb Melted Sweet Butter
1/2 c  Shredded Swiss Cheese
1/2 c  Shredded Fontina
1/2 c  Shredded Mozzarella
1 c  Heavy Cream
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Preheat oven to 375Ã¸F.  In lots of boiling water, add the salt and
rigatoni. Cook until super al dante as they are going into the oven.
Drain and rinse in cold water. In a large bowl, mix the butter into
the pasta until it is well coated. Add the three cheeses and the
cream. Toss well and add 1/2 the parmigiano while tossing. Place in a
buttered casserole and sprinkle the remaining parmigiano on top.
Sprinkle the nutmeg over everything and bake for 15-20 minutes. When
the top of the pasta has turned golden brown, it is done.
