Title: RAISIN FILLING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Chopped raisins
2/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Boiling water
1 1/2 ts Flour
1 tb Lemon juice
Combine raisins, sugar, lemon juice, and flour.  Add water. Cook over
hot water, stirring constantly, until thick and smooth.

Mrs. Leonard Hodge, Murdock, KS.
