Title: QUICK PEACH ICE CREAM
Categories: Fruits, Desserts, Frozen
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM------------------------LIZ VXRF36B-----------------------------
3    Ripe peaches; peeled/sliced
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1/3 c  Superfine sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
Slice peaches and puree; refrigerate. Combine milk and cream with
sugar. Whisk 2 minutes, or until sugar dissolves. Stir in vanilla
extract. Add peach puree, stirring well. Chill. Freeze in ice cream
freezer featuring pre-chilled cannister. Makes 4 servings.

The Tampa Tribune, 07/15/93
