Title: POTATO CHIP BROWNIES
Categories: Brownies, Chocolate
Yield: 12 Servings
2/3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Butter
2    Eggs; well beaten
2    Unsweetened chocolate square
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Potato chips; crushed
Sift flour with baking powder and salt. Melt butter and chocolate.
Beat the eggs well and add sugar gradually, beating well. Add the
chocolate mix and vanilla to eggs and blend. Add flour  and mix well.
Add potato chips. Bake in a lightly greased 8" square pan in a 350~
oven for 30-35 minutes. While warm, cut into squares.
