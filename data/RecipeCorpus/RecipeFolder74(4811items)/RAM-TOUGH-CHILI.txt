Title: RAM TOUGH CHILI
Categories: Chili, Ground beef, Beef, Chuck, Tomato
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Beef chuck, coarsely ground
1 tb Oil
1 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz.)
1 md Onion, finely chopped
1 ts Garlic powder OR
5    Cloves Fresh garlic, minced
1/4 c  Chili powder
1 ts Oregano
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Ground cumin
1/4 ts Tabasco sauce
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
Brown meat in oil in a covered 2 quart pan. Add tomato sauce, onion
and garlic powder. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Add water if too thick. Serve with side dishes of pinto
beans, chopped onions and grated cheddar cheese. Serves 6.
