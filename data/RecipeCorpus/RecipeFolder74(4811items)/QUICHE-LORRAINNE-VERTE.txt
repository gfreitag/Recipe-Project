Title: QUICHE LORRAINNE VERTE
Categories: Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 lb Bacon, cut up
6 oz Swiss cheese, grated
4    Eggs
1 tb Flour
3/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1 pt Half & half
1 tb Butter
4    Green onions, inc. tops*
Pimientos as garnish
1    Unbaked piecrust
* chop green onions (inc. tops)
Fry bacon until cooked, but not TOO crisp.
Place in bottom of unbaked pie crust.
Place swiss cheese strips in a crisscross pattern on top of bacon.
Combine rest of ingredients (except pimientos) in blender, until
foamy. Pour mixture into piecrust.
Top w/pimentoes.  Bake at 375 for 45 minutes.
