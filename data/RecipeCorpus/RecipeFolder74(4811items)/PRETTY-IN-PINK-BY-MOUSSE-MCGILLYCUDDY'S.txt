Title: PRETTY IN PINK BY MOUSSE MCGILLYCUDDY'S
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Parts sweet and sour
Splash of cranberry juice
1 oz Simple syrup
1 oz Lime juice
2 lg Lemon slices squeezed and
-discard
1/2 oz Non-alcoholic triple sec
Blend and serve in a sugar rimmed glass.  Garnish with a lemon wheel.
