Title: ROQUEFORT APPLE DESSERT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
4 md Sized red apples
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 oz Crumbled Roquefort cheese
1/4 c  French dressing
1/4 c  Chopped walnuts
2 tb Seedless raisins
Cut slice from top of each apple; reserve.  Remove cores. Scoop out
pulp, leaving 1/4 inch shells. Brush shells with lemon juice. Dice
apple pulp. Combine diced apple and remaining ingredients; toss
lightly. Fill apple shells with mixture.  Replace apple tops. Serves
4.
