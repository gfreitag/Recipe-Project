Title: RAU TRON NGOT VA CHUA (SWEET & SOUR SALAD)
Categories: Vietnam, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Vinegar
1/3 c  Water
3 tb Sugar, granulated
Use any combination of the following vegetables to prepare the salad:
Onion, radish, carrot, celery, bell pepper. Chop the vegetables
finely, marinate in salad dressing, place in refrigerator for about 1
hour. Some other vegetables do not need  to be marinated; quartered
cucumbers cut diagonally; finely chopped cabbage; coarsely broken
leaf lettuce. Toss the ingredients well with salad dressing and add
to the other vegetables when ready to serve. Source: Vietnamese
Dishes by Duong Thi Thanh Lien
