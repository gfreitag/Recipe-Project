Title: QUICK ROLLS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Self rising flour
2 c  Buttermilk
3/4 c  Oil
2 tb Sugar
4 tb Warm water
1 pk Yeast
Mix water, sugar and yeast.  Add to first three ingredients. Beat with
spoon.  Drop into greased muffin pans. Bake at 400 degrees for 15
minutes. Randy Rigg
