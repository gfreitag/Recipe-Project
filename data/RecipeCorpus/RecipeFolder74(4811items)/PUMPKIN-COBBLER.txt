Title: PUMPKIN COBBLER
Categories: Tarts, Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 9 Servings
1 cn Pumpkin,large can
4    Eggs;large,beaten
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Cloves
2 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Evaporated milk;13 ozs.
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 c  Butter;melted
2 tb Brown sugar
1/2 c  Almonds;slivered,OR
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
Blend together,pumpkin,eggs,sugar,salt,ginger,cloves,cinnamon and
canned milk.Pour into greased 13"x9"x2" pan.Sprinkle dry cake mix
over top of mixture.Drizzle melted butter over dry cake mix.Sprinkle
brown sugar over butter.Top with a sprinkling of nut meats.Bake in a
350 deg.oven for 1 hour and 20 minutes.Makes 9 3"x4 1/4" servings....
