Title: POTATO SOUP FOR CROCKPOT
Categories: Soups, Vegetables, Crockpot, Potatoes
Yield: 8 Servings
8 lg Potatoes -- cubed
2 md Onions -- chopped
2 tb Margarine
2    Chicken boullion cubes
2 tb Parsley -- dry
6 c  Water
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Flour -- mixed with water
Place ingredients 1 through 6 in the crockpot and cook all day on low
to medium.  1/2 hour to onehour before serving; add milk and flour
mixture. After the soup starts to thicken, it is ready to serve.

NOTE:  Add 1/4 pound of Velveeta cheese for Cheese potato soup.
I can evaporated milk may be substituted for the regular milk.
