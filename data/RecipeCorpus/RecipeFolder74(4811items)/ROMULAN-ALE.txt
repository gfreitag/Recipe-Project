Title: ROMULAN ALE
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 Recipe
1    Fifth Bacardi 151
1    Fifth Blue Curaco
2 l  Sprite or 7-up
Mix all ingredients.  Chill for approximately 3 hours and serve.
