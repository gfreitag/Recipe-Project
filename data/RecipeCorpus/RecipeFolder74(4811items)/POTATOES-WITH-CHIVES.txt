Title: POTATOES WITH CHIVES
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Washed New Potatoes, with
-skins on
5 fl Soured Cream
3 tb Mayonnaise
1 tb Garlic Wine Vinegar
2 tb Snipped Chives
Salt and Pepper
Cook potatoes in lightly salted water for 10-15 minutes until just
tender. Drain and place in a bowl.

Mix together all the other ingredients and pour over cooked new
potatoes. Chill until needed.
