Title: RELISH SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Brown sugar, packed
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
2 c  Chopped cabbage
1/2 c  Chopped green bell pepper
1 c  Chopped celery
IN BOWL, COMBINE SALT, brown sugar, dry mustard and cider vinegar,
stirring until blended. Add cabbage, bell pepper and celery and toss
until well coated with dressing. Serve chilled. Makes 3 1/2 Cups
