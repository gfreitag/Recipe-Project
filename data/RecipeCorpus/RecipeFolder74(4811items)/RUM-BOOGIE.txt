Title: RUM BOOGIE
Categories: Boat, Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
3    Wedges Lime-(Key, of course)
1 oz Dark rum
1/2 oz Amaretto
2 oz Coke
In a highball glass squeeze the limes over  the ice. Then add the rum,
Amaretto and Coke.  Stir the sucker up and serve.

(Multi drinks 1 part Amaretto--3 parts rum--4 parts coke. Three lime
wedges per drink.  Best to add the lime straight to the glass rather
than the mixture. Do not prepare too far ahead of time It'll go flat.)
