Title: RO-TEL  MACARONI & CHEESE
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 11 Servings
3 c  Uncooked elbow macaroni;
-cooked and drained
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  Milk
4 c  (1 lb.) shredded cheddar
-cheese
1    10 oz. can ro-tel diced
-tomatoes and green chilies
Recipe by: Back of the Can Recipe Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Place
macaroni in a 9x12 inch baking dish. In a medium saucepan, melt
margarine over low heat.  Stir in flour; cook 1 minute, stirring
constantly until mixture is smooth and bubbly.  Stir in milk; heat to
a boil, stirring constantly.  Add cheese and Rotel. Stir until cheese
in melted. Pour over macaroni and stir to combine. Bake uncovered 30
minutes or until heated through.
