Title: PRUNE MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Shortening
1/4 c  Sugar; granulated
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 ea Egg
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Milk
3/4 c  Prunes; cooked - well drain
-ed; chopped
Mix and bake in hot oven.

Note: Hot oven is 400 - 450 F. Time not given, probably about 25
minutes.

Source: Mrs. Jeneila Gillson, United Grange, Marion County, OH
