Title: RASPBERRY VINAIGRETTE WITH DIJON
Categories: Desserts, Salads
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  Raspberry vinegar
1/2 c  Extra virgin olive oil
1 cl Garlic, crushed
2 ts Dijon mustard
1.  Whisk ingredients together in a small bowl. 2. Serve over chilled
romaine and bibb lettuce.

* The Raspberry Recipe Book, Thomson Berry Farms, Duluth, MN
