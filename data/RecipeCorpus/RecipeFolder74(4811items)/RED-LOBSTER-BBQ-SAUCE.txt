Title: RED LOBSTER BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Bbq, Cake mix, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
1/2 c  Chunky-style Prego sauce
1/2 c  Del Monte Ketchup
1 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Vinegar
1/8 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Chili powder
Combine thoroughly and refrigerate, tightly covered, to use within 90
days or freeze to thaw and use within 1 year. Serve with chicken
sandwich. Source: Gloria Pitzer.
