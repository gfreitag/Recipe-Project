Title: ROAST BEEF POCKET SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1 1/2 ts Chopped fresh or
1/2 ts Dried dill weed
1 ts Prepared mustard
1 c  Chopped red or green bell
-pepper (about I medium)
2    Pita breads (6 inches in
-diameter), cut in half
1/3 lb Thinly sliced lean roast
-beef
1 c  Alfalfa sprouts
Combine savory roast beef, tangy yogurt and fresh vegetables in a pita
bread and what do you get? An incredibly delicious neat-to-eat
sandwich!

Mix yogurt, dill weed and mustard. Stir in bell pepper Fill each pita
bread half with l/3 cup yogurt mixture and 1/4 of the beef and
alfalfa sprouts.

4    servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
