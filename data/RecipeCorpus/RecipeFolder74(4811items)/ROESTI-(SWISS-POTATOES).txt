Title: ROESTI (SWISS POTATOES)
Categories: Dutch oven, Potatoes
Yield: 5 Servings
6    Strips bacon -- diced
1 sm Onion -- diced
2 lg Potato -- precooked
Begin cooking bacon first.  Add diced onion and cook until onion is
tender. Grate potatoes and add in the pan to the bacon and onion. Fry
until heated through in 10 to 20 minutes.
