Title: QUICKIE PEACH COBBLER
Categories: Desserts, Fruits, Favorites
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg -- beaten
3 lg Peaches -- peeled/chopped
1 c  Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Cinnamon
pn Salt
1/2 c  Walnuts -- chopped
Preheat 325F.  Cream butter and sugar.  Add egg and beat well. Add
peaches. Sift together flour, soda, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt. Stir
into peach mixutre.  Pour batter into buttered 9-inch square pan.
Sprinkle nuts on top. Bake 35-40 min or until center is set.
