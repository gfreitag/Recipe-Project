Title: RANCH STYLE BEEF
Categories: Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Boneless beef stew meat --
Cut into 1" cubes
12 ea Small white onions
4    Whole cloves
2 tb Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Bottled brown gravy sauce
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1 ea Bay leaf
1/8 ts Thyme
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Flour
In slow-cooking pot, alternate beef and onions; stick cloves into 4
onions. Sprinkle with sugar and salt. Combine gravy sauce, vinegar,
bay leaf, thyme, and water. Pour over meat. Cover and cook on low for
5 to 7 hours or until tender.  Turn control to high. Dissolve flour
in small amount of water.  Stir into meat juices. Cook on high for 15
to 20 minutes.
