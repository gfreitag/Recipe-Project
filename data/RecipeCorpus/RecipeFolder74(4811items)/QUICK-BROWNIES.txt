Title: QUICK BROWNIES
Categories: Brownies, Chocolate
Yield: 12 Servings
16 oz Semi-sweet chocolate chips
2/3 c  Sweetened condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Walnuts; coarsely chopped
In top of double boiler, placed over simmering water, melt chocolate.
Stir in Sweetened condensed Milk. Remove from heat. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Turn into greased, waxed paper OR metal foil-lined and
greased again 8x8" pan. Bake in 375~ oven until toothpick inserted in
center comes out clean, about 25 minutes. Turn out of pan onto cake
rack. Peel off paper. Cool. With sharp knife cut into 2" squares.
