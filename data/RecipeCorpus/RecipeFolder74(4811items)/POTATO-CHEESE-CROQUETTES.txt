Title: POTATO CHEESE CROQUETTES
Categories: Potatoes, Side dish, Pancakes
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Cold mashed potatoes
1    Egg, beaten
1 c  Cheddar cheese, grated
Salt to taste
3/4 ts Ac'cent
1/2 c  Fine bread crumbs
1/2 c  Milk
Break up potatoes. Beat egg in blender until frothy. Add potato pieces
gradually, beating at medium speed until blended.

Stir in cheese, salt, and Ac'cent. Shape as desired. Dip into crumbs,
them milk, and again into crumbs.

Fry in shallow hot fat until golden brown on all sides.
