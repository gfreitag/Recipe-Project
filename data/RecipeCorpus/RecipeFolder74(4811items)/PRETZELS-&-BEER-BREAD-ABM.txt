Title: PRETZELS & BEER BREAD ABM
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 pk Yeast
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Sugar
3 c  Flour, bread
2 tb Gluten
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Baking soda
1 1/3 c  Pretzel sticks, thin
1    Egg white
1 tb Vegetable oil
12 oz Beer, light
1/4 c  Water; heated
Break pretzel sticks in half. Add ingredients to pan in order listed,
select white bread setting and press "start".
