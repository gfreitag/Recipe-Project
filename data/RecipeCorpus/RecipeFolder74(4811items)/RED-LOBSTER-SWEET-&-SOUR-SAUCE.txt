Title: RED LOBSTER SWEET & SOUR SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Seafood, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Tomato paste -- (6 ounces)
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Dijon mustard
2 tb Horseradish cream sauce
Mix all ingredients together well. Refrigerate, tightly covered, to
serve as an accompaniment to marinated chicken or with shrimp as a
cocktail sauce. Source: Gloria Pitzer.
