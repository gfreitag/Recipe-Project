Title: RED ONION SALAD WITH AVOCADO DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2    Red onions: sliced
1 1/2    Stalks celery: sliced
1/4 lb Cherry tomatoes: halved
1/4 lb Snow pea pods
1/4    Avocado: peeled & chopped
1/2 ts Green peppercorns
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb White wine vinegar
Cut onions into thin slices.  Slice celery and halve tomatoes. Arrange
onions, celery and tomatoes in serving bowl with pea pods. Combine
remaining ingredients in food processor or blender and puree. Pour
over salad.

From the Daytona News-Journal, 7/1/93.
