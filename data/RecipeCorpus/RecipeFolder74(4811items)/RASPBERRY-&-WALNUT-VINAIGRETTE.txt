Title: RASPBERRY & WALNUT VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Vinegars, Raspberry, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 ts Honey mustard
2 tb Raspberry vinegar
Sea salt to taste
3 tb Walnut oil
3 tb Oilive oil
1 tb Minced fresh tarragon
In a bowl, whisk together mustard, vinegar, and salt to taste, add
the oils in a stream, whisking;whisk until it is emulsified Stir in
tarragon. Makes 1/2 cup. ( Fresh fields)
