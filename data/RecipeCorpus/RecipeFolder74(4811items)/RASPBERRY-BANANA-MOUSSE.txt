Title: RASPBERRY BANANA MOUSSE
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ea 10 1/2 oz pkg lite silken
-- tofu, extra firm
1 md Frozen banana, chopped
1 c  Frozen raspberries
1/3 c  Maple syrup
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Vanilla extract
3/4 c  Fresh raspberries, garnish
Place 2 qt metal bowl in the freezer. Blend tofu until smooth. Blend
in the frozen banana & raspberries. Add maple syrup, lemon juice &
vanilla. Blend until smooth.  Pour into the chilled bowl. Cover with
plastic wrap & aluminum foil. Freeze for 4 hours or overnight.
Transfer mousse to a food processor & return the empty bowl to the
freezer. Blend the mousse to an ice-cream like consistency. Return to
the cilled bowl & freeze for at least 2 hours. Scoop into dessert
cups, garnish with fresh raspberries & serve.
