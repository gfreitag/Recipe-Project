Title: QUICK CONEY ISLANDS
Categories: Sandwiches, Snacks
Yield: 4 Servings
4    FRANKS
1/2 lb Ground Beef
1/4 c  Celery
1    Garlic Clove, crushed
1    Salt, dash
4    Coney Buns
3/4 c  Onion, chopped
1 c  Tomato Sauce
1/2 ts Hot Sauce
1    Pepper, dash
Brown beef in skillet. Add 1/2 cup onion and celery; cook until
almost tender. Drain off fat. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer 15
minutes. Warm buns in oven. Heat FRANKS in small amount of water.
Spread mustard on buns. Place 1 FRANK in each bun. Spoon on hot coney
sauce. Add remaining onions on top.
