Title: POPPY SEED CAKE *** BTCW72B
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Yellow or white cake mix
3 oz Instant vanilla pudding
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Oil
5 x  Eggs
2 tb Poppy seed
2 tb Almond extract
Beat for 2 mins. Pour into greased and floured bundt pan. Bake at 300
for 55 mins to 1 hr 15 mins.
