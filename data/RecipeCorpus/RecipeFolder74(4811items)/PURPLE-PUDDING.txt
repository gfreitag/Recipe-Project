Title: PURPLE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Recipe
3/4 c  AM Blue Cornmeal
2 1/2 c  Grape juice
-OR- other dark fruit juice
1 c  Plain yogurt
Natural sweetener to taste
Combine juice and blue cornmeal in a saucepan, and stir over low heat
with a wire whisk until very thick. Combine mixture with yogurt and
sweetener and beat with an electric mixer or in a blender, until
creamy and smooth. Pour into small bowls and chill.
