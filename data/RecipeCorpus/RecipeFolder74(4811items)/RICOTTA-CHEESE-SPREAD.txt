Title: RICOTTA CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Crocker
Yield: 2 + cups
15 oz Ricotta cheese
4    Radishes, chopped
2    Green onions (with tops),
-chopped
1    Clove garlic, finely chopped
2 tb Chopped green pepper
2 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Dairy sour cream
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground red pepper
Raw vegetabtes or
-assorted crackers
Mix all ingredients except raw vegetables. Cover and refrigerate 1
hour. Serve with vegetables. 2 1/2 cups spread; 45 calories per tb.

Source: Betty Crocker's Cookbook, 6th Edition
