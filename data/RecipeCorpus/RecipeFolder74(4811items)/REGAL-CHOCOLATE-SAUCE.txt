Title: REGAL CHOCOLATE SAUCE
Categories: Chocolate, Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Squares Baker's Unsweetened
-Chocolate
1/3 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Margarine or butter
1/4 ts Vanilla
From: Baker's Chocolate Cookbook

MICROWAVE chocolate and water in microwavable bowl on HIGH 1 1/2
minutes. Stir until chocolate is completely melted.

STIR in sugar. Microwave 1 minute. Stir. Microwave 2 minutes longer;
stir in margarine and vanilla.

PrepTime: 10 minutes.
