Title: QUICK PASTA SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Uncooked whole wheat or
-- spinach elbow macaroni
-- shells, or spirals
1 1/2 c  Chopped mixed vegetables
1/2 c  Oil free salad dressing
Cook the macaroni in boiling water just til tender, 8-10 mins. Mix
with the veggies and dressing, and toss to coat. "The McDougall Plan"
by John McDougall Typed by Lisa Greenwood
