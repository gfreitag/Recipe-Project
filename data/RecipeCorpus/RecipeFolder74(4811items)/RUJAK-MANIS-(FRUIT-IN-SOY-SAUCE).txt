Title: RUJAK MANIS (FRUIT IN SOY SAUCE)
Categories: Fruits, Indonesian
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Ketjap manis
1/2 ea Thai chile
1 x  Ginger, fresh; peeled (1/2")
1 ts Lime juice
3 c  Assorted fresh fruit
per serving: 92 Fat grams per serving: 0 Approx. Cook
Calories
Time: 1:00 Cut up fruit into bite-sized chunks. Grind ginger and
pepper. Add ketjap manis and lemon juice. Pour over the fruit and mix
gently. Let stand an hour or more before serving.
