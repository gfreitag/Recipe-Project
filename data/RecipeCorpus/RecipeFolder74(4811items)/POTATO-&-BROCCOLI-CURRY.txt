Title: POTATO & BROCCOLI CURRY
Categories: Indian, Meat, Miscellaneo, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
-In A Saucepan, Saute-----
1 tb Olive Oil
1    Clove Garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Ground Coriander
1/2 ts Ground Turmeric
1/4 ts Ground Cumin
1/4 ts Ground Ginger
1/8 ts Dry Mustard
1 ds Ground Red Pepper
-Add-----
1/2 tb Olive Oil
1 c  Water
1    Potato -- diced (unpeeled
Ok)
-Vegetables-----
1 c  Broccoli -- chopped
1    Carrot -- sliced
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 tb Curry Powder
Fry, stirring often to coat potatoes with seasonings. When potatoes
are almost tender, add last 4 ingredients.

Cover and simmer until broccoliand carrots are tender. Serve with
rice or pasta.
