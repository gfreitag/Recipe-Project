Title: RASBERRY SPINACH SALAD
Categories: Dressings, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
8 c  Spinach leaves; torn
1/2 c  Macadamia nuts; toasted
1 1/2 c  Raspberries; fresh
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
3 tb Raspberry vinegar
3 tb Raspberry jam; seedless
1/2 c  Salad oil
2 tb Sugar
Toast macadamia nuts in 250 degree (F) oven for 30 minutes, flipping
and stirring often.  Cool.  Mix, spinach, nuts and rasberries in a
large bowl. For Dressing: combine all ingredients in a blender and
blend 'til thick. Pour dressing over salald and enjoy the taste of
spring!
