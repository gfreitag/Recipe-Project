Title: ROASTED WHITE CORN SALSA
Categories: New text im, Cooking rig
2 c  sweet white corn -- cut from
cob
Yield: 1 Servings
:
1/4 c  olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
pepper
:
:
1/3 c  red bell pepper -- finely
diced
:
1/3 c  red onion -- finely diced
1/3 c  Chopped or a combination
1 ts serrano chile -- minced,
seeded
:
1 TB herry vinegar
1 ts fresh lemon juice -- or to
taste
:
1 ts honey -- or to taste

Toss corn with oil and lightly season with salt and pepper. Spread
out in a single layer on a baking sheet and roast in a preheated 425
degree oven until very lightly browned.

Cool and add to a bowl with rest of ingredients. Cover and store
refrigerated for up to 5 days.

Yield: approximately 3 cups
