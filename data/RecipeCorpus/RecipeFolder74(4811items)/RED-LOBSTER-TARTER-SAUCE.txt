Title: RED LOBSTER TARTER SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Seafood
Yield: 1 Batch
1/3 c  Miracle Whip Salad Dressing
2/3 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Confectioner's Sugar
3 tb Sweet white onion, chop fine
2 tb Sweet pickle relish from jar
-with  the relish juice
3 ts Chopped fine
1/4 ts Salt
Chop the sweet white onion in food processor, put in small container
and set aside. Chop carrot in food processor, put in small container
and set aside.

Mix all ingredients listed in bowl and stir to blend throughly. Do
not use electric mixer. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight
to let flavors blend.

Enjoy!
