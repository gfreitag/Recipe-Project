Title: ROSS PEROT
Categories: Alcoholic, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Serving
1 1/4 oz Gin
1/2 oz Triple sec
2 oz Cranberry juice
1/2 oz Club soda
Pour into ice-filled glass. Stir. Serves 1.

Per serving: 217 calories, 0 grams fat (0 percent fat from calories),
0 milligrams cholesterol, 0 grams protein, 12 grams carbohydrate, 12
milligrams sodium.

SOURCE: The Vertigo Diner, Raleigh, NC. Recipe appeared in the August
21 edition of the Raleigh News & Observer. Format in Meal-Master by
