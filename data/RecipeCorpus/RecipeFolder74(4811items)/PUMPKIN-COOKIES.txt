Title: PUMPKIN COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 36 Servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Mashed pumpkin
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Nuts
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Baking powder
Icing sugar (optional)
Cream shortening and sugar, then add pumpkin, raisins, nuts and
vanilla and mix well.  Sift together flour, baking soda, cinnamon and
baking powder and add to creamed ingredients. Mix well, then drop ty
teaspoonsful onto greased cookie sheet.  BAte at 350F for 8 to 10
minutes.
When cool, dust with icing sugar if desired.

Source: The Harrowsmith Cookbook, Volume Three
