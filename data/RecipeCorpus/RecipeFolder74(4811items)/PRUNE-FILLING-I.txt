Title: PRUNE FILLING I
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Eggs, well beaten
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Cooked prunes, chopped
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 tb Butter
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Combine all ingredients except flavoring.  Cook over hot water,
stirring constantly, until thick enough to spread. Add flavoring.

Grace Viall Gray, Glen Ellyn, IL.
