Title: REALLY QUICK MEAT BALLS
Categories: Beef, Side dish
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Ground beef
1 cn Evaporated milk
1 c  Fine bread crumbs
1 pk Onion soup mix (dry)
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Salt
Pam or vegetable oil
Mix together all the ingredients. Form into balls. Spray skillet with
Pam. Brown meatballs. Serve
