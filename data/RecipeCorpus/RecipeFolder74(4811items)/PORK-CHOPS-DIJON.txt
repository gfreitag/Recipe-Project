Title: PORK CHOPS DIJON
Categories: Low-cal, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4 x  Pork loin chops, 1/2 thick
1 x  Onion, medium
3 tb Dijon-style mustard
2 tb Italian salad dressing
-low-calorie dressing
1/4 ts Pepper
Trim excess fat from chops.  Spray a 10-inch skillet with nonstick
spray coating.  Brown chops on both sides in hot skillet. Remove
chops. Add onions; cook and stir over medium heat 3 minutes. Push
onions aside; return chops to skillet.  Combine mustard, dressing and
pepper; spread over chops. Cover; cook over medium-low heat 15
minutes or till meat is tender.
