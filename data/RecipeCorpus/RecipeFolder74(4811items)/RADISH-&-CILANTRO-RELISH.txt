Title: RADISH & CILANTRO RELISH
Categories: Relishes, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Radishes; Thinly Sliced
1/2 c  Onion; Chopped, 1 Med
3 tb Orange Juice
2 tb Lime Juice
2 tb Fresh Cilantro; Fine Snipped
2 tb Vegetable Oil
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper; Freshly Ground
Mix all ingredients in glass or plastic bowl.  Cover and refrigerate
at least 1 hour.

Makes 3 cups of relish.
