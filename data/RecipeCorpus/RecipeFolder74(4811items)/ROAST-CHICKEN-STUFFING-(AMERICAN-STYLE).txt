Title: ROAST CHICKEN STUFFING (AMERICAN STYLE)
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Chestnuts -- peeled
1/2 c  Chicken stock
1/2 c  Brandy
3    Whole shallots -- chopped
2 tb Butter
1/2 lb Sausage meat
1/2 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Sage
2 tb Fresh parsley -- chopped
1/4 ts Dried marjoram
3 c  Dried bread -- cubed
Place chestnuts in a saucepan with chicken stock and brandy. Bring to
a boil and simmer 30-40 minutes.

Saute shallots in butter. Add sausage meat and cook 5 minutes,
stirring with a fork to separate sausage crumbs.

Coarsely chop half the chestnuts and grind the other half. Combine all
ingredients and mix well, adding more stock or brandy to moisten the
stuffing.

: Elizabeth Powell
