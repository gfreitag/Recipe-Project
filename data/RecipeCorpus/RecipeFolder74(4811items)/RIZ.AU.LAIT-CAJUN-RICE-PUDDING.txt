Title: RIZ.AU.LAIT CAJUN RICE PUDDING
Categories: Puddings, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Cinnamon
4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Cooked rice
1 1/2 c  Sugar
cook rice and milk together for 2 mins.  Add sugar; stir & cook 1
min. Add vanilla and cinnamon

By Lora Hebert of Galliano, La. From: Lisa Wilson
