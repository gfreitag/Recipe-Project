Title: RICE~ ORANGE & PECAN SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Cooked brown rice
1 c  Orange juice
1 c  Orange sections, or canned
-mandarin orange sections
1/2 c  Golden raisins
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Pecan halves, toasted
Salt and Pepper, to taste
Toss all ingredients together. Serve at room temperature.
