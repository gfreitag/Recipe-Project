Title: ROMANTIC INCENSE (A DOUGH INCENSE)
Categories: Seasonings
Yield: 1 Batch
1/4 c  Ground lavender flowers
1/4 c  Ground rosemary leaves
1/8 c  Ground rose petals
1 c  + 1 tb. red sandalwood
-- powder
1 c  + 1 tb. water
1/2 ts Plus pinch potassium nitrate
Tragacanth powder; as needed
From Sandy Maine's "Herbal Incense" article in "The Herb Companion."
Dec. 1992/Jan. 1993, Vol. 5, No. 2. Pg. 38. Posted by Cathy Harned.
