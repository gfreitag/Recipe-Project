Title: ROCKY ROADHOUSE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
8    Eggs
1/4 c  Oil
1 3/4 c  Sour cream
1 pk Butterscotch pudding mix --
-instant, 8 oz
1 pk Chocolate cake mix, devil's
Food
1 c  Walnuts -- chopped
1/2    Bag miniature marshmallows
8 oz Miniature chocolate chips
8 oz Miniature butterscotch
Chips
Break eggs into a large bowl and mix in the oil, sour cream, and
butterscotch pudding mix. Add cake mix. Stir, but do not overmix.
Stir just long enough for the mix to be absorbed but not completely
smooth. This will be a heavy mix.  Add the rest of the ingredients.
Spoon into a greased and floured cake pan. Bake at 350^ for about 3/4
hour.
