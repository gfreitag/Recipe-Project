Title: RICE TRAIL MIX
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 Cups
2 c  Crispy rice & corn squares
-- (bite-size)
2 c  Puffed rice
2 c  Crispy rice squares
-- (bite-size)
1 c  Dried banana chips
1 c  Dry roasted peanuts
1 c  Chocolate candies
1 c  Seedless raisins
Combine all ingredients.  Store in tightly covered container.

Each 1/2-cup serving provides: * 153 calories * 3.3 grams protein *
5.6 grams fat * 24.7 grams carbohydrate * 83 milligrams sodium * 1
milligram cholesterol
