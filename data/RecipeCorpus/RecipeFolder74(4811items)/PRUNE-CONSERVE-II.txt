Title: PRUNE CONSERVE II
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Prunes
1/4 lb Chopped nuts
1    Orange
1 c  Sugar
Wash prunes.  Cover with water.  Simmer until tender. Cool. Remove
pits. Cut prunes in halves.  Extract juice from orange. Remove white
membrane from orange peel. Chop peel. Combine prunes, orange juice,
peel, and sugar. Mix thoroughly.  Simmer slowly, stirring constantly,
until thick. Add nuts and cook 5 minutes. Mrs. Helen M. Beaver, Port
Ewen, N.Y.
