Title: ROCKY ROAD DROPS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 5 Dozen
1 pk (11 1/2 oz) milk chocolate
-morsels
14 oz Can sweetened condensed
-milk
3 c  Miniature marshmallows
2 c  Dry roasted peanuts
1 c  Raisins
In top of double boiler, over hot (not boiling) water, melt morsels
with sweetened condensed milk; remove from heat. In large bowl,
combine marshmallows, nuts and raisins; stir in chocolate mixture.
Drop by teaspoofuls onto wax paper-lined baking sheets; chill 2 hours
or until firm. Store loosely covered in cool dry place.
