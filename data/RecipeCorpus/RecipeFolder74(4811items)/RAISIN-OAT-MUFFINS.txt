Title: RAISIN OAT MUFFINS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 10 Servings
2 1/2    Rolled oats
1 pt Baking powder
1/2    Raisins
2 pt Raisins
1    Skim milk
1 pt Lemon juice
1/3    Firmly packed brown sugar
1 pt Vegetable oil
2 pt Vegetable oil
2    Egg whites
2 pt Vanilla extract
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly oil 10 muffin cups or spray with
a nonstick cooking spray. Place oats in a blender container and blend
until it has the consistency of flour. Place in a large bowl and add
baking powder. Mix well. Stir in raisins. Place milk in a medium
bowl. Add lemon juice and let stand 1 minute. Add remaining
ingredients. Beat with a fork or wire whisk until blended. Add to dry
mixture, mixing until all ingredients are moistened. Divide batter
evenly into prepared muffin cups. Bake 20 minutes, until a toothpick
inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean. Remove muffins
to arack to cool.
