Title: QUACKER JACKS
Categories: Snacks, Cake mix
Yield: 1 Servings
CONKLIN (VXBD11A)
1 c  Peanuts
2    St marg
5 c  Popped corn
2 c  Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 ts Baking soda
Mix all ingredients together except corn and baking soda. Cook over
low heat till melted then add baking soda and coat corn and peanut
mixture. Spread in cookie sheet and let cool
