Title: QUICK-FIX POINSETTIAS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
Fast & Fancy Cookie Recipe
Roll out dough 1/4" thick; cut out using 2 1/2" flower cutter. Bake 10
minutes. Spread cooled cookies with plain Royal Icing; decorate like
poinsettias with red candied cherry slices for petals & green candied
cherry pieces for centers. Submitted By THESERVS@GATE.NET (THOMAS E.
HAUG) On   MON, 13 NOV 1995 101606 -0500
