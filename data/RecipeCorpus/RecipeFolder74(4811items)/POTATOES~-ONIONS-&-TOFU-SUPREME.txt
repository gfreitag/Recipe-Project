Title: POTATOES~ ONIONS & TOFU SUPREME
Categories: Vegetarian, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
10 oz Tofu chunks
3/4 c  ;water
4 md Potatoes
6    Green onions
1/4 ts Pepper
Saute chopped onions in water only. Steam potatoes.
Then combine chunks tofu, 3/4 cup water, 4 medium potatoes, 4-6
onions (chopped), 1/4 tsp pepper in saucepan and simmer 10 to 15
minutes. Season to taste.
