Title: RED CAKE ICING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
5 tb Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Milk
1 c  Butter, softened
1 c  Sugar
Cook flour and milk until extremely thick.  Cool. Meanwhile, cream
sugar, butter,  and  vanilla until fluffy. When flour and milk
mixture has cooled, add to creamed ingredients and beat until smooth.
