Title: RUBARB RELISH
Categories: Canadian, Relish
Yield: 10 Cups
5 c  Rubarb,diced
3 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Cloves
Combine everything in a pot and bring to a boil,simmer uncovered for 1
hour.Stir often.Place in canning jars (Mason Jars) and seal. Great
with Ham or other meat dishes
