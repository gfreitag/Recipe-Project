Title: RHUBARB & FIG PRESERVES
Categories: Fruits, Preserves
Yield: 1 Batch
3 1/2 qt Rhubarb
1 pt Figs; chopped
8 c  Sugar
1    Lemon
Cut the rhubarb into small pieces, add sugar and let the mixture stand
overnight. In the morning, boil until it is thick and add one pint of
chopped figs plus the juice and rind of one lemon. Cook rapdily until
the mixture is thick and clear. Pack while hot into sterile, hot
jars. Seal immediately.

Arizona Highways magazine
per Sandee Eveland
