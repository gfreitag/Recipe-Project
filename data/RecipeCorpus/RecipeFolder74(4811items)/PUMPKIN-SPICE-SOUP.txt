Title: PUMPKIN SPICE SOUP
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables, Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
16 oz Pumpkin; 1 Can
26 oz Evaporated Skimmed Milk; 2cn
1 tb Molasses
1/2 c  Light Corn Syrup
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pumpkin-Pie Spice
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
1 x  Nutmeg; To Taste
Place all the ingredients in a 2 1/2 to 3-quart mixer bowl. Beat
until well blended.  Refrigerate for several hours and stir before
serving. Sprinkle with nutmeg for a garnish.
