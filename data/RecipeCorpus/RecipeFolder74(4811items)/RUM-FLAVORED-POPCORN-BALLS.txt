Title: RUM FLAVORED POPCORN BALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 qt Popped popcorn
2/3 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
2 1/2 tb Light corn syrup
1 ts Salt
1 tb Rum flavor
1/3 ts Vinegar
Preheat oven to 250. Place popcorn in large 4 inch deep buttered
baking pan.  Keep warm in the oven.  In a large saucepan, stir sugar,
water and corn srup together until sugar is dissolved. Boil without
stirring until mixture reached 250 on a candy thermometer. Remove
from heat and quickly stir in salt, rum flavor and vinegar. Remove
popcorn from oven. Pour syrup mixture over popcorn and mix
