Title: RED BEAN DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  COOKED RED KIDNEY BEANS OR U
1 ts BLACK PEPPER
1/2 ts SALT
1/8 ts TABASCO SAUCE
1 lb CHIPS OR CRACKERS
Drain red beans. Mix them with spices in a blender for a few minutes
until they are pureed. Add more Tabasco to taste. Mix well. Can be
served hot or at room temperature. Serve with crackers or chips.
Yields
1-1/2    cups.
