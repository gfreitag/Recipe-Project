Title: REAL CHOCOLATE BREAD (DAK BREADMASTER RECIPE)
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts, Breadmaker
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Flour, bread
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Unsweeted cocoa
1    Egg, unbeaten
1/4 c  Soft butter or margarine
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Milk, warm
Real Chocolate Bread

Add all the ingredients in the order given, select white bread, and
push start.  DAK/Breadmaker Recipes
