Title: QUICK SWEET POTATO PIE
Categories: New, Text, Import
1 c  cooked mashed sweet
potatoes
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1 ts vanilla extract
1/2    stick melted diet margarine
1/2 c  white sugar
1/2 c  dark brown sugar
1/4 c  egg substitute
ds salt
ds cinnamon and nutmeg
:
:
1    unbaked pie crust

Preheat oven to 400. Mix together sweet potatoes, extract, margarine,
white and brown sugars, egg substitute, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
Blend well. Pour into pie crust and bake 12 minutes at 400. Reduce
oven temperature to
350    and bake until done.
