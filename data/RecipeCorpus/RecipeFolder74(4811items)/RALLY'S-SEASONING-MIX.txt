Title: RALLY'S SEASONING MIX
Categories: Mix, Cake mix
Yield: 1 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 tb Season salt
Combine well and use as a seasoning on fried food as soon as you
remove from hot oil. Apply to food according to taste and store at
room temperature to use in 1 year. Source: Gloria Pitzer's Make Alike
Recipes.
