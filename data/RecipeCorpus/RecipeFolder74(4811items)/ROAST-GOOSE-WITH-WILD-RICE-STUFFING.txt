Title: ROAST GOOSE WITH WILD RICE STUFFING
Categories: Goose
Yield: 1 Servings
14 lb Goose
MMMMM--------------------------STUFFING-------------------------------
2 c  ;Water
1/2 c  Wild rice
1 c  White rice
1/2 c  Onion, diced
1 cn Mandarin oranges
1/2 c  Pine nuts
1 c  Chestnuts, shelled &
-quartered; (opt)
1/2 c  Celery, diced
1/2    Cube of butter
Goose--Clean out excess fat (there will be a lot!) and rinse well.
Stuff, salt & pepper skin, prick well, & tent with tin foil. Roast at
375 F for 4 hours. Remove tent & cook 30 minutes more. (Approx 25
minutes per pound)

Stuffing--Cook rice until done. Saute celery & onion in butter. Mix
all ingredients & stuff goose.
