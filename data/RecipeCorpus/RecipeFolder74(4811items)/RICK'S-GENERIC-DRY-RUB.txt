Title: RICK'S GENERIC DRY RUB
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Paprika
1/4 c  Black pepper
1/4 c  Salt
1 ea ??????, see directions
Use this rub as a base and add your favorite dry spices to make your
smoker the best on the block. Garlic and brown sugar come to mind...

Combine these ingredients in a small bowl and mix well. This rub can
be used on any barbeque item. Just sprinkle lightly on the intended
food and rub. For a spicy taste, sprinkle more heavily. This batch
will do about 6 to 8 slabs of pork ribs.

Source:  Kansas City Barbeque
