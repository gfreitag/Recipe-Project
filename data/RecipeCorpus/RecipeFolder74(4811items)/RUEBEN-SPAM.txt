Title: RUEBEN SPAM
Categories: Meats, Casseroles, Snacks, Pork
Yield: 6 Servings
16 oz Canned sauerkraut, drained
2 md Green peppers, chopped
12 oz Can Spam luncheon meat
-cubed
2 c  Shredded swiss cheese
1 c  Bacon salad dressing
1 lg Tomato, peeled, sliced
1 c  Rye crackers, crumbed
1/4 ts Caraway seeds
1/4 c  Butter, melted
Vegetable cooking spray
Heat oven to 450 deg f. Spread sauerkraut in bottom of 2 quart
casserole dish, coated with cooking spray. Sprinkle with green
peppers. Top with layers of meat, cheese, salad dressing and
tomatoes. Combine cracker crumbs, caraway seeds and butter. Sprinkle
over tomatoes. Bake uncovered, 30 minutes. Serves six.
