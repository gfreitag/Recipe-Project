Title: POTLUCK SPREAD
Categories: Potluck, High fat
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Shredded cheddar cheese,
. chopped
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1 ea Jar chopped bacon bits-real
1 bn Scallions, chopped
Mayonnaise to bind other
. ingredients (and to taste)
Put all ingredients in a bowl adding mayonnaise until the ingredients
bind together.

From the mother of a co-worker, who got it from a friend, who got it
from a co-worker, who got it from???....

Suggestion:  Do <<not>> serve with salted crackers--the taste of the
bacon is lost on these. Sourdough cocktail rounds might be nice as a
backdrop?
