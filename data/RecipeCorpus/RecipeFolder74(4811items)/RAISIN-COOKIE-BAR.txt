Title: RAISIN COOKIE-BAR
Categories: Cookies, Bars
Yield: 24 Bars
1    Egg; unbeaten
2/3 c  C and H Granulated Sugar
1/3 c  Butter; melted
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
2/3 c  Seedless raisins
2 tb C and H Granulated Sugar
- mixed with...
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Beat egg; gradually beating in sugar.  Stir in melted butter and
vanilla. Sift together and add the flour, baking powder and salt; mix
well. Add raisins. Spread in greased 9-inch square pan. Sprinkle top
with sugar-cinnamon mixture.  Bake in 350 degree oven about 20
minutes, or until done.  (Don't overbake.) Leave in pan. Cut into 24
bars.
