Title: POPCORN CRACKLES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Molasses
1 c  Sugar
3 qt Popped corn
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
Melt butter.  Add sugar, salt, and molasses.  boil to the hard crack
stage (285 - 290 F).  Pour over corn, stir while pouring. Spread in
thin layer to cool. Break in pieces. The Household Searchlight
