Title: ROCKY ROAD FROSTING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  BUTTER
1/4 c  COCOA
1/3 c  BUTTERMILK
4 c  POWDERED SUGAR
1/2 c  MINIATURE MARSHMALLOWS
COMBINE BUTTER, COCOA AND BUTTERMILK IN A SMALL PAN AND BRING TO A
BOIL. POUR OVER POWDERED SUGAR AND MARSHMALLOWS. BEAT UNTIL SMOOTH.
SREAD ON CAKE.
