Title: RICE KRISPIE SQUARES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
4 tb Butter
4 c  Marshmallows or 10 oz
5 c  Rice krispie cereal
Melt butter in saucepan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir
till melted. Cook 3 minutes, stirring constantly . Remove from heat,
add Rice Krispies and stir till all are coated. Using buttered
spatula, press evenly into buttered 13x9x2" pan. Cool. Cut into 2"
squares. VARIATIONS: add 1 cup raisins
add 1 cup peanuts add 1/4 cup peanut butter to marshmallows melt 2
squares chocolate with marshmallows for Christmas: add green food
colouring (if desired), shape into "trees" or press into buttered
ring or small Bundt mold. Decorate with red cinnamon candies (for
tree) or spearmint leaves and jelly berries for ring mold (resembles
a wreath) posted by Anne MacLellan Fat grams    per serving:
