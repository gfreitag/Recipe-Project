Title: QUESADILLAS
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 2 Servings
4    (6" square) Flour tortillas
1/2 c  Cheese, grated
1 tb Choice of: chopped black
Olives, onions, tomatoes,
Chilies or jalapeno peppers
Spray cooking surfaces with non-stick spray or brush with margarine.
Place 2 tortillas into sandwich maker. Place 2 tablespoons of mixture
into each triangular pocket. Put 2 tortillas on top. Lower lid, latch
and cook 3-5 minutes or until done.  Serve with sour cream, salsa, or
guacamole.
