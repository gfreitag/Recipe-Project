Title: RED LOBSTER CREAMY CAESAR DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings, Cake mix, Copycat
Yield: 12 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
3/4 c  Bottled Italian dressing
1 tb Parmesan -- grated
1 tb Sugar
1/3 c  Mayo
1 ts Anchovy paste -- or
Soy sauce
Combine all with wire whisk. Keep refrigerated. Use in a week. Source:
Gloria Pitzer.
