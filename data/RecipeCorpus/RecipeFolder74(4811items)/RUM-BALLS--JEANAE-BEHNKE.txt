Title: RUM BALLS--JEANAE BEHNKE
Categories: Cookies, Candies, Alcohol, Resort, Holidays
Yield: 36 Servings
1 c  Crushed vanilla wafers
1 c  Powdered sugar
1 c  Chopped nuts
2 tb Cocoa
2 tb Light corn syrup
1/4 c  Rum
Combine first four ingredients.  Add corn syrup and rum. Mix well.
Shape into 1 inch calls.  Roll in granulated sugar or chopped nuts.
If preferred, 1/4 c heavy cream and 1 t rum extract may be
substituted for the rum. Makes 3 dozen.
