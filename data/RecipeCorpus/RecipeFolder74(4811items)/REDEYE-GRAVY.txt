Title: REDEYE GRAVY
Categories: Sauces
All drippings from a skillet of
Yield: 8 Servings
-ham or bacon
1 cup black coffee (not instant)
1/4    tspn black pepper

Pour the coffee into the skillet containing the hot drippings. Stir
but do not allow to boil. Sprinkle the pepper into the mixture. Pour
off into a bowl or gravy boat. serve hot over grits, mashed potatos,
rice, corn bread, or biscuits.
