Title: REFRESHING STRAWBERRY SOUP
Categories: Soups, Low-fat, Low-cal
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Strawberries, cold
1 c  Low-fat buttermilk
4 ts Sugar
Mint leaves (opt)
In food processor, blend all ingredients. Serve immediately or chill
in refrigerator. Garnish with mint leaves. Variations: Substitute
raspberries, cantaloupe, honey dew melon, or nectarine for
strawberries.
