Title: POPPYSEED & NUT FILLING FOR HAMANTASCHEN
Categories: Kosher, Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Poppy seeds
2 tb Corn syrup
1 tb Chopped citron
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
2 tb Butter
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Raisins
1 c  Milk
Put washed and drained poppy seeds through finest blade of food
chopper and mix with rest of ingredients EXCEPT vanilla. Let mixture
cook over slow heat until thick, stirring often. Cool and add vanilla.
