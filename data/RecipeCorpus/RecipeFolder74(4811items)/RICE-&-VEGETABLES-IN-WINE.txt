Title: RICE & VEGETABLES IN WINE
Categories: Side dish, Rice, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Oil
1 ea Onion, chopped
1 md Zucchini, chopped
1 md Carrot, chopped
1 ea Stalk celery, chopped
1 c  Long grain rice
1 1/4 c  Vegetable stock
1 c  White wine
Heat the oil in saucepan and saute the onion. Add the rest of the
veggies and stir them over a medium heat, until lightly browned. Add
the rice, veg stock and white wine, cover and cook 15-20 mins until
all the liquid has been absorbed.
