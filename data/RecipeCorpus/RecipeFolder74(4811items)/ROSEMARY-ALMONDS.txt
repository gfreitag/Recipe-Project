Title: ROSEMARY ALMONDS
Categories: Snacks, Appetizers, Nuts
Yield: 2 Cups
2 c  Almonds, whole, blanched
1 tb Butter or margarine
-- melted
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cayenne
2 ts Rosemary leaves, crushed
Preheat oven to 350 F (moderate).

Mix all ingredients in a shallow pan.  Roast for 15 to 20 minutes,
stirring occasionally, until lightly browned. Drain on absorbent
paper; cool.

Calories per tablespoon: About 55
