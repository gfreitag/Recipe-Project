Title: RICE PUDDING 5
Categories: Puddings, Desserts, Breakfast
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
2 tb Flour
2 c  Milk
1 ts Coconut flavoring
1 1/2 c  Cooked rice
1/2    Stick oleo or butter
Sift sugar & flour.  Beat eggs until frothy.  Add to flour along with
other ingredients except oleo or butter. Melt oleo in baking dish.
Pour other ingredients into this dish.  Bake at 300 degree F. until
slightly brown on top.  Don't over bake.

By Jean Abercrombie of Raceland, La From A/O/G Louisiana Women's
Ministries.
