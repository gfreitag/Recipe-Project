Title: POST GRAPE-NUTS BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 c  Post Grape-nuts
2 c  Sour milk
4 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Baking soda
4 ts Baking powder
1 pn Salt
2    Eggs, well beaten
Soak Grape-nuts for 10 to 15 minutes in sour milk. Sift together
flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, & salt. Alternately mix in
with dry ingredients the Grape-Nut mixture and the eggs.  Place in a
greased and floured loaf pan and let stand for 30 minutes. Bake at
375 F. for 35 to 40 minutes, or until done.
