Title: PRUNE PICKLES
Categories: Pickles
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Prunes
1 tb Stick cinnamon
1 ts Whole cloves
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Whole allspice
Wash prunes.  Cover with cold water.  Cover and simmer until tender.
Add sugar, vinegar, and spices.  Simmer 20 minutes. Edith Johnson,
Genesee, PA.
