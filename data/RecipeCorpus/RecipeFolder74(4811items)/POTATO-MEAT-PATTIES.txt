Title: POTATO-MEAT PATTIES
Categories: Leftovers, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Leftover mashed potatoes
-- seasoned
1 c  Leftover meat
-- finely chopped
2 tb Onion; finely chopped
2    Egg yolks
2 tb Milk
1/8 ts Pepper
2 tb Oil
Mix potatoes, meat, onion, egg yolks, milk, and pepper. Shape into 6
patties, using about 1/2 cup potato mixture for each.

Heat fat in frypan over moderate heat.  Cook patties until lightly
browned on one side; turn and brown the other side. Serve hot.

Calories per serving: About 175
