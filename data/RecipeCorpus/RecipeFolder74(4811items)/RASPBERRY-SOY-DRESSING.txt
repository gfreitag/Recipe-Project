Title: RASPBERRY SOY DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Vegan
Yield: 1 Recipe
5 tb Raspberry vinegar
2 tb Soy sauce
Crushed garlic to taste
- (optional)
Mix the raspberry vinegar with the soy sauce and add the garlic to
taste if wanted. Leave to stand for a while before serving to allow
the flavours to mingle. A lovely summer dressing for leafy salads.
