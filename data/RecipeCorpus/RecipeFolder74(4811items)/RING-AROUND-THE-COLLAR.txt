Title: RING-AROUND-THE-COLLAR
Categories: Better, Laundry, Try soon
Yield: 1 Good idea
MMMMM----------------------MAKE IT YOURSELF---------------------------
1 tb Ammonia
1/2 ts Detergent
1 c  Warm water
This usually works: Rub warm detergent water before washing and allow
to set 15 mins. For tougher jobs: Work solution in to collar and
allow it to set 30 mins before laundering. Typed by Sherilyn Schamber
