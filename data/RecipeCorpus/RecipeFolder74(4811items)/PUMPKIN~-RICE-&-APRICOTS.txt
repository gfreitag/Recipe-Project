Title: PUMPKIN~ RICE & APRICOTS
Categories: Side dish, Russian, Rice, Fruits, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Oil
1 md Onion, finely chopped
6 c  Pumpkin*, cubed
3/4 c  Dried apricots, chopped
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Water
1 c  Cooked rice
*Or can substitute butternut or buttercup squash or sweet potatoes.
Heat oil in a wide skillet over medium heat. Add onion and cook & stir
occasionally, until it begins to brown. Add pumpkin pieces to skillet
as you prepare it. Stir to coat. Chop apricots and add to skillet
along with salt and 3/4 cup water. Cover and simmer the mixture 15
minutes. Add cooked rice and cook 10 minutes more, or until pumpkin
is tender. Stir often as it cooks.
