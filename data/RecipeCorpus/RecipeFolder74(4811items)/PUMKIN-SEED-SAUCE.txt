Title: PUMKIN SEED SAUCE
Categories: Betty, Crockers, Pasta, Favorites
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Pumpkin Seeds, Roasted --
Shelled
1 sm Onion -- Chopped
1 sl White Bread Slices -- Torn
Into Small Pcs.
1    Clove Garlic -- Crushed
2 tb Oil
2 tb Green Chiles -- Canned And
Chopped
1 cn Chicken Broth
1/2 c  Heavy Cream
1/8 ts Salt
Cook pumpkin seeds, onion, bread and garlic in oil, stirring
frequently, until bread is golden brown. Stir in chilies.

Place mixture in food processor workbowl fitted with steel blade;
cover and process until smooth. Stir in broth, whipping cream and
salt.
