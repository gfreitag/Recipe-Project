Title: POWDERED SUGAR GLAZE FOR COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Powdered sugar
1/4 ts Vanilla
1 tb Milk
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl. Add 1 teaspoon milk at a time
until glaze is of drizzling consistency. Spread on cookies with a
small brush or spatula.
