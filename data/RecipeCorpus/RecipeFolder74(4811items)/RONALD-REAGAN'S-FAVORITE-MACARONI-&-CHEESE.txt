Title: RONALD REAGAN'S FAVORITE MACARONI & CHEESE
Categories: Pasta, Celebrity
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Macaroni
1 ts Butter; melted
1    Eggs; beaten
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dry mustard
1 c  Milk
3 c  Sharp cheese; grated
Boil macaroni and drain thoroughly.  Stir in butter and egg. Mix salt
and mustard with 1 tbsp. hot water. Add to milk. Add the cheese,
leaving enough to sprinkle on top. Pour into buttered casserole, add
milk, sprinkle with cheese. Bake at 350~ for about 45 minutes or
until custard is set and top is crusty.
