Title: RASPBERRY DRESSING
Categories: Sauces, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Plain nonfat yogurt
1/2 c  Raspberries*
1 tb Raspberry or red wine
-vinegar
2 ts Sugar
Place all ingredients in blender. Cover and blend on high speed about
15 seconds or until smooth.

*Frozen unsweetened loose-pack raspberries, thawed and drained, can be
substituted for the fresh raspberries.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
