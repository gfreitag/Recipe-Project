Title: ROSEMARY FOCACCIA BITES
Categories: Snack
Yield: 12 Servings
1    Tube (10oz) refrigerated
Pizza dough
2 tb Oil, preferably olive
1 ts Salt
1 ts Dried rosemary, crushed
1/2 ts Grated orange zest
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Sliced roasted red peppers
From a jar drained
2    Scallions,chopped
(same as green onions)
1/4 c  Kalamata olives, pitted and
Chopped
Preheat oven to 425 degree F. On baking sheet, unroll pizza dough
into a 8 x 12 rectangle.  In small bowl combine oil, rosemary, salt,
zest and pepper.  Brush dough with oil mixture, leaving 1/4 border
around edges. Sprinkle with roasted red peppers, scallions and
olives. Bake until golden bron, about 10-12 minutes. Cut into squares
