Title: POPCORN CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Kids
Yield: 6 Servings
4    Qt. popped corn
1/2 lb Gum drops
1/2 lb Peanuts
1 lb Marshmallows
1/2 c  Melted margarine
1/2 c  Corn oil
Melt marshmallows, margarine, and corn oil.  Pour over popcorn, nuts
and gum drips.  Mix together and pat in a bundt cake pan or angel
food cake pan. Cool.
