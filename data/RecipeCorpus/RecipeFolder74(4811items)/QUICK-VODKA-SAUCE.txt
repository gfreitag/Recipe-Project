Title: QUICK VODKA SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Jar prepared spag. sauce --
1/4 c  Vodka
1 ts Oregano
3 tb Parm cheese
1/2 c  Heavy cream
*I'm fond of Paul Newmans, has a nice fresh taste...but there are some
really good ones out these days). --Mix all ing. and heat over a med
flame, stirring constantly so the mixture does not boil or curdle.
~-The sauce is an orange/pink when you are done and the vodka is only
a very light flavoring that most can not name as vodka. Angenen.
