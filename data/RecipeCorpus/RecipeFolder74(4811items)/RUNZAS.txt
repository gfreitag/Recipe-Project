Title: RUNZAS
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM---------------------------DOUGH--------------------------------
2 c  Warm water
2 pk Yeast
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg
1/4 c  Margarine, melted & cooled
6 1/2 c  Flour
MMMMM-----------------------RUNZA FILLING----------------------------
2 lb Hamburger
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
3 1/2 c  Cabbage; shredded
1/4 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Dash Tabasco sauce
Dough:  Mix water, yeast, sugar and salt; stir until dissolved. Add
egg and margarine.  Stir in flour. Put in tight bowl and refigerate 4
hours or overnight. Roll dough into oblong shape and cut into
squares. Put in hamburger mixture.  Pull up 4 sides and press edges
together. Place on greased cookie sheet and bake 20 minutes in a 350^
oven.

Runza Filling: Brown hamburger and onion; drain off excess. Add
shredded cabbage, seasonings and water. Simmer 15 minutes. Cool
completely before putting in dough.
