Title: PUDDING CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 1 Cake
1/4 lb Butter
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
1/2 c  QUICK Oats
MMMMM------------------------SECOND LAYER-----------------------------
8 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Whipped cream
1 c  Sugar, confectioners
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1 pk French Vanilla pudding,
-Instant
1 pk Chocolate Pudding, instant
3 c  Cold milk
Blend butter with flour, nuts and quick oats. Press in a 9x13" pan
and bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes. Remove from oven and C O O L
!!

For second layer, mix cream cheese, whipped cream and confectioners'
sugar. Spread over COOLED crust.

For third layer, mix both puddings cold milk. Spread over previous
mixture evenly (Usually this part is easy.)

Frost with whipped cream and grate a bit of dark chocolate across the
top for effect.   Chill for several hours.
