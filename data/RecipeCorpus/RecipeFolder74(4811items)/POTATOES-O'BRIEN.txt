Title: POTATOES O'BRIEN
Categories: Potatoes
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1/2 c  Sweet red pepper -- chopped
4 md Red potatoes -- cubed
3 tb Cooking oil
1/4 c  Beef broth
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Salt
In a skillet over medium heat, saute the onion, peppers and potatoes
in oil for 4 minutes.  Combine broth, Worcestershire sauce and salt;
pour over vegetables.  Cover and cook for10 minutes or until potatoes
are tender, stirring occasionally.  Uncover and cook until liquid is
absorbed, about 3 minutes.

: Taste of Home June/July '96
