Title: PRETZELS & BEER BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 15 Servings
-BARB DAY GWHP32A
1    Ingredient
1 pk Yeast
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Sugar
3 c  Better for Bread flour
2 tb Gluten
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Baking soda
1 1/3 c  Pretzel sticks;thin/broken
-in half
1    Egg white
1 tb Vegetable oil
12 oz Light beer plus ->
1/4 c  Water, heated
Add all ingredients into the pan in the order listed. Select white
bread and push "Start" Containing no cholesterol and negligible
sugar, this exciting bread is perfect for party snacks or sandwiches.
