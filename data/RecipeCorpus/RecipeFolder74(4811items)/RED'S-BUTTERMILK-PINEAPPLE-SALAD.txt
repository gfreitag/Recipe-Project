Title: RED'S BUTTERMILK PINEAPPLE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Great, Marie
Yield: 10 Servings
6 oz Pkg apricot JELLO
8 oz CoolWhip
20 oz Crushed Pineapple, drained
2 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Chopped nuts
Heat pineapple to boiling.  Add Jello and stir to dissolve. When
cool, add the buttermilk and fold in the CoolWhip and nuts.

Pour into a 9 X 13 dish and chill.
