Title: QUICK BARBECUE SANDWICHES
Categories: Chicken, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Barbecue sauce
3 pk (3 ounces each) smoked
-Sliced chicken, ham, turkey
-Or pastrami, cut into 1-in
-strips
6    Hamburger buns, split
Stir chicken, ham, turkey, beef or pastrami into sauce. Cover and
simmer about 5 minutes or until meat is hot. Fill buns with meat
mixture. 6 SANDWICHES; 195 CALORIES PER SANDWICH. To Microwave: Mix
sauce ingredients in 4-cup microwavable measure. Microwave uncovered
on high 4 to 6 minutes, stirring after 2 minutes, until sauce is
slightly thickened Stir chicken, ham, turkey, beef or pastrami into
sauce. Cover tightly and microwave on high 5 to 7 minutes or until
meat is hot.
