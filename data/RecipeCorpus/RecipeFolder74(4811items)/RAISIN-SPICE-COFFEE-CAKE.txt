Title: RAISIN-SPICE COFFEE CAKE
Categories: Breads, Crocker
Yield: 9 Servings
2 c  All-purpose flour*
1 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Margarine or butter,
-softened
1 c  Milk
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1    Egg
1/2 c  Raisins
Streusel
Heat oven to 350F. Grease square pan, 9 x 9 x 2 inches. Beat all
ingredients except raisins and Streusel on low speed 30 seconds. Beat
on medium speed, scraping bowl occasionally, 2 minutes. Stir in
raisins.

Spread half of the batter in pan; sprinkle with half of the Streusel.
Top with remaining batter; sprinkle with remaining Streusel. Bake
until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean, 40 to 45
minutes. 1 coffee cake (9 servings); 460 calories per serving.

Streusel Coffee Cake: Omit cinnamon, allspice, nutmeg and raisins in
batter.

*If using self rising flour, omit baking powder and salt.

Source: Betty Crocker's Cookbook, 6th Edition
