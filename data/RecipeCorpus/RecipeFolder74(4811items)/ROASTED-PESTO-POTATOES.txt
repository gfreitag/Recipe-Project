Title: ROASTED PESTO POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables
3/4 c  store bought pesto
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb red potatoes -- cut into 1 "
chunks
:

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. In a large bowl, toss together the pesto
and the potatoes. Spread the potatoes out on a baking sheet. Bake for
45 minutes or until crispy and golden. Serve hot.
