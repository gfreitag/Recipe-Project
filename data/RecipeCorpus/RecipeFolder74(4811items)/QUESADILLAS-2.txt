Title: QUESADILLAS 2
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Pinto beans/ drained and
Rinsed
1    Jalapeno/ seeded and roughly
Chopped
1    Red onion/ roughly chopped
ts Or more commercial chili
Powder
ts Ground cumin
1/2 c  Fresh parsley
Throw everything in a food processor and pulse until still chunky.

Spread 1/2 inch between 2 whole wheat tortillas and heat until
toasted on hot non stick pan. Good with commercial salsa. (Don't put
bean mixture in tortillas until ready to cook)
