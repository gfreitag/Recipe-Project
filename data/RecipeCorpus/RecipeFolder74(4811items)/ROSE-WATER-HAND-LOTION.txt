Title: ROSE WATER HAND LOTION
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pt Borax
1/4 c  Rose water
1/4 c  Glycerin
2 ts Witch hazel
1 ts Oil -- almond
If you wish, substitute elderflower or calendula infusion for the rose
water. Melt the borax in the rose water or other tea over low heat.
Put remaining ingredients in bottle, add tea, and shake until
blended. Source: Victoria Magazine
