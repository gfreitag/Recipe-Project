Title: PUFFED RICE CRISPS
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 2 Dozen
1 c  White sugar
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Vinegar
1    Box puffed rice
1/2 c  Roger's Golden syrup
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Butter
Boil all ingredients except puffed rice to 240 degrees or until a
little will be brittle when dropped into cold water. Mix with puffed
rice and pour into greased pans to cool. Cut into squares before
quite cold.
