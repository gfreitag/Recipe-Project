Title: POTATO DELUXE...JOANNIE B.
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Potato chips
2 lb Hash browns
12 oz Cheddar cheese; shredded
1 pk Margarine
1 c  Am of chicken soup
1 pt Sour cream
1 ea Onion; chopped
DIRECTIONS ~------------------------------------------------------
~--- MIX TOGETHER , PUT IN 9X12 BAKING DISH.
