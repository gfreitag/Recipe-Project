Title: QUICK & EASY DILL MUSTARD
Categories: Spreads, Desserts, Herb/spices
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Dijon Mustard
1 tb Fresh Chopped Dill Or:
1 ts Dried Dill Weed
3 tb Olive Oil
Combine Dijon mustard with fresh chopped dill or dried dill weed and
olive oil.

Serve with pate or fish; or use as a sandwich spread. Also good added
to a salad dressing.

NOTE: Homemade mustards must be stored in the refrigerator.
