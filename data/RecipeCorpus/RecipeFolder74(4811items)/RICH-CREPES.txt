Title: RICH CREPES
Categories: Diabetic
Yield: 36 Servings
3    Eggs
ds Salt
2 tb (30 mL) liquid shortening
1 1/4 c  (310 mL) flour
1/2 c  (125 mL) evaporated skimmed
Milk
1 c  (500 mL) water
Place eggs in blender; whip to mix well. Add salt, shortening, flour,
milk and water. Whip to blend thoroughly. Allow to rest 1 hour at room
temperature before using. Cook crepe in pan according to
manufacturer's directions.

Exchange 1 crepe: 1/4 full-fat milk Calories 1 crepe: 30
