Title: RHUBARB BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
-Source: BREAD1.ZIP
1 1/2 c  Diced rhubarb stalks
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Butter
1 c  Brown sugar
4    -eggs
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Milk
1 ts Vinegar
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Chopped nuts
Sprinkle the sugar over the rhubarb; set aside. Cream the butter
with the brown sugar and beat in the eggs until mixture is light and
fluffy. Mix the flour with the baking soda and baking powder; set
aside. Add vinegar to milk and let stand for 10 minutes; set aside.
Add flour and milk, alternately, to the egg mixture. Stir in vanilla,
nuts and rhubarb. Pour into two greased 9 x 5 x 3" loaf pans. Bake @
325 degrees for 45 to 60 minutes. Turn out on a rack to cool.
