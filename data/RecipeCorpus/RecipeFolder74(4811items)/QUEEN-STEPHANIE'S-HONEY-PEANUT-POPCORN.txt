Title: QUEEN STEPHANIE'S HONEY PEANUT POPCORN
Categories: Candy, Honey
Yield: 1 Servings
4 qt Popcorn
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Peanut butter
Boil sugar and honey for 1 minute. Stir in peanut butter. Cool.
Dribble over popcorn. Toss.
