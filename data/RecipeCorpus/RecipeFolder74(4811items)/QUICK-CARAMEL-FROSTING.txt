Title: QUICK CARAMEL FROSTING
Categories: Frosting
Yield: 1 Batch
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar, brown
1/4 c  Milk
2 c  Sugar, confectioners
-sifted
Melt the butter; add the brown sugar and cook over low heat two
minutes, stirring constantly. Add the milk and continue stirring
until mixture comes to a boil. Remove from heat and cool. Add
confectioner's sugar until the frosting is of a spreading consistency.
