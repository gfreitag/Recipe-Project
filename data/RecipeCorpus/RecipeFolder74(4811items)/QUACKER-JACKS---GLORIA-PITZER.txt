Title: QUACKER JACKS - GLORIA PITZER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Peanuts
1/2 lb Butter
5 c  Popped Corn,
2 c  Brown Sugar
1/2 c  Light Corn Syrup,
1/2 ts Baking Soda.
Mix nuts with corn.  Melt butter and sugar and syrup. When melted add
baking powder then pour over corn and mix.
