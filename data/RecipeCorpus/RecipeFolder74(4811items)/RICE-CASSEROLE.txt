Title: RICE CASSEROLE
Categories: Digest, Ovo-lacto
Yield: 1 Servings
-rice
-chopped onion
-granted non-fat cheese
-tomatoes
-yellow squash
-zucchini
-non-fat sour cream or
-yogurt
1.  Cook rice. 2. Put rice on the bottom of a 9x12" baking dish 3.
Top with chopped onion and grated non-fat cheese 4. Layer on 2
good-sized sliced tomatoes.  You can also use sliced yellow squash or
zucchini. 5. Top with non-fat sour cream or yogurt. 6. Bake at 350
for 30 minutes.
