Title: PREVENTION'S PARSLEY-LADEN TABBOULEH
Categories: Salad, Persia
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Fresh flat-leaf parsley --
Chopped
2 c  Tomatoes -- chopped
1/2 c  Chopped scallions
1/2 c  Chopped celery
2 tb Chopped fresh mint
2 c  Bulgur -- prepared
1 tb Extra virgin olive oil
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Black pepper
Toss together. Recipe By
: "Middle Eastern Light" September 1996
