Title: QUICK MEAT SAUCE FOR SPAGHETTI
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Quart
1/2 c  Olive Oil
1    Clove Garlic, Pressed
1 lb Ground Beef
1/4 lb Lean Pork, Ground
2 c  Plum Tomatoes
1/2 c  Tomato Paste
1/2 c  Beef Stock
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Bay Leaf
1/2 c  Mushrooms, Sliced
Basil and/or Oregano
Parmesan Cheese
Heat olive oil in a medium sized pot. When hot, add garlic, meat,
tomatoes, tomato paste, stock, salt, pepper and the bay leaf. Simmer
uncovered for at least one hour. Add the mushrooms during the last 15
minutes. Season with basil and/or oregano and serve over pasta. Grate
parmesan cheese over each serving.
