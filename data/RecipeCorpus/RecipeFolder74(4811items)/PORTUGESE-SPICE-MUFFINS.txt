Title: PORTUGESE SPICE MUFFINS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
MMMMM---------------------FROM J.R'S KITCHEN--------------------------
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Ginger
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1    Egg beaten
1/4 c  Margarine melted
1/3 c  Molasses
3/4 c  Buttermilk
Mix dry ingredients together. Combine wet ingredients. make a well in
drys and combine liquids lightly. Spoon into muffin cups and bakee
400 15 to 20 minutes or until done.
