Title: RASPBERRY PEACH BREAKFAST SHAKE
Categories: Snacks, Syd's book, Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
10 oz Frozen Raspberries *
1 c  Canned Peach Nectar
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 tb Honey
* Frozen raspberries should be thawed and in light syrup.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Place all ingredients in a blender bowl and
process until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve. 109 calories per
serving. From: Syd's Cookbook.
