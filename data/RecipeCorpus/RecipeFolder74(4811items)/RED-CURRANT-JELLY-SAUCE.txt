Title: RED CURRANT JELLY SAUCE
Categories: Desserts, Toppings
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Heavy cream
1/4 c  Tart red currant jelly
Whip cream until stiff. With a fork, stir the jelly until it is
broken up into small pieces. Fold into the cream. Serve with Southern
Sweet Potato Pudding.
