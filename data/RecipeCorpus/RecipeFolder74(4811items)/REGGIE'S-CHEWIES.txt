Title: REGGIE'S CHEWIES
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
4 tb Liver powder or sub. chicken
Gizzards, finely chopped
2 1/2 c  Rye flour
1/2 c  Powdered milk
1    Egg, well beaten
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Onion or garlic powder
6 tb Shortening or margarine
1 ts Brown sugar
Combine all dry ingredients. Add shortening to dry ingredients. Add
beaten egg and liver powder(or substitution). Add 1/2 cup cold water
or giblet broth and form into a ball. Roll dough 1/2" thick on oiled
sheet and cut with cutter. Bake for 25-30 minutes on
350    oven. Store in refrigerator.
