Title: RED LOBSTER BREAD
Categories: Breads, Copycat
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Bisquick
1/2 c  Cold water
3/4 c  Grated cheddar
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
Butter
Garlic powder
Italian seasoning
Parsley
Mix all together and spoon onto baking sheet. Brush each biscuit with
a mixture of: melted butter, Italian seasoning, garlic powder and
parsley. Bake at 450 for 8-10 mins.
