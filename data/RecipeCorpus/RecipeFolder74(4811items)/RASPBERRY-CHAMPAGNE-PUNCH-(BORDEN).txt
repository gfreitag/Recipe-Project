Title: RASPBERRY CHAMPAGNE PUNCH (BORDEN)
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 3 Quarts
20 oz Frozen red raspberries in
-syrup; thawed
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Sugar
750 ml Rosé
1 qt Raspberry sherbet
750 ml Champagne; or asti spumante,
-chilled
In the blender container, purée the raspberries. In a large punch
bowl, combine the puréed raspberries, lemon juice, sugar and wine;
stir until the sugar dissolves. Just before serving, scoop the
sherbet into the punch bowl; add champagne. Stir gently.

Oregon]
