Title: ROYAL BASMATI
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Brown basmati
4 c  Water or broth
3 tb Olive oil
1 ts Whole cumin seeds (or 1/2
Teaspoon
Powdered cumin)
1 lg Onion peeled and chopped
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Frozen green peas
In a heavy skillet or Dutch oven with a lid heat oil and cumin seeds.
Stir for a few seconds add onions and cook until translucent. Add
rice and continue cooking for 3-4 min. Add liquid and bring to boil.
Cover reduce heat to low and cook 45-50 min. Stir in peas the last
few minutes of cooking. Makes 4 servings.

(*Substitution*  White basmati can be substituted for brown adjust
water to 3 cup water for 2 cup white basmati)
