Title: RICE STICKS WITH VEGETABLES
Categories: Oriental
Yield: 6 Servings
3 qt Water
1 pk Rice sticks (13 3/4 oz)
2    Stalks celery
4 oz Chinese pea pods
1 oz Oriental dried mushrooms *
1/4 c  Oil
1 lb Bean sprouts
1 tb Curry powder
1 c  Chicken broth
Salt
Soy sauce
*Note: Mushrooms should be softened in water. Bring water to boil and
add rice sticks. Cook 2 minutes, then drain. Rinse with cold water
and drain. Cut celery, pea pods and mushrooms into thin slices. Heat
oil until hot and add rice sticks. Cook, stirring, until brown.
Remove rice sticks from pan and drain. Add celery, pea pods,
mushrooms and bean sprouts and cook over high heat 2 minutes,
stirring constantly. Combine curry powder and chicken broth and add
to pan. Season to taste with salt. Pour over rice sticks and toss to
serve. Serve with soy sauce. Makes 6 to 8 servings
