Title: ROSEMARY CHAMOMILE FACIAL RINSE
Categories: Sauces, Seafood
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Dried rosemary
Or 1 tb fresh
2 tb Dried chamomile flowers
Or 1/4 cup fresh
4 c  Water
Boil all ingredients for 15 minutes.   Strain, retain liquid, cool
and put in container. Wipe liquid over face, let remain for 30
minutes. Rinse with cool water
