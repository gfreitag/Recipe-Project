Title: RASPBERRY SQUARES - LM-B
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Margarine
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
12 oz Jar preserves (raspberry or
- other)
2 c  + 1 Tb flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Coconut
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Cream butter and sugar.  Add egg and vanilla; sift flour and baking
powder and salt; add to creamed margarine batter, then add coconut
and nuts. Batter will be very thick. Grease and flour a 9x9 inch pan.
Put about 2/3 of batter in the pan, spread jam over it; sprinkle
remaining batter on top. Bake at 350F for 35-45 minutes. From Linda
Martin-Bronson

: Concord Hospital Admitting Cookbook, Concord, NH
