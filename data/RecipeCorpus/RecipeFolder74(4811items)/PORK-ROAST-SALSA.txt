Title: PORK ROAST SALSA
Categories: Relishes, Sauces
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
-JUDI M. PHELPS
1 sm Can pineapple rings; drained
2 tb Red bell pepper; diced
3    Green onions; sliced fine
-lengthwise
1/4 ts Ground ginger
1 oz Lemon juice
1 oz Tequila
1 tb Fresh mint leaves; chopped
Cut pineapple rings into 1/4-inch pieces.  Mix with all other
ingredients. Chill very well.  Serve with pork roast.

Judi's Notes: This tastes so much better with fresh pineapple. I
added a fresh peach to it and it turned out very well.
