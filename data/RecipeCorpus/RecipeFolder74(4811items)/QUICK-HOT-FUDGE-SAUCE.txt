Title: QUICK HOT FUDGE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa
1/2 c  (Plus 2T) evaporated milk,
(5-ounce can)
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1 ts Vanilla extract
In a small saucepan, stir together sugar and cocoa; blend in
evaporated milk and corn syrup. Cook over medium heat, stirring
constantly, until mixture boils; boil and stir 1 minute. Remove from
heat; stir in butter and vanilla. Serve warm. Makes 1 3/4 cups of
sauce.

Source: Treasury of Desserts, page 301
