Title: REFRIED BEANS 2
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Lard Or Vegetable Oil
2 c  Cooked Pinto Beans; *
2 tb Chile Powder
1 tb Cumin; Ground
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
*  See Recipe # 4 this file. Heat the lard in a 10-inch skillet over
medium heat until hot.  Add the pinto beans and cook for 5 minutes,
stirring occasionally.  Mash the beans then stir in the remaining
ingredients. Add oil to the skillet if necessary then cook and stir
until a smooth paste forms, about 4 minutes. Garnish with shredded
cheese, if desired and serve,
