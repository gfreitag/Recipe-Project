Title: PUMPKIN CAKE (M_C-TX)
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Pumpkin
2 ts Mapleine flavoring
4 ts Baking powder
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  White sugar
2    Egg yolks or 1 egg
1/2 c  Sour milk
1/4 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Chopped floured
Nuts
Cream sugar and shortening.  Add other ingredients in order given.
Bake 1/2 hour at 350  in pan 6 1/2" x 11". Good without frosting
served warm, with apple cider.
