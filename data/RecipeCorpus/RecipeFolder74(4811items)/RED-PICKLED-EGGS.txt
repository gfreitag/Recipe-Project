Title: RED PICKLED EGGS
Categories: Appetizers, Preserves, Relishes
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Hardcooked eggs
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 c  Beet liquid
1/3 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Onion -- chopped
4    Whole cloves
Place peeled eggs in bowl or jar.  Mix vinegar, beet liquid, sugar,
salt, onion and cloves.  Pour over eggs. Cover and refrigerate at
least 2 days. Slice eggs.

Source:  Betty Crocker's Cookbook
