Title: REGINA COGSWELL'S BLUEBERRY CREAM
Categories: Blueberries, Desserts, Recipes fro
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pt Cream -- sweetened lightly
3 c  To 4 c firm, dry blueberries
Cream puffs -- optional
Whip cream, sweetening lightly. Fold in as many dry blueberries as
cream will hold. Serve chilled in small dessert dishes or fill cream
puff shells.=
