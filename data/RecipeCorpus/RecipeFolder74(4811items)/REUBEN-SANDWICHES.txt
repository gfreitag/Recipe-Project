Title: REUBEN SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
Corn beef, boiled
Rye bread
Butter
Sauerkraut
Thousand island dressing
Swiss cheese
Slice and butter the bread.  Place beef on the bread. Duh! Then place
some well drained sauerkraut and thousand island dressing on it. Top
with a slice or two of Swiss cheese and broil or you can grill it.
Randy Rigg
