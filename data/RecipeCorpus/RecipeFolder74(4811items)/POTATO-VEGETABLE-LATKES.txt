Title: POTATO VEGETABLE LATKES
Categories: Vegetables, Microwave, Jewish
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Shredded potatoes
1 1/4 c  Shredded zucchini
1 c  Shredded carrots
1 md Onion, grated
3    Eggs, beaten
1/4 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Dried rosemary
Canola oil

Squeeze shredded vegetables dry.  Blend with remaining ingredients.
Fry in minimum amount of oil, 2 tbs per cake. Serve with sour cream
and/or applesauce.
