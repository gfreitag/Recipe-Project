Title: ROSS'S SPECIAL SEASONING
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Plus 1 teaspoon of chili
Powder
1/2 c  Plus 2 teaspoons salt or
Lite salt
1/4 c  Plus 1 teaspoon brown sugar
1/2 c  Less 1/2 teaspoon cumin
1 1/4 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Plus 1/8 teaspoon mace
2 1/2 ts Garlic powder
Mix all ingredients and use as a marinade for meats. Makes about 2
cups. Per serving: 0g fat, 180 cal,
3.9 mg sod, 0mg chol
