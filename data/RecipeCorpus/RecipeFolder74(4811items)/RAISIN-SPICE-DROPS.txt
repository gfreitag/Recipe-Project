Title: RAISIN SPICE DROPS
Categories: Cookies, Drop
Yield: 42 Cookies
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Shortening, soft
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1/2 ts Soda
1 1/2 c  Sifted all-purpose flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Allspice
2 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
--(quick or old-fashioned)
Pour enough boiling water over raisins to cover them. Cover and let
stand 10 minutes.  Beat shortening and sugar together until creamy.
Add eggs; beat until fluffy.

Drain raisins, reserving 1/2 cup liquid.  Dissolve soda in this
liquid. Sift together flour, salt and spices. Add to creamed mixture
alternately with raisin liquid; blend well. Stir in raisins and oats.

Drop by teaspoonfuls onto greased cooky sheets.  Bake in preheated
moderate oven (350 F.) 12 to 15 minutes.
