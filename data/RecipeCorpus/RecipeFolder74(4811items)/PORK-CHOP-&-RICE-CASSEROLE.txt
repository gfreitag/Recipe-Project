Title: PORK CHOP & RICE CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Pork, Rice
Yield: 8 Servings
8 ea Pork chops 3/4" thick
3/4 c  Rice
1/3 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 ea Salt and pepper to taste
2 c  Tomato juice
3/4 c  Water
1/4 ts Chili powder
Brown pork chops in a little oil in a skillet. Sprinkle rice in the
bottom of a greased 9" x 13" casserole dish. Scatter green pepper and
onion over the rice. Top with pork chops. Add salt and pepper. Pour
tomato juice and water into drippings in skillet. Add chili powder.
Bring to a boil, then pour over pork chops. Cover and bake for 1 hour
at 375 deg f. or until porks chops are cooked through and liquid is
absorbed. Serves 8.
