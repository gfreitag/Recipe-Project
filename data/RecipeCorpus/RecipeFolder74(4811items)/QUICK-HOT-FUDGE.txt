Title: QUICK HOT FUDGE
Categories: Chocolate, Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1 tb Sour cream
4 tb Sweet butter
1 ts Vanilla extract
Combine cocoa, sugar, corn syrup, milk, and sour cream in heavy
saucepan. Heat over medium flame, stirring constantly, until mixture
begins to boil. Immediately turn off heat; stir in butter and
vanilla. Serve warm. Makes enough for 4 good-size hot fudge sundaes.

From:  "Square Meals Cookbook", Courtesy Debbie Carlson - Cooking Echo
