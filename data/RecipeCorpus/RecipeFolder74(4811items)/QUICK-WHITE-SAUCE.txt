Title: QUICK WHITE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
1 c  Milk or half & half
MMMMM------------------EXTRAS FOR ADDED FLAVOR-----------------------
Nutmeg
2 tb Chopped parsley
2 tb Chopped chives
1 ts Lemon juice
In a one quart sauce pan, over low heat, melt butter. Add flour and
stir for 3 to 4 minutes or until well blended. Stir in milk slowly;
cook, stirring constantly until thickened and smooth. Add one or more
of the extras to alter the taste. Serve over vegetables or fish.
