Title: PURLOO
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
3 sl Bacon; chopped
1 md Onion; chopped
1 md Green pepper; chopped
1 c  Thinly sliced okra*
1    Garlic clove; minced
1 c  Cooked ham (about 3 oz.)
-- julienned
3 c  Cooked rice
1 md Tomato; seeded and chopped
1 ts Dried thyme
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dried basil
1/8 ts Red pepper flakes
Cook bacon over medium heat in large skillet until brown; drain fat.
Stir in onion, pepper, okra and garlic; saute 2 to 3 minutes or until
onion is tender. Add ham, continue cooking 3 minutes. Add rice,
tomato, thyme, salt, basil and pepper flakes.  Heat thoroughly; serve
as a side dish or a main dish.

*Thawed, frozen okra or fresh celery may be substituted for fresh
okra, if desired.

Each serving provides: * 353 calories * 12.5 g. protein * 5.2 g. fat
* 62.6 g. carbohydrate * 1.9 g. dietary fiber * 17 mg. cholesterol *
711 mg. sodium
