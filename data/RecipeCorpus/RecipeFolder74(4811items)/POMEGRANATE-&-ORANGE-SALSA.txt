Title: POMEGRANATE & ORANGE SALSA
Categories: Condiments, Fruits
Yield: 2 Cups
1 lg Pomegranate
2 lg Orange; peeled, sectioned
-& chopped
1    Jalapeño chile; deveined &
-minced
1 lg Tomato; peeled, seeded &
-diced
2 tb Scallion; thinly sliced
1 tb Lime juice
1 ts Cilantro, fresh; minced
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
Break the pomegranate apart to release the seeds. Discard membranes
and skin.  Drain seeds and pat dry on paper towels. Place in a medium
bowl. Add the oranges, jalapeño, tomato, scallions, lime juice,
coriander and cumin. Stir well.  Cover and chill at least two hours.
