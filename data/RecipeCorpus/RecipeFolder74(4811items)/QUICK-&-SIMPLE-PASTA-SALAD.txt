Title: QUICK & SIMPLE PASTA SALAD
Categories: Pasta, Low fat, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
250    Grammes uncooked pasta
1    Tin ratatouille
Cook pasta, rinse under cold tap until cold.  Mix with ratatouille.
Easier than anyone would believe possible!
