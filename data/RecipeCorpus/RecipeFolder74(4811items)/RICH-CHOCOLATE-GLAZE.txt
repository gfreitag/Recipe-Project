Title: RICH CHOCOLATE GLAZE
Categories: Cakes, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Confectioners sugar
3 tb Butter, softened
2 tb Hot tap water
1 oz Baking chocolate
1/2 ts Vanilla
In small mixer bowl, beat confectioners sugar, butter, water,
chocolate and vanilla until smooth. Pour drizzle over cake. Makes 3/4
cup glaze.
