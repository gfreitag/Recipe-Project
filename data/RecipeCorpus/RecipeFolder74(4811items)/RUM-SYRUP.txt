Title: RUM SYRUP
Categories: Cakes, Sauces, Sauces and
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
2 sl Lemon
1 sl Orange
1    Cinnamon stick
1    1-inch piece vanilla bean
1/2 c  Rum
Recipe by: Craig Claiborne - The New York Times Cook Book 1. Bring
all the ingredients except the rum to a boil, stiring
until the sugar has dissolved.  Simmer five minutes. 2. Strain
and add the rum.
