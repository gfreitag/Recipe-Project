Title: POLLO LOCO MARINADE
Categories: Marinades, Poultry, Bar-b-q, Mexican
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Lemon ; juiced
1    Orange; juiced
2 tb White vinegar
2    Worchestershire sauce
1/2 c  Chili sauce
2 ts Sugar
2 ts Garlic powder
Tabasco to taste
2 tb Oil
Just marinate chicken in this mixture for 4-24 hours. Grill 20
minutes on each side. Serve with flour tortillas and fresh salsa.
