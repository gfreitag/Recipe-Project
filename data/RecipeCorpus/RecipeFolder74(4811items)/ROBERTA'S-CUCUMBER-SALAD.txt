Title: ROBERTA'S CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Dupree, Salad
3    cucumbers
(2 peeled, 1 unpeeled, both
diced)
Yield: 4 Servings
:
:
1 1/2 c  plain yogurt
1/2 c  finely chopped fresh mint
1 TB lemon juice
Salt and pepper
:

Mix together the cucumbers, yogurt, mint, and lemon juice. Season to
taste with salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate until ready to
serve.
