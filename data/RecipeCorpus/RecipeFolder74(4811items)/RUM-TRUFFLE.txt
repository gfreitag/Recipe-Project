Title: RUM TRUFFLE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 oz Dark rum
1/4 oz Creme de cocoa
1/4 oz Hazelnut liqueur
Generous portion of
-chocolate ice cream
Whipped cream
Preparation : Put all ingredients, except whipped cream, in a
blender. Whip up. Serve in a brandy snifter with the whipped cream on
top.
