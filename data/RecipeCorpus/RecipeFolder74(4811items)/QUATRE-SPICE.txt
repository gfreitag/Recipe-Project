Title: QUATRE SPICE
Categories: Seasonings, Herb/spice
Yield: 1 Servings
5 tb Pepper -- freshly ground
2 tb Cayenne pepper -- ground
2 tb Ginger -- ground
1 tb Cloves -- ground
