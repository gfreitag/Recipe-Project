Title: QUEEN RANCH CHICKEN
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry, Weight watc
Yield: 4 Servings
4 oz Velvetta cheese -- cubed
12 oz Chicken
4 ts Lite margarine
1    Onion -- chopped
1 sm Can of green chilies --
Drained
14 oz Canned stewed tomatoes --
Chopped
4 ts Cornstarch
1 1/2 cn Chicken broth
4    Corn tortillas
Salt & pepper -- to taste
Garlic powder -- to taste

done.

2. Add cornstarch and 1 cup broth.  Cook 5 minutes.

4. In a casserole dish layer tortillas, cheese, and chicken mixture.
