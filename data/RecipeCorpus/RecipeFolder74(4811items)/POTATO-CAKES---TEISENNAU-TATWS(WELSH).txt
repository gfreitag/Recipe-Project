Title: POTATO CAKES - TEISENNAU TATWS(WELSH)
Categories: Brakefast
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Potatoes
4 oz Self-raising flour
1 oz Sugar
1 oz Butter
1/4 ts Cinnamon or mixed spice
1/4 pt Milk or 1 egg
Peel and boil the potatoes in salted water. Drain the potatoes and
mix in all the ingredients. Turn on to a floured board and shape into
rounds about 1 inch thick and cook in a fairly hot oven (400o F. or
Mark 6) for twenty minutes. Serve hot with bacon or top with fried
eggs.
