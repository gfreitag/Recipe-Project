Title: RED PEPPER DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 1 Servings
2 cl Peeled garlic
1/2    Jalapeno pepper seeded
About 1 tablespoon
1/3 c  Pecan pieces
1 c  Drained sweet roasted or
Pimento peppers
1/3 c  Virgin olive oil
1/4 ts Salt
Place garlic and jalapeno pepper in food processor or mill and puree.
Add pecans, pimento peppers, olive oil and salt and process until
mixture is smooth. Mix in Parmesan, if desired.
