Title: ROBB'S MINCEMEAT BARS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 24 Bars
3/4 c  Water
1    9-oz pkg condensed mincemeat
3/4 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/3 c  Molasses
1/4 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Rolled oats
Powder sugar (optional)
For filling, in a saucepan bring water to boiling. Add mincemeat;
reduce heat.  Cover and simmer for 3 minutes, stirring often. Cool.

In a large bowl beat shortening with an electric mixer on medium
speed for 30 seconds.  Gradually add granulated sugar, beating till
fluffy. Beat in molasses.  Add flour; beat till combined.  Use a
wooden spoon to stir in oats.

Press one/half of the oatmeal mixture onto the bottom of a greased
9x9x2-inch baking pan.  Spread mincemeat filling onto oatmeal
mixture. Drop remaining mixture by teaspoonsfuls onto filling. Bake
in a 350 degree oven for 35 minutes or till light brown.  Cool in the
pan on a wire rack. If desired, sprinkle with powdered sugar. Cut
into diamonds or bars. Makes 24 servings.
