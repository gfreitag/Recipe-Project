Title: PUREE OF GREEN BEAN SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 2 Servings
1 pt Green Beans, sliced trimmed
1 c  Onions, Chopped
2 tb Margarine, RC tub
1 c  Milk, low-fat 1%
1/4 c  Chicken Broth, low-sodium
1 ts Flour, all-purpose
6    Chives, trimmed garnish
1.  In 1-quart microwavable casserole combine first 3 ingredients;
micro- wave on High (100 %) for 2 minutes, until margarine is melted.
Add 1/2 cup water and microwave on High for 5 minutes, until beans
are tender. Let cool slightly.

2.  Transfer mixture to blender and process until smooth; add
remaining ingredients and process until combined. Return to casserole
and micro- wave on High for 1 minute, until mixture thickens slightly
and is thoroughly heated.

3.  Pour soup into 2 soup bowls and garnish each portion with 3
trimmed chives.

Approximate Total time:  20 Minutes

Each Serving provides:  1/2 Milk, 1/2 Fat, 3 Vegetables, 20 Op Cal

Per serving:  138 Calories; 7g protein; 4g fat; 21g carbo; 211 mg
calcium; 116 mg sodium; 5 mg cholesterol; 3g dietary fiber
