Title: PUFFY CHEESE SLICES
Categories: Sharon, Pizza, Breakfast
Yield: 8 Servings
5 ea SLICES OF BREAD
2 c  SHREDDED CHEESE
1 ea EGG, SLIGHTLY BEATEN
1/2 ts SALT
SET OVEN TO BROIL. BROIL BREAD UNTIL BROWN ON ONE SIDE. BLEND CHEESE,
EGG, AND SALT. SPREAD OVER UNTOASTED SIDE OF THE BREAD. BROIL UNTIL
CHEESE IS BUBBLES.
