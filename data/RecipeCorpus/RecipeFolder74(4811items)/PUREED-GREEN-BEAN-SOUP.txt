Title: PUREED GREEN BEAN SOUP
Categories: Soups, Cajun
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Green beans (#303) OR>>>
1 pk Frozen green beans, unthawed
1    Beef bouillon cube
Sour cream
1 sm Onion, sliced
2 c  Chicken broth
2 oz Cheddar cheese, grated
Salt & pepper to taste
Worcestershire to taste
Cook beans, onion and broth together, add bouillon cube. Puree in
blender while hot, season with salt, pepper and Worcestershire sauce.
Divide cheese in bottoms of 4 soup cups, add soup and garnish with
sour cream.
