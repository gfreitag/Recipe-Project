Title: QUICK BROCCOLI & RICE CASSEROLE
Categories: Rice, Side dishes
Yield: 2 Servings
10 oz Frozen broccoli with cheese
1/2 c  Quick cooking rice
1/2 c  Whole milk
1 c  Cooked chicken, ham or turke
2 tb Parmesan; grated
Recipe by: Joel Ehrlich <Joel.Ehrlich@SALATA.COM> NOTE: Cooking times
are based on the use of a 625-750 watt microwave oven a food
quantities for 2 servings. Adjust cooking times as required.

Follow package directions for frozen Broccoli w/Cheese Sauce.

Turn into a (1 Qt.) casserole. Add the chicken, ham or turkey. Add the
rice, milk and cheese. Blend thoroughly. Cover. Microwave on high
until the rice is tender (about 6 minutes), stirring once Serve
