Title: QUICK DILL PICKLES IN A CROCK
Categories: Canning
Yield: 1 Servings
2 qt Water
1 c  Salt
1 qt Cider vinegar
1 tb Dill seed
2    Garlic cloves
Peeled and sliced
Cucumbers
Mix all ingredients together and bring to boil. Pour over washed
cucumbers and store in jar or crock. Ready to eat in 2 days. PAUL
MONTGOMERIE   (NZQM86A)
