Title: PURELY PORK RUB
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Salt
2 tb Brown sugar, packed
2 tb White sugar
2 ts Paprika
2 ts Pepper
1 ts Cumin
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Cayenne
Combine ingredients in an airtight container or freezer bag. Shake
and toss to mix.

Source:  The Great Barbecue Companion, Mops, Sops, Sauces, and Rubs
By Bruce Bjorkman
