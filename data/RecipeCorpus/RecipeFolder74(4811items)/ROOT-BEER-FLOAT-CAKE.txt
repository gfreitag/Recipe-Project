Title: ROOT BEER FLOAT CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Birthdays, Kids
Yield: 16 Servings
1 pk Betty Crocker Super Moist
-white cake mix
1 1/4 c  Root beer
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2    Eggs
Root Beer Cream*
ROOT BEER CREAM:
1/2 pk (2.8 Oz.size) whipped
-topping mix (1 envelope)
1/2 c  Chilled root beer
Heat oven to 350Ã¸F.  Generously grease and flour rectangular pan,
13x9x2 inches. Beat cake mix (dry), root beer, oil and eggs in large
bowl on low speed 30 seconds. Beat on high speed 2 minutes, scraping
bowl occasionally. Pour into pan. Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until
toothpick inserted in center comes out clean; cool. Serve with Root
Beer Cream. Sprinkle with crushed root beer-flavored hard candies if
desired.

Root Beer Cream:  Beat topping mix (dry) and root beer in medium bowl
on high speed about 4 minutes or until thickened. Yield: 16 servings.
