Title: RED WINE REDUCTION
Categories: Cooking liv, Italian
1    Spanish onion -- coarsely
chopped
Yield: 1 Servings
:
1    carrot coarsely chopped
1    stalk celery -- coarsely
chopped
:
1    Granny Smith apple --
coarsely chopped
:
2    cloves garlic
2 TB grapeseed oil
1    750 milliliter bottle
Burgundy
:
2 c  Port
1 c  chicken stock

In a medium saucepan, caramelize the onion, carrot, celery, apple, and
garlic in the grapeseed oil. Add the Burgundy and the Port and simmer
over medium heat for 2 hours. Strain and place in a small saucepan
with the chicken stock. Continue to simmer over medium heat for 1
hour, or until reduced to 1/2 cup.

Yield: 1/2 cup
