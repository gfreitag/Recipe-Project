Title: PONDEROSA STEAK AGER
Categories: Marinades, Copycat
Yield: 3 Cups
1/2 c  Ketchup
2 c  Water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Dark vinegar
1/2 ts Smoked salt
1 ea Onion, large, Wedge cut
1 c  Corn oil
3 tb Soy sauce
2 tb French's prepared mustard
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Coarse ground black pepper
Combine everything.  Submerge steaks in it for 24 hours refrigerated
and covered.  Drain Well and broil as usual.
