Title: QUINOA SUMMER SALAD
Categories: Salads, Grains
Yield: 1 Recipe
4 c  Cooked AM Quinoa
1/2 c  Shelled pecans
1/2 c  Chopped green onion
1/2 c  Sliced black olives
3/4 c  Sliced mushrooms
3/4 c  Raisins
-- plumped in hot water
-- and drained
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Tamari
1/3 c  AM Unrefined Olive Oil
1/4 ts Pepper
Mix first six ingredients together in a large bowl. In a separate
container mix the last four ingredients. Pour the liquids over the
salad and toss gently.  For the best flavor let set in the
refrigerator for about one hour before serving.
