Title: QUICK SPAGHETTI SAUCE
Categories: Meats, Sauces, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 lb Ground Beef; Lean
24 oz Tomato Sauce; 3 cn
1/2 c  Water
2 oz Mushrooms;Sliced,Drained,1cn
2 tb Minced Onion Flakes
1 1/2 ts Brown Sugar; Packed
3/4 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Garlic Salt
1/8 ts Marjoram
Saute the ground beef in a skillet and drain off the excess fat. Add
the remaining ingredients and heat to boiling. Simmer for 10 minutes,
stirring occasionally.  Serve over hot cooked spaghetti or other
pasta.
