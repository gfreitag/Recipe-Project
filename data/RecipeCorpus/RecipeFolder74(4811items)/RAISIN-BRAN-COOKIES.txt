Title: RAISIN BRAN COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  KELLOGG'S Raisin Bran, crush
1 c  Whole wheat flour
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
3/4 c  Margarine, softened
2/3 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar (firmly packed)
2 ea Eggs
In large bowl, mix together raisin bran, flours, and soda; set aside.
In large mixing bowl, beat margarine and sugars until light and
fluffy. Add eggs; beat well.  Stir in cereal mixture, thoroughly
combining. Drop by scant 1/4 cup measure onto ungreased cookie sheet.
Bake in preheated, 350 degree F oven about 14 minutes or lightly
browned. Makes 18 cookies.
Nutrition Information Per Cookie: Calories 190, Dietary Fiber 2g,
Fat 9g.
