Title: PORTUGUESE SWEET BREAD (BREAD MACHINE)
Categories: Brd, Machine -, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs
2 tb Butter
1/3 c  Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
3 c  Bread flour
2 1/2 ts Yeast
All ingredients should be at room temperature.

Should you prefer this even sweeter, increase sugar to: 1/2 Cup
