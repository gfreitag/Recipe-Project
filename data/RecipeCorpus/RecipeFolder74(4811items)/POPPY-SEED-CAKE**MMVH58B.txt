Title: POPPY SEED CAKE**MMVH58B
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 9 Servings
1 pk Lemon cake mix
1 pk Vanilla pudding
4    Eggs
1/4 c  Oil
MMMMM---------------------MIX AND LET STAND--------------------------
1 c  Hot water
1/4 c  Poppy seeds
Mix all ingredients 4 minutes.  Grease and flour pan.
Fill pan halfway. Bake 350 degrees for 35 minutes.
