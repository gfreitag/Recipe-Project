Title: RAJMA
Categories: Vegetarian, Indian
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Kidney beans
1 pn Turmeric
1/4 c  Oil
1    In Ginger
3    Tomatoes
3 qt ;Water
1 ts Salt
1    Onion
1 ts Garam masala
1 sm Cilantro, bunch
Wash beans and boil for 2 - 3 hours or 1/2 hour in pressure cooker.
In the meantime make masala of onions, garlic, ginger and tomato as
in ? curry. Add to the beans and cook again till most of the liquid
dries up and the beans are soft and thoroughly cooked. Garnish with
coriander leaves and serve.
