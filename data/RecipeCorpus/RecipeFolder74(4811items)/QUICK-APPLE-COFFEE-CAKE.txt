Title: QUICK APPLE COFFEE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Breads, Fruits, Microwave
Yield: 8 Servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
-at room temperature
1    Egg
1/2 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Flour, sifted
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Uncooked apple slices
1 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Sugar
1. In a large mixing bowl, blend together sugar, butter and egg. Beat
until thoroughly blended. 2. Sift flour, baking powder and salt
together. 3. Stir milk into sugar-butter-egg mixture. 4. Add dry
ingredients to milk mixture and stir until thoroughly blended. 5.
Spread batter into an 8-inch square, heat resistant, non- metallic
baking dish. 6. Arrange apple slices in a design on top, pressing
them slightly into the batter. 7. Combine cinnamon and sugar in a
small bowl and sprinkle on top of apple slices. 8. Heat, covered with
clear plastic wrap, 5 minutes in Microwave Oven. Uncover and heat an
additional 4 minutes or until apples are tender and a toothpick
inserted in center comes out clean. 9. Allow to stand
10    minutes before serving.
