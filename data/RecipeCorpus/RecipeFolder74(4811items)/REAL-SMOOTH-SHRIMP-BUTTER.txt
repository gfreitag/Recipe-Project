Title: REAL SMOOTH SHRIMP BUTTER
Categories: Shrimp, Seafood, Condiments
Yield: 1 2/3 cups
2 1/2 c  Water
3/4 lb Unpeeled medium-size fresh
- shrimp
1/2 c  Butter, softened
1 lg Hard-cooked egg, coarsely
- chopped
1 pk Cream cheese, softened,3-oz.
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped onion
1/4 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Garlic clove
Bring water to a boil; add shrimp, and cook 3 to 5 minutes or until
shrimp turn pink. Drain well; rinse with cold water. Chill, peel,
devein, and coarsely chop shrimp.

Position knife blade in food processor bowl; add shrimp, butter, and
remaining ingredients. Process until smooth, scraping down sides
occasionally.

Line a 2-cup mold or bowl with heavy-duty plastic wrap, allowing
plastic wrap to extend over edges at least 2 inches. Spoon shrimp
mixture into mold; cover and chill at least 4 hours.

Invert mold onto serving plate. Remove mold, and carefully peel away
plastic wrap. Smooth surface with a knife, if necessary. Let stad at
room temperature 20 to 30 minutes before serving. Serve with assorted
crackers.
