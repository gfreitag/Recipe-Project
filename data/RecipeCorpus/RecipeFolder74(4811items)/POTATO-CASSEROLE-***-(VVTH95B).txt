Title: POTATO CASSEROLE *** (VVTH95B)
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Casserole
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Potato; frozen hash browns
1/2 c  Margarine; melted
1 ts Salt
2 tb Onion ;minced
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Cream of chicken soup
1 pn Sour cream
2 c  American cheese
Thaw potatoes before mixing.  Mix all ingredients and put into
13x9-inch greased baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
Hope that you like these.
