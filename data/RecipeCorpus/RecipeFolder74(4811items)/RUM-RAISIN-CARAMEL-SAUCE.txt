Title: RUM RAISIN CARAMEL SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 2 Cups
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
2 tb Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Whipping cream
1/2 c  Raisins
1/8 ts Rum extract
Directions; Melt butter i medium saucepan. Stir in brown sugar and
syrup. Bring to a boil. Cook until sugar is dissolved. Stir
constantly. Stir in whipping cream; return to boil. Remove from heat.
Stir in raisins and rum extract.  Serve warm over ice cream or
dessert. Store in refrigerator.

This sauce makes a wonderful gifts.  Store in half-pint jars and
include a "Store in refrigerator" reminder on the label.
