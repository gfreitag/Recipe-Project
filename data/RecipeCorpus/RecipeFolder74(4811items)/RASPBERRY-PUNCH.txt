Title: RASPBERRY PUNCH
Categories: Christmas, Drinks and, Holiday
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 ga Raspberry sherbet
1    46 oz. can hawaiian punch
1    2 liter 7-up  (can use diet)
Recipe by: Melinda Carmain  <m.carmain1@genie.geis.com> Scoop sherbet
into punch bowl.  Add Hawaiian Punch. Just before serving, add 7-Up.
Stir gently.  The sherbet will dissolve slowly and keep the punch
cold.
