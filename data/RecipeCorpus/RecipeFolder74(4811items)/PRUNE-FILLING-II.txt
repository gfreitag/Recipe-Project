Title: PRUNE FILLING II
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Thick prune pulp
1/3 c  Sugar
Few grains salt
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Marshmallow cream
Combine ingredients.  Cook over hot water, stirring constantly, until
well blended.  Beat until light and fluffy.
