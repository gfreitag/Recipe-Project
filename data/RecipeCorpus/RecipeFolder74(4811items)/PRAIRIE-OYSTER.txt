Title: PRAIRIE OYSTER
Categories: Cocktails
Yield: 1 Servings
1 fl Brandy
1 ts Catsup
1 pn Coarse pepper
1 pn Cayenne pepper
1/2 fl Worcestershire sauce
1/2 fl Vinegar
1 ea Egg yolk
Shake all except egg yolk over ice and strain into old fashoned glass
over two ice cubes. Carefully add yolk without breaking. Should be
gulped in one shot!
