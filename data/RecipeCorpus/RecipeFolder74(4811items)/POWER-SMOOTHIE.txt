Title: POWER SMOOTHIE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ct Plain low-fat yogurt (8 oz)
1/2    Banana
1/2 c  Strawberries and/or peaches
1/2 c  Juice (orange, cranberry,
-apple, etc.)
Blend all ingredients in a food processor or blender until smooth.

Nutritional Analysis: Calories, 284; Protein, 13 grams; Fat, 4 grams;
Carbohydrate, 49 grams; Sodium, 163 milligrams; Cholesterol, 13
milligrams.
