Title: POLYNESIAN FILLETS
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Trout fillets
1 tb Onion; finely chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Catsup
1 tb Melted butter; or oil
Pepper
1 tb Celery; finely chopped
Sprinkle fillets with salt.  Combine remaining ingredients and mix
thoroughly.

Skewer the fillets and place on a rack over coals. Baste fillets with
sauce.  Cover.  Broil several inches from the coals. Cook for 8-12
minutes on each side, or until fish flakes easily when tested with a
fork.

Serves 8.

Approximately 230 calories per serving.

(Adapted from a recipe in "Seafood Recipes for Calorie Counters",
Fisheries and Marine Service, Government of Canada)
