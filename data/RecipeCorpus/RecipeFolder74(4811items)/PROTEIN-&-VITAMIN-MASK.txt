Title: PROTEIN & VITAMIN MASK
Categories: Holiday, Gifts, Personal
Yield: 1 Mask
1/4 c  Barley or oat flour
1 ts Brewer's yeast
2 tb Honey
1    Vitamin E capsule
1    Egg yolk (optional)
2 tb Almond oil
1/4-1/2 c. buttermilk or
-yogurt
Mix ingredients together to form a smooth paste. Apply mixture to
clean face, throat and neck.  Lie down and relax for 15-20 minutes.
Wash off gently with warm water and apply moisturizer.
