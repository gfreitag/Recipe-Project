Title: ROASTED OLIVES
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Black and green olives
Zest from 1 small orange
Leaves from 1 large sprig
-rosemary
1 1/2 tb Extravirgin olive oil
Preheat oven to 250 degrees. Mix all ingredients in an ovenproof bowl.
Place in oven until warmed through, stirring occasionally, about 15
minutes. Yield: 2 cups Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #
