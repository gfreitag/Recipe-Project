Title: RUM RAISIN ICE CREAM^
Categories: Crs, Healthy, Lowfat, Lowchol
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Very ripe banana
1 ts Lowfat yogurt
1/4 ts Rum extract
1 tb Raisins
Peel banana, wrap in plastic and freeze 24 hours. Thaw slightly,
combine in blender with remaining ingredients except raisins. Blend
until smoothl. Stir in raisins. Serve.

Per serving: Calories: 122 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 31g Fat: trace
Sodium: 8mg Cholesterol: trace
