Title: PUMPED UP POPCORN
Categories: Snacks, Popcorn
Yield: 10 Servings
MMMMM-------------------PHYLLIS SMITH  WCDT64B------------------------
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
8    Med. cloves garlic; minced
2 1/4 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper; (scant)
4 oz Bag plain popcorn
Kosher salt
Place oil, smashed, peeled and minced garlic, chili powder and
cayenne in a small saucepan over very low heat. Cook, stirring
occasionally, for five minutes.
Place popcorn in a large bowl. Pour the spice mixture over and toss
to coat. Hands work best for this. Season with salt to taste and
serve. (Compliments of My Little Black Box)
