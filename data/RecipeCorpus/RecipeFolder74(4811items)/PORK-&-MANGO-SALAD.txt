Title: PORK & MANGO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Main dish, Vegetables, Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lg Mango
1/2 c  Yogurt-plain-non fat
1 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Ground ginger
2 c  Cooked pork, lean julienne
4 c  Chopped lettuce
1 c  Orange section
1/2    Avocado, peeled, sliced
Cut mango in half and peel.  Mashed enough mango to measure 1/4 cup.
Cut remaining mango into thin slices Mix mashed mango, yogurt, sugar
and ginger.  Set aside. Place a handful of lettuce on each of four
plate. Arrange mango,pork, orange and avocado atop lettuce. Top the
pork salad with the yogurt mango mixture.
