Title: POLLO LOCO MARINADE. *** NGVP22A
Categories: Marinades, Chicken, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Lemon ; juiced
1 ea Orange; juiced
2 tb White vinegar
2 ea Worchestershire sauce
1/2 c  Chili sauce
2 ts Sugar
2 ts Garlic powder
1 x  Tabasco ; to taste (i use a
2 tb Oil
Just marinate chx. in this mixture for 4 to 24 hrs., grill 20 min. on
each side.  Serve with flour tortillas and fresh salsa. Hope you like
it! FROM:
LESLIE BACKEN   (NGVP22A)
