Title: APPLE CRANBERRY DESSERT-USING MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Bake it all mix
2/3 c  Milk
3/4 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Orange juice
3 c  Cooking apples -- sliced*
1 lb Can whole cranberry sauce**
*3 Medium, pared and cored. **About 1-2/3 cups Combine sugar,
cinnamon and salt in an 8 by 8 by 2-inch baking dish. Add orange
juice, sliced apples and cranberry sauce. Mix lightly but thoroughly.
Bake in a 425 F. oven 15 minutes.  Make a well in the Mix. Add milk
and stir with a fork until all of Mix is moistened. Drop dough by
oven for
spoonfuls on hot apple-cranberry mixture. Return to hot
20 to 25 minutes. Serve warm. Yield: 6 servings
