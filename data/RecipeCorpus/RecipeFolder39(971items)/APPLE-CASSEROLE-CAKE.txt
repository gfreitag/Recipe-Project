Title: APPLE CASSEROLE CAKE
Categories: Eggs, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
Hot fruit (apples, cherries)
MMMMM-----------------POUR INTO A CASSEROLE DISH----------------------
2 tb Sugar
2    Egg whites
4 tb Boiling water
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
Add ingredients in order, mixing as you go. Pour over fruit and bake
at 350 for about 30 minutes.

This is surprisingly good. Ginger is an unusual spice in apple
things, but it works nicely.
