Title: ANASAZI BEAN SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Dips
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  Cooked AM Anasazi Beans
1/2 md Onion
1    Celery stalk
1/4 c  Tofu, drained
1 tb AM Unrefined Olive Oil
1/2 ts Vege-sal
-OR- similar seasoning
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Basil
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Blend together in blender or food processor until smooth. Great for
dips or sandwich spread.
