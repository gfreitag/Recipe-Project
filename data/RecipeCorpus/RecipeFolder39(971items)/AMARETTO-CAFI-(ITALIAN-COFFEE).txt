Title: AMARETTO CAFI (ITALIAN COFFEE)
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Black coffee
1 oz Amaretto
Whipped topping
1    Maraschino cherry
Recipe by: Joe Robertson Preparation Time: 0:05 Fill coffee mug or
cup with hot coffee. Stir in amaretto. Top with pressurized dessert
topping and cherry. Serve with teaspoon.
