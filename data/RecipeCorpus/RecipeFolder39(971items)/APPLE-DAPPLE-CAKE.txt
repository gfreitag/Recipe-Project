Title: APPLE DAPPLE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Oil
2 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
3 c  Flour
1 ts Soda
2 ts Salt
2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Apples
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Pecans
Topping:
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Milk
Heat oven to 325 degrees.  Grease a tube pan.  Mix oil & sugar in lg
mixing bowl.  Add eggs, flour, soda, vanilla, & salt. Beat with
electric mixer until well blended & mixture is stiff. Stir in apples,
coconuts, & pecans. Spoon into cake pan, bake for 1 1/2 hrs.

Topping:

Mix all ingredients well.  Bring to a boil for 2 min. Pour over hot
cake.
