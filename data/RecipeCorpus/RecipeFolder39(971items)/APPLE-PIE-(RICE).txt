Title: APPLE PIE (RICE)
Categories: Pies, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Sugar.
2 tb All-purpose flour.
1 ts Cinnamon.
1/8 ts Nutmeg.
1/8 ts Salt.
-Juice of 1 lemon.
7 md Tart cooking
-apples,pared,cored and
-thinly sliced.
3 tb Butter or margarine.
-Kitchenaid pie pastry for
-double crust 9-inch pie.
INSTRUCTIONS:Combine sugar,flour,cinnamon,nutmeg,lemon juice and
salt;mix with apples.

Divide pastry in half.Roll to 1/8 inch thickness and line a 9-inchpie
plate.Fill with apple mixture and dot with butter.Roll outremaining
crust and place on pie.Cut slits for steam to escapeand seal
edges.Sprinkle with sugar lightly.

Bake at 400^F for 50 minutes. YIELD:One 9-inch pie.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
