Title: APPLE CRUMB SQUARES
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Oats -- Uncooked
1 1/2 c  All-Purpose Flour
3/4 c  Margarine, -- Melted
1 c  Brwon Sugar -- Frimly
Packed
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt -- (optional)
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/4 ts Ground Nutmeg
1 c  Appleaice
1/2 c  Chopped Nuts
Heat oven to 375* F. Grease 13x9-inch baking pan. Combine all
ingredients EXCEPT applesauce and nuts; mix until crumbly. Reserve 1
cup mixture. Press remaining mixture onto bottom of prepared pan.
Bake 15 minutes; cool. Spread applesauce over partially baked crust;
sprinkle with nuts. Top with resevered 1 cup oat mixture. Bake 13 to
15 minutes or until golden brown. Cool in pan on wire rack; cut into
2-inch squares
