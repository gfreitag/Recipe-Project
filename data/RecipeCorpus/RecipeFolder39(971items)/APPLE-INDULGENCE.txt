Title: APPLE INDULGENCE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Dark brown sugar
1/2 c  Honey
1 ea Egg
4 ea Apples, medium, chopped
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
3/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Salt
Cream butter with sugar.  Add honey and egg; beat well. Stir in
apples. Add dry ingredients and mix well. Pour into oiled 8-inch
square pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes. Serve with Caramel
Sauce. Serves 4
