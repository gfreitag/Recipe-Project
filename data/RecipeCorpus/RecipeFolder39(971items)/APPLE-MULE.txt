Title: APPLE MULE
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Jack Daniels
1 oz Southern Comfort
1 oz Amaretto
1 oz Triple Sec
1 1/2 oz Lime juice
2 oz Orange juice
Mix in shaker with ice. Serve as shots. Appropriately named because
they taste similar to a fresh apple, but after a few they will kick
you in the butt.

Recipe by: Bryan and Melissa Townley
