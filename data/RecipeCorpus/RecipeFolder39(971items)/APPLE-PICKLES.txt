Title: APPLE PICKLES
Categories: Pickles
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Pared and quartered apples
1 c  Mild vinegar
1/8 ts Cloves
1    Stick cinnamon
2 c  Sugar
Combine sugar, vinegar, and spices.  Boil 5 minutes. Add fruit and
cook until tender.  Pack in sterilized jars. Fill to within 1/4 inch
of top with sirup. Other fruits such as peaches, pears, and
crab-apples may be substituted for apples. Mrs. G.E. Mayo, Sherburn,
MN.
