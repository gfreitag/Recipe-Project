Title: APPLE WAFFLES
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
3    Whole eggs
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Melted butter or margerine
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1    TBSLP baking powder
2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1    Tart apple, peeled and
-shredded
beat eggs until thick. Beat in milk, butter, and vanilla. Sift
together dry ingredients.  Sift into egg mixture & beat well. Add
grated apple and stir until evenly blended.  Bake per your waffle
iron's directions.
