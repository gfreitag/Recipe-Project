Title: APPLE FACE MASK (TLC)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
150 g  Apple flesh
20 g  Lemon Juice
10 g  Almond Oil
10 g  Wheatgerm Oil
10 g  Egg White
20 dr Essential oil of Marigold
1. Peel and core the apple. Remove any discoloured parts and dice. 2.
Place in a blender add all the other ingredients and blend for a
minute at low speed and a minute at high speed.

Depending on the amount of juice in the apple, the cream may be too
wet or too dry. If so, add a teaspoon of rice flour or more lemon
juice and blend for another minute.
