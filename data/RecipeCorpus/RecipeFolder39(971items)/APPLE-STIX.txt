Title: APPLE STIX
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Sugar-free gelatin; any
-flavor (4 serving size box)
3/4 c  -water; boiling
1/2 c  Apple juice (see note)
Ice cubes (see note)
1 1/2 c  Apple; cut in matchstick
-size pieces
Note: Combine apple juice and enough ice cubes to make
1 1/4    cups.

Prepare as normal using the 'quick set' directions. Use apple juice in
place of cold water. Stir in 1 1/2 cups apple cut in matchstick size
pieces, chill. ---
