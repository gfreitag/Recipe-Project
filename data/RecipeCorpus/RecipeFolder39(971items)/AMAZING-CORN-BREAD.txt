Title: AMAZING CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Cornmeal
1/2 c  Flour {I used whole wheat}
3 tb Baking powder
3 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt (optional)
1    Apple made into
-applesauce or
1/3 c  Unsweetened applesauce
3/4 c  Skim milk {I used soy milk}
2    Egg whites {I used egg
-replacer equivalent}
Preheat oven to 425 degrees.  Grease a pan (Chris used a medium
vision pot sprayed lightly with Pam   - I used a 9" square glass
pyrex pan) and put in oven to preheat. Mix all ingredients, put
batter into heated pan and bake for 20 mins.
