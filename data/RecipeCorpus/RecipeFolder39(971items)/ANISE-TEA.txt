Title: ANISE TEA
Categories: Beverages, Christmas
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Dried anise leaves or
2 tsp. fresh leaves
1 c  Boiling water
1 ts Honey
1    Lemon slice (optional)
Place anise leaves in cup and pour boiling water over them. Let steep
5 - 8 minutes.  Stir in honey and serve with lemon slice. Source:
Quick & Easy Cooking.ch
