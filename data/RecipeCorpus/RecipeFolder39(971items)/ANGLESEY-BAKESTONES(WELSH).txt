Title: ANGLESEY BAKESTONES(WELSH)
Categories: Cakes, Bread, Welsh
Yield: 6 Cakes
1/2 lb Flour
4 oz Castor sugar
1    Egg
4 oz Lard
1 ts Baking powder
Pinch salt
SLAPAN SIR FON Rub the fat into the flour and add the other dry
ingredients. Beat the egg lightly and add with enough milk to make a
soft rolling consistency. Bake spoons- ful of the mixture on a
greased griddle.
