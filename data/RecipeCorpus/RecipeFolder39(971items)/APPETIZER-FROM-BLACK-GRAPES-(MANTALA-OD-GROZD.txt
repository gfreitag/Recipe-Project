Title: APPETIZER FROM BLACK GRAPES (MANTALA OD GROZD
Categories: Fruits, Appetizers, Snacks
Yield: 18 Pieces
Grapes (for 1 gallon juice)
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Lemon juice
Crush grapes until you have a gallon of juice; strain and cook boiling
until 1/2 liquid is left.  Add slowly 1 1/2 (cups? -KM) whole wheat
flour, stirring constantly over low flame. Add 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
and 2 cups sugar before flour.  Add lemon juice.  Pour into 9 x
13-1/3 x 2-inch deep pan.  Let cool in pan 2 days. Slice in 2x3 inch
squares. Serve with drinks.
