Title: AMMONITE CHOWDER
Categories: Cambrian, Critters, Extinct, Seafood, Weird
Yield: 1 Batch
6 1/2 c  Canned tomatoes; peeled
3 md Onions; diced
6 ea Carrots; peeled, sliced
4 md Potatoes; peeled, sliced
3 ea Jalapeno peppers; peeled,
-deveined, sliced
2 tb Beef bouillon
5 lb Ammonites*
1 c  Butter; melted
3 1/4 c  Canned corn
1 ts Salt; or to taste
13 c  Water
1 ds Tabasco; or to taste
*If ammonites are unavailable or extinct in your area, squid or conch
will make an excellent substitute.

In a large pot, break up the tomatoes and add the onion, carrots,
potatoes, jalapeno peppers and beef bouillon. Cook until tender.
