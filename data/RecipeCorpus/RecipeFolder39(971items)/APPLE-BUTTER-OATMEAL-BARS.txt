Title: APPLE BUTTER-OATMEAL BARS
Categories: Bar cookies, Cookies, Travel, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Butter Or Margarine;Softened
1/2 c  Brown Sugar; Packed
1/2 c  Apple Butter
1 ea Egg; Large
2/3 c  Flour; Unbleached
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Oats; Quick Cooking Rolled
1 c  Coconut; Flaked
Cream butter and sugar; blend in apple butter and egg. Stir flour with
soda, baking powder and salt; stir into creamed mixture. Stir in oats
and coconut. Spread in greased 13 X 9 X 2-inch baking pan. Bake at
350 degrees F. about 20 minuts.  If desired, sift powdered sugar over
top while still warm; cool.  Cut into bars.

Makes 36 bars.
