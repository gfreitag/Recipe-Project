Title: APPLE MASH
Categories: Fruits, Irish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Apple, cooking
2 lb Potatoes
1 tb Sugar
2 oz Butter
Peel potatoes.  Cook in salted, boiling water. Meanwhile peel, core,
and slice apples.  Place in a pot with a tablespoon of water, and the
sugar. Cook until soft. When the potatoes are cooked, drain and mash
thoroughly. Beat in the apples and butter.  This mash goes partic-
ularly well with bacon, or fried herring.
