Title: APPLE DUMPLING DESSERT
Categories: Desserts, Fruits, Misc
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Water
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 c  Margarine
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
3/4 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Milk
5    Peeled and grated apples
Sugar
Cinnamon
Combine sugar, water, and cinnamon in med. saucepan and bring to a
boil. Remove from heat. Add margarine adn set aside.

Sift together flour, baking powder, salt and then mix with shortening
and milk.  Roll dough onto floured surface about 1/4 inch thick.
Spread apples over surface of dough. Sprinkle with cinnamon and
sugar. Roll up. Slice into 12 1 inch rolls. Put in 9 x 13 baking pan.
Pour syrup over. Bake 45 min. at 350F
