Title: APPLE & RAISIN NOODLE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 lb Egg noodles
1/2 c  Raisins
6    Eggs; slightly beaten
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 ts Cinnamon
3    Apples; peeled, cored
- & chopped
Preheat  oven to 375F.  Cook noodles,  drain in colander & rinse well
with cold water.  Soak raisins in cup of boiling water to soften. Let
stand 10 min, then drain. Combine ingredients with noodles, mix well
& pour into buttered casserole dish.  Bake for 45 min or till browned
on top. Serve with yogurt or sour cream. SERVES:4
