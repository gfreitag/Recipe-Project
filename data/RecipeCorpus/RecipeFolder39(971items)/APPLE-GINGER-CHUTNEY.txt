Title: APPLE-GINGER CHUTNEY
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 Servings
3 lb Tart apples
-peel/core/finely chop
2 c  Brown sugar; packed
2 1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1 1/2 tb Salt
1 1/2 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Ground allspice
1 ts Ground cloves
1    Green peppers; finely chop
1 md Onion; finely chop
1/2 c  Preserved ginger in syrup
-finely chop
1/2 c  Golden raisins
Grated peeled and juice
Of 1/2 lemon
In pan bring 1st seven ingredients to boil. Simmer 10 minutes. Add
rest of ingredients and cook for l hour. Ladle into prepared jars and
process. Give 1 month to mature.

Source: Judith Piper
