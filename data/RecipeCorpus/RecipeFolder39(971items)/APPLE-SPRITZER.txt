Title: APPLE SPRITZER
Categories: Beverages, Fruits, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Apple cider, preferably
-unfiltered
1/4 c  Club soda
Ice
Green apple slice
Lemon wedge
1. Mix apple cider and club soda together in 10-ounce old-fashioned
glass. Fill with ice. Garnish with green apple slice or lemon wedge.
