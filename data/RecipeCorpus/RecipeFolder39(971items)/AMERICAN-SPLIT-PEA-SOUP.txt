Title: AMERICAN SPLIT PEA SOUP
Categories: Soup
Yield: 4 Servings
8 c  Water, cold
1 ea Ham bones
2 c  Split peas
1 ea Chopped onion, medium
1 ea Carrot, chopped
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Bring water and ham bone to a boil. Add remaining ingredients and
simmer. Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #
