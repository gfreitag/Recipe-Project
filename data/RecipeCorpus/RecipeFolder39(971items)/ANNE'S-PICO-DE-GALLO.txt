Title: ANNE'S PICO DE GALLO
Categories: Mexican, Sauces, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
12 ea Green onions, chopped coarse
2 ea Med. whole tomatoes,
-chopped medium
1 ea Cilantro leaf, chopped fine
2 tb Lemon juice from concentrate
Combine all ingredients and refrigerate at least an hour. Best if
served over fajitas with sour cream and freshly grated cheddar. Use
the cilantro judiciously! It is easy to overpower the remaining
ingredients.
