Title: APPLE SORBET
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
5 ea Granny Smith apples, cored &
-- quartered
1 1/4 c  Unsweetened apple juice
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
1/8 ts Cinnamon
Juice of 1 lemon
Combine apples with 1/4 c apple juice in a non-corrosive pot. Bring
to a boil & simmer 5 minutes. Remove from the heat & let cool. Puree
the apples in a food processor or blender.

Combine the pureed apples, syrup, rest of juice, cinnamon & lemon
juice. Freeze following the instructions for making Lemon Sorbet.

VARIATION:  Calvados Currant Sorbet. Soak 1/4 c dried currants in 1/4
c calvados or applejack for 4 hours or overnight. Drain & add to the
sorbet mixture before freezing.
