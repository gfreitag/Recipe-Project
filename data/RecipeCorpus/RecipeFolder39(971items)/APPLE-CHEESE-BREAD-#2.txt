Title: APPLE CHEESE BREAD #2
Categories: Breads, Cheese, Apples
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
2/3 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1    Apple; peeled and chopped
1/2 c  Sharp Cheddar cheese; grated
1/3 c  Walnuts; chopped
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Baking Soda
1/2 ts Salt
Preheat oven to 350. Cream butter and sugar, beating until light.
Beat in eggs, one at a time. Stir in apples, cheese and nuts. In
separate bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
Gradually and gently stir into apple mixture. Pour into greased loaf
pan and bake 1 hour. Cool 10 minutes before serving. Converted by
MMCONV vers. 1.10
