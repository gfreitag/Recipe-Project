Title: BUTTERFINGER BARS
Categories: Cookies, Lauri
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  Oats
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
1 c  Butter or margarine
2/3 c  Peanut butter
2    Eggs
Mix and bake at 350F. for 15-20 minutes.  (two 9x13 pans or one cookie
sheet.   Melt 3 cups chocolate chips for frosting or use 2 cups
chocolate chips and
2/3    cup peanut butter.
