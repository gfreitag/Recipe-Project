Title: BROTH COOKED PASTA TOSSED WITH OLIVES & PARSL
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Pasta, your choice
1 qt Vegetable stock
3 tb Olive oil
25 ea Kalamata olives, pitted &
-- diced -=OR=- olive paste
1/4 c  Parsley, chopped
Cook the pasta in the boiling stock until *al dente*. Drain. Reserve
the stock for future use. Toss the hot, drained pasta with the
remaining ingredients & serve immediately.
