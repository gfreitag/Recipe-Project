Title: BULLSHOT
Categories: Cocktails
Yield: 1 Servings
2 fl Vodka
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Salt
4 fl Beef bullion
2 ds Tabasco sauce
1 ds Pepper
Mix and serve in old fashoned glass over ice. May also be served hot.
Heat beef bullion to near boiling. Mix in other ingredients and serve
in a mug.
