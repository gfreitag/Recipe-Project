Title: BUFFALO CHILI ONIONS
Categories: Vegetables, Barbecue
Yield: 6 Servings
-JUDI M. PHELPS
1/2 c  Cayenne pepper sauce
1/2 c  Butter or margarine melted;
-(1 stick) OR
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Chili sauce
1 tb Chili powder
4 lg Sweet onions; cut into
-1/2-inch thick slices
Whisk together Cayenne pepper sauce, butter (or margarine or olive
oil), chili sauce, and chili powder in medium bowl until blended;
brush on onion slices.

Place onions on grid.  Grill over medium-high coals 10 minutes or
until tender, turning and basting often with the chili mixture. Serve
warm. Makes 6 servings.
