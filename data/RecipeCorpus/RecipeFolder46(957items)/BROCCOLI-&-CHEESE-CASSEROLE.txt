Title: BROCCOLI & CHEESE CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Box stove top stuffing
2 pk Frozen broccoli but she said
-just one good size bunch
-of
-fresh broccoli that had
-been cut up real fine.
4    Eggs
1/4 lb Butter or margarine
1 1/2 c  Sharp cheddar cheese that is
-grated
Beat the eggs first then add it to the stuffing and add the broccoli
then the butter or margarine that is melted next mix in the cheddar
cheese. Save some the cheese for on top of the casserole. She then
said to put it in and oven heated to 350 degrees for 15 minutes. Then
it is ready to be served.

PS If you use the fresh broccoli please cook it first.
