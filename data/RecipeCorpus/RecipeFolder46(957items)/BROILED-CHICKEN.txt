Title: BROILED CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 lb Cut-up broiler-fryer
-chicken
2 tb Margarine or butter;
-melted
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Set oven control to broil. Brush chicken with 1 tablespoon margarine.
Place chicken, skin sides down, on rack in broiler pan. Place broiler
pan so top of chicken is 7 to 9 inches from heat. Broil 30 minutes.
Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Turn chicken; brush with I tablespoon
margarine. Broil 15 to 25 minutes longer or until done.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
