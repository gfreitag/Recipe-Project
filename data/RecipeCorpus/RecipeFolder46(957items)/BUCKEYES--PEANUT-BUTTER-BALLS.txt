Title: BUCKEYES  PEANUT BUTTER BALLS
Categories: Candy
Yield: 6 Dozen
3 c  Powdered sugar
1/2 c  Margarine, softened
16 oz Peanut butter
1/2    Bag chocolate chips
2/3    To 1 block household wax
Combine powdered sugar, margarine, and peanut butter in a bowl. Chill
Roll into balls, chill until hard. Putting them in the freezer for a
short while may help Melt chocolate and wax in a double boiler. Using
a toothpick dip balls into chocolate and cool on wax paper. Makes
about 6 dozen. These can be frozen.
