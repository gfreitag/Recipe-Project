Title: BUTTER TARTS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg
1/4 c  Corn syrup
2 tb Melted butter
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Raisins
Stir all ingredients to-gether but raisins in a bowl and mix well. add
raisins Fill tart shells and bake in the bottom third of your oven
for 12 minutes at 450 F.
