Title: BRUSCHETTA TURNOVERS
Categories: Vegetables, Cheese, Appetizers, Tex-mex
Yield: 16 Appetizers
3 md Tomatoes, seed and chop
1    Garlic clove, minced
1    Green onion, chopped
1 tb Dried basil leaves
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/3 c  Mozzarella cheese, shredded
4    Tortillas
1 tb Olive oil
Combine tomatoes, garlic, green onion, basil and salt. Sprinkle 1/3
cup cheese over one half of each tortilla. Top with about 1/3 cup
tomato mixture. Fold tortilla in half over top of filling. Seal edges
with toothpicks. Brush both sided with oil. Barbecue over medium
coals or broil under preheated broiler for 4 to 5 minutes on each
side or until golden brown and cheese melts. Cut each tortilla in
half for lunch entree or in 4 triangles each for appetizers. Makes 4
main course servings or 16 appetizers.
