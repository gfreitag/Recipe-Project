Title: BROCCOLI-CHEDDER STUFFED POTATO
Categories: Baked, Potatoes
Yield: 1 Servings
10 oz Idaho potatoes
1/2 c  Broccoli -- steamed and
Chopped
1 tb Nonfat sour cream
3/4 oz Lowfat cheddar cheese --
Shredded
In small bowl, combine potato flesh, broccoli and sour cream.
Sprinkle with cheese and bake and 375 for 15 minutes or until heated.

Per serving: 1V, 1P, 2B,10C 8fat, 7fiber
