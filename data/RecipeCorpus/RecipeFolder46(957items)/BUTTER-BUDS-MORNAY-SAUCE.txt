Title: BUTTER BUDS MORNAY SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Lowcal
Yield: 10 Servings
2 c  WHITE SAUCE (BUTTER BUDS)
1 c  CHEDDAR OR AMERICAN CHEESE
1 ts DRY MUSTARD
1 ts WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
TO BASIC WHITE SAUCE (BUTTER BUDS - RECIPE ABOVE) ADD CHEESE AND STIR
OVER LOW HEAT UNTIL CHEESE IS MELTED. ADD WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE AND
MUSTARD TO TASTE. NOTE: USE ON VEGETABLES, TOAST OR MACARONI
