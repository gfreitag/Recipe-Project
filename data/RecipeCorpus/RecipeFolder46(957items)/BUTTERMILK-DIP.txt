Title: BUTTERMILK DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Crocker
Yield: 1 Cup
1 c  Dairy sour cream
1/3 c  Instant dry buttermilk
1/2 ts Dried dill weed
1/4 ts Salt
1    Clove garlic, crushed
Mix all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. About 1 cup dip; 40
calories per tb.

Source: Betty Crocker's Cookbook, 6th Edition
