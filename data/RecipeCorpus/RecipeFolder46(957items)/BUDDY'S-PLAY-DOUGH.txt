Title: BUDDY'S PLAY DOUGH
Categories: Kids
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Salt
1 tb Oil
2 ts Cream of tartar
4 dr Food coloring
Mix all ingredients in a sauce pan, over low heat. cook until dough
pulls from sides of pan. Knead by hand until dough is smooth in
texture. Store in tightly closed container.
