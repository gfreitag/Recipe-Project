Title: BUTTERMILK DRESSING
Categories: Dressing
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Buttermilk
1/2 ts Garlic powder
2 1/4 tb Parsley flakes
1/2 ts Accent or MSG
2 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Onion powder
1 3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Combine all ingredients, mixing well.  Chill at least 4 hours.
Dressing will keep 2-3 weeks in 'fridge. Recipe can be halved,
doubled, etc. easily.
