Title: BUTTERCREAM FROSTING
Categories: Chocolate, Frosting
Yield: 1 Servings
6 tb Butter
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa
3 c  Powdered sugar
1/3 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
Cream butter in small mixing bowl; add cocoa and sugar alternately
with milk.  Beat until mixture is spraeding consistency. Blend in
vanilla.
