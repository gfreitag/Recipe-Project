Title: BUTTER ICING
Categories: Frosting, Book-insert, Moms
Yield: 1 Recipe
3 c  Confectioners' sugar, sifted
1/3 c  Butter or margarune, softend
MMMMM-------------------------VARIATIONS------------------------------
MMMMM--------------------------STANDARD-------------------------------
3 tb Cream
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
MMMMM---------------------------ORANGE--------------------------------
3 tb OrangeJuice
1 1/2 tb Orange rind, grated
MMMMM---------------------------MOCHA--------------------------------
3 tb Fresh strong coffe
MMMMM-------------------------PINEAPPLE------------------------------
1/2 c  Crushed pineapple, drained
Blend confectioners' sugar and butter. Stir in until smooth one of the
variations.
