Title: BUTTERMILK PECAN PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla
3    Eggs
3 tb Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Buttermilk
1    Pie shell, 9" unbaked
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
Preheat oven to 300F. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy,
adding 1/2 cup sugar at a time. Blend in vanilla. Add eggs, one at a
time. Combine flour and salt; add small amount at a time. Add
buttermilk. Sprinkle pecans in bottom of pie crust; pour custard mix
over the pecans and bake 1 hour 30 minutes at 300F. The top browns as
it cooks. Best served at room temperature.
