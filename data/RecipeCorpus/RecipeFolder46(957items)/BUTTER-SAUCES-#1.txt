Title: BUTTER SAUCES #1
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
3 cl Garlic minced
1    Ancho chile -- briefly
Toasted and ground in a
Spice grinder (approx.
4 ts)
2 tb Chopped fresh oregano
1 tb Gold tequila
3    Generous Tb chopped pecans
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 lb Unsalted butter (1 stick).
ANCHO-OREGANO-PECAN-BUTTER Contributed to the echo by: Janice Norman
To make each sauce, mix all the ingredients together well. Melt to
serve.
