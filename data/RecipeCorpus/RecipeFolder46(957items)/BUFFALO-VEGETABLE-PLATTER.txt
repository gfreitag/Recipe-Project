Title: BUFFALO VEGETABLE PLATTER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 25 Servings
12 1/2 lb Vegetables for deep frying
Mushrooms, green peppers,
Zucchini
1 1/2 c  Margarine -- melted
1 1/2 c  Durkee Hot Sauce
Deep Fry Vegetables crisp and brown. Combine melted margarine and
Durkee Hot Sauce.  Lightly Toss cooked veggies in margarine/hot sauce
mixture. Remove immediately so they don't become soggy. Drain on
paper towel. Serve immediately with bleu cheese or ranch dip.
