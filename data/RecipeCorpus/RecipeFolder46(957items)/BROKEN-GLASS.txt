Title: BROKEN GLASS
Categories: Candies, Tested
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Light corn syrup
1 c  Water
Mix all ingredients and stir over heat until sugar dissolves. Boil to
300F or hard crack on candy thermometer. Remove from heat and add 1/4
teaspoon oil flavoring (peppermint, wintergreen, cinnamon, etc.) and
desired food coloring.  Pour onto well-buttered cookie sheet. Mark
into pieces as soon as it starts to harden around the edge.  Powder
candy with powdered sugar and break into pieces when set.
