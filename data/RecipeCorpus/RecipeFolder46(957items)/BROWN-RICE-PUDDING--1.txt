Title: BROWN RICE PUDDING -1
Categories: Desserts, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
3    Eggs
2 c  Brown Rice -- cooked
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
1/3 c  Honey
1 tb Cinnamon
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Dates -- chopped
1/2 c  Raisins
Combine eggs, cooked rice, peanut butter, honey, and cinnamon; stir in
milk, raisins, dates. Turn into a 10x6x2 baking dish; place first
dish into a larger baking dish and add 1 inch water to the larger
pan. Bake 25 minutes at 350 stir mixture; bake another 25 minutes. or
until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean.

Good topped with whipped cream and peanuts.
