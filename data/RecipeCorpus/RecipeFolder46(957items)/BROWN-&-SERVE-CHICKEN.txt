Title: BROWN & SERVE CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Chicken tenders
1 tb Margarine, melted
1/4 c  Parsley, chopped
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Garlic salt
Place chicken in a baking dish. Combine suagr, margarine and garlic
salt. Bake at 375 for 30 minutes. After 15 minutes, turn chicken and
baste with sauce. Serve with rice.
