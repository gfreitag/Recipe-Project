Title: BROCCOLI TREES (DISNEY KIDS)
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 5 Servings
1/4 c  Light sour cream
1/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 ts Sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Chopped fresh basil
4    Carrots
3 c  Broccoli florets
Prepare a dip by combining the sour cream, mayonnaise, sugar, lemon
ice, and basil in a medium-size mixing bowl. To make the trees, cut
each carrot in half widthwise and then lengthwise into four pieces.20
Assemble on a plate by laying three carrot pieces side by side for a
trunk with the broccoli florets as the leaves. Spread dip under the
trunks for the forest floor.
