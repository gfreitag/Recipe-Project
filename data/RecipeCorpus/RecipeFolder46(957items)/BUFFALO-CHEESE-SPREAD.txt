Title: BUFFALO CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: Cheese, Spreads
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Durkee Hot Sauce -- plus 2 T
12 oz Sharp cheddar cheese --
Shredded
4 oz Blue cheese -- crumbled
8 oz Cream cheese -- softened
1/3 c  Margarine or butter --
Softened
Combine Hot sauce and other ingredients in food processor or mixer.
Beat until smooth.  Chill. Remove 20 minutes before serving. Yields 5
cups.
