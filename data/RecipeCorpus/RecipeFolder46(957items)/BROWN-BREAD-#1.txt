Title: BROWN BREAD #1
Categories: Breads, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Molasses
Sugar
1 ea Egg
1/2 ts Salt
1 pt Milk; sour
1 c  Flour; white
1 qt Graham flour
1 tb Baking soda
1 c  Raisins
Measure 1/2 - 3/4 c. molasses, filling remainder of cup with sugar.
Roll raisins in flour before adding to batter. Bake in very slow oven
1 1/2 hours.

Note: Very slow oven is 240 - 350 F.

Source: Fairview Grange, Morgan County, OH
