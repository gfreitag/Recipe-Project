Title: BROWN BEANS & RICE (M_C-TX)
Categories: Rice, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Cooked brown pinto beans
-plus 1/2 cup bean juice
2 c  Cooked rice
1 lb Hamburger, browned
-and drained
1 1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1/2 ts Salt
Optional:  onions and green
-peppers, sauted and cooked
-with meat
Mix all together.  Bake at 350  about 30 minutes or until bubbly, use
2 quart dish.  Serve hot with corn bread and a green salad.
