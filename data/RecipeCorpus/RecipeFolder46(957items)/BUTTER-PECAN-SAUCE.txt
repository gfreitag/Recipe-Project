Title: BUTTER PECAN SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 2 Cups
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Pecan pieces
1 c  Crystal brown sugar, packed
1/3 c  Heavy cream or 1 can (5 3/8
- oz) evaporated milk
1/4 c  Corn syrup
In heave saucepan, melt butter.  Add pecan.  Heat until pecans are
lightly toasted and butter is lightly browned. Stir in brown sugar,
cream and corn sytup; cook gently until sugar is completely
dissolved. Do not overcook. Serve warm or cold.

This sauce will become an all-time favorite.  Serve this on ice cream
or cake. Delicious.
