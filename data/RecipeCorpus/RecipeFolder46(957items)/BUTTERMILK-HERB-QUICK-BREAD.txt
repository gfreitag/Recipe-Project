Title: BUTTERMILK HERB QUICK BREAD
Categories: Info/tips
1 c  whole-wheat flour
Yield: 10 Servings
1/2 c  flour
1/2 c  cornmeal
2 ts baking powder
1/2 ts baking soda
1/2 ts salt
1 ts dried dill
1/2 ts dried oregano
1/2 ts dried basil
1/2 ts dried thyme
1/2 ts fennel seeds
1 1/4 c  buttermilk
1    egg -- beaten
2 TB honey
2 TB vegetable oil
1 TB sesame seeds

In a bowl, combine all of the dry ingredients except the sesame seeds.

In a smaller bowl, combine the buttermilk, egg, money, and oil. Stir
the wet mixture into the flour mixture and combine until just
blended. Spoon the batter into a foil or waxed paper-lined
8-by-4-inch loaf pan. Sprinkle the batter with sesame seeds.

Bake at 350F, preheated, for 45 to 50 minutes or until a tester
inserted into the center comes out clean. Turn out and cool on a
rack. Makes 10 slices. Recipe By
