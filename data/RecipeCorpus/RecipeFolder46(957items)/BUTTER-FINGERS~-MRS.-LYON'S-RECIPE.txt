Title: BUTTER FINGERS~ MRS. LYON'S RECIPE
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Butter
5 tb Sugar
1 tb Water
2 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Finely ground nuts
Cream butter and sugar, add flour gradually beating after each
addition. Add water, nuts & vanilla. Blend well and chill. Roll
pieces of dough in hands until slightly thicker than a pencil. Cook
until lightly browned then roll in white sugar.
