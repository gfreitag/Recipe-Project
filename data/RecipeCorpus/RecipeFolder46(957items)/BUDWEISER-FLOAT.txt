Title: BUDWEISER FLOAT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
12 oz Can Budweiser
6 oz Chocolate ice cream
Procedure:

Place chocolate ice cream in a 20oz glass.

Add Budweiser, taking care that it doesn't foam over the brim. Consume
through a straw.
