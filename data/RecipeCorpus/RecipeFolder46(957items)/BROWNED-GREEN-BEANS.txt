Title: BROWNED GREEN BEANS
Categories: Cyberealm, Kooknet, Vegetables, Low-fat
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Green beans
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Low-salt chicken broth
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 1/2 tb Fresh lemon juice
Trim ends from beans, and remove strings. Cut beans in half
lengthwise, slicing through the seam on each side of beans. Heat oil
in a large skillet over high heat. Add beans, and cook 5 minutes or
until lightly browned, stirring frequently. Reduce heat to medium and
gradually add broth, salt, and pepper; cook 2 minutes. Remove from
heat; stir in lemon juice. Serving size: 3/4 cup.
