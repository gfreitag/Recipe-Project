Title: BROCCOLI CORN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Frozen chopped broccoli
4    Eggs
1    Stick margarine, melted
1    Jiffy corn bread mix
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 c  Cheese, grated
Mix all ingredients together.  Bake in iron skillet at 350 degrees
for 30 minutes or until done. Randy Rigg
