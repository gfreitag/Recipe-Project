Title: BROWN SUGAR SLICES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Lard
2    Eggs
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cream of tartar
1 ts Vanilla
4 c  Flour
1 c  Chopped walnuts
SOURCE: The Budget  10/30/91

Cream lard and sugar, add vanilla and eggs and mix well. Sift together
flour, soda and cream of tartar and add to first mixture. Add nuts
then form dough into two rolls. Place in plastic wrap and chill
several hours. Cut in 1/2 inch slices and bake in 350 degree oven 10
- 12 minutes.

Sylvia (Cookie Lady) Palm Bay, Fl.
