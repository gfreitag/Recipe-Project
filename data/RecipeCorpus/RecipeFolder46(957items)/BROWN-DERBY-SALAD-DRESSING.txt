Title: BROWN DERBY SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces, Dressings
Yield: 6 Servings
1/3 c  RED WINE VINEGAR
1 tb LEMON JUICE
1 ts WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
1/2 ts SALT
1/2 ts SUGAR
1/2 ts DRY MUSTARD
1/2 ts PEPPER
1 ea CLOVE OF GARLIC
1/2 c  OLIVE OIL OR SALAD OIL
IN A SCREW TOP JAR, COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS EXCEPT THE OIL; COVER AND
SHAKE TO MIX. ADD OIL; COVER AND SHAKE TO MIX. CHILL THOROUGHLY;
SHAKE BEFORE USING
