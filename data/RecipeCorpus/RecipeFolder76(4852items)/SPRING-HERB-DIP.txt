Title: SPRING HERB DIP
Categories: Dips, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
-Karen Thackeray
1 c  Mayonnaise *
1/3 c  Scallions; sliced
1/3 c  Parsley sprigs
1/2    Clove garlic
1 ts Wine vinegar; or lemon juice
*You may use any kind of mayo (reduced calorie, fat free, or
regualr) In blender or food processor, blend all ingredients 30
seconds or until smooth. Cover; chill. Serve with cut-up vegetables.
Makes 1 cup.
