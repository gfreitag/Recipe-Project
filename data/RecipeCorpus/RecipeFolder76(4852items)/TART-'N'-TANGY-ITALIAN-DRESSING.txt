Title: TART 'N' TANGY ITALIAN DRESSING
Categories: Garlic, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Wine vinegar
1 ts Minced onion
1 ts Seasoned salt
3/4 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Chives
1/2 ts Parsley flakes
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Oregano
1/8 ts White pepper
ds Cayenne or red pepper
Combine all ingredients in jar; cover and shake vigorously. Chill 1
hour for flavors to blend.  Shake well before serving. Makes 1 1/2
cups.
