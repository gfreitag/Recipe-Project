Title: SPICY TOP COFFEECAKE
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 9 Servings
1    Quick coffee cake recipe
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- packed
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
Prepare quick coffeecake batter.  Pour into a 9 by 9inch pan, bottom
rubbed with shortening.  Combine remaining ingredients and sprinkle
over unbaked batter.  Bake in a 400 F. oven 22 to 25 minutes. Yield:
One 9-inch square cake
