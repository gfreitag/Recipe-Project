Title: SWEET POTATO CASSEROLE
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 8 Servings
3 c  Cooked mashed sweet potatoes
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs
1/4 c  Softened butter
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Chopped pecans
Mix first 6 ingredients to-gether. Put in 2 qt. casserole. Melt
butter or margarine in small saucepan, add brown sugar, flour and
nuts. Stir well & msprinkle on top of potatoes. Bake uncovered 30
min. in 350 oven. You may add 1/2 tsp. each of cinnamon and nutmeg to
first 6 ingredients. Hot or cold you will think you are eating
dessert. Delicious!
