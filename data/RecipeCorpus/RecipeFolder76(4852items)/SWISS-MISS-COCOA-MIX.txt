Title: SWISS MISS COCOA MIX
Categories: Beverages, Copycat, Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Box of nonfat dry milk
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Cocoa
Sift all ingredients together three times. Store this mixture in a
tightly sealed container in a cool place. When ready to use, you add
a couple of teaspoons to boiling water. Feel free to add the little
marshmallows too! Fomatted by jayne@idt.net
