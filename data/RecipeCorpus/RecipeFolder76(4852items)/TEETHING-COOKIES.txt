Title: TEETHING COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Beaten eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
2    To 2-1/2 cup flour
Beat eggs and add sugar, vanilla and flour and mix well. Roll dough
between 2 sheets of floured wax paper to 1/4 inch thickness. Cut
desired shapes and place on cookie sheet, lightly greased. Let stand
overnight 10-12 hours, then bake at 325 degrees for 20 minutes. These
are hard and dissolve, but don't break up.
