Title: SWEET POTATO PIE WITH BOURBON CREAM SAUCE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Sweet potatoes, drained
4 tb Margarine,melted
3 ea Eggs
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Grated nutmeg
3/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1 ea 9" pie shell, baked
1/4 c  Chopped pecans
Use a food processor or fork to mash sweet potatoes together with
melted margarine.Blend in eggs,sugar,cinnamon and nutmeg. Add milk and
vanilla.Pour mixture into baked pie shell.Microwave on 70% (medium
high) 7 minutes.
Sprinkle pecans over surface of pie.Rotating midway through
cooking,microwave on 70 % (medium high) 6 to 8 minutes or until
center no longer jiggles.Top with Bourbon Cream Sauce.
