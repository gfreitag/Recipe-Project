Title: SWEET & SOUR TURKEY WINGS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
4 ea Uncooked turkey wings
3 c  Chicken broth
1/2 c  Celery -- finely chopped
1 ts Salt
3 tb Cornstarch
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/3 c  Chili sauce
1/2 c  Green pepper -- cut into
Strips
13 1/2 oz Can pineapple chunks --
Drained
Separate wings at joints and discard tips.  In slow-cooking pot,
combine wings, broth, celery, and salt. Cover and cook on low for 4
to 6 hours or until tender. Drain, saving 1 1/2 cups broth for sauce.
In large skillet, combine cornstarch, brown sugar, 1 1/2 cups broth,
vinegar, soy sauce, and chili sauce.  Cook and stir until thick and
clear. Stir in green pepper and pineapple; simmer for 2 minutes.  Add
drained, cooked wings. Bring to simmer.  Serve over rice.
