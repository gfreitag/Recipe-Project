Title: SWEET & SOUR CHILI RIBS
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
4 lb Pork ribs, trimmed
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Vinegar
2 tb Worchestershire sauce
1 ts Chili powder
3/4 c  Water
1    Onion, diced
Spread ribs in bottom of pan, sprinkle with diced onion. Mix other
ingredients and pour over ribs. Bake at 250 for 3 hours. Big Bill
