Title: SPINACH-FETA-CHEESE DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Jaw
Yield: 2 Cups
2 c  Low fat plain yogurt
1 sm Clove garlic
2 tb Chopped fresh dill or 2
Teaspoons dried
1    Package frozen leaf spinach,
Thawed and squeezed out
4 oz Feta cheese, cut into cubes
1 ts Grated lemon rind
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Set coffee filter or double thickness of paper toweling in strainer
over small bowl. Spoon yogurt into filter. Refrigerate; drain for 2
hours. Chop the garlic and dill in a food processor. Add spinach,
feta, rind, salt and pepper. Whirl until cheese is finely grated,
scraping down side of bowl as needed. Add drained yogurt. Pulse with
on/off motion just until mixture is combined. Scrape into serving
bowl. (can be prepared a day ahead and refrigerated.)
