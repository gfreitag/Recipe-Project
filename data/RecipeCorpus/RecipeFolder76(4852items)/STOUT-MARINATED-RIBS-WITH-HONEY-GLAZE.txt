Title: STOUT MARINATED RIBS WITH HONEY GLAZE
Categories: Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM----------------------------RIBS---------------------------------
3 c  Guinness stout
1    Onion, large; sliced
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Sugar, brown
1/4 c  Sesame oil
3 tb Garlic; minced
4 lb Beef short ribs; cut crosswi
MMMMM---------------------------GLAZE--------------------------------
1 c  Honey
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Parsley, fresh; chopped
1 tb Garlic; minced
1 1/2 ts Pepper, black
FOR RIBS: Whisk first 6 ingredients in large bowl to blend. Place
ribs in glass baking dish. Pour marinade over. Cover and refrigerate
overnight.

FOR GLAZE: Whisk all ingredients in medium bowl to blend.

Prepare barbecue (medium heat). Remove ribs from marinade and place on
grill. Grill until cooked through, turning occasionally, about 10
minutes. Brush with glaze and cook 1 minute per side. Serve with
remainig glaze.
Bon Appetit, August 1992
