Title: SPICY BEAN & CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
11 oz Green Beans; Cooked And
-Chopped, About 2 Cups
1 md Cucumber; Peeled and Finely
-Chopped, About 1 Cup
1/2 md Red Onion; Finely Chopped,
-About 1/2 Cup
2 tb Fresh Cilantro; Chopped
2 tb Red Wine Or Cider Vinegar
2 ts Fresh Hot Green Chili;
-Chopped, OR
1/4 ts Cayenne Pepper; Crushed
1 ts Honey
1/2 ts Salt
Mix all of the ingredients together, blending well. Cover and
refrigerate for about 2 hours or until well chilled.

Nutritional Information Per Serving:

Calories:  30    Protein:  1 Gram    Carbohydrates:  6 Grams Fat: 0
Grams Cholesterol:  0 Milligrams  Sodium: 190 Milligrams Potassium:
110 Milligrams
