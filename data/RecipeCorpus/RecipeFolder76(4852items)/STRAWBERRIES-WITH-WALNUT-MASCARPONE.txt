Title: STRAWBERRIES WITH WALNUT MASCARPONE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 pt Strawberries, sliced or
-whole
1/2 c  Walnuts
1 ts Sugar
1/2 c  Mascarpone (Italian cream
-cheese)
1 tb Coffee liqueur
Process walnuts with sugar in food processor until mixture becomes a
paste. Scrape the workbowl and add mascarpone and coffee liqueur, and
process until smooth. Serve as sauce with berries. Source: Fifty Ways
to Cook Most Everything (Andrew Schloss) Posted by Linda Davis
