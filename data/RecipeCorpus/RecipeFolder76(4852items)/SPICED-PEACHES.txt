Title: SPICED PEACHES
Categories: Penndutch, Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
7 lb Peach
4 lb Brown sugar
1 qt Vinegar
1 ts Cloves, whole
1 tb Cinnamon, stick
1 ts Allspice, whole
Tie spices in a bag and add to the vinegar. Bring to a boil and stir
in the sugar. Scald and peel peaches leaving them whole. Drop
peaches, a few at a time, into spiced liquid and cook until soft.
Fill sterilized jars and seal. Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book
~ Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
