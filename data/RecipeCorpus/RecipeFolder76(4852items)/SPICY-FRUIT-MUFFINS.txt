Title: SPICY FRUIT MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cloves
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1 c  Shredded, pared apple
2/3 c  Brown sugar or maple syrup
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
2 ea Eggs, beaten
2/3 c  Apple juice
1/4 c  Butter, melted
1 c  Bran flakes
Sift together flour, baking powder, salt and spices. Stir in apple,
brown sugar and nuts.  In a separate bowl, combine eggs, juice and
butter. Stir into apple mixture just until blended. Fold in bran
flakes. Pour into oiled muffin pans, filling each cup about 2/3 full.
Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.  Makes 12 muffins
