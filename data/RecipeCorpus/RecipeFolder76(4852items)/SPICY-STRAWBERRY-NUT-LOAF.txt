Title: SPICY STRAWBERRY NUT LOAF
Categories: Breads, Fruits, Desserts
Yield: 2 Loaves
2 c  Fresh strawberries, sliced
3 c  All-purpose flour, sifted
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
4    Eggs, beaten
1 1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Pecans, chopped
In a large mixing bowl, sift together flour, sugar, salt, soda and
spices. In a separate smaller bowl, blend eggs and oil, then
gradually stir into dry ingredients alternately with strawberries.
Stir in nuts. Pour batter into two greased, floured pans. Bake in
preheated 350 oven 45-50 minutes.
