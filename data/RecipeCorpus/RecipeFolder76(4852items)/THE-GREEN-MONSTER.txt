Title: THE GREEN MONSTER
Categories: Alcohol, Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Shot Vodka
1    Shot Cointreau
1    Shot Midori
1    Shot Peach Schnapps
1    Shot Southern Comfort
1/2 pt Cider
1/2 pt Lager
Empty all the shots into the pint glass, and the mix in the cider and
lager
