Title: STIR-FRIED BROCCOLI W/ORANGE SAUCE
Categories: Vegetables, Tried
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
3 tb Orange juice
1/4 ts Sugar
2 ts Soy sauce, light
1/2 ts Cornstarch
1 ts Orange zest
1 1/2 tb Chicken stock
1 x  Salt
MMMMM--------------------------BROCCOLI-------------------------------
2 tb Vegetable oil
1 md Scallion,mince
1 ts Ginger,mince
1 x  Steamed/Boiled Broccoli(rec)
1 md Garlic clove,mince
1 ts Vegetable oil
Combine all sauce ingredients in small bowl & set aside.
Heat skillet over high heat. add oil to skillet, when smoking
:
:
add broccoli & stir fry for 1 1/2min, til cooked through. Push
florets to sides of pan, making well in center & put garlic, ginger &
scallion in center. Drizzle remaining oil over center, remove from
heat & stir to combine. Place back on heat & add orange juice
mixture. Stir to lighty coat broccoli as sauce thickens slightly,
Serve. See Cook's,Sept/Oct'95,pp9
