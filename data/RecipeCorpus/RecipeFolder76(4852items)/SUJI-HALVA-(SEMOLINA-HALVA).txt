Title: SUJI HALVA (SEMOLINA HALVA)
Categories: Desserts, Indian
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Suji (semolina)
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Ghee
1 1/2 c  Water
1 oz Sliced almonds
1 oz Raisins
8    Green cardamoms
1. Boil sugar and water together for 5 minutes. 2. Heat ghee add suji
and stir on a slow fire till mixture becomes light creamy in color
and ghee leaves the side of the pan. 3. Add the syrup and stir
briskly till it is absorbed in the semolina. 4. Mix crushed cardamom
seeds, almonds, and raisins. 5. Serve hot.
