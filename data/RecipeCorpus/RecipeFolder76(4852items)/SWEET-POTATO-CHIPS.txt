Title: SWEET POTATO CHIPS
Categories: Appetizers, Dips, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Yams or sweet potatoes --
Sliced 1/8" thick
2 tb Safflower oil
2 tb Spicy vegetable seasoning
Like Mrs. Dash
2 tb Garlic salt
1 ts Paprika
Place sliced potatoes in a bowl, sprinkle with oil and mix thoroughly.
Spread the potatoes in a single layer on a non-stick baking sheet and
sprinkle with vegetable seasoning and garlic salt. Bake for 12-15
minutes in a preheated 450F oven unitl nicely browned. Serve
immediately.
