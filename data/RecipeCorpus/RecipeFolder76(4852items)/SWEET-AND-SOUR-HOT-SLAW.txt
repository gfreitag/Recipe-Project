Title: SWEET-AND-SOUR HOT SLAW
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
1 md Cabbage, shredded
3/4 c  Chopped onion
3/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1 c  Sugar
1 c  White vinegar
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Celery seeds
1 ts Paprika
3/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Ground red pepper
Combine vegetables. Combine sugar and remaining ingredients; stir
sugar mixture into vegetables. Cover and chill. Drain; spoon into a
serving bowl.
