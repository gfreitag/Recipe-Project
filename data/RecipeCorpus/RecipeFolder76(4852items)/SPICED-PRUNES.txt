Title: SPICED PRUNES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Pitted prunes(12oz)
1/3 c  Sweet red wine
1/4 c  Sugar
4    Whole cloves
1    Cinnamon stick,2"
Pack prunes in pint jar. Heat remaining ingredients in 1-quart
saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves;
pour over prunes. Cover and refrigerate several days to blend
flavors. (Add more wine to cover prunes if necessary.)
