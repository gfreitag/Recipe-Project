Title: SWEET'N CREAMY FRUIT DIP *
Categories: Dips, Fruits
Yield: 1 Cups
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
1/2 c  Cool Whip; thawed
1/2 c  Marshmallow creme
In small bowl, beat cream cheese until fluffy; fold in whipped
topping and marshmallow creme until well blended. Serve immediately
with fresh fruit dippers or mini cookies. Store any remaining dip in
refrigerator. Makes 1-1/2 cups.
