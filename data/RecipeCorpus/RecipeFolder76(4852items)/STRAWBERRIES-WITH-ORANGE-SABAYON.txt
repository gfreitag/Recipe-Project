Title: STRAWBERRIES WITH ORANGE SABAYON
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
3    Egg Yolks
1/4 c  Granulated Sugar
1/3 c  Orange Juice
1 1/2 tb Orange Flavoured Liqueur
*OR*
1 1/2 tb Dark Rum
1/3 c  Whipping Cream
3 c  Strawberries, halved
1    Mint Sprigs
Servings:  4

In the top of a double-boiler with simmering water in the bottom part,
combine egg yolks, sugar and juice. Using an electric mixer, beat
mixture until thick and pale, about 4 - 5 minutes. Remove from heat
and stir in orange-flavoured liqueur or rum until smooth. Refrigerate
sauce until cool, about 30 minutes. Gently fold in whipped cream,
cover and refrigerate. To serve, divide strawberries among 4 large
balloon glasses or serving dishes. Spoon some of the sauce over and
garnish with a mint sprig. Serves 4.

From The Gazette, 91/06/26.
