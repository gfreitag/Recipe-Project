Title: STABILIZED WHIPPED CREAM
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Whipping cream
1/2 ts Unflavored gelatin
1 tb Cold water
1 tb Sugar
Combine gelatin and water in a heat-proof cup. Set cup in a pan of
simmering water. Stir until gelatin dissolves; allow to cool. Whip the
cream until soft peaks form; add the dissolved gelatin and 1
tablespoon sugar, if desired. Continue whipping until cream is stiff.
Cover and refrigerate for up to 24 hours. Whisk the cream lightly
before serving.
