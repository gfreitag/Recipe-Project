Title: SUPER PITA BREAD SANDWICH
Categories: Vegetarian, Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
Cucumber; chopped
Tomato; chopped
Onion; chopped
Alfalfa sprouts
2 tb Super sesame sauce (see
-recipe)
Whole wheat pita pocket
Trim top 1/4 of pita bread for easy splitting. Soften for 10 seconds
in microwave and spread with Super Sesame sauce. Stuff with chopped
veggies.
