Title: SPINACH PASTA
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 4 Servings
2 3/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2    Egg; beaten
1/4 c  Spinach, cooked; minced,
-& well drained
1/4 c  -water
1 ts Olive oil
In a mixing bowl, stir together 2-1/4 cups of the flour and salt.
Make a well in the center.  Combine eggs, spinach, water, and oil;
add to flour and mix well. Sprinkle the remaining flour on a board.
Knead for 10 minutes, let rest for 10 minutes, cut into desired
shape. Makes 1-1/4 pounds fresh pasta.
