Title: STEAK PIZZAIOLA
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Main dish, Easy
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Boneless sirloin steak
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Olive oil
1    Onion, thinly sliced
3    Cloves garlic, minced
2    Large tomatoes, peeled,
-seeded and chopped
2 tb Chopped fresh oregano
Between sheets of waxed paper, pound steak with meat mallet to just
under 1/4 inch thickness.  Cut into 1/2 inch wide strips; season with
pinch each of the salt and pepper.

In large skillet, heat oil over high heat; brown meat, in batches,
for 2 minutes.  Remove to plate.

Reduce heat to medium.  Add onion and garlic; cook, stirring, for
about 5 minutes or until softened.  Stir in tomatoes, oregano and
remaining salt and pepper, stirring to scrape up brown bits.

Return meat and any accumulated juices to pan; simmer for 5 minutes or
until sauce is slightly thickened and meat is tender. Makes 4
servings. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Canadian
Living's Best Easy Cooking
