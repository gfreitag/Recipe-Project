Title: STRINGED BEEF ( ROPA VIEJA )
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Cuban
Yield: 8 Servings
2 lb Round beef, already stewed
1/3 c  Oil
1    Onion
2    Cloves garlic
1    Green pepper
1 cn Tomato sauce
1 ts Salt
1    Bay leaf
1/2 c  Dry wine
1 cn Red peppers
Cook the beef in stew or soup until it falls apart. String the beef.
Slice onion and green pepper.  Crush garlic and fry in hot oil with
onion. Add green pepper and fry a little more. Add remaining
ingredients, cover and cook over low heat for 15 or 20 minutes
stirring occasionally to avoid sticking.  The red pepper may be added
now or used to decorate. Serve with rice or salad.
