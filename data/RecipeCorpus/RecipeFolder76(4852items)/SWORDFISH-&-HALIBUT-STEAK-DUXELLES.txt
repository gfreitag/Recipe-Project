Title: SWORDFISH & HALIBUT STEAK DUXELLES
Categories: Seafood, Main dish, Canadian
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Butter; melted
2 tb Parsley; chopped
1/4 c  Green onion; chopped
1 c  Mushrooms; fine chopped
2 tb Lemon juice
4 ea Fish steaks; 1 inch thick
Turbot and Salmon are also suitable. Mix butter, parsley, green onion,
mushrooms and lemon juice. Spread half of mixture in bottom of a
baking dish. Arrange 4 fish steaks on top and add desired seasoning.
Cover with remaining mixture. Cover and microwave at high 6-8
minutes. Let stand covered 2 minutes.
