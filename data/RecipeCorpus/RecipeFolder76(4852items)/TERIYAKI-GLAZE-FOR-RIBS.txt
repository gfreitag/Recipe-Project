Title: TERIYAKI GLAZE FOR RIBS
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  PINEAPPLE JUICE, UNSWEETENED
1/4 c  SOY SAUCE
1/4 c  DRY SHERRY
2 tb BROWN SUGAR
1 tb CORN STARCH
**
2 tb FRESH GINGER ROOT
1 ea SMALL CLOVE OF GARLIC
2 TABLESPOON OF FRESH GROUND GINGER ROOT OR 1/2 TEASPOON OF
**
GROUND
-----------------------------------------------------
~------------- ~-- IN A SMALL SAUCEPAN, COMBINE PINEAPPLE JUICE, SOY
SAUCE, SHEERY, SUGAR, CORNSTARCH, GINGERROOT AND GARLIC. COOK AND
STIR FOR 2 MINUTES AFTER BRINGING TO A BOIL. BRUSH ON RIBS DURING THE
LAST 15 MINUTES OF GRILLING OR BROILING.
