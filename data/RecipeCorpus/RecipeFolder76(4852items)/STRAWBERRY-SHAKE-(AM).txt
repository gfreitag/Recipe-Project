Title: STRAWBERRY SHAKE (AM)
Categories: Beverages, Breakfast
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  Skim milk
1/2 c  -Plain yogurt, or up to...
1 c  Plain yogurt
1 tb Honey; or to taste
2 ts Pure vanilla
2 c  Frozen strawberries
Combine first four ingredients in blender; gradually add strawberries
and process until smooth.
