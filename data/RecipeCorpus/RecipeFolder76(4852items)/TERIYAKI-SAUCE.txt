Title: TERIYAKI SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Oil
3 tb Brown sugar
3 ea Cloves garlic
1 tb Fresh ground ginger
2 tb Wine
Smash the garlic.  I use red wine for beef, white wine for chicken.
Marinate the meat for 4 hours up.  Baste the meat with the sauce
while cooking.   Enjoy, Bill
