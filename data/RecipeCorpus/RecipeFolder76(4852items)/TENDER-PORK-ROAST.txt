Title: TENDER PORK ROAST
Categories: Crockpot, Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Boneless pork roast (about
3 pounds)
1 cn (8 oz.) tomato sauce
3/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Ground mustard
Cut roast in half; place in a 5 qt. slow cooker. Combine remaining
ingredients; pour over roast.  Cover and cook on
low for 8-9 hours or until a meat thermometer read 160ø-170ø. Remove
roast to a serving platter and keep warm. If desired, skim fat from
pan juices and thicken for gravy. Yield 8 serving. Typed in MMFormat
by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Quick Cooking.
