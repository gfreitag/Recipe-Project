Title: SWISS SPAGHETTI CASSEROLE
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Easy
Yield: 1 Servings
3 oz Spaghetti squash
3 oz Swiss chard
3 oz Onion; chopped
3 oz Green pepper; chopped
1 1/2 oz Almonds; blanched, chopped
1 ts Garlic; minced
2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Salt
Sesame oil
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Lightly grease a 4 x 8 baking pan with
sesame o8il. Cut squash in half, remove the seeds and discard
them.Place the squash in a baking pan cut side down, with 1/3 inch
water. Bake for 40 mintues. Then lwoer oven to 375 degrees. When
squash is cool enough to handle,cut into 1/2-inch cubes. Stamswiss
chard for 6 minutes. Combine all ingredients in a baking dish and
bake for 15 minutes.
