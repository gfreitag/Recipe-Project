Title: STRAINED EYEBALLS
Categories: Halloween
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Eggs
6 oz Whipped cream cheese
12    Olives with pimento
Red food coloring or catsup
Hard boil the eggs ,peel and cool. Halve lengthwise and remove yolks.
Fill hole with the creamed cheese and smooth surface as much as
possible. Press an olive into each cream cheese eyeball with the
pimento up for an eerie green iris and red pupil. Dip a toothpick in
catsup and draw broken blood vessels in cream cheese. A Halloween
party must.
