Title: TERIYAKI GRILLED CORN
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Tomato paste
1/4 ts Cornstarch
2 tb Water
1 ts Sesame seeds
6 ea Ears fresh corn
Place all ingredients, except corn, into a pot.  Whisk togetehr &
bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Reduce heat & simmer for 1
minute. Remove from heat.

Prepare grill.  Husk corn & grill covered for 5 to 10 minutes. When
almost done, baste with glaze two or three times.

PER SERVING: 70 Cal.; 1g Prot.; 0.4g Fat; 15g Carb.;
6 mg Sod.; 4g Fiber.
