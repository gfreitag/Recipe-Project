Title: SPICY PUMPKIN MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 12 Servings
MMMMM--------------------------BARB DAY-------------------------------
2 c  Bisquick
1/2 c  Dried apricots
- snipped
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 tb Cinnamon
1/2 tb Ginger
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Pumpkin;canned
1    Egg
MAKES 12 MUFFINS PREHEAT MUFFINS AND
OVEN TEMP: 400
GREASE 12 MUFFIN TINS.  MIX BISQUICK, APRICOTS, SUGAR, AND SPICES
TOGETHER.
