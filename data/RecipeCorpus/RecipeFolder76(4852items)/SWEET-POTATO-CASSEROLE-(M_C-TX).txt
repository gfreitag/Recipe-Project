Title: SWEET POTATO CASSEROLE (M_C-TX)
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles
Yield: 8 Servings
3 c  Mashed sweet potatoes
1 c  White sugar
2    Eggs, beaten
1/3 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Salt
Cinnamon
1    Stick butter, melted
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
3 1/2 tb Melted butter
1/3 c  Flour
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Nuts
Combine all ingredients. Top with topping. Bake in 350 oven for 25
minutes.
