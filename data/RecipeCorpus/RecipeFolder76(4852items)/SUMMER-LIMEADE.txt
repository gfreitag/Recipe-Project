Title: SUMMER LIMEADE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
9    Limes
1/3 c  Sugar
2    Sticks cinnamon
6    Whole cloves
1 c  Water
1 l  Ginger ale
Slice limes in half and squeeze out juice.  Simmer sugar, cinnamon and
cloves in 1 cup of water for 10 minutes. Cool and add lime juice. Fill
glasses with crushed ice or ice cubes and fill each half-full with
lime juice mixture.  Top glasses with ginger ale. Garnish with fresh
fruit.
