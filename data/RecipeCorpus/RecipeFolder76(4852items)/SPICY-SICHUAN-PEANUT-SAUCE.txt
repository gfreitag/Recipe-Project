Title: SPICY SICHUAN PEANUT SAUCE
Categories: Chinese, Sauces
Yield: 1 Cups
1 c  Peanut butter
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Asian sesame oil
3 tb Rice vinegar (not seasoned)
2 tb Finely chopped garlic
2 tb Grated peeled fresh
-gingerroot
1 1/2 ts Asian chili paste or 1 ts
-dried hot red pepper
-flakes, or to taste
1 tb Hoisin sauce or to taste
1 1/2 tb Fresh lime juice
3/4 c  Water
In a blender or food processor blend all ingredients together with
salt to taste until smooth. Transfer sauce to a jar with a tight
fitting lid. Sauce keeps, covered, and chilled, 1 week.
