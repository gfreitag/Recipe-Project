Title: STEAK & ALE HAWAIIAN CHICKEN
Categories: Bbq, Chicken
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Chicken breasts -- boneless
2 c  Pineapple juice
2 tb Worcestire Sauce
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
2 tb Sugar
In a bowl combine all ingredients except for chicken. Stir. Using a
fork, poke about 5-8 sets of holes in each chicken breast. Put
chicken in marinade, making sure it is covered in the mixture. Add
more pineapple juice if necessary. Cover and refrigerate for 24-36
hours. Grill.
