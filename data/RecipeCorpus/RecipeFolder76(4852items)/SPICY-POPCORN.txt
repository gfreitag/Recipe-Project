Title: SPICY POPCORN
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 Servings
1 tb Water
4 ts Margarine
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Turmeric
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts White pepper
1/8 ts Cayenne
10 c  Popcorn -- popped
Preheat oven at 400. Prepare baking sheet with cooking spray. Place
popped po pcorn on prepared baking sheet. Toss combination of
seasonings for the flavo r. Bake for 10 minutes, tossing once.
