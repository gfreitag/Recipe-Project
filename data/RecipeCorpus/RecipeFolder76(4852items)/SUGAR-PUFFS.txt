Title: SUGAR PUFFS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Egg
1/8 ts Salt
1 tb Milk
1 c  Flour, all purpose
Beat together egg, salt and milk.  Add enough flour to make a stiff
dough. Stir until blended.  Turn out on slightly floured board and
roll until as thin as paperl Cover with clean towel adn leave for a
half hour to dry. Cut in one and one-half inch squares. Fry in deep
fryer until a delicate brown. Drain on paper towels. While hot,
sprinkle with confectioners sugar or cinnamon.
