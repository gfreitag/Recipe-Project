Title: SUNDAY SCHOOL ROLLS
Categories: Breads, Jane
Yield: 24 Servings
2 1/2 ts Yeast
1 1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Oil
2    Eggs
1 ts Salt
4 1/2 c  Flour
Mix in bread machine-let rise 8 hrs or overnite. Divide into 24 rolls.
Place on greased cooke sheet and let rise 6 hours. Bake 10-12 min. @
375. Butter on removal from oven.
