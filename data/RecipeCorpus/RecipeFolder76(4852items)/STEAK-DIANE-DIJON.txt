Title: STEAK DIANE DIJON
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Boneless sirloin steak,1/4"
2 tb Dijon mustard
Freshly ground black pepper
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Finely minced fresh onion
1    Medium clove garlic,minced
1 c  Sliced fresh mushrooms
1/2 c  Beef broth
1/4 c  Brandy
Pound steak to 1/8" thickness.Coat one side of steak with
mustard.Sprinkle with pepper.Roll steak to coat both sides.Set
aside.In a large skillet,melt butter.Add onion and garlic.Cook until
onion is tender.Stir in mushrooms and broth.Cook to reduce
slightly.Unroll steak.Place in sauce.Cook as desired,turning
once.Pour brandy over all.Ignite carefully.When flame dies,serve
immediately.
