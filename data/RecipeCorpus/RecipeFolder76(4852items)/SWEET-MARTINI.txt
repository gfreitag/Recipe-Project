Title: SWEET MARTINI
Categories: Mixed drink, Gin, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 oz Gin
1/2 oz Sweet vermouth
1    Green olive
Cracked ice
Fill a mixing glass with ice. Pour in gin and sweet vermouth. Stir (or
shake) and strain into a martini glass. Garnish with an olive.
