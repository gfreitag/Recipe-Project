Title: TEX-MEX PIZZA
Categories: Pizza, Tex-mex
Yield: 6 Servings
1    (12-inch) pizza dough
2 lg Tomatoes, diced
1 tb Chopped jalapeno pepper
4    Green onions, chopped
2    Cloves garlic, minced
2 c  Cheddar cheese, shredded
2 tb Grated parmesan cheese
1    Avocado, chopped
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
Spoon tomatoes over pizza dough, leaving a 1/2" border. Top with
jalapenos, garlic and onion. Sprinkle with cheeses and season with
salt and pepper to taste.  Bake in 500 degree oven for 10 to 14 min.
until bottom of crust is golden brown.  Top with avocado, a dollop of
sour cream and parsley. Pass extra sour cream.
