Title: SPICY VEGETABLE COUSCOUS
Categories: None
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Diced yellow summer squash
1/2 c  Diced zucchini
1/2 c  Diced red onion
1    Clove minced garlic
1 c  Cooked garbanzos
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Dried red pepper flakes
1/2 ts Salt
Pepper to taste
3 c  Cooked couscous (cook in
-chicken broth not water)
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
1. Heat oil in a large skillet, and saut! squash,zucchini,red
onion,and garlic for 5 min.

2. stir in garbanzos and spices. Then gently stir in the cooked
couscous. Cook until hot, about 8 min. Garnish with parsley before
serving.

serves 4
