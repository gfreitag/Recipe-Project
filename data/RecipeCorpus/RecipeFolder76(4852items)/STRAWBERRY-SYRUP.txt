Title: STRAWBERRY SYRUP
Categories: Syrups, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
10 oz Strawberries;
-pkg frozen sliced thawed
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1 ds Salt
In blender; process strawberries until smooth.  In a pan, bring all
ingredients to a boil and boil gently for 5 min., stirring constantly.
Yield:  1 1/2 c. syrup
