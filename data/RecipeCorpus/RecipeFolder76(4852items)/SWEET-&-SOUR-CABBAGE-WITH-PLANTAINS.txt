Title: SWEET & SOUR CABBAGE WITH PLANTAINS
Categories: Vegetarian, Side dish, Diabetic
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Vegetable broth
2 tb Brown sugar; packed**
2 tb Cider vinegar
2 ts Cornstarch
1/4 ts Red pepper sauce
2 ts Vegetable oil
1    Ripe medium plantain; cut
-into 1/4" slices
1/3 c  Green onions; sliced
1/4 c  Red bell pepper; cut into
-1 1/8" strips
4 c  Savoy cabbage; coarsely
-shredded
**PERSONAL NOTE from Ursula
-Taylor - for diabetics -
-use brown sugar substitute.
Mix broth, brown sugar, vinegar, cornstarch and pepper sauce;
reserve. Heat oil in 10" nonstick skillet over medium-high heat until
hot. Add plantain, onions and bell pepper; stir-fry one minute. Add
cabbage; stir-fry 2 minutes. Stir in cornstarch mixture; cook and
stir 10 seconds or until thickened.
