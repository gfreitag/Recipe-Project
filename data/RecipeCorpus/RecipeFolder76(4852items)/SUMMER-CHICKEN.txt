Title: SUMMER CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 lb Chicken breasts and thighs
Water
2    Stalks celery
1    Clove garlic
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  To 1/2 c chopped scallions
Put chicken parts and celery (cut up) in a pot and cover with water.
Bring to a hard boil for 5 minutes then cover the pot tightly and
remove from heat. Do not touch or uncover until completely cool. VERY
completely cooled <much emphasis on this>. Skin chicken and break
into small pieces (save stock for soup, etc..). Mix the chicken
pieces with the remaining ingredients. Serve cold. From Joan Plodzik

: Concord Hospital Admitting Cookbook, Concord, NH
