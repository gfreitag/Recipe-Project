Title: SPICY PAPAYA SALSA
Categories: Fruits, Condiments
Yield: 3 Servings
1    Large papaya
1/2    Small jalapeno pepper
1/4 c  Diced red onion
1/3 c  Diced yellow or red pepper
1 tb Lime juice
1 tb Minced fresh coriander
Dice papaya and minced jalapeno pepper. Combine and toss with
remaining ingredients. Make 3 cups goes great with grilled or broiled
meats, fish or chicken.
