Title: SWEET POTATO PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Eggs; beaten
3/4 c  Sugar; to 1 cup
2 c  Sweet potatoes; raw, grated
1 cn Evaporated milk; 13 oz
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Butter (or marg.); melted
1 c  Flaked coconut
Combine eggs and sugar in a large mixing bowl, beating well; stir in
remaining ingredients.  Pour into a greased 1-1/2 quart casserole.
Bake at 350 degrees for
1    hour.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, October, 1980. Typed for you by
Nancy Coleman.
