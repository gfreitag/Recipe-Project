Title: STARBUCK'S MOCHA FRAPPE'
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
18    Ice cubes (up to 22)
7 oz Double strength coffee,
-chilled
1/4 c  Chocolate sauce (or syrup)
2 T  Vanilla Syrup
Whipped Cream (garnish)
Place ice, coffee, chocolate sauce, and syrup in a blender. Blend
until smooth. Pour into a large, tall (chilled) glass. Garnish with
dollop of whipped cream or scoop of your favorite ice cream. Makes
one 16 oz frappe
