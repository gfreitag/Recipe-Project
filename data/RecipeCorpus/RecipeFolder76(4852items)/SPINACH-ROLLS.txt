Title: SPINACH ROLLS
Categories: Appetizers, Italian, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
Pizza dough
1 lb Fresh spinach, washed,
-drained & chopped
1 sm Onion; chopped
1 c  Mozzarella cheese; shredded
Olive oil
1    Egg; beaten
Recipe by: Little Mom Roll out pizza dough into a thin rectangle and
place on greased cookie sheet.  Spread chopped spinach evenly over
dough. Sprinkle with chopped onion, cheese and pepper. Drizzle olive
oil over, then gently roll dough jelly roll fashion. Make sure seam
is on the bottom. Poke 8-10 holes in dough and brush roll with beaten
eggs. Bake at 350F for 40 minutes.
