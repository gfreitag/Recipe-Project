Title: SPINACH ROMAN STYLE
Categories: Italian, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Washed spinach
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Lard or bacon drippings
1/2 ts Pepper
1 tb Shelled pine nuts
1 tb Raisins
Cook Spinach in 1 cup water, 5 minutes and drain well. Melt leaf lard
or drippings in saucepan, add spinach, salt and pepper and cook 5
minutes longer. Add nuts and raisins and serve. Serves 4.

From: The Talisman Italian Cook Book Shared By: Pat Stockett
