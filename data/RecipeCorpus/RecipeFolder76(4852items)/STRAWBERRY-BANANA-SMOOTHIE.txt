Title: STRAWBERRY-BANANA SMOOTHIE
Categories: Beverages, Fruits
Yield: 2 Servings
2 c  Sliced strawberries (fresh
-or thawed)
1 c  Milk
1    Banana
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Liquid honey
Pinch cinnamon
In blender, combine strawberries, milk, banana, lemon juice, honey and
cinnamon; puree until thick and frothy. Makes 2 servings. Typed in
MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com Source: The Canadian Living 20th
Anniversary Cookbook.
