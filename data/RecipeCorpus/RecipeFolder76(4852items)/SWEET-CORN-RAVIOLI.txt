Title: SWEET CORN RAVIOLI
Categories: New, Text, Import
2 ts chives
Yield: 4 Servings
2    ears fresh corn, kernels
removed from cob
:
1 c  cream
Salt
Pepper
:
:
1/2 lb raw shrimp, -- shelled and
deveined
:
28    round wonton wrappers
2    eggs
Water
:

With a knife, roughly chop shrimp. In a food processor blend shrimp,
corn and cream. Season with salt, pepper and chives. Lay out wanton
wrappers and brush with an egg wash. Place 1 tablespoon of the shrimp
mixture on one side of each wrapper. Fold over each wrapper and seal
with a fork. Bring salted water to a boil and place ravioli in water.
Poach for about 4 minutes. Serve ravioli with corn sauce
