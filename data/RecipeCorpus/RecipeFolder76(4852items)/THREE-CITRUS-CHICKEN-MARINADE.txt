Title: THREE CITRUS CHICKEN MARINADE
Categories: Marinades, Poultry
Yield: 1 Recipe
1/2 c  Fresh lime juice
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/2 c  Fresh orange juice
1/2 c  Olive oil
4    Cloves garlic minced
2 ts Coarsely ground black
-pepper
3    Crushed bay leaves
3 tb Chopped cilantro
Your choice of skinned
-chicken parts
Mix all ingredients in shallow wide mixing bowl. Add chicken and
marinate 1-2 hours.  Grill or broil, brushing marinade on chicken 2-3
times.

Marinade also may be used on salmon, tuna or shrimp. Reduce
marinating time so citrus does not "cook" seafood.
