Title: SPIEDIES (BROOME COUNTY~ NY)
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 Pound meat
1 c  Oil
3/4 c  Wine Vinegar
1/8 c  Sugar
1    Onion
4    Clove Garlic
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2 ts Thyme
1 1/2 ts Marjoram
1 1/2 ts Basil
1 1/2 ts Oregano
Mix all ingredients in a large covered bowl. Place cubed meat to
merinate for atleast 5 days, shaking or turning over each day. Place
on squires to cook on grill. Use Italian bread.
