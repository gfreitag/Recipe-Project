Title: SPICEY FRENCH DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 3 Cups
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour
3/4 c  Red Wine Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Worchestershire sauce
1 1/2 tb Onion powder
2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 ts Celery seed
1 c  Salad oil
Cook sugar, flour and vinegar until thickened.  Add remaining
ingredients except oil.  Blenderize thoroughly and slowly add oil.
