Title: SPINACH CROQUETTES
Categories: Veggies, Mash, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Guessed
MMMMM--------------------QUICK CUISINE, TVFN-------------------------
1 c  Mashed potatoes
10 oz Froz spinach, thaw, squeezed
Salt and pepper
2    Eggs
1/3 c  Parmesan
Tabasco
Nutmeg
Breadcrumbs
Mozzarella, opt
Ham, opt
Oil for frying
Mix all except crumbs; form small patties. If using mozzarella or ham,
place a small amount in the center of each patty. Roll in crumbs; fry.
