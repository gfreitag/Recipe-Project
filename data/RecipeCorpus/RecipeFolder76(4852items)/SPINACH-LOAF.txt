Title: SPINACH LOAF
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Brown rice; cooked
1 c  Celery, stalks and leaves;
-chopped
2 c  Spinach; cooked
Wheat germ
Chop spinach and add other items. Pour into greased loaf pan.
Sprinkle with wheat germ. Bake at 400 deg for 30 minutes. Swiss chard
can be substituted. Serves
2.
