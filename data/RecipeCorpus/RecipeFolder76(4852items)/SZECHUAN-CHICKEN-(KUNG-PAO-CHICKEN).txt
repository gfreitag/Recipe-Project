Title: SZECHUAN CHICKEN (KUNG PAO CHICKEN)
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 1 SERVINGS--------------------------
4 ea Chicken-breast halves;
-skinned, boned, and cubed
-into 3/4 in cubes
1 ea Egg white
1 ea Tb Corn starch
2 ea Tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Unsalted peanuts or cashews
2 ea Scallions; sliced
2 ea Tb Dry sherry
2 ea Tb Hoisin sauce
4 ea Tb Black bean sauce
1/4 ts - 1/2 tsp. chili paste
1 ea Tb Vinegar
1 ts Sugar
Combine the cubed chicken with the egg white and cornstarch.
Refrigerate for 1/2 hour. Heat oil in wok and stir-fry chicken 3 to 4
minutes, until done. Add nuts, scallions, and remaining ingredients.
Heat thoroughly and serve at once with rice. ++++-
