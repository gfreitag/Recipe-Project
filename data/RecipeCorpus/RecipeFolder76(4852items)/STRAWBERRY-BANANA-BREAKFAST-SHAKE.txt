Title: STRAWBERRY BANANA BREAKFAST SHAKE
Categories: Snacks, Syd's book, Breakfast
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Strawberries *
1/2 md Banana, Cut Into Chunks
1/2 c  Orange Juice, Fresh
3 tb Low Fat Yogurt
* Strawberries should be frozen and unsweetened.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Place all ingredients in a blender bowl and
process until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve. 85 calories per
serving. From: Syd's Cookbook.
