Title: SWEDISH MEET BALLS (KOTTBULLAR)
Categories: Beef, Pork, Meats, Swedish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Beef
1/2 lb Pork
1/2 lb Veal
2    Eggs
2/3 c  Bread crumbs
1 c  Milk
2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 ts Ground nutmeg
3 tb Shortening
flour water or stock

Grind meats together 3 times. Mix with eggs, bread crumbs, milk, salt,
pepper and nutmeg and make into small balls. Brown in skillet in hot
shortening. Remove meat balls to larger larger pan. Add 1/2 cup flour
to skillet and brown, stirring to prevent scorching. Add enough water
or stock to make a thin gravy. Pour gravy over meat balls. Cook
gently for 1 hour, stirring often to prevent sticking. Flavor
improves with long simmering. Serve in gravy.
