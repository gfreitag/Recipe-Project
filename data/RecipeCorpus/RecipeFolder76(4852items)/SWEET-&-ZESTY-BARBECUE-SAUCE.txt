Title: SWEET & ZESTY BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Barbecue
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  HEINZ Tomato Ketchup
2 tb Honey
2 tb HEINZ Apple Cider Vinegar
1 tb HEINZ Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 ts Chili powder
Combine ingredients. Brush on turkey pieces several times during last
15 minutes of grilling time.
