Title: SYRIAN SESEME MUFFINS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
MMMMM---------------------FROM J.R'S KITCHEN--------------------------
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  White sugar
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1/3 c  Toasted seseme seeds
1    Egg
1 1/4 c  Buttermilk
4 tb Butter melted
1 tb Seseme seed oil
Mix dry ingredients with seseme seeds in a bowl. Whisk wet ingredients
together. make a well in center of dry ingredients and whisk in wets
quickly. Spoon into muffin tins and sprinkle with a bit more seseme
seeds. Bake 400 15 to 20 minutes or done. makes 12 to 14.These are a
good snack muffin, eat with honey and butter. ~---
