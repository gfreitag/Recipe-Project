Title: THREE MINUTE MICROWAVE CARMELCORN
Categories: Microwave, Desserts, Candies, Knudsen
Yield: 4 Quarts
1/4 c  Corn syrup
1/4 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 ts Baking soda
4 qt Popcorn, popped
Cook corn syrup, margarine, and brown sugar on high for 3 minutes.
Stir and add baking soda. Stir until smooth. Cook 30 seconds more on
high, stir. Pour over popcorn and mix until well coated.
