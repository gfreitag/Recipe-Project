Title: THOUSAND ISLAND DRESSING (HELLMANN'S MAYO)
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 2 Cups
1/3 c  Hellmann's Real Mayonnaise
1    Hard-cooked egg, finely
Chopped
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1 tb Chopped pimiento-stuffed
Olives
1 tb Chopped dill pickle
1 ts Grated onion
1    8-oz cup Dannon Plain Yogurt
Stir together first 6 ingredients.  Fold in yogurt. Chill. This is
from the Hellmann's advertising back in the 70's, and was taken from
the 1979 edition of "Best Recipes From the Backs of Boxes, Bottles,
Cans, and Jars".
