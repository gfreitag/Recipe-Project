Title: SUGAR-SNAP PEA & STRAWBERRY SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM-----------------WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A----------------------
2 tb Ex-virgin olive oil
2 ts Raspberry vinegar
1/4 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 lb Sugar-snap peas w/pods;
-strings removed
1 lb Ripe strawberries; slice
-thick
Combine the oil, vinegar and mustard in a small bowl; whisk
thoroughly and set aside. Steam the sugar-snap peapods for 30 seconds
or until bright green but still crunchy. Remove from the heat, drain,
then quickly run cold water over them to stop the cooking and drain
again. Combine the peas with the berries. Whisk the dressing again
and dizzle over the salad. Serve immediately or chill for no more
than 1 hour. Source: The Main Corpse by Diane Mott Davidson. MM
