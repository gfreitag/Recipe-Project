Title: SPINACH & STRAWBERRY SALAD WITH HONEY DRESS
Categories: Salads, Fruits, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
6 oz Cleaned and trimmed
Fresh spinach
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Rice vinegar
2 tb Water
4 ts Honey
2 ts Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper
To taste
1 c  Thickly sliced fresh
Strawberries
1 sm Thinly sliced red onion
Tear spinach into bite sized pieces and place in salad bowl. In
a separate bowl whisk together vinegars, water, honey, mustard, salt
and pepper.  Add to the spinach and toss lightly. Add strawberries
and onion. Toss lightly. Walt
