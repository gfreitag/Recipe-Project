Title: SWORDFISH WITH MUSTARD-BASIL BUTTER
Categories: Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Unsalted butter (1/2 stick),
-room temperature
2 tb Dijon mustard
2 tb Fresh basil; finely chopped
Vegetable oil
4    8-oz swordfish steaks
-3/4" thick
Fresh basil sprigs
Mix first 3 ingredients in a small bowl. Prepare barbecue or preheat
broiler.  Brush grill with vegetable oil. Season fish with salt and
pepper. Grill fish just until cooked through, about 3 minutes per
side. Transfer to plates.  Top each with spoonful of mustard-basil
butter. Garnish with basil sprigs and serve.
