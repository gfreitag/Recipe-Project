Title: STRAWBERRY-BANANA SHAKE
Categories: Vegetarian, Snacks
Yield: 3 Servings
1 pk Mori-Nu silken soft tofu;
-drained (10.5 oz)
1 sm Banana, ripe
1 c  Strawberries, frozen,
-sweetened, including juice;
-slightly thawed
In a blender or food processor, combine all ingredients until smooth.
Chill and serve. Makes 3 servings of 6 oz each.
