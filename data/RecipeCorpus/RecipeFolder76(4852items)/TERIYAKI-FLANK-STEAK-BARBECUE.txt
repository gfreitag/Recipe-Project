Title: TERIYAKI FLANK STEAK BARBECUE
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Honey
2 tb Cider vinegar
2 tb Scallions or chives
1    Clove garlic, minced fine
1 1/2 ts Ground ginger
Mix all the ingredients together. Marinate a flank steak for four
hours, turning once. Grill over a charcoal fire. Slice thinly and
serve.
