Title: SPICY SPAGHETTI SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 pk EDEN Kamut Spaghetti
15 oz Can EDEN Navy Beans
-- drained
14 1/2 oz Can EDEN Diced Tomatoes
-- with Green Chilies
5    Scallions; chopped
1/4 c  Cilantro, chopped
1 md Red pepper; chopped
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Cumin
2 tb EDEN Shoyu
Break spaghetti into one inch pieces, cook as package directs, drain
and rinse. Add remaining ingredients and serve.

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cooking Time: 10 minutes Yield: 4-5 servings
