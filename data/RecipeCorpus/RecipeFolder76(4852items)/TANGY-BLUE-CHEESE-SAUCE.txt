Title: TANGY BLUE CHEESE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Dairy Sour Cream
1 oz Blue Cheese; Crumbled, 3/4
-Cup
3 tb Milk
1 md Green Onion W/Top; Sliced
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Bottled Hot Pepper Sauce
Put all of the ingredients into a blender container and blend until
the onion is finely chopped.  Heat through, but do not boil. Serve
over vegetables. NOTE: Refrigerate and reheat any unused sauce.

Makes 1 1/4 cups.
