Title: SPICY GINGER SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Rice vinegar
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Oriental sesame oil
2 ts Minced fresh peeled ginger
1 ts Sugar
1    Carrot, shredded
-
1/2 ts Chili oil or
-1/4 ts red pepper
2    Green onions, sliced thin
1/2    Cucumber, seeded, peeled ---
And chopped
1/4 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
Whisk all ingredients together in a large bowl. Serve over 12 oz. (dry
weight) of japanese noodles. Serve well chilled.
