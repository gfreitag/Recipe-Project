Title: STUFFED FIGS & DATES
Categories: Snacks, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
Figs
Pitted dates
Shelled walnut halves
Unsweetened shredded
-- coconut
Cut out the centre of each fig & place a walnut half inside. Cut open
the dates & do the same.  Roll each stuffed fruit in shredded
coconut. Make as many as you want.
