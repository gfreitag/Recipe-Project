Title: SUPER TUPPER BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 1 1/2#loaf
2 ts Bread machine Yeast
3 c  Better for bread flour
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 ts Salt
4 tb Margarine,Corn Oil Etc.
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Water
Add ingredients in order listed. All ingredients should be room
temperature. Fleishman Bread machine yeast in jar recommended.
