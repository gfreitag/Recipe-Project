Title: SUNSHINE CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
3 ts Curry
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Salt
4    Boneless skinless chicken br
1 1/2 c  Orange juice
1 c  Rice
3/4 c  Water
1 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Dried mustard
Fresh parsley
Combine curry powder, 1/4 teaspoon salt, and pepper. Sprinkle over
chicken and rub in.  In 10-inch skillet, combine orange juice, rice,
water, brown sugar, mustard, and remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt. Arrange
chicken on top. Bring to a boil.  cover and simmer 20 minutes.
Sprinkle with parsley before serving.
