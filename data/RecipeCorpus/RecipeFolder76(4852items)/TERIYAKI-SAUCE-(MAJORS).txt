Title: TERIYAKI SAUCE (MAJORS)
Categories: Diabetic, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Soy sauce; (salt free)
2 cl Crushed garlic;
1 ts Dry mustard;
Arificial sweetener to equal
-1 ts sugar
2 ts Grond ginger;
Combine all ingredients in a jar with tight lid. Refrigerate, and
sauce will keep several weeks. Food Exchange per serving: FREE FOOD;
CAL: calories negligible.
