Title: SPINACH DIP II
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
10 oz Frozen spinach; thawed
1/3 c  Chopped green onions
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
16 oz Sour cream
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 pk Knorr Leek Soup mix
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Salt & Pepper if desired
Drain spinach well; squeeze out all excess moisture. Chop well by
hand or in food processor.  Combine with all other ingredients, blend
well and refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
