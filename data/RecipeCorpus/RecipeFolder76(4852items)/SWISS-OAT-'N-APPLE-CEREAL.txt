Title: SWISS OAT 'N APPLE CEREAL
Categories: Cereal
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Natural or chunky applesauce
1 1/3 c  Quaker Oat Bran hot cereal,
-uncooked
1 c  Quaker Oats (quick or old
-fashioned), uncooked
1 c  Unsweetened apple juice
2/3 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Sliced almonds
1 ts Cinnamon
Swiss Oat 'n Apple Cereal

Combine all ingredients; mix well. Cover; refrigerate overnight.
Store in refrig. up to one week. Serve cold or hot with skim milk or
low-fat yogurt if desired.

To heat one serving, place 3/4 cup cereal in a 2-cup microwavable
bowl. Microwave on HIGH 1 minute 30 secondsto 2 minutes or until
heated through. Stir well. (about 345 calories per 3/4 cup serving)

Recipe from the Quaker Oat Bran Cookbook
