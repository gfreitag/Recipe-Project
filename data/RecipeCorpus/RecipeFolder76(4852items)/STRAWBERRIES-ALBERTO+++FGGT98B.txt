Title: STRAWBERRIES ALBERTO+++FGGT98B
Categories: Desserts, Fruits, Italian
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Chianti
2 c  Sugar
6    To
7    Thin slices lemon
2 pn Ripe strawberries
Hulled and sliced
FAWN STREET, BALTIMORE. WINE: BOLLA AMARONE 1. Mix wine with the
sugar and lemon slices. Stir in the strawberries. Chill. Serve. Note:
If the strawberries are very ripe & sweet you may not need so much
sugar.
