Title: STEAMED SQUASH SALAD
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
4 sm Slender zucchini -- cut into
1/4    -inch
Rounds and parboiled for 1
Minute
1/3 c  Extra virgin olive oil
Grated zest and juice of two
lg Limes
1/4 ts Ground cumin and cardamom
1/4 ts Pressed or minced garlic
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper
Parboil zucchini for a minute, drain, pat dry and divide among four
dinner plates. Set zucchini in concentric circles on a plate.

Combine the olive oil, lime peel and juice, cumin, cardamom and
garlic. Season with salt and pepper. Taste and adjust for acidity.
Spoon dressing over zucchini. When chicken is done, set it, skin side
up over zucchini.

Yield: 4 servings
