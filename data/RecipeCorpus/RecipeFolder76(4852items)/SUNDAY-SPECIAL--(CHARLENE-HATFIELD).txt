Title: SUNDAY SPECIAL  (CHARLENE HATFIELD)
Categories: Desserts, Great, Marie
Yield: 10 Servings
20    Oreo cookies
1 qt Vanilla ice cream
4 tb Melted butter
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Evaporated milk
3 oz Chocolate
9 oz Cool whip
Chopped nuts
Blend the oreos in a blender. Combine the oreos and the butter. Put
aside 1/4 cup.  Spread the rest over the bottom of a 13 X 9 pan,
covering the bottom. Slice the ice cream over the top of the cookie
crumbs, covering the bottom.  Mix together 1 cup sugar, 1 cup
evaporated milk, 3 oz. of chocolate and bring to a full boil. LET
COOL!!! Pour over the ice cream. Spread the cool whip over the
chocolate sauce. sprinkle with the reserved Oreo crumbs and the nuts.
Freeze until ready to serve.
