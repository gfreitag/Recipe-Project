Title: TANGY TOMATO SAUCE
Categories: Digest
Yield: 1 Servings
1    28-oz can crushed tomato
1 c  Diced onion
4    Or more cloves minced
-garlic
1 tb Apple cider
1 tb Lemon juice
Saute onion and garlic. Add crushed tomato. Simmer 5 minutes. Add
apple cider and lemon juice. Simmer 5 more minutes. It's done. Shared
by Jane Segelken
