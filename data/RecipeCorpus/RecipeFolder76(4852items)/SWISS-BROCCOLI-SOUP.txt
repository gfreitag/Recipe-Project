Title: SWISS BROCCOLI SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Minced onion
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 cn HEINZ HomeStyle Chicken
-Gravy (12 oz)
1 1/4 c  Milk
1 pk Frozen chopped broccoli
-cooked, drained (10 oz)
1 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
ds Salt
ds Pepper
In 2-quart saucepan, saute onion in butter until tender. Stir in
gravy, milk and broccoli; heat slowly, stirring occasionally. Add
cheese, salt and pepper; heat until cheese is melted, stirring
frequently.

Makes 4 servings (about 4 cups).
