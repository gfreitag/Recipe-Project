Title: TEMPEH & BULGUR SALAD
Categories: Salads, Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Oil
4 oz Tempeh; cubed
1/2    Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Bulgur
4 lg Mushrooms; chopped
1 c  Water
1 tb Soy sauce
1    Celery stalk; diced
1/2 lg Carrot; grated
1    Tomato; diced
2 tb Fresh parsley; minced
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 1/2 ts Honey
1/2 ts Dillweed
1/8 ts Oregano
1 ds White pepper
Heat 1 tb oil in a large skillet & saute tempeh, onion, bulgur &
mushrooms for 3 to 4 minutes.  Add water & soy sauce. Bring to a
boil, cover, reduce heat & simmer 15 minutes.  Let cool to room
temperature. Combine with remaining ingredients & mix well. Chill for
at least 2 hours before serving.
