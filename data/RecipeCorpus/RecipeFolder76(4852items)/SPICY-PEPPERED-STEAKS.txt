Title: SPICY PEPPERED STEAKS
Categories: Beef
Yield: 6 Servings
6 ea Sirloin Steaks
4 ts Whole Peppercorns
1/2 c  Bottled Steak Sauce
2 tb Dijon-sytle Mustard
1 tb Brown Sugar
1 ts Salt
Crush peppercorns coarsely with a rolling pin.  Mix Steak sauce,
mustard, brown sugar and salt.  Marinate overnight or until ready to
cook. Grill as usual, using the marinade as a baste. Very good pepper
steaks!!
