Title: TEX-MEX CAVIAR
Categories: Digest, Mar95
Yield: 1 Servings
1    10-oz package frozen
Black-eyed peas
1/4 c  Chopped sweet red pepper
1/4 c  Chopped sweet green bell
Pepper
1/4 c  Chopped red onion
1 tb Chopped fresh jalapeno
Pepper
2 tb Chopped parsley
1/4 c  FF Italian dressing
Rinse and drain peas.  [Thawed, I assume]  Mix with other ingredients.

(Their nutritional analysis assumed low-fat dressing instead of FF,
so I'm leaving it off.)
