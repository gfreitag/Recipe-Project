Title: STEAMED FISH WITH GINGER & ONIONS
Categories: Fish, Microwave
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Whole red snapper, cleaned
6    Green onions, sliced
Sauce:
1 ts Sesame oil
2 tb Slivered fresh ginger
1/4 c  Sherry, mirin or water
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Vegetable oil
Rinse fish and pierce in several placed with a skewer or sharp knife.
Place in a shallow microwaveable dish. If using 2 fish, arrange about
1 inch apart with thicker sides towards outer edges of dish. Put half
the ginger and green onions inside fish. Pour sherry over and cover
with vented plastic wrap. Combine sauce ingredients in small
microwaveable dish or measure. Set aside. Microwave fish at high for
6 to 8 minutes per lb or until fish flakes easily with a fork and is
tender. Rotate dish if necessary during cooking. Submitted By
