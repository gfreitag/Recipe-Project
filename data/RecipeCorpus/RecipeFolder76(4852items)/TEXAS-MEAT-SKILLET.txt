Title: TEXAS MEAT SKILLET
Categories: Bean, Ground chic
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Ground Meat Mix
1/2 c  Water
14 1/2 oz Crushed tomatoes
15 1/2 oz Dark red kidney beans --
Undrained
1 c  Long-grain rice -- cooked
1/2 c  Bell peppers -- chopped
2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Fat-free cheddar cheese --
Grated
1 c  Low-fat tortilla chips --
Crushed
In a skillet, combine Ground Meat Mix, and water. Cover and cook over
low heat for 15 minutes. Break up meat with a fork. Stir in crushed
tomatoes, kidney beans, rice, bell peppers, chili powder, and salt.
Bring mixture to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer for 20
minutes, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle cheese and tortilla chips
over meat mixture.
