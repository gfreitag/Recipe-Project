Title: STRAWBERRIES & CREME FRAICHE
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Whipping cream
1/3 c  Sour cream or buttermilk
4 c  Strawberries
Combine whipping cream with sour cream or buttermilk in a glass jar or
bowl.  Blend thoroughly. Cover tightly. Leave at room temperature for
24 hours or til serving time. Wash berries. Leave on hulls and serve
creme as a dipping sauce.
