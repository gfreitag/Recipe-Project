Title: SWEDISH RYE BREAD-LOWFAT
Categories: Swedish, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Skim Milk, scalded
2 c  Cool Water
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Smartbeat Butter
Replacement
2 ts Salt
2 pk Yeast
1/4 c  Molasses
5 c  Rye Flour
6 c  White Flour
Mix ingredients. Knead until dough is not sticky. Let rise until
double. Punch down. Shape into loaves. Let rise till double. Bake at
375 for 35 mins. Enjoy.
