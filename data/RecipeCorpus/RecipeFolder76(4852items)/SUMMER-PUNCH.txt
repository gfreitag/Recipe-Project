Title: SUMMER PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 10 Servings
2 l  White wine; chilled
1 l  Club soda; chilled
3 oz Brandy
3 oz Orange liqueur
1 c  Strong tea
3    Lemons; juice
2    Limes; juice
Pour into punch bowl over block of ice. Garnish with sliced fruit and
mint sprigs. Ideal for showers or anniversary parties
