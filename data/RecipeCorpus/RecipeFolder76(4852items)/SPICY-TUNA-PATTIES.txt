Title: SPICY TUNA PATTIES
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Onion (chopped)
2    Cloves garlic (crushed)
1 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Coriander
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Dry mustard
2    Eggs (beaten)
2 cn Tuna
1/2 c  Bread or cracker crumbs
1 tb Parsley
1 ts Lemon juice
2 tb Flour
Salt & pepper
Fry onions in oil until soft then add garlic and seasonings. In a
bowl mix the final 7 ingredients. Next add sauteed onions. Make 8
patties from the mixture and fry in oil until browned.
