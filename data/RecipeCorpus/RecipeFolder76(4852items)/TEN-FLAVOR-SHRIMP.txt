Title: TEN FLAVOR SHRIMP
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  5 oz. deveined medium shrimp
1 1/2 tb Minced shallots or scallions
2 tb Brandy
1 1/2 ts Freshly grated ginger or 1
2 tb Minced parsley
4 ts Grated parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Butter
1 md Clove garlic, mashed
2 ts Soy sauce
Teaspoon powdered ginger
4 ts Fresh bread crumbs
Directions Drain shrimp. Rinse under cold water andthen soak for 10 -
15 minutesin cold water to remove preserving brine. Drain thoroughly
and put on paper towels. Heat butter in skillet until it bubbles. Add
shallots and garlic, and saute gently until golden. Add shrimp and
toss for a minute or two to warm through. Mix in brandy, soy and
ginger, along with parsley. Spoon the shrimp mixture into shells or
ovenproof glass individual serving dishes, leaving any juice in pan.
Sprinkle each serving with bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese and then
dribble pan juices over top. Heat under broiler until mixture is
bubbling ane the crumbs are browned. submitted by marina source
