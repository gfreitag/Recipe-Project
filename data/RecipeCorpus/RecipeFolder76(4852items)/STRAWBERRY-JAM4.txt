Title: STRAWBERRY JAM4
Categories: Canning, Preserves, Etc.
Yield: 1 Servings
2 qt Strawberries
5 1/2 c  Sugar
Boiling water
Wash & hull the berries.  Place in a large pot.  Cover with boiling
water & let stand for 3 minutes.  Drain. Add 2 1/2 cups of water,
boil hard for 3 minutes.  Add remaining sugar & boil hard for another
3 minutes. Let stand overnight, either in large bowls or platters.
Ladle into serile jars. Seal.
