Title: STRAWBERRY POP CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Jello
Yield: 10 Servings
1 pk Yellow or white cake; baked
2 pk Strawberry jello; disolved
1 c  Hot water
1 1/2 c  Strawberry soda
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
1 pk Dream Whip
1 sm Vanilla instant pudding
1 1/2 c  Milk
Poke cake all over top with fork. Mix water, jello and strawberry
pop. Pour over cake. Frost with Dream Whip Mixture.
