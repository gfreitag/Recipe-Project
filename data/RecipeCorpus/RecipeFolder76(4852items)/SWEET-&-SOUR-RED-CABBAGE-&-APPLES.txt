Title: SWEET & SOUR RED CABBAGE & APPLES
Categories: Low-, Fat, Cal
Yield: 2 Servings
1 lg Garlic clove
12 oz Red cabbage
2 ts Sesame oil
1 lb Granny smith apples
1/4 c  Apple cider vinegar
1 tb Soy sauce, low sodium
1 tb Honey
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Ground pepper -- to taste
With a food processor, shred red cabbage and mince garlic clove. Heat
oil in a large nonstick pan and add garlic and cabbage. Saute over
medium heat.

Wash, core, and cut apples into bite-size chunks.  Add apples to
cabbage and stir well. Add remaining ingred. Cover and cook over low
heat til apples are tender, abt 7 min.
