Title: STIR-FRIED BROCCOLI & MUSHROOMS
Categories: Chinese, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Corn Oil
2 c  Sliced Mushrooms
1 sm Onion, cut in thin wedges
1/2 ts Salt
Clove Garlic, minced
1/8 ts Pepper
2 c  Broccoli Florets
In wok or large skillet heat oil over medium-high. Add onion and
garlic; stir-fry 30 seconds. Add broccoli, mushrooms, salt and
pepper; stir-fry 5 minutes or until tender-crisp.
