Title: STARS & STRIPES PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 Quarts
6 oz Pkg Cherry Gelatin
2 c  Boiling water**
2 qt Chilled apple juice
1/2 c  Chilled lemon juice
1 1/2 qt Lemon soda
Cracked ice
Dissolve gelatin in water. Stir in fruit juices and soda; mix well.
Chill with ice in punch bowl.

** DO NOT MIX HOT INGREDIENTS IN PUNCH BOWL! **
