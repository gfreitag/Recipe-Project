Title: SWEET POTATO CORN PONES
Categories: Breads
Yield: 36 Servings
1 ts Baking soda
1 1/4 c  Buttermilk
2 c  Cornmeal
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 c  Cooked mashed sweet potatoes
1    Egg beaten
2 tb Bacon drippings
Dissolve soda in buttermilk.   Combine cornmeal, sugar, and salt; add
buttermilk mixture, sweet potato, egg, and drippings, mixing well.
Shape into balls, using 1 T batter per all. Place on greased griddle,
and lightly press into circles with the back of a fork. Bake at 400
degrees for 20 minutes.
