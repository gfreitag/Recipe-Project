Title: STRAWBERRY-ORANGE MILK SHAKE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  Sliced strawberries
1/2    Banana, peeled and sliced
1 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Buttermilk
Combine all of the ingredients in a blender and process until smoothly
blended. Pour the shake into 2 tall glasses and serve.

Nutrition Info: 124 calories; 1 gram fat; 26 grams carbohydrates; 2 mg
cholesterol Posted to Digest eat-lf.v096.n126
