Title: STUFFED BRINJALS
Categories: Vegetables, Punjabi
Yield: 1 Recipe
250 g  Small brinjals (eggplants)
1 tb Poppy seeds
1 tb Coriander powder
1/2 tb Mango powder
1 md Onion
1/2 ts Turmeric
1    Handful fenugreek leaves
1    1-inch piece of ginger
4    Garlic cloves
4    Green chilies
1    Handful of coriander leaves
Salt; to taste
Chili powder; to taste
2 tb Ghee or oil
On each eggplant, make 4 crosswise cuts about halfway through. Grind
remaining ingredients (except ghee or oil) to a fine paste and stuff
into cuts. Heat ghee or oil in a pan large enough to hold eggplants.
Cook eggplants, covered, over low heat until done, stirring
occasionally. Serve hot.
