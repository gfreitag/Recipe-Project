Title: TACO HOTDISH
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
MMMMM-------------------NANCY HAGFORS GXDB48A------------------------
8 oz Taco chips; crushed
1 lb Hamburger; browned
1 md Onion; chopped
1 cn Tomato soup
1 pk Taco seasoning
8 oz Cottage cheese
3/4 lb Cheddar cheese; grated
Lettuce; shredded
Tomatoes; chopped
Crush taco chips and place in lightly-oiled 9x13-inch pan. Brown
hamburger and onion; drain. Add tomato soup and taco seasoning. Mix
and pour over chips. Spread cottage cheese over meat mixture. Add
cheddar cheese. Cover with foil. Bake 40 minutes at 350-degrees.
Serve with shredded lettuce and chopped tomatoes.
