Title: STEAK TERIYAKI
Categories: Barbeque, Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
3 lb Round or sirloin steaks
(1" Thick)
1/2 c  Soy Sauce
3 tb Brown sugar, packed
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Dry mustart
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Black pepper, coarse ground
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ea 12 oz can flat beer
2 tb Salad oil
Combine marinade ingredients and pour over steak. Cover and
refrigerate 12-24 hours.

Grill 3-4 inches from hot coals, basting frequently with marinade, for
about 20 minutes (10 minutes each side) or to desired doneness. Slice
steak thinly on the diagnonal before serving.

Source: Kansas City Barbeque Society, The Passion of Barbeque
