Title: TERIYAKI STEAK APPETIZERS
Categories: Appetizers, Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 lb Steak, sirloin
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/4 c  Honey, liquid
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Onion, finely chopped
1 ea Garlic clove, crushed
1/2 ts Ginger
Cut steak into long 1/16 inch strips across the grain. Combine
remaining ingredients in large flat pan; add steak strips. Marinate
strips for several hours. Lift strips out of marinade; shake to
remove excess marinade.  Thread strips on metal skewers.  Place on
grill; broil over mediaum heat just until well browned, turning and
brushing with marinade. Serve hot.
