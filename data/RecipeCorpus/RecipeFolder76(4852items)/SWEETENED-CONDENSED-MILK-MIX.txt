Title: SWEETENED CONDENSED MILK MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Hot Water
1 1/4 c  Instant Dry Milk -- nonfat
Ok
Combine sugar and hot water in a blender and blend for 1 minute,
pulsing on and off until sugar has partially dissolved. With blender
running, add instant dry milk powder slowly. Makes about 1 1/2 cups.
Store, covered, in refrigerator if not used immediately.  Can be used
in all recipes.
