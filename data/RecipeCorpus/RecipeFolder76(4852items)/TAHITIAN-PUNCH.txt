Title: TAHITIAN PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Quart
1 1/4 c  Sliced banana
1 1/4 c  Sliced strawberries, chilled
1 1/4 c  Sliced peeled papaya,
-chilled
1 c  Sliced peeled mango, chilled
1 c  Orange juice, chilled
1 c  Crushed ice
1    Carton (8-ounce) plain
-nonfat yogurt
Directions: Place all ingredients in a blender, and process until
smooth. Serve immediately. Yield: 1-1/2 quarts (serving size: 1 cup).

Nutritional Info: CALORIES 107 (4% from fat); PROTEIN 3.3g; FAT 0.5g
(sat 0.1g, mono 0.1g, poly 0.1g); CARB 24.4g; FIBER 2.7g; CHOL 1mg;
IRON 0.3mg; SODIUM 31mg; CALC 95mg
