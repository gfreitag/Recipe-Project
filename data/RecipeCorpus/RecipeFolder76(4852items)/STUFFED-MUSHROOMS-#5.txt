Title: STUFFED MUSHROOMS #5
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Butter
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1/2 c  Finely chopped green pepper
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1 pk (3-oz) cream cheese, soft
1/3 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Worchestershire Sauce
16    Mushroom caps
Paprika
Oven method: (27 minutes cooking time)  Melt butter in small skillet
over medium heat, about 1 minute, add onion, green pepper, and
celery. Saute' until tender, about 6 minutes. Combine onion mixture,
cream cheese, bread crumbs, salt, pepper, lemon juice, and
worcestershire sauce in bowl. Mix well.  Fill mushroom caps heaping
full of mixture. Place in greased 8 inch square baking pan. Sprinkle
with paprika. Bake in 350-degree oven for 20 minutes. Serve with
coctail picks.
