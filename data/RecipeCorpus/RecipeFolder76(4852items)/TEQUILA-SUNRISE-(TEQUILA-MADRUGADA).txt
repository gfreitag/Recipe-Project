Title: TEQUILA SUNRISE (TEQUILA MADRUGADA)
Categories: Mixed, Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 oz Tequila
1/2 oz Grenadine
4 oz Orange juice -- or to fill
Ice cubes
Fill a highball glass with ice. Add tequila and fill with orange
juice; stir. Slowly pour in tequila and let it settle. Before
serving, stir very gently once, to create the "sunset" effect.
