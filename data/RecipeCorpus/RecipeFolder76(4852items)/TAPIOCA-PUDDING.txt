Title: TAPIOCA PUDDING
Categories: Puddings, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Egg
1 c  Milk
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Tapioca
1/8 ts Salt
1/2 ts Vanilla
2 tb Sugar
*******

Separate the egg, mix egg yolk, milk, sugar, tapioca, salt in sauce
pan. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until mixture boils
for 3 min. Cool, stir in vanilla. Beat egg white until foamy, beating
constantly. Add remaining sugar a little at a time. Beat egg white
until stiff, fold beaten egg white into pudding. Chill and serve.
