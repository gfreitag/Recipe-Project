Title: SYR I CHESNOK POD MAYONEM
Categories: Russian, Sauces, Ethnic, Cheese, Vegetables
Yield: 2 Cups
3 ea Garlic cloves, minced
5 tb Mayonaise
1/2 ts Salt
8 oz Muenster cheese, grated
1 tb Chives chopped
Mix the cheese, garlic, and mayonaise together in a bowl. Stir in the
chives & salt. Blend this mixture well. Refrigerate and whenservi9ng
allow to approach room temp. Excellent spread!
