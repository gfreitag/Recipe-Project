Title: SWISS CHEESE~ SOUR CREAM & CHIVE OMELET **K
Categories: Main dish, Eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Sour cream
2 ea Slices Swiss cheese
1 ea Basic omelet
1 tb Chives, chopped
1 x  Salt and pepper
Prepare Basic Omelet.  Place sour cream and Swiss cheese inside
omelet. Add salt, pepper, and chives.
Fold and serve.   Variation on Swiss Cheese and Sour Cream Omelet in
The Frugal Gourmet cookbook.  (KXMT69B)
