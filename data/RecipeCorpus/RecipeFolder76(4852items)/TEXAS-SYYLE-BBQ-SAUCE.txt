Title: TEXAS SYYLE BBQ SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 lb Butter
1 c  Water
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Pepper
1 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Cayenne
1 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
2    Cloves garlic -- minced
1    Juice
2    Bay leaf -- crushed
1 lg Onion -- grated
Lemon
Melt butter. Add vinegar, water, ketup, worcestershire and lemon
juice. Mix mustard, chili powder, cayenne, pepper, salt, sugar and
bay leaves. Add to other mixture. Add garlic and onion. Cook together
for about 10 min. makes about 2 cups, enough to swab 4-5 pounds of
meat.
