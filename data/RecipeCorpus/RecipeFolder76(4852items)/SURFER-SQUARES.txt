Title: SURFER SQUARES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 24 Cookies
1 c  Butterscotch pieces
1/4 c  Brown sugar;(granulated)
1/4 c  Butter
1 ea Egg
3/4 c  Flour; all-purpose
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Semi-sweet chocolate pieces
1 c  Miniature marshmallows
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
1 ts Vanilla extract
Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.  In large saucepan melt
butterscotch pieces, sugar and butter over medium heat, stirring
constantly. Remove from heat.  Add egg; beat well. Add flour, baking
powder and salt. Stir in remaining ingredients.  Spread in greased
8-inch pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes. Cool; cut into
bars. Makes 24.
