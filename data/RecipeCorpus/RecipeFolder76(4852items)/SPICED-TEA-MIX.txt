Title: SPICED TEA MIX
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Jar (9 oz) powdered orange
Breakfast drink
1    Jar (4 oz) lemon flavored
Iced tea mix
1 1/2 c  Sugar (or to taste)
2 ts Ground cinnamon
2 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Ground ginger
Mix drink mix, tea mix, sugar and spices.  Label and store in tightly
covered container at room temperature. Store no longer than 6 months.

To serve:  For each serving, mix 3 teaspoons with boiling liquid* in
mug until mix is dissolved. Garnish with twist of lemon or orange
peel, and a cinnamon stick if desired.

*Variation:  Use cranberry juice, or dry red or white wine.

Makes 5 1/4  cups of mix. Recommended by friend Typed by Gail/Dale
