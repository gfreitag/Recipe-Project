Title: SUNFLOWER LOAF
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Easy
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds; ground
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1 c  Walnuts; ground
1/2 c  Potato, raw; grated
1 ts Salt
1 c  Soy milk
3 tb Onion; grated
1 tb Oil
Mix all ingredients well. Let stand covered for 1/2 hour. Bake at 350
degrees for one hour.
