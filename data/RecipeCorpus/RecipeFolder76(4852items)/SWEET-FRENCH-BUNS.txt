Title: SWEET FRENCH BUNS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Yeast
3 tb Butter
1 c  Milk, scalded
1/4 c  Lukewarm water
4 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
1    Egg
1/4 c  Sugar
Dissolve yeast and 1 tbsp. sugar in lukewarm water. Add 1-1/2 cups
flour. Beat until smooth.  Set aside to rise for 15 minutes. Add the
remaining ingredients.

Bake in hot oven for 15 minutes.
