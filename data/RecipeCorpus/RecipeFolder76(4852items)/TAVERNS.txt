Title: TAVERNS
Categories: Beef grnd, Crockpot, Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
MMMMM------------------------ELAINE RADIS-----------------------------
2 1/2 lb Lean ground beef
1 lg Onion; quartered
1 c  Catsup
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
2 c  Beef broth or water
8    Hamburger rolls; toasted
In a skillet, brown ground beef and drain well. Place ground beef and
remaining ingredients except hamburger rolls in the crockpot. Cover
and cook on low for 6 to 12 hours. Taste for seasoning before
serving. Serve spooned over toasted hamburger buns
