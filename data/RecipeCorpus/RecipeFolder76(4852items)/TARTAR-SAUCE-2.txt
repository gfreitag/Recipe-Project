Title: TARTAR SAUCE 2
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 10 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise Or Salad Dressing
1 tb Lemon Juice
1 lg Dill Pickle; Cut Into Pieces
1/4 sm Onion
1 lg Egg; Hard Cooked, Quartered
1 tb Capers; Drained
1 ts Chives; Snipped
Put all of the ingredients into a blender container and blend until
chopped.  Chill.  Serve with fish.

Makes 2 cups.
