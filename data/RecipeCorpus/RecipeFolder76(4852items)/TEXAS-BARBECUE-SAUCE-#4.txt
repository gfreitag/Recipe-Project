Title: TEXAS BARBECUE SAUCE #4
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 md Onions
2 tb Salad oil
1    12 oz
1/2    Bottle
1/2    Lemon
1 sm Pod
1/2    36 oz can
1/4 cn Ro-Tel green chiles
1/8    Box brown sugar
1 ds Salt
Bottle catsup
Worcestershire sauce
Garlic
Tomato juice
Brown onions in oil; add remaining ingredients. Cook about 30 minutes.

Lillian Swanson
