Title: SWEET YEAST DOUGH
Categories: Breads
Yield: 16 Servings
3/4 c  Cream, heavy
3 tb Milk, canned
2    Egg yolks
6 tb Butter
3 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 ts Cinnamon, ground
3/4 ts Nutmeg, ground
2 1/2 c  Flour, bread
1/2 tb Yeast
"Sandra Farace says she uses this recipe for everything from a
stollen to cinnamon or caramel rolls. Sandra has been baking yeast
breads for 23 years and does NOT use a bread machine.  I have adaped
her rcipe for machines."
