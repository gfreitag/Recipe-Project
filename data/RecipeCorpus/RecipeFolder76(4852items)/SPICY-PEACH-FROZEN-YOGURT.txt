Title: SPICY PEACH FROZEN YOGURT
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Peaches
2/3 c  Sugar
3 c  Yogurt
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/8 ts Cloves
1 ts Vanilla
Peel and chop peaches and add sugar. Set aside for several hours. Beat
smooth and stir in yogurt. Stir in nutmeg, cinnamon, cloves and
vanilla. Chill and stir freeze.

Makes about 1 quart.

Note: This recipe is for a 1-quart machine. Double or triple
ingredients if needed for your machine.

From: Steve Herrick Source: [Ice Cream! The Whole Scoop, by Gail
Damerow, Glenbridge Publishing] Source: [Best Recipes - July/August
1991]
