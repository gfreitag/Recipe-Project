Title: STRAWBERRY-BANANA BREAKFAST TREAT
Categories: Beverages, Breakfast, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Orange juice
3 tb Nonfat dry milk powder
1/4    Banana; cut into pieces
10    Fresh strawberries; hulled
2    Ice cubes
In food processor or blender, combine all ingredients except ice.
Cover and blend on high speed.  With machine running, drop in ice,
one cube at a time. Blend about 10 seconds or until thick and frothy.
To serve, pour into glasses.

Per serving: About 60 cal, 2 g pro, 13 g car, 0 g fat, 0% cal from
fat, 1 mg chol, 19 mg sod.

Source: Woman's Day 'Low-Fat Meals' (Volume IV, Number 3) Typed for
you by Karen Mintzias
