Title: SWEET MUSTARD SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Spreads
Yield: 12 Servings
1/4 c  Mustard Dijon
2 tb Vinegar rice wine
2 tb Mirin
1/4 c  Oil, vegetable
Place mustard in a small bowl. Gradually whisk in the vinegar, then
the mirin and finally the oil. Whisk until smooth. Makes about 2/3
cup. Will keep a short time if refrigerated. Serve with chicken,
poached fish, grilled flank steak.
