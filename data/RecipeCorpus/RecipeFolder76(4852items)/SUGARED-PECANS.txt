Title: SUGARED PECANS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Packed brown sugar
1/2 c  Pecan halves or pieces
Melt butter in a small saucepan over low heat.  Stir in brown sugar
just until melted.  Add pecan halves or pieces. Stir until pecans are
coated with the syrup. (Can be used as a garnish for Pecan Pie)

Origin:  Hearth and Home Companion Shared by: Sharon Stevens
