Title: SWEET 'N' SOUR PORK
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Fresh Pork Shoulder *
1 ea Med. Onion, Sliced
8 oz (1 cn) Pineapple Chunks
1/4 c  Packed Brown Sugar
3 tb Cornstarch
2 tb Lemon Juice
1 tb Imported Soy Sauce
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1/8 ts Ground Ginger
1 ea Small Green Pepper **
6 oz (1 Pk) Frozen Pea Pods
Pork shoulder should be cut into 1/2-inch cubes. ** Green pepper
*
should be seeded and cut into 1-inche pieces.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------------ Mix pork, onion, pineapple (with juice), brown
sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, soy sauce, salt, pepper and ginger in
2-qt casserole. Cover and microwave on medium (50%) until pork is no
longer pink, 24 to 28 minutes, stirring every 3 minutes. Stir in
green pepper and pea pods. Cover and microwave on high (100%) green
pepper and pea pods are tender, 3 to 4 minutes.
