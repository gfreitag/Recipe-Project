Title: SUNNY PEANUT-OAT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  PEANUT BUTTER
1/2 c  HONEY
1/4 ts SALT
1 1/2 c  ROLLED OATS
1/2 c  SUNFLOWER SEED, UNSALTED
In a medium-sized bowl stir together peanut butter, honey, and salt
until well mixed. Stir in oats and seeds. Drop by teaspoon onto
ungreased cookie sheet. Bake at 350F for 10 minutes or until brown.
