Title: SPINACH & APPLE SALAD W/ BACON DRESSING
Categories: Salads
10 oz fresh spinach  -- torn in
bite sized p
Yield: 6 Servings
:
4    bacon slices
1 TB vegetable oil
1 sm onion  -- diced
2 TB cider vinegar
1 ts brown mustard
1/2 ts sugar
1/4 ts salt
1/8 ts pepper
1    apple  -- cored and thinly
sli
:

Place spinach in large bowl. Cook bacon in large skillet over medium
high heat until crisp. Remove to paper toweling to drain. Discard all
but 3 Tablespoons drippings from skillet. Add oil to drippings in
skillet and heat.  Add onion; cook until tender, about 3 minutes.
Stir in vinegar, mustard, sugar, salt and pepper. Bring to boiling.
Remove from heat. Add

apple to spinach.  Pour hot dressing over spinach and apple; toss
well to coat.  Crumble bacon and sprinkle over salad. Serve
immediately.
