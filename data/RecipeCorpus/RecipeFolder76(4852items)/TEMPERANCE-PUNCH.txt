Title: TEMPERANCE PUNCH
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Batch
2 cn Frozen orange juice, large
2 cn Frozen lemonade, large
8 cn Water
2 c  Grenadine
1/2 c  Lemon juice
3 qt Ginger ale; chilled
Mix together; float orange slices on top. Add cherries.

FOR FOUNTAIN:  Strain orange juice & lemonade before adding to
electric fountain.

Beautiful color & taste.
