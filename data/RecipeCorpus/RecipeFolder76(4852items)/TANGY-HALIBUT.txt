Title: TANGY HALIBUT
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Plain low-fat yogurt
1/2 ts Minced or crushed garlic
4 tb Lemon juice
2    Halibut fillets or similar
-whitefish, 3/4 to l-inch
-thick
Turn broiler on. In bowl or pitcher blend yogurt, garlic and lemon
juice. Line broiler pan with foil and place fish on it. Spread half
the sauce on fish, broil 10 minutes, then check for doneness (yogurt
topping will be light golden and fish can be flaked with fork). Broil
longer if necessary, but do not overcook. Transfer fish to warm
plates and serve. Put remaining yogurt in pitcher and pass

Makes  two servings.

Nutrients: calories 341, protein 47.5 grams, fat 7.4 grams,
cholesterol 68.4 mg, fiber 0.13 grams, sodium
251    mg.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
