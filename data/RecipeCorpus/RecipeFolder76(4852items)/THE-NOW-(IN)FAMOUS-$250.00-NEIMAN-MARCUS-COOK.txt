Title: THE NOW (IN)FAMOUS $250.00 NEIMAN MARCUS COOK
Categories: Dessert, Cookies, Cakes & pie
2 c  butter
Yield: 112 Servings
2 ts baking soda
5 c  blended oatmeal
2 c  brown sugar
8 oz Hershey bar -- grated
2 ts baking powder
2 ts vanilla
4 c  flour
2 c  sugar
24 oz chocolate chips
1 ts salt
4    eggs
3 c  chopped nuts (your choice)

Cream butter and both sugars. Add eggs and vanilla; mix together with
flour, oatmeal, salt, baking powder and baking soda. Add chocolate
chips, Hershey bar and nuts. Roll into bars and place 2-inches apart
on a cookie sheet. Bake for 10 minutes at 375-degrees. Note: (Measure
oatmeal and blend in a blender to a fine powder). Makes 112 cookies.
