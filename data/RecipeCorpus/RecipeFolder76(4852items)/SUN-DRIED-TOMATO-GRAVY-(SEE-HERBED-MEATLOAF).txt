Title: SUN DRIED TOMATO GRAVY (SEE HERBED MEATLOAF)
Categories: Sauces and, Cooking lig
Yield: 5 Servings
1/4 c  Sun-dried tomatoes -- about
1 c  Boiling water
2 1/2 tb Flour
1 1/4 c  1% low-fat milk
1/4 c  Meat loaf pan drippings or
Beef broth
1 tb Green onion -- finely
Chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Basil
1/8 ts Pepper
Original:  Reconstitute tomatoes in water, covered, 15 minutes. Drain
well. Chop finely. Set aside. Place flour in a small saucepan over
medium heat; gradually add milk, stirring with a whisk until blended.
Stir in tomatoes, drippings, and remaining ingredients. Cook over
medium heat 10 minutes or until thickened, whisking constantly.

: Cooking Light, Mar 1996, page 84
