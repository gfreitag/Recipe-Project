Title: SQUASH DRESSING (M_C-TX)
Categories: Dressings, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Yellow squash
1/4 c  Water
6 oz Pkg cornbread mix
-baked as directed
2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Chopped celery
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 tb Margarine
1 ts Black pepper
10 oz Can cream of chicken soup
2 ts Poultry seasoning
Preheat over to 350 .  Cook squash in water covered until tender;
drain and mash.  Crumble bread into milk, setaside. Saute celery and
onion in margarine until tender. Add pepper, soup, poultry seasoning
and squash. Stir in cornbread mixture.  Pour in 2 quart casserole.
Bake 1 hour. Very good.
