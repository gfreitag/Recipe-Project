Title: SPICY LENTIL - POTATO STEW
Categories: Stew, Soup, Vegetarian, Mcdougall
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Lentils (uncooked)
1 c  Chopped carrots
1/4 c  Chopped celery leaves
3    Cloves chopped garlic
1/2 ts Dried basil
2 c  Tomato juice
2 md Potatoes (don't peel)
-(chop into 1/2" cubes)
1/2 c  Chopped celery
1 tb Chili powder (or less)
1/2 ts Dried oregano
3/4 c  Water
Throw it all in a large sauce pan, mix it up, and bring it to a boil.
Cover, reduce heat and simmer til veggies are tender (about 45
minutes). Hope you like it, Michele
