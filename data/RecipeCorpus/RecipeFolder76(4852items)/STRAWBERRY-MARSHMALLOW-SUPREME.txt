Title: STRAWBERRY MARSHMALLOW SUPREME
Categories: Pies, Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 8 Servings
1    9 inch grapham cracker pie
-shell
1 c  Milk, scalded
12    Marshmallows, chopped
1 c  Thick cream
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
2 c  Crushed strawberries,
-drained
To scalded milk, add marshmallow pieces.  Let cool to lukewarm. Whip
the cream and blend into milk-marshmallow mixture. Fold in nuts and
berries. Pour into pie shell and chill 8-12 hours.
