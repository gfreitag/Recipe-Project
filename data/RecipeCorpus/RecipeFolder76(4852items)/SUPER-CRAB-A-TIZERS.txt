Title: SUPER CRAB-A-TIZERS
Categories: Fish, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Dozen
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
Vegetable oil
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
2 cn (120g each) Crab meat,
Drained
1 cn (10oz/284ml) Mushrooms,
Drained, chopped
16    Dare Breton crackers,
Crushed
8    Eggs, beaten
In large saucepan saute onion and green pepper in small amount of hot
oil until tender; remove from heat. Add cheese, crab meat, mushrooms
and cracker crumbs to pan; toss well. Stir in beaten eggs. Spoon
mixture into greased miniature muffin pans or tart tins. Bake in 350F
(180C) oven for 15 minutes or until done. Makes 3 to 4 dozen.

Source: Recipes Plus, Vol.4
