Title: SPINACH CRUST
Categories: Vegetarian, Crust
Yield: 8 Servings
3/4 lb Spinach, chopped very fine
3 tb Margarine
3/4 c  Flour, unbleached white
3/4 c  Wheat germ
1/4 ts Salt
ds Nutmeg
Melt the margarine in a castiron skillet, add the spinach, and cook
it quickly over fairly hgh heat--stirring--until it is just limp.

Remove from heat; add remaining ingredients, and mix well.

Pat into an oiled 9" pie pan. Use a fork first, and then your
fingers, to mold the crust.

Prebake for 15 minutes at 375 degrees.

Adapted from The Enchanted Broccoli Forest by Mollie Katzen, MM by
Dianne Smith/DEEANNE
