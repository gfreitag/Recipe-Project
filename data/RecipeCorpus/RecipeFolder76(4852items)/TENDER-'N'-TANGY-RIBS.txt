Title: TENDER 'N' TANGY RIBS
Categories: Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 3 Servings
3/4    To 1 cup vinegar
1/2 c  Ketchup
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 ts Ground mustard
1 ts Paprika
1/2    To 1 tsp. salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 lb Pork spareribs
1 tb Vegetable oil
Combine the first nine ingredients in a slow cooker. Cut ribs into
serving-size pieces; brown in skillet in oil.  Transfer to slow
cooker. Cover and cook on low for 4-6 hours or until tender.  Yield
2-3 servings. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source: Taste
of Home Quick Cooking
