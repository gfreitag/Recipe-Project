Title: TACO BURGERS
Categories: Burgers
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Corn chips, crushed
1 pk Taco seasoning
1 lb Hamburger
1 cn Tomatoes
1 lg Tomato
1 c  Cheese, shredded
1 c  Lettuce, shredded
Combine egg and 1/4 cup water. Stir in chips and half of seasoning
mix. Add beef. Mix well. Add tomato sauce, and rest of seasoning mix.
Make patties. Serve with tomato, lettuce and cheese.
