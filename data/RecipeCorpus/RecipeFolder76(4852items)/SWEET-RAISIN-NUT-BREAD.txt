Title: SWEET RAISIN NUT BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Active Dry Yeast
3 tb Sugar
3 c  Bread Flour
3/4 c  Warm Milk
1/4 c  Warm Water
2 lg Eggs, Room Temperature
4 tb Butter, Melted Then Cooled
1/4 c  Honey
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Raisins
1/3 c  Golden Raisins
3/4 c  Coarsely Chopped Nuts
Simply dump in all ingredients in the order listed, select white
bread and push start.  We've also used pistachios and peanuts.
