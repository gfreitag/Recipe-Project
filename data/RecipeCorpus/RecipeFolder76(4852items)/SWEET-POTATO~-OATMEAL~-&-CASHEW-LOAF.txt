Title: SWEET POTATO~ OATMEAL~ & CASHEW LOAF
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Cashews
1 1/2 c  Rolled oats
1 1/2 c  Sweet potato; grated
1/2 c  Onion; finely chopped
1 c  ;water
2 tb Tamari
2    Garlic cloves; pressed
2 tb Nutritional yeast
1/2 c  Tofu, soft
Grind the cashews in a blender.

Place the ground cashews in a large bowl. Add the remaining
ingredients and mix well.

Generously oil a loaf pan and sprinkle it with additional rolled
oats to keep the loaf from sticking.
Pack the loaf mixture into the pan.

Bake at 350 degrees for about 45 minutes, or until firm and brown.

Serve with your favorite sauce a salad and a steamed vegetable.
