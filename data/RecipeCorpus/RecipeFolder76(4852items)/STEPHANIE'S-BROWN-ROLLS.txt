Title: STEPHANIE'S BROWN ROLLS
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Sugar
1 c  Warm Water
1 tb Yeast
1 c  Oatmeal
2 tb Oil
2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Molasses
1 c  Boiling Water
1 c  Cold Water
6 c  Flour
Dissolve 1 tsp sugar in cup warm water Sprinkle 1 tbsp yeast on top

Mix in bowl 1 cup oatmeal 2 tbsp oil 2 tsp salt 3/4 cup molasses

Add 1 cup boiling water 1 cup cold water

with fork stir yeast, add to mixture.

Add 6 - 6.5 cups of flour.

Knead

Let rise to twice size.  Punch down. Make into rolls. Dob with
margarine on top. Let rise. Bake 350F for 15+ minutes.
