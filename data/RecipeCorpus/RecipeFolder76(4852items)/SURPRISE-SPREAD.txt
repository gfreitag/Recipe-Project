Title: SURPRISE SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Holiday, Kraft, Seafood, Val's
Yield: 10 Servings
1 x  Cream Cheese (250gr)(8oz)
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Seafood cocktail sauce
8 oz Shrimp, cooked, small
2 c  Shredded mozzerella cheese
1 x  Green pepper, diced
3 x  Green onion, sliced
1 x  Tomato, diced
Beat together the cream cheese, sour cream and mayo. Spread over 12"
platter, sprinkle remaining ingredients over mixture in order given.
Chill at least 1 hour to blend flavors. Serve with crackers.

Served at Georges' staff party. She reserved a little mozzerella to
sprinkle on top. Looks very nice. Tastes great.
