Title: STRAWBERRIES ELEGANTE
Categories: Desserts, Fruits, Alcohol, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
-Karen Thackeray
6 c  Strawberry slices; fresh
2 tb Orange flavored liqueur; OR
-orange juice
8 oz Light cream cheese
3 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Orange flavored liqueur; OR
-orange juice
1 tb Skim milk
Toss strawberries with 2 tb liqueur in small bowl. Place cream cheese
product, brown sugar, 1 tb liqueur and milk in food processor or
blender container; process until well blended. Serve over
strawberries. Garnish with fresh mint leaves, if desired.
170 calories; 7 g fat; 220 mg sodium; 20 mg cholesterol Source: No
Guilt Desserts
