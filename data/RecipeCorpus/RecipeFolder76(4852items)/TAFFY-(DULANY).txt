Title: TAFFY (DULANY)
Categories: Candies, Snacks, Desserts, Candies, Snacks
Yield: 24 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar
3 tb Butter
1 c  Water
1 ts Vanilla
Cook sugar, water, vinegar and butter without stirring, to hard-ball
stage. Remove from heat, add vanilla, and pour on buttered platter.
Let stand until cool enough to pull. This may be tinted with food
coloring.

From the Kitchen of: Mildred Dulany  (Alvadore Christian Church
Cookbook) Shared by: Gary & Margie Hartford, Eugene, OR (1:152/19)
