Title: STEWED RHUBARB
Categories: Fruits, Preserves
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Fresh rhubarb, cut into
1-inch pieces
1/2 c  Sugar
Add rhubarb to a saucepan with a small amount of water and simmer
for 5 minutes. Add sugar and simmer for an additional 5 minutes or
until sugar is dissolved.
This is great in ice cream, with plain low-fat yogurt, on pancakes,
with custard or all by itself.
