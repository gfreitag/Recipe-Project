Title: SWEET POTATOES & BROCCOLI - VEGETARIAN COOK
Categories: Diabetic, Vegetarian, Lacto
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Sweet potato; diced
1/2 c  -water
1 c  Broccoli; chopped, cooked
1/2 c  Cottage cheese, low fat
1 tb Sesame seed
In a covered saucepan cook sweet potato in 1/2 c water until almost
done. Add chopped broccoli and extra water if needed. Toss cottage
cheese and sesame seeds with vegetables until heated through.

1 serving = 362 cal, 2 meat. 2 veg, 2 bread, 1 fat
