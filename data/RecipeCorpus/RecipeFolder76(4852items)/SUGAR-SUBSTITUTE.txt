Title: SUGAR SUBSTITUTE
Categories: Hints, Snacks, Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM--------------------CONFECTIONERS SUGAR-------------------------
1 c  Granulated sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
MMMMM------------------------BROWN SUGAR-----------------------------
1 c  Granulated sugar
1 tb Molasses
Confectioners sugar: mix ingredients in a blender for about 45
seconds. Brown sugar: add one or two tablespoons molasses to one cup
sugar and mix well.
