Title: SPICY SHRIMP SPREAD
Categories: Appetizers, Seafood, Jaw
Yield: 1 Party
1    8 oz pkg. cream cheese
2 tb Chopped onion
2 tb Sour cream
1 tb Horseradish
1/4 ts Salt
Dash of pepper
8 oz Cooked shrimp, chopped
Blend first 5 ingredients, then add chopped shrimp. Spoon into shallow
baking dish and bake at 375 for 20 minutes. Serve hot or cold with
crackers. Or use as a stuffing for cherry tomatoes or mushrooms
