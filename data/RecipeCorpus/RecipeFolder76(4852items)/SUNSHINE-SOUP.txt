Title: SUNSHINE SOUP
Categories: Soups, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Squash, cooked and mashed
3    Onions, chopped
1 c  Celery, chopped
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/2 ts Rosemary
1    Qt. chicken stock
1/4 ts Black pepper
2 c  Skim milk
From "Recipes to Lower Your Fat Thermostat" by La Rene Gaunt.

Garnish nutmeg

Combine all ingredients except milk in a soup pot. Cook until onions
and celery are tender.Remove from heat, add milk, sprinkle with
nutmeg and serve immediately.
