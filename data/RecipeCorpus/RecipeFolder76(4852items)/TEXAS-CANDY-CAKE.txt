Title: TEXAS CANDY CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
8 oz Candied cherries, qrtrd.
8 oz Candied pineapple, chopped
8 oz Dates, chopped
8 oz Coconut (or 7 oz pkg.)
4 1/2 c  Nuts, walnuts, pecans, etc.
3 tb Flour
1 cn Condensed milk
Toss fruit and nuts with flour.  Mix in milk thoroughly until all is
well coated.  Press into sprayed bread pans and bake at 250 degrees
1-1/2 hours, or till light brown. Let cool in pans until completely
cool, then slice. Best if it mellows in refrigerator about 2 weeks.
