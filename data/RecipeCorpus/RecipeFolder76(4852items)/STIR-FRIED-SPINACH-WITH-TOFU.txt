Title: STIR-FRIED SPINACH WITH TOFU
Categories: Asian, Vegetables, Rice, Tofu
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Fresh spinach
2 tb Peanut oil
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 lb Firm tofu -- in 1/2" cubes
1 tb Rice wine or dry sherry
Wash and thoroughly dry the spinach. Remove any tough stems. Place a
wok over medium-high heat. When it begins to smoke, add the peanut
oil and the garlic. Stir briefly, then add the spinach and salt.
Stir-fry just until the spinach is wilted. about 1 minute. Gently
stir in the tofu and sprinkle with rice wine. Cook until the tofu is
heated through, about 1 minute. Serve immediately.

: Joanne Hush, Classic Chinese Cooking, pg 144
