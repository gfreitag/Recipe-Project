Title: SPINACH PESTO SAUCE
Categories: Vegetarian, Vegetables, Nuts
Yield: 2 Cups
1 1/2 c  Cleaned spinach;
1/4 c  Pine nuts;
->OR<-
1/4 c  Cashew pieces;
1/2 ts Salt;
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Dry sweet basil;
Put all the ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. The acid
in the spinach will pick up the garlic flavor which is the reason for
using less garlic in this formula. Keep this sauce cold until ready
to use.
