Title: STRAWBERRY JAM3
Categories: Canning, Preserves, Etc.
Yield: 1 Servings
2 qt Fresh or frozen
Strawberries
Unsweetened
1 qt White sugar
Thaw & mash strawberries.  Add white sugar.  Boil for 8 minutes in a
laege pot.  Remove from stove & let cool. Stir & skim off any foam.
Bottle in sterilized jars.
