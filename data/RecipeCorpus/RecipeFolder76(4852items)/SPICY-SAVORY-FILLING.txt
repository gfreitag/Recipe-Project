Title: SPICY SAVORY FILLING
Categories: Sandwiches, Fillings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Minced cooked bacon
1/2 c  Peanut butter
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
Few grains curry powder
Ketchup
:    Mix together the bacon, peanut butter, Worcestershire sauce, and
curry powder. Moisten with the ketchup. Does not keep long. Makes 1
c. Jeannie Allen
