Title: ST. PADDY'S DAY PUNCH *
Categories: Ice cream, Holiday, Punch
Yield: 36 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
1/2 ga Vanilla ice cream
1/2 pt Whole milk
24 oz Gingerale
Peppermint -OR- creme de
- menthe flavoring
Green food coloring
Soften ice cream in punch bowl with milk. Add peppermint flavoring to
taste. At last minute, add gingerale and green food coloring.
