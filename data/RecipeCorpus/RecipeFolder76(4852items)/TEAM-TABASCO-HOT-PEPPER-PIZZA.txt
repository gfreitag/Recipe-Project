Title: TEAM TABASCO HOT PEPPER PIZZA
Categories: Pizza, Maindish, Snacks
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Vegetable oil
1    Red bell pepper, cut
-into thin strips
1    Green bell pepper, cut
-into thin strips
1    Yellow bell pepper, cut
-into thin strips
1/2 c  Sliced pimento-stuffed
-olives
1 1/2 ts Tabasco pepper sauce
10 oz Package prepared pizza crust
1 c  Shredded white Cheddar
-cheese
Preheat oven to 450 degree F.  In 12-inch skillet over medium heat,
cook bell peppers 5 minutes or until tender-crisp.  Stir in olives
and Tabasco sauce. Spoon mixture onto pizza crust.  Sprinkle top with
cheese.

Bake pizza on pizza pan or pizza stone for 10 minutes or until cheese
is melted and crust is crisp.  Makes 4 servings. Nutrition
information per serving: 305 Calories, 10 g protein, 18 g fat, 400 mg
sodium, 30 mg cholesterol
