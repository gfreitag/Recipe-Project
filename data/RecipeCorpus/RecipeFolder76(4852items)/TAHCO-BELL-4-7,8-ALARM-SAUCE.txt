Title: TAHCO BELL 4 7/8 ALARM SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 8 Servings
6 oz Tomato Paste
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Soy Sauce
1 ts Onion salt
1/4 c  Undrained pickle relish
1 tb Postum or Caramel Coloring
1 tb Tobasco sauce (to taste)
1 ts Dark molasses
1 ts Mustard
1/4 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Water
1/2 ts Cumin
10 1/2 oz Tomato soup
1 ts Black pepper
Stir over low heat to blend all flavors.
