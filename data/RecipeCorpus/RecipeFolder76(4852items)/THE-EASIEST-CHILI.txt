Title: THE EASIEST CHILI
Categories: Easy, Meats, Beans
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground meat, lean
3    Green pepper strips, cut up
1    28 oz. can pork and beans
1/2    To 1 cup ketchup
1 ts Sweet paprika
1/2 ts Pepper
1    Onion chopped
1    Stalk celery, cut up
1    28 oz. can kidney beans
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Season salt
3 tb White vinegar
Brown meat, celery, onion and green pepper together in pan. Add
remaining ingredients except vinegar. Simmer 15-20 minutes on medium
heat. Remove from heat and add vinegar. Can be frozen. Note: Use
ketchup amount to your own taste.  Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: Cook for a Cure Cookbook.
