Title: SWEET ANISE MUSTARD
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 Cups
2 tb Anise seeds
1 tb Cardamom seeds
1 1/4 c  Mustard powder
1/2 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
1/4 c  Canola oil
Grind the seeds in a coffee grinder & transfer to a food processor.
Add the rest of the ingredients & blend until smooth. Pour into a
double boiler & cook for 10 minutes, or until the mixture is reduced
by half.

Spoon into a container, cover & refrigerate for up to
3    days before serving.

"Vegetarian Gourmet" Winter, 1995
