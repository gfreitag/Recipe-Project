Title: TACO BELL MEAT SEASONING
Categories: Spices, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Chili powder
1 1/2 ts Cumin; ground
1/2 ts Coriander; ground
1/2 ts Cilantro leaves
1 ts Instant tea powder
1/2 ts Dry minced parsley
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Onion salt
MMMMM------------------------COMBINE WITH-----------------------------
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Ground tumeric
1 ts Sugar
1 tb Paprika
1 tb Seasoned salt
Combine first group of ingredients and sift together 3 times. Funnel
into bottle with tight fitting cap. Keep out of direct sunlight and
use within 6 mos, or refrigerate to use in one year. Makes 3T of
seasoning.

For taco seasoning, use 2T of last group of ingredients <mixed
together> added to the 3T of above seasoning mixture for 1-1 1/2
pounds ground beef. Posted by Suzze Tiernan in Cooking echo Recipe by
Gloria Pitzer
