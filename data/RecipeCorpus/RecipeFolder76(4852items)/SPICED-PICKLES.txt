Title: SPICED PICKLES
Categories: Penndutch, Pickles
Yield: 1 Servings
200 ea Sm Pickles
1 lb Sugar
2 oz Mustard, dry
1 c  Salt
1 qt Cider vinegar
2 oz Cloves, whole
2 oz Cinnamon
Scald the pickles in boiling water. Drain. Mix together the cinnamon
and cloves. Place a layer of pickles in a crock and sprinkle with the
spice mixture; repeat until crock is filled. Combine the sugar, salt,
mustard and vinegar and bring to a boil and cook about 5 minutes.
Pour brine over the pickles, filling the crock. (More brine may be
needed, depending on size of pickles). Cover crock and let stand in a
cold place. Source: Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes,
Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
