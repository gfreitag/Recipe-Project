Title: SWEDISH RED CABBAGE
Categories: Vegetarian, Side dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lg Red cabbage
1    Apple; cored
1    Onion
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Honey (or alternative)
1 tb Vinegar
2 tb ;water
1/4 c  Red wine or sherry
1 c  Green peas
Sea salt
Black pepper
1. Shred cabbage, slice apple and grate the onion.
2.  Heat honey in a heavy pot, add cabbage, stirring to coat, cover
and simmer 10 minutes.
3.  Stir in rest of ingredients except peas, simmer again for 1 to 1
1/2 hours, stir occasionally.
4.  Stir in peas just before serving. This mixture will reduce to
1/2 the original size.
