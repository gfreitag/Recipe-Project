Title: SPICY TARTAR SAUCE FOR SEAFOOD
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 16 Servings
1 c  Sour cream
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 ea Garlic, cloves, crushed
1/4 c  Parsley, finely chopped
1 ts Salt
3 tb Sweet pickle relish
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Tarragon, freshly chopped,
-OR 1 ts dried tarragon
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
1 ts Vinegar
Combine all ingredients and blend well.  Serve as a dipping sauce for
shellfish, or top off grilled fish steaks (Tuna) with dollops.
