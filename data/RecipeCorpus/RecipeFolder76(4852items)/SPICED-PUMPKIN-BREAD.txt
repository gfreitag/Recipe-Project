Title: SPICED PUMPKIN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 24 Servings
1/2 tb Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
3 tb Sugar, brown
1/2 tb Salt
3 ts Pumpkin pie spice
1 c  Pumpkin
3 tb Oil
2    Eggs
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 10 o'clock. Select "white bread" and
push Start.

Tested in DAK 1-1/2 lb R2D2 bread machine.  Sylvia's comment:
