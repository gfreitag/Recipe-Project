Title: TASTY CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Poultry, Main dish, Side dishes, Meats, Untried
Yield: 10 Servings
****** Ingredients ******
3 lb Chicken wings
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Soy sauce
4 tb Brown sugar
1    Crushed clove of garlic
1/4 c  Catsup
Serves 10-12

****** Directions ******

Place cleaned, disjointed wings in foil-lined 9x13-inch pan. Sprinkle
with salt, pepper and paprika. Combine honey, soy sauce, brown sugar,
garlic and catsup. Pour over wings and bake uncovered turning and
basting every 15 mins with sauce. Bake @400 for 1 hour.
