Title: SWEET GRAHAM SNACKS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 36 Servings
12    Graham crackers, broken into
- quarters
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Ground nuts
Line a 15x10x1-inch baking pan with graham crackers. In a saucepan,
bring butter and sugar to a boil; boil for two minutes. Remove from
heat; stir in nuts. Spoon over graham crackers. Bake at 325Ã¸F for 10
minutes. Immediately remove from pan onto foil.  Cool. Break apart.
