Title: TAMER BI SUMSUM - DATES WITH SESAME SEEDS
Categories: Appetizers, Ethnic
Yield: 10 Servings
20    Dates stuffed with almonds
-(Madinah dates)
2 tb Sesame seeds
Place the sesame seeds in a small frying pan over low heat and roast
until golden, stirring constantly. Remove and cool. Roll each date in
the seeds to coat and arrange on a tray. Serve with Gahwa (Arabic
Coffee). Serves 10 people.
