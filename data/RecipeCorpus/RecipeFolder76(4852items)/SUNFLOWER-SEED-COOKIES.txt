Title: SUNFLOWER SEED COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 24 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Butter
1 c  Shortening
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Flour
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Roasted sunflower seeds
