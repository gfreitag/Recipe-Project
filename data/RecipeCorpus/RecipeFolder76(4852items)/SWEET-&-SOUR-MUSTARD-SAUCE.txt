Title: SWEET & SOUR MUSTARD SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 2 Cups
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour
4 ts Dry Mustard
2 c  Cream
2    Egg Yolks
1/2 c  Vinegar
Combine the sugar, flour and dry mustard in the top of a double
boiler over ~NOT IN- boiling water. Mix the cream and the egg yolks.
Gradually add to the mustard mixture. Cook until thickened. Gradually
stir in the vinegar.
