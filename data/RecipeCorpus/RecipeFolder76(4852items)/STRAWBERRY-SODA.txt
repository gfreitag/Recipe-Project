Title: STRAWBERRY SODA
Categories: Beverages, Frozen
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------ALCOHOL FREE ENT---------------------------
2 oz Fresh OR frozen strawberries
1 1/2 oz SIMPLE SYRUP
4 oz Cold milk
4 oz Club soda
1    Scoop strawberry ice cream
Sweetened whipped cream
Maraschino cherry
Another beauty from drugstore days.
10 oz stemmed water goblet. Liquefy in blender, strawberries and
syrup. Pour in glass and add milk, soda, and ice cream. Top with
whipped cream and cherry or fresh strawberry. Serve with straw and
long spoon. Typed by Sherilyn Schamber
