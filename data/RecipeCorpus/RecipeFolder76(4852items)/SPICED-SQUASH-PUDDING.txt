Title: SPICED SQUASH PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour
3/4 ts Salt
1 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
3 lg Eggs
1 1/2 c  Cooked, mashed winter squash
1 1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Light molasses
2 tb Melted butter or margarine
The following pudding can be made with either butternut or Hubbard
squash.

whipped cream or whipped topping (optional)

Combine first 5 ingredients.  Beat in eggs.  Stir in next 4
ingredients. Mix well.  Pour into buttered 1-1/2 quart casserole.
Bake in preheated 350 F oven 1-1/4 hours, or until firm in center.
Serve with whipped cream or whipped topping.  Serves 8.

Origin:  Hearth and Home Companion Shared by: Sharon Stevens
