Title: TABOULI 4
Categories: Low, Fat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Cracked wheat (bulgur
Wheat)
1 c  Fresh parsley
2    Cloves garlic
Juice of one lemon
1/4 ts Salt
Bring 2.5 cups water to a boil.  Add cracked wheat and simmer covered
for 25 minutes.  Let sit for 5 minutes covered. Fluff wheat with a
fork. Using a kitchen scissors, cut up parsley; add to wheat. Finely
chop garlic; add to wheat.  Squeeze lemon over salad, add salt. Mix
well. Let sit overnight.
