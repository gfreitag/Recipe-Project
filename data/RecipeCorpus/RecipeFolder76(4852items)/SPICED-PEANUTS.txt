Title: SPICED PEANUTS
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 2 1/2 cups
2 tb Margarine; melted
1/2 ts Cumin, ground
1/4 ts Red pepper, ground
12 oz Peanuts
Heat oven to 375 degrees. Stir margarine and spices until well
blended. Pour over nuts; mix well. Spoon into 13x9-inch pan. Bake 7
to 10 minutes, stirring halfway through cooking time. Submitted By
