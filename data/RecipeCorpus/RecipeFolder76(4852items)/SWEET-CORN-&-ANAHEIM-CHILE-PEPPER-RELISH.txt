Title: SWEET CORN & ANAHEIM CHILE PEPPER RELISH
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Sweet corn
1/2 c  Red Bermuda onion,dice small
1/2 c  White onions; diced small
1 c  Cucumber,peeled,seeded,diced
1/3 c  Red bell pepper; diced small
1/3 c  Bell pepper; diced small
8 tb Fresh lime juice
Salt; to taste
1 ts Tabasco sauce
1 1/2 ts Cumin
1 1/2 ts Sugar
2 fl White vinegar
2 fl Extra virgin olive oil
3    Green Anaheim chili peppers
3    Red Anaheim chili peppers;s
1    Frisee lettuce
Preparation Time: 1:15 Combine all the ingredients and allow them to
set at room temperature for one hour before using. CHEF'S NOTE:
Refrigerate overnight and the flavor builds. Spoon the relish onto
the well-washed leaves of Frisee lettuce. Serve as part of Grilled
Grouper Savoy Grill. Source:  David Leo Banks of Harry's Savoy Grill,
Wilmington, DE
