Title: ST. PATTY'S PUNCH
Categories: Punch, Alcohol, Holiday
Yield: 52 Servings
20 oz Crushed pineapple in juice;
- chilled
8 dr Green food color (opt)
18 oz Frozen lemonade concentrate;
- thawed
46 oz White grape juice; chilled
46 oz Pineapple juice; chilled
32    Ice cubes
36 oz Beer; chilled
Tint pineapple (with liquid) with 4 drops of the food color. Mix
lemonade concentrate, juices and pineapple. Tint with remaining food
color; pour on ice cubes in punch bowl. Just before serving, stir in
beer. About
52    servings (1/2 cup each).
