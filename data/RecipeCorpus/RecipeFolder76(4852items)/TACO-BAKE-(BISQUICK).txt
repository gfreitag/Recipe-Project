Title: TACO BAKE (BISQUICK)
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Casserole
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 ea Taco seasoning mix (1 1/4
Oz)
1 cn Tomato sauce (15 oz)
1 cn Whole kernel corn, drained
(15 1/4 oz)
2 c  Cheddar cheese (shredded)
2 c  "BISQUICK" Baking mix
1 c  Milk
2 ea Eggs
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Cook ground beef and onion until beef is
brown; drain.  Spoon into ungreased 13x9x2" baking dish. Stir in taco
seasoning mix (dry), tomato sauce and corn. Sprinkle with cheese.

Stir remaining ingredients until blended.  Pour over beef mixture.

Bake 35 min or until light golden brown.  Serve with sour cream,
chopped tomato and shredded lettuce if desired.

Prep time:  18 min Bake time: 35 min
