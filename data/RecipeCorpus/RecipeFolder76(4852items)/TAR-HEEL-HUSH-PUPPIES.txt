Title: TAR HEEL HUSH PUPPIES
Categories: New text im, Bread/rolls, Bread, Breadmaker, Low sodium
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Buttermilk
1 lb Fine cornmeal
1 tb Salt
1 pn Baking soda
1    Egg
1 tb Sugar
Stir, adding water, to thick consistency.  Drop in deep fat by
teaspoonfuls. Cook in temperature of 375 degrees. Serves 6. Piedmont
Region, North Carolina, USA
