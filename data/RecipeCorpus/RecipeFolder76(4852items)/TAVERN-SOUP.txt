Title: TAVERN SOUP
Categories: Soups
1/4 c  celery -- finely chopped
Yield: 7 Servings
1/4 c  carrot -- finely chopped
1/4 c  green pepper -- finely
chopped
:
1/4 c  onion -- finely chopped
41 1/4 oz canned chicken broth
2 TB butter
1 ts salt
1/4 ts pepper
1/3 c  flour
3 c  grated sharp cheddar cheese
12 oz can light beer -- at room
temperature
:

Combine celery, carrot, green pepper, and onion in slow-cooking pot.
Add broth, butter, salt, and pepper. Cover and cook on low for 5 to 6
hours. Strain mixture; puree vegetables in blender and return to pot
with broth. Turn control to high. dissolve flour in small amount of
water; add to broth. Add cheese, 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until
blended. Pour in beef. Cover and cook on high 15 to 20 minutes.
