Title: SWEET POTATO CASSEROLE ---WHNP81A
Categories: Vegetables, Holiday, Family
Yield: 12 Servings
7 ea Sweet potatoes
1/4 c  Butter (1/2 stick)
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Evaporated milk
2 ea Eggs
1 x  Cinnamon or nutmeg; to taste
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1 c  Crushed corn flakes
1/4 c  Butter (1/2 stick)
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Pecans; chopped
Boil sweet potatoes in skins until done; cool, peel, mash and mix
with remaining ingredients, except topping. Place in greased 3 qt.
casserole and cover with topping mixture. Bake 15 minutes at 450 deg.
