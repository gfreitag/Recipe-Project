Title: SWEET CARROT SALAD
Categories: Digest
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Grated carrots
1/4 c  Lime juice
2 tb Honey
1/4 ts Cayenne
Add carrots to honey-lime mixture Mix well and press lightly till
somewhat juicy

Serve at room temperature

Comments: Yummy! vary the amount cayenne according to the desired
hotness
