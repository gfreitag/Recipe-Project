Title: SPICY ORANGE BEETS
Categories: Vegetables, Cajun
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Sugar
1 cn Sliced beets, drained
1/2 c  Apple cider vinegar
2    Bay leaves
1/2 c  Orange juice
Simmer sugar , vinegar and bay leaf in saucepan for 5 minutes.
Stir in orange juice and pour over beets. Cover, chill overnight to
bland flavors. Makes about 2 cups. Two cups sliced fresh beets may be
