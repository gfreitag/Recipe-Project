Title: STIR-FRIED GARLIC SHRIMP
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lg Cloves,garlic,finely
-chopped
2 ts Vegetable oil
-I pound frozen peeled and
-deveined raw medium
-shrimp, thawed
3 c  Sliced fresh mushrooms
-(about 8 ounces)
1 c  L-inch pieces green onions
-(with tops)
1/4 c  Dry white wine
2 c  Hot cooked rice
Stir-frying makes this delicious dish especially fast and easy to
prepare.

Cook garlic in oil in 10-inch nonstick skillet over medium-high heat I
minute, stirring frequently. Add shrimp; stir-fry 1 minute. Stir in
mushrooms, onions and wine; stir-fry about 2 minutes or until shrimp
are pink and vegetables are hot. Serve  over rice.

4    servings-
