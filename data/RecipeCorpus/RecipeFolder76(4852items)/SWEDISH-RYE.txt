Title: SWEDISH RYE
Categories: Dorcas, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Rye flour
1/2 c  Dark molasses
1/4 c  Honey
2 ts Salt
2 c  Boiling water
2 pk Dry yeast
1/2 c  Warm water
5 1/2 c  White flour
Combine first 5 ingredients.  Gradually add boiling water, beat 2
minutes. Dissolve yeast in warm water.
Add yeast and 2 cups white flour to mixture. Beat at high speed 2
minutes. GrAdually add enough flour (about 2 1/2 to 3 cups) to make
soft dough. Knead 8 to 10 minutes. Place in a greased bowl. Let rise
75 to 90 minutes. Punch down and divide in half. Form 2 round loaves.
Let rise 45 to 60 minutes. Bake at 350~ for 35 to 40 minutes.
