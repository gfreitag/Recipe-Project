Title: TANGY SHRIMP NOODLE SALAD
Categories: Fish, Pasta, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Uncooked noodles (about 4
-ounces)
2 c  Coarsely chopped zucchini
-(about 2 medium)
1/2 c  Sliced celery (about I
-medium stalk) '
1/4 c  Sliced ripe olives
1 cn (4 1/4 ounces) tiny shrimp,
-rinsed and drained
-Horseradish Dressing
-(below)
-Salad greens
Cook noodles as directed on package;drain. Mix all ingredients except
salad greens Refrigerate at least 2 hours but no longer than 24
hours. Spoon onto salad greens.

4    servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
