Title: TANDOORI STYLE CHICKEN BREASTS WITH YOGURT CI
Categories: New, Text, Import
6    chicken breasts
Tandoori Marinade
Yogurt cilantro sauce
Tandoori Marinade:
Yield: 4 Servings
:
:
:
1    onion chopped coarsely
2    garlic cloves -- chopped
fine
:
2 TB lime juice
2 TB lemon juice
1 TB ground coriander
2 ts paprika
1 ts ground ginger
1/2 ts ancho chile powder
1/2 ts cascabel chile powder
1/2 ts tumeric
1/2 ts ground cloves
Salt and freshly ground
pepper to taste
:
:
2 c  plain yogurt

In a food processor, puree the onion and garlic. Process in the lemon
and lime juice, add spices and the yogurt and process to blend.
Marinate the chicken breasts in this mixture turning occasionally
overnight in the refrigerator.

Prepare a charcoal or wood fire and let it burn down to embers.

Remove the excess marinade and grill for 5 minutes on each side or
until done. Serve with YogurtCilantro sauce to taste.
