Title: SPICY COGNAC KETCHUP
Categories: Sauce, Alcohol
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/4 c  Courvoisier
2 tb Sweet butter
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
1/4 ts Allspice
1/3 ts Tabasco Sauce
Put all ingredients in small saucepan and simmer 15 minutes, stirring
often. Pour into small bowl and chill.
