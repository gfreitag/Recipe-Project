Title: SWISS STYLE OATS
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Water
1 c  Mixed dried fruit, chopped
1 ts Sea salt (optional)
1 1/3 c  AM Oat Flakes (rolled oats)
2 tb Honey
2 tb AM Wheat Germ
1/4 c  Slivered almonds, toasted
Bring water to a boil; stir in fruit, salt, and honey. Bring to boil
again. Add oats; stir; lower heat to simmer. Cover and simmer 15
minutes (stir before serving). Sprinkle each serving with 1-1/2
teaspoon wheat germ and 1 tablespoon almonds.  Serve with milk.
