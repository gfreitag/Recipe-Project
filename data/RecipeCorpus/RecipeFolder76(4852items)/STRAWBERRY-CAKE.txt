Title: STRAWBERRY CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes, Berries
Yield: 10 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1 tb Butter;or shortening
1    Egg
2/3 c  Milk
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Strawberries;crushed
1 qt Strawberries
Cook balance of quart of berries in 1 cup water and 1/2 cup sugar,
thicken with 1 tbls cornstarch. Serve over cake while still warm. Mix
all ingredients in order given and Bake in med heated oven, 350
degrees until done. try 30-35 min's?
