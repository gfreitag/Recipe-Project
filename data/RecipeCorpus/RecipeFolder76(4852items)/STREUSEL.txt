Title: STREUSEL
Categories: Breads, Crocker
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Firm margarine or butter
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/3 c  Packed brown sugar or
-granulated sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Cut margarine into flour, brown sugar and cinnamon crumbly; stir in
nuts.

Source: Betty Crocker's Cookbook, 6th Edition
