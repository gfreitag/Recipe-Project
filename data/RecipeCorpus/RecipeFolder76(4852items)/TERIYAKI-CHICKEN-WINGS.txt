Title: TERIYAKI CHICKEN WINGS
Categories: Appetizers, Poultry, Chinese
Yield: 20 Servings
10    Chicken Wings
1/2 c  Teriyaki Marinade & Sauce *
1/2 c  Dry Sherry
1/4 c  Honey
2 tb Vegetable Oil
1 ts Dry Mustard
1 ts Ground Ginger
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
*  This recipe came from "Best Recipes - La Choy - Simple Classics"
so the ingredients specify using La Choy. Substitute as needed.
