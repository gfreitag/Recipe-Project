Title: TEA MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter; creamed
2 tb Sugar
1 c  Milk; sweet (1/2 c. cream i
-s better)
1 ea Egg; unbeaten
2 ts Baking powder
1 c  Raisins; or currants
1 1/3 c  Flour
This should make batter a little thicker than ordinary cake batter.
Make in muffin tins.

Note: Time and temperature not given. Assume hot 400 - 450 F. oven at
about 25 minutes.

Source: Agnes De LLoyd, Ridgeville Grange, Lorain County, OH
