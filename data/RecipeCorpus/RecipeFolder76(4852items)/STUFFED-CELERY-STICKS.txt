Title: STUFFED CELERY STICKS
Categories: Appetizers, Crocker
Yield: 14 Appetizers
7 md Stalks celery
2 oz Shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 c  Finely chopped fully cooked
-smoked ham
1/3 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/2 ts Prepared mustard
Cut celery into 3-inch pieces (make sure celery is completely dry).
Mix remaining ingredients; spread about 1 tb in each piece of celery.
Cover and refrigerate 1 hour. About 14 appetizers; 65 calories per
appetizer.

Source: Betty Crockers Cookbook, 6th Edition
