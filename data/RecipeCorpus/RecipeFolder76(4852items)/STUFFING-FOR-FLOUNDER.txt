Title: STUFFING FOR FLOUNDER
Categories: Fish, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Mushrooms(chopped)
6    Scallions
1/4 c  Butter melted
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 tb Ketchup
1 tb Lemon juice
1 c  Flaked crabmeat
Chop all ingredients.  Saute mushrooms and onions 5 minutes in
butter. Stir in bread crumbs and crabmeat.Cook 2 minutes. Add salt,
pepper, lemon juice, and ketchup.  Pour in cream 1/2 and 1/2.  Put in
flounder. Bake 425 for 10-15 minutes/  Pat of butter on top and serve
with mornaise sauce.
