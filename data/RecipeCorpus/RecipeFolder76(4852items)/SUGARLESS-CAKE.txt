Title: SUGARLESS CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Diabetic
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Raisins
2 c  Water
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Shortening
1 c  Rolled oats
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 ts Equal or sweet and low
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
Combine raisins and water & cinnamon in saucepan under low heat for 15
mins.  Let cool.  Add beaten egg & shortening, oats, sweetener &
vanilla. Sift flour, salt, soda & cinnamon. Mix all together well.
Pour into a greased 1 layer cake pan & bake at 350 degrees F. for
30-35 minutes

by Amie Lee Hale From Louisiana Woman's ministries (assemblies of God)
