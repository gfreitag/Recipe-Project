Title: SPICY SAN ANTONIO RUB
Categories: Rubs
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Salt
1/4 c  Pepper
2 tb Garlic Powder
1 tb Cumin
1 tb Cayenne
Mix the ingredients thoroughly in a plastic bowl or sealable freezer
bag. Sprinkle on meat evenly on both sides, shake off excess rub.

Source: The Great Barbeque Companion, Mops, Sops, Sauces, and Rubs by
Bruce Bjorkman
