Title: SWEET & SOUR SAUCE FOR CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
4 oz Ketchup
6 oz Water
4 oz White vinegar
14 oz Sugar
2    Lemons (juice)
2 ts Cornstarch
2 oz Water
In saucepan put first 5 ingredients and bring to a boil, add the
cornstarch mixed with the water. Simmer
1    min. to cook the cornstarch.
