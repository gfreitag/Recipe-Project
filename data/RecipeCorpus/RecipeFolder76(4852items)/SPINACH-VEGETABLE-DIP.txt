Title: SPINACH VEGETABLE DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Relishes
Yield: 20 Servings
1 pk Frozen chopped leaf spinach
1 pt Helman's Mayonaise (16 oz)
1 c  Sour cream (8 oz.)
1/2 c  Chopped onions
Thaw and drain spinaach well.  Thoroughly mix all ingredients
together. Chill, for best results, chill overnight. Serve with fresh
cut vegetables or with pumpernickle bread. Great dip!! Usually have
to double recipe.
