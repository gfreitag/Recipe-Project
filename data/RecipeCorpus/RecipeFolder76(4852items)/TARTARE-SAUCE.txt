Title: TARTARE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise (or salad cream)
2 tb Chopped gherkins
2 tb Chopped capers
1 ts Chopped chives
1/2 ts Tarragon
1/4 ts Chervil
1/4 ts Dry mustard powder
2 ts Anchovy sauce
Put all ingredients into a bowl and mix thoroughly. Allow to stand
for at least 30 minutes - this allows the flavours to permeate.
