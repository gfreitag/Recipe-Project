Title: SPINACH BROWNIES
Categories: Vegetables, Appetizers
Yield: 36 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 c  Milk
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Melted butter
1 lb Sharp Cheddar cheese, grated
1 pk Spinach, cooked, drained
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Marjoram
In a large bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well. Pour into
greased 9"x13" baking dish for 45 minutes at 350. (The Orange Bowl
Cookbook. 1983) Submitted By SALLI SCHWARTZ (HONN)
