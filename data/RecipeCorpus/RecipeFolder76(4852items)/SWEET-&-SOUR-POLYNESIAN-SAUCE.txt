Title: SWEET & SOUR POLYNESIAN SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
1/4 c  Honey
3 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Combine all ingredients and use to brush over ribs, chicken or pork
during the last 15 minutes of cooking (Makes one cup sauce)

Source:  Best Barbeque Recipes by Mildred Fischer
