Title: TACO CHIPS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 6servings
1 pk Corn tortillas
1 x  Crisco
1 x  Season salt
Cut up each corn tortilla in about 6-8 trianges. In large heavy
skillet heat up about 1 inch of grease. Fry triangles( about 30 sec
on each side)until crisp, Drain on paper towels sprinkle (while still
hot) with seasoning salt..Cool and store
