Title: SUBSTITUTE OATMEAL PLAY DOUGH
Categories: Kids, Play dough
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Flour
2 c  Oats
Food coloring
1 c  Water
Stir together flour and oats.  Mix food coloring and water; gradually
add to oat mixture.
