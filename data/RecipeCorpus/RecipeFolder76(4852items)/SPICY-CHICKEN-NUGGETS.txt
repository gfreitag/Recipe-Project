Title: SPICY CHICKEN NUGGETS
Categories: Chicken, Kids, Snacks
Yield: 3 Servings
1 c  Miracle Whip Salad
-Dressing, divided
3 tb Milk
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Ground red pepper
1/4 ts Garlic powder
4    Boneless skinless chicken
-breasts (approx 1 1/4 lbs)
-cut into 1 inch chunks
1 c  Crushed sesame crackers
1/2 c  Salsa
Prep 15 Min./Cook 20 Min.  Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Mix together
1/2 cup salad dressing, milk and seasonings until well blended.
Lightly dip chicken into salad dressing mixture; coat well with
cracker crumbs. Place on greased foil-lined cookie sheet. Bake 18 to
20 minutes or until tender. Mix together 1/2 cup salad dressing and
salsa. Serve with chicken nuggets. Approximately 2 1/2 dozen.
