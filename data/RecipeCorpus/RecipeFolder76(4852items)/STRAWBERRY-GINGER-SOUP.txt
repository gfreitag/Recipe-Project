Title: STRAWBERRY GINGER SOUP
Categories: New text im, Cooking rig
Yield: 4 Servings    2 1/2 c  strawberry juice -- *see
notes
:
1 TB ginger juice (or to taste)
1    ripe banana
1 c  sparkling apple cider
<<or>>
:
1 c  champagne
4 TB yogurt
1 c  mixed blueberries and red
raspberries
Mint sprigs for garnish
:
:

Combine juices and banana in a blender (or use an immersion blender)
and blend until smooth. Add sparkling cider and ladle into chilled
soup bowls. Serve with a dollop of yogurt, a scattering of fresh
berries and a mint sprig or two.

Yield: 4 serving
