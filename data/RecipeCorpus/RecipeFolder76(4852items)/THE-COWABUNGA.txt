Title: THE COWABUNGA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Frozen concentrated orange
-juice
1 c  Strawberry-lemonade juice
1/2 c  Purified water
2 lg Ripe bananas, sliced
1 tb Powdered chocolate
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Tofu
Directions: 1. In a blender, blend juices and water with bananas on
medium speed 1 minute. 2. Add chocolate, vanilla and tofu and blend 1
minute. Makes
4-1/2    cups.
