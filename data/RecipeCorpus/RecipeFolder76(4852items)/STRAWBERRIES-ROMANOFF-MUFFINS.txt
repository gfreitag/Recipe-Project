Title: STRAWBERRIES ROMANOFF MUFFINS
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  (3 oz's.) raisins
-l/2 tsp. cinnamon
-Dash ground nutmeg
1 c  Sour cream
4    English muffins, split and
-lightly toasted
1 1/2    Pt's  fresh strawberries,
-hulled
1 tb . light brown sugar
1/4 c  Toasted almonds, sliced
In blender or food processor, puree raisins with cinnamon and nutmeg;
add 2 tablespoons of the sour cream and blend until combined. Spread
raisin mixture on muffins. Arrange strawberries on top. Combine
remaining sour cream and brown sugar, spoon over strawberries.
Sprinkle with Almonds

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
