Title: STEAMED VEG WITH CHEESE
Categories: V, E, G
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Peanut oil
4 c  Broccoli floweretts
3 md Pepper green
2 c  Onion sliced
2 c  Carrots sliced
4 ts Soy Sauce
8 oz Monterey Jack
3 tb Sesame seeds
In a large skillet, heat oil over moderate high heat. Add onions and
cook 3 minutes. add remaining vegetables, toss to coat with oil and
cover skillet. Cook about 8 minutes until the vegetables are barely
tender but still retain their crunch. Add soy and stir well. Arrange
cheese over vegetables, cover and remove from heat. Let stand for 3
minutes until cheese is melted. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve
immediately.
