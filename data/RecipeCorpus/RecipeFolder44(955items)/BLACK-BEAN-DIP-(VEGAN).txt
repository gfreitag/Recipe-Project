Title: BLACK BEAN DIP (VEGAN)
Categories: Beans, Appetizers, Dips, Prodigy, Dec.
Yield: 4 Servings
4 oz Drained canned black beans
1    Garlic clove; small chop
1 c  Thick & chunky mild salsa
Serve this nutritious dip with vegetable crudites or toasted tortilla
pieces In a food processor combine beans and garlic and process, using
on-off motion until smooth(do not puree) Transfer to a bowl and stir
in salsa. Nutrition (per serving): 59 calories Total Fat 0 g (3% of
calories) Source: Weight Watchers Healthy Lifestyle Cookbook,
