Title: BETTER-THAN-POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Rice
Yield: 50 Servings
3 1/2 c  Uncle Ben's Converted Brand
-Rice
2 qt Water
1 1/2 tb Salt
1 3/4 qt Mayonnaise
3 c  Onions, finely chopped
1/4 c  Prepared mustard
2 ts Salt
1 qt Cucumber, pared and diced
2 c  Radishes, sliced
1.  Cook rice in water with salt according to package directions. 2.
Cool. 3.  Combine mayonnaise, onion, mustard, and salt. Stir into
rice. 4. Cover and throughly chill. 5. Stir in cucumber and radishes
just before serving. Measurements and timing are designed only for
Uncle Ben's Fast Cooking Rice Pilaf, Original Rice Pilaf, Fast
Cooking Spanish Rice, Long Grain & Wild Rice Blend, and CONVERTED
Brand Rice
