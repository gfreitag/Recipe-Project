Title: BERRY REFRESHER
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 3 Servings
1 c  Halved fresh or thawed
-frozen straw
1/2 c  Unsweetened orange juice
2 tb Powdered sugar
8 oz Raspberry low-fat yogurt; (1
- carton)
10    Ice cubes
Combine all ingredients in container of an electric blender; cover and
process until smooth. Yield: 3 cups (serving size: 1 cup). - - - - -
~ - - - - - - - - - - ~ - Serving Ideas
: Serve immediately.
