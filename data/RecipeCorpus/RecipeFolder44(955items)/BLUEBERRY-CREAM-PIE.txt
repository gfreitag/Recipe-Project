Title: BLUEBERRY CREAM PIE
Categories: Pies & past, Apples
Yield: 1 Servings
Graham Cracker Pie Crust, 9
Inch
8 oz Cream Cheese -- softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Heavy Cream
1 pt Blueberries
3 tb Apple Jelly
Combine the cream cheese, sugar and vanilla in a bowl, and mix well.
Whip cream and fold into cream cheese mixture. Spoon filling into
crust. Arrange blueberries on top.  Heat jelly in pan and using
pastry brush, glaze top of pie with jelly.  Chill 3-4 hours.
