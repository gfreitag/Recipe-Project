Title: BISCOTTI PUDDING
Categories: D, E, S, S, E
Yield: 8 Servings
1 pkg. Almond Toast cookies
1/4 C. white Port wine
1 Lg. pkg. instant vanilla pudding 3 C. milk 1 C. whipping cream
(whipped) or cool whip Garnish: Toasted almonds & raspberries

Lay cookies in an 8x10" dish. Sprinkle with the Port wine. Slowly
beat milk and pudding mix for 2 min. Spread over cookies. Chill.
Cover with whipped cream. Garnish with raspberries and almonds.
Alternate: Amaretto and Chocolate pudding mix.
