Title: BLUE SHRIMP FETTUCCINE<-}DGSV43A
Categories: Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
4 oz Blue cheese
8 oz Cream cheese
1/4 c  Parsley; finely chpd frsh
4 ea Green onions; finely chped
1 cl Garlic
1/3 c  Dry white wine
2 tb Cream (1/2 & 1/2)
1 ts Pepper; frsh (to taste)
1 lb Shrimp*
8 oz Fettuccine
Combine room temperature cheeses with wine and cream in 2-qt.
casserole. Add seasonings and shrimp (*cooked, shelled and deveined)
and bake in 375* oven until heated through, about 20 minutes. Toss
with 8 oz. fettucine which has been cooked according to package
directions. From Maytag Farms.
