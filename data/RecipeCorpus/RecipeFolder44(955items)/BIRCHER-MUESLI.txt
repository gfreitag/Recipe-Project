Title: BIRCHER MUESLI
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Uncooked quick oats
2 c  Milk
4    Oranges, sections and juice
6 md Size apples, peeled & grated
3    Bananas, diced
1 c  Chopped pecans
4 c  Plain yogurt
1/4 c  Honey
Wheat or corn flake cereal
For garnish - optional
Raisins for garnish optional
Soak oats in milk 1/2 to 1 hour.

Mix orange sections and juice with apples, bananas, pecans, yogurt and
honey. Stir into oat mixture. Sprinkle with cereal and raisins, if
desired.
