Title: BLT BITES
Categories: Appetizers, Tomatoes, Bacon, Onions
Yield: 16 Servings
16    Cherry tomatoes (up to 20)
1 lb Bacon, cooked, crumbled
1/2 c  Mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/3 c  Green onions, chopped
3 tb Parmesan cheese, grated
2 tb Fresh parsley, snipped
Cut a thin slice off of each tomato top. Scoop out and discard pulp.
Invert the tomatoes on a paper towel to drain. In a small bowl,
combine all ingredients; mix well. Spoon into tomatoes. Refrigerate
several hours.

Yield: 16-20

Source: Taste Of Home, Sept., 1993
