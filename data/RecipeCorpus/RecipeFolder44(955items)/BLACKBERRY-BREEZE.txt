Title: BLACKBERRY BREEZE
Categories: Beverages, Tea
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Boiling water
6    Blackberry-flavor tea bags
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
8 oz Crushed pineapple; undrained
1/3 c  Sugar
18    Ice cubes
In a teapot, pour boiling water over tea bags; cover and steep 5
minutes.

Remove bags.  Chill tea.  In a blender, process the tea, pineapple
juice, pineapple and sugar until smooth. Add ice cubes, one at a
time, processing after each addition until blended. Source:  Taste of
Home mag, June/July 1996
