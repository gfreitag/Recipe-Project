Title: BLACK-EYED PEAS   (FROM MY GARDEN)
Categories: Wasnone
1 lb dried black-eyed peas
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2    stick butter
1    onion -- coarsely cut
1 lg shallot -- quartered
3 c  water -- (3 to 4)
1/4    green pepper -- chopped
1 ts salt
1/2 ts fresh ground pepper

Soak peas overnight, drain, and let stand. In large skillet heat
butter and saute onion and shallot. Add peas. Mix well and cook about
8 minutes . Pour mixture into large pot and add water to cover the
peas. Bring to a bowl and add the green pepper, salt and pepper.
Lower heat and cook for about 1 hour, until the peas are tender.
