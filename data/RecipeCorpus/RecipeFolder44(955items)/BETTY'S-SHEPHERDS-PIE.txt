Title: BETTY'S SHEPHERDS PIE
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
Roast beef (cooked & cut
Into bite size pieces)
Mixed cooked vegetables
1 pk Brown gravy mix
Cooked mashed potatoes
Use any amounts of roast beef and vegetables you want. Mix all
ingredients and put in a greased casserole. Put in oven until hot,
then top with mashed potatoes.
