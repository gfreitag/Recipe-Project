Title: BEST BROCCOLI
Categories: Garlic, Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Fresh broccoli
10 lg Cloves fresh garlic unpeeled
1/3 c  Red wine vinegar
3 tb Olive oil
2 ts Salt
Separate broccoli into flowerets with small stems, then peel main
trunk and slice into strips.  Crush garlic partially, using the flat
side of a knife. Fill large pot with water, add all ingredients being
sure that broccoli stems are submerged, and cook, covered, until
tender. Drain and refrigerate for several hours or overnight.
