Title: BLACK SANGRIA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
Cherries, blackberries and
Black grapes
1    Strip of peel from a whole
Lemon
1/2 c  Strongly brewed black tea
1    Bottle dry red wine
Chilled club soda, to taste
Place fruits and lemon peel in a 2 1/2 quart pitcher. Add the tea and
enough wine to cover. Chill remaining wine Before serving, pour
remaining wine in pitcher, stir, and add ice and club soda to taste
