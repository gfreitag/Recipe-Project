Title: BLACK BEAN & GREEN CHILE SALAD
Categories: Salad, Salads
1/2 lb black beans -- cooked
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  poblano peppers, roasted --
peeled and diced
:
1 md red onion -- diced
1/4 c  cilantro -- chopped
1 TB jalapeno -- minced
2 TB fresh lime juice
1 t  fresh lemon juice
1 ts chipotle puree
2 TB olive oil
salt
freshly ground pepper
:
:

In a large mixing bowl, combine all ingredients well. Season with
salt and pepper.
