Title: BLARNEY STONES
Categories: Holiday, Cakes
Yield: 6 Servings
4    Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Vanilla
2 tb Butter
1 c  Milk; hot
2 c  Cake flour
2 ts Baking powder
Icing
Powdered sugar
Milk
Peanuts or pecans;
- chopped fine or ground
Melt butter in hot milk. Beat the eggs with a rotary beater until
light; beat in sugar, salt, vanilla and butter mixture. Sift flour
and baking powder together; beat in quickly. Pour into a greased
13x9" pan. Pound the pan several times to remove any air bubbles.
Bake for 25-35 minutes at 350~. Cool the cake and cut in 4x1" pieces.
Cover all sides with icing made of powdered sugar and milk to your
desired consistency. Roll in nuts of choice.
