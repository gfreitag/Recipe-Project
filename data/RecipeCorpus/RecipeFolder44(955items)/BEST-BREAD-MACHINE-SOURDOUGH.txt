Title: BEST BREAD MACHINE SOURDOUGH
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 999 Servings
1/2 c  Water, warm
1 c  Sourdough Starter
2 1/4 c  Bread Flour
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Oil
1 ts Salt
3/4 tb Yeast
Place ingredients in order in bread machine.  Makes large loaf in
Hitachi. Use quick setting, if it rises too long, it gets flats This
is the best sourdough for bread machine that I have ever tried.
Adapted from recipe of Helen Fleischer. From the kitcehn of Joan
Mershon
