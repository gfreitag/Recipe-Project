Title: BISCUIT MIX (BISQUICK-ALIKE)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
9 c  Cake Flour, Sifted
1/3 c  Baking Powder
1 c  Powdered Milk
2 tb Powdered Milk
4 ts Salt
1 3/4 c  Vegetable Shortening
Sift all the dry ingredients together. Cut the shortening into the
flour mixture until the mixture resembles coarse cornmeal. Store,
well covered, in a cool dry place. Use for pancakes, waffles,
biscuits, etc.
