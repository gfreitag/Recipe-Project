Title: BILL'S FANTASTIC SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Salad dress
Yield: 6 Servings
*INGREDIENTS*
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Garlic Cloves-Mashed
5 tb Virgin Olive Oil
1 ts Salt
Fresh Pepper to Taste
1/2 c  Red Wine Vinegar
*DIRECTIONS*

In a Blender, Food Processor, or by hand, combine first 5 ingredients
and form a "paste".

When fully combined, add 1/2 cup Red Wine Vinegar (or Vinegar of
choice) and continue until fully emulsified.

TIPS: You may substitute 1/2 cup of Honey for sugar. Adjust ingredient
amounts to your particular taste after first following exact
recipe.

I've even added a few anchovies during first step.

Have fun with this recipe.  It's really delicious. Enjoix!
