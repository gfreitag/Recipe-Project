Title: BETTER THAN BAILEYS
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
6    EGGS
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/2 c  Irish whiskey
1 tb Instant coffee
whip eggs in blender; add milk and vanilla. Liquify coffee with 1/2 of
whiskey, then blender all ingredients serve chilled. I use the
international flavored coffees swiss mocha is my favorite. refrigerate
leftovers
