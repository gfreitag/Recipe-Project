Title: BLACK BEAN RELISH (CHEZ MELANGE)
Categories: Salads, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 Servings
2/3 c  Black beans; cooked until
- tender and drained
1/2 bn Cilantro; chopped
1/2 c  Corn
1/3 c  Red bell peppers; seeded,
- and diced the size
- of the beans
2 tb Olive oil
1    Lime, juiced
2 ds Tabasco sauce (or to taste)
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)
In a medium bowl place all of the ingredients and stir them together.
Let the relish sit for 1 hour before serving it.

Source: Chez Melange - Redondo Beach, California "Southern California
Beach Recipe" by Joan and Carl Stromquist ISBN: 0-9622807-3-9 Typed
for you by Karen Mintzias
