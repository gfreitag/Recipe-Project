Title: BLOODY MARY
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 oz Vodka
3 oz Tomato juice
1 ea Lemon; juiced
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
3 ea Drops tabasco sauce
1 x  Pepper; to taste
1 x  Salt; to taste
Shake with ice and strain into old-fashioned glass over ice cubes. A
wedge of lime may be added.
