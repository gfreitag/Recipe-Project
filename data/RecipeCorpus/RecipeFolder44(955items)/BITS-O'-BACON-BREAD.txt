Title: BITS O' BACON BREAD
Categories: Electric, Whole wheat, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 POUND LOAF
2/3 c  Water
1 2/3 c  Bread Flour
1/3 c  Whole-Wheat Flour
4 ts Sugar
1 ts Salt
4 ts Applesauce
1/2 ts Crushed green peppercorns
1/2 ts Dried sweet basil
1 pn Garlic powder
1/4 c  Bacon (with fat)
2 ts Yeast (active dry)
*** Cook bacon till crisp then crumble and add with fat before
measuring
