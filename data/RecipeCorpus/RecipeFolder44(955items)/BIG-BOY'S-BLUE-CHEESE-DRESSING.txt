Title: BIG BOY'S BLUE CHEESE DRESSING
Categories: Potatoes, Meats
Yield: 2 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Kraft mayo
4 oz Blue cheese -- crumble
1/8 ts Onion powder
Use electric mixer to combine all ingredients until smooth. Tightly
cover and refrigerate. Use within 10 days. Source: Gloria Pitzer
