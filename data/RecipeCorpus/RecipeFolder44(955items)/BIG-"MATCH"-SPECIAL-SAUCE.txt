Title: BIG "MATCH" SPECIAL SAUCE
Categories: Copycat, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Miracle whip salad dressing
1/3 c  Creamy french dressing
1/4 c  Sweet pickle relish
1 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ts Dry minced onion
Recipe by: Gloria Pitzer's Better Crockery Cookbook Stir all
ingredients together with spoon, as listed. Makes 2 cups sauce. Keeps
for one week if refrigerated and well covered. Do Not Freeze. MC
formatting and posting by bobbi744@sojourn.com
