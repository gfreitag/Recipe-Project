Title: BERRY DRESSING (LOW FAT)
Categories: Home cookin
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Chopped or sliced fresh
Berries (strawberries,
Blueberries,
Raspberries)
2 tb Red wine vinegar
2 ts Dry red wine
2 ts Fresh orange juice
1/2 ts Freshly ground black
Pepper
Makes 1 1/2 cups
Use a mix of fresh
Seasonal berries
To create this chunky
Dressing,
Which is similar to a
Fresh salsa.
Try serving it over
Slices of
Watermelon and honeydew
Or in a
x  Cabbage slaw.
In small nonreactive bowl, combine all ingredients and mix until well
blended.  Cover and chill until ready to use.
