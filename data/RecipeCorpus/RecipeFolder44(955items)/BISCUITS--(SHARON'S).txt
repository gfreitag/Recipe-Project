Title: BISCUITS  (SHARON'S)
Categories: Sharon, Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  FLOUR
1 ts SALT
3/4 c  MILK
3 ts BAKING POWDER
1/4 c  SHORTENING
SIFT DRY INGREDIENTS TOGETHER IN A MIXING BOWL. CUT IN SHORTENING.
ADD MILK GRADUALLY AND MIX WITH A FORK. PUT DOUGH ON FLOURED SURFACE
AND KNEAD. LIGHTLY PAT TO A 1 INCH THICKNESS. CUT WITH A FLOURED
BISCUIT CUTTER. BAKE ON AN UNGREASED BAKING SHEET. BAKE AT 375 DEG F.
UNTIL GOLDEN IN COLOR.
