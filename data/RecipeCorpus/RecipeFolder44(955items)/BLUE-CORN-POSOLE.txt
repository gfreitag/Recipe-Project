Title: BLUE CORN POSOLE
Categories: Stews
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Posole
2 lb Beef
3    Garlic
8    Red chile pods
1/2 ts Oregano
1 ts Salt
Soak posole overnight, then rinse well.
Boil posole in salted water for about 2 hours.
Add meat, chile pods (or chile powder), oregano and garlic. Cover and
continue to simmer for another hour or until posole is tender.
Stirring occasionally. Walt
