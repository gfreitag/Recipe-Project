Title: BLACK BEAN TORTILLA MELT - SERVES 6
Categories: Tex-mex, Cilantro, Tortilla
Yield: 6 Servings
1    (15 oz) can black beans
-rinsed and drained
1/2 ts Chili powder
6    (6-inch) corn tortillas
1/4 c  Minced fresh cilantro
1    Lime, cut into six wedges
2    (4 oz) can chopped green
-chiles, undrained
3/4 c  (3 oz) shredded cheddar
-cheese
Fresh or commercial salsa
Mash beans; add chili
-powder, stirring well.
-Spread
About three tablespoons bean
-mixture on each tortilla.
Sprinkle with cilantro, and
-squeeze 1 lime wedge over
Each.  Top each tortilla
-with 2 tablespoons green
-chiles
And 2 tablespoons cheese.
-Bake tortillas at 450~ for
-3 to
5    Minutes or until cheese
-melts. Serve with fresh
-salsa.
