Title: BLANC - MANGE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Milk
2 tb Sugar
3 1/2 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
In saucepan put the sugar, cornstarch and salt. Add milk slowly &
stir, Put on medium heat and stir until thickened & let cook a min.
or two. Remove from heat and add vanilla, pour into dessert bowls &
refrigerate. Put wax paper over each dish to keep the pudding from
scumming over.
