Title: BLUE CHEESE DIP/DRESSING
Categories: S. dressing
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
2 tb Onion; minced
4 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
4 oz Blue cheese; crumbled
1 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Lemon juice
Whisk all ingredients together.  Chill for an hour or more to let
flavor develop before serving.

Source: Roy DeSimone/VT 7122,1044 Formatted by Lisa Crawford, Easter
1996
