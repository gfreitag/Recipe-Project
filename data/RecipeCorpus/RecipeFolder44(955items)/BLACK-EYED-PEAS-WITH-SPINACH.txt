Title: BLACK-EYED PEAS WITH SPINACH
Categories: Low fat, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Onion, chopped
Vegetable broth for
Sauteeing
10 oz Fresh spinach, rinsed,
Stemmed and coarsely chopped
(I think frozen
Would be fine)
3 c  Drained black-eyed peas (two
16 oz Cans)
Ground black pepper to
Taste
pn Cayenne or crushed red
Pepper flakes (optional)
In a large skillet, saute the onions in the broth for a few minutes,
until soft.  Add the spinach to the skillet. Stir for a minute or two
until it wilts. Add the black-eyed peas, black pepper, and cayenne if
desired. Bring to a simmer on medium heat. Serve right away, or cover
and keep warm on low heat.
