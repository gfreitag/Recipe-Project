Title: BLACKENED FISH SEASONING
Categories: Spices, Cajun
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Paprika
2 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Oregano, dried
1 ts Cayenne pepper
3/4 ts White pepper
3/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Thyme, dried
Mix all ingredients well.

From: Paul Prudhomme's Louisiana Kitchen
