Title: BETTER THAN HABITANT PEA SOUP (JUST AS EASY)
Categories: Soups, Appetizers, Vegetables, Mark's
Yield: 6 Servings
2 tb Olive oil
2 ea Dried red chile peppers,
-- chopped
1 c  Potatoes, diced
2 1/2 c  Green peas, fresh or frozen
1/4 ts Coriander powder
1/4 ts Cumin powder
1/8 ts Turmeric
6 ea Peppercorns, crushed
6 c  Stock or water
Salt
Mint leaves for garnish
In a soup pot, warm the olive oil over low heat until hot. Stir in the
chile & spices & heat for 30 seconds. Add the potatoes & peas &
slowly cook for 5 minutes, stirring often, but do not brown. Reduce
heat if necessary. Add stock & raise heat.  Bring to a boil, cover,
reduce heat & cook until the vegetables are very tender, about 25
minutes. Remove from stove & cool for 10 minutes.

In batches, place soup through a blender & blend until smooth. Return
to a clean pot & gently reheat.  Season with salt & pepper & serve
garnished with fresh mint leaves.

Recipe by Mark Satterly
