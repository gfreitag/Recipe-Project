Title: BLUE CHEESE DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 oz Blue cheese or Roquefort
-  crumbled
2 tb Dijon mustard
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
- OR mayonnaise-type
-   salad dressing
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Red wine or Balsamic vinegar
MIX ALL INGREDIENTS together and keep refrigerated until ready to use.
Serve as a dressing over crisp mixed greens. Makes 1 3/4 Cups
