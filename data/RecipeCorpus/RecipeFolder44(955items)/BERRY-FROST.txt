Title: BERRY FROST
Categories: Beverages, Low-cholest, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Frozen unsweetened straw-
Berries (slightly thawed)
1/2 c  Plain low-fat yogurt
1/4 c  Skim milk
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
1    Ice cube
In blender at low speed, blend all ingredients until smooth and
frothy; pour into glass.

APPROXIMATE NUTRIENT ANALYSIS PER SERVING:

150 calories, 9 g protein, 2 g fat, 23 g carbohydrate, 8 mg
cholesterol, 115 g sodium, 550 g potassium

FOOD GROUP UNITS:

1    Fruit, 1/2 Sugar
