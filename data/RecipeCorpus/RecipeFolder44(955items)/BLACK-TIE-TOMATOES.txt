Title: BLACK TIE TOMATOES
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
1/4 c  Plain Low Fat Yogurt
2 tb Low Cal. Mayonnaise
1/4 ts Onion Powder
4 sm Tomatoes, Halved Crosswise
Lettuce Leaves
1 tb + 1 t. Black Caviar,
Rinsed And Drained
Combine Yogurt & Mayonnaise & Onion Powder in Small Bowl. Mix Well.
Cover And Chill At Least One Hour.
Arrange Tomato Halves On Lettuce Lined Plates And Top Each With 1 t.
Yogurt Mixture & 1/2 t. Caviar.
(Fat 1.7. Chol 15.)
