Title: BLUEBERRY BREAD ---WHNP81A
Categories: Breads, Holiday, Family
Yield: 15 Servings
2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
1/4 ts Salt
2 tb Shortening
1 ea Egg
1/4 c  Orange juice
1 tb Grated orange or lemon rind
1 x  Boiling water
1 c  Blueberries; fresh or frozen
1/2 c  Pecans or walnuts; chopped
Preheat oven to 350 deg.  Grease 9 x 5-in. loaf pan. Mix flour,
sugar, baking powder, soda and salt thoroughly. Cut in shortening.
Stir in egg. Add enough boiling water to orange juice and rind to
measure 1 cup. Stir into flour mixture.  Add blueberries and nuts;
stir gently. Pour batter into prepared pan. Bake
60    min. Makes 1 loaf.
