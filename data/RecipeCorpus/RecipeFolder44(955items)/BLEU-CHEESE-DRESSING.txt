Title: BLEU CHEESE DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salad dress
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
2 oz Blue Cheese (1/2 Cup) --
Crumbled
2 tb Milk
2 tb White Vinegar
2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Onion Salt
1/4 ts Dry Mustard
1/8 ts Garlic Powder
Combine all and mix well.  Makes 1 1/2 cups
