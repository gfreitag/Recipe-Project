Title: BETTER BATTER
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Flour
2 tb Baking powder
1 1/4 ts Salt
2 ts Sugar
1 tb Salad oil (pure virgin olive
-is best)
1 c  Water
Passed down from my Grandfather who hails from the English
Midlands....

Combine all that and start dipping and deep frying. The batter turns
out light, crisp and brown.

From: CAROLYN VELASQUEZ
