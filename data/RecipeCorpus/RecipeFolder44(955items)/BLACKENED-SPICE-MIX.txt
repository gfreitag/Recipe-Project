Title: BLACKENED SPICE MIX
Categories: Herb/spice, Cajun
Yield: 1 Servings
2 ts Thyme
2 ts Oregano
2 ts Mustard
6 ts Salt
2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Black pepper
1 ts White pepper
2 ts Onion powder
1 ts Cumin powder
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
2 ts Sweet paprika
Mix all together and store in sealed jar.
