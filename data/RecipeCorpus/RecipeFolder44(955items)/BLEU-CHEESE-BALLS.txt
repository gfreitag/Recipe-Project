Title: BLEU CHEESE BALLS
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 18 Balls
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
2 oz Bleu cheese
3 tb Chopped celery
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Dried parsley
2 tb Dried chives (optional)
Chopped pecans or walnuts
-for rolling
Mix all ingredients; form into balls. Roll in chopped pecans or
walnuts; chill.
