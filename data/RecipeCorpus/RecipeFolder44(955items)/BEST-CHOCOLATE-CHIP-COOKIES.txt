Title: BEST CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 40 Servings
1 c  Margarine
3/4 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 pn Vanilla powder
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Salt
2 c  Chocolate chips
Cream butter and sugars. Beat in eggs. Combine dry ingredients.
Blend. Stir in chocolate chips. Bake at 375* for 12 minutes or until
soft in center. Posted to MC-Recipe Digest V1 #
