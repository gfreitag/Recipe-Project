Title: BLAND DIET FOR FINICKY FELINES
Categories: Pets
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Chicken, boiled or micro-
- waved
1/4 c  Fresh broccoli, steamed
1/4 c  Shredded carrots, steamed
Chicken broth
Mix ingredients with enough chicken broth to hold together. This same
recipe can be used with fish (broil or microwave until it flakes.)
You can also vary the recipe by adding rice or other vegetables.
