Title: Lemon Dill Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Chicken broth
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 ts Dill weed
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
2    Cl Garlic; chopped fine
6    Skinless boneless chicken br
Recipe by: Betty Crocker's New Choice Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15
Mix all ingredients except chicken breasts in glass dish.  Add
chicken; turn to coat with marinade.  Cover and refrigerate at least
3 hours but no longer than 24 hours.  Preheat oven control at broil.
Prepare broiler pan with cooking spray.  Remove chicken from
marinade; reserve marinade. Place chicken in pan.  Broil chicken with
tops 5 to 7 " from heat 7 minutes; turn.  Brush with marinade.  Broil
about 7 minutes longer or until juices run clear.
