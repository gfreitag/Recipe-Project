Title: Herbed Cherry Tomatoes
Categories: Tomatoes, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 pt Cherry tomatoes
1/2 c  Soft breadcrumbs
1/4 c  Plus 2 tbsp minced onion
1/4 c  Plus 2 tbsp minced fresh
-parsley
2 tb Olive oil
1 lg Garlic clove; minced
1/2 ts Dried whole thyme
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Place tomatoes in an 8-inch baking dish.  Combine remaining
ingredients, stirring well; spoon over tomatoes.  Bake at 425 degrees
F for 6 to 8 minutes.
