Title: Low-Calorie French Fries
Categories: Snacks, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 md Potatoes -- * see note
3    Sprays
Paprika -- optional
Salt
Pepper
Celery salt -- optional
Vegetable cooking spray
* Use 2 unpeeled and scrubbed potatoes, baking size.

Cut potatoes in half lengthwise. Slice each half into lengthwise
wedges with some peel on each piece. Soak in cold water for 20
minutes. Drain and dry potatoes. Spray cookie sheet with cooking
spray. Place potatoes into plastic bag or large plastic bowl with
tight fitting lid. Add oil and shake to coat the potatoes. Arrange
potatoes in single layer on cooking sheet. Bake in preheated
450-degree oven for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. To
season, sprinkle with salt, pepper and your choice of paprika or
celery salt. Reduce salt if using celery salt. Also good sprinkled
with chili powder just before end of cooking time.

: Jo Anne Merrill
