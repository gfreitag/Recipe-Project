Title: Greek Honey-Lemon Pork Chops
Categories: Meats, Greek
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Honey
Finely grated zest - 1 lemon
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Chopped fresh mint OR
1/2 ts -- Dried leaf mint; crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground hot red pepper
4    Rib pork chops (about 1 3/4
-- lbs, 3/4-inch thick)
Prep: 15 minutes  Refrigerate: 4 hours  Grill or Broil: 10 to 12
minutes Cost: $ 1.67 per serving.

1. Whisk together hones, lemon zest and juice, olive oil, mint, salt
and pepper in small glass measuring cup.

2. Place chops in glass baking dish.  Pour marinade over; turn to
coat. Cover; refrigerate at least 4 hour or overnight, turning
occasionally.

3. Grill or broil 4 inches from heat 5 to 6 minutes per side or until
cooked through.  Serve with summer squash.

Nutrient Value per serving: 341 calories, 44 g protein, 13 g fat, 9 g
carbohydrate, 398 mg sodium, 125 mg cholesterol.

Exchanges: 6 meat, 1/2 fruit, 2/3 fat.
