Title: Spinach Pom Poms
Categories: Vegetables, Dress/stuff, Cheese/eggs, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea 10oz pkgs frozen spinach
16 pk Stove top stuffing
1 c  Grated parmesan cheese
ds Of nutmeg
6 ea Eggs, beaten
3/4 c  Margarine, softened
Thaw spinach and squeeze out excess water.  Combine remaining
ingredients. Shape into walnut sized balls. (Can be frozen at this
stage) Bake at 350F for 10-15 minutes.  Makes about 65 appetizers.
Origin: Homestyle Canadian Classics, Ladies of the Royal Purple.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95
