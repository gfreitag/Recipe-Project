Title: CHINESE-STYLE CHICKEN IN LETTUCE CUPS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 2 main-cours
3/4 lb Ground chicken
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil OR
1 tb Dried
2 tb Soy sauce
2 tb Fresh time juice
2 lg Garlic cloves, chopped
1 lg Jalapeno chili, chopped
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Garlic chili sauce or hot
-pepper sauce (such as
-Tabasco)
1 tb Oriental sesame oil or
-vegetable oil
Boston lettuce leaves
Chopped green onions (opt.)
Roasted peanuts (opt.)
Combine first 8 ingredients in medium bowl and stir to blend well.
(Can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover and refrigerate.)

Heat sesame oil in heavy medium skillet over high heat. Add chicken
mix- ture and saute until chicken is just cooked through, breaking up
clumps with back of spoon, about 4 minutes. Transfer chicken mixture
to plattcr. Arrange lettuce around edges; serve, passing onions and
peanuts, if desired.
