Title: German-Style Grilled Kielbasa
Categories: Sausage, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Dark beer
2 tb Light-brown sugar
2 tb Dijon-style mustard
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Caraway seeds
1/8 ts Liquid red-pepper seasoning
1 lb Kielbasa
Prep: 15 minutes   Cook: 10 minutes   Grill or Broil: 4 minutes Cost:
$ 1.22 per serving.

1. Combine beer, sugar, mustard, vinegar, caraway seeds and liquid
red- pepper seasoning in large skillet.  Bring to boiling.

2. Score top of kielbasa at 1-inch intervals.  Add to skillet; simmer
over low heat for 10 minutes.

3. Grill or broil kielbasa 3 inches from heat for 4 minutes, turning
halfway during cooking and basting once or twice with beer mixture.
Serve extra beer liquid on side, and steamed parsleyed potatoes with
cherry tomatoes.

Nutrient Value per serving: 416 calories, 16 g protein, 32 g fat, 13 g
carbohydrate, 1,417 mg sodium, 76 mg cholesterol.

Exhanges: 1/3 starch/bread, 2 1/5 meat, 1/4 fruit, 5 1/4 fat.
