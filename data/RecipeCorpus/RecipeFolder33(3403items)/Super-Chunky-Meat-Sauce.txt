Title: Super Chunky Meat Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Meats
Yield: 3 cups
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 1/2 c  Sliced fresh mushrooms
1 c  Chopped green pepper
1 cn Hunt's Ready Tomato Sauces
-Chunky Garlic and Herb
-(15 oz)
1 cn Hunt's Ready Choice-Cut
-Diced Tomatoes (14.5 oz)
2 tb Snipped parsley
8 oz Mostaccioli or fettuccine,
-cooked and drained
Freshly grated Parmesan
-cheese
Crumble meat into a 2-quart microwave-safe casserole. Stir in onion.
Cover; micro-cook on 100% power (HIGH) for 5-7 minutes or until meat
is no longer pink, stirring once. Drain meat; set aside. Wipe out
casserole with paper towels. Combine mushrooms, green pepper, chunky
garlic and herb tomato sauce, and diced tomatoes in casserole. Cover;
cook on high for 7-9 minutes or until vegetables are crisp-tender.
Stir in meat mixture and parsley. Cover; cook on high for 1-2 minutes
or until heated through. Serve over pasta. Sprinkle with Parmesan
cheese. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-14-95
