Title: Bellini
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 serving
3 oz Italian white peach juice
-or peach nectar
1 ds Lemon juice
3 oz Chilled champagne or
-sparkling wine
1 ds Black currant juice or
-grenadine
Pour chilled peach juice into champagne flute. Add dash of lemon juice
(more if peach nectar is substituted) and dash of black currant juice
or grenadine for color. Filled with chilled sparkling wine or
champagne. Add fresh peach slice for garnish.
