Title: CHEESE-FILLED FIGS WITH AMARETTO - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Desserts, Fruit, Alcohol
Yield: 2 servings;
3 oz Cream cheese
2 tb Sour cream
1 tb Powdered sugar
6    Fresh figs or 6 canned
-apricot halves, drained,
-patted dry
2 tb Pine nuts, toasted
1/8 ts Ground cinnamon
1 1/2 tb Amaretto or brandy
Blend cream cheese, sour cream and powdered sugar in food processor
until smooth. Cut deep slit in each fig. Squeeze gently to open.
Spoon cheese mixture into each fig or mound on each apricot halt
Sprinkle with nuts, then cinnamon. Drizzle amaretto over.
