Title: THAI BASIL BEEF - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Meats
Yield: 2 servings
2 ts Oyster sauce*
2 ts Light soy sauce
2 ts Fish sauce (nam pla)
2 ts Garlic chili sauce*
1 ts Sugar
1 ts Minced j alapeiio chili
1 ts Minced garlic
2 tb Vegetable oil
3/4 lb Beef flank steak, cut into
-thin diagonal slices
1/4 c  Canned low-salt chicken
-broth
40    Fresh basil leaves (left
-whole)
Freshly cooked white rice
Mix first 7 ingredients in small bowl. Heat oil in wok or heavy large
skillet over high heat. Add beef and stir-fry until medium-rare,
about 3 minutes. Using slotted spoon, transfer to bowl.

Pour off all but 1 T oil from wok. Add sauce mixture to wok and bring
to boil. Add chicken broth and boil until sauce thickens slightly,
about 2 minutes. Return beef to sauce in wok; add basil leaves and
stir just until beef is cooked through, about 1 minute. Serve over
rice.

*Available at Asian markets and in the Asian section of many
supema@ts.

From: Germaine's-Washington, D.C.
