Title: Plum BBQ Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Onion; choppewd
1/4 c  Chili sauce
16    1/2 oz purple plums, drained
1/4 c  Butter
2 ts Dijon mustard
1    6 oz can frozen lemonade
Recipe by: 1/4 Cook onions in butter in 2 quart pan stirring
occasionally, until tender, about 2 min. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Heat to boiling, reduce to low heat. Simmer uncovered 15
min. stirring occasionally. Makes 2 cups.
