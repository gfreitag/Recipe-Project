Title: Wagon Wheels
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 serving
1/3 c  Cold orange juice
1/3 c  Cold apple cider
Garnish: anchor maraschino cherries to both sides of thick orange
slice with a toothpick.

Fill a tall glass with ice, add juices and stir. Top with a "wagon
wheel" garnish.
