Title: Ham and Tomato Sauce
Categories: Cajun/creol, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
4    Center cut ham slices
-(1/2" thick)
-or
1 1/2 lb Cubed ham
3 md Onions, chopped
1/2    Green pepper, chopped
1    Stalk celery, chopped
1    Garlic clove, chopped
1 tb Flour
1 cn Tomato paste (8 oz)
1 cn Tomato sauce (8 oz)
2 sm Hot peppers
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Olive oil
Saute onion, pepper, celery, and garlic in olive oil. Add flour and
brown slightly. Add tomato sauce , paste, and Worcestershire sauce
and 2 cans water. Add sugar. Squeeze peppers and drop into sauce.
Simmer on low for 1/2 hour, then add ham and simmer for 1 hour
longer. Serve over rice. Serves four generously.
