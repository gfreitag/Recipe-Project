Title: Bobbed Crabs
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 1 servings
No Ingredients Found
Drop crabs into actively boiling water.  When completely red take out
and clean. Drop cleaned crabs into boiling crab apple vinegar.  When
well infused with flavor add some crab spice.  Leave in the mixture
only a little while.  Then drain on racks.  Either eat cold or
grilled with a little butter and pepper. Shared by: Pat Stockett
