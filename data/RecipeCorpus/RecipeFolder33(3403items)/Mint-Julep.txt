Title: Mint Julep
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
2 1/2 oz Bourbon
7    Sprigs mint
1/2 oz Simple syrup
Place six of the mint sprigs in a Collins glass. Add simple syrup and
muddle the sprigs and syrup well. Add 1-1/2 ounce of the Bourbon and
stir. Fill glass with ice cubes and add the rest of the Bourbon. Stir
well and garnish with an orange slice, a lemon slice, and the final
sprig of mint.

SIMPLE SYRUP: Place 1 cup sugar and 2 cups water in a heavy saucepan
and bring to a boil. Boil for five minutes and transfer to a
container to cool. Can be refrigerated for up to 6 weeks.
