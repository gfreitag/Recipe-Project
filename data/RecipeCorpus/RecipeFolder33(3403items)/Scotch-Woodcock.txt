Title: Scotch Woodcock
Categories: Scot/irish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 2 servings
4 sl Toast; buttered *or*
4 sl Fried bread
1 oz Butter
4    Eggs
2 tb Milk
pn Salt
Pepper to taste
Anchovy fillets
Make the toast and butter it or fry the bread. Keep warm. Melt the
butter in a saucepan.  Stir in the eggs and milk, season and cook
over a gentle heat, stirring continuously, until the mixture
thickens. Spoon the eggs on to the toast and top with anchovy
fillets. Delicious as a light supper.
