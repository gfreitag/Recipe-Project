Title: TOMATO AND LEEK SOUP - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Olive oil
1 c  Coarsely chopped leeks
3 3/4 lb Tomatoes, peeled, seeded,
-chopped
3 1/2 c  (or more) canned
-vegetable broth
1 1/2 c  Sauvignon Blanc or
-other dry white wine
1    7-oz jar roasted red bell
-peppers, drained
1/2 c  Chopped fresh basil
1/3 c  Chopped fresh parsley
2 tb Chopped fresh thyme OR
2 ts Dried
1/3 c  Whipping cream
6 tb Tomato paste
4 ts Minced garlic
2 c  Vegetable oil (for deep
-frying)
1 lg Leek (white and pale green
-parts only), halved
-lengthwise, cut into
-matchstick-size strips
Heat olive oil in large saucepan over medium heat. Add chopped leeks;
saute until tender, about 10 minutes. Add tomatoes, 3 1/2 C broth,
wine, peppers, herbs, cream, tomato paste and garlic. Simmer 1 hour,
stirring occasionally. Coarsely puree in batches in processor. (Can
be made 1 day ahead. Cover, chill.)

Heat 2 C oil in another large saucepan to 375'F. Add leek strips and
fry until golden and crisp, about 2 minutes. Using slotted spoon,
trans- fer leek to paper towels and drain.

Bring soup to simmer, thinning with more broth, if desired. Season
with salt and pepper. Ladle soup into bowls. Garnish with fried leek
and serve.
