Title: Hershey's Peanut Butter Thumbprints
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 96 cookies
1 c  Butter, softened (2 sticks)
2 c  Packed light brown sugar
1 1/2 c  REESE'S Creamy or REESE'S
-Crunchy Peanut Butter
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 3/4 c  Finely chopped unsalted
-dry roasted peanuts
MMMMM---------------------THUMBPRINT FILLING--------------------------
1 pk Cream cheese, softened (3oz)
3 tb Light corn syrup
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  HERSHEY'S Semi-Sweet
-Chocolate Chips, melted
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
Heat oven to 350'F. In large mixer bowl, beat butter, brown sugar and
peanut butter until well blended. Add eggs and vanilla; beat well.
Stir together flour, baking powder and baking soda. Gradually add to
peanut butter mixture. Shape dough into 1" balls; roll in peanuts.
Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes or until cookies
are just set. Press thumb gently in center of each cookie; cool
slightly. Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Cool completely.
Prepare THUMBPRINT FILLING. Spoon or pipe about 1/2 teaspoon filling
into each thumbprint.

THUMBPRINT FILLING: In small mixer bowl, beat cream cheese, corn
syrup and vanilla until well blended. Add chocolate, baking well.
Gradually add powdered sugar, beating until well blended and smooth.
1 1/2 cups filling. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-05-95
