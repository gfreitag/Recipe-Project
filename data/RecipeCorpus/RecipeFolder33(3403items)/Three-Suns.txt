Title: Three Suns
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Orange juice
1 qt Apple juice
1 pt Strawberry sherbet
Orange peel for garnish
Pour orange juice and apple juice into a large pitcher and stir well.
Fill tall frosted glasses with cracked ice and add juice. Top each
glass with scoop of sherbet, garnish with orange peel and add a
straw. Makes 8 tall glass servings.
