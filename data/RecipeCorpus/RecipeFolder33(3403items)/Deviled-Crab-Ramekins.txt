Title: Deviled Crab Ramekins
Categories: Casseroles, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
10 oz Crab meat -- canned
2    Hard-boiled eggs
3 tb Margarine -- melted
2    Eggs -- separated
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Mustard
2 tb Vinegar
1 ds Cayenne pepper -- to taste
Soft bread crumbs
1. Drain crab; remove any cartilage.

2. Chop hard-cooked egg whites; mash egg yolks. Combine mashed yolks
with margarine, 1/4 cup boiling water, salt, mustard, vinegar and
cayenne pepper. Mix well. Stir in crab and chopped egg whites.

3. Beat egg whites until soft peaks form; fold into crab mixture.

4. Place in greased shells or ramekins; cover with bread crumbs. Bake
in preheated 350-degree oven until firm and crumbs are brown.

NOTES:  Optional:  Add chopped green onions, or substitute 1
tablespoon lemon juice for the vinegar. Recipe By
: Jo Anne Merrill
