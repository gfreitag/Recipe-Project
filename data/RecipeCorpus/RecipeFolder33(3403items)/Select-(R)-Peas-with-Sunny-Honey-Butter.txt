Title: Select (R) Peas with Sunny Honey Butter
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Green Giant Select Peas(1lb)
2 tb Butter, softened
1 tb Honey
1 ts Orange or lemon peel
Cook peas as directed on package; drain. Stir honey and orange peel
into softened butter. Stir into peas.
