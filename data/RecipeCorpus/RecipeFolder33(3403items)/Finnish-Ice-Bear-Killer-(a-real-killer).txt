Title: Finnish Ice Bear Killer (a real killer)
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
4 oz Ice Cold Koskenkorva (can be
-substituted with Finlandia
-Vodka) MUST BE PLACED IN
-FREEZER AT LEAST THREE
-HOURS PRIOR TO MAKING!
1 oz Bacardi Light Rum
Cherry
Pour ingredients into glass. DO NOT USE ICE! To drink: first eat
cherry. Quickly drink contents of glass. If you are not dead, you
must be Finnish. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-05-95
