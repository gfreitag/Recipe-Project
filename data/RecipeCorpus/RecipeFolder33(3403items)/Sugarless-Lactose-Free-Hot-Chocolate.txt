Title: Sugarless Lactose Free Hot Chocolate
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 servings
2 tb Baking cocoa
2 tb Sugar substitute
1/4 c  Water
2 c  Liquid Non Dairy Creamer
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
Ground cinnamon, optional
Non Dairy Whipped Topping
-optional
In a saucepan, mix the cocoa and sugar substitute; add water.  Bring
to a boil, stirring constantly; boil for 1 minute.

Reduce heat; add non dairy creamer and heat through. Remove from the
heat and stir in vanilla. Pour into 2 cups.

Garnish with Non dairy whipped topping if desired.

Yields:  2 servings
