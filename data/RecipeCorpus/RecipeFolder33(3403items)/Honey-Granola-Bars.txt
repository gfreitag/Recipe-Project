Title: Honey Granola Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 20 bars
1 c  Flour
1 3/4 c  Old-fashioned (not instant)
-oats
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Dark brown sugar
1/3 c  Honey
2/3 c  Butter, melted
1/4 c  Sunflower seeds
1/2 c  Surrants or raisins
1/2 c  Sliced almonds
In small bowl, whisk together flour, oats, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt;
set aside. In large bowl, combine brown sugar, honey and melted
butter. Blend in flour mixture and sunflower seeds, currnats and
almonds. Press dough firmly into bottom of greased, 9"-square pan.
Bake about 30 minutes at 350'F. Let cool completely before cutting
into bars. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-09-95
