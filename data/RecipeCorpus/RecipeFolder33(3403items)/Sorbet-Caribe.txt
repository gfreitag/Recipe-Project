Title: Sorbet Caribe
Categories: Desserts, Caribbean, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Ripe bananas, sliced &
-- frozen
3 oz Pineapple juice
3 oz Frozen pineapple juice
2 tb Unsweetened, shredded
-- coconut
Place the frozen banana slices, fresh & frozen pineapple juice &
coconut in a blender or food processor.  Puree until the mixture is
smooth & resembles soft ice cream or a slush.  Serve immediately in
chilled bowls.
