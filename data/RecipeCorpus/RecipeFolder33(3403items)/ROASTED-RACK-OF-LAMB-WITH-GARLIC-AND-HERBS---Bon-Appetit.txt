Title: ROASTED RACK OF LAMB WITH GARLIC AND HERBS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Dried oregano
2 tb Dried thylme
1 tb Fennel seeds
1 tb Dried rosemary
1 1/2 ts Dried rubbed sage
1/4 c  Olive oil
2 tb Chopped garlic
2    1 1/2-lb racks of lamb
Preheat oven to 400F. Combine first 5 ingredients in small bowl.
Combine oil and garlic in another small bowl. Rub oil mixture over
each lamb rack. Season generously with salt and pepper. Press 3 T
spice mixture all over each lamb rack. Place lamb in baking pan.
Roast until thermometer inserted into center registers 135F for
medium-rare, about 20 minutes. Transfer lamb racks to platter and let
stand 5 minutes.

Carve lamb into individual chops, arrange on platter and serve.
