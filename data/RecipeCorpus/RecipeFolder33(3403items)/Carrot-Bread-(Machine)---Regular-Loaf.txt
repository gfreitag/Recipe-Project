Title: Carrot Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1/2 c  Water
1 1/3 c  White bread flour
2/3 c  Wheat bread flour
2 ts Dry milk
1 ts Salt
2/3 c  Carrots, grated
1 tb Honey
2 tb Yogurt, plain
1 tb Molasses
2 tb Walnuts, chopped
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

[A new favorite of Cheshire Cat!]  The wheat flour gives this bread
body, the nuts and carrots give it texture, and the yogurt keeps it
moist. Serve this bread with steaming bowls of vegatable soup for a
nutritious hearty lunch.
Success Hints
-------------

Use freshly grated carots

Use regular or rapid bake cycle
