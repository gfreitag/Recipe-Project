Title: CILANTRO COCKTAIL SAUCE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 1/3 cups
1 c  Catsup
3 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
2 tb Prepared white horseradish
2 tb Fresh lime juice
Combine all ingredients in small bowl. Season to taste with salt and
pepper. Chill until cold. (Can be made 4 hours ahead. Keep chilled.)
