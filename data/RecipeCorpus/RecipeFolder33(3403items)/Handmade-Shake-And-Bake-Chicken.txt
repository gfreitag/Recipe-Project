Title: Handmade Shake And Bake Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Unbleached flour
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Black pepper
4    Skinless boneless chicken br
1/2 c  Skim milk; at room temperatu
Recipe by: 365 Ways To Cook Chicken Preparation Time: 0:20 Preheat
oven at 375.  Prepare pan with foil, sprayed with cooking spray.
Combine flour, paprika, salt, garlic powder, thyme, and pepper in a
plastic bag. Dip breasts in milk; shake off excess.  Add to bag and
shake to coat evenly. Place chicken on a prepared baking dish.  Bake
40 minutes, until chicken is tender and coating crisp.
