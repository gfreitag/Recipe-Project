Title: Light Rye Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 c  + 2T Water
2 1/4 c  White bread flour
3/4 c  Rye flour
2 1/2 tb Sugar
1 1/4 ts Salt
1 1/4 tb Butter
1 tb Cornmeal
1/2 tb Caraway seeds
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
