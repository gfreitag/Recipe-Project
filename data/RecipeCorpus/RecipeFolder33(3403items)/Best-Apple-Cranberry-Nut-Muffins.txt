Title: Best Apple-Cranberry-Nut Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 18 servings
2 c  Apples -- peeled, shredded
1 1/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Cranberries -- chopped
1 c  Carrots -- shredded
1 c  Black walnuts -- chopped
2 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 tb Double-acting baking powder
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Ground cinnamon
2    Eggs -- beaten
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
In a large mixing bowl, combine apples and sugar. Gently fold in
chopped cranberries, carrots, and nuts. Combine dry ingredients; add
to mixing bowl. Mix well to moisten dry ingredients. Combine eggs and
oil; stir into apple mixture. Fill 18 greased muffin tins 2/3 full.
Bake at 375 degrees for 20-25 minutes. Cool 5 minutes before removing
from pans.

Yield: 18 muffins
