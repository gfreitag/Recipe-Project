Title: Easy Shrimp & Pepper Marinara on Angel Hair Pasta
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Olive oil
1/2 lg Green bell pepper; cut into
-1-inch strips
1 cn (27-oz.) S&W Fisherman's
-Wharf Marinara Pasta Sauce
3/4 lb (to 1 lb.) Raw shrimp;
-peeled & deveined
8 oz Dry angel hair pasta; cooked
-and drained
Heat oil in large skillet; add bell pepper and saute over medium heat
until crisp-tender.  Reduce heat to low; add sauce and shrimp.
Simmer about 5-7 minutes, stirring constantly, until shrimp is pink.
Spoon over pasta; garnish with parsley, lemon peel and grated
Parmesan cheese. Serves 4.

PER SERVING:  390 calories; 38g protein; 63g carbohydrates; 3g fiber;
4g fat; 165mg cholesterol; 1270mg sodium.
