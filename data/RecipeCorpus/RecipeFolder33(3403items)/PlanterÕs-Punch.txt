Title: Planter√ïs Punch
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Dark rum
1/2 oz Grenadine
2 oz Orange juice
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 ts Powdered sugar
3 oz Club soda
Cracked ice
Ice cubes
1    Maraschino cherry
1    Orange slice
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add rum, grenadine, orange
juice, lemon juice, and sugar. Shake and strain into collins glass
filled with ice cubes. Fill with club soda. Garnish with a cherry and
orange slice.
