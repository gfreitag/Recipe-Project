Title: Doggy Treats
Categories: Animal
Yield: 6 servings
1    Jar baby food meat (any
- flavor)
7 tb Wheat germ
4 tb Nonfat powdered milk
Combine all ingredients. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto ungreased cookie
sheets. Flatten slightly with fork.

Bake in 350 degree oven until lightly browned.

Let cool, then serve.
