Title: Sausage Sauce Piquant
Categories: Sausage, Main dish, Cajun/creol
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------FROM: ELLEN CLEARY--------------------------
1 lb Smoked sausage, sliced
2 tb Oil
2 tb Flour
1 ea Mdm onion, chopped
1/2 ea Green pepper, chopped
16 oz Can tomatoes
2 c  Water
Salt and pepper
Rice
Brown sausage.  Drain well.
In a Dutch oven, combine oil and flour and stir constantly over low
heat until you have a deep rich colored roux.  Add onions and green
pepper and saute a few minutes, until tender.  Add sausage, tomatoes,
water, salt, and pepper.  Cover and simmer at least 1 hour. Serve
over rice. Serves 4-6
