Title: Pineapple Zucchini
Categories: Can/preserv, Vegetables, Fruit, Condiments, Relishes
Yield: 1 servings
4 qt Peeled, seeded, cubed or
-grated zucchini
3 c  White sugar
1/2 c  Lemon juice
48 oz Can unsweetened pineapple
-juice
2 ea 3oz boxes pineapple Jello
Mix together the zucchini, sugar, lemon and pineapple juice.  Bring
to a boil and add pineapple jello.  Stir to dissolve.  Cook until
zucchini is soft, but not mushy, about 20-30 minutes.  Cool.  Put
into freezer containers and freeze.  Makes about 12 pints. Origin:
Homestyle, Canadian Classics, Ladies of the Royal Purple. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, July/95.
