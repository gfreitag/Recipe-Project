Title: Best-Ever Meat Loaf
Categories: Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Tomato soup
2 lb Ground beef
1    Envelope onion soup mix
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1    Egg -- beaten
1/4 c  Water
In large bowl, mix thoroughly 1/2 cup tomato soup, beef, onion soup
mix, bread crumbs and egg.  In 2 qt oblong baking dish, firmly shape
meat mixture into 8 x 4 " loaf.  Bake at 350 for 1 1/4 hours or until
meat loaf is no longer pink.  Spoon off fat, reserving 1 to 2 tbsp
drippings.  In 1 qt saucepan over medium heat, combine remaining
tomato soup, water and reserved drippings.  Heat through, stirring
occasionally.  Serve with meat loaf.
