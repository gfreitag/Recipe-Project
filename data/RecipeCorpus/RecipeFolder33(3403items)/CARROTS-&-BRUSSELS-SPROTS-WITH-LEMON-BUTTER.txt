Title: CARROTS & BRUSSELS SPROTS WITH LEMON BUTTER
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 /140
1 lb Carrots, cut in julienne
-strips
1 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed
-and cut in halves
Boiling salted water
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
Grated peel and juice of 1/2
-lemon
1 ts Caraway seeds
1 sm Clove garlic, minced
1. Cook carrots and brussels sprouts together in boiling salted water
until tender, about 10 minutes. Drain and set aside. 2. Melt butter
in saucepan with lemon peel and juice, caraway seeds and garlic. 3.
Add cooked vegetables and heat.
