Title: Bianca Pizza Sauce 2
Categories: Pizza, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
1 c  Ricotta cheese
1/2 c  Half-and-half
1 tb Fresh basil, chopped
-OR
1 ts Dried basil
1 tb Fresh parsley, chopped
Freshly ground black pepper
Combine all ingredients and mix well.
