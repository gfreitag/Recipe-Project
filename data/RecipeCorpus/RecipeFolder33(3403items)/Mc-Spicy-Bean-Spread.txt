Title: Mc Spicy Bean Spread
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Cooked and drained kidney
-or pintos, or 1 can,
-rinsed and drained
1/2    Yellow onion; chopped
1    Tomato; chopped
1 cl Garlic
1 tb Soy sauce; low sodium
1/2 tb Chili powder
1/2 tb Cumin
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Oregano
Process all the ingredients in a food processor, blender or food mill
until smooth.  A little water may be added if necessary during
processing. From the McDougall Plan by John McDougall Typed by Lisa
Greenwood Submitted By LISA GREENWOOD   On   08-04-95
