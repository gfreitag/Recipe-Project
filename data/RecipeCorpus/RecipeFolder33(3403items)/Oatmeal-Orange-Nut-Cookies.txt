Title: Oatmeal-Orange-Nut Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 18 servings
1/3 c  Margarine
1/3 c  Yogurt
1/4 c  Packed brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Rolled oats
1/4 ts Baking soda
2 tb Black walnuts -- chopped
1/4 ts Orange peel -- grated
Place softened margarine, yogurt, sugar and vanilla into mixing bowl.
Mix until well blended. Add remaining ingredients and blend well. Use
a teaspoon or small melon scoop to make rounded balls of dough. Place
2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet; flatten slightly with fork
dipped in flour or sugar. Bake in preheated 325-degree oven for 12-15
minutes. Remove immediately from cookie sheet and allow to cool on
wire rack.
