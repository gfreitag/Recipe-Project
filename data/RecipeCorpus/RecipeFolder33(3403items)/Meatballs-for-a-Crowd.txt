Title: Meatballs for a Crowd
Categories: Meats, Fruit
Yield: 200 servings
MMMMM-------------------------SERVES 200------------------------------
30 ea Lean ground beef
14 1/2 c  Bread crumbs
5 1/2 c  Minced onions
10 ts Pepper
44 ea Eggs
10 1/2 c  Milk
6 1/2 tb Salt
10 ea 19oz large cans tomato soup
20 ea 14oz cans pineapple
-tidbits, drained
20 tb Worcesterhire sauce
5 1/2 c  Brown sugar
11 ts Dry mustard
7 c  Vinegar
Salt and pepper
3/4 c  Chopped onion
Mix the beef, bread, onions pepper, eggs, milk and salt together and
form into 1 inch balls.  Bake on shallow roasting pans in a 400F oven
for 20 minutes, turning halfway.  Place in a large roaster.  Combine
remaining ingredients and pour over meatballs.  Bake at 350F for
about 1-1/2 hours. Origin: Homestyle, Canadian Classics, Ladies of
the Royal Purple. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
