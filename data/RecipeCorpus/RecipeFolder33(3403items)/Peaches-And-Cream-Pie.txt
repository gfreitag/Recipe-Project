Title: Peaches And Cream Pie
Categories: Fruit, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
1    9 inch pie crust
3 1/2 c  Fresh peaches -- peeled and
Sliced
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ds Ground nutmeg
3 tb Tapioca
Half and half
1. Mix sliced peaches with sugar, spices and tapioca. Place in pie
shell. 2. Place shell on a cookie sheet and pour half and half over
fruit until shell is 3/4 full. 3. Bake in preheated 400-degree oven
for 10 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350 degrees and continue baking
for 45-50 minutes longer.

Serve hot or warm.
