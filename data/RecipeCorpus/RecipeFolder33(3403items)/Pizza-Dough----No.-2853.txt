Title: Pizza Dough    No. 2853
Categories: Italian, Pizza, Breads
Yield: 1 8" pie
1 tb Yeast
2 c  White Bread Flour
1/2 c  White Bread Flour
1 1/2 c  White Bread Flour
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Water
2 ts Dried Parsley
2 cl Garlic, Chopped
2 ts Italian Seasoning
1 ts Cornmeal
Mix the yeast, salt and the first measure of flour in a bowl. Heat the
water to 130 degrees. Add to the bowl. Mix until the ingredients are
well blended. Mix in the second measure of flour. Mix until
thoroughly blended. Mix in the third measure of flour. Blend in the
parsley, garlic and Italian seasoning. Knead the dough for 8 minutes
on a floured surface. Roll out to size. Sprinkle the cornmeal on the
pizza pan. Place the rolled out dough on the pizza pan. Let rise for
45 minutes to 1 hour. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees. Cover with
sauce and toppings. Bake for 18 minutes. Serve hot. Brain Dake From:
