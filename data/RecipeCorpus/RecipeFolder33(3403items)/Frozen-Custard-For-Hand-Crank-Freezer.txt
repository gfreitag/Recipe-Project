Title: Frozen Custard For Hand-Crank Freezer
Categories: Ice cream
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 tb Vanilla extract
2 c  Light cream -- *see note
* You can substitute evaporated milk for part or all of the light
cream.

1. Combine milk and sugar in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave for 3
minutes on high or until hot.

2. Beat eggs, adding a bit of the hot milk mixture. Combine rest of
hot milk mixture with the eggs. Beat with a rotary beater until
blended well. Microwave for 5 minutes at medium temperature.

3. Beat WELL and continue cooking for 5-6 minutes longer, or until it
comes to a boil and thickens. Then beat lightly. Cool COMPLETELY and
add vanilla.

4. Prepare ice cream freezer according to manufacturer's directions.
Pour the cooked custard AND 2 cups light cream into freezer and
freeze as directed.
