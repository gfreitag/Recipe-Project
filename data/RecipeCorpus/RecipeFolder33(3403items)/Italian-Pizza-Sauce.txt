Title: Italian Pizza Sauce
Categories: Pizza, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
2 c  Crushed Italian plum
-tomatoes
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
5    Caramelized garlic cloves
6    Fresh basil leaves, chopped
-OR
1 ts Dried basil
1/2 ts Dried oregano
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
From George Hirsch's book "Grilling with Chef George Hirsch."

Combine all ingredients in a 2-quart saucepan and simmer for 4-5
minutes, stirring occasionally.
