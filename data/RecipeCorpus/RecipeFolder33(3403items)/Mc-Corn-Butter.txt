Title: Mc Corn Butter
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Cornmeal
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Additional water
1 ts To 2t lemon juice
1 ts No salt seasoning blend
Place the cornmeal in a saucepan, add the 1c water, and cook, stirring
constantly, until smooth and thick.  Place the cooked cornmeal and
all the remaining ingredients in the blender and process until
smooth. Add more water if necessary to reach desired spreading
consistency. From the McDougall Plan by John McDougall Typed by Lisa
Greenwood Submitted By LISA GREENWOOD   On   08-04-95
