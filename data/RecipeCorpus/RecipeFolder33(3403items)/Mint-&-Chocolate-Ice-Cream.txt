Title: Mint & Chocolate Ice Cream
Categories: Ice cream
Yield: 1 quart
1 1/2 ts Unflavored gelatin
1/4 c  Water
3 c  Heavy whipping cream
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/8 ts Mint extract
12 dr Green food coloring
1 c  Finely chopped bittersweet
-chocolate
In bowl, sprinkle gelatin over water and let stand to soften. Heat 1
cup cream in saucepan to scalding, then remove from heat. Stir in
gelatin and sugar until gelatin is dissolved. Stir in remaining 2
cups cream, evaporated milk, vanilla and mint extracts and food color.

Freeze in cream maker according to manufacturer's directions until
thickened. Fold in chopped chocolate. Spoon into chilled leaf dish.
Freeze. Makes 1 quart, or 8 servings.
