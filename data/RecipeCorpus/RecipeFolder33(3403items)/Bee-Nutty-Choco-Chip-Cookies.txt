Title: Bee Nutty Choco-Chip Cookies
Categories: Snacks, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 16 servings
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
6 oz Chocolate morsels
1/2 c  Roasted peanuts
Combine honey, peanut butter, butter and brown sugar in large bowl;
beat until light and fluffy.  Add egg and vanilla; mix thoroughly.
Combine flour, soda and salt; mix well.  Stir into peanut butter
mixture.  Stir in chocolate morsels and peanuts.  Using 1/4 cup for
each cookie, drop onto ungreased cookie sheet; flatten slightly.
Bake at 350 F 8 to 10 minutes or until lightly browned.  Remove to
rack and cool. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-14-95
