Title: Sugarless Raisin Cookies
Categories: Diabetic, Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 cookies
3/4 c  Raisins
2/3 c  Water;
1 tb Liquid sugar substitute
1 ts Cinnamon;
1/4 ts Nutmeg;
2 tb Margarine;
1    Egg;
1 c  Flour;
1 ts Baking powder;
1/4 ts Salt;
METHOD:  Combine raisins, water, sweetener, spices, and margarine in
saucepan.  Bring to a boil and cook 3 minutes.  Cool.  Stir in egg.
Mix flour, baking powder, and salt.  Add to fruit mixture and mix
well. Drop by spoonful onto a lightly greased baking sheet.  Bake in
350 degree oven for 9 to 10 minutes until light brown. YIELD: 12
servings (2 cookies)

Food Exchanges per serving.  1 BREAD EXCHANGE + 1 FAT EXCHANGE' CAL:
95; CHO: 16mg; PRO: 2g; FAT: 3g; NA: 110mg;
