Title: Chocolate Milk Soda/ Shake
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Boiling water
2 c  Granulated sugar
1/8 ts Salt
1 c  Cocoa powder; sifted
1 ts Vanilla
Recipe by: Jo Anne Merrill Preparation Time: 0:10

In a saucepan, combine 2 cups boiling water, 2 cups sugar, salt and
cocoa powder. Cook and stir over medium heat until smooth.  Cool then
add vanilla. Pour into covered jar and store in refrigerator.  Yield:
3 cups syrup.
