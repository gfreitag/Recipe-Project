Title: Pineapple Mustard Salad Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Fruit, Low cal/fat
Yield: 2 cups
1 c  Apple juice
1 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Pineapple pieces
2 ea Garlic cloves
1/4 ea Avocado, peeled
2 ts Prepared mustard
1/2 ts Tamari
Puree all ingredients in a food processor or blender.  Transfer to a
jar & seal tightly until ready to use.

Goes very well with a tossed salad
