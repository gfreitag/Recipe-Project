Title: Beehives
Categories: Breakfast, Candies, Cake/cookie
Yield: 7 servings
1/4 c  Honey
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Coconut
1 c  Walnuts
1 c  Dates
2 tb Flour
In mixing bowl, combine honey, egg and vanilla.  Beat until well
blended. Stir in coconut and nuts.  Coat dates with 2 tablespoons
flour.  Add to mixture.  Drop by tablespoonfuls onto a greased cookie
sheet.  Bake at 325 degrees F 12 minutes or until slightly brown.
