Title: Tomato Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------YIELD: 3/4 POUND FRESH PASTA---------------------
1 3/4 c  Unbleached all-purpose or
-semolina flour
2 tb Tomato paste
1 lg Egg
1 tb Vegetable oil
Add the flour, tomato paste, egg, and oil to the bowl of a food
processor or electric mixer.  Mix and knead the dough, let it rest,
roll it out, and cut the pasta to size desired. Pasta Salads! From
the collection of Jim Vorheis
