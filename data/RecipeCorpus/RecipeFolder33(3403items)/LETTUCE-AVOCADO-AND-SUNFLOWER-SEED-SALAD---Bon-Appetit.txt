Title: LETTUCE AVOCADO AND SUNFLOWER SEED SALAD - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 1/2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 1/2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Minced garlic
1 tb Mayonnaise
2 sm Heads Bibb lettuce, torn
-into bite-size pieces
1/3 c  Sunflower seeds (about 2 oz)
2    Avocados, peeled, pitted,
-sliced
Combine olive oil, red wine vinegar, balsamic vinegar and garlic in
small bowl and whisk to blend. Whisk in mayonnaise. Season dressing
to taste with salt and pepper.

Combine lettuce and sunflower seeds in bowl; toss with enough
dressing to coat. Season with salt and pepper. Top with avocado
slices. Pass remaining dressing separately.
