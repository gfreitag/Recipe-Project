Title: Aunt Minnie's Oatmeal Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Lard or shortening
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs, beaten
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1 1/2 c  Flour
3 c  Oatmeal
1/2 c  Nuts, chopped
Beat shortening and sugars well. Add eggs and vanilla and beat again.
Stir in remaining ingredients. Shape into a loaf. chill overnight. In
the morning, slice and bake 10 minutes in a 350 degree oven.
