Title: Perfectly Set Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 lb Cranberries
4 c  Sugar
2 1/2 c  Boiling water
1. Bring water to a boil; add cranberries and cook until berries have
popped. 2. Add sugar; cook until the sugar is completely dissolved.
Pour into a serving bowl, let cool slightly, then refrigerate until
properly set.
