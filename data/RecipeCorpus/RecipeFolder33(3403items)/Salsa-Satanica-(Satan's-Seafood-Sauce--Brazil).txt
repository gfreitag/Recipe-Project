Title: Salsa Satanica (Satan's Seafood Sauce- Brazil)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fish/seafoo, Vegetables, So. america
Yield: 1 -1/2 cups
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Crushed onion
1 tb Tarragon vinegar
1/2 ts Garlic salt
2 ts Bottled hot sauce -OR-
-Salsa de Habanero
1/4 c  Brandy
...a surprisingly spicy sauce that is served in Buenos Aires
restaurants and is also called fisherman's sauce.  Serve it over any
kind of seafood.

Melt the butter in a saucepan.  Add the ketchup, Worcestershire
sauce, onion, vinegar, garlic salt, and hot/habanero sauce.  Simmer
for 5 minutes. Add the brandy and simmer 5 more minutes.  Remove from
heat and cool.

Authors' heat scale: medium
