Title: Western Carolina Style
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Ketchup
1 c  Brown sugar -- firm packed
1/2 c  Lemon juice
3 tb Butter
1/4 c  Onion -- minced
1 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Place all ingredients in saucepan. Bring to boil and simmer 30 min.,or
until slightly thickened. Makes 1 1/2 cups.
