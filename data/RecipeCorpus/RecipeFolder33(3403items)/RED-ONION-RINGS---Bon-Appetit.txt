Title: RED-ONION RINGS - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 2 servings
1 lg Red onion, cut into 1/2
-inch-thick rounds,
-separated into rings
2 c  Buttermilk
1 1/2 ts Ground cumin
Vegetable oil (for deep
-frying)
3/4 c  All purpose flour
1/4 c  Cornmeal
1 ts Salt
1 ts Ground pepper
Combine onion rings, buttermilk and 1 t ground cumin in large bowl.
Let soak 1 hour, stirring occasionally.

Pour oil into large saucepan to depth of 1 1/2 inches; heat to 375'F.
Mix flour, cornmeal, salt, pepper and remaining 1/2 t cumin in cake
pan. Drain onion rings. Working in batches, add a few onion rings to
flour mixture and turn to coat. Add to oil and fry until golden
brown, about 45 seconds. Drain on paper towels. Sprinkle with salt
and pepper and serve.
