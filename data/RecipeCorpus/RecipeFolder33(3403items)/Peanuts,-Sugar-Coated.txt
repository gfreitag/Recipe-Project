Title: Peanuts, Sugar Coated
Categories: Holiday, Nuts/grains, Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Water
2 c  Raw shelled peanuts -- skins
On
Preheat oven at 300.  Dissolve sugar in water in a saucepan over
medium heat.  Add peanuts and continue to cook over medium heat,
stirring frequently.  Cook until peanuts are completely sugared
(coated and no syrup).  Pour on a unprepared baking sheet, separate
peanuts with a fork. Bake for 30 minutes, stirring at 10 minute
intervals.  Makes 2 1/2 cups.
