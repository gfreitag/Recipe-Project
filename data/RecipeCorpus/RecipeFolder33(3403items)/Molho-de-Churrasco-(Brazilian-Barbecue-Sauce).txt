Title: Molho de Churrasco (Brazilian Barbecue Sauce)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill, Vegetables, So. america
Yield: 4 cups
8 ea Dried Malagueta chiles;
-seeds and stems removed,
-crushed  -OR-
8 ea Piquin or Thai chiles
3 c  Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
2 cl Garlic; crushed
1 sm Onion; minced
2 tb Italian parsley; minced
1 ts Rosemary; crushed
2 ts Fresh basil leaves; minced
2 ts Fresh thyme leaves; minced
2 ts Fresh marjoram or oregano
-leaves; minced
Combine all ingredients in a glass jar, cover, and shake well.  Let
stand for at least 2 hours to blend the flavors.  Store in a
refrigerator until ready to use.

Authors' heat scale: Medium

_Hot and Spicy Latin Dishes_  from Chili Pepper Magazine Dave DeWitt,
