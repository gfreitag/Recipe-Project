Title: Strawberry Margarita
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Tequila
1/2 oz Strawberry liqueur
1 oz Fresh lime juice
2 oz Frozen strawberries -- in
Syrup
Granulated sugar
Cracked ice
Pour granulated sugar on a small plate. Rub the rim of a chilled
cocktail glass with the lime wedge and dip the rim of the glass in
the sugar until it is coated with a frosting ofsugar.

Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add tequila, strawberry
liqueur, lime juice, and frozen strawberries. Shake and strain into
