Title: Squash Pickles
Categories: Can/preserv, Pickles, Vegetables
Yield: 2 pints
4 md Yellow squash; sliced 1/4
-inch thick
1/2 c  Pickling salt
2 sm Onions; thinly sliced
1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Vinegar (5% acidity)
3 tb Dry mustard
1 tb Ground ginger
1 tb Curry powder
6 ea Whole peppercorns
Layer squash and salt in a large glass or plastic container.
Cover and let stand about 4 hours.  Rinse squash several times in
cold water; drain well.
Place squash and onion in a large Dutch oven.  Combine remaining
ingredients in a small, heavy saucepan, mixing well.  Bring to a
boil; boil 5 minutes, stirring often.
Pour vinegar mixture over squash; bring mixture to a boil, and
cook 5 minutes or just until squash is crisp-tender.
Pack into hot sterilized jars, leaving 1/4-inch headspace.  Cover
at once with metal lids, and screw bands tight.  Process in
boiling-water bath for 15 minutes.
