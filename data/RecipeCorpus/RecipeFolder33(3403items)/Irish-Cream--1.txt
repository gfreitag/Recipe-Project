Title: Irish Cream -1
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
9 1/2 oz Whiskey  -- (I use Jamesons
Iris
1 lg Can Evaporated Milk
1 lg Can Condensed Milk
1/2    To 3/4 Tbs Powdered Instant
-Coffee (not crystals)
2 ts Vanilla
2 ts Glycerine  -- * see note
Blend together for 10 minutes.  A countertop mixer works well.
