Title: Party Spiced Pecans
Categories: Appetizers, Holiday
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg whites
1 lb Pecan halves
Combine sugar, cinnamon and salt in a small bowl; set aside. In a
large mixing bowl, lightly beat egg white. Add pecans and stir until
coated. Sprinkle sugar mixture over pecans; mix well.

Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet. Bake at 300 degrees for 20
minutes. Remove nuts from baking sheet while warm to cool on waxed
paper.

Yield: About 6 cups.

: Jo Anne Merrill
