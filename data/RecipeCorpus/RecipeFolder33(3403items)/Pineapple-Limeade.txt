Title: Pineapple Limeade
Categories: Alcohol, Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
1 1/2 tb Lime juice
1/2 c  Club soda
Ice cubes
Mix all ingredients except club soda; refrigerate until well chilled.

Just before serving, stir in the club soda. Serve in a highball or
collins glass over ice cubes. Garnish with a slice of lime.
