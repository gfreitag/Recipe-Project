Title: LAYERED SALAD WITH CUMIN-HONEY VINAIGRETTE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 6 servings
1 lg Red onion, cubed
2 lg Avocados, peeled, pitted,
-cubed
1 lg Red bell pepper, seeded,
-cubed
1    Cucumber, peeled, seeded,
-cubed
Cumin-Honey Vinaigrette
(separate recipe)
12 c  Mixed greens (such as curly
-endive, spinach & romaine)
Combine onion, avocados, bell pepper and cucumber in large bowl,. Mix
in 3/4 C vinaigrette. Let stand at room temperature 15 minutes.

Place greens in large bowl and toss with 1/4 C vinaigrette. Season
greens with salt and pepper. Arrange half of greens in clear bowl.
Top with half of vegetables. Repeat with remaining greens and
vegetables. Serve, passing remaining vinaigrette separately.
