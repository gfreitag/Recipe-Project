Title: Stir Fried Broccoli and Carrots
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Ginger
1    Cl Garlic; chopped fine
1 1/2 c  Broccoli flowerets; small
2 md Carrots; sliced thin
1 sm Onion; sliced
3/4 c  Chicken broth
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Water; cold
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
Recipe by: Anita A. Matejka Preparation Time: 0:15 Prepare a 12"
skillet with cooking spray; heat until hot.  Add ginger and garlic;
stir fry about 1 minute or until light brown.  Add broccoli, carrots,
and onion; stir fry 1 minute.  Stir in broth and salt; stir into
vegetable mixture. Cook and stir 10 seconds or until thickened.  Add
water and mushrooms; cook and stir 30 seconds.
