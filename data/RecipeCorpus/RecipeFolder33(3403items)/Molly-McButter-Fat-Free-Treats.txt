Title: Molly McButter Fat Free Treats
Categories: Snacks, Fatfree
Yield: 1 servings
6 c  Crispy rice cereal
10 oz Marshmallows
3 tb Molly McButter Natural
-Butter Flavor Sprinkles
3/4 ts Vanilla (optional)
3/4 ts Water
x  Non-stick cooking spray
o Spray large saucepan, spatula and a 13x9x2-inch pan with non-stick
spray.

o In loosely covered saucepan, melt marshmallows over low heat,
stirring until smooth, or microwave covered 2-3 minutes.

o Add Molly, water and vanilla; mix well.  Add cereal and stir quickly
until well coated.  Press mixture evenly into prepared pan.  Cut into
16 squares when cool.

Per serving:  0g fat; 100 calories; 185mg sodium; 0mg cholesterol

Source:  Molly McButter advertisement.
