Title: Tzaziki  (garlik-yogurt)
Categories: Greek, Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Yogurt (drain it very well)
2    Medium sized cucumber
6    ....... (pieces) garlic
Vinegar
Salt
Oil
Peel of the cucumber en cut it in small pieces. Drain it very well,
maybe squeeze it, and add a little salt to it. Add the garlic,
vinegar, yogurt and the oil and mix it very well. You can add pieces
paprika en olives to it.

You can change the number of ..... garlic to your own taste.
