Title: New Mexico Chili Verde Stew
Categories: Chili, Meats
Yield: 8 servings
2 lb Boneless Pork, Cut
-Into 1 1/2" Cubes
1 tb Vegetable Oil
36 oz Canned Corn Kernels,
-Drained
2    Stalks Celery, diced
2 md Potatoes, Diced
2 md Tomatoes, Chopped
12 oz Canned green chile
-peppers, diced
4 c  Chicken broth
2 ts Cumin, ground
1 ts Dried oregano
Salt to taste
Lightly brown the pork cubes in oil in a Dutch oven or deep skillet
over medium high heat. Add the rest of the ingredients. Cover. Simmer
for 1 hour. Serve hot with fresh corn or flour tortillas.
