Title: Brine for Smoked Salmon
Categories: Bbq/grill, Fish/seafoo, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Sugar
2 c  Soy sauce
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1 c  Dry white wine
1/4 c  NON-iodized salt
1 c  Water
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce
From : Sharon Stevens, Date: 03-21-94  14:28, Area: Home_cooking

Soak salmon chunks in brine for 8 or more hours in the refrigerator.

Smoke, following directions for your particular smoker (takes about 2
1/2 to 3 hours in a Mr. Smoker charcoal smoker.
