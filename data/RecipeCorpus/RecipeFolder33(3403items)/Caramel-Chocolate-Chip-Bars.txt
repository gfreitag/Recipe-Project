Title: Caramel Chocolate Chip Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 servings
1 pk Caramel cake mix
2    Eggs
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Margarine -- softened
1 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
Preheat oven to 375.  Grease 13 x 9 pan.  Combine cake mix, eggs,
water, brown sugar and shortening in large bowl.  Stir until well
blended. Mixture will be thick.  Stir in chocolate chips.  Spread in
greased pan. Bake for 24 to 27 minutes.  Cool completely in pan. (***)
