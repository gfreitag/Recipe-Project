Title: Zucchini Brownies
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
3 c  Zucchini -- grated
2/3 c  Applesauce
3 ts Vanilla
1 ts Coconut extract
3 c  Unbleached flour
1 1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Baking soda
1/3 c  Cocoa powder -- sifted
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a 8 x 15" baking pan with cooking spray
and flour.  In a mixing bowl, combine zucchini, applesauce, vanilla,
and coconut extract.  In another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar,
salt, baking powder, baking soda and cocoa powder.  Mix wet
ingredients with dry ingredients just until moistened.  Spread batter
in prepared pan.  Bake for 25 minutes.
