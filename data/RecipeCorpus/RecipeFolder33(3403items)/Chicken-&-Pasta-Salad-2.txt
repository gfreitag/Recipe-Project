Title: Chicken & Pasta Salad 2
Categories: Main dish, Pasta/rice, Poultry, Salads
Yield: 5 servings
3/4 c  Marie's Creamy Ranch
Dressing & Dip
18    Cherry tomatoes -- halved
1 md Cucumber -- peel, seed &
Slice
1/4    Red onion -- in wedges
2 cn Swanson Chunk Chicken (5 oz)
Drained
3 c  Cooked corkscrew macaroni (2
-1/2 c  dry)
3 tb Parsley -- chopped
In large bowl, combine dressing, tomatoes, cucumber, onion and
parsley. Add chicken and macaroni; toss gently to coat.  Cover;
refrigerate at least 4 hours before serving.  If desired, garnish
with fresh basil and additional red onion.
