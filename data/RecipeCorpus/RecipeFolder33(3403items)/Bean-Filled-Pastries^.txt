Title: Bean Filled Pastries^
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 25 servings
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
BH&G Holiday Appetizers 94
1 pk (10oz) refrigerated pizza
Dough
3/4 c  Canned refried beans, with
Or without sausage
1/4 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Salsa
1    Beaten egg
2 ts Water
Paprika
Salsa
Avacado dip
Sour cream
Unroll pizza dough. Roll into a 15" square on a lightly floured
surface. Let dough rest 10 minutes. Cut into 25 3" squares.

Stir together refried beans, onions and the 2 tablespoons salsa.
Spoon a heaping teaspoon filling on each dough square. Brush edges of
each square woth water. Lift one corner and stretch dough to opposite
side, forming a rectangle. Press edges together and seal well with
fork or fingers,.

Place pastries on a greased baking sheet. Prick tops with fork.
Combine the egg and 2 teaspoons water. Brush pastries with mixture.
Sprinkle with paprika.

Bake at 400F. about 12 minutes or till golden. Serve hot with salsa,
avacado dip and sour cream. Makes 25
