Title: Green Potato Pies
Categories: Kids, Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
4    Baking potatoes
1/4 c  Milk
1 tb Margarine
Shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 c  Cooked spinach -- chopped
Place potatoes and cut them in half lenthwise.  Scoop the potatoes
from the skins into a mixing bowl.  Add milk and margarine and mash
the potatoes. Stir in cheese and spinach.  Then scoop the mixture
back into the potato skins.  Place on a baking sheet and bake at 350
for 10 minutes.
