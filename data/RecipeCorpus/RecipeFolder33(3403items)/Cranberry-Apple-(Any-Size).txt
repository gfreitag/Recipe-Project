Title: Cranberry Apple (Any Size)
Categories: Breads, Fruit
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Apples -- peeled/shredded
1 c  Cranberries -- chopped
1 c  Carrots -- shredded
2    Egg whites -- whipped
1/2 c  Applesauce
2 1/2 c  Unbleached flour
1 1/3 c  Granulated sugar
1 tb Baking powder
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
2 ts Cinnamon
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine apples, cranberries carrots, egg whites, and
applesauce.  In another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking
powder, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon.  Fill prepared pan(s) 2/3
full.  Bake for 20 to 25 minutes depending on pan size.
