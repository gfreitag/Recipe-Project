Title: Neysa Koger's Ranger Cookies
Categories: Newspaper, Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 6 doz cooky
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs; beaten
2 c  Flour; all-purpose
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Rolled oats
2 c  Rice Krispies
1 c  Peanut butter chips
1 c  Nuts; chopped
Cream the first four ingredients.  Add the remaining ingredients in
the order given.

Drop by teaspoonfuls on an ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 350Ã¸F for
about 10 minutes or until lightly golden brown.  Cool on a wire rack.

Nutritional Information per cookie:  103 calories, 6g fat, 12g
carbohydrates, 6mg cholesterol, 80mg sodium
