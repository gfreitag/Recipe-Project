Title: Broccoli And Cheese Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Broccoli; florets
2 tb Olive oil
1 c  Chicken broth
2 oz Cheese; cubed
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 tb Parmesan cheese
8 oz Angel hair pasta
Recipe by: The Healing Foods Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15

In a large skillet, stir fry broccoli in oil until crisp and tender.
Transfer to a plate and set aside.  Add the broth, cheese, thyme, and
pepper to the pan. Whisk until creamy.  Return the broccoli to the
pan, stir to coat, and remove for the heat. Sprinkle with Parmesan
cheese.  In a large pot of boiling water, cook pasta until tender.
Drain and add to the broccoli mixture. Toss to combine.
