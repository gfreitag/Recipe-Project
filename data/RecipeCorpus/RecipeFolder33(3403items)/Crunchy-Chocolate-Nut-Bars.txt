Title: Crunchy Chocolate-Nut Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 24 servings
MMMMM---------------------------CRUST--------------------------------
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Packed light brown sugar
1    Egg yolk
1 ts Vanilla extract
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Rolled oats -- not instant
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
3    Semisweet chocolate squares
1 tb Margarine
1/2 c  Nuts -- chopped
1. Crust: In large bowl, beat margarine, sugar, egg yolk, and vanilla
until smooth, using an electric mixer. Add flour and oats; stir until
well combined. 2. Press mixture in bottom of a lightly greased 13 x 9
x 2-inch pan. Bake in preheated 375-degree oven for 15 minutes or
until golden brown. Cool slightly. 3. Topping: Melt chocolate and
margarine over hot, not boiling, water. Spread over warm cookie
crust. Sprinkle evenly with coarsely chopped nuts. 4. While still
warm, cut into bars using a sharp knife. Let cool completely before
removing from pan.

Yield: about 2 dozen bars.
