Title: Herby Grilled Potatoes
Categories: Bbq/grill, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
3/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1 ts Dried oregano leaves,
-crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Pepper
6 md Potatoes
2 md Onions, thinly sliced and
-cut in half crosswise
In saucepan, melt margarine.  Add celery; cook until tender.  Stir in
oregano, salt, garlic powder and pepper.  Cutting not quite through,
slit potatoes into 1/2 inch slices.  Place each potato on a piece of
foil large enough to wrap potato. Drizzle about half of the margarine
mixture into the slits.  Insert a half slice of onion into each slit.
Drizzle with remaining margarine. Bring edges of foil together; seal
securely with double fold. Grill over hot coals 40 to 45 minutes.
Makes 6 servings. Source: Better Homes & Gardens "GOOD FOOD ON A
