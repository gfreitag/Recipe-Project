Title: PAPAYA-WATERMELON SALSA - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salsa/sauce, Condiments
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Diced peeled seeded papaya
1/2 c  Diced seeded watermelon
1/4 c  Diced English hothouse
-cucumber (unpeeled)
1/4 c  Minced red onion
3 tb Fresh lime juice
2 tb Chopped fresh cilantro or
-basil
2 ts Olive oil (preferably
-extra-virgin)
1 ts Minced seeded jalapeno chili
Combine all ingredients in medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
(Can be made 1 hour ahead. Cover and chill.)

NOTE: Good topper for grilled mahimahi, swordfish, tuna or salmon.
For the freshest flavor and texture, serve the salsa within 1 hour of
preparing it.
