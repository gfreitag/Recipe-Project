Title: Spicy Fish
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Olive oil
4 oz Fish (1 inch thick)
2 ts Thyme
1 ts Orange peel; grated
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1    Cl Garlic; crushed
Recipe by: Cooking Light Magazine Preparation Time: 0:20 Preheat oven
at 400.  Spread oil over the bottom of a shallow baking dish; add
salmon, turning to coat. In a small mixing bowl, combine thyme,
orange peel, salt pepper, and garlic.  Rub fish with mixture; cover
and marinate in refrigerator 1 hour.  Bake 18 minutes or until fish
flakes easily when tested with a fork.
