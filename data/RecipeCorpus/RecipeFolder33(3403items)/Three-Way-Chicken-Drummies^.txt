Title: Three Way Chicken Drummies^
Categories: Poultry, Appetizers
Yield: 36 servings
36    Frozen chicken wing
Sections
MMMMM-----------------BARBECUE SAUCE WITH OLIVES----------------------
1 cn (18oz) barbecue sauce
1 cn (2 1/4oz) sliced ripe pitted
Olives
ds Hot pepper sauce, to taste
MMMMM-------------------CHEESE AND SALSA SAUCE------------------------
1 cn (8oz) cheese spread
1 pk (3oz) cream cheese, cubed
3/4 c  Chunky salsa
1 ts Prepared horseradish
MMMMM----------------------FIVE SPICE SAUCE---------------------------
3/4 c  Apple juice
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Dry sherry
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
1/4 ts Five spice powder
Bake frozen chicken wings in a 15x10x1" baking pan at 450F. until no
longer pink, 30-35 minutes.

Meanwhile, make desired sauce. Add cooked chicken to sauce and stir
to coat evenly. Heat through. Serve chicken with tongs and plenty of
napkins.

BBQ Sauce: Heat all ingredients until hot. Add chicken; heat through.

Cheese & Salsa: Heat cheese spread and cream cheese over low heat
just till melted. Stir in salsa and horseradish. Add chicken; heat
through.

Five Spice: Combine all ingredients and cook until thickened and
bubbly. Cook and stir 1 minute more. Add chicken and heat through.
