Title: HONEY-BRAN MUFFINS WITH CHEDDAR CHEESE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Breads, Muffins
Yield: 12 muffins
1/4 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Unprocessed wheat bran*
1 tb Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
Pinch of salt
1 c  Buttermilk
1/3 c  Honey
6 tb (3/4 stick) unsalted butter,
-melted, cooled
1 lg Egg, beaten to blend
1/4 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
-cheese (about 4 oz)
Position rack in center of oven and preheat to 400'F, Line twelve
1/3-C muffin cups with paper muffin liners. Combine first 6
ingredients in large bowl. Whisk buttermilk, honey, melted butter,
egg and vanilla in medium bowl to blend. Add to dry ingredients and
mix just until combined. Fold in cheese. Divide batter equally among
prepared muffin cups. Bake until tops are golden brown and tester
inserted into center of muffins comes out clean, about 20 minutes.
Transfer muffin tin to rack and cool at least 15 minutes.

*Available at natural foods stores and in the cereal or health food
section of most supermarkets across the country.
