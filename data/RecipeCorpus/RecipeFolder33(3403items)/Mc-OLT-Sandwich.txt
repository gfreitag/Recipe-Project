Title: Mc OLT Sandwich
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 1 sandwich
2    Slices whole wheat bread
1    Tomato; sliced
1    Red onion; sliced
Lettuce leaves
Low sodium, oil free Russian
-or Italian salad dressing
Toast the bread.  Spread some dressing on each slice, layer the
tomatoes, and onions on one slice, top with lettuce leaves, and close
the sandwich. From The McDougall Program by John McDougall Typed by
Lisa Greenwood Submitted By LISA GREENWOOD   On   08-04-95
