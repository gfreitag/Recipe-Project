Title: Apfelsine Pudding (Orange Pudding)
Categories: Aust/german, Puddings, Fruit
Yield: 1 recipe
4 lg Oranges
1 c  Sugar
1 qt Milk
3    Egg Yolks
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Cornstarch
3    Egg Whites
4 tb Sugar
Peel and slice oranges, put in flat 9x9x2-inch pan and sprinkle with
1 cup sugar.

Make aboiled custard of milk, yolks of 3 eggs (well beaten), 1/2 cup
sugar, and cornstarch.

When cool pour over oranges; over this spread the whites of the eggs
beaten with 4 tablespoons of sugar.  Place in slow oven at 325Ã¸ F.
until brown.
