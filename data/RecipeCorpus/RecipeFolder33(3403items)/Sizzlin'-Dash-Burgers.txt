Title: Sizzlin' Dash Burgers
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb Ground beef
3 tb Water
1/4 c  Mrs. Dash Onion & Herb
o In large bowl, combine all ingredients.  Mix thoroughly.

o Shape into six 3/4-inch thick patties, and grill or broil.

o For variety, try recipe with Mrs. Dash Garlic & Herb or Mrs. Dash
Table Blend.

Source:  Mrs. Dash advertisement.
