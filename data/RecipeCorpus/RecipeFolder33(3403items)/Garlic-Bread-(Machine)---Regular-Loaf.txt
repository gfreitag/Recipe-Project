Title: Garlic Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
3/4 c  Water
2 c  + 1T White bread flour
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 tb Parmesan, fresh/grated
1/2 ts Sweet basil
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Garlic paste
3/4 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
1 1/2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

The aroma of this bread is "to die for" - and it butters up to make
the easiest garlic toast around!
Success Hints
-------------

Grate your own fresh parmesan cheese.  Pre-ground cheeses often have
salt in them.

1/4 t finely-chopped or pressed garlic may be substituted for paste.

Use regular or rapid bake cycles.
