Title: Zucchini with Parmesan Cheese (Microwave)
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1 3/4 lb Very thinly sliced zucchini
1 ea Lemon; squeezed for juice
3 tb Roast chicken cooking liquid
-or chicken stock
1 1/2 tb Garlic powder
Salt & pepper
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
Put the zucchini in a dish and pour the lemon juice and the chicken
cooking juice (or stock) over.  Sprinkle with garlic powder and
season with salt and pepper.  Cover and microwave on HIGH for 8
minutes. Leave to stand for 3 minutes.
Transfer the zucchini to a serving dish.  Add the butter and Parmesan
cheese.  Stir and serve hot.

Note: The author recommends serving this dish with Roast Chicken, but
notes that it is good with (and can use the juices of) any roast meat.
