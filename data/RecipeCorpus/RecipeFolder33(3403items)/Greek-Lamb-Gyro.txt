Title: Greek Lamb Gyro
Categories: Sandwich, Greek, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM---------------------YIELD: 6 SERVINGS--------------------------
2 md Onions; peeled
2 md Tomatoes; peeled
1/3 c  Olive or salad oil
2 ea Tb Wine vinegar
Salt and pepper, to taste
6 ea Rounds pita bread
1 1/2 lb Hot thinly sliced
-garlic-flavored roast lamb
-OR
-spiced beef, cut in strips
2 c  Lettuce; shredded
1 ts Dried oregano
Cut onions from top to bottom into thin slices.  Cut tomatoes from
top to bottom into thin slices.  Put into two small bowls and set
aside. Combine oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a small bottle or
bowl. Heat Pita bread in a preheated 350F oven about 10 minutes,
until warm. Fold each pita bread in half across the center.  Fill
each with some strips of cooked lamb or beef.  Sprinkle with a little
of the oil-vinegar mixture. Top with some shredded lettuce, sliced
onions and tomatoes, and oregano. Sprinkle again with oil-vinegar
mixture. Serve at once.
