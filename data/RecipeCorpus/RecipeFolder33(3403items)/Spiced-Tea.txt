Title: Spiced Tea
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Pineapple juice
1/2 c  Lemon juice
3 c  Water; cold
3/4 c  Brown sugar; packed
3    Cinnamon sticks
1 ts Cloves
2 ts Allspice
4    Tea bags
Recipe by: Elizabeth Powell Preparation Time: 0:35 Bring pineapple
juice, lemon juice, and water to a boil.  Add sugar and spices and
simmer 20 minutes.  Remove from heat, add tea bags, cover and steep
10 minutes. Remove tea bags and strain. Serve hot or iced.
