Title: Tangy Honey French Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Salad oil
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Salt -- to taste
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Lemon zest
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 ts Celery seed -- optional
1    Garlic clove -- optional
Place all ingredients into a screw-type jar. Shake vigorously. If
using the garlic: cut clove in half, add to dressing and chill for 12
hours or more before using. Remove garlic clove before serving.

This makes about 1-1/4 cup dressing.

: Jo Anne Merrill
