Title: Karen's Sweet Potato Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Margarine
4    Eggs
2 c  Sweet potatoes -- grated
2 c  Milk
1 c  Corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla
1. Grate potatoes in food processor or with hand grater. 2. Blend
sugar and margarine in bowl. Add well-beaten eggs; mix thoroughly.
Add potatoes, milk, corn syrup and vanilla; blend. 3. Pour into
casserole. Bake in preheated 300-degree oven for 1 hour, or until
knife inserted in center comes out clean. Serve warm or cold with
whipped cream or ice cream if desired.

: Jo Anne Merrill
