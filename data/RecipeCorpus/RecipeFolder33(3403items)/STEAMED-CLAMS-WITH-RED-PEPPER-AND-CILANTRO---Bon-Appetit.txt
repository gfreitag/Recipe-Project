Title: STEAMED CLAMS WITH RED PEPPER AND CILANTRO - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Dry white wine
2 tb Finely chopped garlic
3/4 ts Dried crushed red pepper
3 lb Clams, scrubbed
1/3 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
Crusty French bread
Bring wine, garlic and red pepper to boil in heavy large pot. Add
clams, cover and steam until clams open, about 8 mnutes. Discard any
clams that do not open. Using tongs, transfer clams to bowl. Add
cilantro to cooking liquid in pot. Season with salt and pepper. Pour
liquid over clams. Serve with bread.
