Title: Creamsicle
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 oz Vodka
1/2 oz Triple Sec -- or Cointreau
1/2 oz Orange juice
1    Scoop
Vanilla ice cream
Add all ingredients in blender and blend at high speed until smooth,
about 30 seconds. Serve with straw in dessert or cocktail glass.
Tastes just like a Dreamsicle!
