Title: Tocana cu Frunza de Aluat(Stew with pastry wrap)literal t
Categories: Meats, Romanian, Soup/stew, Pastry
Yield: 1 servings
1 x  Tocana de Vita Beef Stew
-recipe
Make tocana de vita and add 1 tbs flour sprinkled over stew. Pour into
casserole dish and cover with pie crust, prick and season with
paprikash. bake for 35 to 40 min or until crust is brown. Serves 6.
