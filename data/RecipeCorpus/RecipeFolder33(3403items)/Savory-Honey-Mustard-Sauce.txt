Title: Savory Honey Mustard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
1 cn Heinz Fat Free or HomeStyle
-Chicken Gravy (12 oz)
1 cn Honey mustard or sweet hot
-mustard (4 oz; 1/2 cup)
Combine ingredients in a small saucepan. Heat thoroughly. Thin sauce
with a little milk, if necessary. Serve over ham or pork. Makes about
1 3/4 cups. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-14-95
