Title: Fiesta Tacos
Categories: Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 cn Campbell's Fiesta Tomato
-Soup
8    Taco shells
1 c  Shredded lettuce
1    Tomato, chopped
1 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
Prep Time: 10 minutes. Cook Time: 10 minutes.

1. In skillet cook beef until browned, stirring to separate meat.
Pour off fat.

2. Add soup and heat. Divide meat mixture among taco shells. Top with
lettuce, tomato and cheese.
