Title: Sophie's Favorite Cabbage
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 8 servings
1    Head cabbage -- sliced
4 sl Bacon -- chopped
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1    Chicken bouillon cube
1 c  Water
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Caraway seed
Saute the bacon until cooked.  Add the remaining ingredients and cook,
covered, until cabbage begins to turn soft.  Bring to a boil, then
reduce heat and simmer, covered, for about an hour and 15 minutes.
