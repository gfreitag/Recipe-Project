Title: Pork Chops With Apple Rings
Categories: Fruit, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
6    Pork chops
4    Apples -- * see note
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2 tb Margarine
* Use apples that are unpeeled, cored, and sliced into rings.

Brown chops on both sides. Place apples rings in greased baking dish.
Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon. Dot with margarine. Place pork
chops on top of apples. Cover. Bake in preheated 350-degree oven for
1-1/2 hours.
