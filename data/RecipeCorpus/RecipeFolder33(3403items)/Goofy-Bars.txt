Title: Goofy Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 ea Boxed cake mix (yellow or
-white)
2 ea Eggs broken into a one cup
-measuring cup
Water to fill cup to 2/3
-cup line
1 c  Brown sugar
Mix all and put into 9X13 baking pan. Cover top with marshmallows and
chocolate chips. Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees. My notes: Try spice
cake mix and butterscotch chips. Finely chopped nuts might taste
pretty good, too.
