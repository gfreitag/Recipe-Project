Title: Italian Popcorn
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 servings
1 qt Popcorn; popped
1 ts Italian seasoning
1/8 ts Cayenne; or to taste
1 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
Recipe by: The Joslin Diabetes Gourmet Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15
Preheat oven at 400.  Prepare cookie sheet with cooking spray.  Place
popped popcorn on a prepared baking sheet.  Toss combination of
seasonings for the flavor.  Bake for 10 minutes, tossing once.
