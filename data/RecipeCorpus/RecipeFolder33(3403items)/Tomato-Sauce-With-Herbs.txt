Title: Tomato Sauce With Herbs
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Olive oil
2    Cl Garlic; minced
1/2 c  Bell peppers; chopped
2 c  Tomatoes; cored/halved/seede
2 tb Basil
2 tb Parsley
2 ts Oregano
2 ts Marjoram
1 ts Rosemary
Recipe by: Pasta Light Preparation Time: 0:20 In a skillet, heat oil
over moderate heat.  Add garlic and peppers until tender. Add
tomatoes, basil, parsley, oregano, marjoram, and rosemary. Cook just
until tomatoes' juices thicken, 7 to 10 minutes.  Pour over cooked
pasta.
