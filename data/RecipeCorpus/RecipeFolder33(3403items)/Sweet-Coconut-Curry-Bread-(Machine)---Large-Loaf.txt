Title: Sweet Coconut Curry Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 1/4 c  Water
3 c  White bread flour
2 tb Dry milk
2 tb Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Coconut flakes
1 tb Curry powder
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

Colorful and unexpected, this bread is as distinctive as the country
inspired it.  The raisins and coconut lend a slight sweetness, but the
flavor and aroma are distinctly curry.  Serve it with your favoritet
rice and meat dish.

Success Hints
-------------

If you like more kick in your curry, use hot curry.

Use regular, rapid or delayed time bake cycle.
