Title: Spam Ball
Categories: Spam, Appetizers
Yield: 8 servings
1 cn Spam (12 oz)
4 tb Pimiento-stuffed olives
1 tb Horseradish mustard
Hot pepper sauce to taste
Carla Scott of Chester, Idaho, won first place in the recent Empire
Fair Spam recipe competition in Sioux Falls. Her creation, an
appetizer called Spam Ball, earned her a $100 first prize and the
right to compete in the national Spam recipe competition.

Blend the above ingredients together in a blender. Form in a ball on a
serving dish. Chill.

Mix 4 ounces softened cream cheese with 2 teaspoons milk; frost Spam
ball. Chill.

Remove from refrigerator 15 minutes before serving. Trim with parsley
flakes.
