Title: BRUSCHETTA WITH BRAISED ESCAROLE & ROASTED GARLIC - Bon A
Categories: Bon appetit, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
2    Whole heads garlic
2 ts Plus 1 T olive oil
-(preferably extra-virgin)
1 1/2 lb Escarole, trimmed, cut
-into 1-inch pieces
1 lg Garlic clove, minced
4    French bread slices
-(about 4x6 inches each),
-halved, toasted
1    Lemon, cut into 8 wedges
Preheat oven to 350'F, Cut 1/2 inch off top end (opposite root end)
of each garlic head, exposing top of garlic cloves. Remove any loose
papery outer skin. Place garlic cut side up in small glass baking
dish. Drizzle with 1 t oil. Cover dish vath foil and roast garlic 30
minutes. Turn garlic cut side down. Roast uncovered until tender,
about 30 minutes. Cool to room temperature.

Separate garlic into individual cloves. Pinch each clove at root end
to release garlic into small bowl. Mash with fork. Stir in 1 t oil.
Season with salt and pepper. (Can be prepared 3 days ahead. Cover and
refrigerate. Bring to room temperature before using.)

Cook escarole in large pot of boiling salted water until just tender,
about 4 minutes. Drain well. Heat remaining 1 T oil in heavy large
skillet over medium heat. Add 1 minced garlic clove; saute 30
seconds. Add escarole and saute until heated through, about 3
minutes. Season with salt and pepper.

Spread roasted garlic puree on bread slices. Using tongs, place
escarole atop garlic. Serve with lemon wedges.
