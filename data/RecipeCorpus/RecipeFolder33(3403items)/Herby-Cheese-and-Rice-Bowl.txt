Title: Herby Cheese and Rice Bowl
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian, Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 unknown
c  White rice
2 tb Olive oil
3 tb Chopped fresh basil or
-parsley or a mixture of the
-two=OR= 1tb dried herbs
1 1/2 c  Mozzarella cheese; chopped
2 tb TO 3 tb grated Parmesan
-cheese
If necessary, wash the rice in cold water once or twice, then drain.
Cover the rice with twice its volume of water (1 cup rice to 2 cups
water) in a medium-sized saucepan.  Bring to a boil, then cover the
pan, reduce the heat and leave the rice to simmer for aboout 20
minutes or until all the water is absorbed.
Empty the rice into a large bowl and stir in the olive oil and
herbs. Stir the Mozzarella into the hot rice - the Mozzarella must
melt. Sprinkle the Parmesan over the top of the dish and serve
immediately.
30 minutes to make.  Good source of protein, vitamin A, calcium,
iron.
Source:  Linda McCartney's Home Cooking.
