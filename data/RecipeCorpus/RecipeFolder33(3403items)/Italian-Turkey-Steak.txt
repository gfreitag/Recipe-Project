Title: Italian Turkey Steak
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
4    Honeysuckle White Turkey
-Steaks
1/2 c  Non-fat Italian salad
-dressing
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs, mixed
-with
1 ts Dried Italian seasoning
1 tb Olive oil
Dip steaks in salad dressing, roll in seasoned crumbs. Spray skillet
with non-stick cooking spray. Saute in olive oil on both sides until
lightly browned and tender. If desired, serve on pasta with low-fat
marinara sauce and a sprinkling of parmesan cheese.

350 calories; 10 grams of fat.
