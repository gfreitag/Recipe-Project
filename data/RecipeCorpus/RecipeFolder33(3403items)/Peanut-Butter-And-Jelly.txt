Title: Peanut Butter And Jelly
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Unbleached flour
6 tb Granulated sugar
1 1/2 tb Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
6 tb Peanut butter
3/4 c  Skim milk
1/4 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
1    Egg white -- whipped
1/4 c  Grape or strawberry jelly
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray.  In a mixing
bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.  In another
mixing bowl, combine peanut butter, milk, applesauce, egg white, and
jelly.  Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients just until
moistened.  Bake for 25 to 30 minutes depending on pan size.
