Title: Savory Chickpeas
Categories: Indian, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Dried chickpeas
1 ts Ground tumeric
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Red pepper powder
Salt
Lime juice
Sort and wash the chickpeas.  Place the chickpeas in a bowl with
enough water to cover.  Soak for 8 hours or overnight.  Drain.  Place
the chickpeas in a large, heavy saucepan.  Add enough water to cover
by 2". Bring to a boil.  Add the tumeric.  Cover and simmer for 1 1/2
to 2 hours, or until the chickpeas are tender to the bite.  Drain the
chickpeas while hot.  In a bowl combine the chickpeas, cumin, red
pepper powder and lime juice to taste.  Add salt if desired.  Can be
made 1 day in advance. Refrigerate.  Serve at room temperature.  203
calories and 3 grams fat.
