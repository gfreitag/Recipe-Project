Title: Pasta with Spinach Pesto
Categories: Herbs/spice, Pasta/rice, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
12 oz Pasta
1 1/2 c  Fresh spinach, packed
1/4 c  Water or chicken stock
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Pine nuts
3 tb Parmensan cheese
1 1/2 ts Crushed garlic
Pepper
1.  Cook pasta in boiling water according to package instructions or
firm to the bite.  Drain and place in serving bowl. 2. Meanwhile, in
food processor, puree spinach, water, oil, nuts,cheese, garlic, and
pepper until smooth.  Pour over pasta.

tip:  Refrigeraate sauce up to 3 days ahead or freeze for up to 6
weeks.
