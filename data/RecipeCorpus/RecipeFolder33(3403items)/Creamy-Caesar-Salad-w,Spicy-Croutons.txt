Title: Creamy Caesar Salad w/Spicy Croutons
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1    Garlic clove; halved
1/2 c  Mayonnaise; nonfat
2 tb Vinegar, red wine
2 ts Mustard, Dijon style
2 ts Wine, white
Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Anchovy paste
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Oil, olive
3/4 ts Cajun seasoning
1    Garlic clove; minced
2 c  Bread, sourdough, cubes
-3/4" thick
18 c  Lettuce, romaine; torn
1/3 c  Cheese, Parmesan, fresh
-grated
Drop the garlic halves through the opening in blender lid with
blender on; process until minced.  Add mayonnaise and the next 5
ingredients (mayonnaise throught pepper); process until well-blended.
Cover and chill at least 1 hour.

Combine oil, Cajun seasoning, and minced garlic in a medium microwave
safe bowl.  Microwave at HIGH for 20 seconds.  Add bread cubes; toss
gently to coat.  Spread bread cubes in a single layer on a baking
sheet; bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes or until golden brown.

Place letturce in a large bowl.  Add dressing; toss gently to coat.
Sprinkle with cheese, and top with croutons.

Calories 137 (27% from fat); Protein 7.7g; Fat 4.1g (sat 1.3g, mono
1.6g, poly 0.4g); Carb 18.2g; Fiber 4.1g: Chol 4mg; Iron 3mg; Sodium
836mg; Calc 176mg
