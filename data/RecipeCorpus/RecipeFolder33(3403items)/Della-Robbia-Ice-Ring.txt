Title: Della Robbia Ice Ring
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 8 servings
2 1/2 c  Ginger Ale  -- Chilled
1/2 c  Lemon Juice
Fruits *  -- See Directions
Fruits should include the following: Canned apricot halves,
*
drained, Seedless Green Grapes, Strawberries, Maraschino Cherries,
Strips of Orange Peel, Curled, and Mint Leaves.

Mix ginger ale and lemon juice.  Freeze in decorative mold by putting
an inch of the liquid in mold, then adding fruit in layers, more
liquid and then freeze.  If fruit tends to float in the liquid, then
freeze the mold between each layer of fruit that you add.
