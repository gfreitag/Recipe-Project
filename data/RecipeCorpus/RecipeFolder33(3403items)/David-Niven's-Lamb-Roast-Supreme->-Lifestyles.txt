Title: David Niven's Lamb Roast Supreme > Lifestyles
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
5 lb Lamb shoulder roast
2/3 tb Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 tb Flour
3 lg Green peppers
2 c  Cooked rice
1/2 c  Celery; chopped
4 tb Chili sauce
Wipe roast with a damp cloth. Combine salt, pepper and flour. Rub the
roast well. Place in an open pan.

Bake at 300~ for 2-1/2 hours.

Cut green peppers in halves. Remove centers and seeds. Parboil until
slightly tender. Drain.

Mix together rice, celery, and chili sauce. Fill peppers. Place around
roast. Bake at 300~ for 40 minutes or until peppers are tender and
roast is done.

From: Lifestyles of the Rich & Famous Cookbook
