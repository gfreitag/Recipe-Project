Title: Turkey Wild Rice Supreme
Categories: Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 12 servings
2 lb Ground turkey
1 cn Mushrooms -- drained
1/2 c  Celery -- chopped
1/2 c  Carrots -- shredded
1 c  Onion -- chopped
1/2 c  Butter
2 c  Sour cream
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
6 c  Cooked wild rice
1/2 c  Almond slivers
Brown turkey, set aside.  Saute mushrooms, celery, carrots and onions
in butter 5-10 minutes.  Combine sour cream, soy sauce, salt, and
pepper.  Add wild rice, turkey, sauteed vegetables and almonds
(reserving a few for garnish.)  Toss lightly.  Place mixture in a
lightly greased 3-quart casserole.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 1
hour.  Stir several times during baking.  Add more water if needed,
and season to taste.  Garnish with reserved almonds and parsley
sprigs, if desired.
