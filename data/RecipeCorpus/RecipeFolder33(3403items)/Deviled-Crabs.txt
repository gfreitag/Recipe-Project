Title: Deviled Crabs
Categories: Cajun/creol, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Fresh parsley, chopped fine
1    Whole bell pepper
2 c  Celery
4    Green onions, with tops
pn Of sweet basil
2 lb Crabmeat
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs
pn Of marjoram
Tabasco sauce
pn Of black pepper
Salt
Red pepper
Whipped butter
4    Boiled eggs
Half & Half cream
Saute in a generous amount of whipped butter. Cook until onions are
clear and the celery is crunchy, but NOT brown. Turn heat off.

Add crabmeat. Mix well. Add 1 1/2 cups homemade bread crumbs. Then
add the 3 or 4 finely grated hard-boiled eggs; add Half & Half to
this mixture to moisten. Bake in shells in a 350 degree oven for 20
to 25 minutes. Serves 6-8 happy guests.
