Title: BULGUR AND HERB PILAF - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Olive oil
1/2 md Onion, finely chopped
1 c  Bulgur*
2 c  Canned chicken broth
Ground white pepper
1/4 c  Finely diced carrot
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
2 ts Chopped fresh oregano
2 ts Chopped fresh thyme
Heat olive oil in heavy large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and
saute until golden, about 5 minutes. Add bulgur; stir until toasted,
about 3 minutes. Stir in broth and season with salt and pepper. Bring
to boil. Add carrot and celery. Reduce heat to low, cover and cook
until liquid is absorbed, about 13 minutes. Remove from heat and add
herbs.

*Available at natural foods stores and some supermarkets across the
country.
