Title: Peanut Butter Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
3/4 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
2 1/2 tb Brown sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 c  Peanut butter
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

Top with honey or jam for a peanut butter sandwich in half the time! A
treat for kids & adults alike!

Success Hints
-------------

Use your choice of creamy or crunchy peanut butter.

Use regular, rapid or delayed time bake cycle.
