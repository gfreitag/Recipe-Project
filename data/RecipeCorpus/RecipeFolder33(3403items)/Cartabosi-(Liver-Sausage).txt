Title: Cartabosi (Liver Sausage)
Categories: Meats, Romanian, Sausage
Yield: 1 servings
5 lb Pigs heads, kidney and toung
2 x  Onions, medium, chopped
1 x  Salt and seasoned pepper
1 x  Casings
Boil heads, kidney and tounge, save water. Grind meats.
Saute onions in a little fat. Add to meat mixture and add salt and
pepper to taste. Stuff mixture into casings then place in saved water
and steam for a short time.
