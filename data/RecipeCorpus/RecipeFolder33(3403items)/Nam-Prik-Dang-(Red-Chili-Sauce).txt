Title: Nam Prik Dang (Red Chili Sauce)
Categories: Thai
Yield: 1 servings
40    Chilies, red; fresh --
Chopped
8    Garlic cloves -- crushed
4 tb Sugar -- brown
4 tb Fish sauce (Nam Pla)
1/2 c  Lime juice -- fresh
In a blender, grind all ingredients to a smooth sauce. Put in tightly
closed jar and refrigerate.

Tamarind liquid may be substituted for the lime juice.

GEnie R.WEISSGERBE "Delightful Thai Cooking" p. 15
