Title: GRILLED SALMON SALAD W/ VEGETABLE & BALSAMIC DRESSING - B
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads, Fish/seafoo, Dress/stuff, Bbq/grill
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/4 c  Olive oil
6 tb Fresh lime juice
6    8-oz salmon fillets
-(about 1 inch thick)
3 sm Tomatoes, peeled, seeded,
-chopped (about 1/2 C)
1 sm Carrot, cut into
-matchstick-size pieces
1 sm Zucchini, cut into
-matchstick-size pieces
1/4 c  Chopped fresh Italian
-parsley
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
3 tb Sliced pitted green olives
1 tb Minced fresh basil
1 tb Minced fresh tarragon
1 tb Capers
10 c  Mixed baby greens
2    Heads Belgian endive, sliced
-crosswise
Mix 1/2 C oil and lime juice in small bowl. Season to taste with salt
and pepper. Place salmon in dish large enough to hold fillets in
single layer. Pour marinade over. Cover and refrigerate 2 hours,
turning once.

Combine 3/4 C oil, tomatoes, carrot, zucchini, parsley, vinegar,
olives, basil, tarragon and capers in bowl. Season to taste with salt
and pepper. (Dressing can be prepared 2 hours ahead; cover and let
stand at room temperature.)

Prepare barbecue (medium-high heat) or preheat broiler. Grill salmon
until just cooked through, about 4 minutes per side. Toss greens and
endive with enough vegetable dressing to coat. Divide among 6 plates.
Place salmon atop greens. Spoon additional vegetable dressing over
and serve.
