Title: LINGUINE WITH TRIPLE-RED-PEPPER SAUCE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Olive oil
2 lg Garlic cloves, chopped
1    Red bell pepper, diced
1/2 ts Dried crushed red pepper
1    7-oz jar roasted red
-peppers, drained, diced
1/2 lb Linguine, freshly cooked
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Chopped fresh parsley
Additional grated Parmesan
-cheese
Heat oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add garlic,
bell pepper and crushed red pepper; saute until bell pepper softens,
about 5 minutes. Remove from heat. Add roasted peppers, linguine and
1/2 C Parmesan; toss until coated. Season with salt and pepper.
Divide pasta among 4 plates. Spiinkle with parsley and serve, passing
additional Parmesan cheese separately.
