Title: Chantilly Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
4    Potatoes; quartered
3 tb Margarine; or less
1/2 c  Fat-free plain yogurt
1/4 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper
Recipe by: Elizabeth Powell Preparation Time: 1:30

Preheat oven at 475. Prepare a 1 1/2 quart baking dish with cooking
spray. Boil potatoes until tender.  Mash with margarine and 1/4 cup
yogurt. Season with salt and pepper.  Whip the remaining 1/4 cup
yogurt until stiff. Spread mashed potatoes in a  baking dish.
Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese. Bake until the cheese melts and
the top is golden brown, about 20 minutes.
