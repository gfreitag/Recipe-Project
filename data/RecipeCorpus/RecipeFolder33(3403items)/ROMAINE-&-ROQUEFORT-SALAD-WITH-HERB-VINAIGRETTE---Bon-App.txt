Title: ROMAINE & ROQUEFORT SALAD WITH HERB VINAIGRETTE - Bon App
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil
2 tb Dry red wine
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 tb Minced shallots
1 tb Chopped fresh chives
-or green onions
1 tb Chopped fresh parsley
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Sugar
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 c  Olive oil
2    Heads romaine lettuce, each
-quartered through stem
4 oz Roquefort cheese, crumbled
Whisk first 10 ingredients together in medium bowl. Gradually whisk in
olive oil. Season to taste with salt and pepper. (Dressing can be
prepared 4 hour ahead. Cover and refrigerate. Bring to room
temperture before using.

Arrange 1 lettuce quarter on each of 8 plates. Drizzle with dressing.
Sprinkle salad with cheese and serve.
