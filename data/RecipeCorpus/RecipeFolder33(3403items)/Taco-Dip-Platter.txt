Title: Taco Dip Platter
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 20 servings
1 ea 30 oz can Refried beans
1 cn Frito-Lay Jalepeno bean dip
3 ea Ripe Avocadoes
2 ea Tb Lemon Juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 c  Sour cream
1 ea Pk Lawry's Taco seasoning
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
2 c  Chopped Lettuce
2 c  Chopped Tomatoes
1/2 c  Diced Onion
1 c  Sliced Black Olives
1 ea Small can diced Ortega
-chilles
2 c  Grated Cheddar cheese
1 ea Spread 1 can Refried Beans
-on round cake plate; then
-spread 1 can
Jalepeno Bean Dip on top.
2 ea Next, mash Avocadoes with
-lemon juice, salt &
-pepper. Spread diced
chilles and mashed Avocadoes on top. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
3. Make mixture of Sour Cream, Taco seasoning, Mayonnaise. Spread on
top. 4. Add on to top in order; Lettuce, Tomato, Onion, Grated Cheese
and Sliced Olives. Refrigerate until ready to eat. Serve with chips!
