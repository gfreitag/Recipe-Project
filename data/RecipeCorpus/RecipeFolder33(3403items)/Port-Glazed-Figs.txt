Title: Port-Glazed Figs
Categories: Vegetables, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 c  Liqueur, creme de cassis
-black currant-flavored
1    Wine, port (750 millilter
-bottle)
1 lb Figs, fresh; (about 6 lg.
-or 24 small)
2 c  Yogurt, vanilla; low fat
Combine creme de cassis and wine in a large suacepan; bring to a
boil. Add figs; reduce heat to medium, and simmer 3 to 10 minutes.
Remove figs with a slotted spoon, and place in a bowl.

Cook wine mixture over medium heat 35 minutes or until slightly
syrupy and reduced to 1 cup.  Pour sauce into a bowl; let cool to
room temperature. Serve figs and sauce with frozen yogurt.

Serving size:  1 large or 4 small figs, 1/3 cup frozen yogurt, and
about 2 1/2 tablespoons sauce.

Calories 158 (9% from fat); Protein 2.6g; Fat 1/4g (sat 0.9g, mono
9.1g, poly 0.5g); Carb 36.6g; fiber 2.8g; Chol 6mg; Iron 0.8mg;
Sodium 33mg; Calc 96mg
