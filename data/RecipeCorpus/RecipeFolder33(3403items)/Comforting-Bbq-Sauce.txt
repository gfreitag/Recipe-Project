Title: Comforting Bbq Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Onion -- chopped
1 c  Bell pepper -- chopped
1 c  Peanut oil
3 c  Steak sauce
3 c  Ketchup
1 c  Southern Comfort
1 c  Celery -- chopped
1 c  Fresh parsley -- chopped
2 tb Garlic -- chopped
1/2 c  Louisiana hot sauce
3 ts Salt
In a large skillet, saute onions, celery, bell pepper and parsley in
peanut oil until onions are clear or tender. Add garlic and cook a
little longer. Add steak sauce and kethup. Salt to taste. Add
Southern Comfort. Bring to boil. Lower heat and cover. Cook 2-3
hours. This sauce can be stored in the refrigerator for several weeks.

Makes 3 quarts to 1 gallon
