Title: Tangy Sweet BBQ sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Poultry, Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3 lg Onion; chopped
6 tb Butter
6 tb Vinegar
1/2 c  Lemon juice
6 tb Brown sugar
6 ts Mustard
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
3 ts Chili powder
3 c  Ketchup
1 1/2 c  Water
Salt; to taste
Tabasco sauce; to taste
Cayenne; to taste
Chop onions. Saute in butter until lightly colored Add all other
ingredients and simmer 30 min. Makes about 7 cups.
