Title: Karen's Chocolate Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Unsweetened chocolate
5 tb Butter
4 c  Confectioners' sugar
1 1/2 ts Vanilla extract
1/4 ts Salt
10 tb Milk
Heat the chocolate and butter in a saucepan over very low heat,
stirring till melted.  Cool completely: combine the vanilla,
confectioners' sugar, and salt in a clean mixing bowl.  Gradually
beat in the milk till the frosting is spreadable.  Fill and frost
cake. Submitted By MONICA WILLYARD   On   08-02-95
