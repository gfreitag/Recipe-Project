Title: Italian Dressing Mix Powder
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Italian
Yield: 1 servings
(One envelope of commercial
-dressing mix equals this
-entire recipe)
2 ts Onion powder
1 tb White sugar or sugar
-substitute
1/8 ts Black pepper
1/8 ts Powdered allspice
1 ts Dry minced onions
1 ts Dry celery flakes
1/8 ts Crushed marjoram leaves
1/4 ts Dry oregano, crushed
1 ea Clove garlic peeled, sliced
-fine or 2 tsp bottled
-minced garlic
1/8 ts Paprika
2 ea Squares of soda crackers.
Mix all ingredients in blender.  Keep covered.  Will keep up to 3
months. TO USE:  Combine all the above with 1/2 cup vinegar, 2/3 cup
cold water and 1/3 cup corn oil.  Mix well.  Keeps in refrigerator up
to 1 month. Shake well before using.  Makes 1-1/2 cup prepared
dressing. Origin: Women's Circle, for cooks on the go. Shared by:
Sharon Stevens, Jan/94
