Title: Powter Bean and Cabbage Stew
Categories: Vegetables, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Veg oil
1 lg Onion; chopped
3 md Potatoes; peeled and cut
-in 1 inch cubes
3 md Carrots; sliced
1/2 ts Caraway seeds
2 1/2 c  Vegetable broth
4 c  Cabbage chunks
1 c  Frozen peas
1    Can great north. beans
1 1/2 ts Salt; or to taste
1/2 ts Black pepper
Heat oil in large skillet.  Add onion, potatoes, and carrots.  Cook
until lightly browned, about 10 mins.

Add caraway seeds and saute for 1 more minute.  Add broth and cook
until carrots are almost tender, about 7-10 mins.

Add cabbage and peas and cook for 10 mins, covered.  Add beans, salt,
and pepper, and cook 5 more mins uncovered.

Check vegetables and seasonings and serve.
