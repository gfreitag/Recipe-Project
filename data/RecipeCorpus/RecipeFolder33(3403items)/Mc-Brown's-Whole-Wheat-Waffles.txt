Title: Mc Brown's Whole Wheat Waffles
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 8 waffles
2 c  Whole wheat pastry flour
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt; optional
1 ts Active dry yeast
1/4 c  Water
1 ts Honey
1/2 c  Cooked oat bran*
1 c  Low fat soy milk
1 ts Lemon juice
*to make 1/2c cooked oat bran, cook 1/4c uncooked oat bran in 3/4c
water for 2 mins.

Combine the first 4 ingredients and set aside.  Combine the yeast,
water, honey, and cooked oat bran and let rest a few mins.  Then add
the soy milk and lemon juice, mix well, and combine with the dry
ingredients. Pour into a hot waffle iron and cook 7 mins.

Variation--add 2T tahini with the soy milk
