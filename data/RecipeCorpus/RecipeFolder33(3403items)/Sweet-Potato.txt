Title: Sweet Potato
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- packed
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Cloves
1    Egg white -- whipped
1/2 c  Applesauce
1 c  Orange juice -- at room
Temperature
1 ts Vanilla
1 cn 12 oz sweet potato --
- mashed/cooked
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda,
cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, allspice, and cloves.  In another mixing
bowl, combine egg white, applesauce, orange juice, vanilla, and sweet
potato.  Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients just until
combined.  Pour mixture into prepared pan(s).  Bake for 15 to 20
minutes depending on pan size.
