Title: White Sangria
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 4 servings
2    Limes  -- sliced
2    Lemons  -- sliced
2 lg Red Delicious apples  --
Sliced
2    750 milliliter bottles
Chablis
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Brandy
2    33.8 oz.bottles club soda
Combine sliced fruit, wine, sugar and brandy;  chill at least 2 hours.
Gently stir in club soda and serve sangria over ice.
