Title: Stuffed zucchini-Courgettes Farcie
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Microwave, Fish/seafoo
Yield: 4 servings
2    Zucchini; split lengthwise
2 oz Rice; pre-cooked
2 tb Chicken bouillon
1 ts Parsley; minced
Salt & pepper
3 1/2 oz Crabmeat; flaked
Paprika
Parsley for garnish
Scrape the flesh out of the zucchini, leaving a shell no more than
1/2 inch thick.  Mash the pulp & put it in a casserole with the rice,
the bouillon, parsley, salt & pepper.  Microwave at HIGH 4-6 minutes
until the rice is tender & liquid is absorbed. Add the crabmeat and
mix well. Fill zucchini shells with the mixture. Place on a plate &
sprinkle with paprika. Cover with waxed paper and cook 4-5 minutes on
HIGH, turning halfway. Cook just until zucchini are tender. Let
stand, covered, 2-3 minutes. Garnish with parsley before serving.
