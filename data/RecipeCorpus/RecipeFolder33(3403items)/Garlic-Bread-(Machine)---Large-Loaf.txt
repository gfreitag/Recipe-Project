Title: Garlic Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 c  + 2T Water
3 c  + 2T White bread flour
1 1/2 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 tb Butter
3 tb Parmesan, fresh/grated
3/4 ts Sweet basil
3/4 ts Garlic powder
3/4 ts Garlic paste
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

The aroma of this bread is "to die for" - and it butters up to make
the easiest garlic toast around!
Success Hints
-------------

Grate your own fresh parmesan cheese.  Pre-ground cheeses often have
salt in them.

1/2 t finely-chopped or pressed garlic may be substituted for paste.

Use regular or rapid bake cycles.
