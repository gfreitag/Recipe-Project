Title: Nam Prik Gang Pet Dang (Red Curry Paste)
Categories: Thai
Yield: 1 servings
20    Chilies, green -- dried
And chopped
2    Onion, red -- chopped
6    Garlic cloves -- crushed
4 ts Coriander -- ground
2 ts Cumin -- ground
2 ts Shrimp paste (Kapi)
4 ts Salt
2 ts Lemon grass -- ground
2 ts Galangal -- ground
Grind all ingredients together in blender - to a paste.

GEnie - R.WEISSGERBE "Delightful Thai Cooking" p. 21
