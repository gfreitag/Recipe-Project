Title: No-Sugar Fruit/Nut Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 32 servings
1 c  Chopped dates
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Dried prunes -- chopped
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Margarine
1/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs -- beaten
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Mixed nuts -- chopped
In a saucepan combine dates, raisins, prunes and water. Bring to boil
and boil 5 minutes. Stir in margarine and salt. Set aside to cool. Add
remaining ingredients to fruit; stir just until dry ingredients are
moistened. Spoon into greased MINI-muffin pans. Bake in preheated
350-degree oven for 15 minutes. (If desired can bake in a greased 11
x 7 x 2-inch pan for 30 minutes.)

Yield: 32 mini-muffins.
