Title: Sweet And Sour Fish Sauce (Nam Pla Prig)
Categories: Thai, Condiments
Yield: 6 servings
4 md Clove Garlic -- Finely
Chopped
1 tb Hot Red Chili Or Cayenne --
Pepper Flakes, Crush
6 tb Fresh Lime Juice
1/4 c  Fish Sauce (Nam Pla)
1/2 c  White Sugar
1 c  Warm Water
Yield: 1 3/4 Cups Of Sauce Combine all the ingredients in a small
bowl and stir well.

Serve at room temperature.
