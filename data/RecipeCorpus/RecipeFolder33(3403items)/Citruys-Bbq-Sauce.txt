Title: Citruys Bbq Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Onion -- fine chop
1/4 ts Red pepper
1 ts Veg. oil
1/2 c  Lime juice
2 ts Lemon juice
1 ts Salt
1 ts Red chili peppers -- ground
1    Ancho chili  seeded -- fine
Chop
1 c  Orange juice
2 ts Sugar
1 ts Cilantro -- snipped
Cook onion, red chiles, red pepper and ancho chile in oil, stirring
frequently, until onion is tender about 5 min. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Heat to boiling, reduce heat to low. Simmer uncovered for
about 10 min. stirring occasionally.
