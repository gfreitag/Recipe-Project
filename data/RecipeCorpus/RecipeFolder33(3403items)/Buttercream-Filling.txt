Title: Buttercream Filling
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Milk
2 tb Cornstarch
1/2 c  Butter, softened
1/2 c  Vegetable shortening
1 c  Granulated sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
Gradually whisk the milk into the cornstarch in a small saucepan
until smooth.  Cook over medium-low heat whisking constantly, until
mix thickens and boils.  Place the saucepan in a large bowl of ice
water, stirring occasionally, until cold.  Beat the butter and
shortening in a mixing bowl till creamy.  Beat in the sugar till
light and fluffy.  Add the cooled cornstarch mixture and vanilla:
beat until creamy.  Spread filling between the layers of the cake.
