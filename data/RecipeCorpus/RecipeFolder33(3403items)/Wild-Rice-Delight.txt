Title: Wild Rice Delight
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Wild rice
1 cn Golden mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
4    Partridge or chicken
Breasts
1 cn Mushrooms
1 cn Water chestnuts
2 lg Shallots
Cook wild rice.  Mix in mushroom soups and mushrooms.  Slice water
chestnuts and chop shallots; mix.  Put rice mixture in glass pan.
Split breasts, roll in flour, then brown in butter.  Lay breasts on
top of rice. Bake at 325 degrees for 1 1/2 hours covered.  Season to
taste.
