Title: Rum Mango Daiquiri
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 serving
1 oz Rum
1/4 oz Curacao
2 oz Mango juice or nectar
1/2 oz Lime juice
1/2 oz Sugar syrup
Mix all ingredients in a blender with plenty of cracked ice. Blend
until frappeed; pour into a chilled wine glass. Serve with a straw
and garnish with a mango or lime slice.
