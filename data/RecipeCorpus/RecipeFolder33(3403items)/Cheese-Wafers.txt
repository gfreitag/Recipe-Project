Title: Cheese Wafers
Categories: Snacks, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  Soft butter or margarine
2 c  Flour
2 oz Sharp cheddar cheese, grated
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Rice Krispies
Cut butter into flour, add cheese and seasoning.  Fold in cereal,
shape into balls and press lightly on ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake
in 350Ã¸ F. about 10 minutes.
