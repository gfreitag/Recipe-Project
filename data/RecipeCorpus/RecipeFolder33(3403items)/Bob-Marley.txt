Title: Bob Marley
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 oz Dark rum or Tia Maria
1 oz Pineapple juice
Pour rum or Tia Maria in a cordial glass. Float the pineappple juice
on top by holding a teaspoon bottom-side up over the glass and
pouring the pineapple juice over it slowly.
