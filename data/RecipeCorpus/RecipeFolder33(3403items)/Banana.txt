Title: Banana
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 servings
2    Egg whites -- whipped
1/2 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
1 c  Water -- at room
Temperature
1 c  Granulated sugar
2 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cloves
1 c  Bananas -- mashed
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine egg whites, applesauce, and water.  In a
another mixing bowl, combine sugar, flour, baking powder, baking
soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves.  Mix wet ingredients with dry
ingredients just until moistened.  Pour batter into prepared pan(s).
Bake 45 to 50 minutes depending on pan size.
