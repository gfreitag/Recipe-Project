Title: Judith Comfort's Crispy Oven-Fried Chicken (Sharon Steven
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Egg
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Flour
2 tb Sesame seeds
1 tb Baking powder
1 tb Paprika
1 ts Salt
3 lb Chicken thighs
1/4 c  Butter, melted
In bowl, whisk together egg and milk.  In lg bag, shake together
flour, sesame seeds, baking powder, paprika and salt.  Dip chicken
pieces, one at a time into egg mixture; shake chicken in flour
mixture. Place on foil-lined baking sheet; drizzle with butter. Bake
in 350 oven for 1 hour or until chicken is crispy on outside and no
longer pink inside. Makes 6 servings. Canadian Living Magazine, May
1989
