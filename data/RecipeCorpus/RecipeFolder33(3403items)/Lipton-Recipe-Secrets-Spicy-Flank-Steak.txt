Title: Lipton Recipe Secrets Spicy Flank Steak
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 1/2 lb Flank Steak
1/4 c  Olive or Vegetable Oil
2 tb Brown Sugar, firmly packed
1    Env. Lipton Recipe Secrets
Fiesta Herb w/ Red Pepper
Soup Mix
With knife, score top of flank steak. In small bowl, blend Fiesta
Herb Mix, oil and sugar. Brush 1/2 of the mixture on meat. Broil or
grill, turning once and brushing with remaining mixture, until done.
Serve, if desired, with assorted grilled peppers and garnish with
fresh rosemary.
