Title: Cinco de Mayo Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 c  Water
3 c  White bread flour
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Olive oil
1 c  Corn meal
1/2 c  Creamed corn
1/4 c  Green chilies, canned/diced
1/2 tb Jalapeno peppers,    "
2 ts Cilantro
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

Served with chili, this lightly textured loaf will convince you never
to back to plain corn bread again!

Success Hints
-------------

Drain canned chilies and jalapeno peppers well.  Be careful! Jalapeno
and chili juice can sting.

This recipe can be used with regular and rapid bake cycles.
