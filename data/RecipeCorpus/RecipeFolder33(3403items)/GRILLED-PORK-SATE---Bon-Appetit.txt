Title: GRILLED PORK SATE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Main dish, Meats, Bbq/grill
Yield: 2 servings;
3 tb Chunky peanut butter
2 tb Fresh lime juice
2 tb Low-sodium soy sauce
1 tb Minced peeled fresh ginger
2 sm Garlic cloves, minced
1/4 ts (generous) dried crushed
-red pepper
8    To 10 oz pork cutlets (about
-1/4 inch thick), cut into
-1/2-inch-wide strips
4    8-inch bamboo skewers,
-soaked in water 10 minutes
1/4 c  Thinly sliced green onions
Prepare barbecue (medium-high heat). Whisk peanut butter, lime juice,
soy sauce, ginger, garlic and crushed red pepper in shallow baking
dish until combined. Set aside 2 T sauce. Add pork strips to
remaining sauce in baking dish and toss to coat completely. Thread
pork strips onto skewers. Let stand 10 minutes.

Grill until pork is slightly charred and cooked through, about 3
minutes per side. Brush with reserved 2 tablespoons sauce and cook 30
seconds. Transfer to platter. Sprinkle with green onions and serve.

Bon Appetit/August/94 Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
