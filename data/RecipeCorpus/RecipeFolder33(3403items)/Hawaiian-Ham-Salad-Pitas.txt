Title: Hawaiian Ham Salad Pitas
Categories: Sandwich, Meats
Yield: 2 cups
1 cn Crushed pineapple, well
-drained (8 oz)
1 cn UNDERWOOD Deviled Ham or
-Honey Ham Spread (4.25 oz)
1/4 c  Pecan pieces
3 tb Mayonnaise
2 tb Sliced green onion
3    Whole wheat pita bread
-pockets, cut in half
Shredded lettuce
1. In small bowl, combine all ingredients except pita bread and
lettuce. Refrigerate to chill.

2. Fill pita pockets with ham salad and shredded lettuce.
