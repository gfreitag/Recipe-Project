Title: GRILLED GREENS & RED ONION SALAD W/ GOAT CHEESE - Bon App
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads, Bbq/grill
Yield: 4 servings
1 lg Red onion
1    Head radicchio, quartered
-through base
1    Head frisee lettuce or curly
-endive, quartered through
-base
1 lg Head Belgian endive,
-quartered through base
1/2    Lemon
6 tb Olive oil
1/3 c  Balsamic vinegar
1/4 c  Chopped fresh basil
4 oz Soft fresh goat cheese
-(such as Montrachet),
-crumbled
Preheat oven to 400'F. Wrap onion in foil. Roast until soft, about 40
minutes. Cool. Peel onion; cut into quarters through stem end. Place
onion, radicchio, frisee and endive on baking sheet. Squeeze lemon
over. Let stand 30 minutes.

Prepare barbecue (medium-high heat) or preheat broiler. Whisk oil,
vine- gar and basil in bowl. Season with salt and pepper. Grill
greens and onion until slightly charred, turning often, about 4
minutes. Place on platter. Drizzle dressing over. Sprinkle with
cheese and serve.
