Title: Five Spice Fish
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 8 servings
2 lb Fillets
2/3 c  Onion; chopped
1 1/2 tb Ginger
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Cloves
Recipe by: One Year of Healthy Hearty & Simple One Dish Meals
Preparation Time: 0:20 Cut fillets in 2'" x 2" long slices. Mix
fillets with onions, ginger, soy sauce, sugar, oil, and spices. Cover
and chill for several hours.  Broil on both sides.
