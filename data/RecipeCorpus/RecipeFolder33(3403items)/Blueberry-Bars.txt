Title: Blueberry Bars
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 16 servings
MMMMM-------------------------CRUST----------------------------------
1 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Margarine -- softened
1/4 c  Powdered sugar -- sifted
MMMMM------------------------FILLING---------------------------------
2    Egg whites -- whipped
1 tb Lemon juice -- at room
Temperature
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Granulated sugar
1/4 c  Unbleached flour
1 c  Blueberries
1/2 c  Coconut -- shredded
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a 8" square pan with cooking spray and
flour. To prepare crust, combine 1 cup flour, margarine, and powdered
sugar in a mixing bowl.  Spread over bottom of prepared pan.  Bake
for 20 to 25 minutes.  Meanwhile, to prepare filling, combine, egg
whites, lemon juice, and vanilla in a small mixing bowl.  In a larger
mixing bowl, combine sugar, remaining flour, blueberries, coconut,
baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.  Spread over top of baked layer.
Bake another 30 minutes. Cut into 2" bars when cooled.
