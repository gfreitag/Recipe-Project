Title: Easy Chicken & Pasta
Categories: Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Skinless, boneless chicken
-breasts, cut up
1 cn Campbell's Cream of Chicken
-Soup (10 3/4 oz)
1 pk Frozen seasoned pasta and
-vegetable combination
-(16 oz)
1. In skillet, heat 1 tablespoon oil. Add chicken and cook until
browned.

2. Add soup, 1/2 cup water and vegetable combination.

3. Cover and cook 5 minutes or until vegetables are tender, stirring
often. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-12-95
