Title: Cocoa Spice Cake
Categories: Low cal/fat, Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Margarine -- melted
3/4 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
1    Egg white -- whipped
1/4 c  Cocoa powder -- sifted
1 1/4 c  Unbleached flour
1 c  Granulated sugar
3/4 c  Baking soda
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a 9" square pan with cooking spray and
flour. In a mixing bowl, combine, margarine, applesauce, and egg
white. In another mixing bowl, combine cocoa powder, flour, sugar,
baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.  Mix wet ingredients with dry
ingredients just until moistened. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake for 35 minutes.
