Title: SPINACH AND SMOKED CHICKEN SALAD - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads, Poultry
Yield: 2 servings
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Drained oil-packed sun-dried
-tomatoes, chopped
2 tb Red wine vinegar
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
3/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Chopped shallots
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
2    Pancetta slices or thick
-bacon
6 c  Fresh spinach leaves
6 oz Smoked chicken or turkey,
-sliced
1/2 c  Crumbled blue cheese
Croutons
FOR DRESSING: Combine first 3 ingredients in processor and process to
finely chop tomatoes. Blend in oil. Transfer to bowl. Mix in
shallots. (Can be made 1 day ahead. Cover and chill. Before using,
bring to room temperature and whisk to blend.)

FOR SALAD: Cook pancetta in heavy small skillet over medium-high heat
until crisp. Drain on paper towels. Toss spinach with enough dressing
to coat. Divide between plates. Fan chicken atop spinach. Top with
pancetta and cheese. Garnish with croutons. Serve, passing more
dressing separately.

Bon Appetit/January/1994  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
