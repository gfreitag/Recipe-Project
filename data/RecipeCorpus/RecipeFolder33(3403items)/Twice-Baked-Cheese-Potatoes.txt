Title: Twice Baked Cheese Potatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
3 md Potatoes*; baked
1 c  Beans; of choice
3 c  Spinach; chopped
1 c  Fat-free cottage cheese
1 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
1 lg Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Egg whites; at room temperat
2 tb Chives
2 tb Fat-free sour cream
2 tb Fat-free sour cream
2 tb Chives
Recipe by: Anita A. Matejka Preparation Time: 0:15 Preheat oven at
375. Prepare a 13 x 9 x2" pan with cooking spray.  Bake potatoes 1 to
1/4 hours or until tender.  Cool just until easy to handle. Cut
potatoes lengthwise into halves; scoop out pulp, leaving thins shells.
Place potato pulp and beans in blender; cover and blend until
smooth. Mix potato mixture and remaining ingredients except 2
tablespoons sour cream and 2 tablespoons chives.  Divide mixture
among potato shells.  Bake uncovered 15 to 20 minutes or until hot
and light brown.  Top with sour cream and chives.
