Title: Sweet and Sour BBQ Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Poultry, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
12 oz Onion; chopped
4    Cl Garlic; minced
2    Fresh ginger root
1 pt Red wine
Olive oil
Flour to thicken
Juice of:
2    Orange
2    Lime
1    Lemon
2 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Capers
1 tb Malt
2 tb Molasses
2 ts Mustard
1 ts Chili sauce or tabasco
MMMMM---------------------------SPICES--------------------------------
pn Nutmeg
2 lb Cardamon; ground
4    Cloves; ground
1/2 ts Allspice
Black pepper
Chop the onions, garlic and ginger and slowly cook in the oil until
soft. Add flour to thicken ( thin if you want to marinade). Add
everything else and cook for half an hour.
