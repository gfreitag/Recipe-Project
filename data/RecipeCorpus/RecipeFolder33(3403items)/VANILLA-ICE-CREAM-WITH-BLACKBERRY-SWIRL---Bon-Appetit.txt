Title: VANILLA ICE CREAM WITH BLACKBERRY SWIRL - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 8 servings
4    1/2-pint baskets
-blackberries
3/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
2 pt Vanilla ice cream, softened
Blend berries, sugar and lemon juice in processor until berries are
coarsely chopped. Transfer to bowl. Cover and let stand 2 to 6 hours
at room temperature.

Add ice cream to berry sauce; fold together. Scoop into bowls; serve.
