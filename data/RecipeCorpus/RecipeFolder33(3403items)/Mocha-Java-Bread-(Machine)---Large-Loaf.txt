Title: Mocha Java Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
1 c  + 2T Water
2 1/3 c  White bread flour
2 tb Dry milk
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Rye flour
2 tb Brown sugar
1    Egg
2 tb Instant Mocha *
1/2 c  Pecans, chopped
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This is a coffee-lover's bread.  It goes great with a cup of rich
French Roast any time of day, making any coffee break feel like a
brunch. Serve it with a light spread of cream cheese for an even
richer flavor.

Success Hints
-------------

* Sugar-free coffee mix.  Try your favorite instant coffee mixes,
such as Cappuccino or Vienna.

For an alternative, use sugar-free cocoa mix instead of the coffee
mix.

Use regular or rapid bake cycle.
