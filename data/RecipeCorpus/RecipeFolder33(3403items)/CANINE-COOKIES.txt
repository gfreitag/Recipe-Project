Title: CANINE COOKIES
Categories: Cake/cookie, Pets
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Old fashioned rolled oats
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Boiling water
Add & stir:
3/4 c  Cornmeal
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Chicken or beef instant
-bouillon
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Shredded cheddar cheese
1    Egg, beaten
Add in, one cup at a time:
2 c  To 3 c whole wheat or bread
-flour
"This is a great recipe that I make once in awhile for our 2 dogs.
They love them and I know whats in them!!" - K. Conder

Combine & let stand 15 minutes:

Add flour to form a stiff dough. Knead until smooth and no longer
sticky, 3 4 minutes. Roll or pat out dough to 1/2" thickness; cut w/
a bone cookie cutter or sm.biscuit cutter. Place 1" apart on a
greased cookie sheet. Bake at 325 for 35 to 40 minutes or until
golden brown. Cool completely.Store loosely covered.
