Title: Banana Daiquiri
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 oz Light rum
1 tb Triple sec
1/2 oz Lime juice
1 oz Banana liqueur
1/2    Banana
1/2 oz Heavy cream
1/2 c  Crushed ice
Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend at high speed until
smooth. Pour into a sour, champagne, or cocktail glass.
