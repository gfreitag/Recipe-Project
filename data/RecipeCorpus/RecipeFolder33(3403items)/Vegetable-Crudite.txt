Title: Vegetable Crudite
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Canned corn, drained
1 sm Green onion, chopped
1    Green pepper, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
1 lg Fresh tomato, chopped
1/4 c  Fresh parsley, chopped
1/4 c  Extra virgin olive oil
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
Salt
Pepper
1    Scallion, chopped
Mix corn with onion, green pepper, garlic and tomato. In a separate
small bowl or cup, mix olive oil and vinegar. Pour over vegetable,
toss with parsley; season with salt and pepper. Garnish each serving
with scallions. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-07-95
