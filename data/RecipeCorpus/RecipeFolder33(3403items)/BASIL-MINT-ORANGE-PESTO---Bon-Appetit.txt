Title: BASIL-MINT-ORANGE PESTO - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Herbs/spice, Condiments
Yield: 3 /4 cup
1 c  Packed fresh basil leaves
1 c  Packed fresh mint leaves
1/2 c  Walnuts (about 2 1/4 oz)
2 tb Frozen orange juice
-concentrate, thawed
2    Garlic cloves
1/4 c  (or more) olive oil
In processor, blend first 5 ingredients until almost smooth. With
machine running, gradually add 1/4 C olive oil; process until smooth.
If pesto is dry, mix im more oil by spoonfuls. Season with salt and
pepper. (Can be made ahead. See note at end of Sun-dried Tomato and
Olive Pesto recipe.)

Bon Appetit/August/94 Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
