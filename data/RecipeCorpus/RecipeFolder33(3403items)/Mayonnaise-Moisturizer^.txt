Title: Mayonnaise Moisturizer^
Categories: Information, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1    Egg
1 c  Salad oil
2 tb Lemon juice
Beat egg and oil slowly; add vinegar. Apply to face and leave on 1/2
hour. Remove with warm water. Many of the recipes in this collection
did not contain amounts or oven temperatures. I have typed them in as
they appear in the book, typos and all.
