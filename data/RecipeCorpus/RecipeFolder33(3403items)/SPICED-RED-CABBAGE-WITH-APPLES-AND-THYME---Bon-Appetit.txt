Title: SPICED RED CABBAGE WITH APPLES AND THYME - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
2 lb Red cabbage, cored, shredded
1 lb Tart green apples (such as
-Granny Smith), peeled,
-cored, finely chopped
1 lg Onion, finely chopped
1/2 c  Dry red wine
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 tb Red wine vinegar
2 tb Firmly packed dark brown
-sugar
2    Garlic cloves, chopped
1 tb Grated orange peel
1 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground allspice
Preheat oven to 400'F. Place all ingredients in heavy large ovenproof
Dutch oven. Cover; bake until cabbage is tender, about 1 hour.
Transfer Dutch oven to stovetop, uncover and boil until juices
thicken slightly, about 5 minutes. Season to taste with salt and
pepper.

Bon Appetit/October/94  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
