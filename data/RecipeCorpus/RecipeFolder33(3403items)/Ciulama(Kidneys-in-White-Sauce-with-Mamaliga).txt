Title: Ciulama(Kidneys in White Sauce with Mamaliga)
Categories: Meats, Romanian
Yield: 1 servings
1 x  Clean kidney
Clean the fat from calfs or pigs kidney. Scald several times  and
boil until tender with soup greens but without salt. When kidneys are
tender, prepare a white sauce as follows: blend two tablespoons of
flour and a very little finely chopped onion. Parch but do not brown.
Thin with buillion from the kidney, add salt and let simmer ten min
or until as thick as heavy cream. Meanwhile slice kidneys very thin
and add back to sauce and serve hot without cooking again. Serve with
mamaliga(Cornmeal Mush) Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   07-27-95
