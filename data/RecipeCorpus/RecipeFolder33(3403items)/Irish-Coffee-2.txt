Title: Irish Coffee 2
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 c  Warm Water
1 tb Instant Coffee Crystals
1/4 c  Irish Whiskey
Brown Sugar To Taste
Dessert Topping *
*    Dessert topping should be in a pressurized can.

Mix first four ingredients then add a bit of dessert topping or
sweetened whipped cream.
