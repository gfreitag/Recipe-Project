Title: Bees in the Garden Coleslaw
Categories: Breakfast, Snacks, Salads
Yield: 8 servings
1    Green cabbage
1    Green bell pepper
1/2 c  Sweet red pepper
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Honey
2 tb Vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Celery seed
1/4 ts Black pepper
Toss cabbage and peppers in large bowl.  Combine mayonnaise, honey,
vinegar, salt, mustard, celery seed and black pepper in medium bowl,
then toss with cabbage mixture. Mix well; cover and refrigerate until
thoroughly chilled.
