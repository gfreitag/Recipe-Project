Title: Classic Lasagna
Categories: Italian, Pasta/rice, Casseroles
Yield: 12 servings
3 c  Ricotta cheese
3 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
3/4 c  Kraft 100% Grated Parmesan
-Cheese
2    Eggs
1 lb Ground beef
1 cn Prego Traditional Spaghetti
-Sauce (48 oz)
12    Cooked lasagna noodles
Prep time: 30 minutes. Cook time: 30 minutes.

Mix ricotta, mozzarella, 1/2 cup Parmesan and eggs.

Brown beef, stirring to separate meat. Pour off fat. Stir in Prego
sauce.

In EACH of two 11x7x1" baking dishes, layer 1 CUP Prego mixture, 2
noodles and 1 1/2 CUPS cheese mixture. Repeat layers. Top with
remaining noodles, Prego mixture and Parmesan.*

Bake at 400'F. 30 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes.

* TIP: Cover and freeze. Bake frozen lasagna, uncovered, at 350'F.
for 1 hour 15 minutes.
