Title: Spinach-Citrus Salad^
Categories: Vegetables, Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
Fast & Healthy 9/92
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
1 cn (15oz) mandarin orange
Segments, drained
1/2 md Sweet red onion, thinly
Sliced, separated into
Rings
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Sesame seed
5 c  Torn fresh spinach
In shallow bowl, combine mandarin orange segments and onion rings.
Add red wine vinegar and sugar; mix gently. Sprinkle with sesame
seed. Cover; refrigerate until serving time to blend flavors. Just
before serving, place spinach in medium bowl. Add orange mixture;
toss to coat. 6 (1 cup) servings.

Per serving: 60 calories, 2 g protein, 12 g carbohydrate, 2 g fiber,
1 g fat (15%), 0 mg cholesterol, 40 mg sodium, 360 mg potassium, 1
vegetable and 1/2 fruit exchange.
