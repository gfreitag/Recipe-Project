Title: Pineapple-Graham Cracker Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 20 servings
1/4 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Packed light brown sugar
1 lg Egg
1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/2 c  Black walnuts -- chopped
1 c  Crushed pineapple --
Drained
1. Place softened margarine and brown sugar in a bowl and, using hand
mixer or wooden spoon, cream mixture until light and fluffy. 2. Beat
in the egg. Stir in sifted flour, crumbs, well-drained pineapple and
nuts. 3. Pour into a lightly greased 8 x 8 x 2-inch pan. Bake in
preheated 350-degree oven 30-35 minutes or until brown and surface
feels firm to the touch. 4. Let cool completely in pan, then cut into
20 bars.
