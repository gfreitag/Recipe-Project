Title: RECONSTITUTED DRIED PORCINI - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Vegetables
Yield: 1 oz
1 oz Dried porcini mushrooms
1 c  Hot water
Combine porcini and water in medium bowl. Let stand until porcini
soften, about 40 minutes. Pour mixture into strainer set over small
bowl. Press porcini to release excess liquid. Coarsely chop porcini.
Pour soaking liquid into measuring cup, leaving any sediment behind;
reserve soaking liquid.
