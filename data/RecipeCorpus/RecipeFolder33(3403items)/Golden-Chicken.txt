Title: Golden Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 24 servings
24    Skinless boneless chicken br
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
2 3/4 c  Honey
1 1/3 c  Dijon mustard
2 tb Sesame seeds
1 tb Lemon juice
Combine all ingredients except chicken.  Heat over low heat about five
minutes or until simmering.  Do not boil.  Remove sauce from heat and
keep warm.  Dip each chicken piece in sauce, then place in a pan
coated with a non-stick spray.  Bake at 325 degrees F 15 to 20
minutes or to an internal temperature of 155-160 degrees F.  Spoon
remaining sauce over chicken and serve.
