Title: Aunt Dodie's Cookies^
Categories: Cake/cookie, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1    Egg, well beaten
1 1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
pn Salt
1    Lemon rind, grated
Walnut halves
Mix sugar and butter together until creamy. Beat in egg. Add flour,
baking powder, salt and lemon rind. Mold into balls half the size of
a walnut. Lay on greased tine and press down with a fork dipped in
flour. Place half walnut on each and bake in a moderate oven until
browned.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
