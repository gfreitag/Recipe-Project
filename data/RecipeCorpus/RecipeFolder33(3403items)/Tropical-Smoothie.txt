Title: Tropical Smoothie
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 c  Pineapple juice --
Unsweetened
1 c  Strawberries -- sliced
1    Banana -- sliced OR
Mango -- diced OR
Papaya -- diced OR
* If using mangos or papaya, make sure they are ripe.

1. Peel and dice the fruit. 2. Have the pineapple juice well-chilled.
3. Combine all ingredients in a blender. Puree until thick and very
smooth. Serve in a glass garnished with a whole strawberry and
perhaps a mint sprig.
