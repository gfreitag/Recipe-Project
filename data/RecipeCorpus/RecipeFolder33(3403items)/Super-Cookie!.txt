Title: Super Cookie!
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 cookie
1 pk Pillsbury refrigerated
-cookie dough (20 oz)
1. PRESS OUT refrigerated cookie dough evenly in bottom of ungreased
12" pizza pan or 13x9" baking pan.

2. BAKE in preheated 350'F. oven for 12-18 minutes or until deep
golden brown.

3. COOL, cut and serve.
