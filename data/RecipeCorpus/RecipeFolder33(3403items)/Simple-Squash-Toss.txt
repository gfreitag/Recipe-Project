Title: Simple Squash Toss
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 6 servings
2 lb (about 8) medium-sized
-yellow squash
2 oz Jar diced pimiento; drained
2 tb Water
2 tb Butter or margarine
1/4 ts Dried whole basil
1/4 ts Garlic powder
Salt & pepper to taste
Wash squash, and cut into 1/2-inch-thick slices; place in a 2-quart
casserole.  Sprinkle with pimientos.  Add water, and dot with butter;
sprinkle with basil & garlic powder.  Cover with heavy-duty plastic
wrap, and microwave at HIGH for 12 minutes, stirring every 4 minutes.
Let stand, covered, 10 minutes before serving.
