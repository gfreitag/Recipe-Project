Title: Chunky Chicken Soup, Mediterranean Style
Categories: Poultry, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Olive oil
1 md Onions; chopped
1 md Bell pepper; chopped
2    Cl Garlic; minced
1 cn Stewed tomatoes; (14 1/2 oz)
4 c  Chicken broth
2 c  Water
1/2 c  Rice
1 tb Basil
6 md Carrots; sliced
1 lb Skinless boneless chicken
-breasts
1 pk Green beans; (10 oz)
1/4 ts Black pepper
Recipe by: Reader's Digest Live Longer Cookbook Preparation Time:
0:15 In a large pan, heat oil over moderate heat.  Add onion,  green
pepper, and garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or
until vegetables are soft.  Stir in tomatoes, broth, water, rice, and
basil and bring to a boil.  Adjust heat so the mixture simmers
gently, cover, and cook for 10 minutes.  Add carrots, and cook 5
minutes longer.  Add chicken and green beans and cook, uncovered, for
5 minutes or until chicken is just cooked through.  Stir in the black
pepper.
