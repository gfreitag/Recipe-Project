Title: Old Fashioned Rice Pudding
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 10 servings
4 c  Cold milk
1 c  Minute Rice
1    Pkg Vanilla Pudding
1/4 c  Raisins (optional)
1    Egg well beaten
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/8 ts Ground nutmeg
Combine milk,rice,pudding mix, raisins and egg in medium saucepan.
Bring to a full boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.  Remove
from heat. Cool five minutes stirring twice.  Pour into individual
dessert dishes or serving bowl.  Sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg;
serve warm. For chilled pudding, place plastic wrap directly on to
pudding. Cool slightly; chill about one hour.  Stir before serving;
sprinkle with cinnamon and nutmeg. Makes ten servings.
