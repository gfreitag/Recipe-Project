Title: Remoulade Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 cup
3 tb Mayonnaise
1 ts Capers
2 tb FINES HERBES
FINES HERBES is a standard seasoning mix used in French cooking. For
this dressing, we used a little more than one teaspoon each of minced
fresh basil and thyme, 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh rosemary and 1
tablespoon minced fresh chives. Adapted from "Fancy Salads of the Big
Hotels" (The Hotel Industry, 1923).

In small bowl combine mayonnaise, capers and FINES HERBES. Makes
about 1/4 cup plus 1 tablespoon dressing.

Each 1-tablespoon serving contains about: 66 calories; 61 milligrams
sodium; 4 milligrams cholesterol; 7 grams fat; 1 gram carbohydrates;
1 gram protein; 0.14 gram fiber.
