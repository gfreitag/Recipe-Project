Title: Tea Cakes
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
Text Only
Prepare one-egg cake batter, and bake in small muffin tins.  Ice with
butter icing.  Food coloring in a number of the pastel shades may be
added to portions of the butter icing. The Household Searchlight
