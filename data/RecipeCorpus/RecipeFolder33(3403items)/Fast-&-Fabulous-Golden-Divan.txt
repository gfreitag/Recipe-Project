Title: Fast & Fabulous Golden Divan
Categories: Casseroles, Main dish
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/4 c  Water
1 md Onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Margarine
8 oz Herb-seasoned stuffing
Cubes
2 c  Broccoli flowerets --
Cooked
2 c  Cooked chicken or turkey --
Cubed
1 cn Golden corn soup
1 c  Cheddar cheese -- shredded
1/2 c  Milk
4 oz Sliced mushrooms -- drained
In 2 qt saucepan over high heat, heat water, onion and margarine to
boiling.  Remove from heat.  Add stuffing; toss to mix well.  Spoon
into 2 qt oblong baking dish.  Arrange broccoli and turkey over
stuffing.  In medium bowl, combine soup, 1/2 cup cheese, milk and
mushrooms.  Pour over broccoli and turkey.  Sprinkle remaining 1/2
cup cheese over soup mixture. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until hot
and bubbling.
