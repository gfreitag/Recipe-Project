Title: Banana Apple
Categories: Breads, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
2    Egg whites -- whipped
3 tb Fat-free sour cream
1 c  Bananas -- mashed
1 ts Vanilla
2    Apples -- chopped
2 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1/2 c  Granulated sugar -- at room
Temperature
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine applesauce, egg whites, sour cream,
bananas, vanilla, and apples.  In another mixing bowl, combine flour,
sugars, baking powder, baking soda, and  cinnamon.  Mix wet
ingredients with dry ingredients just until moistened.  Pour into
prepared pan(s).  Bake about 1 hour depending on pan size.
