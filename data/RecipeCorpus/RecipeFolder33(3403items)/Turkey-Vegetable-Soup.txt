Title: Turkey Vegetable Soup
Categories: Vegetables, Poultry, Soup/stew
Yield: 10 servings
2 tb Olive oil
3 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1 1/2 c  Onions-- chopped
1 c  Carrots; chopped
3    Cl Garlic; minced
10 c  Chicken broth
2 c  Rice; cooked
1/2 ts Black pepper
1 tb Thyme
2 c  Green beans; bize size
2 c  Turkey; diced/cooked
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Parsley
Recipe by: Anita A. Matejka Preparation Time: 0:20

In a large saucepan, heat oil over medium heat. Then, add mushrooms
onions, carrots, celery, and garlic. Cook for 5 minutes or until the
vegetables are tender. Add chicken broth, rice, pepper, and thyme.
Raise heat to high and bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer
uncovered, for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Add green benas and
simmer for 7 minutes. Stir in turkey and simmer another 5 minutes or
until vegetables are tender. Stir in lemon juice and parsley.
