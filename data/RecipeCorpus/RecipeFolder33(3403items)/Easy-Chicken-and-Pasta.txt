Title: Easy Chicken and Pasta
Categories: Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Skinless, boneless chicken
-breasts, cut up
1 cn Campbell's Condensed Cream
-of Chicken Soup (10.75 oz)
1/2 c  Water
1 pk Frozen seasoned pasta and
-vegetable combo (16 oz)
Prep Time: 10 minutes. Cook Time: 20 minutes.

1. In skillet over medium-high heat, heat 1 tablespoon oil.

2. Add chicken and brown.

3. Add soup, water and vegetable combination.

4. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 5 minutes or until
vegetables are tender.
