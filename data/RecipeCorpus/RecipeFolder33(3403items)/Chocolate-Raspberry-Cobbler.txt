Title: Chocolate Raspberry Cobbler
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2    Pts raspberries
2 ts Cornstarch
3/4 c  Flour
1/3 c  Brown sugar
4 tb Chilled butter
1 oz Finely chopped semisweet
-chocolate.
Gently toss raspberries with cornstarch and place in 8-inch baking
dish. In a bowl, combine flour and brown sugar, cut in butter until
mixture resembles coarse meal.  Mix in chocolate.  Cover raspberries
with chocolate-crumb mixture.  Bake in preheated 450 deg F oven for
20 minutes, until top is browned and berries are bubbling. Source:
Fifty Ways to Cook Most Everything (Andrew Schloss)
