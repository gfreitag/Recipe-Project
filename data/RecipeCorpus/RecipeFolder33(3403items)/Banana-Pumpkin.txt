Title: Banana Pumpkin
Categories: Breads, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
2    Egg whites -- whipped
3 tb Orange juice -- at room
Temperature
1 c  Bananas -- mashed
1/2 c  Pumpkin
2 1/2 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1 ts Cinnamon
1/8 ts Cloves
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 c  Raisins
Preheat oven to 350.  Prepare a pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine applesauce, egg whites, orange juice,
bananas, and pumpkin.  In another mixing bowl, combine flour, sugars,
cinnamon, cloves, baking powder, baking soda, and raisins.  Mix wet
ingredients with dry ingredients just until moistened.  Pour into
prepared pan(s).  Bake for 1 1/4 hours depending on pan size.
