Title: Grilled BBQ Chicken
Categories: Poultry, Bbq/grill
Yield: 8 servings
2 ea Chickens (2-2 1/2 lb each)
-quartered, rinsed, pat dry
Salt and pepper, to taste
1 c  Barbecue sauce; any kind
Preheat oven to 400 F.
Place chicken in a large roasting pan.  Sprinkle with salt and
pepper; lightly brush with 1/4 cup barbecue sauce.  Bake 15 minutes,
reduce heat to 350 F., then bake 40 minutes longer.
Preheat grill with medium-hot coals.  Grill chicken 15 minutes (3-4
inches from heat), brushing with rest of sauce and turning halfway
through grilling.
Serves 8.  Per serving:  344 calories, 17 g fat, 110 mg cholesterol.
Source:  Parade Magazine (The Dallas Morning News) July 30, 1995.
