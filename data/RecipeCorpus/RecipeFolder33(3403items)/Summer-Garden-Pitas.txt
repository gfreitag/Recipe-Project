Title: Summer Garden Pitas
Categories: Kids, Sandwich, Snacks
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Tomato
1/2 c  Yellow squash
1/2 c  Zucchini
1/4 c  Parsley
2 tb Chives
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Wine vinegar
Cracked black pepper
4    6-inch pocket bread
8    Lettuce leaves
8 sl Fat Free Past. Process Chees
Combine tomatoes, squash, zucchini, parsley and chives; mix lightly
with oil and vinegar. Season to taste with pepper. For each sandwich,
fill pita half with lettuce leaf, cheese slice and 1/4 cup vegetable
mixture. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-14-95
