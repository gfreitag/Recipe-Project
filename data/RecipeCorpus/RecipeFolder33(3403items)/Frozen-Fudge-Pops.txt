Title: Frozen Fudge Pops
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Milk
1/3 c  Honey
3 tb Unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Butter or margarine
Combine all ingredients in saucepan.  Cook and stir over low heat on
stove until little bubbles appear and mixture thickens.  Remove from
heat; cool slightly and pour into popsicle molds.  Freeze 2 to 4
hours or until firm. Store in freezer.
