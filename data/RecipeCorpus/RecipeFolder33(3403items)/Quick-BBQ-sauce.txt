Title: Quick BBQ sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Onion; finely chopped
2 tb Butter
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1    Cl Garlic; finely chopped
1 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 ts Mustard
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce; to taste
In a small saucepan, cook onion and garlic in butter intil tender. Add
remaining ingredients bring to boil. Reduce heat, simmer uncoverd 15
to 20 min. Use as a basting sauce for chicken, pork or beef.

Can be made in microwave.
