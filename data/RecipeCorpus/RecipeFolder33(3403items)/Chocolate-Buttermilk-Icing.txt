Title: Chocolate Buttermilk Icing
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 c  Margarine
3 tb Cocoa
1/3 c  Water
2 tb Buttermilk Powder
3 1/2 c  Powdered Sugar, sifted
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Nuts, chopped (optional)
Combine margarine, cocoa and water in a saucepan and heat until
margarine and cocoa are melted.  Stir in buttermilk powder and bring
to a rapid boil. Remove from heat and add powdered sugar, vanilla and
nuts (if desired). Stir until consistency is smooth.

Spread frosting on slightly warm cake or brownies.
