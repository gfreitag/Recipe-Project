Title: Microwave 'penicillin' Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Poultry, Microwave
Yield: 10 servings
1    Broiler-fryer chicken, cut
-up (3-4 lb)
3 md Carrots, peeled and sliced
2    Celery ribs with tops,
-sliced
1 lg Onion, chopped
2    Garlic cloves, finely
-chopped
1 tb Finely chopped fresh ginger
-root
1    Bay leaf
1/2 ts Dried thyme
2 cn Condensed chicken broth
-(10.5 oz)
Water
3 c  Cooked fine egg noodles
-OR
10    Matzo balls
Place chicken parts, neck and giblets in a 4-quart casserole. Add
carrots, celery, onion, garlic, ginger root, bay leaf and thyme. Add
broth plus 2 empty cans water. Cover with lid or vented plastic wrap.
Microwave on high 20 minutes.

Rearrange chicken and vegetables. Re-cover and microwave on high 15-20
minutes, or until chicken tests done. Remove bay leaf, giblets and
neck; discard. Transfer chicken to a platter; cool and debone.

Return chicken meat to soup along with noodles. Microwave on high 5
minutes, or to serving temperature.
