Title: OPEN-FACE MONTEREY JACK MELT - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Sandwich, Main dish
Yield: 2 servings,
4    Slices whole grain bread,
-lightly toasted
4 tb Bottled Thousand Island
-dressing
1 sm Red onion, thinly sliced
6 oz Sliced smoked turkey breast
1    Avocado, peeled, thinly
-sliced
1 c  Grated hot pepper Monterey
-Jack cheese (about 3 oz)
1 c  (generous) alfalfa sprouts
Preheat broiler. Place bread on baking sheet. Spread 1 T Thousand
Island dressing on each slice of bread. Top with onion slices, turkey
and avocado. Sprinkle sandwiches with cheese. Broil until cheese
melts and bubbles. Top sandvaches with sprouts and serve.

Bon Appetit/July/94  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
