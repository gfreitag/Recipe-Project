Title: Potatoes Romanoff
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
4    Potatoes; quartered
2 tb Margarine
1 c  Fat-free sour cream
1 md Onion; sliced
1 1/4 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
1 ts Salt
1/8 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
Recipe by: Elizabeth Powell Preparation Time: 1:30 Preheat oven at
350. Prepare a 1 1/2 quart baking dish with cooking spray. Boil
potatoes until tender.  Mash with margarine, sour cream, onions, 3/4
cup cheese, salt and pepper.  Turn mixture into baking dish. Sprinkle
with remaining cheese and paprika.  Bake uncovered, for 40 minutes.
