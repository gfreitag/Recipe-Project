Title: French Fried Frog Legs
Categories: Game
Yield: 16 legs
16    Frog legs
Salt
Freshly ground pepper
Fine bread crumbs
1    Egg, lightly beaten
2 tb Milk
Season the frog legs with salt and pepper, roll in bread crumbs, then
dip into the egg-milk mixture, and roll again in the bread crumbs.
Fry in deep fat at 375'F., allowing about 3 minutes to brown.
