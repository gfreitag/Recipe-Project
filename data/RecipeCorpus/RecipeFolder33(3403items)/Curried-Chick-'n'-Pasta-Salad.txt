Title: Curried Chick 'n' Pasta Salad
Categories: Salads, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Rotini pasta
2 c  Diced cooked chicken
3 c  Diced tart red apples
1 c  Sliced celery
1/2 c  Sliced green onions w/tops
3/4 c  Picante sauce
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Mango chutney
3/4 ts Curry powder
Cook pasta according to package directions; drain. Transfer to large
bowl. Add chicken, apples, celery and green onions, In small bowl,
combine picante sauce, mayonnaise, chutney and curry powder, mixing
well. Pour over pasta mixture; mix well. Chill. Serve with additional
sauce. Makes 4-6 servings, about 7 cups salad.
