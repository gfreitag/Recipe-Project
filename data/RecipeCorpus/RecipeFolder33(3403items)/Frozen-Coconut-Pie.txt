Title: Frozen Coconut Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Pastry
Yield: 8 servings
3 oz Cream cheese -- softened
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Milk
1 1/3 c  Coconut
8 oz Cool Whip* -- thawed
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    Graham cracker pie crust, 9
Inch
Beat cream cheese and sugar in large bowl.  Gradually add milk; beat
until smooth.  Stir in coconut, cool whip and vanilla.  Spoon into
crust. Freeze 4 hours or until firm.  Let stand at room temperature
15 minutes until it can be cut easily.  Store leftover pie in freezer.
