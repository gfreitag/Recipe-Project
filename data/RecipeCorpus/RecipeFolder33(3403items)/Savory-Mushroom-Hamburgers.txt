Title: Savory Mushroom Hamburgers
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Olive oil
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/4 ts Lemon juice
1    Cl Garlic; minced
1 sm Onion; sliced
1 c  Mushrooms; sliced
1 lb Turkey; ground/cooked
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
Recipe by: Betty Crocker, Best Recipes For Ground Turkey Preparation
Time: 0:20 Heat oil in a large skillet. Add Worcestershire sauce,
lemon juice, garlic, onion, and mushrooms; cook and stir over medium
heat 2 minutes. Remove from heat.  Mix meat, salt, and pepper.  Shape
mixture into 4 patties, about 3/4" thick.  Push mushroom-onion
mixture to side of skillet. Cook patties in same skillet over
medium-high heat, about 10 minutes, turning once, to desired
doneness.  Serve mushroom-onion mixture over patties.
