Title: Christmas Beer Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Beer
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
Topping:
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Cinnamon
2/3 c  Chopped nuts
Cream butter and sugar; add dry ingredients alternately with liquids.
Spoon 1/3 batter into a greased 10 inch tube pan.  Sprinkle 1/3 of
the topping on batter.  Repeat 2 times.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45
to 50 minutes. Randy Rigg
