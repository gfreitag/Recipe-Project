Title: Summer Vegetables
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 4 servings
Vegetable cooking spray
4    Cloves garlic, minced
2 c  Sliced zucchini
2 c  Sliced yellow squash
1 c  Chopped tomato
1/2 c  Julienne-sliced green pepper
1/2 c  Ready-to-serve, no-salt-
-added chicken broth
1 tb Chopped fresh basil  -OR-
1 ts Dried whole basil
Coat a large nonstick skillet with cooking spray; place over
medium-high heat until hot. Add garlic, and cook 1 minute. Add
zucchini and next 4 ingredients. Cook 3 minutes or until vegetables
are crisp-tender, stirring constantly. Stir in basil. Yield: 4
servings.

Nutrition Info: 2.2g protein, 0.5g fat, 8.3g carbohydrate, 0mg
cholesterol, 8mg sodium, 31mg calcium.

Recipe from Frances Wiltshire; printed in "Southern Living" June, 1991
