Title: Peanut Butter
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1 1/4 ts Baking soda
3/4 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Skim milk -- at room
Temperature
1 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
1    Egg white -- whipped
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Peanut butter
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugars, baking soda, baking powder,
and salt. In another mixing bowl, combine milk, applesauce, egg
white, vanilla, and peanut butter.  Mix dry ingredients with wet
ingredients just until moistened.  Pour batter in the prepared pan.
Bake for 1 hour and 10 minutes depending on pan size.
