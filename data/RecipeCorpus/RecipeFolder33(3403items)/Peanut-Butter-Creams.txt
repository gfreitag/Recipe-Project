Title: Peanut Butter Creams
Categories: Candies
Yield: 57 servings
1/4 c  Cream cheese
2 c  Powdered sugar -- sifted
3 tb Peanut butter
1 tb Butter
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Peanuts -- chopped fine
In a mixing bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar, peanut butter, butter,
and vanilla.  If necessary add a little more powdered sugar.  Cover
and chill for 1 to 2 hours.  Form into small balls and roll each ball
in peanuts. Place in paper candy cups, cover and keep refrigerated
for up to 4 days. Return to room temperature about 2 hours before
eating.
