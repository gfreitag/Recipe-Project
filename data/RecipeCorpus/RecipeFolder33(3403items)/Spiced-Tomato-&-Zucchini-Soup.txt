Title: Spiced Tomato & Zucchini Soup
Categories: Vegetables, Tomatoes, Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
1    28 oz can tomatoes; undraine
1 md Zucchini; sliced thin
1 1/2 c  Corn; frozen
1 c  Chicken broth
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Chili powder
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Cinnamon
Recipe by: Fresh Food Favorites Preparation Time: 0:15 In a skillet
combine all ingredients.  Cover and simmer until vegetables are
tender and flavors are blended, stirring every 5 minutes.
