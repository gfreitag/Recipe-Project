Title: Bianca Pizza Sauce
Categories: Pizza, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
1 c  Mascarpone cheese
Substitute:
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Cream cheese
1 c  Heavy cream
1 tb Hazelnut liqueur or sherry
2 tb Chopped shallots or onion
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well.
