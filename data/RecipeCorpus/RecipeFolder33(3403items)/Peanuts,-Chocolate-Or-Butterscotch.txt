Title: Peanuts, Chocolate Or Butterscotch
Categories: Holiday, Candies
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Semi sweet or butterscotch
Chips -- melted
2 1/2 c  Roasted shelled peanuts --
Skins on
Melt chocolate or butterscotch chips in a double boiler over hot (not
boiling) water.  Add peanuts and stir to coat.  Turn out on waxed
paper, separate with fork.  Allow to cool.
