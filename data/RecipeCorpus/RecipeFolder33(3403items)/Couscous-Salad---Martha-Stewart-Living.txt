Title: Couscous Salad - Martha Stewart Living
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------YIELD: 8-------------------------------
2 c  Water
1 1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Couscous
1 sm Red onion, diced
2 sm Cucumbers, peeled if waxy,
-seeded, and diced
2 ea Ripe tomatoes, seeded and
-diced
3/4 c  Currants (opt.)
1/2 c  Chopped flat-leaf parsley
Couscous Dressing (separate
-recipe)
1 ea Bring water to a boil; stir
-in salt and couscous.
-Remove pan from
heat, cover, and let stand 5 minutes. Transfer couscous to a large
bowl and break up any clumps. 2. Toss couscous with remaining
ingredients. Add dressing and mix well. Martha Stewart Living/June &
July/93 Scanned & fixed by Di Pahl
