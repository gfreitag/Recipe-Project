Title: Hot Ham & Cheese Pockets
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 4 sandwiches
4 sl Ham (thin slices)
4 sl Cheddar cheese (thin slices)
1 cn Pillsbury Refrigerated
-Crescent Rolls (8 oz)
1. Heat oven to 375'F. Separate dough into 4 rectangles; firmly press
perforations to seal. On ungreased cookie sheet, press or roll each
to form 7x5" rectangle.

2. Place one ham slice and one cheese slice on each rectangle. Fold
dough in half over filling; press edges to seal.

3. Bake for 10-13 minutes.
