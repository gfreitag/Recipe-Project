Title: Corn Relish SL
Categories: Can/preserv, Vegetables, Relishes
Yield: 6 pints
18 ea Ears fresh corn (approx)
7 qt Water
1 sm Head cabbage; chopped
1 c  Onion; chopped
1 c  Green pepper; chopped
1 c  Sweet red pepper; chopped
1 c  To 2 cups sugar
2 tb Dry mustard
1 tb Celery seeds
1 tb Salt
1 tb Ground turmeric
1 qt Vinegar (5% acidity)
1 c  Water
Remove husks and silks from corn just before cooking.  Bring 7
quarts water to a boil; add corn.  Bring water to a boil; add corn.
Bring water to a second boil; boil 5 minutes. Cut corn from cob;
measuring about 2 quarts of kernels.
Combine corn kernels and remaining ingredients in a large saucepan;
simmer over low heat 20 minutes.  Bring mixture to a boil.
Pack into hot sterilized jars, leaving 1/4-inch headspace.  Cover
at once with metal lids, and screw bands tightly.  Process in
boiling-water bath for 15 minutes.
