Title: Pinto Bean Tacos
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
4    Taco shells
1    15 oz can pinto beans; rinse
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Bell peppers; chopped
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1/2 pk Taco seasoning mix
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Cumin
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
Recipe by: Quick, Healthy, And Delicious Cooking Preparation Time:
0:15 Heat taco shells according to package directions.  Meanwhile, in
a saucepan combine pinto beans, tomato sauce, peppers, onions, taco
seasoning mix, chili powder, oregano, and cumin.  Bring to a boiling.
Reduce heat; simmer occasionally.  Mash beans slightly.  Spoon
mixture into taco shells. Top with cheese.
