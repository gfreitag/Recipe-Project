Title: Fat Tuesday Creamsicle
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 serving
3 oz Orange juice
3 oz Punch
1 1/2 oz Grain alcohol or vodka
1/2 oz Colada mix
1 oz Creme de banana flavoring
Mix all ingredients in a blender with cracked ice; blend until
frappeed and pour into a chilled glass. The drink can be mixed and
served over shaved ice, if desired.
