Title: White Russian
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 oz Vodka
1 1/2 oz Kahlua
1 1/2 oz Heavy cream
Ice cubes
Fill a rocks glass with ice. Add vodka and kahlua and stir. Float
cream on top by pouring it slowly over a teaspoon or bar spoon held
bottom-side up. Serve with a stir stick.
