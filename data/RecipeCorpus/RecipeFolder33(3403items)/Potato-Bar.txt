Title: Potato Bar
Categories: Potatoes, Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
4    Baking potato
1 c  Guacamole
1 c  Tomato
1/4 c  Green onion
1 c  Plain lowfat yogurt
1 c  Cheddar cheese
1/4 c  Bacon bits
1/4 c  Butter
Bake potatoes, one per person, split and fluff with fork.  Each
person can select his or her own topping.

TOPPING VARIATIONS: - Combine lowfat cottage cheese, chopped green
and red bell peppers and sliced green onions. - Kabobs of grilled
meat or poultry and vegetables of your choice. - Combine plain lowfat
yogurt, minced onion and parsley, white wine vinegar and a dash of
paprika. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-14-95
