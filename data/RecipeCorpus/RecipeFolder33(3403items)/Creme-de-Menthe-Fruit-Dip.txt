Title: Creme de Menthe Fruit Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Fruit, Alcohol
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
3 tb Creme de menthe
1/2 c  Finely chopped nuts
20 ea Green maraschino cherries,
-drained and chopped fine
2 ts Lime juice
Combine all ingredients and chill thoroughly.  Serve with sliced or
halved fresh fruit such as pineapple, melon, banana, strawberries or
kiwi. Makes 2 cups. Origin: Homestyle Canadian Classics, Ladies of
the Royal Purple. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
