Title: Frozen Daiquiri
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
2 oz Rum
1 1/2 oz Lime juice
1 ts Powdered sugar
4 oz Crushed ice
Fill a blender with the crushed ice. Add rum, lime juice and sugar.
Blend until smooth and pour into a cocktail glass. Garnish with a
lime slice.
