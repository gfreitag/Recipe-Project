Title: Thai Fried Rice
Categories: Thai, Pasta/rice
Yield: 6 servings
VPRJ01A
3 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Onion -- finely chopped
1 tb Garlic -- chopped
5 c  Cooked long-grain rice
1 c  Chicken -- cooked & diced
2    Eggs -- lightly beaten
3 tb Fish soy
1/2 ts White pepper
-
1 tb Coriander -- chopped
1 c  Cucumbers -- sliced
2 tb Red and green peppers --
Chop
3    Green onions -- chopped
GARNISHES-----
Heat the oil in a wok over med-high heat.  Add the onion and stir-fry
3 minutes;  add the garlic and stir-fry until onion and garlic are
golden. Add the rice and diced chicken and stir-fry for 3 minutes.
Add the beaten egg and stir vigorously until cooked. Sprinkle with
fish soy and white pepper and remove from heat. Serve with garnishes.
