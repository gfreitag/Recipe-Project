Title: Hot Buttered Rum
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
2 oz Rum
6 oz Water
2 ts Butter
1 ts Sugar
Bring water to a boil. Put one teaspoon sugar in a coffee mug and pour
boiling water on top; stir until sugar dissolves. Add rum and butter
and stir until butter melts.
