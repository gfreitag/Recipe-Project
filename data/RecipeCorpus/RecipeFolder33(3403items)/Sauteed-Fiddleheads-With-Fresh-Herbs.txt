Title: Sauteed Fiddleheads With Fresh Herbs
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Olive oil
3/4 c  Onions, chopped
2 ts Garlic, minced
1/2 c  Carrots, julienned
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Cilantro, chopped
1 tb Basil, chopped
2 c  Fiddleheads, trimmed
Heat the oil in a pot.  Saute the onions, garlic, carrots, salt &
fresh herbs for about 3 minutes.  Add the fiddleheads & saute for a
further 5 minutes.  Stir occasionally in order to ensure even
cooking. The fiddleheads should be a rich green colour & semi soft.
Serve hot.
