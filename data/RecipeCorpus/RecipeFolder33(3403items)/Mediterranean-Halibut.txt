Title: Mediterranean Halibut
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Italian salad dressing
2 ea Cloves garlic; finely
-minced
1 ts Minced fresh OR
1/4 ts Dried thyme
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Water
4 ea Halibut steaks, about 1/2
-inch thick
4 ea Slices tomato
1/2 c  Crumbled feta cheese
In a 13- x 9- x 2-inch baking dish, combine olive oil, salad dressing,
garlic, thyme, lemon juice and water.  Add halibut.  Turn once. Cover
and refrigerate for 8 hours or overnight, turning at least once. When
ready to bake, preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Pour off marinade;
reserve. Place tomato slices on halibut and dribble 1/2 cup marinade
over top. Bake uncovered, for 20 minutes, or until fish flakes easily
with a fork. Baste once with extra marinade.

To serve, sprinkle feta cheesse over top.
