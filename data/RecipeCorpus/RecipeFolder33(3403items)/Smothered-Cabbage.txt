Title: Smothered Cabbage
Categories: Cajun/creol, Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 6 servings
1 ea Large head of cabbage -
-cleaned and chopped
1 c  Chopped ham
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Sugar
Salt and black pepper
In a large Dutch oven, cook the ham pieces and chopped onion in
vegetable oil for 5 minutes. Add the chopped cabbage and seasonings;
cover and cook for 30 minutes or until cabbage is tender.  Stir
occasionally during cooking.

Recipe from "Who's Your Mama, Are You Catholic, and Can You Make A
Roux" by Marcelle Bienvenu.

Source:  Michelle M. Bass
