Title: Virgin Mary
Categories: Alcohol, Mixed drink, Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
4 oz Tomato juice
1/2 oz Lemon juice
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Tabasco sauce
1 pn Celery salt -- to taste
1 pn Salt -- to taste
1 pn Pepper -- to taste
1    Celery stalk
1    Lime slice
Cracked ice
Ice cubes
Combine liquid ingredients and seasonings in a mixing glass. Shake
with ice and strain into a highball or Collins glass filled with ice
cubes. Garnish with celery stalk and wedge of lime.
