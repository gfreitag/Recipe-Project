Title: Almond Poppy Bread (Machine) - Large Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
3/4 c  Water
3 c  White bread flour
1 1/2 tb Dry milk
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Butter
3/4 c  Lemon yogurt
2 tb Honey
1/2 c  Almonds, sliced and toasted
1 tb Lemon peel, dried
3 tb Poppy seeds
2 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
3 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

This soft yellow bread is reminiscent of your favorite poppy seed
muffin, but versatile enough to take you from breakfast to dessert.
Start the day enjoying it with a touch of sweet butter on a
freshly-toasted slice. Savor with tea in the afternoon.  After
dinner, celebrate this winning dessert bread by topping with slices
of cheesy lemon spread. Mmmm!

Success Hints
-------------

Toast almonds lightly in a shallow pan.  STIRRING FREQUENTLY, bake at
350 degrees for 5 minutes or until golden brown.

Use regular or rapid bake cycle.
