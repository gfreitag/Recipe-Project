Title: PETA Tofu Whipped Cream
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Soft tofu
1 tb Vanilla extract
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Soy milk
Combine tofu, vanilla, and sugar in blender or food proc and process
until smooth.  With the machine running, gradually add the soy milk
thru the feeder cap.  Refrigerate until serving time.  It will keep
for a week but can also be frozen.
