Title: Ghoul-Aid 2
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Mixed drink
Yield: 8 servings
1 pk Kool-Aid  -- grape
1 pk Kool-Aid  -- orange
2 c  Sugar
3 qt Water
1 l  Ginger ale  -- cold
Use the unsweetened Kool-Aid

In a large non-metal pitcher, combine envelopes of Kool-Aid with
sugar and water.  Stir until thoroughly mixed and Kool-Aid is
dissolved. Add ginger ale and serve immediately.  Makes about 8-10
servings.
