Title: Confetti Corn Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
5    Jalapeno or serrano chiles,
-stems removed, chopped
1 sm Bell pepper, stems and
-seeds removed, diced
1 md Tomato, diced
1/2 cn Whole kernel corn
1 sm Red onion, chopped
1    Garlic clove, minced
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb Lime juice
1 tb Finely chopped fresh basil
Combine all the ingredients and allow to sit for a couple of hours
before serving. This brightly colored salsa goes well with grilled of
barbecued meats and chicken.
