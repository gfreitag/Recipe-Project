Title: White Trash Cajun Anisette
Categories: Beverages, Oldies, Alcohol, Cajun/creol
Yield: 1 servings
8 c  Sugar
12 c  Water
1 ts Oil of anise
Red food coloring
Vodka
Boil sugar and water.  Then turn off fire.  Add 1 tsp oil of anise.
Color it pink with food coloring, and when cold, add a fifth of
vodka. Let stand six weeks before using, and you can't tell if from
bought. Origin: White Trash Cooking. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
July/95.
