Title: SPICED APPLE-PEAR BUTTER - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Dips/spread
Yield: 3 1/2 cups
2 1/2 lb Rome Beauty or other baking
-apples, peeled, cored,
-coarsely chopped
2 lb Pears, peeled, cored,
-coarsely chopped
1 c  Apple cider or apple juice
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/2 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Firmly packed golden brown
-sugar
1 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
1    3x1/2-inch strip orange
-peel (orange part only)
Position rack in center of oven; preheat to 350'F. Combine first 4
ingredients in saucepan. Bring to boil. Reduce heat; cover. Simmer
until fruit is vety tender, about 15 minutes. Puree fruit mixture in
processor. Mix in 1/2 C sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon and nutmeg.
Transfer mixture to 8 inch square glass baking dish; add orange peel.
Bake uncovered until mixture is dark and thick, stirring often, about
1 hour 20 minutes. Discard peel. Transfer butter to bowl; cool
completely. (Can be made 1 week ahead. Cover and chill.)
