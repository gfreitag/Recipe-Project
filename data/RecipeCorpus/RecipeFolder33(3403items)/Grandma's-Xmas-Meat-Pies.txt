Title: Grandma's Xmas Meat Pies
Categories: Holiday
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 lb Beef
3/4 lb Pork
2    Carrots
1 ts Sage
Salt and pepper to taste
3/4 lb Veal
1 lg Onion
2    Potatoes
1 tb Accent'flavor
1/2 c  Celery
Directions: Grind meat, add the chopped vegetables, 1 tin consomme,
1/2 tin water, cook on stove 20 minutes. Stir to prevent sticking put
in individual pie plates lined with pastry shell. Bake 400 F 20
minutes.

Note: These can be made in a 9" pie shell instead of individual pies
