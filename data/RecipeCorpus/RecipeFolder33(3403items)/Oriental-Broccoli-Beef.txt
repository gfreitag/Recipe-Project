Title: Oriental Broccoli Beef
Categories: Meats, Vegetables, Wok
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 lb Flank steak
1 1/2 lb Broccoli
2 ts Sherry
1/8 ts Black prpper
2 ts Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Sugar
3/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Oil
2 ts Cornstarch
Broccoli flowers are a pleasant contrast to flank steak strips in this
delicious, easy Chinese dish.

Put meat in freezer for several minutes to firm. Cut firm meat across
grain into 1/4 inch slices.

Rinse broccoli. Cut flowers from stalks. Cut stalks into 1/4 inch
slices.

Combine 2 teaspoons sherry, 1/8 teaspoon pepper, 2 teaspoons soy
sauce, 1/2 teaspoon salt, 1/2 teaspoon sugar, and 1/2 cup water in
bowl.

Heat 2 tablespoons oil in heavy skillet or electric fry pan set at 360
degrees. Add broccoli stalk slices. Cover skillet and shake. Cook 2
minutes. Add broccoli flowers. Cover and shake, cook 2 more minutes.
Broccoli should be crisp/tender. Place broccoli on hot platter.

Place remaining 2 tablespoons oil in hot skillet. Add sliced meat.
