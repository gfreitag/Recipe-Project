Title: BBQ Rum Ribs
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce, Meats
Yield: 1 servings
4 lb Spareribs
1/4 c  Ketchup
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 c  Dark rum
2    Cl Garlic; crushed
1 c  Brown sugar; packed
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Chili sauce
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
ds Pepper
Wrap ribs in double thickness of foil and bak for 1 1/2 hours at 350°.
Unwrap and drain drippings. Combine all ingredients and pour over
ribs. Marinate at room temperature for 1 hour. Bake at 350° for 30
min., basting with sauce, or grill 30 min., turning and basting.
