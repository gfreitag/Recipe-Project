Title: Smoky Cheese Chile Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 10 servings
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Cornmeal
1 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 cn Canned Dole Crushed
Pineapple
1    Egg
1    Egg white
1/2 c  Lowfat milk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Canned diced mild
-green chilies
1 1/4 c  Cheddar cheese
1.  Combine dry ingredients in large bowl. 2. Combine pineapple, egg,
egg white, milk, oil and chiles.  Stir into dry mixture just until
moistened. Stir in cheese. 3. Spoon batter into paper lined muffin
cups coated with cooking spray. Bake in 400 degree F. oven 18 to 20
minutes.

Makes 10 large muffins.
