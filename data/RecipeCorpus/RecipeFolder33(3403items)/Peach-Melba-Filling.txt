Title: Peach Melba Filling
Categories: Italian
Yield: 8 meringues
1 pk Frozen raspberries, thawed
-(10 oz)
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Lemon juice
Peach frozen yogurt
Italian Meringue Shells
Combine thawed frozen raspberries with sugar and lemon juice and
puree in a food processor or blender. Fill meringue with peach frozen
yogurt and cover with sauce.
