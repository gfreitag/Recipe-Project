Title: Black Pepper Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------YIELD: 3/4 POUND FRESH PASTA---------------------
2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 c  Unbleached all-purpose or
-semolina flour
2 lg Eggs
1 tb Vegetable oil
Mix the black pepper with the flour in the bowl of a food processor or
electric mixer.  Then add the eggs and oil.  Mix and knead the dough,
let it rest, roll it out, and cut the pasta to size desired. Pasta
Salads! From the collection of Jim Vorheis
