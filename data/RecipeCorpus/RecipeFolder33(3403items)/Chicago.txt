Title: Chicago
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
1 oz Cognac
1/2 oz Triple Sec (or Cointreau)
1/2 oz Persico
dr Angostura Bitters
Sodawater or Champagne
Shake all ingredients except soda or Champagne with ice. Pour into a
grogg glass and fill up with soda or Champagne.
