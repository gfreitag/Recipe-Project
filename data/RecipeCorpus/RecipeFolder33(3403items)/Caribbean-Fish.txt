Title: Caribbean Fish
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 lb Fillets
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts White pepper
1 cn Tomato paste; (6 oz)
1 1/2 ts Coconut extract (which is fa
1/2 c  Water
Recipe by: Lean And Luscious Preparation Time: 0:20

Preheat oven at 350. Place fillets in baking pan lined with foil, with
cooking spray. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over fish.
Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes.
