Title: Chartreuse Cocktail 2
Categories: Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 oz Tequila; 3 T
1 oz Green Charteuse Liqueur; 2
1 oz Lime Juice; 1 T
1/4 c  Crushed Ice
Pour all ingredients over ice in tightly covered container.  Shake
until very cold.  Pour into serving glass; garnish with lime slice,
if desired.
