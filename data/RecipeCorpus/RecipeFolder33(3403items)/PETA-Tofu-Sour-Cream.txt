Title: PETA Tofu Sour Cream
Categories: Dress/stuff, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 cup
1/4 lb Soft tofu; patted dry
1/4 lb Firm tofu; patted dry
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Soy sauce; or tamari
Put all in blender and blend until creamy.
