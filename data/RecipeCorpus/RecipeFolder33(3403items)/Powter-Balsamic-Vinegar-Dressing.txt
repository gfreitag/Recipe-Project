Title: Powter Balsamic Vinegar Dressing
Categories: Dress/stuff
Yield: 24 servings
8 tb Water
2 c  Balsamic vinegar
4 tb Dijon mustard; fat free
8 tb Lemon juice
4 cl Garlic; crushed
2 ts Salt
2 ts Oregano
Black pepper to taste
2 ts Sugar
2 tb Parsley; chopped
Mix all in a jar and shake vigorously.
0% cals from fat
From Food by Susan Powter
