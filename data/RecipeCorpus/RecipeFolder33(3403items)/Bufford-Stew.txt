Title: Bufford Stew
Categories: Fish/seafoo, Soup/stew, Sausage
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
1 ea Bottle liquid concentrate
-crab boil (ignore
-directions, use
Whole bottle)
1 ea Lemon
MMMMM----------------------FOR EACH PERSON---------------------------
1/2 lb Shrimp in shell (uncooked)
1/2 lb Summer sausage cut in
-"wheels"
1 ea Ear fresh or frozen corn on
-cob
1 ea Potato cut in chunks
In a large pot bring water to boil, add lemon and crab boil and
potatoes and corn.  Cook until potatoes and corn are done, add shrimp
and sausage. Cook 3 to 4 minutes (until shrimp are done). Serve with
cocktail or tartar sauce and hot mustard for the sausage. Allow about
1 six pack of very cold beer per person From the files of Earl Shelsby
