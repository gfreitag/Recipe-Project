Title: Carrot
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Unbleached flour
1 c  Brown sugar -- packed
2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Allspice
1 c  Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
3    Egg whites -- whipped
1 c  Carrots -- shredded
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare pan(s) with cooking spray and flour.
In a mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder,
cinnamon, ginger, and allspice.  In another mixing bowl, combine
applesauce, egg white, and carrots.  Mix dry ingredients with wet
ingredients just until moistened.  Pour batter into prepared pan(s).
Bake for 30 minutes depending on pan size.
