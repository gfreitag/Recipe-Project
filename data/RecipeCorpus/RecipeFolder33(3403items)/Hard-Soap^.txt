Title: Hard Soap^
Categories: Information, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
7 lb Tallow
3 lb Rosin
2 lb Potash
6 ga Water
Mix ingredients; boil 3-5 hours. Pour into wash tub. Cut into bars
and lay in the sun 2-3 days to harden. this will last an ordinary
family a year and save on money spent for soap.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
