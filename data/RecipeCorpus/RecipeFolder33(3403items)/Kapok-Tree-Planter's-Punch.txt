Title: Kapok Tree Planter's Punch
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
2 oz Orange juice
2 oz Pineapple
2 oz Lime juice  -- sweetened
1 1/4 oz White rum
1 1/2 oz Amber rum
1 1/4 oz Myer's Jamaican rum
2 tb Grenadine  -- divided
1 1/4 oz Myer's Jamaican rum
Mix orange juice, pineapple juice, lime juice, white rum, amber rum,
1 1/4 oz. Jamaican rum, and 1 T grenadine; shake well.

Pour into glass filled with ice.  Add 1 T grenadine and top with 1
1/4 oz. Jamaican rum.

Source:  Kapok Tree Restaurant
