Title: Oasis in the Desert
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Cocktails
Yield: 1 serving
2 oz Vodka
1 oz Blue Curacao
ds Sweet & Sour
Orange juice
Pineapple juice
Pour all ingredients into mixer with a lot of ice. Blend until slush
like. Serve in tall glass and decorate with pineapple.
