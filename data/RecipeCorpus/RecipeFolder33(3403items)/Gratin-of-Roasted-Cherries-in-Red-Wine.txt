Title: Gratin of Roasted Cherries in Red Wine
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Dry red wine
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
1    3-inch cinnamon stick
2    Whole cloves
1    3-inch strip lemon zest
(yellow part of peel)
2 lb Bing cherries, stemmed and
-pitted
1. Combine wine, sugar, cinnamon stick, cloves and lemon zest in a
saucepan.  Bring to a boil, reduce the heat and simmer 5 minutes.

2. Prepare the cherries and place in a shallow baking dish.  Pour the
syrup over the cherries and bake in a preheated 350-degree oven 30
minutes.

3. Let the cherries cool a bit in the syrup.  Serve warm, room
temperature or chilled with ice cream or sweetened creme fraiche.

From "Spur of the Moment Cook" by Perla Meyers
