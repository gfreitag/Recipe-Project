Title: Crockpot chicken
Categories: Crockpot
Yield: 1 servings
5 lb Chicken; skin removed
2 c  Cola; not diet
2 c  Ketchup
pn Garlic salt
Heat ketchup, cola, and seasonings in a saucepan. Simmer for
:
about 5 minutes. Put chicken in crockpot. Pour sauce over and cook on
low for about 6 hours. This chicken falls off the bone. I serve it
with chicken rice-a-roni or fries.
