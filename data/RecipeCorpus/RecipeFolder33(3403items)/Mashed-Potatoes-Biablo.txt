Title: Mashed Potatoes Biablo
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 6 servings
8 md Potatoes, peeled
1 c  Soy milk
2 tb Margarine
2 ts Lemon juice
1 tb Dijon
1 ts Turmeric
1 ds Cayenne
2 tb Parsley, chopped
Place potatoes in a large pot, cover with water, lightly salted, &
boil until tender.  Drain & mash, mixing in enough soy milk to make
the potatoes moist & fluffy.  Stir in remaining ingredients & serve.

NOTE: Warm the milk to prevent the potaotes becoming heavy & lumpy as
you mash them.
