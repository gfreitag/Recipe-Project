Title: The Pastor's Wassail
Categories: Beverages, Alcohol, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sugar
4    Cinnamon Sticks
3    Lemon Slices
2 c  Pineapple Juice
2 c  Orange Juice
6 c  Claret Wine
1/2 c  Lemon Juice
1 c  Dry Sherry
Boil first three ingredients with 1/2 cup of water for 5 minutes.
Remove lemon slices, any seeds, and cinnamon sticks. Heat the
remaining ingredients until very warm. Do not boil! Combine with the
syrup and serve very warm. Note: When serving, one should say ---
"Wass hael", an old Saxon phrase, meaning "to be whole" or "be well".

From:  Civil War in the Kitchen Cookbook by Hugh and Judy Gowan
