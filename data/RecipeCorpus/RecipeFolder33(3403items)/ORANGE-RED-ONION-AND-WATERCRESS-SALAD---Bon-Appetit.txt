Title: ORANGE-RED ONION AND WATERCRESS SALAD - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 c  Orange juice
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1 tb Balsamic vinegar or
-red wine vinegar
1 ts Orange flower water (opt.)
2 lg Bunches watercress, trimmed
4    Oranges, peel and white pith
-removed, cut crosswise into
-1/3-inch-thick rounds
1 sm Red onion, thinly sliced
Whisk first 4 ingredients in bowl. Add orange flower water, if
desired. Season dressing to taste with salt and pepper. (Can be
prepared 1 day ahead. Cover, chill. Bring to room temperature before
using.) Arrange watercress, oranges and onion on platter. Pour
vinaigrette over.

Bon Appetit/January/1994  Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
