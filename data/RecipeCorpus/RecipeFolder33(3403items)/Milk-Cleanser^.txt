Title: Milk Cleanser^
Categories: Information, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Milk
1/8 ts Oil
1/8 ts Honey
Mix ingredients well. Apply to face, blot off with damp cloth.
Especially good for dry skin.
