Title: Vodka Collins
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 oz Vodka
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
1 ts Powdered sugar
6 oz Club soda -- or to fill
Cracked ice
Ice cubes
1    Orange slice
1    Maraschino cherry
Fill a mixing glass with ice. Add vodka, lemon juice, and powdered
sugar and shake well. Strain into a Collins glass filled with ice
cubes. Fill with club soda and stir. Garnish with a slice of orange
and a cherry. Serve with a straw.
