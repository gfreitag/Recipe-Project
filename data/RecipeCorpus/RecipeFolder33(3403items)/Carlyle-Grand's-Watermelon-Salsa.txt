Title: Carlyle Grand's Watermelon Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 8 servings
4 c  Chopped seedless watermelon
-(or remove seeds from
-regular watermelon)
1 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
1 tb Chopped fresh mint
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
2 tb Lime juice
1/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Chopped red onion
1 ts Freshly ground black pepper
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Chill before serving.

Per serving: 60 calories, 1 gram protein, 15 grams carbohydrates,
trace fat, 0 gram saturated fat, 0 milligrams cholesterol, 4
milligrams sodium. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-07-95
