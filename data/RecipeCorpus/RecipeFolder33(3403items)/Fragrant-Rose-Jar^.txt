Title: Fragrant Rose Jar^
Categories: Herbs/spice, Oldies, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 pt Dried rose leaves
1/4 oz Orrisroot
2 ts Ginger
2 ts Nutmeg
2 ts Allspice
4 tb Dried lemon verbena
4 tb Dried lavender
Coarse salt
Grain alcohol
Pack thoroughly dried rose petals in a jar, sprinkling each layer with
salt. Cover and let stand two weeks to a month. Then, blend spices and
herbs in a large bowl. Add petals from jar and mix well. Return
mixture to jar, cover and let rest another month. When ready for use,
shake jar well and add a few drops of alcohol.
