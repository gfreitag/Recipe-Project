Title: Cucumber-Cheese Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 recipe
3 oz Pkg cream sheese
1 md Cucumber
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Grated onion
1 tb Salad dressing
Salt and pepper ~
Coarsely grate unpared cucumber, then drain, reserving liquid. Stir
cream cheese to soften; add lemon juice and salad dressing. Stir in
onions and cucumber.  Season to taste.  A few drops Tabasco sauce may
be added. If too thick, thin liquid drained from cucumber.
