Title: Yum Nua
Categories: Salads, Meats
Yield: 2 servings
Shimizu BWHT68A
1/4 c  Cucumber; cut in half --
Thinly, crosswise
Half -- lengthwise then
1 1/2 tb FRESH lime juice
1 ts Sugar -- taste) finely
Choppe
2 tb Cilantro leaves -- chopped
10 oz Piece of top round beef --
Lengthwise then slic
1/4 c  Red or yellow onion -- cut
Thinly -- crosswise
1 1/2 tb Fish sauce (Nam Pla)
1 ts Chili pepper (or more to
1/4 c  Tomato -- diced
(Thai Beef Salad) Charcoal grill beef over medium-high heat for 2-3
mins on each side or until medium rare. Slice beef thinly, across the
grain, into bite size pieces. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix
well. Garnish with chopped cilantro leaves. Serve on fresh iceberg or
romaine lettuce bed with or without steamed sticky rice. Enjoy!!
Happy (Thai) Cooking!! **:Gail in IL
