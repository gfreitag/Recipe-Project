Title: Pizza Pleasure Bread (Machine) - Regular Loaf
Categories: Breads, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 loaf
3/4 c  Water
2 c  White bread flour
1 tb Dry milk
1 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
1 tb Butter
1/3 c  Pepperoni, chopped
1/4 c  Mozzeralla, shredded
2 ts Parmesan, grated
1/4 c  Mushrooms, canned/drained
2 tb Onion flakes
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Oregano
1 ts Yeast, fast rise or machine
- or -
2 ts Yeast, active dry
Source:  Electric Bread

With this bread, you can take your pizza on a real picnic - it's that
close to the real thing!  Finally... a pizza with a perfect crust!

Success Hints
-------------

Use stick perreroni and dice into 1/4" chunks.

As with al cheese breads, loaf appearance will be unusual due to the
moisture of the cheese.

Use regular or rapid bake cycle.
