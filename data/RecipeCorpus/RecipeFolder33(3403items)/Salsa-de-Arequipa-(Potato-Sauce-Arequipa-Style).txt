Title: Salsa de Arequipa (Potato Sauce Arequipa-Style)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables, So. america
Yield: 2 -1/2 cups
8 ea Dried yellow Aji chiles
OR-
6 ea Mew Mexican red chiles;
-see note 1
3 ea Saltine-type crackers;
-crumbled
1/4 c  Walnuts, ground in a spice
-mill
1/2 c  Monterey Jack cheese; grated
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Onion; chopped
1 cl Garlic; mashed
2 ea Hard-boiled eggs; minced
12 ea Cooked shrimp; peeled and
-mashed
Milk
Salt & freshly ground black
-pepper to taste
Note 1: Seeds and stems removed, soaked in water to soften, and
pureed, or substitute 6 New Mexican red chiles

In a large bowl, combine the chiles, crackers, walnuts, cheese,
vegetable oil, garlic, eggs, and shrimp and mix well to make a thick
paste. Drizzle in the milk as needed and, using a potato masher,
transform the paste into a thick sauce.  Add salt and pepper to taste.

The Authors' heat scale: Medium
