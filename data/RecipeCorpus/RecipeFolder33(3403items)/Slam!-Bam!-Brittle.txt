Title: Slam! Bam! Brittle
Categories: Candies, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Honey
1 c  Roasted peanuts
2 tb Butter
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Baking soda
Combine sugar and honey in 2-quart microwave-safe container.
Microcook at HIGH (100%) 3 minutes; stir and microcook at HIGH 3
minutes longer.  Stir in peanuts.  Microcook at HIGH 1 minute; stir
in butter and vanilla.  Stir in soda until light and foamy.  Spread
onto lightly cookie sheet; cool. Break into pieces.

Recipe developed for 600 to 700 watt microwave ovens.
