Title: Tom Kah Gai
Categories: Soup/stew, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
3    Boneless chicken breasts
4 c  Coconut milk
4    Fresh or dried galanga
1 sm White onion -- cut bite
Sized
1 tb Fish sauce (Nam Pla)
1/4 ts MSG (opt)
5    Fresh corianders leaves
1/2    Fresh basil leave -- cut up
Bite sized
2 c  Water -- thinly sliced
1    Green onion -- cut in 2" pcs
To
3    Fresh chilis -- sliced
Up
Taste
(Chicken in Coconut Milk Soup) In a large sauce pan, mix coconut milk
and water. Bring to a boil. Add chicken, onions galanga, MSG, chilis
and fish sauce. Cook for 5-10 minutes. Reduce heat, simmer 10 minutes
longer. Sprinkle basil and coriander leaves over the soup before
serving. Serve hot over warm cooked rice. -Sopit.
