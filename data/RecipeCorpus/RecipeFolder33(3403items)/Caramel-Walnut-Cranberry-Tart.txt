Title: Caramel Walnut Cranberry Tart
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Holiday
Yield: 20 servings
4 1/2 oz Butter
3 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Salt
4 1/2 tb Cider vinegar
2 ts Vanilla
3 c  Walnuts
1/2 c  Fresh cranberry
6    Egg
2    Pastry crust
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Melt butter and add sugar, salt,
vinegar, and vanilla.  Beat eggs and add butter mixture. Fold in
walnuts and cranberries, and set aside. Prepare a pastry crust and
pre bake 10 minutes. Fill with walnut filling bake 25 minutes at 350
degrees F. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On   08-14-95
