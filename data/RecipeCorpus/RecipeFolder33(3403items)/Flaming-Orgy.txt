Title: Flaming Orgy
Categories: Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 oz Wild Turkey
1/2 oz Bacardi 151
2 dr Grenadine
Take 1 part Wild Turkey bourbon, float 1 part Bacardi 151 on top
(pouring over the back of a spoon works well to float it), touch it
with the barest hint of grenadine for color. light the rum.
