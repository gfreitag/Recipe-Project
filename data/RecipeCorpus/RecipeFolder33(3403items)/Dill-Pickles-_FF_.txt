Title: Dill Pickles _FF_
Categories: Can/preserv, Pickles, Vegetables
Yield: 8 quarts
50 ea Unwaxed cucumbers 3 to 4
-inches long
1 qt Cider vinegar
3/4 c  Salt
2 qt Water
Fresh dill sprigs
Garlic cloves; peeled
Put the cucumbers in the sink, cover with cold water and let stand
overnight.  Drain and pack them into hot, *sterilized* jars.  Combine
the vinegar and salt with the water in a pot and bring to the boiling
point. Pour over the cucumbers, leaving 1/4 inch headspace.  Add a
sprig or two of dill and a clove of peeled garlic to each jar and
seal. Or close the jars and process in a boiling-water bath for 15
minutes.
