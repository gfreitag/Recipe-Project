Title: Thai Coconut Milk Custard
Categories: Thai, Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
5 lg Eggs
1/2 c  Sugar -- brown
1 c  Coconut milk
Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Place rack in center of oven.

Beat eggs, sugar, and coconut milk until frothy. Pour mixture into 8"
by 8" baking dish or individual c. Place in larger pan filled with
hot water. Water should be halfway up the sides of baking dish.

Bake 30-45 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
Be careful not to overbake.

If serving hot, spoon into dishes; if serving cold, allow mixture to
cool before cutting into squares.

VARIATION Add 1/2 c cooked rice to make rice pudding From "A Taste of
