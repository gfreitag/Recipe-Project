Title: Kellogg's Crispix Mix Original
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 12 servings
7 c  Kellogg's Crispix cereal
1 c  Dry roasted mixed nuts
1 c  Pretzels
3 tb Reduced-calorie margarine,
-melted
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1/4 ts Onion salt
2 ts Lemon juice
4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1. Combine Kellogg's Crispix cereal, nuts and pretzels in a 13x9x2"
baking pan. Set aside.

2. Stir together remaining ingredients. Gently stir spices and
margarine into cereal mixture until evenly coated.

3. Bake at 250'F. about 45 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes. Spread
on paper towels to cool. Store in airtight container.

NOTE: If desired, 3 tablespoons regular margarine can be used in
place of reduced-calorie margarine.
