Title: Scalloped Rhubarb^
Categories: Fruit, Side dish, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/4 c  Butter, melted
4 c  Rhubarb, in one inch pieces
2 c  Sugar
Little cinnamon
Few lemon rind gratings
Few orange rind gratings
Fill a buttered baking dish, in layers, in order given. Cover tightly
and bake in a moderate oven about 30 minutes. You might want to serve
this with some whipped cream or maybe just some regular cream to
moisten it up a bit.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
