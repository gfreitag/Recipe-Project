Title: Stuffed Zucchini #1
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
4    Med. Zucchini
2 tb Butter Or Margarine
1/4 c  Grated Parmesian Cheese
1/4 ts Salt
3    Green Onions, Sliced
1    Slice Bread, Cubed
1    Med Tomato, Chopped
Dash Pepper
Cut zucchini lengthwise in halves.  Scoop out pulp, leaving 1/4-inch
wall; chop pulp and reserve.  Place zucchini shells cut sides down in
dish. Cover loosely and microwave on high (100%) until crisp tender,
about 5 to 6 minutes.

Cover and microwave reserved pulp, the onions and margarine in a 1
1/2-qt casserole or bowl on high (100%) until tender, 6 to 7 minutes.
Stir in bread cubes, cheese, tomato, salt and pepper.

Turn zucchini shell cut sides up; spoon mixture into shells.  Cover
loosely and microwave on high (100%) until hot, 2 to 3 minutes.
