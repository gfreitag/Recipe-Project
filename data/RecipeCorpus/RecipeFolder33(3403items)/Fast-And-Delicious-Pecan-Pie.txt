Title: Fast And Delicious Pecan Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
4    Eggs
3/4 c  Sugar
4 tb Butter
2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Buttermilk baking mix
1 c  Pecans
1 tb Vanilla extract
Grease a 10" pie plate. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Blend all
ingredients (except pecans) in blender until smooth. Add pecans. Bake
for about 45 minutes. Serve with ice cream; also good cold.
