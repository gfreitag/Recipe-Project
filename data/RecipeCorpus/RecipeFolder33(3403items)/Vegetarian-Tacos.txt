Title: Vegetarian Tacos
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
2 cn Kidney beans; (19 oz) draine
1 ts Chili powder
1/2 c  Bell peppers
1 lg Onion; chopped
8    Taco shells
1 pk Taco seasoning mix
1 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
Recipe by: The Kitchen Survival Guide Preparation Time: 0:30 In a
skillet over low heat cook onions, and peppers.  Drain beans and
place in a skillet.  Mash with a spoon.  Add chili powder and taco
seasoning mix in skillet until thoroughly combined.  Divide bean
mixture among taco shells and sprinkle with cheese.
