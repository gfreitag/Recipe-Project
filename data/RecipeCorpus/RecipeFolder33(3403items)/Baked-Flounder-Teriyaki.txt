Title: Baked Flounder Teriyaki
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
6    Fish fillets
1/3 c  Apple juice
3 tb Lemon juice
2 tb Onions; chopped
2 ts Ginger
2 ts Honey
1 ts Olive oil
1/4 ts Black pepper
2    Cl Garlic; chopped fine
Recipe by: Betty Crocker's New Choice Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:15

Preheat oven at 375.  Prepare a 13 x 9 x 2 pan lined with foil, with
cooking spray. Place fillets in pan.  Mix remaining ingredients;
spoon over fish.  Cover with foil and refrigerate 1 hour.  Bake
covered for 15 to 20 minutes or until fillets flake easily with fork.
