Title: (Machine) Doggie Biscuits
Categories: Animal
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Hot water or meat juice
1/3 c  Margarine
1/2 c  Powdered milk
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg, beaten
3 c  Whole wheat flour
Mix well - roll in to small logs in your hands and bake at 325
degrees for about 50 mins.
