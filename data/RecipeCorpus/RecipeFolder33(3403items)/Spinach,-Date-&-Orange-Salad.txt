Title: Spinach, Date & Orange Salad
Categories: Salads, Fruit, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Spinach, rinsed & patted dry
2 ea Oranges, in segments
12 ea Dates, halved
6 tb Almonds, slivered
x  Marie's Lite Vinaigrette
Arrange spinach individually on 6 plates or place in large salad
bowl. Top evenly with oranges, dates and almonds.  Serve with Marie's
Lite Red Wine Vinaigrette.  124 calories and 3 grams fat. Source:
