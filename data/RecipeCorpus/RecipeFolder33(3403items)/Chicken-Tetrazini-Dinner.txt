Title: Chicken Tetrazini Dinner
Categories: Poultry, Tasteofhome, Pasta/rice, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 servings
1 1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Margarine or butter
1 pk Lipton Noodles &
-Sauce-Chicken Flavor
-Tetrazzini
2 c  Cut-up cooked chicken or
-turkey
1/2 c  Thawed frozen or drained
-canned peas
2 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
-(optional)
From : Sallie Krebs, Sat 06 Aug 95 04:00, Area: COOKING

In medium saucepan, bring water, milk and margarine to the boiling
point Stir in Noodles & Sauce-Chicken Flavor Tetrazzini and continue
boiling over medium heat, stirring occasionally, 6 minutes. Stir in
chicken, peas and cheese. Continue boiling 2 minutes or until noodles
are tender and chicken is heated through.

Makes about 2 servings.

Source: Midwest Living, August 1995
