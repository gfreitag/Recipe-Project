Title: Herb Stuffed Eggs
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 24 appetizers
12    Hard-cooked eggs
1/2 c  Garlic & herb cheese spread
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  GREY POUPON Dijon or Country
-Dijon Mustard
1/4 c  Finely chopped scallions
Halve eggs lengthwise. Scopp out yolks into bowl, set egg white halves
aside. Mash yolks; blend in cheese spread, mayonnaise, mustard and
scallions until smooth. Spoon or pipe yolk mixture into egg white
halves. Submitted By MICHAEL ORCHEKOWSKI   On   08-14-95
