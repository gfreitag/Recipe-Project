Title: Beans and Turkey
Categories: Vegetables, Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Turkey; ground
1 lg Onion; diced
2    Cl Garlic; minced
1 c  Bell peppers; chopped
2 c  Stewed tomatoes
1 c  Kidney beans; cooked
1/2 ts Chili powder; or to taste
Recipe by: Cooking For Good Health Preparation Time: 0:15

Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare a 1-1/2 quart baking dish with cooking
spray. Brown meat in a medium-size skillet until no pink remains;
drain off fat. Add onion, garlic, peppers, tomatoes and cook 10
minutes.  Add beans and chili powder. Spoon mixture into sprayed
baking dish.  Cover and bake 1-1/2 hours. Remove cover. Bake 20
minutes more.  Serve hot.
