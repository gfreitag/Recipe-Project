Title: Peppery Cheese Twists
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 servings
Makes about 18 twists
<Contributed by Ed Lawyer>
MMMMM-----------------****** INGREDIENTS ******----------------------
1 ea Sheet frozen puff pastry,
-thawed
1 ea Egg white, beaten
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 1/2 ts Cracked black pepper [use
-whole peppercorns &
-smash-glide
Under a heavy pot or pan]
MMMMM------------------****** DIRECTIONS ******-----------------------
Preheat oven to 375F. On lightly floured surface, roll pastry into
14x11" rectangle. Brush with egg white. Sprinkle with cheese and
pepper; gently press into pastry. Cut crosswise into 3/4"-wide
strips; shape into twists. Place on baking sheet; press down ends.
Bake for 15 minutes, or until crisp & browned. <29 Minute Meals>
