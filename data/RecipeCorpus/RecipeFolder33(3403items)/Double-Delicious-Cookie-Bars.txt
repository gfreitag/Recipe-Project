Title: Double Delicious Cookie Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 36 bars
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 cn Eagle Brand or Eagle Brand
-Low Fat Sweetened
-Condensed Milk (NOT
-evaporated milk; 14 oz)
1 pk Semi-sweet chocolate chips
-(12 oz)
1 c  Peanut butter chips
Prep Time: 10 minutes.

Preheat oven to 350'F. (325'F. for glass dish). In 13x9" baking pan,
melt margarine in oven. Sprinkle crumbs evenly over margarine; pour
Eagle Brand evenly over crumbs. Top with chips; press down firmly.
Bake 25-30 minutes or until lightly browned. Cool. Cut into bars.
Store loosely covered at room temperature.
