Title: Daiquiri
Categories: Mixed drink, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 1 servings
2 oz Rum
1 1/2 oz Lime juice
1 ts Powdered sugar
Cracked ice
Fill a mixing glass with ice. Add rum, lime juice, and powdered sugar.
Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with a lime
slice.
