Title: Cheese Chowder
Categories: Low cal/fat, Soup/stew, Potatoes, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 8 servings
3 c  Potato
2 c  Carrot chunks
1 c  Celery chunks
1 c  Water
10 oz Canned low sodium chicken br
1 lb Healthy Choice Fat Free Proc
1/4 c  Parsley
Cracked black pepper
Cook potatoes, carrots and celery in chicken broth and water until
vegetables are tender.  Combine cheese and 1/2 cup of broth from
cooked vegetables in microwave-proof bowl.  Microwave on HIGH 6
minutes or until cheese melts, stirring every 2 minutes.  Add melted
cheese to vegetables and broth; stir in parsley.  Add more water for
thinner chowder.  Season to taste with pepper.
