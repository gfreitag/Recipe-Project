Title: Chicken And Wild Rice
Categories: Poultry, Pasta/rice
Yield: 12 servings
6 c  Cooked wild rice
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1 cn Chicken broth
1 cn Mushrooms -- drained
3 c  Cooked chicken -- diced
1/4 c  Green pepper -- diced
1/4 c  Red pepper -- diced
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 pk Almond slivers
Mix all ingredients except almonds.  Spread into buttered 9x13 inch
pan. Sprinkle almonds over top.  Bake covered for 45 minutes at 350
degrees. Remove cover and continue baking 15 minutes, or until heated
through.
