Title: Apple Cranberry Bars
Categories: Breads, Fruit
Yield: 48 servings
1 c  Margarine -- softened
2 c  Apples -- peeled/chopped
1 c  Cranberries -- chopped
2    Egg whites -- whipped
2 ts Orange peel -- grated
2 ts Vanilla
2 1/4 c  Unbleached flour
1 1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
MMMMM------------------------TOPPING---------------------------------
1/3 c  Margarine -- softened
3 oz Fat-free cream cheese --
Softened
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Powdered sugar --sifted
1 ts Orange peel -- grated
4 ts Skim milk -- at room
Temperature
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Prepare a 15 x 10 x 1" jelly roll pan
with cooking spray and flour.  In a mixing bowl, combine the
margarine, apples, cranberries, egg whites, orange peel and vanilla.
In another mixing bowl, combine flour, baking soda and salt.  Mix dry
ingredients with wet ingredients just until moistened.  Spread
mixture into prepared pan.  Bake for 30 to 35 minutes.  Meanwhile, to
prepare topping, combine the margarine, cream cheese, vanilla, orange
peel, and milk.  Top when bars have cooled.
