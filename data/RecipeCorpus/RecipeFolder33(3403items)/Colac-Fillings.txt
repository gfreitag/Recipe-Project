Title: Colac Fillings
Categories: Cake/cookie, Romanian
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 pt Sour cream
1 ts Cornstarch
4 tb Sugar
1/2 lb Cream cheese
3 x  Egg yolks
1 ts Flavoring, vanilla or orange
Cream cheese well, add cornstarch and egg yolks and mix well. Add
sour cream 1 or 2 Tb at a time. Work fast and mix thouroughly. At the
end add the sugar and flavoring. Spread over dough, roll and bake in
moderate oven. Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   08-11-95
