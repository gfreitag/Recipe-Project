Title: LEMON AND LIME GRANITA - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/4 c  Water
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Fresh lemonjuice
1/2 c  Fresh lime juice
Combine water and sugar in small saucepan. Stir over medium heat until
sugar dissolves and mixture boils. Cool. Mix in lemon and lime
juices. Pour into shallow pan. Freeze until mmixture is frozen,
stirring frequently with fork, about 6 hours. (Can be prepared 2 days
ahead. Keep frozen.)

Using fork, scrape surface of granita to form icy crystals. Scoop
crystals into dessert glasses and serve.
