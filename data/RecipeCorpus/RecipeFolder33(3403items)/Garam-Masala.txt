Title: Garam Masala
Categories: Indian, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Black cardamom seeds
2    Inch cinnamon stick --
Crushed
2 ts Whole cloves
4 ts Black peppercorns
Dry roast the spices separately in a skillet over moderate heat until
aromatic.  Grind to a fine powder in a spice grinder.  Store in
airtight container for about 3 months.  About 6 tbsp.
