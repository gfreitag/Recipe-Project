Title: BBQ Sauce-Glaze
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Poultry
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 tb Pork drippings
1/2 ga Ketchup
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2    57 sauce
1/2 tb Black pepper
2 tb Red pepper
1 tb Garlic salt
1/4 c  Chili powder
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
Combine all ingredients and simmer till thick.

When you use this remember its realy a glaze and not a sauce, this
recipe makes about a gallon.

Use beer to thin befor using.
