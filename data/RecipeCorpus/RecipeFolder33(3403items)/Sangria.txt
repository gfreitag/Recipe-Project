Title: Sangria
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
2 qt Burgandy Wine
2 c  Orange Juice
1/2 c  Sugar -- * see note
1/4 c  Triple Sec
Citrus Fruit -- cut up
* that has been mixed with the rind of one orange (press the oil out
of the rind into sugar)

Mix together and chill.
