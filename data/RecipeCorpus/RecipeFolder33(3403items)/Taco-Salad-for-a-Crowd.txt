Title: Taco Salad for a Crowd
Categories: Salads
Yield: 50 servings
SErve 50-60
5 lb Ground beef
3 ea Taco seasoning, or more, to
-taste
5 ea Heads of lettuce, torn into
-small pieces
3 bn Green onions, chopped
1 lg Cucumber, chopped
2 c  Chopped celery
10 ea Tomatoes, cut into wedges
2-3 lbs cheddar cheese,
-grated
2 c  French dressing or taco
-sauce
30 oz Taco chips, crushed
Brown ground beef and drain off fat.  Add taco seasoning and mix
well. Set aside to completely cool.  In large bowl, mix the lettuce
with the onions, cucumber, celery and tomato.  When beef is
completely cooled, add to salad mixture and toss to mix well.
Sprinkle cheese over top, and chill. Just prior to serving, sprinkle
the taco chips over the top, and add salad dressing.  Mix well and
serve immediately. Origin: Homestyle Canadian Classics, Ladies of the
Royal Purple. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
