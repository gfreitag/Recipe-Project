Title: Halibut Steak With Coffee Butter
Categories: Fish/seafoo
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Instant coffee powder
1/4 c  Margarine -- melted
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 lb Halibut steaks
Combine all ingredients except fish. Brush the mixture very thickly
onto fish. Broil 3-4 inches from grill for 10 minutes. Turn and brush
fish again with sauce. Broil 10 minutes longer or until fish flakes
easily. Brush the Halibut steaks with the coffee butter again and
serve.

: Jo Anne Merrill
