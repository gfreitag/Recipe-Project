Title: Low Fat Fudge Brownie Sundae
Categories: Low cal/fat, Desserts
Yield: 16 servings
1 c  All-purpose flour
3/4 c  Granulated sugar
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Skim milk
1/4 c  Applesauce
1 ts Vanilla extract
3/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa
1 3/4 c  Hot water
Frozen yogurt
1.  Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Spray an 8 x 8-inch baking pan
with cooking spray. 2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar,
1/4 cup cocoa, baking powder and salt. Add milk, Mott's Natural Apple
Sauce and vanilla to dry ingredients. Stir to blend. 3. Spoon batter
into prepared pan. 4. In a medium-size bowl, combine brown sugar, 1/4
cup cocoa and hot water. Stir to blend. 5. Pour over batter in
prepared pan. 6. Bake for 40 minutes.  Remove cake from oven and cool
in pan on a wire rack. 7. Serve with a scoop of low fat frozen yogurt
and spoon the chocolate sauce that has formed on the bottom of the
pan over the brownie as a topping. Submitted By DAN KLEPACH   On
08-14-95
