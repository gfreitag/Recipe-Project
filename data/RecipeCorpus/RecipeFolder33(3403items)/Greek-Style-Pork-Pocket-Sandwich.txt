Title: Greek Style Pork Pocket Sandwich
Categories: Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Pork strips
1/2 c  Caesar salad dressing
2    Pitas -- halved
4 tb Cucumber dressing
1 sm Red onion -- thinly sliced
Stir togethre pork strips and dressing; place in a shallow pan and
roast in a preheated 450 oven until pork is crisp and lightly
browned, about 10 minutes.  Open each pita half to form a pocket.
Distribute pork among each half.  Top each sandwich with a tbsp or
cucumber dressing and some sliced red onion.  353 calories and 17
grams fat.
