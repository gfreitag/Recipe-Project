Title: Italian Salad Dressing
Categories: Salads, Italian
Yield: 2 cups
1 c  Olive oil
4 tb Onion, minced
2 tb Parmesan cheese, grated
4 ts Salt
1 ts Accent (optional)
1/4 ts Worcestershire sauce
1    Small clove of garlic
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1 1/2 ts Basil
1 1/2 ts Oregano
1 1/2 ts Sugar
4    Grinds black pepper
1/3 c  Tarragon vinegar
2 tb Lemon juice
In a blender, blend all the ingredients except the vinegar and lemon
juice for 30 seconds.  Add the vinegar and lemon juice and blend for
an additional 30 seconds.  Shake well before using.
