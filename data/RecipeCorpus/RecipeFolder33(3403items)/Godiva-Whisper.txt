Title: Godiva Whisper
Categories: Godiva, Alcohol, Mixed drink
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 oz Godiva Liqueur
1/2 oz Cognac -- Martell & copy
3    Scoops
Vanilla Ice Cream
Combine ingredients in a blender until smooth. Serve in a snifter or
cocktail glass. Also delicious with coffee or chocolate ice cream.
