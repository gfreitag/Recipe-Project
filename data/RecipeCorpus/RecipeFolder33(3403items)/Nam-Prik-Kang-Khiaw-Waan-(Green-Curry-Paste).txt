Title: Nam Prik Kang Khiaw-Waan (Green Curry Paste)
Categories: Thai
Yield: 1 servings
20    Chilies, green -- chopped
8    Garlic cloves -- crushed
2    Onions -- chopped
1 c  Coriander -- chopped
4 ts Salt
4 ts Coriander -- ground
2 ts Cumin -- ground
2 ts Shrimp paste (Kapi)
2 ts Galangal -- ground
2 ts Lemon grass -- ground
Combine all ingredients in blender, grind into a paste.

GEnie - R.WEISSGERBE "Delightful Thai Cooking" p. 20
