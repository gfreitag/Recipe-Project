Title: Chili Con Carne for a Crowd
Categories: Chili, Meats, Tomatoes
Yield: 50 servings
MMMMM-------------------------SERVES 50------------------------------
1/4 c  Shortening or vegetable oil
3 c  Chopped onions
6 ea Garlic cloves, mashed
8 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Chili powder or more to
-taste
1 tb Salt
1/2 ts Red pepper
1 ts Black pepper
2 ea 48oz cans tomato soup or
-tomatoes
7 qt Red kidney beans (14 19oz
-cans) drained and rinsed
Brown the beef, garlic and onions until done.  Add seasonings, soup
and beans.  cook slowly for about 30-60 minutes, stirring
occasionally. VARIATION: For deluxe chili, provide bowls of grated
cheese, chopped onions, tomato and sour cream as toppings for
individual servings. Origin: Homestyle, Canadian Classics. Ladies of
the Royal Purple. Shared by: Sharon Stevens, July/95.
