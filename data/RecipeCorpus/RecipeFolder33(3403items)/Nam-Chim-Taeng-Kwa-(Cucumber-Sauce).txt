Title: Nam Chim Taeng Kwa (Cucumber Sauce)
Categories: Thai, Pasta/rice
Yield: 2 servings
1/2 c  Vinegar -- rice
1/4 c  Water
1/2 c  Vinegar -- white
1 ts Salt
1 c  Cucumber; peeled -- thin
Slice
1/2 ts Pepper, cayenne -- powder
In a medium saucepan combine vinegars, water, salt, sugar and boil
over high heat. Let cool. Pour sauce over cucumber before service.
Garnish with cayenne powder. Serve with satay.

GEnie - R.WEISSGERBE "Delightful Thai Cooking" p. 16
