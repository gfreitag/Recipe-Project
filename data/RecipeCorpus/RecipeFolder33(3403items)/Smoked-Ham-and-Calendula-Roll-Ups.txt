Title: Smoked Ham and Calendula Roll-Ups
Categories: Newspaper, Meats, Sandwich
Yield: 4 roll-ups
1 pk Cream cheese; 8 oz
2 tb Mayonnaise; low-fat
1 tb Horseradish
3 ts Lemon juice
2 tb Sweet pickle relish
1    Apple; tart, cored, peeled,
. & finely diced
1 c  Calendula petals; washed
4    Flour tortillas; 12-inch
8 oz Smoked ham; wafer-thin slice
Lettuce leaves
Calendula petals for garnish
Blend the cream cheese with the mayonnaise, horseradish, lemon juice
and pickle relish in a bowl.

Gently stir in the apple and calendula petals.

Using a spatula, spread the mixture evenly over the entire surface of
each tortilla.

Cover with a single layer of ham.  Roll up, jely-roll style.

Tightly wrap each in plastic wrap and chill until serving time.

To serve, cut roll-ups to desired thickness.

Arrange on a serving platter over a bed of lettuce leaves.  Sprinkle
with additional calendula petals.
