Title: Lone Star Steak Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Butter
3/4 ts Black pepper
1/2 c  Lemon juice
1/2 ts Dry mustard
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 ds Tabasco sauce
1    Cl Garlic; minced
Salt; to taste
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan. Heat till butter melts.
