Title: Ajad (Thai Satay Cucumber Salad)
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Cucumbers
2 md Chilies -- sliced thin
1/4 c  Red onions -- thinly sliced
3/4 c  Vinegar
10 tb Sugar
1 ts Salt
Peel, seed and quarter the cucumbers lengthwise then cut into 1/8
inch long pieces. Combine vinegar, sugar and salt in a small
saucepan. Cook over low heat until sugar and salt is dissolved.
Remove from heat and cool. Stir in chilies, onions and cucumbers.
Serve with meat Satay and peanut sauce.
