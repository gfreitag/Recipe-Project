Title: ROASTED RED PEPPER ROUILLE - Bon Appetit
Categories: Bon appetit, Condiments
Yield: 1 1/3 cups
1 lg Red bell pepper
2    Hard-boiled eggs (yolks
-only)
1 tb Minced garlic
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
5 tb Extra-virgin olive oil
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
Char pepper over gas flame or in broiler until blackened on all sides.
Place pepper in paper bag and seal; let stand 15 minutes. Peel, stem
and seed pepper. Puree in processor. Transfer to small bowl.

Combine egg yolks, garlic and cayenne pepper in processor and blend
until mixture is as smooth possible. Gradually add olive oil and
process until thickened. Transfer mixture to medium bowl. Stir in red
bell pepper puree and mayonnaise. Season to taste with and pepper.
(Rouille can be prepared 2 days ahead. Cover and refrigerate.)

Bon Appetit/August/94 Scanned & edited by Di Pahl & <gg>
