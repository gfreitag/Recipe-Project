Title: Onion Pasta
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Minced dried onion
1/3 c  Water
2 1/3 c  Flour
2 ea Beaten eggs
1 ts Olive oil or cooking oil
To soften minced dried onion, combine it with water.  In a mixing
bowl, place 2 cups of the flour. Combine eggs, olive oil, and
onion-water mixture; add to flour and mix well. Sprinkle remaining
flour onto kneading surface and knead for 10 minutes, cover and let
rest 10 minutes and then roll and cut into desired shapes.  Makes 1
pound pasta. Origin: Better Homes and Gardens Pasta Book Shared by:
Sharon Stevens Oct/91
