Title: Spam Avec Des Fungii Et Oeuf (Spam W/ Mushrooms And Eggs)
Categories: Meats, Spam, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
SPAM AVEC DES FUNGII ET OEUF
============================
-
MMMMM------------------ASSEMBLE THE INGREDIENTS-----------------------
x  SPAM
x  Mushrooms cultivated
3 ea Eggs
4 ea Green Onions
1 ea Garlic clove
x  Dash paprika
x  Dash cayenne pepper
1/2 ea Tb Butter
4 ea Tb creme
x  Maggi (to taste)
x  British Crumpets
MMMMM---------------------------RECIPE--------------------------------
Heat a medium Iron skillet on medium heat and add the butter and coat
pan. add the garlic, onion, and fungus and saute till odors emerge
and coated. Add minced SPAM and mix well.  add spices and remix. add
eggs and fold into omelet if desired.  garnish with baked crumpets
drizzled with Maggi (a refined Soy extract popular on this planet).
Enjoy with a Cabernet, or Mimosa, as is your desire!
