Title: Summer Couscous
Categories: Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Couscous; boiled
1 1/2 c  Water; boiled
1/4 c  Carrot; chopped fine
1/4 c  Green pepper; chopped fine
2 tb Parsley
6 tb Olive oil
2 tb Lemon juice
1    Cl Garlic; crushed
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Black pepper
Recipe by: Anita A. Matejka Preparation Time: 0:20 Place the couscous
in a medium bowl; add the boiling water; cover and let stand until
all the water is absorbed and mixture has cooled slightly, about
twenty minutes. Add the chopped vegetables.  In a small bowl, whisk
the oil, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper.  Add to the salad;
toss to blend.
