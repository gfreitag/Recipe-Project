Title: Pumpkin Apple Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Unbleached flour
2 tb Unbleached flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cloves
1/8 ts Ginger
12 ts Granulated sugar
2 tb Applesauce -- at room
Temperature
1    Egg white -- whipped
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Pumpkin
1 sm Apple -- peeled/shredded
1/4 c  Raisins
Preheat oven at 350.  Prepare 6 muffin tins with cooking spray and
flour. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, baking powder, baking soda,
salt, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, ginger, and sugar.  In another mixing
bowl, combine applesauce, egg white, vanilla, pumpkin, apple, and
raisins.  Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients just until
moistened.  Use an ice cream scoop to fill muffin tins two thirds
full.  Bake, 35 minutes or until browned.
