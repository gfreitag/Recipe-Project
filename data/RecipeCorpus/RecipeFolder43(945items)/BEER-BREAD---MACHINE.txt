Title: BEER BREAD - MACHINE
Categories:
Yield: 14 Servings
1 pk Yeast
1 c  Rye Flour
2 1/2 c  Bread Flour
2 tb Wheat Germ
1 ts Salt
1 c  Beer -- flat, room temp
2 tb Molasses
2 ts Orange Rind -- grated
6 ts Applesauce, Unsweetened
1/4 c  Water -- 120 deg. F.
Put ingredients in according to instructions given by your machine.
