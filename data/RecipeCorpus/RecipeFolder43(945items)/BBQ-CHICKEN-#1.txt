Title: BBQ CHICKEN #1
Categories: Poultry, China
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Chicken, frying; cut up
1/2 c  Chinese-style barbecue sauce
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Sesame oil
Combine all ingredients in plastic bag. Press air out; seal bag
securely. Rotate gently to coat chicken. Refrigerate 4 to 6 hours.
Grill or broil 4 to 5" from heat, 12-16 minutes on each side, or
until done.

Origin: Oriental Classics Cookbook - GEnie D.WEISSGERBE
