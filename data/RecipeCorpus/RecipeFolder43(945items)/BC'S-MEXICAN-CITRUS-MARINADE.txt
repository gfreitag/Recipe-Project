Title: BC'S MEXICAN CITRUS MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Lime juice
1/4 c  Olive or vegetable oil
2 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
Or parsley
2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Red pepper sauce
1/2    Small onion, chopped
Mix all ingredients in shallow nonmetal dish or resealable plastic
bag. Makes about 1 cup.
