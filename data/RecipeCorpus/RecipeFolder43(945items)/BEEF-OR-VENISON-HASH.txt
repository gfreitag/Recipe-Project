Title: BEEF OR VENISON HASH
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Cajun
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Bacon drippings
1 lg Onion, chopped
3 c  Cooked, chopped beef or>>
Venison roast.
2    Med. potatoes cut in 16 pcs.
2 tb Flour
1    Clove garlic, minced
3 c  Beef broth
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/4 ts Seasoned salt
1/2 tb Chili powder
Brown onion and potatoes in bacon fat.  Add four and brown. Add
broth and other ingredients.  Let simmer until tender.
