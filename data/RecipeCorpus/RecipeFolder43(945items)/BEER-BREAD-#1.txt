Title: BEER BREAD #1
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
From: NCBK03B
3 c  Self-rising flour*
3 tb Sugar
12 oz Warm beer.
Place in a greased and floured loaf pan.  Bake at 350 for 1 hour. * Or
regular flour, 3 & half tsp. baking powder, and 1 & half tsp. salt
This is the only simple recipe I know and it happens to be the one
you want. I think this is one of the only breads you can't mess up on.
