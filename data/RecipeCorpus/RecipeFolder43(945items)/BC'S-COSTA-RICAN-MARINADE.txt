Title: BC'S COSTA RICAN MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 ts Grated lime peel
1/3 c  Lime juice
1/4 c  Tomato juice
1 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
Or parsley
2 ts Vegetable oil
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Red pepper sauce
2    Cloves garlic, minced
Mix all ingredients in shallow nonmetal dish or resealable plastic
bag. Add up to 3 pounds chicken, pork or beef, turning to coat with
marinade. Cover dish or seal bag and refrigerate, turning meat
occasionally, at least 8 hours but no longer than 24 hours. Remove
meat from marinade; discard marinade. Grill meat as desired.
