Title: BENIHAHA'S SUPERB SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings, Sauces, Oriental
Yield: 1 Cups
1/2 c  Soybean oil
1/4 c  White vinegar
3 tb Tomato paste
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 c  Celery, sliced thin
1/4 c  Onions, coarsely chopped
Combine all ingredients in an electric blender. Mix at medium speed
for 3 to 5 seconds, until celery is finely grated. Add desired amount
to salad and toss.
