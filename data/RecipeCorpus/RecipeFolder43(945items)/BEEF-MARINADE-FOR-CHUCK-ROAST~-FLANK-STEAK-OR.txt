Title: BEEF MARINADE FOR CHUCK ROAST~ FLANK STEAK OR
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  WINE VINEGAR
1/4 c  KETCHUP
2 tb SOY SAUCE
1 ts SALT ( OPTIONAL )
2 ts WORCESTERSHIRE SAUCE
1 ts PREPARED MUSTARD
1/4 ts GARLIC POWDER
1/4 ts PEPPER
COMBINE MARINATE INGREDIENTS IN A GLASS MEASURING CUP. WHISK UNTIL
BLENDED. POUR OVER MEAT AND COVER FOR AND REFRIGERATE FOR ABOUT 6
HOURS. GOOD FOR CHUCK ROAST, FLANK STEAK OR ETC.
