Title: BBQ SAUCE 2
Categories: Barbecue, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Brown sugar
1/4 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 c  Tomato sauce
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Paprika
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes; to taste
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1/4 c  Ketchup
Combine everything and simmer for 15 min.  Refrigerate overnight, if
possible. Serve on side with ribs.
