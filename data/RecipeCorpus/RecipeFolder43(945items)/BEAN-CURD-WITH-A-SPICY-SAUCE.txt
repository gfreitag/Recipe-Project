Title: BEAN CURD WITH A SPICY SAUCE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
2 ts Cornstarch
1/2 c  Stock
1 ts Chili paste with garlic
2 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Vegetable oil
Salt to taste
1/2 ts Sugar
MMMMM-------------------------BEAN CURD------------------------------
2 tb Oil
3    Garlic cloves
1 ts Fresh ginger
3    Scallions
1 lb Bean curd
- cut into 1-inch cubes
Whisk together the sauce ingredients & set aside. In a wok, saute
garlic & ginger.  Add scallions, followed by the tofu. Cook for a few
minutes. Pour suace over the sauted ingredients.  Mix gently & simmer
carefully. Let the sauce thicken, stirring gently every now & then.
Serve over rice.
