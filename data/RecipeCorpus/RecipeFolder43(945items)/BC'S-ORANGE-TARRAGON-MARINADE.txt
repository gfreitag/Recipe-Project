Title: BC'S ORANGE-TARRAGON MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 c  White wine vinegar
2 tb Chopped shallots
1 ts Dried tarragon leaves
2 ts Grated orange peel
1/2 ts Salt
Mix all ingredients in shallow nonmetal dish or resealable plastic
bag. Makes about 1 1/4 cups
