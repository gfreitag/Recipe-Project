Title: BECHAMEL OR VELOUTE SAUCE
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
4 tb Butter
5 tb Flour
2 3/4 c  Hot milk or chicken broth
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper
1/4 c  Light cream
3/4 c  Grated Swiss cheese or
Fontina
Heat butter and add flour. Cook slowly for a minute or two. Add the
hot liquid and bring to a boil, stirring continuously. Season to
taste with salt and pepper; beat in cream. Off heat, add cheese and
adjust the seasoning.

Yield: 3 cups of sauce
