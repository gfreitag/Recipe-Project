Title: BC'S SPICY MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
4 md Green onions, sliced
2    Jalapeno chilies, seeded
And chopped
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1/4 c  Honey
2 tb Chopped fresh thyme leaves
Or 2 t dried thyme leaves
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
Place all ingredients in food processor or blender. Cover and process
about 20 seconds or until smooth. Makes about 1 cup.
