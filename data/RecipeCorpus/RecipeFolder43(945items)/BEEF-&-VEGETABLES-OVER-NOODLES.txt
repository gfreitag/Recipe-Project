Title: BEEF & VEGETABLES OVER NOODLES
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Vegetables, Budget
Yield: 4 Servings
12 oz Round steak,1/2" thick strip
1 tb Olive oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Broccoli,flowerets & stems
1/2 lb Mushrooms,sliced
1    Onion,small,sliced
4 tb Beef broth
3 tb Reduced-fat sour cream
12 oz Wide egg noodles,cooked
Brown meat in oil in large nonstick skillet, 2-3 minutes. Add salt and
pepper. Remove meat. Add broccoli, mushrooms and onion to skillet. Add
broth as needed to prevent sticking; cook, stirring, 2-3 minutes. Add
beef; heat through. Remove from heat. Stir in sour cream. Serve over
noodles.
