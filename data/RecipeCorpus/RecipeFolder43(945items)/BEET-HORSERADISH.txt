Title: BEET HORSERADISH
Categories: Ethnic, Relishes
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Redbeets, boiled
1 ts Salt
2 oz Horseradish, fresh
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Vinegar
1 ds Pepper
1 ts Sugar
Grate beets and horseradish. Combine vinegar, sugar, salt,
pepper and water. Bring to a boil. Pour over grated beets and
horseradish. Mix thoroughly. Store in jar in refrigerator.
