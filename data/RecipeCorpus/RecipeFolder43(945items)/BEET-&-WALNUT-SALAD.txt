Title: BEET & WALNUT SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Salad
3    Beets; cooked and peeled
2    Tart apples; peeled & cored
1/2 c  Feta or goat cheese
1/2 c  Walnuts
1/3 c  Thinly sliced scallions
1/3 c  Basic vinaigrette dressing
Cube the beets and the apples and mix together in a bowl wiht the
cheese, the walnuts and the scallions. Drizzle dressing over and stir
to coat. Serve on a bed of butterhead lettuce.

From: The Cook's Garden catalog, Spring/Summer 1993 (page 88)
