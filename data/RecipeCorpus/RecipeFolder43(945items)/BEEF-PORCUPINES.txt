Title: BEEF PORCUPINES
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Raw rice
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Oil
2 cn 8 oz tomato sauce
1 c  Water
Mix beef rice onions and seasonings. Form small balls. Fry in oil
turning frequently until light brown, but not crusty on all sides.
Add tomato sauce and water. Mix well. Cover and simmer 45 minutes.
