Title: BEEF TACO SALAD
Categories: Tex-mex
Yield: 2 Servings
2 c  Ground beef
1    Tomato; fresh,chop
1 sm Onion; diced
1    Garlic clove
1 ds Salt
2    Chili peppers; diced
1 ds Black pepper
Lettuce; shredded
Cheddar cheese, shredded
Recipe by: Jo Anne Merrill, recipe from Del Mar Ca Brown meat in
skillet over medium heat; drain well. Put tomato, onion, garlic,
salt, black pepper and chili peppers in blender; chop fine but do not
puree. Use chili peppers of your choice, hot or mild. Add mixture to
browned meat, simmer until all liquid is absorbed; cool. Place meat
mixture on shredded lettuce and add cheese to garnish.
