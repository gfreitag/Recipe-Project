Title: BEEF SHANK SOUP
Categories: Soups
1 lb beef shanks
Yield: 7 Servings
8 c  water
1 ea onion -- chopped
1/2 c  celery -- chopped
1 ea carrot -- chopped
1 1/2 ts salt
2 TB parsley -- finely chopped
1 ea bay leaf
4    whole peppercorns
1    clove garlic -- cut in half
1/4 ts dried thyme
cheesecloth - about 4"
square
:
:

In slow-cooking pot, combine beef with water, onion, celery, carrot,
salt and parsley. Place bay leaf, peppercorns, thyme and garlic in
center of cheesecloth.  Pull up on each corner, forming a bag and
tie. Drop in pot with meat. Cover and cook on low 6 to 8 hours, or
until meat is tender. Remove spice bag. Ladel soup into bowls.
