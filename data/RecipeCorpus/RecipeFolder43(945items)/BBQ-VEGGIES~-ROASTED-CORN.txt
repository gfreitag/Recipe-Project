Title: BBQ VEGGIES~ ROASTED CORN
Categories: Sides
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
2 tb Chives  -- finely chopped
2 tb Parsley  -- chopped
Salt and pepper
8    Ears corn
prepare corn by peeling back the husk( do not remove) and removing the
silk.  If  the husks feel dry, soak the whole ears in water for 15-30
minutes to replenish the moisture. Melt butter and combine with
chives and parsley.  brush corn with butter mixture; salt and
pepper/. recover the corn with the husks.  roast husks over hot
coals, turning frequently for 30 min
