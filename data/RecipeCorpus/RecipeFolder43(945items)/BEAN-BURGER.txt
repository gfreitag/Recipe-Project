Title: BEAN BURGER
Categories: Meats
Yield: 2 Servings
2 lb Ground beef
1 md Onion
1/2 c  Catsup
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 cn Kidney beans
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Chili powder
Salt and pepper
Brown ground beef and onion.  Add catsup and Worcestershire sauce.
Simmer for 20 minutes.  Add kidney beans, garlic powder, chili powder
and salt and pepper to taste.  Simmer for 30 minutes.  Serve on
toasted bun and tip with cheese. Randy Rigg
