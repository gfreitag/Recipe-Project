Title: BEANS~ LEMON PEPPER GREEN BEANS
Categories: Sides
Yield: 1 Servings
****  NONE
Cook and drain 1 lb of fresh or drain 1 lb of fresh or frozen whole
green Beanws.  Saute 1/4 cup of slivered almonds in 2 tbsp of butter
until Lightly browned. Add 2 tsp of lemon and pepper seasoning salt.
Toss Almonds with the green beans.
