Title: BC'S GINGER-LIME MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Lime juice
2 tb Olive or vegetable oil
1 ts Finely chopped gingerroot
1/4 ts Salt
Dash of ground red pepper
-(cayenne)
1    Clove garlic, crushed
Mix all ingredients. Makes about 1/3 cup
