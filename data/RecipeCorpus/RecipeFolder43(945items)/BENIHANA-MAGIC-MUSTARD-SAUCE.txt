Title: BENIHANA MAGIC MUSTARD SAUCE
Categories: Chinese, Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Powdered mustard
2 tb Hot water
1/2 c  Soy sauce or tamari sauce
2 tb Sesame seeds, toasted
1    Garlic clove
In a small bowl, blend mustard and water into a paste. Pour paste into
blender container; add remaining ingredients and process about 1
minute or until smooth. Serving suggestion for Sauces: Use as a
dipping sauce for chicken or steak, or as a dressing for bean or
alfalfa sprout salad.
