Title: BAYERISCHER WURSTSALAT (BARVARIAN SAUSAGE SAL
Categories: German, Salads, Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 lb Knockwurst; Cooked / Cooled
2    Pickles; Small
1    Onion; Medium
3 tb Vinegar
1 tb Mustard; Prepared *
2 tb Vegetable Oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Sugar
1 tb Capers
1 tb Parsley; Chopped
*  Mustard must be the strong Djon or Gulden Type.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++

Cut the knockwurst into small cubes.  Mince the pickles and onion. Mix
together the vinegar, mustard and oil. Add salt, pepper, paprika and
sugar. Adjust seasonings if desired. Add the capers; mix well. Stir
in the chopped knockwurst, pickles, and onions. Just before serving,
garnish with chopped parsley.
