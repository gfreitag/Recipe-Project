Title: BEEF RICE DISH
Categories: Beef, Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Rice
1 cn Beef Consomme' Cambell
1 md Onion
1/4 c  Butter or Margarine
1 sm Jar sliced Mushrooms
Saute onion in butter until tender.  Mix together with all other
ingredients in casserole, cover tight. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
