Title: BENIHANA'S GINGER SAUCE
Categories: Spices, Sauces, Oriental
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Vinegar
1    Small onion, sliced
1    Small piece of ginger root
Place all ingredients in an electric blender and set at high speed.
Mix for 2 minutes or until ginger and onions are finely chopped.
*Note* If fresh ginger is not available, substitute 1/8 teaspoon
powdered ginger.
