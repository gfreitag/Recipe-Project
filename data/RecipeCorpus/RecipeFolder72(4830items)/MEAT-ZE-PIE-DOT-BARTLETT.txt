Title: MEAT-ZE-PIE DOT BARTLETT
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Party
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
2/3 c  Evaporated milk; 6 oz
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
1/2 ts Garlic powder; salt
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Tomato paste; or katsup
2 oz Mushrooms;sliced opt.
1 c  Cheddar cheese;sharp shred
1/4 ts Oregano
2 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
1/2 pk Pepperoni; opt
DIRECTIONS ~------------------------------------------------------
~--- Combine ground meat with milk, bread crumbs, and garlic. Press
into shape of pie plate (9 in) Spread tomato paste over meat .
(optional, place pepperoni over this) Top with cheddar cheese, (or
mottzerella) , sprinkle with oregano, then parmesan cheese. bake at
375    for about 25 min.
