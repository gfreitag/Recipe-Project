Title: MASHED POTATOES (ELAINE)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
4 lg Potaotes, peeled and diced
1 c  Low-fat soy milk
1/4 ts Onion powder
steam potatoes over boiling water for 15 min.  place in a bowl; add
the soy milk and onion powder.  mash until smooth.
