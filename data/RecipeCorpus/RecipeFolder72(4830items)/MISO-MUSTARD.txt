Title: MISO MUSTARD
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 2 Oz.
1 ts EDEN Hot Mustard
1/4 c  EDEN OrganiC Miso
--(any variety)
Mix miso and mustard until smooth.  Great on sandwiches or with raw or
steamed vegetables.
