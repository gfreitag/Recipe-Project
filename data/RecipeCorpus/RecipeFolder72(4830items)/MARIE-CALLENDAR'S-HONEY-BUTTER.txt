Title: MARIE CALLENDAR'S HONEY BUTTER
Categories: Spreads, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
Van Geffen VGHC42A
1/4 lb Butter
1/2 c  Honey
6 sl Bacon; cook crisp --
Crumble
Beat butter in a 1-1/2 qt bowl with electric mixer, high speed, till
light and fluffy. Gradually on reduced speed, beat in honey and
bacon, beating till thoroughly combined and smooth. Keep refrigerated
in covered container to serve within a week. Source: Gloria Pitzer's
Secret Fast Food Recipes
