Title: MEAT CROQUETTES
Categories: Beef
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Cooked ground meat
Salt and pepper
2 tb Water
1 c  Thick white sauce
Celery salt
1    Egg, slightly beaten
Combine meat and white sauce.  Season to taste.  Mix thoroughly.
Cool. Form in balls, cones, or cylinders. Roll in crumbs. Fry in deep
fat (385F) until brown. Drain on crumpled absorbent paper. 8 servings.
