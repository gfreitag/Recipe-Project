Title: NEITHER CAKE NOR CANDY (BROWNIES)
Categories: Penndutch, Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 ea Chocolate, unsweetened, squa
2 tb Butter
1 c  Sugar
2 ea Egg, well beaten
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Raisins, chopped
1/2 c  Nuts, chopped
Melt chocolate and butter together over hot water. Add the sugar and
vanilla; mix well. Then add the well beaten eggs. Sift the flour and
stir in with the nuts and raisins. Bake in a well-greased shallow pan
at 350-F for 20 minutes. When cool, cut in squares. Source:
Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old Recipes, Culinary Arts Press,
1936.
