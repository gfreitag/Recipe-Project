Title: NORTHWEST SALMON HAZELNUT & JUNIPER BERRY SAU
Categories: Main dish, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Salmon steaks
1/4 c  Oregon hazelnuts; broken
3    Juniper berries; crushed
1/2 c  Brandy
1/2 c  Cream
SautÂ‚ salmon steaks and remove from pan. Add hazelnuts and Juniper
berries. Deglaze with brandy. When mixture is reduced and alcohol is
gone, add cream and cook until thickened. Salt to taste. Pour over
warm salmon steaks.

NOTE: You can add broken Oregon hazelnuts to any quick sauce to
achieve a nutty flavor. (It must be a quick sauce to maintain the
crunch.)
