Title: MOMS FILLED COOKIES  *** JO
Categories: Cookies, Desserts, American
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Butter or substitute
1 c  Sugar & cream
1 ea Egg beaten well
1/2 c  Sweet milk
2 ts Cream of tartar
1 ts Soda
3 1/4 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Chopped dates
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
1 tb Flour
1/2 cup sweet milk & dissolve 2 teas of cream of tartar about 3 1/4
c.flour ,1 teas. of soda,1/4 teas salt & sift together. 1 teas
vanilla . roll thin Place cookie on cookie sheet then put 1 teas of
filling on each and another cookie over filling and press edge.(use
enough flour only to make a soft dough)  1 cup of chopped dates 1/2
cup of sugar, 1/2 cup of water, 1 tblsp of flour . cook over slow
heat until thick.. Bake in 375 degree oven about 10 min. . I
sometimes use 1/2 raisins and 1/2 dates.
