Title: MEAT & POULTRY SEASONING
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Ground black pepper
2 tb Onion powder
2 tb Paprika
2 tb Salt
1 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Ground thyme
Sprinkle on steaks, chops and chicken before grilling or broiling.
While you're at it, keep these in mind for gifts. Just put them in a
pretty jar, and attach a few recipe suggestions with a ribbon. Makes
a scant
1/2    cup.
