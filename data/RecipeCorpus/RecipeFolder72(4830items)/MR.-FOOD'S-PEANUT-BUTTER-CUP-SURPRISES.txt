Title: MR. FOOD'S PEANUT BUTTER CUP SURPRISES
Categories: Desserts, Cupcakes
Yield: 24 Servings
1 3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 c  Milk
1/3 c  Vegetable shortening
1/3 c  Peanut butter
1 ts Vanilla extract
2    Eggs
24    Miniature milk chocolate
- covered peanut butter
- cups, unwrapped
======================================================= ======
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Line 24 medium-sized muffin tins
with paper baking cups. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients
except the peanut butter cups. With an electric beater, blend at low
speed until moistened. Then beat at medium speed fo Note: Serve warm
or cool, plain, or "fancied up" with sprinkles or frosting!
