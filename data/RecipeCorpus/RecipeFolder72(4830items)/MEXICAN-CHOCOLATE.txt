Title: MEXICAN CHOCOLATE
Categories: Desserts, Candies, Chocolate
Yield: 4 Servings
-LISA HLAVATY   (FDGN81A)
2 oz Squares chocolate; grated
1/2 c  -boiling water
2 c  Milk
3 tb Sugar
1 c  Heavy cream
1 ts Cinnamon
1    Egg; beaten slightly
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Vanilla
Boil chocolate and water 5 minutes; add remaining ingredients. Cook in
double boiler 1 hour, beating at intervals.
