Title: MULTI BLEND FRUIT COBBLER
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 1/2 tb Butter
1 1/2 c  AM Multi Blend Flour
1 ts Non-alum baking powder
1/2 c  Honey
1    Egg, beaten or egg replacer
1 c  Milk or milk substitute
2 c  Fruit
-- fresh or canned, drained
Melt butter in a 9" x 9" pan.  Mix the Multi Blend flour, baking
powder, honey, egg and milk.  Pour mixture into butter. Add the fruit
on top of batter. Bake 30 minutes at 400 F.
