Title: MR. FOOD'S ORANGE BURST FRUIT DIP
Categories: Sauces, Dips
Yield: 99 Servings
About 2 cups
16 oz Sour cream
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Fresh orange juice
1 tb Orange zest
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a medium-sized bowl, combine all the
ingredients and whisk until smooth and creamy. Store in an airtight
container and chill for at least
30    minutes before serving.
