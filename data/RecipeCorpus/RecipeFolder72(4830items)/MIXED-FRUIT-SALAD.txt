Title: MIXED FRUIT SALAD
Categories: Diabetic, Low-cal, Salads, Fruits, Apples
Yield: 16 Servings
1 c  Apples;chopped
1 c  Bananas;sliced
8 oz Pineapple chunks;unsweetened
--drained
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Celery;chopped
1/3 c  Mayo;reduced calories
1 tb Lemon juice
16    Lettuce leaves;optional
Combine all ingredients except lettuce leaves. Toss gently to coat,
and chill 1 to 2 hours. Serve on lettuce leaves, if desired.

Yield : 16 - 1/4 cup servings. Each serving may be exchanged for 1
Fruit.
