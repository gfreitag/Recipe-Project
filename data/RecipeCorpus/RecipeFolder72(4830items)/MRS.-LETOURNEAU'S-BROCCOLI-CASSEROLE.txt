Title: MRS. LETOURNEAU'S BROCCOLI CASSEROLE
Categories: Cheese, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
2    10 oz pk frozen chopped
-broccoli
2 c  Cooked rice
2 c  Shredded cheddar cheese --
Sharp
4    Eggs -- slightly beaten
4 tb Margarine
1 c  Milk
4 tb Onion -- chopped
2 ts Salt
Cook broccoli according to package directions; drain. Combine
remaining ingredients and cook over moderate heat until slightly
thickened. Place broccoli in greased shallow 1 quart casserole. Pour
sauce over broccoli. Bake at 350F for 20-25 minutes or until set.
