Title: MUSHROOM SAUCE (USDA)
Categories: Freezes wel, Usda
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Canned mushrooms -- sliced
3 tb Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Milk
Melt butter.  Brown mushrooms lightly in melted butter. Stir in flour
and salt.  Add milk slowly. Cook until thickened, stirring constantly.

~ - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Serving Ideas : Serve over Ham Turnovers or Meatballs (separate
recipes)

NOTES : This recipe is for 6 servings (about 1/4 cup each) to
accompany Ham Turnovers or Meatballs, two freeze-ahead meals. The
sauce is made at the time of serving. You will want to have the
ingredients on hand when you are ready to use the frozen food.
