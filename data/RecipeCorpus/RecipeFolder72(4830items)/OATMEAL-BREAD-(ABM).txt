Title: OATMEAL BREAD (ABM)
Categories: Digest, Oct., Abm, Lacto
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------1 1/2 LB LOAF----------------------------
3/4 c  Rolled oats
1 1/4 c  Skim milk
(can use water, but I
Havent tried that)
3 c  Bread flour
3 tb Molasses
1 1/2 tb Sugar
2 ts Yeast
Place all ingredients in bread pan, set crust to Light setting, and
begin.

(adapted from _Bread machine magic_, by Linda Rehberg and Lois Conway
