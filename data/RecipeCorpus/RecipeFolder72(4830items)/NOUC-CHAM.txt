Title: NOUC CHAM
Categories: New, Text, Import
2    cloves garlic, -- finely
chopped
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1    fresh hot chili pepper (
about 1 1/2-inch  -- finely
cho
:
:
1 TB plus 1 teaspoon sugar
1/2    lime
1/4 c  fish sauce
5 TB water

In a small bowl place garlic and chili. Squeeze in the lime and using
a small knife, remove some of the pulp. Add the fish sauce, and
water. Mix well, place in a nice bowl, and set aside.

Yield: about 3/4 cup
