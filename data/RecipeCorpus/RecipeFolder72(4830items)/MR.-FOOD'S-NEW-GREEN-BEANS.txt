Title: MR. FOOD'S NEW GREEN BEANS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Milk
10 3/4 oz Condensed cream of
- mushroom soup
1 ts Soy sauce
1 ds Black pepper
4 c  Cut fresh green beans,
- cooked
1 cn 2.8 ounces French-fried
- onions
======================================================= =======
Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. In a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish that
has been coated with nonstick vegetable spray, combine the milk,
soup, soy sauce, and pepper. Stir in the cooked green beans and half
the onions. Bake for 25 minutes or until hot; stir. Note: You can
make a lighter version of these delicious beans by using reduced-fat
cream of mushroom soup and reducing the amount of milk to 1/4 cup.
