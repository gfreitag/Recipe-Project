Title: OAT BRITTLE
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flaked oat cereal, uncooked
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Baking soda
2 tb Melted butter
Caramelize sugar.  Stir constantly.  Remove from fire immediately.
Stir in the oat cereal quickly.  Add salt, baking soda, and butter.
Pour onto well-buttered baking sheet. Stretch and pull by means of
spatulas into a very thin sheet. The Household Searchlight
