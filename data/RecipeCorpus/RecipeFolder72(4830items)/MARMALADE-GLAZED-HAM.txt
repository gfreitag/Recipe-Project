Title: MARMALADE-GLAZED HAM
Categories: Main dish, White wine
Yield: 15 Servings
5 lb Fully-cooked boneless ham
1/3 c  Sweet white wine
1/8 ts Ground cloves
1/2 c  Orange marmalade
1 tb Prepared mustard
Heat oven to 325'. Place ham fat side up on rack in open shallow
roasting pan. Roast uncovered 1 hour. Remove from oven.
Score fat surface of ham lightly into uniform diamond shapes. Mix
remaining ingredients; spoon over scored ham. Return to oven. Roast
40 with almonds. Bake uncovered until bubbly, about 25 minutes.
