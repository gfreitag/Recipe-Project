Title: MUSTARD BASED CENTRAL SOUTH CAROLINA BBQ SAUC
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Batch
3 tb Peanut Oil
2 ea Minced Garlic Cloves
1 ea Minced Onion
1/2 c  Catsup
1/3 c  Apple Cider Vinegar
2 tb Lemon Juice
2 tb Honey
1 tb Brown Sugar
2 ts Dry Mustard
1 ts Ginger
1 pn Salt
Combine ingredients and set aside.  Grill meat (do not add anything).
During the last 4-10 minutes baste with this sauce. Boil the remaining
sauce and spoon 2 T's onto each plate, adding the meat portion.
