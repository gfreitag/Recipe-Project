Title: MINCED LAMB FONDUE
Categories: Fondue, Lamb
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/4 lb Minced lean lamb
3    Spring onions -- finely
Chopped
2 oz Fresh bread crumbs
2 tb Parsley -- fresh fine
Chopped
Salt and pepper
MMMMM-----------------------MUSHROOM SAUCE----------------------------
6 oz Mushroom -- finely chopped
6 ts Plain flour
10    Fluid ounces  milk
1 tb Dry sherry
Put all the ingredients for the lamb balls into a bowl. Season and mix
together. With wetted hands, shape mixture into 20-24 balls, the size
of a walnut and place on a serving plate.

To make mushroom sauce, melt butter in a saucepan, add mushrooms and
cook gently for 5 minutes. Stir in flour, then gradually add milk and
bring to the boil, stirring. Simmer for a further 5 minutes, then
season and add sherry. Serve warm with lamb balls cooked in the hot
oil.
