Title: O'HENRY BARS
Categories: Desserts, Cookies
Yield: 36 Servings
KIM ROMERO KKPX21B-----
1 c  Butter -- soft
1/3 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
4 c  Quick oats
TOPPING-----
1 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  Peanut Butter
Preheat oven to 300~. Mix all ingredients, except chocolate chips and
peanut butter, to a crumble. Pat into a 11x13" lightly greased pan.
Bake no more than 12 minutes. Will be soft and runny when taken out
of oven, but will set when cooling. TOPPING-Melt chocolate chips and
peanut butter together and then spread evenly on top. Chill until
chocolate is set.
