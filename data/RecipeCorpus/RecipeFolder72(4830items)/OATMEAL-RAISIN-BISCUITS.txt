Title: OATMEAL RAISIN BISCUITS
Categories: Bread, Misc
Yield: 6 Servings
1 3/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Oatmeal
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda and 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Raisins
3/4 c  Buttermilk
Mix first 5 ingredients in bowl and cut in the butter. Add the
raisins and buttermilk and stir until blended, turn out and knead 10
times. Roll out and cut and place on sheet 1" apart. Bake at 425 for
12-15 min.
