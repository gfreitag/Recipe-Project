Title: MIDDLE EASTERN SALAD
Categories: Salads, S.powter, Vegetables, Low cal/fat, Diabetic
Yield: 1 -4 serving
2 c  Roma tomatoes, finely
-chopped
2 c  Cucumbers, finely chopped
1/2 c  Red onion, finely chopped
1/3 c  Baby dill pickles, finely
-chopped
Toss all ingredients together and chill. Delicious! This one must be
in your refrigerator at all time. Serves 1-4 Calories: 25 (1 cup)
Fat: 0 gm 0% fat

From: Stop the Insanity by Susan Powter
