Title: NO GUILT POTATO SALAD
Categories: Low-cal, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Cooked unpared red potatoes
-cubed
1/4 c  Chopped celery
1/2 ts Celery seed
1/4 c  Chopped scallions
1/2 ts Seasoning salt
3 tb Chopped fresh dill
1/4 c  Low cal ranch dressing
Pepper to taste
1. Using a rubber scraper, in a medium bowl, combine all ingredients
stirring to coat. Refrigerate.
