Title: MISSIIAGAN-PAKWEJIGAN (SUNFLOWER BANNOCK)
Categories: Bakery, Native, Mohawk, Breads
Yield: 18 Sm. loaves
3 1/4 c  Sunflower seeds
3 1/4 c  Water
2 1/2 ts Salt
6 tb Corn flour
2/3 c  Corn oil
Put the sunflower seeds, water & salt into a pot, cover & let simmer
for 1 1/2 hours.  When well cookked, crush the seeds to amke a paste.
Add the corn flour, 1 tablespoon at a time to thicken. Work with your
hands; cool a little. Make small, flat pancakes of approximately 5"
diameter. Heat oil & fry both sides, adding more oil if necessary.
Drain well & eat.
