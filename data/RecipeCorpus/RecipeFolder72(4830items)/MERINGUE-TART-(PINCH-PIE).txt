Title: MERINGUE TART (PINCH PIE)
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
3    Egg whites
1/2 ts Baking Powder
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Vinegar
1 ts Water
1 c  Sugar
Combine all ingredients in bowl of mixer except sugar. Beat at high
speed until stiff, then add the sugar 1 tblsp. at a time. Shape on
greased cookie sheet like nests and bake in 250 oven for one hour or
longer.Serve filled with ice cream and fruit or topping of your
choice.
