Title: MRS. LYON'S LEMON BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 8 Servings
6 tb Margarine
1 c  White sugar
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Milk
1    Grated rind of lemon
1/2 c  Nuts
Cream sugar and margarine, add beaten eggs , add milk alternately
with dry ingredients. Add the lemon rind and walnuts. Bake in loaf
pan 1 hour in 350 oven. When baked pour over bread while still in pan
the juice of lemon mixed with 1/2 cup of sugar, let cool.
