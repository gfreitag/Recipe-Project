Title: OATMEAL SESAME BREAD
Categories: Oat, Dg, Nuts, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Water
1 tb Applesauce
1 tb Brown Sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon,Ground
2/3 ts Salt
2 1/2 tb Sesame Seeds,Dried & Hulled
1/3 c  Oats
1/3 c  Whole-Wheat Flour
1 1/3 c  Bread Flour
1 ts Yeast
Put in pan. Push Start. D. German I - Small - Page 61
