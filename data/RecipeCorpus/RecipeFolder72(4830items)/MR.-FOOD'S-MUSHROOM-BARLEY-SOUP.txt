Title: MR. FOOD'S MUSHROOM BARLEY SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
4 lg Onions, chopped
6 lg Carrots, peeled and sliced
1 lb Mushrooms, thinly sliced
1 lb Stew beef, cut into
- small cubes
8 c  Beef broth (made from a
- soup base or a mix)
1 c  Medium barley
1/2 ts Poon salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
=======================================================
============== ==== == Place all the ingredients in a soup pot and
bring to a boil, stirring occasionally. Reduce the heat and simmer,
partially covered, for about 1 hour. NOTE: If you'd like, add 1 or 2
shots of Irish whiskey with the other ingredients for added flavor.
It'll make it really Irish! In addition to the base recipe
ingredients, you might want to add some cut-up hot dogs and a

rosemary for Italian-style soup.
