Title: MISS CORBETT'S FAST OATMEAL CANDY
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Cocoa
1 pn Salt
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Crunchy peanut butter
3 c  Quick-cooking or instant
-oatmeal, uncooked
Miss Corbett's Fast Oatmeal Candy

Mix  sugar,  cocoa,  milk and butter and put  on medium heat. Bring
to a boil.  Remove from heat and cool one minute. Add vanilla, salt,
peanut butter, oatmeal. Stir well. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto waxed
paper. Let cool.

*Ellen*
