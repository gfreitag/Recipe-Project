Title: MCDANIEL CHICKEN CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Rice, long-grained
1 1/2 c  Mushrooms, freshly sliced
3 lb Chicken pieces or
-2 1/2 to 3 lb broiler-fryer
-cut up
10 3/4 oz Cream of onion soup, conden.
-or 10 1/2 oz can condensed
-French onion soup
10 3/4 oz Cream of mushroom soup-
-condensed
1 1/4 c  Water
1 pk Onion-soup mix, regular
1.  In a 13 x 9 x 2-inch baking dish or pan, layer the rice and
mushrooms,
then the chicken pieces.  In a bowl, combine the condensed cream
of
onion soup, mushroom soup, water, and dry onion-soup mix. Pour
mixture
over the chicken.  Cover with foil.

2.  Bake in a 375 deg. oven for 1 3/4 to 2 hours, or till the rice is
tender and the chicken is done.
