Title: MOM'S PUMPKIN COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Holiday
Yield: 10 Dozen
3 c  Pumpkin
1 c  Shortening
2 c  White sugar
4 c  Flour
2 ts Soda
2 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
2 ts Vanilla
2    Eggs
2 ts Cinnamon
1 lg Bag chocolate chips
1    Jar marichino cherries
-chopped
Cream together pumpkin, shortening, white sugar. Then add and mix
flour, soda, salt, baking powder. Then add and mix vanilla, eggs,
cinnamon. Then add and mix chocolate chips, cherries, nuts. Drop by
spoonful on greased cooie sheets. 12 - 14 minutes at 375~
