Title: MOCK RASPBERRY JAM
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
5 c  Green tomatoes
4 c  Sugar
6 oz Raspberry jello (2 pkgs)
In blender or processor, process green tomatoes; add sugar. Boil 20
minutes. Skim. Add jello, stir.  Pour into sterilized jars. It must
be kept in the refrigerator! Can also be frozen.
