Title: MIXED GREEN SALAD WITH DIJON-TARRAGON DRESSIN
Categories: Low-fat/low, Salad dress, Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
1    Head romaine lettuce
2    Heads boston lettuce
2 c  Escarole -- or chicory
Greens
4 tb Lowfat mayonnaise
4 tb Nonfat yogurt -- plain
4 tb Dijon mustard
4 tb Tarragon
Rinse greens, pat dry and tear into bite-size pieces. Place in a large
bowl. In a separate bowl, whick together remaining ingred. dor
dressing. Just before serving, pour dressing over greens and toss
until evenly coated.
