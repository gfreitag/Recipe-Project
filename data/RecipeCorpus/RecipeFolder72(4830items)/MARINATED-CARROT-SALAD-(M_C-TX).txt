Title: MARINATED CARROT SALAD (M_C-TX)
Categories: Salads
Yield: 8 Servings
2 pk Carrots
1 lg Onion
1 lg Bell pepper
1 cn Concentrated tomato soup
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Wesson oil
Cube, salt and cook carrots until tender. Drain and add onion and bell
pepper. Bring to a boil: tomato soup, sugar, vinegar and Wesson oil.
Pour this mixture over the carrots, onions and bell peppers.
Refrigerate. This will keep for several days.
