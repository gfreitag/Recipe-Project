Title: MEATBALLS IN CHIPOTLE SAUCE
Categories: Mexican, Meats, Beef, Pork, Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
Chipotle Sauce; *
1 lb Ground Beef
1 lb Ground Pork
2    Eggs; Large
1/2 c  Dry Bread Crumbs
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Onion; Finely Chopped
2 tb Fresh Cilantro; Snipped
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
*  See Sowest 2 for recipe. Prepare Chipotle Sauce and set aside. Mix
remaining ingredients together and shape into 1 1/2-inch meatballs.
Heat sauce and meatballs to boiling; then reduce the heat. Cover and
simmer until meatballs are done, about 20 minutes. Serve with hot
cooked rice or noodles, if desired.
