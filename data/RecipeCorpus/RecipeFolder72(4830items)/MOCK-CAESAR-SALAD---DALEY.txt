Title: MOCK CAESAR SALAD - DALEY
Categories: Italian, Salads, Cheese, Low-fat/low, Garlic
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Cloves garlic -- peeled and
Minced
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Lemon juice
3 c  Belgian endive
6 c  Romaine lettuce -- torn
1/8 ts Black pepper -- freshly
Cracked
1 tb Parmesan cheese -- grated
1 1/3 c  Tomato -- chopped
Put the garlic, soy sauce, and lemon juice in a salad bowl and whisk
thoroughly.  Add the endive and Romaine lettuce. Toss to coat.
Sprinkle the black pepper and parmesan cheese on top.

Garnish with chopped tomato.

: Rosie Daley from "In the Kitchen with Rosie"
