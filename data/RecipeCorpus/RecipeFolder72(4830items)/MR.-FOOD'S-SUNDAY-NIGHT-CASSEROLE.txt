Title: MR. FOOD'S SUNDAY NIGHT CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
3/4 c  Uncooked elbow macaroni
1 lb Lean ground beef
1/2 c  Sliced celery
1/2 c  Diced onion
2 tb Honey
28 oz Whole peeled tomatoes,
- broken up (undrained)
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
