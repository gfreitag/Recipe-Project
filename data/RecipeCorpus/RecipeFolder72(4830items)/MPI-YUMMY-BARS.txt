Title: MPI YUMMY BARS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 24 Servings
1 1/3 c  FLOUR
1 1/3 c  QUICK COOKING OATS
1 c  BROWN SUGAR
1/2 ts BAKING SODA
1/4 ts SALT
1 c  BUTTER, MELTED
12 oz CHOCOLATE CHIPS
12 oz JAR OF CARAMEL TOPPING
3 tb FLOUR
1 c  CHOPPED NUTS, OPTIONAL
IN A LARGE BOWL COMBINE FLOUR, OATS, BROWN SUGAR, BAKING SODA, SALT
AND MELTED BUTTER. BLEND WELL TO FORM CRUMBLY MIXTURE. PRESS HALF THE
MIXTURE INTO BOTTOM OF A 9 X 13 PAN. BAKE AT 350 DEG F. FOR 10
MINUTES. REMOVE AND SPRINKLE WITH CHOCOLATE CHIPS AND NUTS. COMBINE
CARAMEL TOPPING AND 3 T FLOUR. POUR EVENLY OVER CHIPS. SPRINKLE WITH
THE REMAINING OAT MIXTURE AND BAKE FOR 20 TO 25 MINUTES. COOL
COMPETELY BEFORE CUTTING INTO BARS. BAKE AT 350 DEG F. FOR A TOTAL OF
30 TO 35 MINUTES. MAKES 24 BARS
