Title: NEWBURG CARROTS (M_C-TX)
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 5 Servings
4 c  Sliced carrots
1 1/2 c  Water
2 tb Butter
2 tb Flour
3/4 c  Chicken broth
1/2 c  Heavy cream
1 tb Green bell pepper
2 tb Onion, chopped
2 tb Parsley
Cook 15 minutes or until tender.  Drain if any water is left in pan.
Sauce: 2 tb butter, 2 tb flour. Melt butter and add flour. Add 3/4
cup chicken broth and 1/2 cup heavycream. Also add 1 tablespoon green
bell pepper, 2 tablespoons onion, chopped, and 2 tablespoons parsley.
Put carrots into the sauce and simmer 5 or 10 minutes.
