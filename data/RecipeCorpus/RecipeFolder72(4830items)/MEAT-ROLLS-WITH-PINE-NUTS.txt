Title: MEAT ROLLS WITH PINE NUTS
Categories: Ground meat, Nuts
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Pine nuts
1 tb Butter
1 lb Ground lamb -- or beef
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1 md Onion -- minced
Salt and pepper
8 oz Tomato sauce -- canned
Brown nuts in butter. Mix together the lamb, parsley, onion, salt and
pepper to taste. Shape into 4 rounds with hands; flatten and top each
round with 1 tbs pine nuts. Cover pine nuts by rolling up meat Place
in a shallow baking dish and pour tomato sauce over meat rolls. Bake
in a 350-degree oven for 30 minutes.
