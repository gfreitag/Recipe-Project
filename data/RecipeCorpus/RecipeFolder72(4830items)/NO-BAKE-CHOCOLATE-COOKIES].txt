Title: NO BAKE CHOCOLATE COOKIES]
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
3 c  Oatmeal
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Milk
4 tb Cocoa
1 ts Vanilla
Put oatmeal and peanut butter in large bowl. Mix rest of
ingredients in sauce pan and bring to a boil and boil 1 minute.
Remove from heat and add 1 tsp. vanilla, pour over oatmeal peanut
butter mixture, mix well and press in 8x8 pan or drop from spoon onto
wax paper. Cool.
