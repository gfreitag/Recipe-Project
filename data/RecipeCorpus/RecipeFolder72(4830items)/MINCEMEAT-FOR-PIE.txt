Title: MINCEMEAT FOR PIE
Categories: Meats
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/2 qt Ground beef
4 1/2 qt Apples, chopped
1 qt Water
1 1/2 lb Raisins
1 1/2 qt Maple syrup
10 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Pepper
2 ts Salt
9    Cloves
4    Ts nutmeg
Combine all ingredients.  Cook over very low heat until apples are
soft and mixture is very thick.  May be canned or frozen. Makes 12
pies.
