Title: MEUNIERE SAUCE 1
Categories: Sauces, Seafood, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 ts Salt
2 tb Lemon juice
Put in sauce pan and melt together-let sit until ready to cook. Cook
seafood in sauce.  DO NOT OVERCOOK.  For example, shrimp are done
when they change color to pink, about 2 to 4 minutes. Serve with
lemon wedges to garnish. Spoon excess sauce from pan over seafood.
Variation: Dredge in seafood flour, with a little salt mixed in,
prior to cooking.
