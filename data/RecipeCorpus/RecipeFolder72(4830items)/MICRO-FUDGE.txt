Title: MICRO FUDGE
Categories: Chocolate, Microwave, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Box Confectioner's Sugar
1/2 c  Cocoa (real)
1    Stick Margarine, softened
4 lg To 6 lg Marshmallows
1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
Mix sugar and cocoa in microwave-safe bowl.  Lay margarine and
marshmallows on top. Microwave at 2 minutes on high. Add vanilla and
(up to) 1/4 cup milk; "stir like hell." Chill on dish.
