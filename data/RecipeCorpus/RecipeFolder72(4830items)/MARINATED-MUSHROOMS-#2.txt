Title: MARINATED MUSHROOMS #2
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Fresh mushrooms
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Red wine vinegar
6 tb Sherry
4 tb Sugar
1 c  Finely minced onions
Mix all ingredients but the mushrooms in a saucepan and bring to a
boil. Pour over mushrooms that have been cleaned and halved or
quartered depending on size. Marinate and chill fo 24 hours before
serving. Small button mushrooms can be used whole.
