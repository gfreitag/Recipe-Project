Title: MR. FOOD'S TURKEY ROLL-UPS
Categories: Meats, Turkey
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Cornbread stuffing mix
1 c  Water
1/3 c  Butter, melted
1/2 c  Sweetened dried cranberries
8 3/4 oz Whole kernel corn, drained
12    Thick slices deli turkey
- breast (about 2 pounds)
12 oz Turkey gravy
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. In a
medium-sized bowl, combine the stuffing mix, water, butter,
cranberries, and corn; mix well. Place 1/4 cup of the stuffing
mixture at the smaller end of each slice of turkey and roll up
crepe-style. Place the turkey Note: Make sure to ask the deli to
slice the turkey thickly so that there are about 6 slices to the
pound.
