Title: MINI MEAT LOAF
Categories: Meats, Beef
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Saltine crackers
1 c  Cubed raw beef
2    1" pieces onion
2    Sprigs parsley
1    Egg
1 tb Ketchup
2    Dashes pepper
2 tb Ketchup
Place crackers in food processor bowl and cover unit.With hand on
cover process until finely crumbled.Add 1/2 cup meat,cover and
process 3 to 4 fast pulses.Remove cover and add remaining meat,onion
and parsley;cover and process 4 to 6 fast pulses.Remove cover and add
egg, 1 tbsp. ketchup and pepper.Cover and process 2 fast pulses.Pat
into 3" x 1 1/4" mini loaf pan.Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for
30 minutes.Add 2 tbsp. ketchup to top and continue to bake an
additional 20 minutes.Makes 1 to 2 servings.
