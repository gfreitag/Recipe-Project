Title: MELON WEDGES WITH HONEY-LIME DRESSING
Categories: Fruit, Salads/dres
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Honey
2 tb Lime juice
4 lg Honeydew melon -- wedges
1 c  Blueberries
4 sl Lime -- halved
In small bowl, blend honey with lime juice. To serve, fill melon
wedges with berries and drizzle with honey. Garnish with lime slices.
