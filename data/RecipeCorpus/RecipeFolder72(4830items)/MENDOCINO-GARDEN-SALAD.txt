Title: MENDOCINO GARDEN SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
8 c  Torn green leaf lettuce
1/2 md Cucumber; grooved and sliced
1 c  Shredded red cabbage
4    Green onions, thinly
-slivered lengthwise
1/2 c  Slivered sweet red or green
-bell pepper
MMMMM------------------GARLIC-RED WINE VINEGAR-----------------------
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 cl Garlic; minced or pressed
1/4 ts Dried marjoram
1/8 ts Salt
1 ds Seasoned pepper
2 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Salad oil
Recipe by: the California Culinary Academy Preparation Time: 0:10

1. In a salad bowl lightly mix lettuce, cucumber, and red cabbage.

2. Mix lightly with Garlic-Red Wine Vinegar Dressing.

3. Top with onions and red pepper.

Garlic-Red Wine Vinegar Dressing: In a medium bowl mix vinegar,
mustard, garlic, marjoram, salt, and pepper. Using a whisk or fork,
gradually mix in oil until well blended and slightly thickened.

Makes about 1/3 cup.
