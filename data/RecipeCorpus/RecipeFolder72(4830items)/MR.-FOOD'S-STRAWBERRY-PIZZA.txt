Title: MR. FOOD'S STRAWBERRY PIZZA
Categories: Casseroles, Pizza
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 c  Butter, softened
1 1/2 c  Confectioners sugar divided
8 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 ts Vanilla extract
8 oz Frozen whipped topping,
- thawed
1    Pint fresh\ strawberries,
- cleaned, hulled, and
- sliced
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 350 F. In a medium-sized
bowl, make a soft dough by mixing together the flour, butter, and 1/2
cup confectioners' sugar. Spread the dough onto a greased 12-inch
pizza pan and bake far 20 to 25 minutes or until golden; let cool.
