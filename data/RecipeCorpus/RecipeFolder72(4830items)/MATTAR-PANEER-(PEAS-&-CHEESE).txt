Title: MATTAR PANEER (PEAS & CHEESE)
Categories: Vegetables, Indian
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Onions
2    Tomatoes
4    Cloves garlic
1/2 pk Frozen peas
1    Cube ginger
1/4 ts Turmeric
To taste salt
To taste pepper
1/2 ts Garam Masala
2 c  Water
1. Cut paneer in 1" cubes and deep fry. 2. Make masala with onion,
garlic, ginger, and tomatoes. 3. Season and add turmeric 4. Add peas
and paneer
