Title: NORTH CAROLINA: WESTERN VI (SMOKED PORK)
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Ketchup
1 ea Small bottle Heinz 57 Sauce
1 tb Celery seed
1 c  Onion, finely chopped
2 ea Cloves garlic, minced
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 tb Dry mustard
-Red Pepper Flakes to taste
Carmelize butter and sugar until bubbly.  Add vinegar and whisk. Add
rest of ingredients and simmer until thickened.
