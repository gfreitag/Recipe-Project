Title: MOTHER'S MOCHA NUT BUTTER BALLS  (CHRISTMAS)
Categories: Cookies, Holidays
Yield: 1 Pan
1 c  Butter; softened
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla
2 ts Instant coffe powder
1/4 c  Cocoa
1 3/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Pecans; finely chopped
Oven 325~.

Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla until light.  Add next four
ingredients.  Mix well.  Add nuts.  Shape in 1" balls on ungreased
sheets.
Bake at 325~ for about 17 to 20 minutes.  Roll in powdered sugar
while warm.
Store in tin box between layers of wax paper.
