Title: OFFICIAL SAN FRANCISCO 49ERS PLAYOFF GUACAMOL
Categories: Mexican, Condiment, Ceideburg
Yield: 4 Servings
Stephen Ceideburg
4    Avocados, peeled and seeded
16 oz Cream cheese, softened
1 md Onion, finely chopped
1 md Tomato, finely chopped
1 ts Garlic salt
1/4 c  Fresh cilantro, finely
-chopped
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
Tortilla chips or sourdough
-bread
Mash avocados with fork, leaving some chunks. Add cream cheese and
blend with blender or food processor. Add onion, toma- to, garlic
salt and cilantro, and mix until well-blended. Add lemon juice to
taste as desired. Serve with tortilla chips or sourdough bread. To
store cover with plastic wrap or aluminum foil and refrigerate.

From the Oregonian's FOODday, 1/26/93.
