Title: MOCK TUNA SALAD
Categories: Digest, Oct.
Yield: 1 Servings
1    15 ounce can chickpeas,
Drained
1    Stalk celery, finely
Chopped
1 md Carrot, grated (optional)
1    Green onion, finely chopped
2 ts Eggless mayonaise
1 tb Sweet pickle relish
1/4 ts Sale (optional)
Mash the chickpeas with a fork or potato masher. Leave some chunks.
Add the celery, carrot, green onion, mayonnaise, and relish. Add salt
to taste. Serve

on whole wheat bread or in pita bread with lettuse and sliced
tomatoes.

For a fat-free version, substitute 2 teaspoons of mustard for the 2
teaspoons of mayonnaise.

This recipe comes from the "Food for Life" by Dr. Neal Barnard.
