Title: OATMEAL  WHOOPIE  PIES
Categories: Cookie
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
3/4 c  Margarine or shortening
2    Eggs
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Oatmeal
1 ts Baking powder
2 1/2 c  Flour
3 tb Boiling water
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1    Beaten egg white
2 tb Milk
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Confectioners sugar
Cream together brown sugar, margarine and eggs.  Add dry ingredients.
Drop by heaping teaspoons on greased cookie sheet. Bake 350 degrees
for 8 to 10 minutes. Mix filling and fill center of whoopie pies.
