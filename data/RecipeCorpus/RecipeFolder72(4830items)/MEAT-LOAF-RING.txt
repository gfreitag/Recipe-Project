Title: MEAT LOAF RING
Categories: Beef, Microwave
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Ground beef, lean
1/4 ts Pepper
1    Egg; beaten
1 ts Garlic salt; OR
1    Garlic clove; minced
6    Soda crackers; OR
1/4 c  Bread crumbs, dry
1 sm Onion; minced
1 tb Parsley, fresh; minced
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Bell pepper, green;
-chopped
1/4 c  Celery; chopped
1/2 c  Tomato juice
1/4 c  Catsup
Thoroughly combine all the ingredients except the catsup. Pack
lightly into a nine-inch glass dish, making a well in center. Place
an empty glass, right side down, in the center of the dish. Microwave
on HIGH for six minutes, rotating one-quarter turn halfway through
cooking. Drizzle the catsup over the top. Microwave on HIGH for five
minutes longer, rotating one-quarter turn halfway through cooking.
Let the loaf Rest for five minutes.  Remove glass; baste off
drippings.

If time is not a factor, you can cook it with the following times:
MEDIUM-HIGH (70%) for nine to ten minutes in step 1. MEDIUM-HIGH
(70%) for seven to nine minutes in step 2.

Source: Let's Cook Microwave by Barbara Harris
