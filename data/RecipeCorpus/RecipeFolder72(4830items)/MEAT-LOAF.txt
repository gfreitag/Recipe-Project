Title: MEAT LOAF
Categories: Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Bread crumbs
2 ea Eggs, beaten
1 ea Onion, small chopped
1 tb Parsley, chopped
1/2 c  Milk
4 ea Bacon strips
1 ds Salt
1 ds Pepper
Combine all ingredients except bacon. Place in loaf pan with bacon on
top. Bake 45 minutes at 350 deg. You may substitute bacon by
spreading 2 tablespoons salad oil over top.
