Title: MIDNIGHT CRAVING BY GEORGE'S AT THE COVE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
2    Scoops vanilla ice cream
4 oz Milk
1 pt Fresh raspberries
Hershey Chocolate syrup
Blend raspberries, ice cream and milk. Pour into well chilled, tall
glass. Swirl chocolate syrup on top and serve. A pickle can be added
for garnish.
