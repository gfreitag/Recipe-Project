Title: OIL COOKIES
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Oil, preferably corn oil
1 ts Almond flavoring
1 ts Baking powder
1 tb -Water; warm
1 ts Baking soda
4 c  Flour
1 pn -Salt
Beat eggs and sugar.  Add oil and water. Sift flour, salt, and baking
powder and soda.  Add in small amounts. Roll out (after chilling) or
drop onto the baking sheet. Bake in a moderate oven 375 degress for
10 minutes. They are tasty but they need a better name. Source: Katie
Kopec (via mom)
