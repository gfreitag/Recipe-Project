Title: MIDDLE EASTERN STYLE BEEF
Categories: Beef
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Lean round steak,cut into
Strips
1 sm Onion,chopped
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 cn 10 1/2 oz. condensed beef
Broth
1/2    Soup can water
1 1/2 c  Frozen broccoli, cauliflower
And carrots,thawed
1/4 c  Raisins
1 tb Lemon juice
1/8 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 c  Dry rice
1/2 c  Sliced almonds
Brown beef and onion in hot butter in a large skillet.Add broth
water,vegetables,raisins,lemon juice and cinnamon.Bring to a boil
Stir in rice and almonds.Cover;remove from heat.Let stand 5
minutes.Fluff with a fork.Makes 4 servings.
