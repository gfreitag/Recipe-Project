Title: NO PEEKIE CHICKIE BY HELEN
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk Uncle Bens Rice & Wild Rice
1 cn Cream of Celery soup
10    Chicken-pieces
1 cn Mushroon soup
1 cn Water
1 pk Onion soup mix (put aside)
1. Mix ingredients in a 9 x 13" pan. 2. Lay pieces of chicken on top
(dried with paper) 3. Take one package onion soup mix, sprinkle over
chicken. 4. Cover with foil. 5. Bake 2 hrs at 350 degrees. DO NOT
PEEK!
