Title: NO CHOLESTEROL HOLLANDAISE
Categories: Sauces, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Cup
1 c  EDENsoy
-OR- any EDEN foOd beverage,
-Original flavor
2 tb EDEN Safflower Oil
1/4 ts LIMA Sea Salt
1 tb EDEN Kudzu
-OR- arrowroot,
-- dissolved in...
2 tb -Water
1 tb Lemon juice
Paprika or ground nutmeg
-- for garnish
Combine EDENSOY, Oil, salt, and dissolved kudzu or arrowroot. Simmer
about 3-5 minutes, stirring constantly with wire whisk until mixture
becomes thick. Add lemon juice. Pour hot over food and garnish with
paprika or ground nutmeg. Delicious over cooked vegetables, fish,
tempeh, tofu, or seafood. No eggs!

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cooking Time: 10 minutes Yield: 1 cup
