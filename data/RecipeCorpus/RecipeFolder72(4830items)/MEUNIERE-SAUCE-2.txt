Title: MEUNIERE SAUCE 2
Categories: Sauces, Fish, Seafood
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Melted butter
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
-Dash of pepper
Saute seafood in butter.  DO NOT OVERCOOK.  For example, shrimp are
done when they change color to pink, about 2 to 4 minutes. Remove the
seafood. Brown the butter, add lemon juice and seasonings to it; pour
over seafood.
