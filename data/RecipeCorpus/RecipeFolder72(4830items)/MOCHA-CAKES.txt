Title: MOCHA CAKES
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 40 Servings
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
3    Eggs well beaten
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Milk
Cream sugar and butter, add well beaten eggs. Add flour& baking powder
mixture alternately with the milk. Put in greased and floured 13x9
inch pan and bake 25-30 min, in 350 oven. Let cool in pan. Next day
cut in small squares and icing all over and roll in crushed peanuts.
Delicious but long to make ( 3 hours usually )
