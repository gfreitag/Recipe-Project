Title: NUT BREAD/BAKE IT ALL MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 8 Servings
3 c  Bake it all mix*
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Nuts -- chopped
*Do not pack. Break egg into bowl; beat. Stir in vanilla, milk, sugar
and nuts. Add Mix and beat until well blended. Turn into a 9 by 5 by
3-inch loaf pan, bottom rubbed with shortening. Bake in a 350 F. oven
about 45 minutes.  Let stand about 10 minutes, then turn out on a
rack to cool.
Twelve hours storage
improves flavor and texture.
FRUIT BREAD:  Use chopped prunes or dried apricots instead
:
of nuts in the Nut Bread recipe.
