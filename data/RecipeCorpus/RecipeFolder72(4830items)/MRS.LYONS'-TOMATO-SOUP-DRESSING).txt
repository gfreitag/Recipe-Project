Title: MRS.LYONS' TOMATO SOUP DRESSING)
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Tomato soup ( can)
3/4 c  Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 c  Onion chopped
1 ts Garlic (1 clove  minced)
1 1/2 c  Vegetable oil
Combine all ingredients and beat with electric beater until well
blended. Refrigerate.
