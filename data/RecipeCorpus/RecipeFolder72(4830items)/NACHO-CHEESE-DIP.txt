Title: NACHO CHEESE DIP
Categories: Mexican, Dip, Restaurants
Yield: 3 Servings
MMMMM-------------------MICKEY'S GOURMET CKBK------------------------
1/2 lb Provolone, grated
1/2 lb American cheese, grated
3/4 c  Heavy cream
8 oz Cream cheese
1/4 ts Garlic powder
3/4 ts Worcestershire
1/8 ts Cayenne
1/8 ts Yellow food coloring
Fiesta Fun Center Snack Bar, Disney's Contemporary Resort Melt
provolone in top of double boiler over boiling water. Add American
cheese and stir in cream. Add cream cheese and stir until all is
melted. Remove from heat; whip in seasonings and food coloring. Serve
warm iwth crackers, chips, or vegs.
