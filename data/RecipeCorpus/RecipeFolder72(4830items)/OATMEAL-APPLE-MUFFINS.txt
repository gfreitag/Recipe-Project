Title: OATMEAL-APPLE MUFFINS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
2 oz Egg Beaters
1 c  Skim milk
3 ts Polyunsaturated oil
1 c  Whole wheat flour
3 ts Baking powder
1 c  Uncooked rolled oats
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Peeled, chopped apples
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.  and grease muffin tins. Mix egg
whites, milk, and oil in bowl.  Add dry ingredients and mix. Fold in
raisins and apples. Place batter in muffin tins; fill each 3/4 full.
Bake about 20 minutes or until centers seem dry.
