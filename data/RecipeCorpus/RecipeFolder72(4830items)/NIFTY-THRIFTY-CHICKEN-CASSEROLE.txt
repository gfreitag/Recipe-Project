Title: NIFTY THRIFTY CHICKEN CASSEROLE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Dry uncooked noodles
2 tb Margarine
1 ts Celery salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 lb Torn spinach -- 8 cups
2 c  Chopped cooked chicken,
Turkey, ham
Or 2 cans drained tuna
1 1/2 c  Cottage cheese
1    2 oz. jar
1 c  Shredded Cheddar cheese
Pimiento -- draned, chopped
Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain. Add margarine,
celery salt and pepper. Toss lightly. In large bowl combine noodles,
spinach, meat, mushrooms, cottage cheese, pimiento and Cheddar
cheese. Spoon into 3 qt. casserole. Bake for 30-35 mins. at 350 F.
Stir 2-3 times while cooking so spinach doesn't dry out.
