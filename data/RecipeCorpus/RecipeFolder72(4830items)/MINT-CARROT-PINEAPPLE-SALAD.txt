Title: MINT CARROT PINEAPPLE SALAD
Categories: Salads, Fruits, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
4 md Carrots; Shredded, About 2
-Cups
3/4 c  Pineapple; Finely Chopped
1/3 c  Fat Free Plain Yogurt
2 tb Fresh Mint Leaves; Chopped
2 tb Lemon Juice
1 ts Honey
1/2 ts Ground Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground Cumin
3 c  Leaf Lettuce; Torn Into Bite
-Sized Pieces, About 1/2
-Large Head
Mix all of the ingredients except the lettuce in a glass or plastic
bowl, blending well.  Cover and refrigerate about 2 hours or until
well chilled. Serve on the lettuce.

Nutritional Information Per Serving:

Calories:  50    Protein:  2 Grams    Carbohydrates: 10 Grams Fat: 0
Grams Cholesterol:  0 Milligrams
Sodium:  30 Milligrams Potassium: 290 Milligrams
