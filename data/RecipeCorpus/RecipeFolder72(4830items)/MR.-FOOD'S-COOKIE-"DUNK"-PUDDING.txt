Title: MR. FOOD'S COOKIE "DUNK" PUDDING
Categories: Desserts, Puddings
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Cold milk
4 oz Instant vanilla pudding and
- pie filling
20    To 30 miniature-sized
- cookies (your favorites)
=======================================================
============== ==== == Using the 2 cups milk, make the pudding
according to the package directions. Once it has thickened, place it
in a large bowl, if it isn't already, and fold in the cookies. Cover
and refrigerate for 20 minutes before serving.
