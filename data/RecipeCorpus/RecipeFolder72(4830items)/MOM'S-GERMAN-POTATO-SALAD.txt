Title: MOM'S GERMAN POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Cheese/eggs, German
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Potatoes, medium
1/4 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Bacon, chopped
2    Eggs
1/4 c  White vinegar
Salt to taste
Pepper to taste
Boil potatoes with salt in water until soft. Fry bacon until crisp.
Save 1/4 cup bacon grease. While potatoes are warm cut into cubes Mix
potatoes, onions, salt and pepper in a bowl. To the frying pan add
the grease, eggs and vinegar, cook until the eggs are done. Add the
potato mixture and mix. Variation:  use green onions
increase the vinegar
increase the onions
increase bacon
