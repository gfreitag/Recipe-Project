Title: NEW ENGLAND STYLE POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads
3 lb potatoes  -- cooked
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  celery  -- diced
5 TB vinegar
2 ts salt
4    eggs  -- hard-cooked,
sieved
:
1 pt sour cream
1 ts pepper
1 1/2 TB mustard
1/2    clove garlic  -- crushed
1/2 c  olives -- sliced

Peel potatoes, cut into 1/2 inch cubes and chill. Add celery, 3 tbs.
vinegar and salt. Fold eggs into sour cream, add remaining vinegar,
pepper, mustard, garlic and onion. Place potato mixture in salad
bowl, add

dressing and toss well. Garnish with olives.
