Title: MARG'S GAZPACHO SOUP
Categories: Soups, Vegetables
Yield: 12 Servings
48 oz Can tomato juice
1/4 c  Salad oil
MMMMM-------------------------SEASONINGS------------------------------
4 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Garlic salt
ds Accent seasoning
2 ts Salt
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 ts Vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
MMMMM-------------------------VEGETABLES------------------------------
1 c  Carrots, grated
1 c  Carrots, finely sliced
1    Cucumber, finely diced
1    Green pepper, finely diced
3    Tomatoes, chopped
1 c  Celery, chopped fine (opt)
3 lg Radishes, thinly sliced
Parsley flakes
1/4 c  Onions, finely diced
-OR-
1 c  Green onions including stems
-(or use some of both)
Cauliflower fleurets (opt)
Broccoli buds (optional)
Beat tomato juice and salad oil in a blender. Add the seasonings to
the previous mixture and blend. Then chill this mixture while
preparing the vegetables. Add the prepared vegetables to the mixture
and chill again. Sprinkle with parsley before serving cold.
