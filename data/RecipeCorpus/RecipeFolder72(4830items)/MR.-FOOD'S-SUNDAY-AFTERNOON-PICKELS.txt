Title: MR. FOOD'S SUNDAY AFTERNOON PICKELS
Categories: Pickels, Relish
Yield: 7 Cups
1 c  White vinegar
1 c  Water
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
8 c  Peeled & thinly sliced Kirby
Cucumbers (3 lbs)
-Handful of chopped scallion
-or dill (optional)
Source: Mr. Food - From My Kitchen to Yours

In a large bowl, combine vinegar, water, sugar and salt; mix well. Add
cucumbers and toss until well coated.

Cover and chill for at least 2 hours, or overnight. These will keep
several weeks refrigerated in a sealed container.

Suzy's Mom's Variation: Slice one large onion into thinly sliced
rings and add to mixture along with the cucumbers.
