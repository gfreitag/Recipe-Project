Title: MEATLOAF SECOND TIME AROUND IDEAS
Categories: New, Text, Import

Yield: 4 Servings  :

Crumble and reheat in soups Slice thinly, layer on bread. Top with
salsa and thin slices of Monterey Jack cheese; broil until heated
through. Julienne some peppers, onions and mushrooms and saute.
Julienne leftover meatloaf and saute alongside the vegetables to
reheat the meat.
