Title: MR. FOOD'S MUSHROOM SALAD
Categories: Salads, Mushroom
Yield: 99 Servings
About 4 cups
2 lb Fresh mushrooms, quartered
1/4 c  Italian dressing
2 ts Minced garlic
1/4 ts Black pepper
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a large skillet, combine all the
ingredients and cook over medium-high heat for 8 to 10 minutes or
until tender, stirring occasionally. Serve immediately or cover and
chill until ready to use hot or cold. NOTE: Serve as a topping for
hamburgers, over mixed greens, or fresh sliced tomatoes. It's great,
too, on toasted bread, topped with shredded cheese.
