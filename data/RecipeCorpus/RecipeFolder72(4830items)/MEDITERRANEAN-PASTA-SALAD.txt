Title: MEDITERRANEAN PASTA SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 pk Any EDEN Spirals or Shells
16    Pitted black olives
2 cn EDEN Diced Tomatoes
-- (14.5 oz. each), drained
1 sm Red onion; finely chopped
1/4 c  Celery leaves, chopped
-OR- parsley
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 c  EDEN Extra Virgin Olive Oil
1/4 c  EDEN Red Wine Vinegar
2    Cloves garlic; minced
1 tb Dried oregano
1 ts EDEN Shoyu
Prepare pasta as package directs. Rinse and drain. Add olives
tomatoes, onion, celery leaves or parsley. Mix. Prepare dressing. Mix
into salad. A great salad served warm or cold!
