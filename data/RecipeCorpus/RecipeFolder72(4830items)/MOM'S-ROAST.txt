Title: MOM'S ROAST
Categories: Meats, Mom
Yield: 1 Roast
Eye of round, symmetrical
Sherry, dry
Gunk (see recipe)
Make sure meat is not thinner at one end than the other. (Can
substitute chuck or rump roast.)  Cover FROZEN roast with sherry and
gunk; put a little water and sherry in the bottom of the pan.

Cook in 350 degree oven; don't turn while cooking.
