Title: MARSHMALLOW NUT LOAF
Categories: Breads, No bake, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Marshmallows
1/2 c  Sweet cream
1/2 c  Dates
1/2 lb Graham crackers
1/2 lb Walnut or pecan meats
Soften marshmallows in cream, finely crumb graham crackers, quarter
dates lengthwise, coarsely cut or break walnut or pecan meats. Mix
and work into loaf, keep in cool place, slice when ready to serve,
either alone or with whipped cream.

Source: Mrs. Bessie Davis, Leroy Grange, Lake County, OH
