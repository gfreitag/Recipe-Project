Title: MARK'S FABULOUS VINAIGRETTE SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Extra Virgin olive oil
1/2 c  Malt vinegar
1 lg Garlic clove, crushed
1/4 ts Dijon mustard
2 ts Tarragon
1 ts Dillweed
Salt & pepper
Combine all ingredients in a salad bottle.  Shake well. If keeping in
the fridge, remember to warm up to room temperature about an hour
before serving.
