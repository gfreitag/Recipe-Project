Title: MR. FOOD'S SPECIAL CHICKEN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Chicken
Yield: 3 Servings
3 c  Cooked chicken, diced
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Honey
1/2 ts Curry powder
1/4 ts Celery salt
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients;
mix well. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour, or until ready to
serve. NOTE: You can use canned chicken or leftover turkey instead of
the chicken. And you can add garlic powder or onion powder,
scallions, or even grapes or pecans. Give it your own special touches!
