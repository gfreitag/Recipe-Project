Title: MUSHROOMS RICE BAKE
Categories: Rice
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sliced fresh Mushrooms
1/2 c  Chopped Onion
2 tb Margarine
2    Eggs
3 oz Packages Cream Cheese
(soft)
7 oz Can Evaporated Milk
3 c  Cooked Rice
1/4 c  Chopped Parsley
1 ts Salt
Saute' mushrooms and onions in margarine.  Beat together eggs and
cream cheese.  Stir in milk.  Stir in cooked rice, parsley and salt.
Add mushroom mixture.  Bake in 10 x 6 baking dish.  Bake uncovered in
350 degree oven 40-45 minutes.
