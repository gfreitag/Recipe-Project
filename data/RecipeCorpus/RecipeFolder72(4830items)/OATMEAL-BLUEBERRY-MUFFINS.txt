Title: OATMEAL BLUEBERRY MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Snacks
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Rolled oats
1/3 c  Salad oil
1 ea Egg
1 c  Buttermilk or sour milk
1/2 c  Brown Sugar (packed)
1 1/2 c  Blueberries
1 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 ts Soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Soak oats in milk until moisture is absorbed. Add salad oil, egg,
brown sugar and blueberries and mix thoroughly.

Mix together flour, soda, cinnamon, cloves, baking powder, salt,
allspice and nurmeg and add to oat mixture. Mix thoroughly.

Fill greased muffin cups 2/3 full. Bake in a 400 degree oven for 20
to 25 minutes until golden brown. Serve hot.

From the Kitche of: Gary & Margie Hartford, Eugene, OR (1:152/19)
