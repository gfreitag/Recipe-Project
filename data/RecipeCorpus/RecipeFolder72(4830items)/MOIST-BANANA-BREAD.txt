Title: MOIST BANANA BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
3    Or 4 ripe bananas
1    Egg
1 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Melted butter or margarine
1/2 c  Walnuts (optional)
1/2 c  Chocolate chips (optional)
Mash bananas by hand or with mixer until fairly smooth (may still
appear slightly lumpy).  Add all other ingredients in order, mixing
as you go. Add melted butter last. Pour into 1 greased and floured
loaf pan or 2 small loaf pans and bake at 325 degrees for 50 to 60
minutes. Note: If possible, grease pan with Butter flavored Crisco or
use butter.
