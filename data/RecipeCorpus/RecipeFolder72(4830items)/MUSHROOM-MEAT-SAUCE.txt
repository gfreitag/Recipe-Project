Title: MUSHROOM MEAT SAUCE
Categories: Pasta, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 c  Sliced mushrooms
1    Clove Garlic -- chopped
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/8 ts Cayenne Pepper
2 1/2 c  Cooked tomatoes
1/2 cn Tomato paste (6 Ounce)
Salt
Saut√© the beef, mushrooms, and garlic in the olive oil for 5 minutes
stirring constantly. Add the cayenne pepper and tomatoes and simmer
over low heat for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in the
tomato paste and salt to taste. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes
longer, stirring occasionally.
