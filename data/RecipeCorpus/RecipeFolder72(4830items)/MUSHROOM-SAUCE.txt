Title: MUSHROOM SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
Brown Sauce
Prepare Brown Sauce.  Cook and stir in 1 cup sliced mushrooms or one
can drained.  Saute in margerine until lightly browned. Stir
mushrooms and a few drops of worcestershire sauce into the sauce.
Serve with meat, omelets, and fish.
