Title: MICROWAVED SOLE PARMESAN
Categories: Seafood, Cheese
Yield: 4 Servings
4    8-oz. sole fillets
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Green onion; chopped
3 tb Parmesan cheese
1/3 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
Paprika
Arrange fillets on oiled microwave safe dish.  Combine mayonnaise,
green onion,

and parmesan cheese.  Spread on each filet.  Sprinkle with bread
crumbs and paprika.  Cover with waxed paper. Microwave on high power,
2 minutes for each fillet. Do not turn fish.
