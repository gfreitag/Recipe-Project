Title: MARINATED TROUT (PSTRAGI MARYNOWANE)
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lb Trout fillets
1 lg Onion; diced
2    Carrots; diced
1    Celery root; diced
1    Parsley root; diced
2 qt Water
1 c  Dry white wine
2 tb Vinegar
1    Bay leaf
6    Peppercorns
1 ds Salt; to taste
Recipe by: Alliette Ziolkowski Saute diced veggies and cook in water
alone until half done (about 10 minut Add wine, vinegar & salt and
spices.. Cut fish into slices, add to pot. Cook at slow boil for
another 10 to 15 minutes or untill fish and veggies a Alow to cool.
place in glass jars, cover tightly and store in fridge.
