Title: MERSEY POINT SOLOMON GUNDY
Categories: Seafood, Appetizers, Canadian
Yield: 1 Batch
250 g  Solomon gundy; drained
1 sm Onion; chopped or sliced
1 c  Miracle whip light
1/2    Canteloupe (optl)
Mix and refrigerate for at least four hours. Gives this appetizer a
real twist by adding cubes of canteloupe
