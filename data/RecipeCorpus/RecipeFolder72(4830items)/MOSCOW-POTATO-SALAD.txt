Title: MOSCOW POTATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Med Idaho potatoes, peeled
And diced
4    Boneless chicken breast
Halves, coked & diced
1 lg Cucumber, seeded, diced
2    Hard cooked eggs, chopped
4 ts Fresh snipped dill
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
Lettuce leaves
1 c  Mayonnaise
The idaho Potato commission obtained this recipe on a trip to Moscow.
Cook potatoes in water until tender. Drain. In a large bowl toss
potatoes with chciken, cucumbers and eggs, dill, salt and pepper.
Line a large platter with lettuce leaves and dome on mixture. Spread
with mayonnaise to completely cover salad dome.
