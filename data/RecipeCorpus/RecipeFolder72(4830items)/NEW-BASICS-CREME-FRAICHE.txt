Title: NEW BASICS CREME FRAICHE
Categories: Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 2 Servings
-New Basic's CB
1/2 c  Heavy or whipping cream
-(not ultra-pasteurized)
1/2 c  Sour cream
More tart than whipped heavy cream and a bit richer than sour cream,
Creme Fraiche is a delicious addition to sauces and is just the right
topping for rich chocolate desserts. 1 cup

Whisk the creams together in a small bowl. Pour the mixture into a
jar, cover, and let stand in a warm place for 12 hours. Then stir, and
refrigerate for 24 hours.

Source: New Basic's Cookbook MM by Cathy Svitek
