Title: MR. FOOD'S RASPBERRY CHICKEN 12/4/95
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Chicken leg quarters
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 tb Melted butter
1/2 c  Raspberry jam --
(red/seedless)
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Soy sauce
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.  Place the chicken leg quarters in a
large baking pan that has been coated with nonstick vegetable spray;
sprinkle the chicken with the salt and black pepper, then drizzle
with the melted butter. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or until the chicken
is golden and cooked through. In a small saucepan, combine the
remaining ingredients to make a raspberry sauce; mix well. Cook over
low heat for about 1 minute, until the sauce is smooth, stirring
occasionally. Remove the chicken from the pan and place on a serving
platter. Top with the sauce and serve.
