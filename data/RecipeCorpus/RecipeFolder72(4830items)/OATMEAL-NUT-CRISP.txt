Title: OATMEAL NUT CRISP
Categories: Desserts, Snacks, Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Butter
3 c  Oatmeal
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1    Egg
2/3 c  Nuts (chopped)
FROM THE KITCHEN OF: Gary & Margie Hartford [Eugene OR]

Brown butter, add oatmeal and toast until brown. Cool while beating
egg until thick and lemon colored. Fold into oat mixture; add nuts.
Drop by teaspoonfuls about 2 inches apart. Bake 15 to 20 minutes at
300 degrees F.

From Helen Foster   (Alvadore Christian Church Cookbook)
