Title: MEAT STUFFED ROLLS
Categories: Sandwiches, Beef
Yield: 10 Servings
MMMMM---------------------JET PABST CWNK50B--------------------------
20 x  Hard rolls
1 2/3 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1 cn Black olives;chopped
6 x  Hard boiled eggs;chopped
2 1/2 c  Cheddar cheese; grated
Cook beef and drain.  Add tomato sauce, olives and cheese. Stir well.
Add chopped eggs last.  Cook over low heat until cheese milts. Cut
the rolls in half and pick out the middle of the roll to make room
for the meat mixture. Stuff rolls and wrap in foil. Bake in a
300    oven for 10 to 15 minutes. Formatted by E. Lamparelli
