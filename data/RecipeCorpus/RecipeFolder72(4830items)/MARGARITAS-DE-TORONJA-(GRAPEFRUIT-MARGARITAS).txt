Title: MARGARITAS DE TORONJA (GRAPEFRUIT MARGARITAS)
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 8 Servings
Grapefruit slices
- cut in quarters
Salt
3/4 c  Grapefruit juice
6 oz Tequila
2 oz Triple sec
2 c  Cracked ice
Moisten rims of cocktail glasses with cut grapefruit. Swirl in mound
of salt to coat edges. Combine grapefruit juice, tequila, triple sec
and cracked ice in blender. Blend until smooth. Pour into prepared
glasses. Place a grapefruit quarter on each rim.
