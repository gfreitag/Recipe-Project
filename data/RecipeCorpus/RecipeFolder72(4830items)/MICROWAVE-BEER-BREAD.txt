Title: MICROWAVE BEER BREAD
Categories: Breads, Microwave
Yield: 1 Loaf
2 c  Self-rising Flour
1 c  Flour
3 tb Sugar
12 oz Beer
1 tb Butter; softened
Cornflake Crumbs
Mix together all ingredients. Spray a glass loaf pan with cooking
spray; sprinkle bottom and sides with crumbs. Spoon batter into pan
and top with butter and a light sprinkling of cornflake crumbs.
Microwave on MEDIUM for 9 minutes, then on HIGH for 2 minutes.
