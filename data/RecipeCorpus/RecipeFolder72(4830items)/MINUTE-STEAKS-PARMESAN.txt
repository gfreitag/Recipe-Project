Title: MINUTE STEAKS PARMESAN
Categories: Main dish, Beef, Italian
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Egg white; slightly beaten
2 ts Water
1 ds Pepper
1/2 c  Crackers ; finely crushed s
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese ; grated
4 c  Steaks ; (about 4 oz. each)
2 tb Margarine
8 c  Pizza sauce
In shallow bowl, combine egg white, water & pepper. Set aside. On a
plate, combine cracker crumbs & cheese.Dip each cube steak into the
egg mixture, then the cracker/cheese mixture. In a large skillet,
melt margarine; brown steaks on both sides. Add pizza sauce & simmer
3-5 minutes. Garnish w/ remaining crumb mixture. Serve immediately.
Serves 4. Serve this w/ Honey Butter Corn & an Italian Salad.
