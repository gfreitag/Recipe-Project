Title: MJEDDRAH
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Lentils, washed
4 c  Water
3 tb Olive oil
2 lg Onions, coarsely chopped
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c  Brown rice
Bring lentils & water to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover & simmer for 25
minutes.  Heat 2 tb oil in a skillet & saute onions & salt till the
onions are transulcent. Heat remaining oil in another skillet & saute
the rice for 3 minutes.  Combine lentils, rice & onion, cover tightly
& simmer till the lentils & rice are render, about 1 hour. You may
have to add up to 2 extra cups water. Stir occasionally.

Serve with a green salad.

Frances Moore Lappe, "Diet for a Small Planet"
