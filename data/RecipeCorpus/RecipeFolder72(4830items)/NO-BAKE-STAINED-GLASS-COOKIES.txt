Title: NO-BAKE STAINED GLASS COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Chocolate, No-bake
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-----------------------PATTI VDRJ67A----------------------------
12 oz Semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Walnuts, chopped
10 1/2 oz Colored mini marshmallows
2 lb Flaked coconut
Melt chocolate chips and butter; let cool. Add nuts and marshmallows.
Mix. Spoon mixture in log form across an 18" piece of waxed paper.
Coat roll in coconut. Bring paper up around roll and pinch ends of
paper together to cover roll; refrigerate for at least 3-4 hours.
Slice in approximately 1/2 inch thick slices.
