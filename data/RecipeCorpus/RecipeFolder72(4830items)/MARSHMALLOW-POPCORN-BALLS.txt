Title: MARSHMALLOW POPCORN BALLS
Categories: Snacks, Popcorn
Yield: 14 Sm balls
8 c  Popped corn
1 c  Peanuts
20    Regular-sized marshmallows
1/2    Stick margarine
Wax paper
Mix peanuts and popcorn in a large bowl.  Melt margarine and
marshmallows over low heat.  Pour marshmallow mixture over popcorn.
Grease hands. Form popcorn and marshmallow mixture into 14 small
balls. Place on wax paper. Cover with plastic wrap when cool.
