Title: MR. FOOD'S HONEY-MUSTARD FLAVORED STUFFED TOM
Categories: Vegetables, Tomatoes
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Seasoned bread crumbs
2 tb Honey-flavored mustard
1 tb Olive oil
======================================================= ======= 2
large tomatoes (about 1 pound), stored at room temperature until
fully ripe. Cut each tomato in half horizontally. Gently squeeze the
halves to release the seeds and pulp, reserving the seeds and pulp
for use in sauces, soups, etc. Preheat the oven to 4 Stir with a
spoon until well mixed. Fill the tomato halves evenly with the
stuffing, then arrange in a shallow baking dish. Bake for 20 minutes,
or until slightly softened and hot.
