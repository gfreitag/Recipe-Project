Title: MR. FOOD'S CHOCOLATE SHERBET
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Unsweetened cocoa 1 cup milk
6 tb Sugar
2 tb Cold water
1/4 c  Hot water
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a small saucepan combine the cocoa and
sugar over low heat. Slowly stir in the hot water. Continue stirring
over low heat for 2 to 3 minutes, or until the sugar is dissolved and
the mixture is warm. Remove from the heat and gradually stir In the
milk. NOTE: I suggest making a double batch. Know why? Because it
disappears so fast!
