Title: MUSTARD~ SWEET & HOT
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Medium Onion, Chopped
1    Clove Garlic, Minced
1 c  Dry White Wine
2/3 c  Dry Mustard
1 1/2 ts Olive or Vegetable Oil
1 tb Honey
1 ts Salt
3    Drops Tabasco
Combine onion garlic and wine in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
Simmer 5 minutes. Remove from heat and cool. Strain into dry mustard.
Add oil,honey, salt, and tabasco. Cook over low heat until smooth and
thick. Yield about
16    Oz.

NOTE: Homemade mustards must be stored in the refrigerator. SOURCE:
