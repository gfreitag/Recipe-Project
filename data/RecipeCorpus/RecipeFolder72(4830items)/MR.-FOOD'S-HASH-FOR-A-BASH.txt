Title: MR. FOOD'S HASH FOR A BASH
Categories: Casseroles, Corned
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Butter
2 md Onions, diced 2 cups
1 lb Frozen hash brown potato
- cubes, thawed
1 lb Diced cooked corned beef 3
- cups
8    Eggs
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 350F. In a large skillet,
heat the butter over medium-high heat and saut√© the onions for 3 to 4
minutes, until tender. Add the potato cubes and cook for 10 to 12
minutes, stirring occasionally. Add the corned beef and cool for 5 to
6 minutes, until heated through. Place the mixture in a 9X13 baking
dish that has been coated with nonstick vegetable spray and, with a
serving-sized spoon, make 8 evenly spaced indention's about 1/2 inch
deep in the mixture. Crack the eggs one at a time and place each egg
in an indention. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 20 to
25    minutes, or until the eggs are cooked to your liking.
