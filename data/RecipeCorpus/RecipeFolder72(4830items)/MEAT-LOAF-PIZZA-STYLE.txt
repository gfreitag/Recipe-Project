Title: MEAT LOAF PIZZA STYLE
Categories: Clay, Pot
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  White bread crumbs
1 c  Skim milk
2 lb Ground chicken breast,
Skinless
2    Egg whites -- whipped
1/2 c  Onions -- chopped
1/2 c  Bell peppers -- chopped
1/2 c  Fat-free parmesan cheese
1 ts Salt
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Pizza seasoning
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 c  Homemade Pizza Sauce
1 c  Fat-free mozzarella cheese
Grated
1 c  Mushrooms -- sliced
Soak top and bottom of a clay pot in water for at least 15 minutes.
In a mixing bowl, combine bread crumbs, milk, chicken, egg whites,
onions, bell peppers, parmesan cheese, salt, oregano, pizza
seasoning, and black pepper. Press in pot. Cover and place in cold
oven. Set temperature to 400. Bake for 1 hour. Spread sauce on top.
Then, top with cheese and mushrooms. Cover, return to oven and bake
10 minutes more.
