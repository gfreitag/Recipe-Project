Title: MOLASSES TAFFY (PULL CANDY)
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Molasses
3 tb Butter
1 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Soda
Combine all ingredients and boil until hard ball stage. Wet a shallow
pan and pour in candy. When cool enough to handle, pull until light a
color as desired is reached. Twist and cut into sticks.
