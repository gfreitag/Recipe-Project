Title: NEVER FAIL DIVINITY (CANDY)
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  White syrup
1/2 c  Hot water
1/4 ts Salt
2 ea Egg whites
1 ts Vanilla
Mix sugar, syrup, water, and salt.  Boil till sugar melts, then cook
without stirring tohard ball or 250 degrees on thermometer. Beat 2 egg
whites till stiff. Pour sugar mixture into egg. Add 1 teaspoon
vanilla and beat till it holds shape.  Add nuts if desired. Pour into
greased 9x13 pan.
