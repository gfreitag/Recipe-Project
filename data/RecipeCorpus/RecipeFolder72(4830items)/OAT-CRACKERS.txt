Title: OAT CRACKERS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 c  Rolled oats
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Wheat germ
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Oil
2/3 c  Water
Mix dry & wewt ingredients separately.  Then combine them together.
Roll out dough 1/8" thick onto an oiled cookie sheet. Score & bake at
325F for 25 to 30 minutes.
