Title: NEIMAN MARCUS CHOCOLATE CHIP COOKIE
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 112 Servings
2 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Brown sugar
4    Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
4 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
24 oz Chocolate chips
8 oz Hershey bar -- grated
3 c  Walnuts -- chopped
5 c  Blended oatmeal -- * see
Note
Cream butter and sugars.  Add eggs and vanilla.  Mix together with
flour, oatmeal, salt and baking powder and soda. Add chocolate and
nuts. Place 2" apart.
