Title: NUTTY ORANGE POPCORN
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 3 Quarts
3 qt Popped pocorn
1 c  Peanuts; or cashews
1/2 c  Butter; melted
1 tb Orange peel; shredded
1/4 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Raisins
In a large baking pan, combine the popcorn and nuts.,  Combine butter,
honey, orange peel, and nutmeg.  Pour over popcorn and toss well.
Bake at 300 F for 20 to 25 minutes, stirring once or twice.  Stir in
raisins, and allow to cool.  Break into pieces to serve.
