Title: MEXICALI CHEESE DIP
Categories: Cincodemayo
Yield: 3 Cups
1 cn (16 oz.) spicy refried beans
3/4 c  Picante sauce
1 pk (6 oz.) frozen guacamole dip
1 c  (4 oz.) sharp Cheddar
-cheese, shredded
Sour cream as garnish
Preheat oven to 350 F. Combine refried beans and picante sauce.
Spread into a 9-inch baking dish. Top with guacamole dip. Sprinkle
with cheese. Bake 20 to 25 minutes. Garnish with dollops of sour
cream. Makes about 3 cups.
