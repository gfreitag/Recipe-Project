Title: MR. FOOD'S READY MACARONI & CHEESE
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Hot cooked elbow macaroni
10 3/4 oz Cond cream of mushroom soup
16 oz Shredded Cheddar Cheese
- , divided
1/2 c  Picante sauce
1/2 c  Crumbled tortilla chips
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 440 F. In a 1 1/2-quart
baling dish, combine the cookies macaroni, mushroom soup, 1 1/22 cups
cheese, and picante sauce. Bake for 25 minutes. Remove from the oven
and top with the tortilla chips and the remaining 1//2 cup cheese.
Return to the oven and bake 5 more minutes, or until the cheese
melts. Serve immediately.
