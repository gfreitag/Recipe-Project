Title: NELL'S FRIED CABBAGE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Head cabbage
1 lb Bacon
1/2 lg Onion
Garlic salt
Salt and pepper
Pimiento -- optional
ds Worcestershire sauce
Fry bacon; leave all of the grease in the pan.  Cut the onion up fine
and add it and chopped cabbage to the grease. Add a dash of
Worcestershire sauce. Sprinkle with garlic saltgenerously. Add salt
and pepper according to taste. Sprinkle with crumbled bacon and serve.
