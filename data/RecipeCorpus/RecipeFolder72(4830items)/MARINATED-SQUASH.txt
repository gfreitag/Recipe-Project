Title: MARINATED SQUASH
Categories: Appetizers, Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
Fresh raw squash
Parmesan/Romano cheese(opt.)
Bottled Ital. salad dressing
Place thinly sliced, washed, raw squash in large jar, packing as
tightly as possible. Cover with bottled dressing to about 1/2" of top
of jar. Romano or Parmesan cheese may be added if desired.
Refrigerate for abt. 2 wks.  The squash will be firm and crunchy.
