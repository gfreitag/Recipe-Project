Title: MRS. LYONS' SALAD DRESSING)
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Flour
2 tb Dry mustard
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Rich milk
1 tb Butter (size of an egg)
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
Heat first 6 ingredients in top of double boiler until good and hot
then add the vinegar and salt and cook till thick.
