Title: MERLE'S MOP SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Vinegar, cider or wine
5 tb Worcestershire sauce
2/3 c  Salad oil
3 tb Butter
1 ea Lemon, thinly sliced
3 ea Cloves, crushed
2 tb Ginger, grated
2 tb Dry mustard
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and heat until flavors are
nicely blended.  Use to baste any meat or poultry.
