Title: NICELY SPICED PORK LOIN
Categories: Meats
Yield: 8 Servings
4    Cloves garlic, minced
1 ts Salt
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Paprika
2 ts Each ground cumin and
-coriander
1 ts Ginger
1/4 ts Cinnamon
1    Boneless pork loin roast
(about 4 lb.)
In bowl, mash garlic with salt; mix in oil, lemon juice, paprika,
cumin, coriander, ginger and cinnamon.

Cut 1 inch deep slits all over top and sides of pork; rub paste all
over roast.  Roast on roasting rack in pan in 325øF oven for about 1
hour and 45 minutes or until meat thermometer registers 160øF.

Transfer to platter and tent with foil.  Let stand for 10 minutes
before carving.  Makes 8 servings.  Typed in MMFormat by
cjhartlin@msn.com Source: The Canadian Living 20th Anniversary
Cookbook.
