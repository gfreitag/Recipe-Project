Title: NAVY BEAN MUSHROOM SAUTE
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 2 Servings
3 oz Mushrooms; sliced
3 oz Onions; sliced
2 tb Safflower oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic; minced
1/2 ts Fresh parsley; chopped
3 oz Navy beans; cooked
3 oz Amaranth; cooked
Saute mushrooms and onions in skillet with safflwoer oil until
onions are translucent. Add salt, garlic and parsley. Combine with
navy beans and amaranth. Mix well and serve warm.
