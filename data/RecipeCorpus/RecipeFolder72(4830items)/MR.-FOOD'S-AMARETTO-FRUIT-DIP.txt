Title: MR. FOOD'S AMARETTO FRUIT DIP
Categories: Sauces, Dips
Yield: 99 Servings
About 1 1/2 cups
8 oz Reduced-fat cream cheese,
- softened
7 oz Marshmallow creme
2 tb Amaretto or almond extract
=======================================================
============== ==== == Place all the ingredients in a medium-sized
bowl; beat with an electric beater on medium speed until well
blended. Serve immediately or cover and chill until ready to use.
NOTE: Use this for topping or filling cakes, or serve as a fruit dip
with whole fresh strawberries or slices of apples, peaches, pears, or
any of your favorite fruits.
