Title: MERRY MUNCHIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Butter or margarine, soft
1 1/2 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Chopped walnuts
Heat oven to 350 degrees F.  Grease cookie sheets. Combine butter,
brown sugar, egg, and vanilla; beat until light and fluffy. Stir in
flour, baking powder and salt; mix well. Stir in raisins and walnuts.

Drop by tablespoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets. Bake in upper
third of oven at 350 degrees F for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from
cookie sheets; cool on wire racks. Makes 3 dozen cookies.
