Title: MY MONTANA FRIEND'S SHRIMP CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles
2 c  rice -- cooked
Yield: 2 Servings
1 c  tomato juice
3/4 c  mayonaise
1 cn broken shrimp (May use
more)
:
1 md onion -- grated
1/2 c  milk
1/2 c  green pepper -- chopped

Mix all ingredients together well and bake at 350 degrees for 45
minutes.
