Title: MOM'S (DETER) FAMOUS MACARONI SALAD
Categories: American, Pasta, Salads, Favorites
Yield: 10 Servings
2 c  Macaroni -- cooked
4    Eggs -- chopped
4 tb Green onion -- chopped
2 tb Sweet pickle juice -- to
Taste
Salt and pepper -- to taste
1    Jar pimiento
2 tb Olives -- chopped
1/2 c  Mayonnaise -- to taste
Blend all ingredients together.  Add pickle juice to taste. Sample
generously before serving!
