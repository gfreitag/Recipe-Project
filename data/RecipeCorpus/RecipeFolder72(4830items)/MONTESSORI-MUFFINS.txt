Title: MONTESSORI MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Davidson
Yield: 12 Muffins
2 c  Flour, whole wheat
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Prunes, pitted; chopped
1    Egg; beaten
1/4 c  Oil
1/2 c  Molasses
1 1/2 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 400 F.  Combine whole wheat flour, baking powder,
salt and prunes in a bowl.  Stir together egg, oil, molasses, and
milk in another bowl. Combine the mixtures, mixing just until
blended. Spoon into a greased 12-cup muffin tin.  Bake for 20 to 25
minutes, until a toothpick tests clean.

---*Dying for Chocolate*
