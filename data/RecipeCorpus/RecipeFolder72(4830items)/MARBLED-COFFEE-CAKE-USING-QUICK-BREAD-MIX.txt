Title: MARBLED COFFEE CAKE USING QUICK BREAD MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 9 Servings
2 c  Quick Bread Mix
1/4 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Milk
1    Egg -- beaten
2 tb Shortening
2 tb Molasses
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground nutmeg
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1/4 c  Brown sugar -- firmly
Packed
2 tb Quick Bread Mix
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1 tb Butter OR margarine
Combine quick bread mix and sugar in mixing bowl; add milk, egg, and
shortening, and beat at medium speed of an electric mixer until
smooth. Remove 1 cup batter; combine with molassas, 1/2 teaspoon of
cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves.  Place spoonfuls of plain batter into a
greased 8-inch square baking pan in a checkerboard pattern. Spoon
spiced batter in spaces around  plain batter, gently swirl with a
knife. Combine brown sugar, 2 Tablespoons Quick Bread Mix, and 1/4
teaspoon cinnamon in a small bowl. Cut in butter with   a pastry
blender until mixture resembles coars meal. Sprinkle mixture over
batter. Bake at 375 F. for 25 to 30 minutes. Serve warm.
Yield:  9 servings
