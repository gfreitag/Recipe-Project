Title: MR. FOOD'S TERIYAKI MARINADE
Categories: Sauces, Marinade
Yield: 99 Servings
About 3/4 cup
3/4 c  Prepared teriyaki sauce
2 tb Dry sherry
1 tb Chopped fresh ginger
=======================================================
============== ==== == Directions: In a small bowl, combine all of
the ingredients and mix well. Use as a marinade for your favorite
meat, marinating for at least 6 to 8 hours, or overnight, in a
resealable plastic bag, turning occasionally.
