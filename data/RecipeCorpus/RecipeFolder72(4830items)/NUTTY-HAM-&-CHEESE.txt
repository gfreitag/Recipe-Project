Title: NUTTY HAM & CHEESE
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Sub roll (8 inch), split
3 tb Softened cream cheese
2 tb Coarsely chopped pecans
2 tb Chopped green onion
1/8 ts Garlic powder
2    Leaves curly lettuce
4 oz Sliced ham
2 sl Swiss cheese, halved
Diagonally
1/3 c  Alfalfa sprouts
Combine cream cheese, pecans, green onions, and garlic powder; spread
on roll.  Layer the remaining ingredients in the order listed. From:
