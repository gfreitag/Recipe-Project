Title: NUTTINI
Categories: Alcoholic
Yield: 1 Serving
1 1/2 oz Vodka
1 oz Frangelico
- (hazelnut liqueur)
Add ice to liquors, shake until very cold, strain into chilled martini
class. Serves 1.

Per serving: 207 calories, 0 grams fat (0 percent fat from calories),
0 milligrams cholesterol, 0 grams protein, 13 grams carbohydrate, 2
milligrams sodium.

SOURCE: The Sting-Ray Room, Raleigh, NC. Recipe appeared in the
August 21 edition of the Raleigh News & Observer. Format in
