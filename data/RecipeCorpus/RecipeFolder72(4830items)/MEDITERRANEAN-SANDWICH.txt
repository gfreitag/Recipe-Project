Title: MEDITERRANEAN SANDWICH
Categories: Sandwiches, Poultry, Jaw
Yield: 6 Sandwiches
2 c  Cubed cooked chicken
1/2 c  Chopped red bell pepper
1/2 c  Chopped cucumber
1/2 c  Shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 ts Dried oregano leaves
4 1/4 oz Can chopped ripe olives
1    14 to 16 inch baguette, cut
Horizontally in half
Set oven control to broil. Mix all ingredients except baguette (no
kidding..doh!). Spoon about 1 1/2 cups chicken mixture onto each bread
half. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Broil with tops 4 to 6 inches
from heat about 5 minutes or until hot. Cut each half into 3 slices.
