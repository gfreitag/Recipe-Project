Title: MATZO MEAL MIX
Categories: Kosher, Instruction
Yield: 1 Cup
1 1/2 tb Toasted sesame seeds
1/2 c  Ea. matzo meal & cake meal
1/4 c  Potato starch
1 tb Ea. dried rosemary & oregan.
1 ts Ea. cinnamon and ginger
2 tb Onion powder
2 ts Mild curry powder
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1/4 ts Salt (optional)
Place all ingreds. in bowl, stirring well to combine.

Spoon into glass jar or container with lid, leaving 2-inch space
at top. Shake or stir each time before using. Store at room
temperature, or refrigerate.

Yield:  Abt. 1 2/3 c.; recipe may be doubled or tripled as desired.

Kosher answer to "Shake & Bake". Frances Prince's New Jewish
Cuisine
