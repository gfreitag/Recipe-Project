Title: MR. FOOD'S WALNUT CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 c  1 stick butter, at
- room temperature
2 1/4 c  Sugar, divided
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
2/3 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
Grated rind of 1 lemon
1 c  Water
======================================================= ======

the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and cinnamon; set aside. In a
large bowl, cream the butter, then add 1 cup sugar and the eggs.

is best served warm. If you're not going to serve it right after
baking, just pop it in the microwave (if it's in a microwave-proof
pan) on low power for about 1 minute, or in a 250 degree (F) oven for
10 to 15 minutes before serving.
