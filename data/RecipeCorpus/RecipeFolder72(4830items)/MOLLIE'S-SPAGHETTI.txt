Title: MOLLIE'S SPAGHETTI
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Hamburger meat
1    Minced garlic
1 tb Whole basil
1 lb Can of tomatoes
2    6oz. cans tomatoe paste
1    Small mushrooms
12 oz Noodle
1 ts Salt
1 c  Water
Brown hamburger meat and drain.  Add next 5 ingredients and simmer,
covered 14-20 minutes/  Stir often. Cook noodles in salted water. Add
italian seasoning, oregano, and onion to taste.
