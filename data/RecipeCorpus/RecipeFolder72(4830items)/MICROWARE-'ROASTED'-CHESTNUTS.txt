Title: MICROWARE 'ROASTED' CHESTNUTS
Categories: Digest, Sept.
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Chestnuts
Cut large X into flat side of Chestnut using knife. Arrange the cut
Chestnuts in microwave pan Microwave on high (100%) for 2.5 minutes.
While Chestnuts are still warm - peel
