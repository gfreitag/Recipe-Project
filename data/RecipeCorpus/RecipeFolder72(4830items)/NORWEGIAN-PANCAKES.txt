Title: NORWEGIAN PANCAKES
Categories: Dessert, Norwegian
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Melted butter
2/3 c  Milk
2    Egg yolks
2    Egg whites
1/4 c  Thick cream
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Flour
Mix flour, baking powder, milk, and egg yolks into nice smooth
batter. Add the cream and melted butter. Add the whites of eggs
beaten stiff. Fry in 8-12" frying pan. When fried, put on jam of any
kind, then fold in four and serve as dessert.
