Title: MAYONNAISE SAUCE
Categories: Herbs, Spreads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 ts Fresh  dill, finely chopped
1 ts Fresh parsley,finely chopped
1 cl Garlic finely chopped
1 ts Ginger root finely grated
1 tb Lemon juice
Mix all ingredients together and store in refrigerator until ready
for use.

Mayonnaise is a wonderful vehicle for the taste of fresh herbs. Chive,
parsley, basil, dill, fennel, and tarrragon all work well. and a more
intense flavor can be created by adding a clove of fresh garlic or a
few drops of hot chili oil.
