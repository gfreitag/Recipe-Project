Title: MOM'S POTATO CASSEROLE
Categories: Breakfast, Casseroles, Potatoes
2 lb hashed brown potatoes --
frozen
Yield: 24 Servings
:
3/4 c  margarine, imitation --
melted, divided
:
1 ts salt
1/2 ts pepper
1 cn cream
1 c  fat-free sour cream
1 c  cheddar cheese -- grated
2 c  corn flakes
paprika
:

Mix potatoes, 1/2 c margarine, salt, pepper, onions soup, sour cream
and cheese together.  Spread into greased 9x13" pan. Mix 1/4 c
margarine with corn flakes, sprinkle over potatoes. Sprinkle with
paprika. Bake 350 for 1 hour.
