Title: MIXED BERRY DESSERT PIZZA
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 1 16" pizza
1    16" Dessert Pizza Shell,
-prebaked
2 c  Ricotta cheese
1/2 c  Confectioners' sugar
1/2 c  Almonds, sliced/toasted
1 ts Almond extract
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
2 c  Mixed berries
1/2 c  Seedless raspberry jam,
-heated
Blend ricotta cheese, powdered sugar, almonds and almond extract;
spread over cooled crust, leaving a border around the edges. Top
ricotta mixture with berries and brush with warm raspberry jam. Serve
chilled or room temperature.

Source: Pizza Today, January 1993.
