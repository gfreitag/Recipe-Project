Title: MR. FOOD'S MUSTARD PEPPER STEAK
Categories: Meats, Flank
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Italian dressing
2 tb Dijon-style mustard
2 ts Cracked or coarsely
- ground black pepper
1 lb To 1 1/4 pounds beef flank
- steak, cut about 3/4
- inch thick
=======================================================
============== ==== == Directions: In a small bowl, combine the
Italian dressing and mustard. Using your hands, rub the pepper into
both sides of the steak; place the steak in a shallow dish. Pour 1/4
cup of the marinade mixture over the steak, coating both sides
evenly; cover
