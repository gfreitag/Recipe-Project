Title: MUSTARD RUB
Categories: Sauces, Meats
Yield: 125 Ml
3 tb Dried oregano leaves
2 tb Chili powder
1 tb Garlic powder
1/2 c  Grainy Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper to taste
In a small bowl, combine oregano, chili powder and garlic powder; add
mustard; stir well to mix; add salt and pepper.
Cover and refrigerate.
Spread or pat onto beef up to 5 hours in advance of cooking. Store
in a refrigerator.

Yield: 1/2 cup (125 ml) Source: Appeal Magazine/ Vol 3. No. 3/ Spring
1994
