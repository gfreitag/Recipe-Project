Title: MEL'S MOPPIN' SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Cider vinegar
5 tb Worcestershire Sauce
2/3 c  Vegetable oil
3 tb Butter
1 ea Lemon, thinly slices, with
- peel on
3 ea Cloves garlic, minced
3 tb Ginger, grated
2 tb Dry mustard
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and heat until flavors are
nicely blended, about 15 minutes.  After it cools, strain the lemon
slices out.

Source:  The Great Barbeque Companion, mops, sops, sauces and rubs by
Bruce Bjorkman.
