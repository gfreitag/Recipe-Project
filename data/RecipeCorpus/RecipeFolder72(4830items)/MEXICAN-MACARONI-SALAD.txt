Title: MEXICAN MACARONI SALAD
Categories: Salads, Pasta
Yield: 8 Servings
-Pam Coombes
1 c  Hellman's Real Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Skim milk
2 ts Instant chopped onion
1 ts Salt
1 ts Chili powder
1/8 ts Garlic powder
6 dr Hot pepper sauce
1    8oz pkg elbow macaroni,
Cooked & drained
1    16oz can red kidney beans,
Well drained
1 c  Diced part-skim Mozzarella
Cheese (4oz)
In a large bowl stir together first 7 ingredients. Add remaining
ingredients; toss to coat well. Cover; Chill at least 2 hours.
