Title: MUSHROOM-ONION TOPPER
Categories: Relishes, Vegetables, Sauces, Hamburger
Yield: 6 Servings
1 tb Margarine Or Butter
1 ea Onion; Thinly Sliced
4 oz Mushrooms; 1 can, *
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
1/8 ts Pepper
*    Use 1 4-oz can of mushroom stems and pieces.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Melt margarine in a small skillet. Add onion
slices and cook and stir until tender.  Stir in remaining ingredients
and heat through. Serve, hot, over patties.
