Title: NEW ORLEANS WHISKEY SAUCE
Categories: Cajun/creol, Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1/2 c  Butter -- melted
1 c  Sugar
1 ea Egg
1 1/2 oz Whiskey (bourbon)
Cream the butter and sugar in a double boiler over boiling water. Add
the egg and stir rapidly, so the egg doesn't curdle. When well mixed,
allow to cool. Add whiskey.

Serve over New Orleans Bread Pudding.

: La Bouche Creole - p. 235
