Title: MONK'S BEANS
Categories: Beans, Soups, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 Servings
4 cn Black Beans -- undrained
2 cn Tomatoes, Canned -- whole,
Undrained
1 lg Onion -- finely chopped
2    Cloves Garlic -- or more, to
Taste
2 tb Green Peppercorns -- in
Brine*
2 ts Ground Cumin
Cook beans, tomatoes, and onion in crockpot on low all day, until
onion is done.  Add garlic, green peppercorns, and cumin 1/2 hour
before serving.
