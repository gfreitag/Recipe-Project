Title: MINTED PEA BREAD ABM
Categories: Breads
Yield: 24 Ounces
MMMMM-----------------BEST BREAD MACHINE CB EVER----------------------
1 1/2 c  Peas; fresh or frozen
2 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
3 tb Wheat bran
1 1/2 tb Sugar
1/2 tb Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1/3 c  Fresh mint leaves; chopped
- or 1 tb dried
7/8 c  Water
Bring all ingredients to room temperature and pour into bakery, in
order. Set "baking control" at 10 o'clock. Select "white bread" and
push Start.

In hot & humid weather, use 1/8 c less water.

Serve this cooling bread with cold creamed soup or gazpacho, with
tomato and mozzarella salad, or with cold fish. It is also refreshing
with spicy Middle Eastern or Indian food.
