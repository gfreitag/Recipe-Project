Title: MEUNIERE SAUCE
Categories: Creole, Fish, Seafood, Sauces
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Butter or margarine
3 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Hot pepper sauce
2 tb Green onions,chopped
1 tb Minced parsley
1/4 ts White pepper
1 ds Worcestershire sauce
Combine all ingredients and simmer 5 minutes.
