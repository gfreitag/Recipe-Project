Title: NO-HASSLE WASSAIL
Categories: Alcohol, Punch, Hot beverag, Apples
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ga Apple cider
2 qt Apricot nectar
3 c  Dark rum
1 1/2 c  Brown sugar; firm pack
1/4 c  Lemon juice
6    Sticks cinnamon; 3" each
3 sm Oranges
1 1/2 ts Whole cloves
Combine cider, nectar, rum, brown sugar, lemon juice, and cinnamon
sticks in a large Dutch oven. Bring to a boil; reduce heat, and
simmer, uncovered, 1 hour. Stud oranges with cloves, and add to cider
mixture. Serve hot. YIELD: 1-1/2 gallons
