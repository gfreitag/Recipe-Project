Title: NO-BAKE RICE KRISPIE-DATE NUT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 2 Dozen
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
1    Egg; beaten
1 c  Dates; chopped
2 c  Rice Krispies
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 ds Salt
Coconut, shredded
In a saucepan, cook butter, sugar, egg and dates for five minutes,
stirring constantly. Cool to lukewarm; add to the Rice Krispies,
nuts, vanilla and salt. Mix well; form into balls about the size of
walnuts. Roll in coconut.
