Title: MR. FOOD'S HONEY-MUSTARD DRESSING
Categories: Sauces, Dressings
Yield: 99 Servings
About 1 1/4 cups
1 c  Light mayonnaise
1/4 c  Honey
2 tb Prepared yellow mustard
2 tb Dried parsley flakes
1 tb White vinegar
1 ts Onion powder
=======================================================
============== ==== == Place all the ingredients in a medium-sized
bowl; whisk until smooth and creamy. Serve immediately or store in
the refrigerator in an airtight container until ready to use. NOTE:
Perfect on grilled chicken or as a topping for a cool summer tossed
salad.
