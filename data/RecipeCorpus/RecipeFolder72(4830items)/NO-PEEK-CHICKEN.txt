Title: NO-PEEK CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Casseroles, Rice
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Chicken, broiler-fryer,
- cut in serving pieces
1 1/2 c  Minute Rice, uncooked
1 cn Condensed Cream of Chicken
- soup (10 3/4 oz)
1 cn Condensed Cream of Celery
- soup (10 3/4 oz)
1 pk Dry Onion Soup & Dip Mix
- (1 1/2 oz)
Preheat oven to 300F. Mix Minute Rice and both cans of soup in 13" x
9" baking dish. Distribute evenly in bottom of dish. Place chicken
pieces in single layer on top of rice-soup mixture. Sprinkle dry
onion soup over top. Cover tightly and bake at 300F for 2 1/2 hours.
