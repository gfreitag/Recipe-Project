Title: MUNG BEANS WITH BROWN SUGAR
Categories: Desserts, Thai, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Whole, dried mung beans
1 c  Light brown sugar
1 tb Ginger, chopped
Soak the mung beans in 2 c cold water for 30 minutes. Remove any
beans that float to the top.  Drain.  In a large pot, combine beans
with 7 c cold water.  Simmer, uncovered, over medium heat for 30 to
35 minutes. With a slotted spoon, remove any floating skins.  Stir in
the sugar & ginger. Cover, reduce heat to low & continue cooking for
15 minutes. Serve hot.
