Title: NO BAKE BEST BLUEBERRY PIE
Categories: Fruits, Pies & past, No bake
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Baked Pie Crust
Filling-----
3 c  Fresh Or Frozen Blueberries
1/3 c  Sugar
1 tb Cornstarch -- (plus 2 tsp.)
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Ground Cinnamon
2/3 c  Water
1/4 c  Medium Sherry
2 ts Lemon Juice
Spread the berries in the pie shell.

Mix sugar, cornstarch, salt and cinnomon in a 4 cup glass measure.
Stir in the water.  Microwave uncovered on high stirring every minute
until thickened and clear, 3 to 4 minutes.

Stir in the sherry and lemon juice.  Pon on the fruit in the pie
shell. Refrigerate at least 2 hours.

Serve with sour cream topping: Mix 1 cup sour cream, 2 tablespoons
sugar and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
