Title: MINT PATTIES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 8 Servings
1/3 c  Corn syrup
1/4 c  Margarine
4 c  Sifted powdered sugar
2    Drops red food coloring
1 ts Peppermint extract
In a small bowl combine the corn syrup, margarine and peppermint
extract Beat until well combined. Gradually add 2 cups of the
powdered sugar, beating well. Add as much more sugar as you can mix
in with a spoon. Turn out on to surface lightly coated with powdered
sugar and knead in as much of the rest of sugar to make a stiff
dough. Divide into three parts and add food coloring to each , red,
green and yellow etc. Shape into 3/4 inch balls and place on  wax
paper covered baking sheets and press down with tines of fork. Let
dry for several hours.
