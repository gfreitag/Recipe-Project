Title: MINT & YOGURT BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Yeast
3 c  Bread flour
1 c  Plain yogurt
3 tb Chopped fresh mint
1    Fresh yellow lemon rind,
-grated
2 ts Honey
1/4 c  Plus 1 Tbsp warm water
Add ingredients into the pan in the order listed, select white bread
and push "Start".
