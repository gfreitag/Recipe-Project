Title: OATMEAL MUFFINS (CROUSE)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Oatmeal
1 1/2 c  Sour milk
2    Eggs
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Shortening
1 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
Soak oatmeal  in sour milk overnight. In morning add the other
ingredients. Chopped dates or raisins may be added. Bake 25 minutes.
Makes 14 muffins.

Note: Assume hot oven 400 - 450 F.

Source: Mrs. Vernon E. Crouse, North Lima Grange, Mahoning County, OH
