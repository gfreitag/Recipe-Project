Title: MINCEMEAT II
Categories: Pies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb Hamburger
2 ts Salt
3/4 lb Suet
4 lb Apples
1 ts Ea.cinnamon,nutmeg,cloves
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 1/2 c  Sugar
3 1/2 c  Raisins
2 pk Seeded raisins
Put the meat, salt and water to cover in a large pot. Simmer until
tender. Add the diced apple, suet and spices, vinegar, sugar and
raisins and cook till the suet is dissolved anf raisins and apples
tender.If more water is needed add it. Stir often to keep fruit from
sticking.
