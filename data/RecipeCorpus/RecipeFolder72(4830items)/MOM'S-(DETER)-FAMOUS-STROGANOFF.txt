Title: MOM'S (DETER) FAMOUS STROGANOFF
Categories: Beef, Pasta, Main dish, Garlic, Favorites
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Clove garlic
1 c  Onions
2 lb Ground round
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 pk Noodles -- cooked
1 c  Sour cream
Parsley -- chopped
1/8 ts Rosemary -- crushed
Saute garlic, onions, and ground round.  Add salt, pepper, paprika,
and soup. Simmer for 5 minutes. When ready to serve, add sour cream,
parsley, and rosemary.  Serve over noodles.
