Title: MASTER MIX BANANA BREAD
Categories: Healthy
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Master mix
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 c  Tub margarine
2    Bananas; mashed
4    Egg whites; stiffly beaten
1/2 c  Walnuts; chopped
Recipe by: The Living Heart Diet by M. E. DeBakey ISBN 0-671-61998-5
1.    Preheat oven to 350 F.

2. Combine Master Mix, sugar, and baking soda in a large bowl.

3. Blend in margarine and bananas until smooth.

4. Fold in beaten egg whites and walnuts.

5. Turn into a 9 x 5-inch loaf pan that has been brushed with
margarine.

6. Bake for 50-60 mins or until a cake tester inserted in center
comes out clean.

7. Cool 10 mins, turn onto rack, and cool completely.

YIELD: 1 loaf (20 slices)

1 slice = 140 Cal; 2 Prot; 6.6 Fat; 19 Carb; 0.9 Sat; 1.7 Mono; 3.7
Poly; 0 Chol; 176 Na
