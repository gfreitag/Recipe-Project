Title: MOCK WHIPPED CREAM FROSTING
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
In small pan
Cook until thickened:
2 tb Flour
1/2 c  Milk
Cool
Blend in electric mixer:
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
Add 2 mixtures together, a dash of salt, flavoring, (a drop of color,
if desired), and beat on High speed for
4    minutes.  Helen Jolly
