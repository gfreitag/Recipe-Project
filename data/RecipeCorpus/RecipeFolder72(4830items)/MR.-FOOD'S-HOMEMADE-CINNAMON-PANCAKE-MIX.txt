Title: MR. FOOD'S HOMEMADE CINNAMON PANCAKE MIX
Categories: Mixes, Pancakes
Yield: 99 Servings
About 7 cups mix
5 c  All-purpose flour
1 1/4 c  Instant nonfat dry milk
1/3 c  Sugar
2 tb Baking powder
1/2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 tb Salt
=======================================================
============== ==== == Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl;
mix well. Place in a large airtight container and store in a cool,
dry place. Use within 6 to 8 months to make a few batches of Homemade
Cinnamon Pancakes:
