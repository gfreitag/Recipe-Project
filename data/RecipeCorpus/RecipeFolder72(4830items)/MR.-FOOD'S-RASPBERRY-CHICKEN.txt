Title: MR. FOOD'S RASPBERRY CHICKEN
Categories: Meats, Chicken
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken leg quarters
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 tb Melted butter
1/2 c  Seedless red raspberry jam
2 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Soy sauce
1/8 ts Crushed red pepper
======================================================= ======
Preheat the oven to 375 degrees. Place the chicken leg quarters in a
large baking pan that has been coated with nonstick vegetable spray;
sprinkle the chicken with the salt and black pepper, then drizzle
with the melted butter. Bake for 45-50 minutes, or Note: This chicken
looks especially inviting when garnished with fresh parsley sprigs.
