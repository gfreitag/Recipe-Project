Title: MARMALADE DRUMSTICKS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 1 Servings
12    Chicken drumsticks (about
2 1/2    Pounds)
3/4 c  Orange marmalade
2 c  Herb-seasoned stuffing mix,
-crushed
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
Spread the drumsticks with marmalade. Coat evenly with stuffing
crumbs. Place in a single layer in a greased shallow baking pan.
Drizzle with butter. Bake at 350! for 40 minutes or until the chicken
tests done.

Yield: 6 servings.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
