Title: NANCY'S HONEY MUSTARD GRILLED CHICKEN OR PORK
Categories: Poultry, Meats, Grill, Marinades
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM--------------------NANCY BROWN WCPW93B-------------------------
Chicken or pork
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Lemon juice, fresh
1/4 c  Soy sauce
2    Garlic cloves; crushed
Combine ingredients. Marinate chicken or pork for 2-8 hours, then
grill.
