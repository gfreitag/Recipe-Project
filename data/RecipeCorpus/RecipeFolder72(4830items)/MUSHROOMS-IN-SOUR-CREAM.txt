Title: MUSHROOMS IN SOUR CREAM
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Chopped onion
1 lb Sliced mushrooms
1 c  Sour cream
1 ts Dill weed
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1 ea Pepper
Melt butter in fry pan. Add onion and saute until clear. Add
mushrooms and saute until lightly browned. Add remaining ingredients.
Stir to heat through but not boil. Serve.
