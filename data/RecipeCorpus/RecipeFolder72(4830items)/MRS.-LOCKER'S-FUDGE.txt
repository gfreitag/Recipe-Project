Title: MRS. LOCKER'S FUDGE
Categories: Candies, Fudge, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
Sugar Mix
3 c  Sugar
1 sm Can evaporated milk
3/4 c  Margarine
Chocolate mix
12 oz Semi sweet
Chocolate chips
1 sm Jar marshmallow fluff
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 ts Vanilla
Stir all the sugar mix together and boil slowly (low flame) to 238'
(use a candy thermometer).

Put the chocolate mix into a bowl and stir together: Add the chocolate
mixture to the heated sugar mixture and stir until the chocolate is
melted. Spread the mixture into a large greased (with butter) baking
pan. Cool and cut, then refrigerate.  Walt MM
