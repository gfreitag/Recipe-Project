Title: OATMEAL COOKED IN MILK
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
1 ts Salt
2 c  Milk
2 c  Water
2 c  Quaker Oats, uncooked
-- (quick or old-fashioned)
Add salt to milk and water in saucepan; bring to a boil.

For FLAKY OATMEAL: Stir in oats.  Cook 1 minute for quick oats,
stirring occasionally.  Cook 5 minutes or longer for old fashioned
oats. Cover pan, remove from heat and let stand a few minutes.

For CREAMY OATMEAL: Stir in oats.  Bring to a boil and cook as above.

CHOCOLATE OATMEAL: Use 2 cups chocolate dairy drink in place of milk
in above recipe.  Cook as directed.
