Title: NASTURTIUM SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegan
Yield: 2 Servings
10    Nasturtium flowers
1/2    Radiocchio lettuce
-- shredded
1    Small crisp lettuce
-- washed
10    Sprigs of parsley
6    Black olives, stoned
3    Tomatoes; cut into wedges
4    Spring onions; slivered
1/2    Ripe avocado; diced
5 tb Vinaigrette
Serves 2-3

Put the prepared ingredients into a large salad bowl, reserving four
of the flowers. Toss in the vinaigrette, then decorate the salad with
the remaining flowers.
