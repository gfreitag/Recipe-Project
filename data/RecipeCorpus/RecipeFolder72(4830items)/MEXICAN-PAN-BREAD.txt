Title: MEXICAN PAN BREAD
Categories: Digest, July
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Mexe-beans
1    Chopped onion
2    Cloves garlic, chopped
1    Egg replacer
1 c  Cornmeal
1 ts Chile powder
1 ts Cumin
Preheat oven to 350F Mix everything, pour in a Pam'ed iron skillet,
bake 10 minutes.

Salud!
