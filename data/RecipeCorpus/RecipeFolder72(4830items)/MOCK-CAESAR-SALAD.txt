Title: MOCK CAESAR SALAD
Categories: Appetizers, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
2    Cl Garlic; peeled and minced
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Lemon juice
3 c  Belgian endive
6 c  Romaine lettuce; torn
1/8 ts Black pepper; freshly cracke
1 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
1 1/3 c  Tomato; chopped
Recipe by: Rosie Daley from "In the Kitchen with Rosie" Put the
garlic, soy sauce, and lemon juice in a salad bowl and whisk
thoroughly. Add the endive and Romaine lettuce. Toss to coat.
Sprinkle the black pepper and parmesan cheese on top.

Garnish with chopped tomato.
