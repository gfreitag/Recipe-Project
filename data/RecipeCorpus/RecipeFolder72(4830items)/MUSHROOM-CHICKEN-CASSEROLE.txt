Title: MUSHROOM-CHICKEN CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Left chick
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Cooked rice
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Cooked chicken; chopped
1 c  Mushrooms; drained
1    Pimento; sliced
1/2 c  Cheese; grated
Combine all ingredients except cheese in bowl; mix well. Spoon into
buttered baking dish. Bake at 350 for 27 minutes. Sprinkle with
cheese bake additional 3 minutes.
