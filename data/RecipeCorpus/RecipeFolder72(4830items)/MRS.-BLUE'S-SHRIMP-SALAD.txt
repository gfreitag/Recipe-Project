Title: MRS. BLUE'S SHRIMP SALAD
Categories: Shrimp, Seafood, Salads, Bubba gump
Yield: 4 Servings
4 1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 lb Unpeeled large fresh shrimp
1/2 c  Chopped green pepper
1/2 c  Sliced pimiento-stuffed
- olives
1/2 c  Commercial salad dressing
1/4 c  Chopped celery
3 tb Swet pickle relish
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
1    Green onion, minced
Curly leaf lettuce
Shredded iceberg lettuce
Bring water to a boil; add shrimp, and cook 3 to 5 minutes or until
shrimp turn pink. Drain well; rinse with cold water. Chill. Peel and
devein shrimp. Place shrimp in a large shallow dish. Combine green
pepper and next 8 ingredients; pour over shrimp, stirring gently.
Cover and marinate in refrigerator 2 to 3 hours, stirring
occasionally.

Arrange leaf lettuce on individual serving plates, top with shredded
iceberg lettuce. Spoon shrimp mixture evenly over shredded lettuce.
