Title: MEXICAN FUDGE
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Monterey Jack cheese with
Jalapeno pepper
1 lb Cheddar cheese
1 cn Evaporated milk (13 oz.)
2    Eggs
3 tb Flour
Grate cheese and lay in greased 9" x 13" pan.  Beat milk, eggs, and
flour together, pour over cheeses. Bake 40 minutes at 350 degrees F.
Cut into squares or diamonds, serve hot or cold.
