Title: MINTED RICE SALAD
Categories: New, Text, Import
6 TB each vegetable and walnut
oil
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1/4 c  white wine vinegar
1 bn mint leaves, -- rinsed
Salt and pepper
:
3 c  orzo rice, cooked until al
dente -- (6 cups cooked)
:
2 c  cooked wild rice

In a blend combine the oils, vinegar and mint; season to taste with
salt and pepper. Combine the dressing with warm orzo and wild rice;
serve at room temperature.

Yield: 8 servings
