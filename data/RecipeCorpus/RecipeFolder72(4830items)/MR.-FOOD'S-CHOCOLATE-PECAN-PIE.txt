Title: MR. FOOD'S CHOCOLATE PECAN PIE
Categories: Desserts, Pies, Chocolate
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Light corn syrup
1 c  Firmly packed light brown
- sugar
3    Eggs
12 ts Vanilla extract
1 1/2 c  Coarsely chopped pecans
4 1/2 oz Semi sweet choc. chips
1/4 c  1/2 stick butter, melted
1    9" Pie Shell frozen ready
-to-bake deep dish, thawed
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. In a large
bowl, combine the corn syrup, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla; stir
with a spoon until thoroughly mixed. Add the pecans, chocolate chips,
and butter; mix well. Pour into the pie shell and bake for 55 to 60
minutes, or until firm. Note: Don't be concerned if the filling in
the center sinks a bit as it cools. By the way, did you know you can
buy pecans already broken into pieces? Look for them in the baking
section of your supermarket.
