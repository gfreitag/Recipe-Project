Title: MEAT LOAF (CAMPING)
Categories: Beef, Camping, Resort
Yield: 2 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
3/4 c  Quick oats
1    Eggs
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 c  Bell pepper
1 pk Onion soup mix
3/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Marjoram
Mix all ingredients and put in casserole pan.  Place in dutch oven.
Bake 1 hour, covered.
