Title: OATMEAL MACAROON COOKIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 3 Dozen
1 c  Butter
1 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Brown sugar
1    Egg
2 1/2 c  Quick oatmeal
1 c  Flour
1 c  Coconut
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Soda dissolved in 2 T. hot
-water
Drop on ungreased cookie sheet and bake in 425 degree oven. Watch them
carefully because they burn easily. They usually take me about 9-10
minutes. Remove while they still look a little gooey.
Let them sit a minute before removing from pan.
