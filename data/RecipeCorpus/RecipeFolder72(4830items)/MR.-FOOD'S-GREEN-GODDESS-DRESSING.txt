Title: MR. FOOD'S GREEN GODDESS DRESSING
Categories: Sauces, Dressing
Yield: 99 Servings
4 cups
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Cider vinegar
2 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream
6    Anchovy fillets
1 bn Fresh parsley, coarsely
- chopped
2    Scallions, coarsely chopped
1 ts Dried tarragon
=======================================================
============== ==== == Place all the ingredients in a blender and
blend on medium speed until very smooth. Serve immediately or chill
in an airtight container until ready to use. NOTE: It's so simple to
turn out this creamy unique taste that's tops on salads, veggies, and
even fish!
