Title: MENUDO
Categories: Beef, Soups, Mexican
Yield: 6 Servings
5 lb Tripe.
Wash well and cut into cubes. Put in a deep pot with water and add 3
tsp salt 4 cloves garlic i tsp cumin salt to taste 1/4 cup chili
powder Cook until tender, about three hours, 1/2 hour before finished
cooking, put in large can of hominy drained. makes ten to twelve
servings.
