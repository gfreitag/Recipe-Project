Title: MISS PEARL'S TOMATO-PAPAYA-MACADAMIA NUT SALS
Categories: Salsa, Fruits/nuts
Yield: 4 Cups
6    Tomaotes, blanched, peeled,
Seeded, and chopped
1    Papaya, peeled, seeded, and
Diced
1/2    Red Onion, diced
1/4 c  Macadamia Nuts, toasted and
Chopped
2 tb Mint, chopped
1 tb Cilantro, chopped
1/4 c  Fresh Lime juice
1/4 c  Olive Oil
Salt and Pepper
Combine all ingredients, and adjust seasonings to taste. Best served
with grilled fish or chicken.

08-24-94
