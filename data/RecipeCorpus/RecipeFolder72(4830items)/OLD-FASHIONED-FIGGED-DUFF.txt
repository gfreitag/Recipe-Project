Title: OLD FASHIONED FIGGED DUFF
Categories: Dessert, Nfld, Traditional
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Bread crumbs
1/4 c  Melted butter
1 ts Ginger
1 ts Allspice
1 ts Cinnamon
1 tb Hot water
1 c  Raisins
3 tb Molasses
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Soda
Soak bread in water for a few minutes.  Squeeze out water (measure
without pressing down).  Combine bread and raisins, sugar, salt,
spices and mix with a fork. Add molasses, melted butter, and soda
which has been dissolved in hot water.  Add flour and mix well.  Pour
into dampened puedding bag. Cook with Jigg's dinner for one hour.
