Title: NOODLES ROMANOFF
Categories: Main dish, Cheese/eggs, Casseroles, Microwave
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Cooked egg noodles
1 c  Cottage cheese
1 c  Commercial sour cream
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1    Clove garlic, peeled and
-crushed
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
2 dr Tabasco sauce (to taste)
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Grated sharp Cheddar cheese
1. Combine all of the ingredients except the Cheddar cheese in a
greased, 8-inch, heat-resistant, non-metallic square baking dish. 2.
Sprinkle the grated cheese over the top and heat, uncovered, in
Microwave Oven 12 to 14 minutes or until cheese melts.
