Title: MARINADE  FOR  FISH  STEAKS - CHICKEN  BREAST
Categories: Fish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 lb Fish steaks for skinless
Chicken breasts (If fish,
Suggest swordfish, monkfish
Halibut, etc.)
2 tb Ketchup
1 tb Fresh lemon juice or
Equivalent of concentrate
1    Clove garlic, minced
1 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Salt
1 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Dry vermouth
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
Place meat (fish or chicken breasts) in shallow casserole. Combine
ketchup, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, olive oil, orange juice,
vermouth and parsley; pour over meat. Marinate several hours or
overnight in refrigerator. Preheat broiler and grease broiler pan
with oil. Broil fish for 5-15 minutes or until it flakes easily. For
chicken, broil 25-30 minutes or until done. Heat marinade and spoon
over fish. Serves 4. The chicken breast option is great on the grill.
May want to double marinade amounts and use extra as a sauce over
potatoes or rice. Basic recipe from "Eater's Choice, A Food Lover's
Guide to Lower Cholesterol".
