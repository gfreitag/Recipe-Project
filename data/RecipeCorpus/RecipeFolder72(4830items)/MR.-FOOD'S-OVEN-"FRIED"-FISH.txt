Title: MR. FOOD'S OVEN-"FRIED" FISH
Categories: Seafood, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Egg, beaten
1 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 ts Dried dillweed
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1 c  Cornflake crumbs
2 lb Fresh or frozen cod, haddock
-, or whiting fillets
- (thawed if frozen)
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F. In a
shallow bowl, combine the egg, oil, dillweed, salt, and pepper. Place
corn flake crumbs in another shallow bowl. Cut the fish into 3-inch
pieces and dip in the egg mixture, then the crumbs. Place on cookie
sheets th Note: For a little novelty, each time you make this try a
different dried herb - like basil, cilantro, or chives - in place of
the dillweed.
