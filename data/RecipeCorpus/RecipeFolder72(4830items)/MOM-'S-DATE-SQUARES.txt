Title: MOM 'S DATE SQUARES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 3/4 c  Oatmeal
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Soda
3/4 c  Shortening
Cut shortening into dry ingredients and rub with hands until
mealy.Put half in pan, cover with date filling and put rest of crumbs
on top and press down. cook in 350 oven 25 min.
