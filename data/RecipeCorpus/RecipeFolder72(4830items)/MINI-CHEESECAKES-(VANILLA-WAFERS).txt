Title: MINI-CHEESECAKES (VANILLA WAFERS)
Categories: Desserts, Cakes, Cheesecakes
Yield: 12 Servings
MMMMM--------------------------XXXJ77A-------------------------------
12    Vanilla Wafers
1/2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
2    8 0z. pkg Cream Cheese
1 ts Vanilla
Line muffin tin with foil liners.  Place one vanilla wafer in
each liner.  Mix cream cheese; vanilla and sugar on medium speed until
well-blended.
Add eggs.  Mix well.  Pour over wafers; filling each
3/4    full. Bake 25 min. at 325`.
Remove from pan when cool.  Chill.
Top with fruit; preserves; nuts or chocolate.  Be creative.
