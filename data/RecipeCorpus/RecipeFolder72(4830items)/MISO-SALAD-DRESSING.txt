Title: MISO SALAD DRESSING
Categories: Dressings
Yield: 1 Batch
1/3 c  EDEN Shiro Miso
1 ts Sesame tahini
2 ts EDEN Hot Mustard
2 ts EDEN Brown Rice Vinegar
1/3 c  Water
Blend all ingredients in a blender or food processor until smooth.

Yield: 2/3 cup
