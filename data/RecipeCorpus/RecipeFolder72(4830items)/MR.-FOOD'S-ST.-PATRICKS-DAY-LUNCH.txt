Title: MR. FOOD'S ST. PATRICKS DAY LUNCH
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
1    8 or 9" unbaked pie shell,
- thawed if frozen
3    Eggs
1 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Crushed red pepper
1/4 ts Ground nutmeg
10 oz Frozen spinach,
- thawed and well drained
6 oz Frozen baby shrimp, thawed
6 oz Shredded mozzarella cheese,
- divided
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat the oven to 350°F. Bake the pie shell
for 5 to 8 minutes, until lightly browned. In a large bowl, beat the
eggs, milk, salt, red pepper, and nutmeg. Mix in the spinach, shrimp,
and 1 cup of the cheese. Pour the mixture into the prepared pie
shell, NOTE: Feeling adventurous? Make your own pie crust!
