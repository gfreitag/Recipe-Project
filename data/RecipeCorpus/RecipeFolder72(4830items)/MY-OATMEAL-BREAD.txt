Title: MY OATMEAL BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 2 Servings
2 c  Water; boiling
5 tb Margarine
3 ts Salt
6 tb Honey
2 tb Molasses, dark, unsulphured
1 c  Oats
2    Egg, extra large, lightly
-beaten
6 c  Bread flour
2 pk Yeast, sprinkled over flour
Blend all together and knead.  Let rise til double and bake at 375 30
to 45 minutes
