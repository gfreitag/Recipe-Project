Title: OFFICIAL ALL-STAR CAFE'S MALTED MILK BALL CAK
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Restaurants, Dkuhnen msn
Yield: 1 Cake
1    Box white cake mix
1/2 c  Chocolate malt powder (may
Substitute Ovaltine)
1 c  Chopped malted milk balls
MMMMM---------------------------ICING--------------------------------
2 c  Fudge icing
1/4 c  Chocolate malt powder
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Malted Milk Balls
Make cake according to directions on the box, adding the malt powder
at the same time as the dry mix.  When the batter has been placed
into the greased cake pans, sprinkle the chopped malted milk balls
evenly over the batter. Bake according to directions on the box. When
the cake is cool, mix malt powder with fudge icing. Serve with ice
cream and malted milk balls. From The Official All-Star Cafe, Times
Square, New York, NY.
