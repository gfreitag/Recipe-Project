Title: NOODLES WITH BLUE CHEESE
Categories: Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
10 oz Noodles or fettucine
1/2 lb Blue cheese (any type)
1/4 c  Whipping cream or 1/2 & 1/2
1/4 c  Grated parmesan or romano
1 x  Fresh ground black pepper
While pasta is cooking in boiling salt water, crumble cheese and mix
it with the cream and pepper. Drain the pasta, and place in a frying
pan. Add the cheese mixture and cook for 2 to 3 minutes. Serve hot
with grated cheese. From the Frugal Gourmet, posted by Doreen Richard.
