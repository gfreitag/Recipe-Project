Title: MOCK HOLLANDAISE SAUCE (LACTO)
Categories: Digest, Feb95
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  No-fat/lowfat sour cream
1/4 c  No fat/low fat mayonnaisse
1 ts Spicey mustard
1 tb Lemon juice
1 pn Red pepper flakes.
Mix the above ingredients in the top of a double boiler (adding lemon
juice last and slowly).  Heat thoroughly about 15 minutes or until
completely mixed. You can control the taste by experimenting with
different types of mustard. More pepper flakes will give the dish a
rewardingly healthful twang.
