Title: MOLASSES POPCORN BALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 10 Servings
4 qt Popped Corn
2 c  Molasses
1 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Butter
1 ts Soda
Boil Molasses, sugar, butter, and salt, stirring occasionally untill
mixture forms hard ball in cold water. Remove from fire, add soda and
mix well.  Pour over popped corn, stirring so that each kernel may be
coated. Form into a ball with well buttered hands QUICKLY!
