Title: MAYONNAISE CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Soda
1 ts Baking powder
6 tb Cocoa
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Water
1 ts Vanilla
Sift the first five ingredients together.  Add the rest of the
ingredients and mix well.  Pour into greased and floured 9x13 inch
pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Randy Rigg
