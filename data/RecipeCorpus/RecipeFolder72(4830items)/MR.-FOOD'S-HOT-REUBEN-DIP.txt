Title: MR. FOOD'S HOT REUBEN DIP
Categories: Sauces, Dips
Yield: 99 Servings
Makes about 5 cups
16 oz Sauerkraut, drained &
- squeezed dry
1 sm Onion, finely chopped
1/4 lb Cooked corned beef
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Sour cream
2 tb Prepared horseradish
4 oz Shredded Swiss cheese
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place sauerkraut,
onion & corned beef in a blender or food processor. Process until
mixture is finely chopped. Combine mixture with remaining ingredients
in a 1-quart casserole dish that has been coated with nonstick
vegetable
