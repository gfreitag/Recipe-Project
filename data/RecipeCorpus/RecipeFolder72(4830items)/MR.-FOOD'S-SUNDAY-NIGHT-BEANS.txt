Title: MR. FOOD'S SUNDAY NIGHT BEANS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Vegetable oil
2 cl Garlic, crushed
1 md Onion, cut into 1/4-inch
- wedges
32 oz Great Northern beans,
- rinsed and drained
1/2 ts Salt
1 ds Black pepper
======================================================= ====== In a
large skillet, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Saute the garlic
and onions for 7-10 minutes, until the onions are lightly browned.
Add the remaining ingredients and mix until heated through. Serve
immediately.
