Title: MAYONAISE CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
3 c  Flour
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 1/2 c  Salad dressing
6 tb Cocoa
1 1/2 c  Water
3 ts Vanilla
3 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking Powder
Grease and Flour 9 x 13x 2 oblong pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Mix thoroughly all ingredients in turn. Bake 25 to 30 minutes, cake
is done when tooth pick comes out clean.
