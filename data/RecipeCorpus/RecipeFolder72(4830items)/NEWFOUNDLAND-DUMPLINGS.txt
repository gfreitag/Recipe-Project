Title: NEWFOUNDLAND DUMPLINGS
Categories: Breads, Canadian, Stews
Yield: 7 Servings
1 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
Blend together the flour,baking powder and salt.Stir in 1/2 cup of
milk.Drop by large spoonful over hot stew or soup.Cover and simmer 15
minutes.Serve with the stew or soup.
