Title: MEAT LOAF~ JOYCE'S
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Tomato vegetable soup mix
1/2 c  Boiling water
2 lb Hamburger
2 c  Soft bread crumbs
1 ts Salt
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Ketchup
Combine soup mix and boiling water and let stand 5 min. Mix hamburg,
bread crumbs, salt and eggs well then add the soup mix and mix well.
Put in greased loaf pan and bake 40 min. then spread ketchup on top
and continue to bake until done.
