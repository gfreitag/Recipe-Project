Title: MUSHROOM~ PEPPER & ONION SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 20 Servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
1    medium onion, cut into wedges
1    green pepper, cut into thin strips
1/4 ts dried thyme leaves, crushed
1    can condensed Cream of
-Mushroom Soup
1/2 c  water

In 1 1/2-quart saucepan over medium heat, in hot oil, cook onion,
pepper and thyme until vegetables are tender. Stir in soup and water.
Cook 5 minutes. Serve over grilled or broiled steak. Makes about 2
1/2 cups sauce.
