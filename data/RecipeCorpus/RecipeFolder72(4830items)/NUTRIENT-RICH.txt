Title: NUTRIENT-RICH: TORTELLINI SOUP WITH PEAS
Categories: Soups, Pastanoodle
Yield: 3 Servings
3    Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 lb Frozen cheese tortellini
10 oz Canned chicken broth
1 c  Frozen peas
1/4 ts Pepper
1/4 c  Parmesan, freshly grated
in saucepan, bring 3 cups water and garlic to boil. Add frozen
tortellini; return to boil. Reduce heat to medium-high; cook,
stirring occasionally, for 15 minutes. Add chicken broth, 1-1/4 cups
water and peas; return to boil. Reduce heat to medium; cook for 5
minutes or until pasta is tender but firm. Stir in pepper and
sprinkle with cheese.

Per Serving: about 325 calories, 20 g protein, 8 g fat, 42 g
carbohydrate high source fibre, good source calcium, good source iron.

Source: Canadian Living magazine Oct 94 Presented in article by Rose
Murray: "Health & Well-Fare: Cooking On Your Own"
