Title: NEW ENGLAND CHOCOLATE ZUCCHINI CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 2 Cakes
MMMMM---------------------FROM: LINDA FIELDS--------------------------
1/3 c  Margarine
1 3/4 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Sour milk ** see note
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 c  Chocolate chips
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Cinnamon
2    Whole eggs
2 1/2 c  Flour
4 tb Cocoa
1/2 ts Ground cloves
2 c  Finely diced cooked zucchini
**To make the sour milk, substitute 1/2 cup milk, 1/4 tsp. baking
soda, 1 tsp. baking powder.. mix and let stand 5 minutes before using)

Cream margarine, sugar and oil together. Add eggs, vanilla and sour
milk to the mixture and beat until smooth. Mix dry ingredients and
add to creamed mixture. Beat well. Stir in zucchini. Pour into 2
greased bread pans. Sprinkle with chocolate chips. Bake at 325F for
40-45 minutes. Cool. Needs no frosting, but is very good with cream
cheese frosting.

Source: Linda Fields, Cyberealm BBS Watertown NY 315-786-1120
