Title: OAT FLAKE GARBANZO COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 1/2 c  AM Oat Flour
1 c  AM Brown Rice Flour
1/2 c  AM Garbanzo Flour
1/2 c  Chopped nuts and/or raisin
1 ts Non-alum baking powder
1/2 ts Sea salt (optional)
1/2 c  AM Unrefined Vegetable oil
1/4 c  Honey or maple syrup
-OR- other syrup
1/2 c  Water or juice
1 ts Vanilla
Mix dry ingredients together.  Mix liquids together. Combine
mixtures. Drop by teaspoonful on an oiled cookie sheet. Bake at 350
F. for 8 to 10 minutes or until edges are lightly browned. Cool on
pan.
