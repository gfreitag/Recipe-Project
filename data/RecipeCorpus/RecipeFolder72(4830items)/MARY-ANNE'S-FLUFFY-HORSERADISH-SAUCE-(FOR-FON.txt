Title: MARY ANNE'S FLUFFY HORSERADISH SAUCE (FOR FON
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 1/4 cups
1 c  Sour Cream
1/4 c  Prepared Horse Radish Sauce,
(drained)
1 tb Lemon Juice
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Minced Chives
Mix all ingredients together the night before your fondue and
refrigerate overnight. Stir before serving.
