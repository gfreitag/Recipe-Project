Title: MEAT-FILLED POTATO BALLS
Categories: Kosher, Main dish, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Mashed potatoes
2/3 c  Matzo meal
2 c  Cooked chicken or meat,>>>
Finely ground.
3 tb Fat
3    Eggs
1/4 ts Pepper
2 ts Salt
Mix potatoes with fat, 2 beaten eggs, matzo meal, salt and pepper.
Bind meat with other egg and season to taste. To form balls, take 1
tb. of potato, place a small quantity of meat on it and cover with
another spoonful of potato.  Roll in floured or dampened hands to
form a ball, being sure to cover the  meat completely. Place balls on
a greased pan and bake in 400 F. oven until browned. Variation: Make
very small and serve as hors d'oeuvres.
