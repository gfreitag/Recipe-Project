Title: NYC HOT DOG RELISH
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Water
1    Med onion, chopped
1/3 c  Ketchup
4 tb Cider vinegar
1 ts Coleman's dry mustard
1 ts Paprika
Freshly ground pepper
In saucepan combine all ingredients.  Bring to boil. reduce heat;
simmer 5 to 10 minutes, until desired consistency.
