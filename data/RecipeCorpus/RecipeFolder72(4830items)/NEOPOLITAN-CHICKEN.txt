Title: NEOPOLITAN CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lb Chicken -- cut up
1 md Onion -- chopped
4 md Potatoes -- in 1" pieces
1 lg Green pepper -- in strips
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1 ts Salt
1 ds Pepper
1/2 ts Dried basil
1 lb Jar
Spaghetti sauce
Combine chicken, onion, potatoes, green pepper, parsley, garlic, salt,
pepper, basil and spaghetti sauce in Crockpot. Cook on Low for 6-8
hours or on High for 3-4 hours. Skim fat off top and serve in bowls.
Can omit potatoes and serve over spaghetti.
