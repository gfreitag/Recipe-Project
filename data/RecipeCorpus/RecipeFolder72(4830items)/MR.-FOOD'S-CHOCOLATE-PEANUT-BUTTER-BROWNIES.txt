Title: MR. FOOD'S CHOCOLATE PEANUT BUTTER BROWNIES
Categories: Desserts, Brownies
Yield: 9 Servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter
- (creamy or chunky)
1/4 c  (1/2 stick) butter, softened
1 c  Firmly packed light brown
- sugar
2 ea Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Self-rising flour
6 oz Semisweet chocolate chips
=======================================================
============== ==== == Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Beat peanut
butter, brown sugar & butter together until light & fluffy. Add eggs
one at a time, beating well after each addition. Blend in vanilla.
