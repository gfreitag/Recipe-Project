Title: MOCK CRAB CAKES
Categories: Cakes, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Grated zucchini
1 c  Italian bread crumbs
2    Eggs, beaten
1 tb Mayonnaise
1 ts Crab spice or to taste
Grated onion, optional
Grated celery, optional
Grate zucchini.  Mix all ingredients together.  Fry in skillet until
browned on both sides.
