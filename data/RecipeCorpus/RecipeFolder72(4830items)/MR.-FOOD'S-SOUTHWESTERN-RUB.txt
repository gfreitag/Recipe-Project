Title: MR. FOOD'S SOUTHWESTERN RUB
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 99 Servings
Season 2 pounds of meat
1 1/2 ts Chili powder
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Dried oregano
1/4 ts Dried cumin
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients.
Rub over 2 pounds of the meat of your choice and grill to desired
doneness.
