Title: MOROCCAN RED PEPPER SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Cayenne pepper
1 tb Ground cumin
2    Cloves garlic -- peeled
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Olive oil
Crush spices, garlic, and salt together (with mortar and pestle) to
form a paste.  Heat a frying pan, add the olive oil and spice
mixture. Cook, stirring constantly over medium heat, for 5 minutes.
Serve with couscous dishes.
