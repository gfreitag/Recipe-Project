Title: MARINATED VEGETABLES
Categories: Appetizers, Low-fat
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Fresh Broccoli
3/4 c  Carrots, cut diagonally
1 c  Celery, cut diagonally
1/2 c  Red pepper, sliced
1/4 c  Pitted olives*
1 cn 14-oz, artichoke hearts, in
-water
1/4 c  Fat-free Italian Dressing
1/8 ts Red pepper
Steam broccoli and carrots till crisp; rinse in cold water to stop the
cooking process.  Toss broccoli, carrots and remaining veggies in a
large bowl.  Add dressing and red pepper and toss again. Chill a few
hours; serve on a large platter.
