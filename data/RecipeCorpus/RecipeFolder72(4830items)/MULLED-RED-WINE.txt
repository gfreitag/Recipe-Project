Title: MULLED RED WINE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Serving
1 ts Superfine sugar
1/4 c  ;Water
5 oz Red wine
1 1/2 ts Lemon juice
1 ds Orange bitters
1 pn Ground cinnamon
Combine sugar, water in a small saucepan.  Stir over low heat until
sugar dissolves.  Remove from heat. Add wine, lemon juice, bitters,
cinnamon; stir.  Pour drink into a warmed 8 oz. mug. Mull with a red
hot poker.
