Title: MR. FOOD'S MYSTERY CRACKERS
Categories: Grains, Crackers
Yield: 99 Servings
Makes 4 cups
11 oz Oyster crackers
3/4 c  Butter-flavored popcorn oil
1 ea Envelope 0.6 ounces instant
- cream of chicken soup mix
1 ea Envelope (0.525 ounces)
- instant cream of mushroom
- soup mix
=======================================================
============== ==== == Pour crackers into a large (4-quart) airtight
container. Pour the oil over the crackers and stir gently. Sprinkle
the soup mixes over the mixture and continue to stir gently. Cover
and set aside for two hours, then stir again to completely coat the
cracke
