Title: MCDONALD'S PEPPERCORN DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings, Copycat
Yield: 6 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
1/2 c  Bottled buttermilk or ranch
1/4 c  Kraft's mayo
1 1/2 ts Dijon mustard
1/2 ts Coarse black pepper
ds Worcestershire
Mix all ingredients. Keep refrigerated and tightly covered to use in
10 days. Source: Gloria Pitzer.
