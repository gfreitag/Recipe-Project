Title: MIXED VEGETABLE & FRANKFURTER CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Fish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 pk Frozen veggies del sol(16oz)
2    Tomatoes,large,chopped
1/2 ts Paprika
Butter or margarine
1/3 c  Vegetable or olive oil
2    Garlic cloves,crushed
1 tb Edam cheese,grated
1 lb Frankfurters,skinless
1. Cook vegetables according to package directions; drain and spread
evenly in a 9-inch square baking dish. Set aside.
2. Combine oil, tomatoes, garlic and paprika is a saucepan; cook 20
minutes.
3. Pour over vegetables; sprinkle with grated cheese.
4. Dot with butter; lay sliced frankfurters on top.
5. Bake in preheated 400'F. oven 15 minutes, or until top is browned
and cheese completely melted.
NOTE: Vegetables del sol contains broccoli, carrots and cauliflower.
Frankfurters can either be beef or turkey.
