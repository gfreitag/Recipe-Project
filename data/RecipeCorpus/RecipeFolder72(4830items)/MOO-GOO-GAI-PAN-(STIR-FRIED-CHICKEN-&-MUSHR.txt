Title: MOO GOO GAI PAN (STIR-FRIED CHICKEN & MUSHR
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
8 tb Peanut oil
2    Green onions, sliced
1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms, sliced
2    Whole chicken breasts>>>>
Boned and sliced.
2 tb Soy sauce (more if desired)
1 ts Salt
Have all ingredients ready before beginning.  Heat 2 tbs. oil in a
large frying pan or wok.  When oil is very hot, stir-fry the onions
and mushrooms for 3 min. Remove to a bowl, and add 6 tbs. more oil to
pan. Stir-fry chicken until no longer pink, abt. 5 min. Return the
mushrooms and onions to the skillet, and add soy sauce and salt to
taste. Remove to a plate and serve immediately.  NOTE:  the chicken
pieces may be marinated for 15 to 30 min. before frying in a mixture
of 1 egg white, tsp. cornstarch, 2 tsp. dry sherry, and salt and
pepper to taste. This tenderizes the chicken and adds flavor.  Sliced
water chestnuts and snowpeas may be stir fried with the mushrooms.

Recipe:  The Continental Key Club, Fairmont, WV
