Title: MATZO MEAL DOUGHNUTS
Categories: Kosher, Desserts, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
2/3 c  Water
1/3 c  Shortening
1 c  Matzo meal
1 tb Sugar
3    Eggs
1/4 ts Salt
Boil fat, water, sugar and salt.  Add the matzo meal, stirring well.
Let mixture boil for a second.  Remove from fire and beat well. Add
eggs one at a time beating well after each addition. Take bits of the
dough into well greased hands and roll into balls 2 inches in
diameter. Dip finger in water and make a hole through the center of
erach ball. Place on a greased pan and bake at 375 - 400 F. for 1 hr.
Roll in Confectioners' sugar while hot.
