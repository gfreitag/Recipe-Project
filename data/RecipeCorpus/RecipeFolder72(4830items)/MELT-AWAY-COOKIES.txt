Title: MELT-AWAY COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Tested
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
1 ts Vanilla extract
2 1/4 c  Soft-a-silk cake flour
3/4 c  Walnut meal
Recipe by: Cindy Anderson Mix butter, sugar, and vanilla; then add
flour, then walnut meal. Roll into small balls and bake 6-8 minutes
at 400F. While still warm, roll in powdered sugar.
