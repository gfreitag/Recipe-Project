Title: MUSTARD DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Spreads
Yield: 1 Cup
1/2 c  Sour cream
1/2 c  Plain yogurt
1 tb Finely chopped fresh parsley
1 ts Onion powder
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1 tb Dijon mustard
Mix all ingredients. Cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour. Serve with
meatballs, cocktail sausages or fresh vegetables if desired.

20    calories per tablespoon.

Source: Betty Crocker's 40th Anniversary Edition Cookbook, page 19
