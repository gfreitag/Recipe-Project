Title: MEXICAN FRUIT CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
2 ea Eggs
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Crushed Pineapple
1 x  FROSTING IS NEXT
1/2 c  Stick cube oleo
1 x  Box powdered sugar
2 c  Flour
2 ts Soda
1 x  Frosting is next
8 oz Philadelphia Cream Cheese
1 ts Vanilla
Bake at 350 degrees in a greased & floured pan.
