Title: MEXICAN TEA PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Mexican
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Tequila
2 c  Tea; Strong, Cold
1 c  Pinapple Juice
1/4 c  Honey
1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Lime Juice
1/4 c  Lemon Juice
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon; Ground
1 1/2 ts Aromatic Bitters
Mix all ingredients; refrigerate until chilled.  Stir before serving.
Serve over ice.
