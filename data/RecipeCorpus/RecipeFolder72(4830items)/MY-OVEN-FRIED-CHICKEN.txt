Title: MY OVEN FRIED CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry, Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken parts
1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Fresh ground pepper
1/4 ts Paprika
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Onion powder
Preheat oven to 450 Combine all dry ingredients in a plastic bag.
Wash your chicken and shake off excess water.  Drop in bag and shake
each piece. Place in a metal pan skin side down and put in preheated
oven. Turn pieces over after about 20 minutes. Continue baking til
crisp and browned.
