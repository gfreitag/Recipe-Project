Title: OATMEAL BREAD *** (GDCM85A)
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 16 Servings
3/4 c  Instant oatmeal;
1/4 c  Whole wheat flour
2 c  Bread flour
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Molasses
1 1/2 ts Salt 1
1/2 tb Powdered milk; (use butterm
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Simple delicious recipe, used in Hitachi B/M- put oatmeal in first on
top of 1 3/16 C of water to let soak a few minutes longer. Let b/m do
its thing; have made +/- 20 loaves of this recipe, never a bad one.
