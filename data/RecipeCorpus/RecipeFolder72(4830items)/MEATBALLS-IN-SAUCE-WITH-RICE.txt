Title: MEATBALLS IN SAUCE WITH RICE
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2    Recipe Meatball mixture
-(See separate recipe.)
1/2 c  Tomato puree
1 tb Onion; chopped
2 ts Vinegar
1/2 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Oregano leaves
1 ds Pepper
2 tb Water
1 1/2 c  Rice, cooked, unsalted
2 servings of 6 meatballs, 1/4 cup sauce, and 3/4 cu rice each 440
calories per serving 1. Thaw frozen meatball mixture in refrigerator.

2. Divide into 12 portions. Shape into balls.

3. Brown meatballs on all sides in hot frypan. Drain.

4. Mix remaining ingredients except rice. Pour over meatballs.

5. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and boil gently until sauce
thickens--about 10 minutes. Spoon sauce over meatballs once during
cooking.

6. Serve over rice. * Thrifty Meals for Two: Making Food Dollars
