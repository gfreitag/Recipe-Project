Title: MUSHROOM SAUCE FOR STEAKS
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Mushrooms, undrained>>>>>
Either sliced, or stems &>>
Pieces.
1 c  Water, divided
1/2 x  Envelope, onion soup mix
2 tb Cornstarch
Combine mushrooms with juice, onion soup mix and 3/4 cup water.
Bring to a boil.  Mix cornstarch and remaining water; add to mushroom
mixture and thicken.
