Title: NO-HASSLE WASSAIL -FGGT98B
Categories: Beverages, German, American
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ga Apple cider
2 ea Quarts apricot nectar
3 c  Dark rum
1 1/2 c  Firmly packed brown
1 x  Sugar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
6 ea (3-inch) sticks cinnamon
3 ea Sm Oranges
1 1/2 ea Teaspoons whole cloves
COOKING WITH CLASS--FREDERICK COMMUNITY COLLEGE, FREDERICK, MD Combine
cider, nectar, rum, brown sugar, lemon juice, and cinnamon sticks in a
large Dutch oven. Bring to a boil; reduce heat, and simmer,
uncovered, 1 chour.  Stud oranges with cloves, and add to cider
mixture. Serve hot. YIELD:  1-1/2 gallons
