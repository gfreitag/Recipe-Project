Title: NUTRITIONAL YEAST PARMESAN
Categories: Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Nutritional yeast flakes
1/4 c  Sesame seeds
1/4 ts Salt
Grind all ingredients in food processor or blender until completely
milled. Store in glass jar in refrigerator for months. Use over
spaghetti, sprinkled over salads, or in any recipe where you would
use parmesan cheese.
