Title: MARLBORO COUNTRY GUACAMOLE
Categories: Salads, Tex-mex, Mexican
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea Large ripe avocado (about 8
1/4 c  Finely chopped onion
1 tb Seeded and finely chopped pi
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
Peel, pit and mash avocado; add remaining ingredients and mix
thoroughly. Makes about a cup.* From "Famouse Chili Recipes From
Marlboro Country". We like to put a little chopped fresh onion in our
guacamole too.
