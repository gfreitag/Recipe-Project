Title: MOLHO APIMENTADO  - SPICY SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Molho Campanha (see recipe)
1/2 c  Cooking liquid from the
-feijoada's beans
3    Or 4 preserved malagueta
-peppers, minced, to taste
Mix all of the ingredients together in a nonreactive bowl and serve
at room temperature with the feijoada.
