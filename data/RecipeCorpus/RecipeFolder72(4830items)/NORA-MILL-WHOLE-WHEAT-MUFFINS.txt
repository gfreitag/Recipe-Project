Title: NORA MILL WHOLE WHEAT MUFFINS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
2 c  NORA MILL whole wheat m. mix
2 ea Eggs
1 c  Buttermilk (milk is okay)
1/2 ea Melted butter or margarine
Preheat oven to 475 degrees F.  Grease muffin tins. Beat eggs and
butter- milk (or milk).  Then add in butter. Stir in mix (do not over
mix, leave it lumpy).  Divide into muffin tins and bake for 20
minutes. Muffins should be firm and pulling away from the sides of
pan.
