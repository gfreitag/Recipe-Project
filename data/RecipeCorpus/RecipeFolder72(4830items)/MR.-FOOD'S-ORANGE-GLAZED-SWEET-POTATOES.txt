Title: MR. FOOD'S ORANGE-GLAZED SWEET POTATOES
Categories: Vegetables, Potatoes
Yield: 6 Servings
2 ts Grated orange rind
3 tb Melted butter
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Orange juice
1 tb Cornstarch or
- all-purpose flour
1/4 c  Firmly packed brown sugar
1 pn Salt
6 md Sweet potatoes, peeled,
- boiled, and cut into
- 1-inch cubes
======================================================= ======

medium heat, combine all the ingredients except the potatoes. Heat
