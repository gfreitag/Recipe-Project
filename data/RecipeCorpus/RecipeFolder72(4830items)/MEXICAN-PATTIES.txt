Title: MEXICAN PATTIES
Categories: Beef, Ground meat, Mexican, Try
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Pretzel crumbs
1/2 c  Chopped onion
4 oz Can green chiles
-deseeded and chopped
2 tb Chorizo sausage, chopped
1 tb Chili seasoning
2    Cloves garlic, crushed
8 oz Monterey Jack Cheese
2    Green peppers
Preheat oven to 375F. Combine all ingredients, except cheese and the 2
green peppers, in a medium bowl until well blended. Make 10 meatballs
with a 1 inch cube of Jack cheese in the center of each. Cut peppers
into quarters and remove seeds. Place meatballs into the center of
each pepper and bake 20-30 minutes.

Serves 4-6.

Source: The back of the Bachman's Pretzel Bag
