Title: NEW ENGLAND CLAM CHOWDER***FGGT98B
Categories: Soups, Mw, American
Yield: 6 Servings
3    Slices raw bacon
8 oz Minced clam
Canned undraind
1 1/2 c  Peeled potatoes
Cubed
1/3 c  Chopped onion
2 tb Flour
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Light cream
Or undiluted milk
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/4 ts Fresh chives
Chopped
3 tb Sherry
1. Place bacon in a deep 2-qut. heat-resistant, non-metallic
casserole. 2. Cook at FULL POWER, covered with a paper towel, 4
minutes, or until bacon is crisp. 3. Remove cooked bacon with a
slotted spoon. Crumble bacon and set aside. Reserve drippings in
casserole. 4. Drain liquid from clams and add to bacon drippings. Set
clams aside. 5. Add potatoes and onions to casserole. 6. Cook at FULL
POWER covered in microwave oven 8 minutes or until vegetables are
tender; stir occasionally. 7. Blend flour into vegetable mixture.  8.
Gradually stir in milk until smooth. 9. Cook at FULL POWER,
uncovered, in microwave oven 4 minutes or until thickened and smooth.
10. Stir in cream, salt, pepper and reserved clams and sherry. 11.
Cook at FULL POWER, uncovered, in microwave oven 4 minutes. Garnish
with crumbled bacon and chives. Source: Micro Cookbook
