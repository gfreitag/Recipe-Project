Title: NUTMEG PECAN CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
2 c  Unbleached flour
1/2 c  Butter
1    Egg, lightly beaten
1 ts Nutmeg; freshly grated
1 c  Sour cream
1 ts Baking soda
3/4 c  Pecans; chopped
Mix together sugar and flour, then cut butter into small pieces and
work into sugar and flour with your fingers to make coarse crumbs.
Put half the crumbs in the bottom of a 9" greased baking dish. Stir
in egg and nutmeg to remaining crumbs. Mix sour cream with soda and
add to crumb mixture. Spoon batter into dish and top with nuts. Bake
in a preheated 350 degree oven for 35 to 40 minutes.
