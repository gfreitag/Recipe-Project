Title: NEAPOLITAN PORK CHOPS
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Olive oil
3    Cl Garlic; minced
6    Lean pork chops
Salt and pepper
1    Onion; chopped
1 c  Beef broth
Worcestershire sauce
Chili powder
6 tb Tomato paste
6 tb White wine
1    Green pepper; chopped
1 lb Fresh mushrooms; sliced
Heat oil.  Brown garlic, add pork chops and brown. Add remaining
ingredients, cover and simmer 30-35 minutes.
