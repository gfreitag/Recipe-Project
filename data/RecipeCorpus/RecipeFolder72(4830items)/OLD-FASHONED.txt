Title: OLD FASHONED
Categories: Cocktails
Yield: 1 Servings
2 fl Bourbon whiskey
1 ea Sugar cube, small
1 ds Angoustora bitters
1 ts Water
Place sugar, bitters and water in old fashoned glass. Muddle well. Add
whiskey and stir. Add ice cubes and a twist of lemon peel. Garnish
with lemon and orange slice, and a cherry. Serve with a swizzle stick.
