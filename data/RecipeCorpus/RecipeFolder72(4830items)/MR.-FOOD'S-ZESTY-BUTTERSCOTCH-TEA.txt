Title: MR. FOOD'S ZESTY BUTTERSCOTCH TEA
Categories: Beverages, Adult
Yield: 1 Servings
To 1 cup of hot tea add:
2    Butterscotch hard candies
1 tb Honey
1/2 ts Lemon juice
1    Cinnamon stick
======================================================= ====== Stir
until candies melt, or remove any remaining pieces before drinking
Remember: Never drink and drive!
