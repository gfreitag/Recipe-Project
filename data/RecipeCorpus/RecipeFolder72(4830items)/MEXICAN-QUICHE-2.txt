Title: MEXICAN QUICHE 2
Categories: Mexican, Eggs/cheese
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Tortilla, 6" flour
3 oz Sausage
1    Egg
1/8 ts Salt
2 oz Cheese, jack
1 tb Green pepper, chopped
1/4 c  Milk
Heat tortilla and place in small greased dish. Top with half of the
cheese. Cook sausage and pepper and add to dish. Mix together
remaining ingredients and pour over dish. Top with rest of cheese.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes.
