Title: OATMEAL APPLE RAISIN MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Muffins, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1 ea Egg
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Raisins
1 ea Chopped apple
1/2 c  Oil
1 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Quick oats
1/3 c  Sugar
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Nutmeg
2 ts Cinnamon
Beat egg; stir in remaining ingredients, mixing just enough to
moisten. Pour into 12 greased muffin cups until 3/4 full. Bake at
400Ã¸ for 15 to 20 minutes. Serve cool or piping hot with butter.
