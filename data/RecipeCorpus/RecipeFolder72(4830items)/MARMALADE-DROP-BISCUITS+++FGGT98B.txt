Title: MARMALADE DROP BISCUITS+++FGGT98B
Categories: Master mix, Biscuits, Jewel
Yield: 9 Servings
1    Baking powder drop recipe
2 tb Orange marmalade
After Baking Powder Drop Biscuit dough has been dropped onto
cooky sheet, make a small dent in top of each. Add a dab of orange
marmalade. Bake.  Two Tablespoons marmalade are sufficient for the
whole recipe. Yield:  9 to 10 biscuits
