Title: MARINATED WAKAME
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ts EDEN Wakame Flakes
3 tb Water or stock
2 tb EDEN Mirin
Soak flakes in liquid for 10 minutes. Simmer or steam over low heat
for 5 minutes. Delicious in salads or as a bed for grilled fish.
Garnish with mayonnaise for a taste treat!

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cooking Time: 5 minutes Yields: 4-5 servings
