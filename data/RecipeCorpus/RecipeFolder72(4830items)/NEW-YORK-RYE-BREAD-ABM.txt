Title: NEW YORK RYE BREAD ABM
Categories: Cyberealm, Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 1/2 ts Dry yeast
2 c  Bread flour
1 c  Rye flour
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Melted butter
3 ts Caraway seeds
1/2 ts Salt
7/8 c  Warm water
Add the ingredients to the pan in the order listed. Select "white
bread". Select "medium color". Press start.

From Mike Coticchio. Posted by Joel Erlich.
