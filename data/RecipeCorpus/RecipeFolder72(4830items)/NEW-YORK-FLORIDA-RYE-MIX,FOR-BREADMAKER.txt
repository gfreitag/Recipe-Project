Title: NEW YORK-FLORIDA RYE MIX/FOR BREADMAKER
Categories: Mixes, Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
1 qt Rye flour +
1/2 c  Gluten*
3 qt Bread flour *
1/2 c  Buttermilk  powder
1/4 c  Sugar
4 ts Salt
6 tb Caraway seeds
This is a mix recipe; divide dry ingredients by four and follow the
recipe and instructions on NEW YORK-FLORIDA RYE. for your breadmaker.
