Title: MARYANNE'S CHEESECAKE
Categories: Desserts, Silverman, Cheesecakes
Yield: 1 Cake
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Flour
3 tb Cornstarch
32 oz Cream cheese (softened)
1    Stick butter (softened)
1 tb Vanilla
1 pt Sour cream
-lemon rind
Crust

2 c  graham
1/2 c  sugar
2/3 c  melted salt butter

mix crust ingredients and fill a large spring pan. Beat filling
together until smooth, Fill crust, Bake 90 min @ 225 degrees Open
door & let cool slowly, Best made 2-3 days in advance
