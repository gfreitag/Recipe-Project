Title: MINTED CANTALOUP & BLUEBERRIES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
2 tb Mint; crushed
1 c  Blueberries
2 c  Cantaloupe balls;
x  Mint; for garnish
Recipe by: Home Cooking,  8/94 Combine sugar, water and crushed mint
in saucepan.  Boil for 3 min. Strain and chill. Add blueberries and
cantaloupe balls to syrup. Serve either as a salad or with whipped
topping for dessert.

Serves 3 - 4
