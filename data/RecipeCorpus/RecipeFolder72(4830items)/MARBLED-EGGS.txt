Title: MARBLED EGGS
Categories: Chinese, Eggs, Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Eggs
2 tb Indian tea leaves
Hard boil the eggs for 10 minutes, then slightly crack the shells all
round but do not remove the shell. Boil tea leaves in 1/2 litre water
for 5 minutes. Immerse the cracked eggs in the tea for 45 minutes or
until cool. When you remove the shell where the cracks where will be
a dark line and make them look as if they are made of marble.
