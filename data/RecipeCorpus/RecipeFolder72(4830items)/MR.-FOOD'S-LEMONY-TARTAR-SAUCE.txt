Title: MR. FOOD'S LEMONY TARTAR SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 99 Servings
1 1/4 cups
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Pickle relish, drained
1 tb Chopped scallions
4 ts Fresh lemon juice
=======================================================
============== ==== == In a small bowl, combine all the ingredients
and mix well. Cover and chill until ready to serve. NOTE: This will
last for 2 to 3 weeks in the refrigerator if covered tightly - so go
ahead and make a double batch!
