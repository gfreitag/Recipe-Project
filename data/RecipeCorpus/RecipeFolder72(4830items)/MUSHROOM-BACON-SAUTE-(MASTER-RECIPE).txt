Title: MUSHROOM BACON SAUTE (MASTER RECIPE)
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 3 Servings
12    Bacon Slices -- 12 Oz
1 1/2 lb Fresh mushrooms --  sliced
About 8 cups
1 c  Onions -- chopped
1 1/2 tb Garlic --  minced
2 ts Oregano leaves --  crushed
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Ground black pepper
Use this recipe to add to: salads, pasta, rice, pizza, meats, etc. In
a large skillet over medium heat cook bacon until crisp, about 5
minutes. Transfer to a paper towel; crumble, set aside. Pour off all
but 4 tablespoons bacon   fat. Add mushrooms, onions and garlic;
cook, stirring occasionally, until most of the liquid evaporates, 7
to 8 minutes. Stir in crumbled bacon, oregano, salt and black pepper,
recipes.
Refrigerate or freeze in 2 portions and use in
