Title: NUT RAISIN BREAD ( MRS. LYONS' RECIPE)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
4 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 ea Egg
1 c  Walnuts chopped
1 c  Raisins
Mix dry ingredients, add milk and beaten egg, then nuts and
raisins.Put in greased loaf pan and let stand 20 minutes. Bake 1 hour
in 350 oven if using pyrex pan, 375 oven if using metal pan.
