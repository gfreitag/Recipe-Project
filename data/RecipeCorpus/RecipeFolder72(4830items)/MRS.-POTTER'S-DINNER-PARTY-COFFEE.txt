Title: MRS. POTTER'S DINNER PARTY COFFEE
Categories: Wasnone
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Very hot dicaffeinated
Coffee -- instant O.K.
2 tb Sugar
1/4 c  Rum -- light OR dark
Combine very hot coffee, sugar and rum in heated pot. Makes 4-6
demitasse for 4-6. Double as needed.
