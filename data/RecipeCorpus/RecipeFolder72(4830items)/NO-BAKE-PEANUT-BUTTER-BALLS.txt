Title: NO-BAKE PEANUT BUTTER BALLS
Categories: Bakery, Cookies, Desserts, Vegetarian
Yield: 50 Balls
1/2 c  Dates, chopped
1/2 c  Raisins, chopped
1 c  Walnuts, coarsely chopped
1/4 c  Molasses
1 c  Peanut butter
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Shredded coconut
Combine dates, raisins & nuts in a medium sized bowl. Stir in the
molasses. Add peanut butter, stirring until well combined. Stir in 2
tb of the orange juice. Add enough of the remaining orange juice to
form a moist mixture that can be formed into balls.  Roll into 1/2"
balls & roll each ball into the shredded coconut. Store in an
airtight container out of direct sunlight.  Let sit for at least 1
day before serving.
