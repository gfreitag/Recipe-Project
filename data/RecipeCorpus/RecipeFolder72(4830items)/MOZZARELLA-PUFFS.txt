Title: MOZZARELLA PUFFS
Categories:
Yield: 10 Servings
1    Tube refrigerated buttermilk
-biscuits
1 ts Dried oregano
3 oz Mozzarella cheese
2 tb Pizza sauce
Make an indentation in the center of each biscuit; sprinkle with
oregano. Cut the mozzarella into 10 cubes, 3/4 inch each; place a
cube in the center of each biscuit. Pinch dough tightly around cheese
to seal. Place seam side down on an ungreased baking sheet. Spread
pizza sauce over tops. Bake at 375 for 10-12 minutes or until golden
brown.

: Taste Of Home June/July '96
