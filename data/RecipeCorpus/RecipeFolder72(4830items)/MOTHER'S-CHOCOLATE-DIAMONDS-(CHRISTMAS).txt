Title: MOTHER'S CHOCOLATE DIAMONDS (CHRISTMAS)
Categories: Cookies, Holidays
Yield: 1 Pan
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
3 x  Unsweetened choc.; squares
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 c  Flour
1 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Pecans; chopped
Oven 400~.

Grease 9x13x2 inch baking pan.  Melt butter and chocolate. Stir in
sugar.  Add eggs and beat well. Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
Bake at 400~ for 15 min. Let cool in pan. Cut into diamonds. Store in
tin box between layers of wax paper.
