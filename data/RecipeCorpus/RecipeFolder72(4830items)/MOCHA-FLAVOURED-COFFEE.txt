Title: MOCHA FLAVOURED COFFEE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Nondairy creamer
-dry
1/3 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Dry instant coffee
2    Tblsp cocoa
Place all ingredients in mixer, beat at high until well blended. Mix
1 1/2 Tblsp mix w/ 3/4 cup hot water. Store in air tight jar.

14 servings From: Ronda Eikenberry
