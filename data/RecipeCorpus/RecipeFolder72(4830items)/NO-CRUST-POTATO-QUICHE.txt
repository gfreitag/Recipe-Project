Title: NO CRUST POTATO QUICHE
Categories: Cheese, Cheese/eggs, Potatoes, Quiche
5    eggs -- beaten
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  cheddar cheese -- shredded
3/4 c  cottage cheese -- small
curd
:
12 oz package frozen hash brown
potatoes -- unthawed
:
1/4 ts salt
1/4 ts black pepper -- freshly
ground
:
1 ds tobasco sauce
1 ds paprika
6 TB bacon -- crumbled

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.  Combine everything except bacon &
paprika. Pour into greased 9" pie plate. Sprinkle with paprika Bake
at 350-degree oven, 25 minutes or until set. Remove from oven,
sprinkle with bacon, bake another 5 minutes.
