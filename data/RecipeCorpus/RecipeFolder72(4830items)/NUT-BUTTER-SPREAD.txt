Title: NUT BUTTER SPREAD
Categories: Spreads
Yield: 2 Cups
2 c  Nuts; cashews, pecans,
-walnuts, almonds
1 c  Sunflower seed kernels
1 c  Sesame seeds
Oil
Honey; to taste
Place the nuts, sunflower seed kernels and seeds in an electric
blender and blend until fine. Add enough oil to blend them to a
paste. Sweeten with honey.

Store in covered jar in the refrigerator. Yield: about two cups.

Source: "The New York Times Natural Foods Cookbook" by Jean Hewitt
