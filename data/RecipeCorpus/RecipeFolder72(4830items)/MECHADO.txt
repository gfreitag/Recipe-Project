Title: MECHADO
Categories: Beef, Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Beef top round; whole
14    Cooking oil
4    Garlic; sliced thinly
1    Onion; sliced
2    Potato; quartered
; fried
1    Carrot; cut into  2" pieces
1    Lime; sliced with rind
2    Soy sauce
12    Peppercorns; crushed
1    Tomato sauce
1    Bay leaf
1    Salt
1 1/2    Hot water
Recipe by: Nila Sweeney Preparation Time: 1:00 1. Brown meat in
cooking oil, set aside.

2. In the same oil, saute garlic and onion.  But in lime, soy,
pepper, tom sauce, bay leaf and salt. Simmer for 3-5min.

3. Add meat and water.  Cover and simmer until meat is tender.

4. Add potatoes and carrot.  When vegetables are done, slice meat.
Arrang on a platter with vegetables. Strain sauce and pout over meat.
