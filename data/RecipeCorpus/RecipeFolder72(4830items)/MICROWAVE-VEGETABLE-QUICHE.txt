Title: MICROWAVE VEGETABLE QUICHE
Categories: Main dishes, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Zucchini -- sliced
12    Mushroom -- sliced
1 c  Peppers -- sliced
1 lb Spinach -- cooked and
Chopped
4    Eggs -- beaten
3/4 c  Cheddar cheese -- grated
1 c  Mozzarella cheese -- grated
1/2 c  Cream
1 tb Flour
1 tb Oil
2 ts Dill -- chopped
1/4 c  Butter
Paprika
In 8 cup casserole, combine veggies and butter.  Cover and cook at
100% for 5-7 minutes, stirring once midway. Add spinach and set
aside. In another bowl, mix egg, flour, cream,oil, salt, pepper and
cheddar cheese. Pour over veggis, cover with mozzarella, sprinkle
with paprica. Put on a raised rack and cook at 70% for 15-17 minutes.
Let stand 5 minutes. From Pat Crafts
