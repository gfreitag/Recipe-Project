Title: NO KNEAD ITALIAN WHOLE WHEAT BREAD
Categories: Bakery, Breads
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 tb Dry yeast
1 c  Warm water
1 ts Salt
2 tb Vegetable oil
3 c  Whole wheat flour
Cornmeal
In a bowl, dissolve yeast in water.  Add salt & oil. Beat in flour, 1
c at a time.  Use your fingers to ensure that the ingredients have
been thoroughly mixed.

Bang down the dough on a counter surface.  Shape into a small round
bread & place in a 9" round pan sprinkled with cornmeal. Allow to
rise till doubled. Preheat oven to 375F & bake for 35 to 45 minutes.

Anne Lerner, "Breads You Wouldn't Believe"
