Title: NUT ROLLS
Categories: Ethnic, Breads, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
5 c  Flour (sifted)
1 ts Salt
1/2 lb Butter
1 pk Yeast
1/2 lb Crisco
1/2 c  Milk
3 ea Eggs
1/2 pt Sour Cream
1 ts Baking Powder
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/4 lb Butter
1 lb Ground nuts
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Sugar (to taste)
Sift flour, baking powder and salt; work in butter and crisco
with hands. Add eggs and work together. Add yeast to dough. Add milk
and sour cream. Knead for a few minutes. Set aside for about 2 hours
to rise. Roll out on board which has been covered with sugar. Fill
with nut filling. Bake in moderate oven about 25 minutes.
Filling: Melt butter in pan. Add honey and cook for a few
minutes. Add ground nuts and sugar. Cool.
Original recipe called for "cake yeast". You might want to start
the dry yeast in a small amount of lukewarm water before adding.
