Title: MR. FOOD'S SPICY SPUD CHICKEN
Categories: Meats, Chicken
Yield: 3 Servings
1/3 c  Butter, melted
2/3 c  Instant mashed potato
- flakes
3 tb Dry taco seasoning mix
1/8 ts Cayenne pepper
1/3 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
2 1/2 lb To 3 lb chicken,
- cut into eighths
======================================================= ======

shallow dish. In a large bowl, combine the remaining ingredients
except the chicken. Dip the chicken pieces into the melted butter,
