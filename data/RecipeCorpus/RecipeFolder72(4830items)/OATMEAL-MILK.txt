Title: OATMEAL MILK
Categories: Beverages, Low-fat, Mcdougall
Yield: 6 Servings
4 c  Water
2 c  Oatmeal; cooked
1    Banana
1 ts Vanilla
Recipe by: McDougall Preparation Time: 0:05 Place all ingredients in
blender and process until smooth. Refrigerate. Shake before using.
