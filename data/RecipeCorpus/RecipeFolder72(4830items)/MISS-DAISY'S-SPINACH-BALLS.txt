Title: MISS DAISY'S SPINACH BALLS
Categories: Low fat, Appetizers
1 pk frozen chopped spinach --
(10 ounce)
Yield: 5 Servings
:
1/2 c  very fine breadcrumbs
1    egg -- lightly beaten
1/4 c  part-skim ricotta cheese
1/4 c  grated  cheese
3    green onions -- chopped
fine
:
1/4 ts ground cumin
2 ts freshly squeezed lemon
juice
:

Assemble all ingredients and utensils. Cook the spinach according to
the package directions. Drain and squeeze out the excess water. Stir
in the remaining ingredients and shape the mixture into 1-inch balls.
Arrange the balls on a nonstick baking sheet. Bake in a 400 degree
oven for 12 to 15 minutes or until lightly browned. Yields 15
appetizers or 5 servings of 3 appetizers each. Typos by Brenda Adams
