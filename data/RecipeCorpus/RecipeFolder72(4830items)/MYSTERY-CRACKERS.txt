Title: MYSTERY CRACKERS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
11 oz Oyster crackers
3/4 c  Buttery Popping Oil for
-popcorn
1 1/2    Envelopes Cup O'Soup Cream
-of Chicken
1 tb Parsley
1 ts Garlic powder
Pour crackers into an air-tight container. Pour oil over crackers.
Stir gently with wooden spoon. Sprinkle soup, garlic powder and
parsley into crackers and stir gently. Stir every so often until oil
is absorbed. Store in container.
