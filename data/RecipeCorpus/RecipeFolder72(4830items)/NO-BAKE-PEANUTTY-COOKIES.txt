Title: NO-BAKE PEANUTTY COOKIES
Categories: Desserts, Easy
Yield: 5 Dozen
2 c  Roasted Honey nut Skippy
Creamy or Super Chunk Peanut
-butter
2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1 c  Confectioners' sugar
1/2 c  Karo Light or Dark Corn
-Syrup
1/4 c  Semisweet chocolate chips,
-melted
Colored sprinkles (optional)
In large bowl, combine peanut butter, graham cracker crumbs,
confectioners' sugar and corn syrup.  Mix until smooth. Shape into 1
inch balls. Place on waxed-paper-lined cookie sheet.

Drizzle melted chocolate over balls; roll in colored sprinkles, if
desired. Store covered in refrigerator. Makes about 5 dozen cookies.
