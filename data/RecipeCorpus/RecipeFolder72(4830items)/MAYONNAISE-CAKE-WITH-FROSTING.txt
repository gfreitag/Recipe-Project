Title: MAYONNAISE CAKE WITH FROSTING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Flour
1 c  Sugar
1/3 c  Cocoa
1/8 ts Salt
2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Water -- lukewarm
1 ts Vanilla
Frosting:
1    Box powdered sugar
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Cocoa
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Cream
2 tb Perked coffee
Cake:  Sift dry ingredients.  Add mayonnaise and water; stir well. Add
vanilla.  Pour into greased and floured pan (9x13?) and bake at 350F
for 25 minutes.

Frosting:  Beat ingredients well and spread on cooled cake.

: Bell's Best #2 (Mississippi)
