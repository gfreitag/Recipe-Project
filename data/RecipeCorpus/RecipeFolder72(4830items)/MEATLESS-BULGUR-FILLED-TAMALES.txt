Title: MEATLESS BULGUR-FILLED TAMALES
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Tex-mex
Yield: 9 Servings
1    Recipe Basic Masa Dough
--see recipe
18    Corn husks or parchment
--papers
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1 c  Bulgur wheat, uncooked
8    Tomatoes, sun dried, minced
1/4 c  Onion, dried
2 3/4 c  Boiling water
4 tb Miso, brown rice
2 tb Arrowroot powder
To make filling:

a. In a medium sized mixing bowl, combine bulgur, tomatoes, pepper
and onion.
b. Add boiling water. Stir and cover with a dinner plate. Let sit
for 15 minutes.
c.  Add miso and arrowroot, stirring thoroughly.
d.  Fill and cook tamales acording to "Tamales Basic Procedure".
(See recipe).

Per serving: (2 tamales) 384 cal, 4 g prot, 420 mg sod, 81 g carb, 3
g. fat 0 mg chol, 17 mg calcium

From _Vegetarian Gourmet_ Summer 1993
