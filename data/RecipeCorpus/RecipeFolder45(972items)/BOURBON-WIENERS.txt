Title: BOURBON WIENERS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 2 Servings
1 pk Ballpark brand wieners
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 c  Catsup
3/4 c  Bourbon, Jack Daniels
Dry mustard to taste
Mix everything, hic!, except wieners.  Cut wieners, no pun intended.
Add and simmer for 1 hour, the longer the better! Serve and enjoy.
