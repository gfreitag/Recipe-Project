Title: BRAIN CELL SALAD^
Categories: Crs, Kids, Hlween, Gross
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk (6oz) blueberry jello mix
1 ct (16oz) small curd cottage
Cheese
1 cn (16 1/2oz) can blueberries
In syrup -=OR=-
3/4 c  Frozen blueberries, thawed
Blue food coloring
MMMMM---------------------------TOOLS--------------------------------
2    Mixing bowls
cn Opener
Spoon
6    Salad plates
With an adult's help, prepare jello according to package directions.
Chill 4-5 hours or until firm.

Scoop cottage cheese into a bowl. Drain and set aside the syrup from
the blueberries. Add the berries to the cottage cheese and mix well.
Add three drops of food coloring to turn the corrage cheese a nice
grayish color with blended.

To serve salad, place a few spoonfuls of firm gelatin, (congealed
brain fluid), onto individual serving plates. Top with a scoop of
cottage cheese (brain tissue) mixture and serve. Serves 6 psycho
surgeons.
