Title: BRAZIL NUT DELIGHTS
Categories: Snack
Yield: 20 Prune-nuts
20    Pitted prunes
20    Raw Brazil nuts
20    1x1/4" long slivers of
-lemon zest (outside peel
-of 1 lemon)
1/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
Insert 1 piece zest and 1 nut in each prune. Blend sugar and
cinnamon. Roll prune in sugar. Serve as dessert or snack.

VARIATION: Use any combination of walnuts, almonds, dates, dried figs.

Per prune-nut: 56 calories, 0.9 gram protein, 7.3 grams
carbohydrates, 1.2 gram fiber, 3.2 grams fat (0.8 saturated, 1.1
monounsaturated), 0.5 milligrams sodium.
