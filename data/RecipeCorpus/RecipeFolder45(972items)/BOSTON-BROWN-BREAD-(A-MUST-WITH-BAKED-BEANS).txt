Title: BOSTON BROWN BREAD (A MUST WITH BAKED BEANS)
Categories: Breads
Yield: 10 Servings
1/2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Corn Meal
3/4 c  Dark molasses
2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Sugar
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
3 c  Whole wheat flour
1    Egg
1/2 c  Melted shortening
Sift flour, baking soda, sugar, and salt together. Mix in corn meal
and whole wheat flour.  Add remaining ingredients, mixing only untill
all flour is moistened. Pour into 2 greased 9x5x3 inch loaf pans.
Bake at 300F for 1 hour and 15 minutes.  Remove from pans and cool.
