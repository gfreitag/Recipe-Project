Title: BRAZILIAN RICE
Categories: Rice, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk Frozen spinach -- chopped
1 c  Rice -- cooked
1/4 c  Butter or margarine --
Melted
4    Eggs -- beaten
1 lb Cheese -- grated
1 c  Milk
1 tb Onion -- chopped
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Rosemary
Cook spinach without seasoning and drain well. Combine with remaining
ingredients and bake in a 2-quart baking dish at 350 for 30 minutes.
Cut into squares and serve. May be prepared one day in advance.
