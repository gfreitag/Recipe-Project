Title: BOUQUET GARNI - LL
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Parsley stalk
1    Bay leaf
2    Sprigs thyme
1    Sprig marjoram (optional)
Combine all ingredients in small piece of muslin or double thickness
of cheesecloth; tie securely with string. Source: The Encyclopedia of
Creative Cooking, edited by Charlotte Turgeon.
