Title: BOURBON BARBECUE SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Butter
1 tb Oil, olive
2 c  Onions, chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
1/2 c  Molasses
1 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Vinegar, wine, red
1 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Black pepper
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Grated lemon rind
1 tb Soy sauce
1 tb Hungarian paprika
1/3 c  Bourbon
In large, heavy saucepan, melt butter with oil.  Saute onions until
tender. Add garlic and cook 2 minutes longer.

Combine molasses, catsup, vinegar, mustard, pepper, lemon juice, lemon
rind, soy sauce, paparika, and bourbon. Stir into onion mixture.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Cool and refrigerate in covered container.
