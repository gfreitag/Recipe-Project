Title: BRAZILIAN HOLIDAY CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  SKINNED, BONED, CHICKEN   *
1 ea LARGE ONION, SLICED
1 1/2 ea GREEN PEPPER, DICED
1 ea RED PEPPER
1/2 ts GARLIC POWDER
1 ea BAY LEAF
1 ts GARLIC POWDER
1/4 ts CUMIN
1/4 ts OREGANO
30 oz TOMATO SAUCE
15 oz SAUCE CAN WATER
1 tb FRESH LEMON JUICE
5 c  CHOPPED CELERY
CHICKEN BREAST MEAT CUT INTO 1" SQUARE PIECES, BONELESS,
*
SKINLESS *------------------------------------------------------
~------------- ~-* COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS EXCEPT CELERY IN A LARGE
SAUCE PAN. BRING TO A BOIL. REDUCE HEAT, COVER AND SIMMER FOR 1 HOUR
AND 20 MINUTES. ADD CELERY AND COOK FOR 25 MINUTES MORE OT UNTIL
CELERY IS TENDER CRISP. SERVE WITH RICE.
