Title: BONNIE'S BREAD PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
2    Eggs, beaten
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/4 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  Bread or cinnamon roll cubes
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Raisins

Mix eggs, milk, vanilla, cinnamon, sugar and salt together. Stir in
raisins. Pour over bread cubes in a buttered casserole dish. Bake in
275 - 300 degree oven until all bubbly and done.
