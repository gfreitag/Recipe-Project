Title: BREADED CUTLETS
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Cajun
Yield: 6 Servings
1    Egg
1 tb Water
2 ts Minced chives
1/4 c  Flour
1 c  Soft bread crumbs
Lemon wedges (optional)
2 tb Dijon style mustard
6 sl Cooked turkey, chicken or
Roast beef or pork roast.
Cooking oil
Cut meat slices 1/4" thick.  Beat egg, mustard, water and chives
together.  Dredge meat slices in flour, dip in egg mixture, coat with
crumbs.  Fry meat slices in 1/4" hot cooking oil in skillet until
golden on both sides, turning once.  Add more oil, if needed. Serve
with lemon wedges.
