Title: BREAKFAST SAUSAGE
Categories: Meats, Breakfast
Yield: 10 Servings
5 tb SALT
1 tb GROUND RED PEPPER
1 tb PEPPER
2 tb RUBBED SAGE
1 ts GINGER
1 tb NUTMEG
2 c  ICE WATER OR TOMATO Juice
GRIND THE MEAT THROUGH A 3/16 GRINDER PLATE AND MIX WELL WITH THE
OTHER INGREDIENTS. STUFF THE CASINGS AND BE SURE NOT TO LEAVE AT ROOM
TEMP. TOO LONG.
LORNA- N.J.
