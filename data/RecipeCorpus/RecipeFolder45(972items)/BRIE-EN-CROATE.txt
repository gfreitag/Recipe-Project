Title: BRIE EN CROATE
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese
Yield: 5 Servings
1 ea Brie round
1 ea Pasrty dough
1 ea Egg
2 tb Milk
Completty enclose cheese in pastry.  Make egg wash of egg and
milk. Put brie on baking sheet with seam side of dough down. Brush
with egg wash. Cut garnish of leaves, flowers, etc., from scraps of
pastry and decorate top of Brie.  Bake at 350F about 30 minutes or
until golden brown.
