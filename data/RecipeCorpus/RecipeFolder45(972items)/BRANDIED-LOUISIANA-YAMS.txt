Title: BRANDIED LOUISIANA YAMS
Categories: Kosher, Cajun, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
4 tb Margarine, pareve
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1 ts Orange peel, grated
1/2 c  Brandy
8    Med.yams,cooked,peel,slice>>
OR
2    1 lb.canned yams, drained>>
Sliced.
Melt margarine in large skillet.  Add sugar and stir over low heat
until melted.  Add yams, orange peel, and brandy and cook over low
heat 15 min. basting yams frequently.
