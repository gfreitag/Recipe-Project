Title: BLUEBERRY SALSA--NEW YORK TIMES
Categories: Relishes
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Blueberries, cleaned
1/2 md Red onion, diced small
2    Jalapenos, seeded & minced
1    Red bell pepper, diced
3 tb Cilantro, chopped
1/4 c  Lime juice
1 ts Kosher salt
1. Coarsely chop 1 1/2 cups blueberries.  In a bowl, combine the
chopped and whole berries and the remaining ingredients. Let stand 1
hour.
