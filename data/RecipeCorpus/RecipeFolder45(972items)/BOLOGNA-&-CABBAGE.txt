Title: BOLOGNA & CABBAGE
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Bologna
8 c  Shredded cabbage
4    Potatoes cut into 1/2"
-slices
4    Carrots, sliced
2/3 c  Beef bouillon
1/2 ts Pepper
1 ts Salt
Put all ingredients in a Dutch oven. Cover and cook 1 hour on low
heat on stove or in (350F) oven.  Uncover and cook 40 minutes more or
until potatoes and carrots are tender.
