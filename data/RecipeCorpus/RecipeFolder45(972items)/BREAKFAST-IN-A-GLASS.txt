Title: BREAKFAST IN A GLASS
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Serving
1    Bananas
1/3 c  Orange juice
2 ts Wheat germ
1 tb Honey
1 c  Crushed ice
1    Carton Yogurt (any flavor)
Slice banana into blender, add remaining ingredients and blend at high
speed for 1 minute.

from Nell Grothe, published in the Honey Cookbook, Concho Valley
Beekeepers Association, no ISBN

typed and posted by teri Chesser 3/96
