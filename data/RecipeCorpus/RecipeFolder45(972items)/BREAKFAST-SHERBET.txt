Title: BREAKFAST SHERBET
Categories: Magazine, Breakfast, Beverages
Yield: 8 Servings
5 c  Honeydew melon; chopped
3 md Kiwi fruit; peeled & quarter
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Granulated sugar
1 tb Lime juice
1. In a food processor, combine all ingredients; process until
smooth. Pour the mixture into a medium bowl; cover with plastic wrap
and freeze 3 to 4 hours, until firm.

2. Remove from the freezer and let stand 15 minutes. With an electric
mixer, beat until smooth.  Cover and freeze 3 to 4 hours longer, until
firm.
