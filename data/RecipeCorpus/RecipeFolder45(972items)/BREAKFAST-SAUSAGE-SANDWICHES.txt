Title: BREAKFAST SAUSAGE SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
Softened butter or margarine
8 sl Bread
1 lb Pork sausage, cooked
Crumbled, and drained
1 c  (about
4 oz ) shredded cheddar cheese
2    Eggs, beaten
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 1/2 ts Prepared mustard
Spread butter on one side of each bread slice.  Place 4 slices,
buttered side down, in a single layer in a lightly greased 8-inch
square baking dish.  Top each bread slice with sausage and remaining
bread slices, buttered side up.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Combine
remaining ingredients; pour over sandwiches.  Cover and refrigerate
at least 8 hours.Remove from refrigerator; let stand 30 minutes.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.  Yield:  4 servings.
