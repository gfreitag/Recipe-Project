Title: BOSTON CHICKEN'S CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Stuffings, Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 4 Servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN
VGHC42A-----
2 md Cucumbers w/skin;half, seed
1/2    " slices
1/4    Red Spanish onion -- 1/2"
Dice
1 lg Ripe tomato -- dice
8 oz Paul Newman's Olive Oil and
Vinegar Dressing
1 ts Dry dill weed
1/2 ts Dry parsley -- mince
1/4 c  Olive oil
Combine cucumbers with the rest of the ingredients in non-metal
refrigerator container, tightly covered. Marinate for 24 hours in
fridge before serving. Do not freeze. Source: Gloria Pitzer.
