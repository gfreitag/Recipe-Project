Title: BRIE TOPPED WITH BLUEBERRY CHUTNEY
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese, Sauces and
Yield: 1 Servings
1    8-12 Oz Piece Of Brie Cheese
Blueberry Chutney-----
1 c  Blueberries
2 tb Onions -- chopped
1 1/2 tb Fresh Ginger Root -- grated
1/4 c  Brown Sugar, Packed
2 tb Cider Vinegar
1 1/2 tb Corn Starch
1/8 tb Salt
1    3-Inch Cinnamon Stick
Combine all ingredients in a large sauce pan.  Bring to a boil over
medium heat.  Boil 1 minute.  Remove cinnamon stick. Cover and
refrigerate 30 - 45 minutes.

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Bake brie on an ungreased cookie sheet
for 10 - 12 minutes, or until soft. Place brie on a serving plate,
and top with the cold chutney. Serve with a very mild-flavored cracker
