Title: BOBBIE'S CHILI SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
12 c  Tomatoes -- peeled and
Chopped
3 c  Celery -- chopped
2 c  Onion -- chopped
1 c  Green pepper -- chopped
1/4 c  Salt
2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Pepper
1 1/2 ts Mixed pickling spice
1 c  White vinegar
Combine 1st five ingredients and let stand overnight. Drain in
colander but do not press.  Place in a large kettle and add remaining
ingredients (pickling spice in cheesecloth). Bring to a boil, reduce
heat and simmer for 15 minutes.  Fill jars and process for 10
minutes. Fills 12 - 8oz. jars.
