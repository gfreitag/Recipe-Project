Title: BRAN 'N MOLASSES MUFFINS :::GWHP32A
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/2 c  All-Bran Cereal
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Molasses
1 ea Egg
1/3 c  Butter
1/2 c  Raisins; seedless
1 1/2 c  Flour; sifted/all purp.
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
Combine cereal, milk and molasses.  Let stand until most of the
moisture is absorbed.  Melt butter.
Combine egg and butter with the cereal mix and beat well. Stir in
raisins. Sift together flour, baking powder, soda and salt. Add to
cereal mixture, stirring only until combined. Fill greased muffin pan
cups 3/4 full. Bake at 400 degrees about 20 minutes, or until muffins
are lightly browned. Serve hot.
