Title: BREAKFAST BAKED POTATOES
Categories: Breakfast, Vegetables
Yield: 2 Servings
2    Small potatoes
1    Egg, fork-beaten with a
-little milk
2    Slices toast, crumbled
1 sl Cheddar or Process cheese
Salt, pepper to taste
Garlic powder to taste
Scrub and pare potatoes, but do not peel.  Slice lengthwise into
fourths. Butter a shallow microwave-safe baking dish. Dip potato
slices first into the egg, then into the crumbs to coat. Place the
slices in the baking dish. Top with any remaining crumbs. Dot with a
tablespoon of butter, if desired. Microbake on 100% power 3-5 min, or
until potato is tender and batter is set.  If desired, top with the
slice of cheese and mw 1 minute more to melt. Sprinkle with
seasonings.
