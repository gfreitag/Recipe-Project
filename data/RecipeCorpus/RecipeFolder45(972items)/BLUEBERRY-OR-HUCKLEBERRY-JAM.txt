Title: BLUEBERRY OR HUCKLEBERRY JAM
Categories: Condiments, Jams
Yield: 6 Servings
4 1/2 c  Berries
1    Bottle fruit pectin
7 c  Sugar
1    Lemon
Wash fruit thoroughly.  Crush.  Add lemon juice.  Add grated rind of
1/2 lemon.  Add sugar.  Mix thoroughly. Heat rapidly to full rolling
boil. Stir constantly before and while boiling. Boil hard 2 minutes.
Remove from fire and stir in fruit pectin. Skim. The Household
Searchlight
