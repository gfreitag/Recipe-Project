Title: BOTCHED-UP PUMPKIN PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/3 c  Pumpkin; cooked & pureed
1 1/3 c  Condensed milk
1    Egg
1/2 ts Salt
1 1/4 ts Cinnamon, ground
1/2 ts Nutmeg, ground
1/2 ts Ginger, ground
1/4 ts Cloves, ground
1 c  ;Water, hot
Beat all ingredients together.  Pour into a 9-inch pastry shell. Bake
at 375 F. for 55 to 65 minutes.
