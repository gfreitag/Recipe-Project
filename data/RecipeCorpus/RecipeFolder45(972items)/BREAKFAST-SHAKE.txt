Title: BREAKFAST SHAKE
Categories: None, Breakfast
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Yogurt, low-fat, plain and
1/4 c  Milk, low-fat
1 c  Fruit, fresh, cut up, (e.g.
-banana, pineapple,
-mango-the riper the better)
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
3    Ice cubes, or
1/3 c  Ice, crushed
Place all the ingredients in a blender and process until well
combined and frothy.
