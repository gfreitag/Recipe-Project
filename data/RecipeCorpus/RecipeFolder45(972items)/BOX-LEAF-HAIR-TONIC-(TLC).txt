Title: BOX LEAF HAIR TONIC (TLC)
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Handfuls fresh box leaves
1 2/3 l  Spring water
25 ml Eau de cologne
MMMMM--------------------------UTENSILS-------------------------------
lg Pan
Measuring jug
Strainer
Funnel
Glass bottle
1. Put box leaves in a large pan. Pour the water over and bring to the
boil.

2. Simmer with lid on for 15 minutes.

3. Leave to cool for at least two hours.

4. Strain and add eau de cologne

5. Pour through funnel into bottles.
