Title: BOURBON MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Bourbon
1/4 c  Soy Sauce
1/4 c  Dijon Mustard
1/4 c  Brown Sugar
1 ea Small onion, chopped fine
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1 ds Worcestershire Sauce *
* ds is MM abbreviation for dash

Combine all ingredients in a small bowl, mix well and refrigerate.
Use this marinade on all types of meat, chicken, fish, vegetables,
etc.
