Title: BOURBON-MARINATED SMOKED SALMON
Categories: Fish, Grilled foo, Condiments, Digest, Spices
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Fresh salmon -- skinned and
Boned
Vegetable oil
1 c  Bourbon
1 c  Soy sauce
2 2/3 tb Fresh ginger
2 2/3 tb Black pepper
1/2 ts Chili powder
1 1/2 ts Garlic -- minced
3/4 oz Sesame oil
Place salmon in large shallow dish; cover with vegetable oil. Mix all
other ingredients together to make marinade; marinate 6 hours or
overnight. Grill over mesquite until flaky. Recipe By : Gourmet Gals
& Guys
