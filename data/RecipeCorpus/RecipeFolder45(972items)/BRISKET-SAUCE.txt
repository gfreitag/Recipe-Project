Title: BRISKET SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Beef, Barbeque, Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Margarine
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped bell pepper
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Mustard
2 tb Sweet pickle relish,
-heaping
2 tb Vinegar
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1    29-oz can tomato puree
In a large saucepan, saute onion and pepper in mArgarine until
tender. Mix in remaining ingredients. Bring to a boil and simmer 5
minutes.
