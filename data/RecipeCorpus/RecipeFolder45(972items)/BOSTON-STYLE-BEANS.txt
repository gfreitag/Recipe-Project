Title: BOSTON-STYLE BEANS
Categories: Crockpot, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Dried beans
6 c  Water
1 1/2 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts Pepper
1 tb Salt
1 lg Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Molasses
1/2 c  Ketchup
3 tb Minced salt pork
Cover beans with 3 times as much water in large saucepan. Simmer for
30 minutes.  Let stand for 1 1/2 hours, or until beans are tender.
Drain.

Place beans in slow cooker.  Add 6 cups water, dry mustard, pepper,
salt, onion, brown sugar, molasses, ketchup and pork. Cook on high
for 4-6 hours or on low for 10-12 hours. Serves 8. Source: Home
