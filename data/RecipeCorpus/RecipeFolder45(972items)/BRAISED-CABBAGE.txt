Title: BRAISED CABBAGE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Cabbage, shredded
3 ea Carrot, grated
3 tb Butter
4 ea Chive, stalks
1 x  Salt
1 x  Pepper
In skillet heat butter until foamy. Add chives and saute' until
tender. Add green cabbage and carrots. Saute' over high heat until
tender but crisp, about 4 minutes. Add salt and pepper to taste. If
you don't have scallions, onions are the next best substitute.
