Title: BRAISED RED ONIONS
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Butter/margarine
4 lg Red onions, thinly sliced
Salt & freshly ground black
-- pepper
Melt the butter in heavy-based pot over a moderate heat. Add the
onions & cook gently till translucent. Season, then cover & simmer
over a very low heat for 20 minutes. Stir & transfer to a warmed
serving dish.

Sally Anne Scott, "Versatile Vegetables & Salads"
