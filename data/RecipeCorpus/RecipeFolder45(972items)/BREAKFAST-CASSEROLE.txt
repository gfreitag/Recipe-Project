Title: BREAKFAST CASSEROLE
Categories: Breakfast, Casseroles
Yield: 8 Servings
1 cn Crescent rolls
1 lb Sausage, cooked and drained
Fresh mushrooms, sliced
3/4 lb Monterey Jack cheese, grated
6    Eggs, beaten
1 cn Cream of onion soup
Line a 13x9 inch Pyrex dish with rolls, sealing perforations. Cover
with sausage, mushrooms and half the cheese. Mix eggs with soup and
pour over casserole.  Sprinkle remaining cheese on top. Chill
overnight. Bake at 350 for 1 hour.  Note:  For variation, you can use
cooked, crumbled bacon or bite-sized pieces of ham instead of sausage.
