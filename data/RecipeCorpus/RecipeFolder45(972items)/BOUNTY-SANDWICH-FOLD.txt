Title: BOUNTY SANDWICH FOLD
Categories: Microwave, Sandwiches
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ea Paper towel
BOUNTY MICROWAVE TIPS *** PLACE 1 PAPER TOWEL ON COUNTER WITH SANDWICH
DIAGONALLY IN CENTER.  FOLD 3 CONERS TOWARD CENTER, COVERING THE
SANDWICH LIKE AN ENVELOPE. FOLD REMAINING CORNER OVER OTHER 3
CORNERS. PLACE FOLDED SIDE DOWN ON MICROWAVE PLATE.  MICROWAVE AS
DIRECTED IN RECEIPE.
