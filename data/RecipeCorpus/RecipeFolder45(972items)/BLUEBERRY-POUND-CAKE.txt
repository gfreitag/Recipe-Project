Title: BLUEBERRY POUND CAKE
Categories: Cake
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Butter, softened
2 c  Sugar
4    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
2 c  Blueberries
Cream butter and sugar.  Add eggs, one at a time; beat well until
light and fluffy.  Add vanilla.  Sift 2 cups of flour, salt and
baking powder together.  Add sifted ingredients to creamed mixture
and beat. Dredge berries in remaining flour.  Fold berry mixture
gently into creamed mixture.  Pour into a 10 inch tube pan which has
been buttered and coated with sugar.  Bake at 325 degrees for 1 hour
and 15 minutes. Randy Rigg
