Title: BRAN BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/8 c  Water
2 2/3 c  Bread flour
3/8 c  Wheat bran
1 1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Sugar
1 1/2 tb Butter, unsalted
1 1/2 tb Dry milk
1 ts Active dry yeast
Put the ingredients in the bread pan in the order suggested by the
manufacturer and process on the basic bread cycle. 1-1/2 lb. loaf.
