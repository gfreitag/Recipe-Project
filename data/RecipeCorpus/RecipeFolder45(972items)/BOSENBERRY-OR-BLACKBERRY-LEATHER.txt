Title: BOSENBERRY OR BLACKBERRY LEATHER
Categories: Digest, Sept.
Yield: 1 Servings
Puree 1 quart of berries.
Add 1/4 cup sugar
Just heat through on the stovetop.  Strain through cheesecloth to
remove seeds.  Dry as above.
