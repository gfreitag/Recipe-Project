Title: BLUEBERRY SAUCE
Categories: Breakfast, Fruits, Low-fat, Sauces, Mcdougall
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
4 ts Cornstarch
1 c  Blueberries; thawed or fresh
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook Preparation Time: 0:05
Place the juice in a saucepan with the cornstarch. Cook, stirring
until thickened.  Add the blueberries. Heat through. Serve warm.
66 calories, 0.2 grams fat per 1/2 cup serving. From the
collection of Sue Smith, S.Smith34, Uploaded June 16, 1994
