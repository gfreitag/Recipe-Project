Title: BOLOGNA OMELET
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
4    Eggs, well beaten
1/4 ts Baking powder
2 tb Diced onion
2 tb Diced green peppers
2 tb Diced celery
1/2    Slice bologna, cut into
-small pieces
1    Slice American cheese, cut
-into small pieces
Mix all ingredients together in bowl. Fry over medium heat in
preheated, greased frying pan, turning once.
