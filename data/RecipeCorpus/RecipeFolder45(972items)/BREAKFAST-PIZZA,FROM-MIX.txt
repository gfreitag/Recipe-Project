Title: BREAKFAST PIZZA/FROM MIX
Categories: Mixes, Breakfast
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Bake it all mix -- do not
Pack
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 c  Apricot or peach preserves
1/2 ts Lemon juice
1 pk Breakfast sausage links* --
(8 ounces)
*Cut into pennywise slices (Brown 'n Serve). Make a well in the Mix.
Add milk and stir with a fork about 25 strokes. Turn onto a sheet of
waxed paper.  Knead well 6 times. Roll out on lightly floured pastry
cloth or board to a 14-inch circle. Place on pizza pan or cooky
sheet. Spread preserves mixed with lemon juice in a thin layer over
the dough. Sprinkle sausage over the top. Bake in a 450 F. oven 15 to
18 minutes. Yield: One 14-inch pie
