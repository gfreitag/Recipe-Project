Title: IRISH OATMEAL WITH DRIED FRUIT & HONEY - MA
Categories: Break/brnch, Soup/stews, Irish, Fruits
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Apple cider or juice
1    Cinnamon stick
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Irish oatmeal
5 oz Dried peach, apricot, and
-pear halves in any
-combination, cut into 3/8
-inch dice (about 1 C)
Honey or dark-brown sugar
1/2 c  Low-fat milk, warmed
1. In a heavy saucepan, bring 2 C water, the apple cider, cinnamon
stick, and salt to a boil. Add oatmeal, stir until smooth, and return
to a boil. Lower heat and simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 30
minutes.

2. Remove saucepan from heat. Discard the cinnamon stick and stir in
all but about 1/4 C of the dried fruit.

3. Divide oatmeal among 4 bowls and top with honey, if desired.
Garnish with remaining fruit; serve with warm milk.
