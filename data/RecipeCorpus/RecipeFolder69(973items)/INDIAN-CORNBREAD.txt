Title: INDIAN CORNBREAD
Categories: Vegetarian, Misc, Bread, Corn bread, Side dish
Yield: 12 Servings
3 c  Yellow cornmeal
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 cn Evaporated skim milk
4 ea Egg
4 oz Sour cream, light
4 oz Cheddar cheese -- grated
1 cn Creamed corn
1    Jar pimentoe
2 ts Cunmin
1 ts Salt
mix ingredients, bake in greased iron skillet or baking dish @ 350
till done. About 40min.
