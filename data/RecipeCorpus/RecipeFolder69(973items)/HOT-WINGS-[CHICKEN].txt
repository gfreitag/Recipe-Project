Title: HOT WINGS [CHICKEN]
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Butter -- margarine melted
1/4 c  Hot pepper sauce
3 tb Vinegar
24    Chicken drumetts
. Preheat oven to 350. In small bowl,whisk together butter, pepper
sauce and vinegar. Dip drumetts in butter mixture; arrange in single
layer in large baking pan. Bake until chicken is browned, 30 to 40
minutes. Sprinkle with paprika. Serve with salad dressing and celery
sticks.
