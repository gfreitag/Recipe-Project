Title: ICING CREAM CHEESE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
4 oz Cream cheese, softened
2 oz Butter
1/2 lb Sugar; icing
1 ts Vanilla
2 tb Cocoa (sifted)optional
1    Ea
Soften the cheese and butter at leaving at room temperature. Beat in
the sugar and then add the vanilla and cocoa (if you desire a
cholocate icing;otherwise use almond or other flavoring for a white
frosting.) This frosting is best on carrot cake or on the cholocate
zucchini cake.
