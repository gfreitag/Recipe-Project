Title: IMPOSSIBLE SOUTHWESTERN PIE
Categories: Impossibles, Pies, Beans, Vegetables, Bakery
Yield: 6 Servings
-----waldine van geffen
-vghc42a-----
1 1/2 c  Frozen whole kernel corn
8 md Green onions; chop
15 oz Black beans; rinse; drain
1/3 c  Cheddar; shred
1/2 c  Bisquick reduced fat or;
-original
baking mix
Milk
Thick-and-chunky salsa
1/2    cup
1/2  cup
2    Eggs
Thick-and-chunky salsa

Heat oven to 400~. Grease glass pie plate, 9x1-1/4". Layer corn,
onions and beans in pie plate; stir gently. Sprinkle with cheese.
Stir baking mix, milk, 1/2 c salsa and the eggs with fork until
blended. Pour into pie plate. Bake 35-40 minutes or until knife
inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 5 minutes. Serve with salsa.
205 cal; 5 gr fat; 22% fat. Source: New Bisquick Family Favorites. MM
