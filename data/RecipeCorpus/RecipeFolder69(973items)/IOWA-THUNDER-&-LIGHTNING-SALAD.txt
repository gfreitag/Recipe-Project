Title: IOWA THUNDER & LIGHTNING SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Shredded cabbage
5 ea Medium tomatoes, chopped
1 ea Hot pepper (optional)
1 ea Large onion, sliced
2 ea Cucumbers, sliced
2 ea Bell peppers, cubed
Dressing:
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Honey
1/2 c  Olive oil
Combine dressing ingredients and toss vegetables together. Add
dressing to vegetables. Mix well. Place in an airtight container and
chill 2 days before serving.
