Title: IMPOSSIBLE BACON BREAKFAST PIE (QUICHE)
Categories: Breakfast, Pies, Pork
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Bacon
1 cn Green Chili (chopped)
1 c  Grated cheese (Jack)
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Bisquick
6 oz Sour cream
3    Eggs
1    Salt & Pepper to taste
Brown, drain and crumble bacon. Put in bottm of 11X7 greased dosh.
Top with onion and cheese. Put rest in blender for 1 min. Pour into
pan. Bake at 350 until tests done with knife (35-45 min)
