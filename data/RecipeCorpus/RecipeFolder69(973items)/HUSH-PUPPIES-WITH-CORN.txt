Title: HUSH PUPPIES WITH CORN
Categories: Breads
Yield: 30 Servings
1 c  Flour
1 c  Cornmeal, yellow
2 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 c  Corn, creamed
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
1    Egg; beaten
2 ts Bacon dripping
Oil; for frying
Combine dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.  Add corn, onion,
egg, and bacon dripping; stir well. Carefully drop batter by level
table- spoons into deep hot oil (370 F); cook only a few at a time,
turning once. Fry until the hush puppies are golden brown (3 to 5
minutes). Drain well on paper towels.
