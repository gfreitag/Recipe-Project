Title: IRISH POTATO STEW
Categories: Potatoes, Stews
5 md to 6 potatoes -- peeled,
coarely chop
Yield: 4 Servings
:
1 sm onion
roux
:
2    to 3 slices bacon-chopped
salt and pepper -- to taste
boiling water
:
:

Peel potatoes and cut coarsley. Make a roux. Brown one onion in roux.
Add chopped bacon, if desired. Add potatoes and let smother for about
5 minu tes. Add boiling water, salt and pepper to taste and let cook
until potat oes are done.
