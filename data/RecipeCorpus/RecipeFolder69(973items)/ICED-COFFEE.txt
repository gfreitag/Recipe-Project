Title: ICED COFFEE
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Coffee; instant, regular or
1/4 c  Sugar or low calorie sweeten
1 l  Milk; cold
Dissolve instant coffee and sugar in hot water. Stir in 1 litre of
cold milk and and add ice.   For mocha flavour, use chocolate milk
and reduce the sugar to taste. For single serving: dissolve 1 tbsp of
instant coffee and 2 tsp sugar in 1 tbsp hot water. Add 1 cup of cold
milk and stir.
