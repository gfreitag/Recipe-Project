Title: INSALATA VERDE E ROSSA (RED & GREEN SALAD)
Categories: Salads, Italian, Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Dandelion greens
1 lg Bunch watercress
12 lg Red radishes
Salt
6 tb Olive oil
2 tb Red wine vinegar
Salt & pepper
Rinse the dandelion leaves & watercress.  Wash the radishes & remove
the stems, leaves & roots.  Quarter, or if especially large, cut into
eighths. Put dandelion leaves & radishes in a bowl, cover with cold
water, add salt & leave for 1 hour.  Shortly before serving, drain &
dry them & place in a serving bowl. Add the watercress. Beat together
the olive oil & red wine vinegar.  Pour over the salad & toss well.
