Title: INDONESIAN MARINADE
Categories: Sauces
4 TB lime juice
Yield: 1 Servings
2 TB lemon juice
1 TB ginger -- minced
1 TB garlic -- minced
2 TB soy sauce

thin

4 ds Habanero chile sauce
3 TB cilantro -- chopped

olive oil until smooth. Use this marinade for chicken or fish.
