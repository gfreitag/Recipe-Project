Title: INSTANT "RUSSIAN" TEA
Categories: Beverages, Russian
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Tang
3/4 c  Plain instant tea
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon
3 oz Country Time lemonade mix
1/2 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Allspice
Mix everything together and store in a glass container with a tight
fitting lid. Use 2 heaping teaspoons per teacup of hot water.
