Title: INSTANT RASPBERRY CORDIAL JAM - GLORIOUS LIQU
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
12 oz Raspberry jam
1 tb To 2 Chambord or other
Raspberry liqueur
Stir liqueur into jam; cover and refrigerate at least one day to allow
flavors to meld.
