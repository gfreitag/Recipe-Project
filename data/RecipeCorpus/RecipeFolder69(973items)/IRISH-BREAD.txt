Title: IRISH BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 lb Butter
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs
1 c  Milk
2 1/2 c  Flour
1 c  Raisins
Cream butter and sugar. Mix flour, salt, baking powder, and add to
butter mixture alternately with milk. Add raisins. Pour into 8" X 8"
pan. Bake at 350 degrees one hour. Mrs. Bernard Mahon
