Title: HULI-HULI CHICKEN SEASONING
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
MARY WILSON  BWVB02B
2 1/2 oz Paprika
2 tb Black pepper
1/3 c  Salt
1 ts MSG (ajinomoto) -- opt'l
Mix well and sprinkle liberally on fryer or Cornish hens. Bird must
be slit open.  Broil until nice and brown. Refrigerate leftover
seasoning.
