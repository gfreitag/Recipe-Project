Title: HUSH PUPPIES WITH ONIONS
Categories: Side dish
Yield: 8 Servings
1 3/4 c  Cornmeal
4 tb Flour, all-purpose
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
6 tb Onion; chopped
1    Egg; beaten
2 c  Water; boiling
Salad oil
Combine dry ingredients, onion, and egg; add boiling water, stirring
constantly until mixture is smooth. Add more water if necessary. Drop
by spoonfuls into deep, hot oil. Cook until golden brown.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman.
