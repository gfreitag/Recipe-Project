Title: IRISH YOGURT BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Flour
3/4 ts Baking soda
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt
1 c  Currants
2 tb Caraway seed
2    Eggs
1 c  Plain low fat yogurt; mixed
Stir the dry ingredients together. Add the currants and caraway
seeds; Add eggs. Add yogurt and water mixture and stir until a sticky
batter is formed. knead on a well floured surface for 1 minute then
shape into a ball and  place in a well greased round casserole. Mark
a cross in the center with a sharp knife and bake in 350 oven for 1
hour and 15 minutes before removing bread from casserole, then allow
to cool on a wire rack. Slice thinly to serve. Freezes well and is
best the day after baking
