Title: IMPOSSIBLE CRANBERRY-ORANGE PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
10 oz Frozen Cranberry-Orange
Relish; thawed
3/4 c  Milk
1/3 c  Margarine or butter;melted
1 ts Vanilla
4    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Bisquick Baking Mix
Whipped Cream
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease pie plate, 10 x 1 1/2 inches.
Sprinkle relish evenly in plate.  Beat remaining ingredients except
whipped cream until smooth, 30 seconds in blender on high or 1 minute
with hand beater. Pour into plate.  Bake until knife inserted in
center comes out clean, 35 to 40 minutes; cool. Refrigerate at least
6 hours but no longer than 24 hours.  Garnish with Whipped Cream.

Recipe from the Bisquick Recipe Club, Prodigy Beth Lane
