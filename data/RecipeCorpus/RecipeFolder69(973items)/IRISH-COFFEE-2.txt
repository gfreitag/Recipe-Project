Title: IRISH COFFEE 2
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 2 Servings
1 1/2 c  Warm Water
1 tb Instant Coffee Crystals
1/4 c  Irish Whiskey
Brown Sugar To Taste
Dessert Topping *
*    Dessert topping should be in a pressurized can. Mix first four
ingredients then add a bit of dessert topping or sweetened whipped
cream.
