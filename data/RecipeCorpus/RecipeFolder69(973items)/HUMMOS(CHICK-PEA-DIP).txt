Title: HUMMOS(CHICK PEA DIP)
Categories: Lebanese, Dips
Yield: 6 Servings
1 cn Chick peas(semi drained)
1 sm Lemmon(juiced)
2 cl Garlic(crushed)
1 tb Tahinni(sesame seed paste)
Optional(instead use a litte
Olive oil)
Salt & freshly ground pepper
*adjust above amounts to
Suit your taste
*Blend all ingredients. *place in an attractive bowl. *cut small
pitas into triagles for dipping. Posted by
