Title: ICE CREAM SANDWICH
Categories: Ww, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
-Joyce Burton -- PDPP83A
1 tb Crunchy peanut butter
2    Graham cracker squares
1/2 c  WW vanilla ice milk
Spread peanut butter on 1 Graham cracker square.  Top with vanilla
ice milk and another Graham cracker square.

Each Serving = 1 Pro., 1 Fat, 1 Fruit, 1/2 Milk, 1 Bread.
