Title: IMPOSSIBLE HAM & SWISS PIE
Categories: Eggs, Pies, Easy
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Ham ; cooked, smoked
- cut up
1 c  Cheese; Swiss, shredded
1/3 c  Green onion; chopped or
- onion, chopped
2 c  Milk
4    Eggs
1 c  Bisquick or baking mix
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Heat oven to 400F. Grease pie plate, 10x1 1/2" inches. Sprinkle ham,
cheese nad onions in plate. beat remaining ingredients till smooth,
15 seconds in blender or 1 minute with hand beater. Pour into plate.
Bake till golden brown or till knife inserted in centre comes out
clean, 35 to 40 minutes. Cool 5 minutes. from _The Best of Bisquick_
