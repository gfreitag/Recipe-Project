Title: HUNGARIAN TURKEY SKILLET
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/4 lb Turkey thigh -- frozen
Defrost
Skin, debone -- cut into
1/4    "
2 c  Onions -- cut into strips
2    Green peppers -- cut in
Strips
1    Red bell pepper -- cut in
Strip
2    Tomatoes; peeled -- diced
1 c  -- water
Salt & Pepper to taste
Combine all ingredients in heavy skillet. Simmer, covered, 35 minutes
or until meat is tender. Uncover and continue to simmer until sauce
is thick. Per serving: Calories--211.1 Carbohydrate--12.5 grams
Fat--6.5 grams
