Title: ILENE & IRENE'S SPECIAL POTATO DISH
Categories: Potato cass, Restaurants
Yield: 10 Servings
8 c  Cooked potatoes, diced
6 sl Bacon, diced
1 lg Onion, diced
1/4 lb Velveeta cheese, diced
1/4 c  Green olives, chpd
1 c  Mayonnaise
In a 9x12 inch baking dish mix all ingredients together. Chill
overnight. Bake in a 350 oven for 1 hour. Source: The Chicago Street
Inn
