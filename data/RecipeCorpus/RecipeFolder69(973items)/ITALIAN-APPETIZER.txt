Title: ITALIAN APPETIZER
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
7    CHUCK OZBURN
(Dina Ramacciotti 9/13/78)
4 1/2 c  Zucchini -- - about 6 med
Size
Sliced or grated
1/2 c  Onion -- minced
1    Handfull parlsey -- chopped
10 ml Garlic -- - minced
1 1/2 c  Bisquick
3/4 c  Oil -- Salt to taste or
1 1/2 ts Seasoning salt
3/4 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
6    Eggs -- - slightly beaten
Preheat oven to 350~F;  combine all ingredients in  a large bowl in
the order given; mix thoroughly; pour into an oiled 9 X 13 Pyrex
baking dish; bake 35  to 40 minutes or until lightly browned; cool on
a wire rack and cut into small squares; can be served warm or cold.

Note:   If served  as a  cold appetizer,  cut into smaller pieces.
