Title: IMPOSSIBLE MEXICAN PIE
Categories: Beef, Main dishes
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 1/4 oz Taco seasoning mix
4 oz Green chiles
Drained, seeded and chopped
1 c  Shredded Monterey Jack
Cheese
(about 4 ounces)
1 1/4 c  Milk
3/4 c  BisquickÂ® baking mix
3    Eggs
1/8 ts Red pepper sauce
Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Lightly grease pie plate, 10 x 1 1/2
inches. Cook and stir ground beef and onion until beef is brown;
drain. Stir in seasoning mix Spread beef mixture in pie plate.
Sprinkle with chilies and cheese. Beat remaining ingredients until
smooth, 15 seconds

in blender on high speed or l minute with hand beater. Pour into pie
plate. Bake until golden brown and knife inserted halfway between
center and edge comes out cleans 25 to 30 minutes. Let stand 5 minutes

before cutting.  Refrigerate any remaining pie.  6 to
8    servings.
