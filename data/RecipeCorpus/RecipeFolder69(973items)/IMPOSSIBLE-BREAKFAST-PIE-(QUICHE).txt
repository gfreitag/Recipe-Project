Title: IMPOSSIBLE BREAKFAST PIE (QUICHE)
Categories: Breakfast, Eggs, Pies
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Sausage (bulk)
1    Green onion bunch, chopped
1 c  Mozzerella, grated
1 1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Bisquick
6 oz Sour cream
3    Eggs
1    Salt & pepper to taste
Brown, drain and crumble sausage. put in bottom of 11X7 greased pan.
Top with onions and cheese.  Put rest in blender for 1 min. Pout over
meat mixture. Bake at 350 for 35-45 min until tests done with knife.
