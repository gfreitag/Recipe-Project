Title: IMPOSSIBLE COCONUT PIE #2
Categories: Pies, Pastries
Yield: 6 Servings
4    Eggs
1    Stick margarine -- softened
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Milk
3/4 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Flour
1/8 ts Salt
3/4 ts Baking powder
1 c  Coconut (reserve 1/2 cup)
Put all ingredients into a blender and blend 30 seconds. Grease and
flour 10-inch pie pan or 9-inch square pan. Pour batter into pan and
sprinkle remaining coconut over batter. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 to
45 minutes and coconut is golden brown.
