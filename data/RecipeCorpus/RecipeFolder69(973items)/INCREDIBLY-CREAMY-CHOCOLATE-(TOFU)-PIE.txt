Title: INCREDIBLY CREAMY CHOCOLATE (TOFU) PIE
Categories: Vegetables, Sept.
Yield: 8 Servings
1    10 oz. pk. Sunspire
Chocolate Chips or Carob
Chips
2    10 5 oz. pks. Mori-Nu Silken
Tofu (Firm)
3 tb Light honey
1    9" Pride O'the Farm Graham
Cracker or Cookie Pie Crust
Heat Sunspire Chips in a double boiler or pan sitting in water until
smooth.  Add honey.  In a blender or processor blend Mori-Nu Tofu
until smooth.  Add the chocolate/honey mixture to tofu and whip until
creamy. Pour filling into Pride O'the Farm Pie Crust.  Chill over
night. Slice and serve.
