Title: ITALIAN BEAN-AND-TOMATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Italian
Yield: 1 Servings    1 1/2 c  romaine lettuce -- shredded
1/2 c  tomato -- seeded, coarsely
chopped, unpeeled
:
1/2 c  great northern beans, canned
drained
:
1/4 ts dried rosemary -- crushed
1/4 ts pepper -- fresh-ground
2 TB low sodium chicken broth
2 TB balsamic vinegar
1 ts olive oil

Combine first 3 ingredients in a bowl; toss gently. Combine rosemary
and next 4 ingredients; stir well. Pour over lettuce mixture, and
toss gently.

: Cooking Light, Sept 1993, p. 106
