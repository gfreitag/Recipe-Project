Title: HOTTER THAN HELL BANANAS A LA BALOO
Categories: Bbq, Side dish, Swedish, Hot
Yield: 8 Servings
8    Bananas (ripe)
100 ml Sunflower honey (or other
- honey)
50 ml Sambal Olek (Indonesian)
30 ml Kikkoman Soy Sauce (Japanese
1. Mix sunflower-honey, Sambal Olek and Kikkoman Soy Sauce. 2.
Marinate the bananas for 12 hours. 3. Grill the bananas (brush with
the marinade when grilling).

Perfect side-dish with hot BBQ meat.
