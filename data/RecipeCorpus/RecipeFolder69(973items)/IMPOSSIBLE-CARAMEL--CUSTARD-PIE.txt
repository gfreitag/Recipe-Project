Title: IMPOSSIBLE CARAMEL- CUSTARD PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Milk
1/4 c  Butter; melted
4    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Brown sugar; firmly packed
1/2 c  Bisquick baking mix
1/2 c  Flaked coconut; (optional)
Combine all ingredients except coconut in a blender; blend at
high speed for 15 seconds or until smooth. Pour into a greased
10-inch pie plate.
Sprinkle coconut on top, if desired.  Bake at 350^ for 40 to 45
minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cool
completely before cutting. YIELD: 1 10-inch pie.
