Title: HUNGARIUM GULYAS (GOULASH) BY ALICE
Categories: Main dish, Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Beef
2    Carrots, large
1 cn Stewed Tomatoes-Puree
1/4 ts Paprika
1 ts Onion-chopped-dried
2    Celery-stalks
2    Potatoes-med size cubed
1 ts Salt
Cut meat into squares, wash, add paprika, salt, stewed tomatoes and
1/4 cup water. Cook slowly for 1 hour. Add all of the washed and
diced vegetables, but potatoes. Add another cup of water and cook
slowly for 1/2 hour. Add potatoes, continue cooking for 15 minutes.
Add 2 cups of cold water. Let it come to a boil and cook for 10
minutes. Serve as a main dish.
