Title: ITALIAN CHEESE PUDDING
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Ricotta cheese
1/4 c  Grated milk chocolate
1/4 c  Finely chopped walnuts
2 tb Cream, approximately
Italian Cheese Pudding

Budino Di Ricotta

Put the ricotta, chocolate and nuts into the container of a food
processor and blend thoroughly. Add enough cream to make a smooth
consistency. Serve with cookies.
