Title: IRISH COFFEE II
Categories: Beverages, Microwave, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Sugar
3/4 c  Strong black coffee to
1 c  Strong black coffee
1 1/2 oz (1 Jigger) Irish Whiskey
Whipped cream
1. Dissolve sugar in black coffee in an Irish coffee glass or a
heat-resistant, non-metallic glass, cup or mug. (DO NOT USE A GLASS
WITH METALLIC TRIM.) 2. Heat, uncovered, in Microwave Oven 1 to 2
minutes or until hot. 3. Stir in Irish Whiskey. 4. Carefully float a
spoonful of whipped cream on top.
