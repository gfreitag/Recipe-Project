Title: INCREDIBLE HASH BROWN POTATOES
Categories: Casseroles, Potatoes, Soups/stews
Yield: 10 Servings
3 lb Frozen hash brown potatoes-
Thawed and drained
16 oz Cheddar cheese;grated
2 cn Cream of potato soup
1 1/2 c  Sour cream
3/4 c  Parmesan cheese;grated
Combine the first 4 ingredients.  Place in a 9x13" baking dish. Top
with Parmesan cheese. Bake at 350 for 1 hour. From: Misty Graves
Date: 23 Sep 96
