Title: IMPOSSIBLE QUICHE
Categories: Bisquick, Main dish
Yield: 2 Pies
1/2 c  Bisquick
4    Eggs
3 tb Butter
2 c  Milk
1/2 lb Swiss cheese; shredded
2 tb Onion; chopped
1/2 c  Any vegetable;chopped
Bacon;cooked crisp
Grease 2 pie plates.  Heat oven to 400~.  Put all ingredients in
blender and mix well.  You can either blend the vegetable (which
makes them really tiny) or chop them and add after blending. Bake at
400~ for about 40 minutes.  This makes 9" pie plates.
