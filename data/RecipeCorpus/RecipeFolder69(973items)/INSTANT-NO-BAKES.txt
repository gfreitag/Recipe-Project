Title: INSTANT NO-BAKES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 3 Dozen
2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Shortening
6 oz Evaporated milk
4 oz Instant lemon pudding
3 1/2 c  Rolled oats
6 oz Butterscotch chips
1/2 c  Nuts; chopped
Combine sugar, shortening and milk; bring to boil. Remove from heat;
add pudding mix. Cool to lukewarm; add oats, chips and nuts. Drop on
waxed paper by teaspoonfuls.
