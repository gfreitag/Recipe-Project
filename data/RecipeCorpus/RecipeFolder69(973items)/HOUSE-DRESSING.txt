Title: HOUSE DRESSING
Categories: Dressings, Salads
Yield: 3 Cups
1    To 2 cloves garlic, minced
1 ts White vinegar
1 ts Steak sauce
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Dry mustard
6 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
3 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Sour cream (optional)
Mix together garlic, vinegar, steak sauce, Worcestershire sauce,
mustard, cheese, Mayo, and sour cream. Refrigerate.

NOTE:  Flavor improves with time (up to two weeks). An excellent dip
for vegetables also.
