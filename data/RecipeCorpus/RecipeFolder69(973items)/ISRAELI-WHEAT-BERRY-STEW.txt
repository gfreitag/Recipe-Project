Title: ISRAELI WHEAT BERRY STEW
Categories: Crockpot, Stews
5 c  water
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Great northern beans
1 c  wheat berries
6 sm potatoes -- cut in half
1 lg onion -- sliced
4    cloves garlic -- minced
5 ts cumin -- ground
3 ts tumeric
1/2 ts black pepper -- ground

Mix together all ingredients in crockpot.  Cook at high 8 to 10 hours.
