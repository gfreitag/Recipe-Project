Title: IMPOSSIBLE BREAKFAST QUICHE
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 2 Servings
3    Eggs
1/2 c  Bisquick
1/2 c  Butter
1 1/2 c  Milk
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Black pepper
1/2 c  Sausage
4 oz Cheese, shredded
Cook and crumble sausage.

Preheat oven to 350.  Blend all but cheese and meat for 15 seconds in
blender.  Sprinkle with cheese and meat on top. Bake 45 minutes. Cool
for 5 minutes.
