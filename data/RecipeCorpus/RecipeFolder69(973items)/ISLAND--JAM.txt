Title: ISLAND  JAM
Categories: J, M
Yield: 1 Servings
4 c  Cantaloupe, peeled and
Diced
3    Oranges, peeled and diced
1/4 c  Lemon juice
4 c  Sugar
1 ts Lemon rind
1 ts Orange rind
1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Bananas
Combine cantaloupe, oranges, and 1/4 cup lemon juice in heavy
saucepan. Bring to a boil and simmer for 15 mintues. Add sugar, lemon
rind, orange rind, and salt. Continue simmering for 30 mintues. Add 3
cups sliced bananas and continue simmering for an additional 15
mintues. Pour into jelly jars and cover with paraffin. Can be frozen.
Yield 8 (6 ounce) jars.
