Title: HUSH PUPPY GRITS (THT)
Categories: Grits
Yield: 14 To 15
1    Beaten egg
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 c  Grits
1/4 c  Allpurpose flour
2 ts Sugar
3/4 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Sea salt
Shortening or cooking oil
- for deepfat frying
Freshly ground black
- pepper to taste
Recipe by: TOO HOT TAMALES SHOW #TH6285 Preparation Time: In a bowl
stir together the egg and buttermilk. In another bowl combine the
grits, flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and 1/4 teaspoon sea
salt. Add the egg mixture to the grits mixture. Stir just until moist.

Heat the shortening to 375 degrees. Drop the batter by tablespoons
into the deep hot fat. Fry about 3 minutes or until golden brown,
turning once.

Drain the hush puppies on paper towels.

Yield: 14 to 15
