Title: IMPOSSIBLE CHOCOLATE PIE
Categories: Desserts, Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
2    Eggs
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Butter
1 ts Vanilla
2 oz Melted chocolate
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Bisquick
Preheat oven to 350.  Put everything in blender for 1 min. pour into
pie plate.  Bake for 30 minutes until tests done with knife. Cool
completly and top with whipped cream.
