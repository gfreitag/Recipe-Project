Title: HOT WHITE CHOCOLATE MOCHA
Categories: Beverages, Chocolate
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Chopped white chocolate
1 tb Instant espresso powder
1 1/2 c  Hot milk
1 ts Pure vanilla extract
Cinnamon or sifted cocoa
Place the chocolate in a blender.  Add the espresso powder, hot milk
and vanilla.  Blend until frothy.

Pour the hot chocolate into mugs an dust with cinnamon or cocoa.
*Lower Fat Version: Use low-fat milk.
