Title: IMPOSSIBLE CHICKEN-PESTO PIE
Categories: Impossible, Bisquick, Chicken, Poultry
Yield: 8 Servings
2 c  Cooked chicken;cut-up
1/2 c  Bisquick
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Pesto sauce
2 oz Shredded mozzarella cheese
Heat oven to 400~. In greased 9" pie plate sprinkle chicken. In
medium bowl stir baking mix, milk, eggs and pesto sauce with fork
until blended; pour into pie plate. Bake 30-35 minutes or until knife
inserted in center comes out clean. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 1-2
minutes longer or until melted. Cool 5 minutes. Serve. High altitude:
Bake 35-40 minutes.
