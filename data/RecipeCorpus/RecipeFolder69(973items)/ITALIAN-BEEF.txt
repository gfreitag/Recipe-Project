Title: ITALIAN BEEF
Categories: Italian, Beef
Yield: 14 Servings
10 lb Round or rump roast
1/2 c  Vinegar
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 cl Garlic,minced
1 tb Oregano
1 sm Onion,diced
Salt
Pepper
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
7 3/4 c  Water
3    Beef bouillon cubes
8 tb Worcestershire sauce
4 ts Oregano
1 pk Italian Dressing Mix
2 ts Basil
1 pk Au Jus Mix
Mix vinegar,2 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce,1 tbsp. oregano and the
garlic. Pour over seasoned meat,slicing onion on top.Cover lightly;
roast @ 350 degrees 3 hours or until done.Slice when cool. Before
serving,combine sauce ingredients;bring to boil.Cool 10 minutes,then
add sliced beef.Serve on poorboy buns.May be refrigerated 2 to 3
weeks.Makes 10 to 16 servings.
