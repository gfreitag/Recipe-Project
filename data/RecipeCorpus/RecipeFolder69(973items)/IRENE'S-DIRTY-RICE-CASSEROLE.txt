Title: IRENE'S DIRTY RICE CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Ground beef, Rice, Pork & ham, Entre' or s
Yield: 1 Servings
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 lb Mild pork sausage
1    Onion -- chopped
1/2    Bell pepper -- chopped
1    Rib celery -- chopped
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Cream of celery soup
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 1/2 c  Raw rice
Saute ground beef, pork sausage, and then pour off drippings. Saute
onion, bell pepper and celery.  Add all cream of mushroom soup,
celery soup, chicken soup and rice. Pour into greased 13 x 9 x 2 inch
dish. Cover tightly with foil.  Bake at 325 degrees for 1
1/2    hours.

Tastes just like maw-maw's homemade dirty rice.
