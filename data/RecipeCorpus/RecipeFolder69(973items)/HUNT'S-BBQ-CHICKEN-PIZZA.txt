Title: HUNT'S BBQ CHICKEN PIZZA
Categories: Pizza
Yield: 1 12" pie
1    Prepared pizza shell
2/3 c  Hunt's BBQ sauce
6 oz Chicken, cooked/thin sliced
6 oz Mozzarella cheese, shredded
2 oz Extra-sharp cheddar shredded
1/2 c  Red onion, quartered/sliced
1 1/2 ts Cilantro,chopped or to taste
Combine the BBQ sauce and chicken. Spread 12 oz of mixture on pizza
shell. Spread mozzarella and cheddar over this. Top with onion and
cilantro. Bake at 450 degrees F until cheese is melted and crust is
golden.

Source: Pizza Today June 1992.
