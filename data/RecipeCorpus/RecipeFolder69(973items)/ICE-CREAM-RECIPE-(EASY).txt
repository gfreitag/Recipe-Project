Title: ICE CREAM RECIPE (EASY)
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 6 Servings
5    Eggs
Pinch of Salt
2 c  Sugar
2 pk Instant pudding mix
1 cn Evaporated milk
2    Qt. milk
Beat eggs well.  add salt.  blend in sugar and pudding mix. add
milks. stir well.  Pour in container, (plastic works best), store in
freezer at least over night. Waah--Laah, you have ice cream.
