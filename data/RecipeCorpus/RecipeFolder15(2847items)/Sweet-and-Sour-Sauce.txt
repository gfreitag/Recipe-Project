Title: Sweet and Sour Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 10 servings
1 ts DRY ENGLISH MUSTARD
1 ts CIDER VINEGAR
3 tb FRESH ORANGE JUICE
1 x  SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE
1/2 c  BITTER ORANGE MARMALADE
1/2 c  PEACH JAM
2 ts FRESH GROUND GINGER
1 tb GRATED HORSERADISH
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS IN A SMALL BOWL AND LET STAND FOR 15 MINUTES.
