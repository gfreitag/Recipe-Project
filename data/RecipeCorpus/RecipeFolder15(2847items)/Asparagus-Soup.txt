Title: Asparagus Soup
Categories: Soups
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea chicken bouillon cube
1 c  cream
2 tb tarragon
2 tb basil
1 ea clove garlic, pressed
1 tb soy sauce
2 lb asparagus, sliced into 1"
3 ea leeks, sliced
4 ea green onion, sliced thinly
1 ea red potato, chopped small
3 tb butter
4 c  water
Saute vegetables in butter for 5 minutes.  Add water and bouillon
cube; Simmer 5 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer 10
minutes more.  Serve.  Serves 4
