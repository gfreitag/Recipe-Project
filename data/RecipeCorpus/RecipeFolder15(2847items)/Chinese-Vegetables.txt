Title: Chinese Vegetables
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Bamboo shoots
1/3 c  Water chestnut
2 tb Cornstarch
1 tb Soy sauce
Rice, cooked
2 c  Celery, sliced
1 c  Onion, sliced
2 tb Peanut oil
10 oz Chicken broth, can
16 oz Bean sprout;can
In a skillet or wok, cook diagonally sliced celery and onion in oil till
tender.
Add remaining ingredients except rice. Cook over low heat, stirring
till thickened.
Serve over rice with additional soy sauce.
