Title: Jack's Punch
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 12 servings
4 ea LARGE BANANAS, MASHED
2 c  SUGAR
1 x  LEMON LIME SODA, AS NEEDED
1 c  LEMON JUICE
3 c  ORANGE JUICE
40 oz PINEAPPLE JUICE
MIX ALL INGREDIENTS EXCEPT FOR LEMON-LIME SODA. FREEZE MIXTURE. TO SERVE
PARTLY THAW AND MIX WITH EQUAL PART SODA. STIR LIGHTLY.
