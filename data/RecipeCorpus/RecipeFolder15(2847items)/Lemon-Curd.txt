Title: Lemon Curd
Categories: Desserts, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 2 servings
3 ea lemons, juiced
4 ea eggs, beaten
3/4 c  butter
2 c  sugar
Melt butter in double boiler.  Mix in sugar and lemon juice until
dissolved.  Add beaten eggs and stir until it thickens.  Serve on warm
toast.  Makes 2 1/2 cups. Stores well.
