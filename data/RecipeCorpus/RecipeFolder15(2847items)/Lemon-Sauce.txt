Title: Lemon Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Sugar
Few drops yellow food color
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Water
4 ts Cornstarch
Combine all ingredients, except for food color, in a small saucepan. Whisk
over medium heat until thickened. Add food coloring and stir to mix.
Adjust lemon and sugar to taste.
