Title: Rice Pudding
Categories: Rice, Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts SALT
1 ds NUTMEG
1 ea GRATED LEMON RIND TO TASTE
1/3 c  UNCOOKED RICE
1 qt MILK
1/3 c  SUGAR
MIX ALL INGREDIENTS AND PLACE IN A BAKING DISH. BAKE AT 300 DEG F. FOR 3
HOURS, STIRRING THREE TIMES DURING THE FIRST HOUR. THIS RECIPE MAY BE
DOUBLED IN SIZE.
