Title: Dott Kremer's Rolled Cookies
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 15 servings
1 x  EGG
1 tb BAKING POWDER
1 ts VANILLA
1 c  SUGAR
1/2 c  CRISCO
2 tb MILK
MIX ALL INGREDIENTS WELL - CHILL - ROLL TO 1/4" THICK
CUT AS DESIRED AND BAKE AT 350 DEG F FOR 15 TO 20 MINUTES. SPRINKLE WITH
SUGAR AND SERVE OT STORE.
