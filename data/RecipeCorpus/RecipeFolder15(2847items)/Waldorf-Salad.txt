Title: Waldorf Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
3 ea ORANGES
4 c  DICED SPICY APPLES
4 c  CUT-UP CELERY
2 c  MAYONNAISE
1 c  MALAGA GRAPES
1 ea HEAD LETTUCE
1 c  NUT MEATS
3 ea BANANAS
DICE ALL FRUIT CAREFULLY. CUT CELERY INTO SMALL PIECES. MIX WELL.
ADD MAYONNAISE AND TOSS TO WELL COAT FRUIT. SERVE ON TOP OF LETTUCE
LEAVES.
CHILL WELL BEFORE SERVING.
