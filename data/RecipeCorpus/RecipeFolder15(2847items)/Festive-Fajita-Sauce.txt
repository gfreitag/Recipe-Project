Title: Festive Fajita Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  CILANTRO
1 ea SLICED JALAPENO PEPPER
1 ea DICED RED PEPPER
1 ea SALT & PEPPER TO TASTE
1/2 c  LIME JUICE
1/4 c  OIL
1 ea ONION, SLICED
1 tb MINCED GARLIC
USE ON SEAFOOD, CHICKEN, TOFU, TEMPEH, FLANK STEAK. MIX ALL INGREDIENTS
WELL. SPREAD ON MEAT AND BROIL OR GRILL.
