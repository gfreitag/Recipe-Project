Title: Five-Spice Bouquet
Categories: Spices
Yield: 1 servings
1    Cardamon
1    Ginger root
1    Small piece of star anise
1    Cassia bark
1    Orange peel
Put ingredients together in tea strainer or cheesecloth bag.  Add to soups
or sauces during cooking; remove before serving.  Makes 1 bouquet
