Title: Pumpkin Cake (M_c-Tx)
Categories: Cakes, Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 12 servings
2    Egg yolks or 1 egg
1/2 c  Sour milk
1/4 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Chopped floured
Nuts
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Shortening
3/4 c  Pumpkin
2 ts Mapleine flavoring
4 ts Baking powder
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  White sugar
Cream sugar and shortening.  Add other ingredients in order given.  Bake
1/2 hour at 350  in pan 6 1/2" x 11".  Good without frosting served warm,
with apple cider.
