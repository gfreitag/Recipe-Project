Title: Black Jack Bbq Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
3 tb Chili powder
2 c  Chopped onions
6 x  Cloves garlic, minced
1 c  Strong black coffee
1 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Minced hot chili peppers
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer 25 mins. Strain or puree
in a blender or food processor. Refrigerate between uses.
