Title: Italian Slaw
Categories: Salads, Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
-------------------------------BOIL TOGETHER-------------------------------
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
1 ts Celery seed
3/4 c  Oil
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Sugar
Pour over:  1 medium head green cabbage, shredded and 1 medium onion (cut
into rings).  Let stand overnight.  This keeps in refrigerator 10 days.
Cindy Mercer
