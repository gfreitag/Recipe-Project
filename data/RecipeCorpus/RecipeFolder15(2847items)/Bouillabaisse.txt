Title: Bouillabaisse
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
2 ea stalks celery, sliced
1 ts grated nutmeg
1 ts basil
1 ts thyme
1 ea bay leaf
2 tb soy sauce
2 qt water
12 ea slices french bread
1 1/2 lb mussels, cut in pieces
1 c  white wine
1 1/2 lb sole, cut in 1" pieces
1 1/2 lb snapper, cut in 1" pieces
2 ts ground saffron
4 tb + 2 tsp butter
5 ea garlic cloves, crushed
2 ea onions, sliced
1 ea carrot, sliced
Steam mussels 3-4 minutes.  Dissolve saffron in warm wine; set aside.
Melt butter; add garlic, onion, carrot, celery, seasonings, and soy sauce.
Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.  Add water and wine; bring to boiling; add
fish.  Reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.  Serve with toasted bread.
Serves 6
