Title: Cottage Cheese Pie (M_c-Tx)
Categories: Pies, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 tb Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 lb Cottage cheese
1/2 c  Sugar
3    Well beaten eggs
Sieve cottage cheese.  Mix ingredients and put in pie shell.  Sprinkle
cinnamon on top.  Bake for 20 minutes at 350  or 375  until done. Mrs. Erma
E. Derr
