Title: Barbecue Spice Rub
Categories: Spices, Sauces
Yield: 1 servings
1 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Onion powder
2 tb Salt
2 tb Sugar
1 tb Brown sugar
2 ts Chili powder
2 ts Paprika
Combine all spices in a small bowl. Store in an air tight container. To
use: rub on meat before grilling.
