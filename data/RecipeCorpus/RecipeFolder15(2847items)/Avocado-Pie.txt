Title: Avocado Pie
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1 ea graham cracker crust, baked
1 c  crushed pecans
1 c  whipping cream, whipped (opt
1 ea ripe avocado, mashed
1 pk (8 oz) cream cheese
1 cn (13 oz) sweetened condensed
1/3 c  lemon juice
Blend avocado, cream cheese, milk and lemon juice in blender until
smooth.  Pour into baked 9-inch graham cracker crust.  Chill 1 hour
before serving.  Top with crushed pecans and whipped cream, if
desired.  Serves 8
