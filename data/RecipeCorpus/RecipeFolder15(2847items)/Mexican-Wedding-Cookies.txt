Title: Mexican Wedding Cookies
Categories: Mexican, Cookies
Yield: 36 servings
1 c  Powdered sugar
1 c  Ground nuts
1 c  Butter, softened
2 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Vanilla
Combine all ingredients. Form into 1 1/2" balls. Bake on cookie sheet at
350 for about 10-15 minutes or until set. Roll in powdered sugar while
still warm.
