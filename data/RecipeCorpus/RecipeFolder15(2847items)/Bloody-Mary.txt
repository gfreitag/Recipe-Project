Title: Bloody Mary
Categories: Beverages, Cocktails
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Drops tabasco sauce
1 x  Pepper; to taste
1 x  Salt; to taste
1 1/2 oz Vodka
3 oz Tomato juice
1 ea Lemon; juiced
1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
Shake with ice and strain into old-fashioned glass over ice cubes. A wedge
of lime may be added.
