Title: Brandy Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Nutmeg
1/4 c  Brandy
1 ts Real Vanilla
1 c  Water
2 tb Corn Starch
2 tb Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
Mix dry ingredients and then stir them into a cup of boiling water.  Boil
for 5 minutes and then add butter, brandy, and vanilla.  Serve hot over
mince pie or gingerbread.
