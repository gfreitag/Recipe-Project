Title: Pineapple Glaze
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Pineapple, crushed
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Mustard
1 c  Brown sugar
1 tb Cornstarch
1/4 ts Salt
Used on Baked Ham.
Mix brown sugar, cornstarch and salt in saucepan. Stir in pineapple,
lemon juice and mustard.
Cook over medium heat, stirring till thick. Boil, stirring 1 minute.
