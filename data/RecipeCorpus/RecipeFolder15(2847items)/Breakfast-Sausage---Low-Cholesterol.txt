Title: Breakfast Sausage - Low Cholesterol
Categories: Breakfast, Meats, Poultry
Yield: 8 servings
1 ts GROUND SAGE
1 x  SMALL ONION CHOPPED
1/4 c  BREAD CRUMBS
1 tb OIL
1 lb GROUND TURKEY
1/2 ts SALT
1/2 ts PEPPER
1/2 ts PAPRIKA
MIX ALL INGREDIENTS WELL. CHILL OVERNIGHT. FRY SLOWLY IN A LITTLE OIL
UNTIL BROWN ON BOTH SIDES.
