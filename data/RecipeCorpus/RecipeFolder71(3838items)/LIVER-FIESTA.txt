Title: LIVER FIESTA
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Sliced liver; thinly
3 tb Flour;
1/2 ts Salt;
3 tb Margarine -=OR=-
3 tb Salad oil;
1/2 c  Onion; sliced thinly
1/2 c  Bell pepper; sliced thinly
-- (optional)
1 c  Tomatoes; stewed
1/3 c  All-purpose barbecue sauce;
-- any flavor
MMMMM------------------------MY ADDITIONS-----------------------------
1/4    Green pepper;
1/4    Red pepper;
1/4    Yellow pepper;
1/2 c  Mushrooms;
Remove heavy membranes and veins from liver, if any. Cut into 4 x
1-1/2" strips.  Dredge liver in flour and salt. Lightly brown in
margarine or oil in skillet. Remover skillet; keep warm. Saute onion
and green pepper (I used yellow, red, green and about a cup of bell
pepper. Next time I will also add some mushrooms.) in skillet until
onion is transparent. Add tomatoes with barbacue sauce.  Bring to a
boil. Add liver; heat thoroughly, about 10 minutes. Makes 4 servings.
