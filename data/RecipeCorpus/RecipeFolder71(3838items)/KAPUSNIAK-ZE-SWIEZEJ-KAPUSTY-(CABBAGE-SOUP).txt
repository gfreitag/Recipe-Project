Title: KAPUSNIAK ZE SWIEZEJ KAPUSTY (CABBAGE SOUP)
Categories: Poland, Soups/stews, Ham/pork
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 lb Pork, lean; cut into small
-pieces
1 tb Butter, unsalted
1 cn Beef broth (10 1/2 oz)
1 cn Soup, tomato
2    Soup can of water
4 c  Cabbage; shredded
1/2 c  Onion; chopped
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
1    Bay leaf
1 tb Sherry
Sour Cream
Brown pork in butter. Add remaining ingredients except
sour cream. Cover; cook over low heat--30 min.; stir
often. Serve with spoonsfull of sour cream.  A Balkan
stew-soup to make a supper. Origin: A Campbell
cookbook--Cooking with Soup. GEnie F&WRT
