Title: LEMON BASIL HERB-AND-SPICE-SEASONED SALT SUBS
Categories: Misc
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Dried lemon basil or Dill
- finely crumbled
1 ts Dried oregano leaves
- finely crumbled
2 tb Onion powder;  -=OR=-
- finely ground onion flakes
1 ts Celery seed
2 tb Sesame seeds, toasted
1/4 ts Grated dried lemon peel
1 pn Freshly ground pepper
1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Garlic powder
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and blend well. Put into a
shaker with large holes.  Store in a cool dark place. Makes about 3
ounces.
