Title: LOG ROLL
Categories: Candies
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar;granulated
1/2 c  White corn syrup
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Butter
1/2 lb Milk chocolate
1 c  Nuts;chopped
-pecans are preferable
Mix sugar, syrup, milk and butter in pan. Cook to firm-ball stage.
Set aside to cool, then add vanilla and beat until firm. shape in
rolls about 1 1/2 inch through, and in length easy to roll (like a
candy bar). When cold, dip in chocolate which has been melted over
hot water. Roll in chopped nuts and slice when cold.
