Title: LOBSTER CHOWDER
Categories: Seafood, Stews
Yield: 1 Batch
2 c  Lobster meat
2 c  Potato, raw; diced
4 T  Butter
1 sm Onion; sliced
1 c  ;Water
Paprika
2 c  Milk, hot
Salt & pepper
Simmer the lobster meat in the butter over low heat for 7 to 8
minutes. Combine potatoes, onion and water and boil until tender. Add
hot milk and lobster mixture.  Season to taste and add a dash of
paprika.
