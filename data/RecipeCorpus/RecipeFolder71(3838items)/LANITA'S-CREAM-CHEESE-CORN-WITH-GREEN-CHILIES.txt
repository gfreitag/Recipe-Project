Title: LANITA'S CREAM CHEESE CORN WITH GREEN CHILIES
Categories: Side dish, Jaw, Vegetables, Mexican
Yield: 8 Servings
2 tb Butter
1    8 oz pkg. cream cheese
2    16 oz cans whole kernel
Corn, drained
1    4 oz can chopped green
Chilies
2 ts Minced onion
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic salt
Cayenne pepper to taste
Melt butter in 2 quart casserole. Blend cream cheese until mixed and
softened. Add remaining ingredients. Bake at 350 for 25 to 30 minutes.
