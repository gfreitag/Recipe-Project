Title: MANGO FRAPE
Categories: Beverages, Breakfast, Fruit
1 lg mango -- chopped
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  yogurt -- * see note
1 c  skim milk -- chilled
1/2 c  ice

Place all ingrediants into blend and hit the "kill" button. Pour into
tall glasses, garnish with mint.
