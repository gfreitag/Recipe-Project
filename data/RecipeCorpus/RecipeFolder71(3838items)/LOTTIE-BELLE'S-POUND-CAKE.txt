Title: LOTTIE BELLE'S POUND CAKE
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
3 c  Sugar
8 oz Butter
5    Eggs
1 tb Vanilla extract
1 tb Almond extract
3 c  Cake flour
8 oz Whipping cream
Grease and flour one 9-inch bundt pan. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
(or you can grease and sugar the pan for a sugary crust!)

Cream butter and sugar until fluffy. Continue beating and add eggs
one at a time.  Add vanilla and lemon extracts. Alternate the four
and whipping cream until all are combined.

Bake at 350 degrees for about one hour.
