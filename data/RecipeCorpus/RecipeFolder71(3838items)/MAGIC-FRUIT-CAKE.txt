Title: MAGIC FRUIT CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Fruits, Desserts
Yield: 12 Servings
1 c  Mincemeat (15 oz) jar
1 c  Eagle Brand Milk(can)
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Each red and green cherries.
1 ea Egg
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Baking powder
Stir well to-gether and pour into greased and floured tube pan and
bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours. (Batter may seem thin but do not add
extra flour).
