Title: LEMON FIG ICE-CREAM
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 c  Whipping cream
1 c  Milk
1    Egg, well beaten
Few grains salt
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Chopped preserved figs, and
-juice
Juice 2 lemons
Combine egg, sugar, salt, figs and juice, lemon juice, and milk. Pour
into freezer.  Partially freeze. Carefully fold in whipped cream.
Continue freezing until firm. 8 servings. The Household Searchlight
