Title: MAPLE BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 ts Yeast
3 c  Bread Flour
1 tb Butter
2 tb Dry Milk
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Maple Syrup warm
3/4 c  Water warm
Put all ingredients in Hitachi Bread Machine and set bread color for
light. Now start the regular bread cycle and that's it.

SOURCE:*Jim Bodle 3/92
