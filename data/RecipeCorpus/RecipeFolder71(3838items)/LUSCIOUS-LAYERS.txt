Title: LUSCIOUS LAYERS
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk JELL-O INSTANT PUDDING
1 1/2 c  MILK (COLD)
1 c  COOLWHIP
PREPARE PUDDING AS PER DIRECTIONS ON PACKAGE. SPOON 1/4 CUP PUDDING
INTO EACH OF FOUR GLASSES. FOLD REMAINING PUDDING INTO COOLWHIP.
SPOON OVER PLAIN PUDDING IN GLASSES. GARNISH WITH MORE COOLWHIP IF
DESIRED.
