Title: KOHL'S CREAM CHEESE FROSTING
Categories: Improv, Kohl, Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 1 Serving
8 oz Package cream cheese
1    Stick butter or oleo
1    Box powdered sugar
2 ts Vanilla
Soften cream cheese to room temperature. Blend all
ingredients together until smooth enough to spread on
a cooled cake.

NOTE: Especially great on a carrot cake.
Taken From: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK
A Collection of Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce &
Clem Kohl
