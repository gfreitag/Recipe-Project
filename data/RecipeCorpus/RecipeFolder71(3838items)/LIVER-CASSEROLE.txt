Title: LIVER CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish, Casseroles, Sthrn/livng
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Liver, beef; cut in 1"pieces
2 tb Oil, salad
1    Onion; chopped
1 c  Celery; chopped
1 cn Soup, mushroom; undiluted
1 c  Cheese, Cheddar; shredded
2 c  Beans, lima; cooked
1/4 c  Milk
Brown liver in oil.  Add onion and celery; simmer 10 minutes. Remove
from heat, and stir in remaining ingredients. Spoon mixture into a
1-1/2 quart casserole.  Bake at 300 degrees for 30 minutes.
