Title: MALAYSIAN TEA
Categories: Tea, Beverages, Malasia
Yield: 8 Servings
8 c  Boiling water
4    Bags green tea or
8 ts Loose green tea leaves
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cardamon
2 tb Sugar or other sweetener
Place all ingredients in a teapot and allow to steep for 2 minutes or
to taste.  Serve alone or with slivered almonds.

Source: Vegetarian Journal  Sep/Oct 1996
