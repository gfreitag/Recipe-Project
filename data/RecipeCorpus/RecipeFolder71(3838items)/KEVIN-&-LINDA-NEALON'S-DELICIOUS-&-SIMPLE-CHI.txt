Title: KEVIN & LINDA NEALON'S DELICIOUS & SIMPLE CHI
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
1 tb Olive oil
1 lg Onion; diced
1/2    Green bell pepper; chopped
3 cn Crushed tomatoes (28-oz ea.)
2 cn Dark red kidney beans
-- (40-oz each), drained
3 tb Chili powder (or to taste)
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar (optional)
Heat the oilve oil in a very large frying pan or Dutch
oven over medium heat.  Saute the onions and pepper
until tender, about 5 to 7 minutes.

Add the remaining ingredients and bring the mixture to
a boil.  Lower the heat and simmer, covered, for 1
hour.

Preparation time: 10 minutes Cooking time: 1 hour
