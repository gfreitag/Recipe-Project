Title: LIGURIAN CAVIALE DEI POVERI (SIMULATED CAVIAR
Categories: Appetizers, Italian
Yield: 1 Cup
3/4 c  Italian black olives,pitted
1/4 c  Capers; drained
1/4 c  Anchovy fillets
1/4 c  Extra virgin olive oil
4 tb Lemon juice; fresh squeezed
In food processor or blender, mix all ingredients together to obtain a
smooth spread, about 10 seconds. Serve this olive paste "peasant
caviar" in a bowl as a side dish to be spread on rustic Italian bread
for picnics, barbecues, or a buffet appetizer. Chef's tip: to pit,
place olive on a board and press with a knife blade. The pit should
easily be removed with your fingers.
