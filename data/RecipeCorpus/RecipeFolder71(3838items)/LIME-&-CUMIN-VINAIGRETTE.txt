Title: LIME & CUMIN VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Sauces, Dressings
Yield: 2 Servings
2 tb Fresh lime juice
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
1/2 ts Cumin
1/2 ts Chili powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
In a bowl whisk together the lime juice, the lemon juice, the cumin,
the chili powder, and the salt, add the oil in a stream, whisking,
and whisk the vinaigrette until it is emulsified. Makes about 1/2 cup.
