Title: MAPLE CREAM CANDY
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Cream
3 tb Butter
Stir all together. Boil until it drops from spoon. Add vanilla. Beat
until it thickens. Pour into buttered pan.

From the book "Treasured Mennonite Recipes" from the Mennonite
Community Relief Sales Volume 1
