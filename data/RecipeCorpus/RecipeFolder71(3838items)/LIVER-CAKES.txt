Title: LIVER CAKES
Categories: Penndutch, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Liver, ground
1 x  Bacon, sliced
1 ea Sm Onion, finely chopped
1 tb Flour
1 ea Egg, well beaten
1 x  Salt & pepper
To the ground liver add the egg, flour, onion, and salt & pepper. Mix
well. Shape into round cakes and around each cake wrap a slice of
bacon, fastening each with a toothpick. Put on hot skillet and fry
until well done. Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old
Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
