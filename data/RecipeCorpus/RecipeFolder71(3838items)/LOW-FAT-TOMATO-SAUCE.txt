Title: LOW-FAT TOMATO SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Olive oil
1    Onion -- chopped
20 ml Garlic -- minced
2 tb Parsley -- minced
4 c  Plum tomatoes -- crushed
1/4 sm Green pepper -- chopped
Put all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer for about 45 minutes.
