Title: LIGHT RYE
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
4 ts Active dry yeast
2 tb Caraway seeds
7/8 c  Rye flour
3 c  Bread flour
2 tb Butter
3 tb Sugar, scant
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 1/2 c  Water
Add all ingredients and "start".

Note:  Don't use gluten.
