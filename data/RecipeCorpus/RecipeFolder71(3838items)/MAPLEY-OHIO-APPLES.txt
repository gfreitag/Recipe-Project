Title: MAPLEY OHIO APPLES
Categories: Desserts, Apples
Yield: 8 Servings
8    Tart Juicy Apples
6 tb Light Brown Sugar
1 tb Cinnamon
1 tb Butter
2 tb Lemon Juice
1 c  Nuts; chopped
3/4 c  Pure Maple Syrup
1/2 c  Water
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Core apples and pare them about 1/3 down from the stem end. Mix sugar,
spices, nuts and fill center of each apple, press in firmly. Pat
surplus sugar mix on pared portion of apple. Top with dot of butter.
Place apples in shallow baking pan.
Combine maple syrup, water, lemon juice and salt, heat quickly to
boiling and remove from fire and add vanilla. Pour syrup mixture
gently on top of each apple and around apples.
Bake 350 for 40-45 min.  Baste with pan liquid at the end of first 15
min., then 2 more times.  Test with toothpick for doneness at the end
of 30 min.  Serve with cream.
