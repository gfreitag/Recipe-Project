Title: LEFTOVER POTATO CAKES VERSION 2
Categories: Breakfast, Kooknet, Cyberealm
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Leftover Mashed Potatoes
Butter
Crushed Cornflakes
Roll the potatoes into balls and flatten them.  Dip them in Melted
butter. Roll them in crushed cornflakes. Place them in a greased pan.
Bake the balls until they are well heated.
