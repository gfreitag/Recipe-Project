Title: LEMONY ICED TEA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
8 ts Bags
1 c  Sugar
1 ga Water
1 sm Can lemonade concentrate
Place tea bags in pan.  Add only enough water to cover bags and bring
to a boil.  Remove tea bags and add sugar. Add 1 gallon water. Stir
in lemonade and chill. If you want stronger tea, let tea bags stand
for a time in the refrigerator.
