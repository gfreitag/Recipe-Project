Title: LES' COTTAGE CHEESE GARDEN SALAD
Categories: Salad, Side dishes, Cheese
Yield: 6 Servings
24 oz Cottage Cheese, small curd
1    Stalk celery, diced
1/2    Carrot, grated
1/2    Green Bell Pepper, seeded,
-diced
4    Scallions, diced
1    Plum tomato, seeded, diced
Salt and Pepper, to taste
Mix all ingredients together. Refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours.
(Overnight is best. Serve on a bed of fresh spinach or lettuce.
Garnish with chopped or whole scallions.
