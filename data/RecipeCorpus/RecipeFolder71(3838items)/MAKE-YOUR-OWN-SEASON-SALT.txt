Title: MAKE YOUR OWN SEASON SALT
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
2 tb Salt
2 tb Paprika
1 ts Ground pepper
1 ts Onion salt
1 ts Garlic salt
MMMMM------------------------PREPARATION-----------------------------
Combine.  Blend thoroughly, put into a shaker and use in place on
other seasonings. Source: Cookbook USA
