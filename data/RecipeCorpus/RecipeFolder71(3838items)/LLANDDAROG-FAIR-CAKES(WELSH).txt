Title: LLANDDAROG FAIR CAKES(WELSH)
Categories: Cakes, Welsh
Yield: 6 Cakes
12 oz Seff-raising flour
8 oz Butter
6 oz Castor sugar
3 tb Beer
4 oz Mixed fruit
TEISENNAU FFAIR LLANDDAROG Rub the butter into: the flour and add the
sugar. IMix in the beer to form a soft dough. Roll out on a floured
board to a thickness of 1/2 inch. Cut into rounds and press the fruit
into ths surface. Cook in a moderate oven (376/F. or Mark 5) for 15
minutes.
