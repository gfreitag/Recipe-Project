Title: MAPLE WALNUT RAISED ROLLS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Scalded milk
2 tb Shortening, melted in milk
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1    Yeast cake
2 1/2 c  Flour
Raise about an hour, or until light.  Roll out about 1/2 inch thick
and sprinkle with broken walnuts and a very little maple syrup. Roll
up and cut off in 1-inch slices. Place in muffin tins, each one
containing a tablespoon of maple syrup and a few nuts. Allow to rise
and bake 15 to 20 minutes in about 400 oven.

From: A Vermont Cook Book Shared By: Pat Stockett
