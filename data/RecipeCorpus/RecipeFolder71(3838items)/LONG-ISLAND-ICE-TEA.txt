Title: LONG ISLAND ICE TEA
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
Equal parts:
Mr. Boston Rum
Mr. Boston Gin
Mr. Boston Vodka
Mr. Boston Triple Sec
Lemon Juice
Orange Juice
Mix in a highball glass over ice and add a dash of cola. I guess you
can try it with any kind of rum, gin, vodka and triple sec. Well,
good luck with the recipe and DON'T DRINK AND DRIVE !!
