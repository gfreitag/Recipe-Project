Title: KOHL'S CARROT CAKE #2
Categories: Improv, Kohl, Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 1 Serving
1 1/2 c  Cooking oil
2 c  White sugar
4    Eggs, beaten
2 c  Flour
2 ts Cinnamon
2 ts Baking soda
2 ts Baking powder
3 c  Grated carrots
1 c  Chopped pecans
Beat together: Oil and sugar. Add eggs and beat well.
Sift flour, cinnamon, soda and baking powder. Blend
with eggs mixture. Fold in carrots and nuts.  Bake at
350 degrees for 1 hour in well greased pan. If you use
layer cake pans, bake 30 minutes.

Frost with cream cheese frosting.

Taken From: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK
A Collection of Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce &
Clem Kohl
