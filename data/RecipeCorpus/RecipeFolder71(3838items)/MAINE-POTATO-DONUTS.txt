Title: MAINE POTATO DONUTS
Categories: Doughnuts
1 c  sugar
Yield: 1 Servings
2    eggs
3/4 c  milk
3/4 c  cold mashed potato
3 3/4 c  flour
1/4 c  melted shortening
1 ts vanilla
4 ts baking powder
3/4 ts ginger

Mix sugar and eggs, add milk and potato. Add shortening and vanilla.
Sift dry ingredients, add and mix well. Chill 1 hour before frying.
Roll, cut out and fry in deep fat at 375.
