Title: LENTIL-NUT LOAF
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Lentils, washed
2 c  Water
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 c  Mushrooms, chopped
2 tb Oil
1 c  Nuts, ground
1 c  Breadcrumbs
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb Soy sauce
Salt & pepper
1 tb Mixed herbs
Cook lentils in water till soft (1/2 hour).  Saute onions & mushrooms
in oil till soft.  Mix with remaining ingredients. Place in a greased
baking pan & bake at 350F for 30 minutes. Garnish with parsley.
