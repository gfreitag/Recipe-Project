Title: KID'S KRUNCHY KRAZY KORN
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 2 Servings
MMMMM-------------------PILLSBURY HOLIDAY CKBK------------------------
8 c  Puffed popcorn curls; from
8 oz. pkg.*
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter
1/4 c  Light corn syrup
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Vanilla
Heat oven to 250F.  Place popcorn curls in 15x10x1"
baking pan. In lrg. saucepan, combine brown sugar,
margarine and cornsyrup; bring to a boil over med.
heat, stirring constantly. Simmer 2 mins.; remove from
heat. Stir in baking soda and vanilla.  Pour mixture
evenly over popcorn curls; mix well. Bake at 250 for
30 mins., stirring twice during baking. Immediately
remove from pan; cool completely on waxed paper or
greased foil. Store in tightly covered container.
Makes 8 cups.

*TIP:  Eight cups popped popcorn can be substituted
for puffed popcorn curls. 1/2 cup peanuts can be added
to popcorn curls.
