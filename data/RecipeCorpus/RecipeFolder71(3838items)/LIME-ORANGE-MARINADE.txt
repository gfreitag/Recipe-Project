Title: LIME-ORANGE MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
4 ea Cloves garlic,finely chopped
1 1/2 c  Lime juice
1 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Peanut oil
2 ts Ground black pepper
Chop garlic and mix with remaining ingredients in a bowl. Marinate
ribs, chops, or thick cuts of beef with this tangy, spicy
combination. Always try to use fresh juices. When they're not readily
available, use the frozen or bottled variety.

Source:  Mesquite Cooking, by John 'Boog' Powell
