Title: MANGO SALSA (JURASSIC)
Categories: Press, Salsa, Theme resta, Fruit
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Whole mangos
2/3 c  Chopped red bell pepper
2/3 c  Chopped green bell pepper
1 tb Minced seeded jalapeno --
Or
Serrano pepper
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
Peel two 14- to 16-ounce ripe but firm mangos.  Cut flesh away from
the center pit. Dice. Place in medium bowl. Add sweet peppers. Add
seasonings. Gently toss to blend. Refrigerate from 1 to 3 hours
before serving. Recipe By
: Jurassic Cove Cafe / Philip Costner, executive chef (1996)
