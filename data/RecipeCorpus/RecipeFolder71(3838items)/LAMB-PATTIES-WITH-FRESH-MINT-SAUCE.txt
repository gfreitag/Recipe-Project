Title: LAMB PATTIES WITH FRESH MINT SAUCE
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Lean ground lamb
2/3 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/3 c  Dry red wine
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Dried rosemary leaves,
-crushed
2 sm Cloves garlic, finely
-chopped
-Fresh Mint Sauce (below)
Set oven control to broil. Spray broiler pan rack with nonstick
cooking spray. Mix all ingredients except Fresh Mint Sauce. Shape
lamb mixture into 4 patties, each about I inch thick. Place patties
on rack in broiler pan. Broil with tops about 3 inches  from heat 5
to 7 minutes on each side for medium or until desired doneness. Serve
with Fresh Mint Sauce. Microwave Directions: Mix all ingredients
except Fresh Mint Sauce. Shape lamb mixture into 4 patties, each
about l inch thick. Place patties on microwavable rack in
microwavable dish. Cover with vented plastic  wrap and microwave on
high 6 to 7 minutes, rotating dish 1/2 turn after 3 minutes, until
patties are almost done.

Let stand covered 3 minutes. Serve with Fresh Mint Sauce.

4 servings. From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska. Feb 1994
