Title: MAKING BACON
Categories: Microwave, Pork, Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
4 ea Slices bacon
3 ea Paper towels
LAYER 3 PAPER TOWELS ON MICROWAVE PLATE.  ARRANGE REGULAR SLICED
BACON ON PLATE WITH NO SPACE BETWEEN SLICES. COVER WITH ONE PAPER
TOWEL. MICROWAVE AT HIGH AS DIRECTED BELOW. DO NOT OVERCOOK.
IMMEDIATELY RE- MOVE BACON FROM TOWEL. BACON TIME 2 SLICES 1 3/4 TO 3
MIN. 4 SLICES 3 3/4 TO 5 MIN.
4    slices makes 1 serving.
