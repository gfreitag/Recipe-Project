Title: KIELBASI BEAN EGG DROP SOUP
Categories: Soups/stews
Yield: 8 Servings
1 pk (16 oz) dry lima beans
5 md Carrots - sliced
10 oz Frozen corn
1/2    Ring kielbasi - thin sliced
2 md Eggs
3 tb Butter
1/2 ts Adobo seasoning
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Parsley
1/2 ts Onion flakes
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 tb Maple syrup
1 tb Minced garlic
Pre-soak lima beans, then boil with carrots 15-20
mins. Add other ingredients. Be sure soup is HOT when
adding eggs and stir immediately. Heat additional
30-45 minutes over med heat.

Source: Patricia E. Beuerlein
