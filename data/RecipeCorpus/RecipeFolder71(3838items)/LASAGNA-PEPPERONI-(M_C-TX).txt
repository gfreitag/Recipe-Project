Title: LASAGNA PEPPERONI (M_C-TX)
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 8 Servings
8 oz Package lasagna noodles
-cooked
1 lg Jar spaghetti sauce
A pound ground beef browned
Pepperoni slices
Italian spices:  garlic
-basil, oregano
1 c  Parmesan cheese, shredded
4 c  Mozzarella cheese, shredded
Mix together sauce and ground beef. Layer noodles, sauce, pepperoni
and cheeses.  Sprinkle each layer lightly with spices. Top with
cheeses and spices. Bake at 350 until bubbling. Serves 8.
