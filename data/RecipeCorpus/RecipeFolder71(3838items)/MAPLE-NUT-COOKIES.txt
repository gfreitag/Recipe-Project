Title: MAPLE NUT COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 18 Servings
1 c  Maple syrup
1 tb Molasses
1 c  Butter
3 ea Eggs
3 c  Whole wheat pastry flour
1 c  Walnuts
Cream together maple syrup, molasses, butter and eggs. Stir in flour
and walnuts by hand.  Drop by teaspoonsful onto unoiled cookie
sheets. Bake at 350 degrees for 7-10 minutes. Cookies will be golden
brown when done. Makes 36 cookies
