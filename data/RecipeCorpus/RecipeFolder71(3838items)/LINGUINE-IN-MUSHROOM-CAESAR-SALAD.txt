Title: LINGUINE IN MUSHROOM CAESAR SALAD
Categories: Salads, Cols.l&e
Yield: 4 Servings
8 oz Uncooked linguine
2 tb Olive oil
1 c  Sliced onion
1 lb Button mushrooms, sliced
1 ts Minced garlic
1    Jar roasted red peppers,
Drained and chopped
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
1 1/2 c  Caesar or italian flavoured
Croutons
1/3 c  Freshly grated Parmesan ches
Cook the linguine in salted boiling until firm-tender, about 10
minutes; drain, reserving one-half cup cooking liquid. Put pasta in
large bowl and cover to keep warm. While pasta is cooking, heat oil
in a skillet over medium-high heat and saute onion until soft, about
5 minutes. Reduce heat to medium, add mushrooms and garlic, and cook,
stirring, until mushrooms give up their liquied, about 6 minutes.
Stir in roasted peppers, salt and pepper to taste, and reserved
cooking liquid; heat through. Pour over linguine; add croutons and
cheese, and toss.
