Title: KOHL'S PINEAPPLE CHEESE SALAD
Categories: Improv, Kohl, Fruits, Salads
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 lg Can crushed pineapple, drain
-and save the juice
1 c  American cheese, cut up
1 c  Or more marshmallows
1/2 c  Sugar
1 tb Flour
1    Egg yolk
Salt
1/2 c  Pineapple juice
Put drained pineapple into a salad bowl. Add cheese
and marshmallows. Toss lightly. Make a dressing with
the juice, sugar, flour, egg yolk, and salt. If
dressing is too thick, add water. Pour over pineapple
mixture and stir gently. Chill before serving.

From: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK
A Collection of Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce &
Clem Kohl
