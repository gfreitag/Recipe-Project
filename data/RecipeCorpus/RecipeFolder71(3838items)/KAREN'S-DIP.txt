Title: KAREN'S DIP
Categories:
Yield: 12 Servings
16 oz Cheese, cream
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Grated parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Chopped green onion
10 sl Bacon, cooked and crumbled
5 oz Jar green olives, drained
-And chopped
2 dr Tabasco
Combine all ingredients.  Blend well.  Chill.  Serve
as a dip or form into a ball.  Serve with wheat
crackers.
