Title: MACARONI & CHEESE .002
Categories: Pasta, Main dish, Posted-bbs
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Macaroni, elbow
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 lb Cheese; grated
Salt
Black pepper, ground
Bread crumbs
1. Boil macaroni in salted water until tender. Drain and rinse with
hot water. Place in casserole dish.

2. In a saucepan, heat milk and cheese until the cheese is melted.
Add seasonings and pour over macaroni in casserole dish.

3. Mix with a fork, cover with crumbs and bake in moderate oven,
350F, for 15 minutes.

Adapted from "The American Woman's Cook Book" 1949. CompuChef
conversion by Rick Weissgerber
