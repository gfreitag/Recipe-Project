Title: KIJIGA
Categories: Beverages, Native, Cree
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 c  Maple sugar
10 c  Water
Pit all ingredients into a deep saucepan & simmer for
20 to 25 minutes, stirring gently.  Serve either hot
or ice cold. Apparently this drink can be served
either with cognac or p'tit caribou, a liqueur known
to many tribes & nations.
