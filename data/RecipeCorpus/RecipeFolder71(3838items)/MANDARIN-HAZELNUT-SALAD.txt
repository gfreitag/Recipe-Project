Title: MANDARIN HAZELNUT SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
8 c  Mixed greens
-torn into bite size pieces
2    Green onions; chopped
1/4 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Drained Mandarin oranges
MMMMM----------------------PARSLEY DRESSING---------------------------
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Cider vinegar
Salt and pepper to taste
1 ds Tabasco sauce
Mix all ingredients and allow flavors to blend.

1 cup roasted Oregon hazelnuts, carmelized*

Toss greens, onions, celery and oranges with dressing. Sprinkle with
hazelnuts.

*Carmelized hazelnuts: To carmelize hazelnuts, slowly melt 2
tablespoons of sugar in heavy skillet. Mix nuts, covering well with
sugar. Cook until a light amber color. Allow to cool on wax paper
before use.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings
