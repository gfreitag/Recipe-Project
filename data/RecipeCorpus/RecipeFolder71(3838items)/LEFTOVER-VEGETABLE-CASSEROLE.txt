Title: LEFTOVER VEGETABLE CASSEROLE
Categories: Vegetables, Casseroles, Leftovers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Cooked corn kernels
1 c  Cooked green beans
1 c  Finely chopped celery
1 c  Minced onion
1/2 c  Green pepper, finely chopped
4 c  Sharp cheddar, grated
1 c  Sour cream
1 cn Cream of celery soup
Mix all ingredients and place in a large, greased casserole dish.
Top with enough bread crumbs to cover and 1/4 cup melted butter,
drizzled evenly over top.  Slivered almonds can be sprinkled on top
if desired. bake at 350f for 45 minutes.
