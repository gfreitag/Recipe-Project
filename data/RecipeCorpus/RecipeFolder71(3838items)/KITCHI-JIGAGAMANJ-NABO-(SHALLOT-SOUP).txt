Title: KITCHI-JIGAGAMANJ-NABO (SHALLOT SOUP)
Categories: Soups, Native, Algonquin, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
2 1/2 lb Shallots
8 c  Water
3/4 c  Watercress
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
Finely chop the shallots.  Put all the ingredients
into a soup pot & simmer for 45 minutes.  Season &
serve.
