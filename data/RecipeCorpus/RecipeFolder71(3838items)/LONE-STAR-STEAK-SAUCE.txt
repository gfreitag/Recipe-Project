Title: LONE STAR STEAK SAUCE
Categories: Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce
3/4 ts Black Pepper
2 ea Drops Tabasco
1/2 c  Lemon Juice
1 ea Sm. Clove Garlic, Minced
1/2 ts Dry Mustard
1 x  Salt To Taste
Combine all ingredients, heat until butter melts. Broiler juices may
be added.  Serve with Steak Salt.
