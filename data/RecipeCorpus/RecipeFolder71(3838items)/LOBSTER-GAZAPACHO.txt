Title: LOBSTER GAZAPACHO
Categories: Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 8 Servings
12 oz Lobster meat
1    Cucumber
1    Yellow bell pepper, diced
1    Red bell pepper, diced
1/2    Red onion, diced
3    Tomatoes, diced
1    Jalapeno chile peper
2    Avocados
8    Artichoke hearts, diced
1 bn Cilantro, chopped
3 tb Balsamic vinegar
6 tb Olive oil
1    Lemon, juiced
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Black pepper
1/8 ts Sugar
Cook, chill, and mince lobster meat.  Peel, seed, and dice cucumber.
Seed and finely mince jalapeno.  Peel, pit, and dice avocados.

In large bowl, gently mix all ingredients together. Let soup sit for
4 hrs.

Serve soup cool, in chilled bowls.

From: Southern California Beach Recipe
