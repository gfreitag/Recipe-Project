Title: MANGO SORBET
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Sugar
3 ea Mangoes
Juice of 1 lime
Juice of 1 lemon
1 tb White rum, optional
Make a syrup by combining water & sugar in a non-corrosive pot &
bring to a boil.  Simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat & let cool.
Peel & puree the mangoes.  Strain them through a fine sieve. Combine
all the ingredients & freeze following instructions for making Lemon
Sorbet.
