Title: LOW-FAT COLESLAW
Categories: Salads, Low-cal
Yield: 4 Servings
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
1 ts Tahini
1/2 ts Dijon mustard
1/4 ts Celery seed
Dash Worchestershire sauce
1 3/4 c  Nonfat mayonnaise
1 1/2 c  Shredded green cabbage
1/2 c  Shredded red cabbage
Whisk together vinegar, tahini, mustard, celery seed and Worch.
sauce. Stir in mayonnaise. Add cabbages, toss well. Per serving: 22
calories, 0.7 g fat (25%)
