Title: LINGUINI WITH WHITE CLAM SAUCE
Categories: Fish and se, Italian, Pasta
Yield: 1 Servings
13 oz Clams, Canned -- chopped (2
Cans) *
1/4 c  Olive Oil
4    Cloves Garlic -- minced
1/4 c  Fresh Parsley -- chopped
Salt And Pepper
* with juice from one can.

Heat olive oil in skillet.  Add garlic.  Add parsley, clams, juice
and salt and pepper.  Heat 2 minutes and serve with linguini noodles.
