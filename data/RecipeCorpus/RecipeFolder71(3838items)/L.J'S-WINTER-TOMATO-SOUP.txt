Title: L.J'S WINTER TOMATO SOUP
Categories: Soups, Tomatoes, Alcohol
Yield: 2 Servings
1 cn Campbells tomato soup 10oz
10 oz Milk
5 oz Water
1 cn Mushroom pieces 10oz
1 c  Spaghetti noodles 1"pieces
1/4 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Garlic powder
1/4 ts Pepper (black)
1/4 ts Paprika (Hungarian)
10 dr Tobasco sauce
2 oz Navy rum (Dark)
1 tb Chicken stock
1 ts Soy sauce
Except for the spaghetti, mix all ingredients together. Bring to the
boil then turn heat to low. Break the spaghetti into 1 inch long
pieces, then in a seperate pot, cook until tender; drain and place
spaghetti into soup. Stir and bring soup to the boil agian then turn
heat to low.
