Title: KIWIFRUIT & STRAWBERRIES
Categories: Desserts, Fruits, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ea Kiwifruit
2 c  Strawberries
1 tb Orange juice; frozen
-- concentrates mixed with
-- 1 tb water
1 ts Pine nuts, toasted
Peel kiwifruit and slice in thin rounds.  Arrange on
dessert plates. Wash, hull and slice the strawberries
and arrange them over the kiwifruit. Drizzle orange
juice over each dish and top with pine nuts.

Betty Marks, "Light & Easy Diabetic Cuisine"  Posted
by Nancy O'Brion
