Title: LINDA'S CURRY DIP
Categories: Condiments, Appetizers
Yield: 20 Servings
32 oz Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Catsup
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1 tb Curry powder
6 dr Tabasco
Combine all ingredients; refrigerate overnight.  Core one head red
cabbage, forming a well; fill with dip. Serve with sliced raw
vegetables.
