Title: LEMON DRESSING FOR FRUIT SALAD
Categories: Side dish, White wine
Yield: 8 Servings
1/3 c  Lemonade or limeade
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1 ts Poppy seed
1/3 c  Dry white wine
2 tb Honey
Fruit salad
Beat all ingredients except fruit salad with rotary beater. Serve
with with your favorite fruit salad.
