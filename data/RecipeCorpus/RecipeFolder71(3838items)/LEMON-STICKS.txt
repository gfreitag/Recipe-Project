Title: LEMON STICKS
Categories: Cookies, Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 ea Frozen sheet of puff paste
1/2 ea Egg
1 x  Grated zest of 1/2 lemon
1 x  Powdered sugar
Let the puff paste thaw, cut into 4 long slices. Paint with the egg
and litter the lemon zest over it. Twist the slices, just a little.
Bake in 225 centigrades for 12-15 minutes. When cold litter some
powdered sugar over them.

Annica
