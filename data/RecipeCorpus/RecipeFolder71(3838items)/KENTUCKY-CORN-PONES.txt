Title: KENTUCKY CORN PONES
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Cornmeal
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Whipping cream
1 tb Butter
1 ts Baking powder
Combine cornmeal and salt; add boiling water and stir
well. Add remaining ingredients.  Drop mixture by
tablespoonfuls onto a greased griddle. Bake at 450 for
15 minutes or until edges are lightly browned.
