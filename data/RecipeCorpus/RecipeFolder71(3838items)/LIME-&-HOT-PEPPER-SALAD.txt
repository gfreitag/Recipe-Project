Title: LIME & HOT PEPPER SALAD
Categories: Poultry, Chinese, Salads
3 TB Soy sauce
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 TB Lime peel -- grated
1 TB Lime juice
1/2 TB Hot pepper sauce
4    Chicken Breasts Without Skin
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
4 c  Lettuce -- shredded

Combine soy sauce lime peel, juice, pepper sauce. Pour 2 Tbl over
chicken in a large plastic bag. Press air out of bag; close top
securely; turn bag over to coat. Refrigerate 30 minutes; turn bag
over occasionally. Meanwhile, blend remaining sauce mixture with oil
in small bowl with fork. Line platter with lettuce. Remove chicken
from marinade and broil 7 minutes or each side; Arrange over lettuce.
Serve with remaining dressing.
