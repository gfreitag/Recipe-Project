Title: LAST MINUTE CHILI
Categories: Chili
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Chili mix
Chopped green and red pepper
2 lb Ground beef
2 1/3 c  Cold water
1 cn (8 oz) tomato sauce
Sour cream
Shredded mild cheddar cheese
Prepare chili as directed on package using ground beef, water and
tomato sauce. To serve, spoon into individual serving bowls and top
with sour cream, cheese and peppers.
