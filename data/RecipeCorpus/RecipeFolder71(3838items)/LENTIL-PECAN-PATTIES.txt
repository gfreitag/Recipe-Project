Title: LENTIL PECAN PATTIES
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
2 c  cooked lentils
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  oatmeal
1 x  onion, minced
1 x  mushrooms, chopped (sm can)
1 c  pecans chopped fine
3 T  soy sauce (or substitute)
1/2 c  water
1/2 t  marjoram
1/4 t  sage

Thoroughly mix all ingredients. Form into patties and bake at 350
degrees for 30-40 minutes (turning after 15-20 minutes) until browned
on both sides. ~--
