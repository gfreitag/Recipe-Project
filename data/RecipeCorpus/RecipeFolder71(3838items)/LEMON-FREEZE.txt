Title: LEMON FREEZE
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 2 Servings
2 c  Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Grated lemon zest
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Honey
1 1/2 c  Cold heavy cream
1/2 c  Water
Combine all ingredients in a food processor. Puree until smooth. Pour
into a 9x9-inch metal baking dish and freeze for about 4 hours until
solid.

Yield: 2 servings
