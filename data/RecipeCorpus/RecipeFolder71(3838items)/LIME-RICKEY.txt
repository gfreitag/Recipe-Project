Title: LIME RICKEY
Categories: Non-alcohol, Cocktail, Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Fresh lime juice
1/2 c  Simple Syrup*
1/2 ts Bitters (12 dashes)
3 c  Club soda
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Lime-peel spirals
* See recipe "Simple Syrup"

1.  Mix lime juice, syrup and bitters in glass measure. 2. Pour into 4
ice-filled tall glasses.  Top each with 3/4 cup soda. Garnish with
lime peel.

Source:  Woman's Day 12/17/96
