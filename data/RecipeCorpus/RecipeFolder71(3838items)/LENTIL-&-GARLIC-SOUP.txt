Title: LENTIL & GARLIC SOUP
Categories: Pulses and, Soups
Yield: 1 Servings
7 c  Chicken Broth
2 c  Dry Lentils
2    Tomatoes -- peeled &
Quartered
4    Cloves Garlic -- coarsely
Chopped
2    Onions -- quartered
2 ts Ground Cumin
In a large pot, bring broth to a boil.  Add lentils, tomatoes,
garlic, and onions.  Partially cover pan and simmer 45 minutes or
until lentils are tender.  Put soup in blender. Return to pot and
season with cumin, salt, and pepper.
