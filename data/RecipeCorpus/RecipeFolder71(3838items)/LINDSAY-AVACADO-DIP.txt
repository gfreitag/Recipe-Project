Title: LINDSAY AVACADO DIP
Categories: Dips, Appetizers, Sauces
Yield: 999 Servings
10 lb Avacados (about 20)
3/4 lb Black Olives, chopped
- 3 cups
1/2 c  Green onion, sliced
3/4 c  Lime juice
1/2 c  Salsa
1 1/2 tb Salt (to taste)
Mash avacados.  Combine rest of ingredients.  Add additional Salsa or
seasonings as desired.  Makes 1 gallon
