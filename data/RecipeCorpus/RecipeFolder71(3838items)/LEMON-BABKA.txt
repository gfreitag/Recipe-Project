Title: LEMON BABKA
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar Lemon Glaze
1 pk Yeast 1/4 cup sugar
1/2 ts Salt 1/4 cup water
3/4 c  Milk 2 tbsp. lemon juice
1/2 c  Butter Heat 5 min. until
-syrupy
1/4 c  Water
3    Egg yolks
1 c  Raisins
Lemon Babka

Combine 1-1/2 cups flour, sugar, yeast, salt.  Heat milk, butter and
water until very warm--Add to dry ingredints--beat 2 min. at medium
speed of mixer. Add egg yolks, 1/2 cup flour, beat 2 min. on high
speed. Let rise one hour, stir down batter, put in tube, bundt. Cook
at 350 35 - 40 min.
