Title: KANSAS CITY BURGERS **
Categories: Ground beef, Grill
Yield: 4 Burgers
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
1 lb Ground beef
1/2 c  Frozen hash brown potatoes;
- thawed
2 tb Barbecue sauce
4    Buns; split, butter, toast
4 sl Swiss cheese
4 sl Bacon; cooked, crumbled
2    Green onions; sliced
Add thawed hash brown potatoes and barbecue sauce to
ground beef. Mix well. Shape into 4 patties. Grill or
broil to desired doneness. Place each cooked burger on
bun; top with cheese slice, crumbled bacon and sliced
green onion.
