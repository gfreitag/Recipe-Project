Title: LEMON AMBROSIA CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 10 Servings
MMMMM-------------------LISA CRAWLEY  TSPN00B------------------------
1 pk Lemon cake mix
1 pk Instant lemon pudding (sm)
3/4 c  Coconut
1 1/4 c  Water
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
2    Eggs
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
1    Box creamy frosting mix
2    Boxes confectioners' sugar
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Milk
Mix in a 13 x 9 ungreased pan with a fork.  Bake at
350    for 35-40 min.
