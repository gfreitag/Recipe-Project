Title: MANDARIN MARINADE
Categories: Marinades
Yield: 1 Batch
1 c  Lemon juice
1 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Honey
1/2 c  Soy sauce
1 tb Ginger, ground
1 ts Minced Garlic (Instant)
1 ts Salt
1 ts Basil
1/2 ts Marjoram
Combine all ingredients and mix well.  Refrigerate at least two hours
to allow flavors to blend.
