Title: MAPLE CREAM BALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Maple sirup
2 ts Butter or butter substitute
1/8 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
2/3 c  Chopped nuts
Boil sirup and salt to soft ball stage (234 - 238 F). Remove from
fire. Cool to room temperature.  Add butter. Add flavoring. Beat
until creamy. Add 1/2 the nuts. Continue beating until mixture is
sufficiently firm to mold into small balls. Boll balls in remainder
of nuts. Place on waxed paper to harden. Eva Riggs, Brittmount, MI.
