Title: MANHATTAN BURGERS **
Categories: Ground beef, Grill
Yield: 4 Burgers
MMMMM-------------------------BILLS20086------------------------------
1 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Pizza sauce
2 tb Mushrooms; chopped
3 tb Pepperoni; chopped
Italian bread; toasted
4 sl Mozzarella or provolone
1/4 c  Pepperoni; sliced
8    Green pepper rings
Add pizza sauce, chopped mushrooms and chopped pepperoni to ground
beef. Mix well. Shape into 4 patties. Grill or broil to desired
doneness. Place cooked burgers on Italian bread slice. Top with a
slice of cheese, some sliced pepperoni and 2 green pepper rings each.
