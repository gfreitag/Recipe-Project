Title: MACARONI PIE: PASTIERE
Categories: Pasta, Ethnic, Italian
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Cottage cheese
4 c  Elbow macaroni
4    Eggs
1 c  Grated fresh Romano cheese
1/2 c  Chopped fresh parsley
In a large bowl beat eggs and mix in cottage cheese, grated cheese and
parsley and combine well.Mix in the cooked macaroni. Generously
grease a 8 x 12 Pyrex pan.  Add mixture. bake in a 325 oven for 1
hour or until knife inserted in center comes out clean. Cut into
pieces to serve.
