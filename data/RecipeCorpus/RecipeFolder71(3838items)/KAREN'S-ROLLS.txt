Title: KAREN'S ROLLS
Categories: Breads
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
2 pk Yeast
5 c  Flour, all purpose
1/4 c  Water, (110F)
1 c  Milk, heated
1/4 c  Margerine, melted
2    Eggs, beaten
Mix sugar, salt, yeast, 1 1/2 cup of flour. Heat milk,
water, margerine till warm. Mix together, add flour
knead till smooth. Raise, shape, raise again.  Bake at
(rolls 350 20+ mins) (bread 400 30+ mins)
