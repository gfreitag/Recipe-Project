Title: LOTS OF COOKIES
Categories: Cookies, Chocolate
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Butter
1 c  Brown sugar
1 c  Granulated sugar
2    Beaten eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Baking soda
2 c  Flour
2 c  Rice Krispies
2 c  Oatmeal
12 oz Chocolate chips (yeah,
-right...)
per serving:
Calories
per serving:
Approx. Cook Time: 10 Cholesterol per serving: Marks:
:

*DIRECTIONS*

Cream butter and sugars, add eggs.  Blend in sifted flour, remainder
of dry ingredients.  Stir in Rice Krispies and Oatmeal. Add chocolate
chips.

Bake at 350 F for 10-12 minutes.

(Warning--this sucker makes a LOT of cookies.  Good to have around
when you have to come up with a batch but don't have the time.)

Source:  Molly Brannan
