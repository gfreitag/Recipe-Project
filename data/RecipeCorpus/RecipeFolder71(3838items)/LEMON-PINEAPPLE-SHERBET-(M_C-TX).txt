Title: LEMON-PINEAPPLE SHERBET (M_C-TX)
Categories: Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 4 Servings
2/3 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Pineapple juice (or
-crushed pineapple)
1 tb Lemon juice (2 is better)
1 ts Lemon rind grated
1 pt Milk
Mix and freeze.  If freezing in refrigerator, stir several times.
This is simple but very good.
