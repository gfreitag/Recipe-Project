Title: LEFTOVER RICE MUFFINS
Categories: Rice, Breads
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/4 c  Whole wheat flour
2 tb Brown sugar
2 ts Baking powder
2    Eggs
2/3 c  Skim milk
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/4 ts Almond extract
1 c  Cooked brown rice
Contributed to the echo by: BOYD NARON LEFTOVER RICE MUFFINS Stir
first three ingredients together. Beat remaining ingredients,
stirring rice in last. Combine two mixtures just until moistened.
Spoon into 12 muffin cups. Bake at 440 degrees for 20 minutes.

127 ea 27 calories per serving.
