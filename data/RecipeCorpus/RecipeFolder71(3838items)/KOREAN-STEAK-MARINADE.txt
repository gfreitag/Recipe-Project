2    Cloves

-

-tabasco (or hot oil or
-cayenne)

marinade if desired. *-* I've marinated T-bones and
beef kabobs in this, and grilled 'em on the Farberware
indoor grill in the dead of winter (using the drops of
Liquid Smoke).  Tasted like a slice of summer! ;}}}
For outdoor grilling, just omit the Liquid Smoke.
