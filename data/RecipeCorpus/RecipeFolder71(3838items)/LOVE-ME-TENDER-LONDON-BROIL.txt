Title: LOVE-ME-TENDER LONDON BROIL
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  HELLMANN'S or BEST FOODS
Real or light mayonnaise or
Low fat
1/3 c  Soy sauce
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Prepared mustard
1    Clove garlic, minced
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/4 ts Pepper
3 lb Beef top round steak,
-2 inches thick
1.  In large shallow dish combine mayonnaise, soy sauce, lemon juice,
mustard, garlic, ginger and pepper. Add steak, turning to coat.

2.  Cover; chill several hours or overnight.

3.  Grill or broil about 6 inches from source of heat, turning once,
25 to 30 minutes or until desired doneness. Slice diagonally across
grain.
