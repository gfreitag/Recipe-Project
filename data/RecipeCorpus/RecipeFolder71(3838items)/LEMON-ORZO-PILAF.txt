Title: LEMON-ORZO PILAF
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 4 Servings
14 3/4 cn Chicken broth,reduced-sodium
1 c  Water
1 c  Orzo pasta
1 ts Dried leaf basil,crumbled
1 ts Grated lemon rind
Bring chicken broth and water to boiling in medium-size saucepan.
Stir in orzo, basil and lemon. Return to boiling. Lower heat and
simmer 10 minutes or until tender. Remove from heat and serve.
