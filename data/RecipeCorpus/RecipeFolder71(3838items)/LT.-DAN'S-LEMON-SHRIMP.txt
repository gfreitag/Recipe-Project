Title: LT. DAN'S LEMON SHRIMP
Categories: Shrimp, Seafood, Bubba gump
Yield: 4 Servings
20    Unpeeled large fresh shrimp
1/4 c  Butter or margarine
3/4 c  Fresh crabmeat, drained and
- flaked
1/4 c  Dry white Vermouth
2 tb Minced fresh parsley
1 1/2 tb Lemon juice
2    Garlic cloves, minced
Peel and devein shrimp, leaving tails intact; set aside.

Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat; add shrimp,
crabmeat, and next 4 ingredients. Bring to a boil; reduce heat, and
simmer, uncovered, 4 to 5 minutes.

Spoon mixture evenly into 4 individual serving dishes, arranging
shrimp around crabmeat. Garnish with fresh parsley sprigs and lemon
wedges, if desired.
