Title: LUCY'S APPLESAUCE MEATBALLS
Categories: Beef, Vegetables, Crockpot, Pasta/rice
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb BEEF, GROUND
2 c  CORN FLAKES
1    EGG -- SLIGHTLY BEATEN
3 tb ONION, DRIED MINCED
2/3 c  APPLESAUCE
1 1/2 ts SALT
1/8 ts PEPPER
10 1/2 oz TOMATO SOUP, CANNED,
CONDENSED
1/2 c  WATER
COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS EXCEPT SOUP AND WATER. SHAPE MEATBALLS.
COMBINE SOUP AND WATER AND POUR OVER MEATBALLS. BAKE @ 350 ABOUT 1
HOUR OR UNTIL DONE.
