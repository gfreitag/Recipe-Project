Title: MARASCHINO CHERRIES
Categories: Pickles
Yield: 6 Servings
4 1/2 lb Pitted red cherries
4 1/2 lb White sugar
3 c  Water
Juice of 1 lemon
1 oz Almond extract
1 oz Red coloring
MMMMM---------------------------BRINE--------------------------------
2 qt Water
2 tb Salt
1 ts Alum
Soak pitted cherries overnight in heated brine.  The next morning,
drain cherries.  Rinse in cold water. Combine cherries, water, sugar,
lemon juice, and red coloring. Heat to boiling point. Let stand 24
hours. Again boil juices, pour over cherries and let stand 24 hours.
Bring to boil again. Add almond extract and cherries. Pack in hot
sterilized jars and seal. **Water Bath Time from The Ball Blue Book-
pints 20 min, quarts 25 min.
