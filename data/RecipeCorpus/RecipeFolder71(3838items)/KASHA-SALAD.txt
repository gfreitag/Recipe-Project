Title: KASHA SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Kasha
1 c  Peas, cooked
1 c  Mushroom, sliced
2 ea Tomato, medium chopped
1 ea Cucumber, peeled,chopped
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1/4 c  Scallion, chopped
4 tb Parsley, chopped
2 tb Pimento, chopped
2 ea Garlic cloves, crushed
1/2 c  Salad oil
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 ts Salt
4 c  Water
1 x  Pepper
Place kasha in pot of water or chicken bouillon, some
salt and pepper; bring to a boil, cover and reduce
heat. Cook until tender, about 15 minutes. Combine
cooked kasha with remaining ingredients, chill and
serve over a bed of lettuce.
