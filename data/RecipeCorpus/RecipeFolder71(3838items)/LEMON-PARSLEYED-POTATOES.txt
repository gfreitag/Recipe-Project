Title: LEMON PARSLEYED POTATOES
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 lb Potato; tiny new  or medium
1/4 c  Parsley
1 tb Lemon juice
Scrub potatoes.and cook until tender; drain.  Place in serving bowl,
keep warm. Meanwhile,  melt butter, stir in parsley and lemon juice.
Pour over potatoes and stir lightly to coat.
