Title: MAPLE ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Osg
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Maple syrup
4 ea Egg yolks
Salt
1 1/2 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Cream
1/2 c  Pecanse; chopped
Heat syrup and pour over egg yolks. Cool, add salt, vanilla and
whipped cream. Beat thoroughly and freeze.

Source: Mrs. John Beatty, Oxford Grange, Erie County, OH
