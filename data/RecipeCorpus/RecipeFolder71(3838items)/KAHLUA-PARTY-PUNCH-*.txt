Title: KAHLUA PARTY PUNCH *
Categories: Alcohol, Punch, Apples
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
2 c  Kahlua
12 oz Apple juice concentrate
1/2 c  Lemon juice
25 oz Apple juice
1    5th Dry champagne
1 qt Lemon-lime soda OR
- Club soda
Ice
Lemon slices
Orange slices
Chill all ingredients well. Combine kahlua with
undiluted apple juice concentrate and lemon juice.
Pour over a small chunk of ice in punch bowl. Add
sparkling apple juice, soda and champagne; stir
gently. Garnish with thin lemon and orange slices.
Makes 30, 1/2 cup servings.
