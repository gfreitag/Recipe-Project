Title: KLEIN'S KANCHI CHICKEN
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Crockpot
Yield: 6 Servings
6    Chicken breasts
1/4 c  Italian salad dressing
1/4 c  Water
1    Enevlope dry onion soup mix
Garlic salt to taste
Place chicken in slow cooker. Sprinkle with garlic
salt and onion soup mix. Pour water and salad dressing
over chicken.cover and cook on low or medium for 8 to
10    hours.
