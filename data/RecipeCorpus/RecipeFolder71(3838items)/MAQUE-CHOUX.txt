Title: MAQUE CHOUX
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables, Cajun
Yield: 6 Servings
12    Ears corn
1    Med. onion, finely chopped
1/2    Green pepper, finely chopped
1/4 ts Sugar (or more)
2 tb Butter or oleo, melted
2 tb Tomatoes & green chiles
Salt & pepper to taste
Cut corn from cob, scraping cob to remove pulp. Combine corn and
remaining ingredients in heavy saucepan. Cook over low heat 20-30
minutes or until corn is tender, stir frequently. Courtesy Telephone
