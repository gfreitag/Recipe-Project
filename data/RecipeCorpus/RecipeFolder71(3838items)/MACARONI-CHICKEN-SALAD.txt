Title: MACARONI CHICKEN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Poultry, Pasta
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Mayonnaise or salad
-dressing
1 tb Lemon juice
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 c  Cut-up cooked chicken
2 c  Cooked macaroni or pasta
2 md Stalks celery chopped
-(about 1 cup)
1/2 c  Frozen green peas, thawed
1/2 c  Cashew halves, if desired
Mix mayonnaise, lemon juice, salt and pepper until blended. Toss with
remaining ingredients. Cover and refrigerate about 2 hours or until
chilled.

5    servings.

Macaroni Chicken-Cantaloupe Salads: Spoon salad onto cantaloupe
wedges.

From the files of Al Rice, North Pole Alaska.    Feb 1994
