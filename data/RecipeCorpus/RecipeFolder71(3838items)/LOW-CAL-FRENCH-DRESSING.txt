Title: LOW-CAL FRENCH DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Sauces, Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Canned tomatoes
1/4 ts Onion powder
2 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Sweetener
1/4 c  Cooked rice or pasta
1/8 ts Celery salt
1/8 ts Salt
1/8 ts Garlic powder
1/8 ts Basil
Blend all ingredients till smooth.  Keep refrigerated.
