Title: LEMON MOLASSES SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  Molasses
1/3 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Butter
Heat molasses to boiling, stirring constantly.  Add butter. Boil 1
minute. Add lemon juice.  Mix thoroughly. Serve with steamed fruit
puddings.

Elizabeth Shaffer, Albuquerque, N.M.
