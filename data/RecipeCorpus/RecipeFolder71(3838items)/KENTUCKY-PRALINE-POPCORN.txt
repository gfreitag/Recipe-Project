Title: KENTUCKY PRALINE POPCORN
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM-------------------------ORVILLE R------------------------------
4 qt Popped popcorn, light salted
2 c  Chpd pecans
3/4 c  Butter
3/4 c  Brown sugar
In lg bowl or roaster, mix popcorn and pecans. Combine
butter and brown sugar in sm saucepan. Heat, stirring
popcorn mixture. Mix well to coat. Makes 4 qts.
