Title: LEMON MERINGUE PIE 3
Categories: Diabetic, Desserts, Pies
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 c  Water
4 pk Equal (or 1T sweetener)
4 1/2 ts Cornstarch
2    Eggs
1/3 c  Lemon juice
4    Egg whites (meringue)
4 pk Equal (meringue)
1 1/2 ts Cream of tartar (meringue)
Cook all ingredients in double boiler until thickened. Pour into pie
shell. Top with meringue which has been beaten stiff. Bake 450F for
15 minutes or until golden brown.
