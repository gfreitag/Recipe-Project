Title: MANGO CREAM ICE CREAM
Categories: Desserts, Ice cream
Yield: 1 Servings
1 3/4 c  Mango puree ( from 4 mangos
-)
1 tb Lime juice
1 c  Sugar
1 3/4 c  Of sour cream
Mango Cream Ice Cream

Blend mango puree, lime juice and sugar and chill. Beat smooth and
stir in sour cream.  Freee in an ice-cream maker.

Makes 1 qt.
