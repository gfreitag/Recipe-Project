Title: LYNN'S ARTICHOKE TOMATO ALFREDO
Categories: Low, Fat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Artichokes, save juice, cut
Into small pieces
2    Fresh garden tomatoes,
Chopped
1    Onion, diced
1    2 cups mushrooms, sliced
(Sally's idea!)
Lots of fresh chopped basil
Non-fat milk
2 tb Flour
Toss chopped artichokes and juice into pan.  Thicken with flour and
milk to desired thickness.  Add onion, mushrooms, tomatoes and basil.
Cook for a short time leaving vegetables firm and tasty and pretty.
Cook up a batch of your favorite spaghetti noodles.  Rinse. Toss
artichoke sauce on top and voila.
