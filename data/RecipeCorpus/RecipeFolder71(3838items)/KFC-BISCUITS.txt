Title: KFC BISCUITS
Categories: Breads, Biscuits, Dressings, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Self-rising flour
1 tb Sugar
1 c  Milk
1/3 c  Mayo
Combine flour, sugar, milk and mayo into smooth dough.
Divide batter equally between 10 paper-lined cupcake
wells and bake 350~ about 30 minutes or until golden
brown.
