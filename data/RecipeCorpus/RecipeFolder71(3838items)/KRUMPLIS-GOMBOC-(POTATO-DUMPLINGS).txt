Title: KRUMPLIS GOMBOC (POTATO DUMPLINGS)
Categories: Soups
Yield: 6 Servings
2 md Potatoes
2 qt Water
1 tb Butter, unsalted
1 tb Flour, all-purpose; heaping
1    Eggs
1 pn Salt
Boil unpeeled potatoes in 2 quarts of water. When
done, peel and puree while still warm. Add butter,
flour, egg, and salt. Mix the dough and knead it well.

With a spoon, cut off pieces of dough. Flour your
hands and roll the pieces into round dumplings. Drop
dumplings into boiling soup and cook for about 5
minutes, until tender.
