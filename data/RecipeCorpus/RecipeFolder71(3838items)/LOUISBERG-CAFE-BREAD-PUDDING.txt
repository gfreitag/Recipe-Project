Title: LOUISBERG CAFE BREAD PUDDING
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 24 Servings
6    Eggs -- beaten
6 c  Milk
3/4 c  Sugar
1 c  Raisins
12 c  Broken bread, rolls or
Doughnuts
Combine first 4 ingredients in a large bowl. Blend in
= pieces of bread. Pour mixture into a 13x9x2 baking pan. = Bake at
350 for 45 minutes or until set.
