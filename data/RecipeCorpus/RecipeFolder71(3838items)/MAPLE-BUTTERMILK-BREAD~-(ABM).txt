Title: MAPLE-BUTTERMILK BREAD~ (ABM)
Categories: Bread/abm, Low-cal, Low-cholest, Low-fat
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Plus 1 tsp. pure maple syrup
1 c  Water
2 c  Unbleached white flour
1 c  Whole-wheat flour
4 tb Powdered buttermilk
1 ts Salt
1 tb Yeast
Recipe by: Prevention Cuisine  Nov/94 Place all ingredients in the
order listed into the bread machine. Program the machine for basic
bread setting and press start.

Yields: 1 loaf/10 slices.

(All flours are approximate measures. You may use more or less,
depending on the weight and absorbency of your flour.)

Per serving: 175 calories, 2.1g fat (11% of calories), 2.4g dietary
fiber, 5.7g protein, 33.8g carbohydrates, 2.1g cholesterol, 248mg
sodium.

Adapted from Bread Machine Baking: Perfect Every Time (William
Morrow and Co., Inc., 1993) Typed for you by Marjorie Scofield 5/4/95
