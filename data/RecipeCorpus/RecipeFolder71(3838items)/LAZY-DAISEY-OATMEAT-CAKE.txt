Title: LAZY DAISEY OATMEAT CAKE
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Quick oats
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 1/4 c  Boiling water
2    Eggs well beaten
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cloves
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Melted butter
3 tb Milk
3/4 c  Shredded coconut
1/3 c  Nuts
Combine oats, butter and boiling water.  Set aside for 20 minutes.
Beate together the eggs , sugars and vanilla. Sift dry ingredients
and add, 13x 9 inch baking pan. Bake at 350 F for 30 to 35 minutes.
or until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.  Cool for a few
minutes. Then combine frosting ingredients, spread on cake and place
under a broiler for one minute or until frosting begins to bubble.
