Title: MACARONI WITH BROCCOLI  & BLUE CHEESE SAUCE
Categories: Pasta
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Wholewheat macaroni
2 c  Broccoli
2 tb Margarine
1/4 c  Wholewheat flour
1/2 ts Dried mustard
Salt & pepper
2/3 c  Milk
1/2 c  Yogurt
1 1/2 c  Stilton or other blue
Cheese
Cook macaroni until just cooked.  Cook broccoli for 3 min. in boiling
salted water.

Melt margarine, add flour, mustard, salt & pepper and cook for 2 min.
stirring occasionally.  Gradually add milk with a whisk. Stir briskly
until smooth.  Remove from the heat; stir in yogurt and blue cheese.
Reserve a few t. of sauce for the top and mix the remaining sauce
into the macaroni, gently folding in the broccoli.

Grease an ovenproof dish, fill with the mixture, spread remaining
sauce on top and bake for 20 min. at 350.
