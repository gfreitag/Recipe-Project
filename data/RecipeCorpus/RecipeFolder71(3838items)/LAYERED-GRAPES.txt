Title: LAYERED GRAPES
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 2 Servings
1/2 c  Heavy cream, whipped
1/4 c  Chunky blue cheese dressing
2 tb Chopped toasted walnuts
1 tb Sugar
Blue grapes, halved & seeded
Mix whipped cream, dressing, walnuts, and sugar. Chill about 30
minutes. Layer mixture with grapes in crystal parfait glasses. Prep
time: 5 minutes. Servings 2  Chilling time: 30 minutes. Typed in
