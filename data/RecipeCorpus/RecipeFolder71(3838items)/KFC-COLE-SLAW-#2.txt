Title: KFC COLE SLAW #2
Categories: Salads, Vegetables, Dressings, Copycat
Yield: 1 Servings
6 c  Cabbage -- chop
1 c  Carrots -- shred fine
1/4 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Mayo
1/2 c  Buttermilk
1/2 ts Celery seed
3 dr Hot pepper sauce
3 tb Dry minced onion
Toss cabbage lightly with sugar, carrots, salt and
pepper. Drench all with milk. cover and refo about 15
minutes. Meanwhile combine the mayo, buttermilk,
celery seed pepper sauce and onion. Mix well with
cabbage. Refo again at least 1 hour. Before serving
drain some of the liquid. This settles quite a bit.
Keeps for 24 hours.
