Title: LU'S SPECIAL CHICKEN SAUCE
Categories: Chinese, Sauces, Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
Soy Sauce
Garlic, minced
Ginger, minced
Green Onion, sliced
White Pepper
Black Pepper
Honey or Jam or combo
Oil, hot
Combine all ingredients except oil.  Heat enough oil to cover bottom
of frying pan until oil is smoking. Pour oil CAREFULLY over other
ingredients and let steep to combine flavors.
