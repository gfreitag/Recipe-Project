Title: MAGIC DIP
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Cream cheese, softened
1 c  Semi-sweet real chocolate
Chips
1/2 c  Flaked coconut, toasted
1/2 c  Chopped peanuts
Spread cream cheese on bottom of 9 inch microwavable pie plate or
quiche plate. Top with remaining ingredients. Microwave on medium
(50%) 3 to 4 minutes or until warm. Serve with graham crackers.
Garnish, if desired. Makes 6 to 8 servings.  *Great for kids.ch
