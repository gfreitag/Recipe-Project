Title: LEMON DAISY
Categories: Non-alcohol, Cocktail, Beverages
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Grenadine syrup
1/3 c  Fresh lemon juice
2 c  Club soda
2 c  Lemon-flavored soda
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Lemon peel
1.  Mix grenadine and lemon juice in a glass measure. 2. Pour into
ice-filled wine goblets.  Top each with 1/2 cup of each soda. Garnish
with peel.

Source:  Woman's Day 12/17/96
