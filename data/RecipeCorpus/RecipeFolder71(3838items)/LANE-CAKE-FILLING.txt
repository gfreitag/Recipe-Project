Title: LANE CAKE FILLING
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
8    Egg yolks
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Flaked coconut
1/2 c  Bourbon
1/2 c  Maraschino cherries; sliced
Recipe by: Southern Living Preparation Time: 0:30 Combine egg yolks,
sugar, and butter in a heavy saucepan. Cook over medium heat,
stirring constantly, until thickened (about 20 minutes). Remove from
heat, and stir in remaining ingredients.  Cool completely. Yield:
3-1/2 cups.
