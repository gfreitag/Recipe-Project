Title: MAGIC MAYONNAISE
Categories: Dressings, Dkuhnen msn
Yield: 10 Servings
1    Can sweetened condensed milk
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Salt
Pinch of cayenne pepper
1    Egg
In small mixer bowl, combine all ingredients; beat until mixture
thickens, about 2 minutes.  Chill to blend flavors. Store leftovers
in a clean jar. Source: The Toronto Star Newspaper.
