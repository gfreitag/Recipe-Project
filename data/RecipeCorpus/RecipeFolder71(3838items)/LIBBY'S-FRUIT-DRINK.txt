Title: LIBBY'S FRUIT DRINK
Categories: Digest, Feb95
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Frozen banana (freezing it
Makes the drink super cold
Without
Diluting it with ice)
1/2    Fresh papaya
10    To 12 raspberries (fresh or
Frozen)
1/2 c  Water or fruit juice
1 tb Toasted wheat germ
(optional)
puree in blender 30-45 seconds. makes about sixteen delicious,
filling, vegan, nutritious ounces.
