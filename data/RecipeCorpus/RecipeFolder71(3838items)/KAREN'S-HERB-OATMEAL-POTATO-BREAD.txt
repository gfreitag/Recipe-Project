Title: KAREN'S HERB OATMEAL-POTATO BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Mashed potato
1    Extra-large egg
4 tb Butter, at room temperature
OR use vegetable oil
3/4 c  Water
3/4 ts Dried thyme, rosemary,
Oregano, or basil OR
A combination
2 1/2 c  Unbleached white flour
1/2 c  Quick-cooking oats
1 ts Salt
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Active dry yeast
AFTER BEEP
2 tb Sunflower seeds
Place all the ingredients except the sunflower seeds
in machine in order suggested by manufacturer and
program for raisin bread. When beeper sounds, add the
sunflower seeds.
