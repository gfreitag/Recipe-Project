Title: KHEERE KA RAITA (YOGURT WITH CUCUMBER & MINT)
Categories: Sauces, Indian
Yield: 1 Batch
2 2/3 c  Yogurt, plain
1    Cucumber; peeled & grated
2 tb Mint, fresh; finely chopped
1/2 ts Cumin seeds; roasted
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
Put the yogurt in a bowl.  Beat lightly with a fork or
whisk until smooth and creamy.  Add all the other
ingredients and mix. Cover and refrigerate until ready
to eat.
