Title: LOADED CORNBREAD
Categories: Quickbreads
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pk (12-ounce) STOUFFER'S Corn
-Souffle, thawed
1 pk (8 1/2-ounce) corn muffin
-mix
1 c  (4 -ounces) shredded mild
-Cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Minced pecans or bacon
1/4 c  Minced green onions
Thaw Corn Souffle in microwave at MEDIUM (50% power) 6 to 7 minutes.
Spoon thawed souffle mixture into a medium bowl; add remaining
ingredients and stir just until blended. Pour into a greased 8 inch
square baking pan. Bake at 400 for 20 √êto 25 minutes or until a
wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.
