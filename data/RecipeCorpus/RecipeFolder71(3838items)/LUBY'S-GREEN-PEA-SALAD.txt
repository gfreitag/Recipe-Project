Title: LUBY'S GREEN PEA SALAD
Categories: Houston chr, Salads/dres, Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
32 oz Green peas,
-rinsed and drained
1 c  Cheddar cheese, 4 oz.;
-finely diced
1 c  Celery; diced
1/2 c  Sweet pickles; thinly sliced
1/2 c  Red bell pepper or pimiento
-diced
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
Salt; to taste
Freshly ground black pepper
-to taste
Recipe by: Luby's 50th Anniversary Recipe Collection Combine peas,
cheese, celery, pickles, bell pepper, mayonnaise, salt and pepper in
medium-size bowl.  Toss lightly to coat evenly. Cover and refrigerate
at least 2 hours. Source: Houston Chronicle, Nov. 13, 1996, p. 4F
