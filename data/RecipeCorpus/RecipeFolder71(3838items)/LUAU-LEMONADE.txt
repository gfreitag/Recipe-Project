Title: LUAU LEMONADE
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------ALCOHOL FREE ENT---------------------------
2 sl Fresh pineapple
1 oz Confectioners' sugar
1/2 oz Raspberry syrup OR juice
-from canned red raspberries
1    Ice cube
2 oz Club soda
Lemon slice, garnish
Lemonade made with no lemons.
10 oz Collins glass Combine in blender pineapple, sugar, syrup
and ice. Blend until smooth. Add club soda and blend 10 sec more.
Serve over ice. Typed by Sherilyn Schamber
