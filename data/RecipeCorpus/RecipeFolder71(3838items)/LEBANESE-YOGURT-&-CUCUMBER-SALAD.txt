Title: LEBANESE YOGURT & CUCUMBER SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Yogurt -- plain
12    Fresh mint leaves
2    Cloves garlic -- peeled
Salt -- to taste
2    Or 3 cucumbers - peeled
-& thinly sliced
Drain the yogurt in a cheesecloth-lined colander for several hours,
discarding the liquid that has collected. Place the mint and garlic
together in a salad bowl and crush with a little salt. Add the drained
yogurt and cucumbers.  Mix and chill before serving.
