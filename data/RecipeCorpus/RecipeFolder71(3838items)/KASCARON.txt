Title: KASCARON
Categories: Appetizers, Germany, German
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------YIELD: 1 RECIPE---------------------------
1 ea Pk mochiko (2 lbs)
2 ea Pk sweetened flake coconut
-(14 oz)
4 c  Water
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Honey
Oil for deep frying
Mix the first 4 ingredients together.  Shape into 1
1/2-inch balls.  Deep fry in 350 degree oil until
golden brown. Stir occasionally to prevent balls from
sticking together. Put honey in a large pan and add
balls hen toss gently to coat them evenly. Skewer 3 to
a stick.
