Title: KIWIFRUIT BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Liquid honey
1/3 c  Vegetable oil
1    Egg
1 1/2 c  Whole wheat
Flour
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground cloves
1 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1 c  Kiwifruit pulp
( A moist loaf, low in fat and sugar)

(about 3 large)

Beat honey, oil and egg well.

Sift flour, spices and baking powder and salt and add
to oil mixture.

Lightly stir in remaining ingredients.

Spoon into 8-inch oiled loaf pan and bake at 350 F for
40-45 minutes.

Allow to cool.

Source: New Zealand Kiwifruit Cookbook by Jan Bilton
