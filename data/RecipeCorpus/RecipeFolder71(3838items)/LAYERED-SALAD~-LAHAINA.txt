Title: LAYERED SALAD~ LAHAINA
Categories: Salads, Hawaiian
Yield: 10 Servings
MMMMM----------------COOKING WITH HAWAIIAN MAGIC---------------------
1    Head lettuce, finely chpd
2 c  Celery, thinly sliced
1/2 c  Green onions, thinly sliced
8 oz Cn sliced water chestnuts
20 oz Pkg petite peas
1 1/2 c  Mayo
1 ts Seasoned salt
1 tb Sugar
Garlic powder
1/2 c  Parmesan
1 lb Bacon, cooked, crumbled
3    Hard cooked eggs, to 4
Layer first 6 ingreds in order given. Over mayo, sprinkle seasoned
salt, sugar, garlic powder and Parmesan. Cover; chill well (better
OVERNIGHT). Before serving, add bacon and garnish with eggs.
