Title: LOUISIANA EPICURE'S SAUCE
Categories: Cajun, Fish, Sauces
Yield: 24 Servings
2 qt Heavy cream,whipped
2 c  Mayonnaise
1 c  Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Dijon mustard(or to taste)
1/2 c  Horseradish,prepared
2 tb Sugar
1 ts Cayenne pepper
1. Combine all ingredients; store in refrigerator until ready to use.
2. Just before serving fish, spread 1/4 cup sauce on each fillet;
pass under broiler.
