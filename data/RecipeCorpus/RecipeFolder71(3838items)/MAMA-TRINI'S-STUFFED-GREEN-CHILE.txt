Title: MAMA TRINI'S STUFFED GREEN CHILE
Categories: Chiles, Cheese, Mexican, New mex
Yield: 8 Servings
8    Fresh roasted and
Peeled green chiles
1/2 c  Grated mozzarella
Cheese
2 md Fresh tomatoes,
Finely chopped
1/2-1 ts garlic salt

Slit each chile and stuff with cheese and tomatoes. Sprinkle with
garlic salt.  Place stuffed chiles, in single layer only, in 8" x 13"
baking dish. Bake at 350 degrees until cheese melts, about 10 - 15
minutes. Makes 8. Walt Judy Howle
