Title: KOLACHE DOUGH
Categories: Cookies, Ethnic
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Flour
8 oz Cream cheese
1/2 lb Butter
1    Egg
2 ts Sugar
Sift flour and sugar together.  Work in butter, cream
cheese and egg yolk. Knead together and let stand
overnight in refrigerator.  Roll out thin on flour and
sugar.  Cut into squares.  Fill with walnuts, lekvar
(prunes), poppy seed or apricot.
