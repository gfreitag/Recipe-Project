Title: LEMON PIE-FILLING MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/2 c  Presweetened Lemonade Mix
1 1/4 c  Sugar Or To Taste
1 c  Plus 2 T Cornstarch
1 ts Salt
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl, and mix well. Put into a 1
quart airtight container and label as Lemon Pie-filling Mix. Store in
a cool dry palce and use within 6 to 8 months.

Makes about 4 1/4 cups of mix.
