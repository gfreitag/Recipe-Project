Title: LETTUCE & GREEN BEAN SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1/3 c  Onion; chopped
1 ts Salt
ds Pepper
1/4 c  Cider vinegar; plus
2 tb Cider vinegar
1/2 ts Sugar
1 md Head lettuce; torn in bite-
-size pieces
4 c  Green beans; cooked, drained
3 sl Bacon; diced
2    Eggs; hard-cooked, chopped
Combine first 5 ingredients, and set aside.  Place lettuce in a large
salad bowl, and pour hot green beans on top. POur onion mixture over
beans. Cook bacon until crisp; pour bacon and drippings over green
beans. Toss lightly, and sprinkle eggs over top.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, sometime in 1980. Typed for you by
Nancy Coleman.
