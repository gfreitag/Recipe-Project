Title: LAZY MAN'S STEW
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 5 Servings
2 lb Chuck stew meat
1 pk Dehydrated onion soup
1 cn Mushroom soup, undiluted
8 oz Jar sliced mushrooms
(with the juice)
Combine all ingredients, put in casserole dish and bake 3 hours at 325
degrees F, covered. Serve with noodles or rice.
