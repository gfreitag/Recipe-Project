Title: LEMON BUTTER
Categories: Penndutch, Desserts, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Butter
3 ea Egg, well beaten
1/2 c  Water
3 ea Lemon, juice of
3 ea Lemon, grated rind of
Cream the butter and sugar thoroughly; add the eggs and mix well. Add
the water and lemon juice and rind. Cook in top of double boiler
until thick. Source:  Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book - Fine Old
Recipes, Culinary Arts Press, 1936.
