Title: MAGARITA CHICKEN
Categories: Jvn, Poultry, Barbecue
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Boneless chicken breasts
1/2 c  Tequila
1/2 c  Fresh lime juice
Combine chickenm tequila & lime juice in small shallow dish or zip
lock bag. Marinte at least 30 min &a up to 2 hours, turning
ocassionally. Prepare barbecue (medium-high heat) or preheat broiler.
Drain chicken; reserve marinade. Season chicken with salt & pepper.
Grill or broil until just cooked thru, ocassionally basting chicken
with some reserved marinade, about 4 min. per side. Boil remaining
marinade in small saucepan 1 min. Cut each breast crosswise into thin
slices. Garnish with lime slices. Serve, passing marinade. The
chicken is also good in tacos with salsa.
