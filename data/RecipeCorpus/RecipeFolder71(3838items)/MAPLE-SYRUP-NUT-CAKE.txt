Title: MAPLE SYRUP NUT CAKE
Categories: Cakes, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
2 1/4 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1 c  Maple syrup
1/2 c  Milk
1 c  Chopped nuts
1/2 c  White sugar
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Soft shortening
2 ea Eggs
Sift dry ingredients together into bowl.  Add syrup, shortening and
milk; beat 2 minutes.  Add eggs and beat 2 minutes. Add choppeed nuts
and mix well.  Pour into 2 greased and floured 9" pans. Bake in 350Ã¸
oven for 25 to 30 minutes.  Frost. By Pamela Graves - Serves 18
