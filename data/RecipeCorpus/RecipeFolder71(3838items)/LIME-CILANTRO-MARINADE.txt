Title: LIME-CILANTRO MARINADE
Categories: Marinade, Marinades
Yield: 1 Servings
1/3 c  Fresh lime juice
2 ts Coarsely grated lime peel
1/4 c  Olive oil
20 ml Garlic -- minced
1/3 c  Chopped fresh cilantro
1 1/2 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 1/2 ts Dried oregano
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Coarsely ground black
Pepper
In a large, shallow glass baking dish, combine ingredients.

Refrigerate unused marinade up to several days.

Uses:  As a marinade for chicken, steaks, fish or shrimp (enough for
about 2 pounds). Marinate at least 30 minutes. Baste with leftover
marinade. If cooking in the oven, serve with pan drippings.

Source:  "The Ultimate Barbecue Sauce Cookbook", by Jim Auchmutey and
: Susan Puckett,  1995.  Longstreet Press, Inc.
