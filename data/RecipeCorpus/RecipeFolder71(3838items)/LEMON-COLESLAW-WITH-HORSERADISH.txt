Title: LEMON COLESLAW WITH HORSERADISH
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
2 tb Olive oil
1 tb White wine vinegar
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Sour cream
2 tb Sugar
2 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Prepared horseradish
1/2 ts Celery seeds
1/2 ea Red bell pepper; cut into
-matchsticks
1/4 ea Red onion; cut into
-matchsticks
2 tb Fresh parsley; chopped
8 c  Cabbage; shredded
1/2 ea Green bell pepper; cut into
-matchsticks
1 ea Carrot; shredded
2 ts Lemon peel; grated
Combine first 11 ingredients in bowl; whisk to blend. Refrigerate
dressing until cold. (Can be prepared 1 day ahead). Combine cabbage,
bell peppers, onion, carrot, parsley and lemon peel in large bowl.
Toss with enough dressing to season to taste and serve.
