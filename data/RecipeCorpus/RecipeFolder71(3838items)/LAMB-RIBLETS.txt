Title: LAMB RIBLETS
Categories: Other meat
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Lamb Riblets
1/4 c  Butter or Margarine
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Cinnamon
1 c  Brown Sugar
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 c  Orange Juice
1 ea Lemon, Thinly Sliced
1/2 c  Pitted dried prunes,
-finely chopped
1/4 c  Dried apricots, finely
-chopped
1 tb Each, salt and pepper
Melt butter in a sauce pan, add salt, cinnamon, sugar and nutmeg and
stir until smooth.  Add remaining ingredients, cover and cook over
low heat 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Brush or marinate lamb
ribs in sauce before grilling.

Rub lamb riblets with salt and pepper and thread on barbecue spit or
place in crown on grill.  Cook, 5 to 6 inches above coals for about
45 minutes or until well done. Brush with sauce every 15 minutes.
Serve riblets with remaining sauce.
