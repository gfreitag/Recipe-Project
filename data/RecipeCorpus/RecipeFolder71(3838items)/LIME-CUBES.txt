Title: LIME CUBES
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 4 Servings
1 pk JELL-O Brand Lime Flavor
-Gelatin
1 c  Boiling water
1/2 c  Cold water
Prepare JELL-O with boiling water and cold water. Pour into an 8"
square pan. Refrigerate 3 hours or until firm. Cut into cubes. Spoon
into 4 dessert glasses and serve.
