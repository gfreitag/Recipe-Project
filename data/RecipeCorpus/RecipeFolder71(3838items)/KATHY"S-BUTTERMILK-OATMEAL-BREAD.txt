Title: KATHY"S BUTTERMILK OATMEAL BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Loaf
3 c  Bread Flour
2 tb Granulated Sugar
1 ts Salt
2 tb Margarine
4 tb Buttermilk powder
1/4 c  Oatmeal
2 ts Yeast
1 1/4 c  Warm water
Place ingredients in pan in order given by
manufacturer. Regular white cycle or timer. Medium
crust.
