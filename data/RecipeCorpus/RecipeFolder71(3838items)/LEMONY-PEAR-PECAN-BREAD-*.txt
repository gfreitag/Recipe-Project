Title: LEMONY PEAR PECAN BREAD *
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM----------------------PATTI - VDRJ67A---------------------------
MMMMM-----------------------1-1/2 LB LOAF----------------------------
1 c  Strained pear baby food
2 tb Water
1 tb Butter or margarine
1 ts Salt
3 c  Bread flour
1/2 c  Pecans; chopped, toasted
1 tb Brown sugar; packed
2 ts Lemon peel; finely shredded
2 ts Bread machine yeast
Add ingredients in order given by manufacturer, adding pears with
water and pecans with flour. Basic/white bread cycle. Light color
setting, but medium/normal color setting for large capacity machines
with glass domes to ensure complete baking.

NOTE: Can use strained peach or apricot baby food also.
