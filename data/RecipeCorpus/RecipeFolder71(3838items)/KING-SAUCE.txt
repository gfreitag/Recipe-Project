Title: KING SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Hoisin sauce
1 c  Soy sauce
2 tb White wine vinegar
2 tb Dry sherry
4 tb Oriental sesame seed oil
4 tb Dijon mustard
3 tb Garlic -- minced
1    Juice of lemon
Pepper -- to taste
1 ts Cumin
2 ts Fennel seed -- crushed
2 tb Mirin
1/3 c  Orange juice
Combine all the ingredients in a large bowl and adjust
to taste. flavors tend to dilute during cooking so
keep them strong. Use as marinade or a basting sauce.
Rub the sauce into the meat and refrigerate coverd for
8-10 hours.
