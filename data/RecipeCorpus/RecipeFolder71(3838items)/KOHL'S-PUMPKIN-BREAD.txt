Title: KOHL'S PUMPKIN BREAD
Categories: Improv, Kohl, Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Cinnamon
3 c  Sugar
1 c  Corn oil
4    Eggs
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Pumpkin
1/3 c  Water
2 ts Baking soda
3 c  Flour
Mix together nutmeg, cinnamon, sugar, oil, eggs, and
salt. Add pumpkin, water, soda, and flour. Grease pans
and fill 2/3 full. Bake 350 degrees for 1 hour in a
preheated oven. Cool 10 minutes before removing bread
from the pans.

Taken from: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK
A Collection of Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce &
Clem Kohl
