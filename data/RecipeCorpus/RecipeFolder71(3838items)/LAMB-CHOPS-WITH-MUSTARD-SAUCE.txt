Title: LAMB CHOPS WITH MUSTARD SAUCE
Categories: Meats, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Dijon style mustard
2 tb Cooking oil
1/4 c  Dry vermouth
1/4 ts Freshly ground black pepper
4    Lamb chops,3/4" thick
Combine mustard,oil,vermouth and black pepper.Coat chops with
mixture.Broil or grill chops,basting frequently,until cooked as
desired.
