Title: Layered Mexican Dish
Categories: Meat, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings

1 ea Minced onion
1 cn Refried beans

6 oz Monteray jack cheese (shred)
MMMMM------------------------FIFTH LAYER-----------------------------
1 cn Small black olives (chopped)
MMMMM----------------------------TOP---------------------------------
2 ea Green onions (chopped)
2 ea Tomatoes (chopped)
