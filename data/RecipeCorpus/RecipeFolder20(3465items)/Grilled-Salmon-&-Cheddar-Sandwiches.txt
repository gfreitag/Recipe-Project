Title: Grilled Salmon & Cheddar Sandwiches
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Fish, Main dish, Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Onion; Grated
1 tb Lemon Juice
1 lb Salmon; 1 Cn
10 oz Cheddar; Md, *
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
*    Slice the Cheddar Cheese into 4 slices of 2 1/2 ozs each.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++ Mix the salmon with the onion, lemon juice, and
mayonnaise. Spread the mixture on thick slices of French bread and
top with a slice of cheddar cheese.  Add a top slice of bread and
butter both sides of the sandwich generously.  Grill until brown,
then turn and brown the other side, and the cheese is melted.  Serve
hot.
