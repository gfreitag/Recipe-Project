Title: Cocktail Crab Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Seafood, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Cream Cheese; Softened
1/8 t  Salt
2 tb Prepared Horseradish
3 tb White Onion; Diced
3/4 c  Catsup
1/8 t  Hot Sauce
1/2 c  Crabmeat; Flaked, OR
6 oz Crabmeat; Flaked, Drain Well
Combine the catsup, horseradish and hot sauce.  Remove any remaining
filament from the crabmeat.  Add the crabmeat to the catsup mixture
and continue to blend.
Add all other ingredients and blend till smooth. Serve at room
temperature. Makes about 2 1/2 cups of dip. SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Salty
Potato Chips, Potato Skins, Green Bell Peppers, Turnips
