Title: Zucchini Relish
Categories: Vegetable, Relish, Tex/mexican
Yield: 4 servings
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Sugar
1/4 t  Pepper
2 c  Zucchini; Shredded
1/4 c  Fresh Cilantro; Snipped
2 T  Lime Juice
2 T  Vegetable Or Olive Oil
Mix all ingredients in glass or plastic bowl.  Cover and refrigerate
at least 1 hour. Makes about 1 1/4 cups relish.
