Title: Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Cajun/creol, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3 T  Lea & Perrins
1 c  Dry white wine
1/2 t  Mint, dried
3 T  Vinegar
1 T  Liquid smoke
2 c  Ketchup
1/2 T  Louisiana hot sauce
3 c  Onions, chopped
1/4 c  Honey
1 T  Garlic, chopped
2 T  Lemon juice
1 c  Sweet pepper, chopped
1 T  Salt
1/2 c  Parsley, dried
Place all ingredients in a pot that is big enough to hold them. Bring
to a boil.  Cook, covered, on low heat for several hours. From Justin
Wilson's "Outside Cooking with Inside Help"
