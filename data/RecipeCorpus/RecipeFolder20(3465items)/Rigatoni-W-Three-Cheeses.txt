Title: Rigatoni W Three Cheeses
Categories: Pasta, Italian, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Shredded mozzarella
1 c  Heavy cream
1/2 c  Grated parmigiano cheese
1/2 t  Nutmeg
3 T  Salt
1 lb Rigatoni
3 1/2 T  Melted sweet butter
1/2 c  Shredded swiss cheese
1/2 c  Shredded fontina
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In lots of boiling water, add the salt
and rigatoni.
Cook until super al  dante  as  they are going into the oven. Drain
and rinse  in  cold water. In a large bowl, mix the butter into the
pasta until it is well coated. Add  the  three  cheeses  and the
cream. Toss well and add  1/2  the parmigiano
while tossing.   Place in a buttered casserole and
sprinkle the  remaining parmigiano on top.   Sprinkle the nutmeg over
everything and  bake for 15 to 20 minutes. When the top of the pasta
has turned golden brown, it is done.
