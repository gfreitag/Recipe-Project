Title: Cranberry-Raisin Sauce
Categories: Vegetable, Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Meat
Yield: 4 servings  1 c  Orange Juice 1/2 c Cranberries, fresh or frozen 1 tb Cornstarch
1/8 ts Ground Cloves 4 tb Raisins, dark or golden
Cinnamon Stick

Combine the juice and cranberries in a saucepan.  Cook over medium
heat just until the berries "pop." Add the remaining ingredients and
cook until the mixture is thick. Serve hot over baked ham.
