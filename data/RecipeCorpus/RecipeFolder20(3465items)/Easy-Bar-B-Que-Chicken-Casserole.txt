Title: Easy Bar-B-Que Chicken Casserole
Categories: Casserole, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Onion,instant minced
1/4 ts Soy sauce
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1 cn Pork and beans(16oz)
4    Chicken pieces
1/4 c  Catsup
2 tb Peach preserves
1. Place beans in a 2-quart casserole; top with casserole.~ 2. Mix
together remaining ingredients; pour over chicken and beans.~ 3.
Cover and bake in preheated 325'F. oven 1 3/4 hours.~
