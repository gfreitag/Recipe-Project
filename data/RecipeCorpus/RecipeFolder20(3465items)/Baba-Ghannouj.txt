Title: Baba Ghannouj
Categories: Appetizer, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1/4 t  Black Pepper
1 ds Ground Cumin
2 T  Sesame seeds
2 T  Finely chopped fresh Parsley
1 x  Med Eggplant, peeled  *
1/4 c  Tahini
1 T  Lemon juice
1 x  Clove garlic
* and cut into 1/2" cubes (about 3 cups)
Steam eggplant until tender, about 8 minutes.
Place cooked eggplant in food processor. Add tahini (sesame
paste),lemon juice, and garlic. Puree.
Stir in remaining ingredients; spoon into serving bowl. Garnish with
parsley sprig. Serve warm or chill. Makes 2 cups. HINTS: - smaller
eggplants have thinner peels and a sweeter flavor. Select
