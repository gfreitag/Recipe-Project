Title: Fresh Strawberry Cupcakes
Categories: Fruit, Cake/cookie
Yield: 24 servings
2 x  Eggs
1/2 c  Vegetable Oil
1/2 t  Almond or vanilla extract
10 oz Pkg frzn sweet Strawberries*
1 3/4 c  All-purpose Flour
1 t  Baking Soda
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Chopped Walnuts
* or 1 cup crushed fresh strawberries mixed with 3 T sugar Mix
together flour, baking soda, sugar and nuts in a bowl. In another
bowl, beat together eggs, flavoring and oil until well blended. Add
strawberries and beat at slow speed of mixer until berries are
partially broken. Beat in flour mixture slowly until smooth, but
pieces of berries are still visible. Pour batter into greased muffin
tins, filling to half.
Bake at 350 deg F for 30 minutes, or until they test done. Or, bake
in greased 9x5x3" loaf pan for at 350 deg F for 1 hour, or until done.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar if desired.
