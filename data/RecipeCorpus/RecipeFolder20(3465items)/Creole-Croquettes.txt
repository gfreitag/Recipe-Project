Title: Creole Croquettes
Categories: Meat, Seafood, Main dish, Poultry, Cajun/creol
Yield: 4 servings
1/2    Green pepper
1    Egg
3    Cooked Irish potatoes
1 c  Bread or cracker crumbs
Salt and cayenne pepper to
-taste
2 c  Leftover meat or chicken
1 tb Shortening
1    Onion
1    Clove garlic
1    Branch celery
4    Sprigs parsley
Grind meat or poultry with vegetables through the meat chopper, then
add the potatoes mashed.  Season and stir the beaten egg in the
mixture. Shape in small balls, roll in cracker or bread crumbs, fry
quickly in deep hot fat until golden brown.  Drain on absorbent
paper. Serve hot. Serves 4.
