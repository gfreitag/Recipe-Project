Title: Better Than Sex Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 10 servings
Pudding mix
1 c  Sour cream
4 lg Eggs
1/4 c  Coffee
2 ts Vanilla, divided
1 c  Water
1/2 c  Oil
1 pk Duncan Hines chocolate
Butter or deep chocolate
Cake mix
3/4 c  Pecans, toasted & chopped
2    12-oz pkgs semisweet choc
Chips (divided)
1    4-oz pkg instant chocolate
Coat nuts & half the chips with 1 Tbs cake mix and set aside.
Combine cake mix, pudding mix, sour cream, eggs, oil, coffee & 1 tsp
vanilla. Beat at med speed for 3 mins.  Fold in chocolate chips and
nuts.  Turn into greased & floured tube pan and bake at 350 for 1 hr
or until done. Cool for 15 mins and turn onto rack.  Cool completely
before glazing.(Cake freezes well without glaze) GLAZE: Combine
remaining chips with 1 cp water in top of double boiler
over simmering water.  Stir until smooth & shiny.  Remove from
heat and add 1 tsp vanilla.  Chill until slightly thickened,
then drizzle over cake. *This comes from the
