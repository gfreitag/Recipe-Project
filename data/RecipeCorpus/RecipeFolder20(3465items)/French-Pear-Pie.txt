Title: French Pear Pie
Categories: Dessert, Fruit, Pie/tart
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 t  Lemon Rind
1 ea Unbaked 9-inch Pie Shell
6 c  Bartlett Pears; *
3 tb Orange Juice; Frozen, Conc.
MMMMM--------------------SPICY CRUMB TOPPING-------------------------
1/2 t  Ginger; Ground
1/8 t  Salt
1/4 c  Butter Or Regular Margarine
3/4 c  Unbleached Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
1 t  Cinnamon; Ground
*    The Bartlett Pears should be pared and thinly sliced.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++ Combine the pears, orange juice concentrate and
lemon rind in a bowl and toss lightly.
Arrange the pear mixture in the unbaked pie shell.
Prepare the Spicy Crumb Topping and sprinkle over the pear mixture.
Bake in a preheated 400 degree F. oven for 40 minutes or until the
pears are tender. Cover loosely with aluminum foil after 30 minutes
if the top becomes too brown.  Cool on a wire rack before serving.
SPICY CRUMB TOPPING: Combine the flour, sugar, spices and salt in a
bowl.
Cut in the margarine, using a pastry blender, until the mixture is
crumbly.
