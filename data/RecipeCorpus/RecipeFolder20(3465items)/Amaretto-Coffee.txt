Title: Amaretto Coffee
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 2 servings
1/3 c  Amaretto
1 x  Dessert Topping *
1 1/2 c  Warm Water
1 T  Instant Coffee Crystals
*    Dessert topping should be in a pressurized can.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- In a 2-cup measure stir together water and instant
coffee crystals. Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power for about 4
minutes or just till steaming hot.  Stir in Amaretto.  Serve in mugs.
Top each mug of coffee mixture with some dessert topping.
