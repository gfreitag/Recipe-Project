Title: Shartlesville Corn Pudding
Categories: Penndutch, Vegetable
Yield: 1 servings
2 ea Egg, separated
1/4 t  Salt
6 ea Corn, grated
4 T  Milk
1/8 t  Pepper
Add the beaten yolks of eggs to the corn and mix thoroughly. Stir in
the milk and seasonings. Add the stiffly beaten whites. Pour into a
buttered baking dish, set dish in pan with hot water and bake at
350-F about 30 minutes.
