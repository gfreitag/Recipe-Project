Title: Micro-Wave Peanut Brittle
Categories: Candy
Yield: 12 servings
1 t  Butter
1 t  Vanilla
1 t  Baking soda
1 c  Raw peanuts
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  White corn syrup
1/8 t  Salt
Stir together peanuts, sugar, syrup, salt.  Cook 4 minutes on high.
Stirring well, cook another 4 minutes. Add butter and vanilla to
peanut mixture stirring to blend. Return and cook 2 minutes.
(Peanuts will be slightly browned and syrup hot).
Add baking soda and gently stir till foamy.  Pour onto aluminum
foil. Let cool, break into pieces and store air tight container.
