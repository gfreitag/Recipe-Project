Title: Garlic Soup
Categories: Vegetable, Soup/stew, Tex/mexican
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
1 ea Egg; Large, Slightly Beaten
3 ea Cloves Garlic; Crushed
2 T  Vegetable Oil
2 ea White Bread; Slices, *
4 c  Chicken Broth
*    Bread should be cut into small pieces.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Cook and stir garlic in oil in a 3-quart saucepan
until brown. Stir in bread; cook and stir until light brown. Stir in
broth, salt and pepper. Heat to boiling; reduce heat.  Cover and
simmer for 20 minutes. Stir at least half of the hot mixture
gradually into egg. Stir back into hot mixture in saucepan.  Boil and
stir 1 minute.
Sprinkle with snipped parsley, if desired.
