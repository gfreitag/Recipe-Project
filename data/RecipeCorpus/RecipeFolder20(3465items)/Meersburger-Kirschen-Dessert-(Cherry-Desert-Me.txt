Title: Meersburger Kirschen-Dessert (Cherry Desert Me
Categories: German, Fruit, Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Cream Cheese; *
1/2 t  Vaillla Extract
2 oz Almonds; Ground **
1 c  Cream; Heavy
1 lb Cherries; Tart, Fresh
3 T  Kirsch
6 T  Sugar
2 T  ;Water
12 ea Ladyfingers
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
1 x  Pistachio Nuts; Chopped
*    Soften Cream Cheese to room temperature. ** Grind Almonds in the
blender, if ground almonds are not available.
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++ Wash and drain cherries.  Remove stones and
reserve 8 whole cherries for garnish.
Place cherries in a bowl; add kirsch.  In a small pan boil 3 T sugar
and the water for a minute to make a thin sugar syrup. Add syrup to
cherries; stir to blend. Cover and let soak for 20 minutes. Cut the
ladyfingers in half, divide into 4 portions, and place in individual
glass dishes. Arrange cherries on top.
Thoroughly blend cream cheese, 3 T sugar, vanilla extract, and ground
almonds.  Whip the cream and carefully fold it into the cream-cheese
mixture.
Spoon over the cherries.  Garnish with the chopped pistachio nuts and
whole cherries.
