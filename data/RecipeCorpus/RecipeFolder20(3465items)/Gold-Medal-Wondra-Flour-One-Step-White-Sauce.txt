Title: Gold Medal Wondra Flour One-Step White Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Cold milk
2 tb Gold Medal Wondra Flour
2 tb Margarine or butter
Directions: Heat all ingredients to boiling over medium heat, stirring
constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. 1 cup sauce.

Cheese sauce:  Add 1/4 teaspoon dry mustand and stir 1/2 cup shredded
Chedder or American cheese into hot sauce until melted.

High altitude (3500 to 6500 feet):  For Cheese Sauce, stir cheese into
sauce over low heat until melted.
