Title: Cold Cherry Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Water
2    Cloves
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Brandy
1 ts Sugar
2 lb Pitted red cherries
1    Stick cinnamon
2    Drops almond extract
2 c  Red wine
2    Egg yolks
In a pot combine the cherries, water, cinnamon, cloves, almond
extract, and salt.  Cook until cherries are soft (if fresh or
frozen), or until heated through (if canned).  Remove from heat.
Gently mash cherries. Add wine, brandy, and sugar.  Blend in egg
yolks.  Cook until slightly thickened. Chill.
