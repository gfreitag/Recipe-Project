Title: Swiss Cheese Sauce W/mustard
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2 tb Mustard, country Dijon-style
1 1/2 qt Chef-Mate Swiss Cheese Sauce
1.  Combine Swiss Cheese Sauce and mustard. 2. Heat to serving
temperature, stirring constantly.

Makes 24 2 ounce servings

Source: Carnation Company
