Title: Egg-Free, Wheat-Free, Milk-Free Meatloaf
Categories: Vegetable, Meat
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Broth; up to 1 cup
-gravy, tomato juice,etc
1 c  Chopped Vegetables
-optional
-peas, carrots,
-green beans, celery
Green peppers etc
1 1/2 lb Ground Beef
1/4 c  Raw, grated potato
-up to 1/2 cup
1/4 c  Chopped Onion
1/4 c  Grated Carrot
3/4 ts -Salt;or less
1 ds -Pepper
Mix all ingredients.  Bake at 350* for 1 hour.  You do not have to
add the optional vegetables, but it is a good way for children to
learn to enjoy them.

CALORIES: 1466
PROTEIN:  135.5 grams;
