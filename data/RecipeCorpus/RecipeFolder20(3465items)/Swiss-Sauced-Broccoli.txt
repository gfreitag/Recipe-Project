Title: Swiss-Sauced Broccoli
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Vegetable, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
1 x  Dash Salt
1 x  Dash White Pepper
1/3 c  Milk
1/4 c  Shredded Swiss Cheese
6 oz Fresh Broccoli
2 T  Water
1/8 t  Salt
2 t  Butter Or Margarine
1 1/2 t  Unbleached Flour
Wash broccoli; remove outer leaves and tough part of stalks. Cut
broccoli stalks lengthwise into uniform spears, following the
branching lines. In a 1-quart casserole combine broccoli, water and
1/8 t salt.
Micro-cook, covered, on 100% power for 5 to 6 minutes or just till
tender. Let stand, covered, while preparing sauce. For sauce, in a
1-cup measure micro-cook butter or margarine, uncovered, on 100%
power for 30 to 45 seconds or till melted.  Stir in flour, dash salt,
and pepper. Stir in milk.
Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power for 1 to 2 minutes or till
thickened and bubbly, stirring every 30 seconds. Stir in shredded
Swiss cheese til melted. Drain Broccoli. Serve sauce atop broccoli.
