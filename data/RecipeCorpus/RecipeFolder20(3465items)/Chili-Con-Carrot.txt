Title: Chili Con Carrot
Categories: Appetizer, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
2 t  Dried Parsley; Crushed
1 tb Dijon Mustard
1 tb Chili Powder
2 tb Fresh Parsley; Chopped, OR
1/2 c  Carrot; Diced
2 c  Sour Cream
1/2 c  Yellow Onion; Diced
1 t  Cumin; Ground
Combine all of the ingredients, blending thoroughly.
Cover and chill. Makes about 3 cups of dip SUGGESTED DIPPERS:
Brussels Sprouts, Salami, Cocktail Rye Bread
