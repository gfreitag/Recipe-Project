Title: Zucchini Crispies
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 24 servings
1/4 ts Dried Marjoram
1/4 ts Dried Thyme
3/4 c  Soft Breadcrumbs
1/2 ts Paprika
1 tb Oleo Melted
2 md Zucchini, Cut Into
1/2 Inch Slices
2 tb Low Cal. Mayonnaise
2 tb Minced Green Onions
1 tb Dijon Mustard
Place Zucchini Slices On A Baking Sheet Coated With Cooking Spray.
Combine Mayonnaise, Onion, Mustard, Marjoram & Dried Thyme; Stir
Well.Spread 1/2 t. Mayonnaise Mixture Over Each Zucchini Slice.
Combine Breadcrumbs, Paprika & Melted Oleo; Stir Well. Sprinkle
Evenly Over Zucchini Slices. Bake At 450 For 5 Min. OR Until
Breadcrumbs Are Browned. Serve Warm. (Fat 0.9. Chol. 0.)
