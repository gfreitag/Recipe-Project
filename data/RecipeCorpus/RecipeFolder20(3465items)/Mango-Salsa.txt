Title: Mango Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
1 ea Juice of 1 lemon
1 t  Olive oil
1 ea Mango
1 ea Jalapeno peppr
2 T  Fresh corriander
Dice Mango, jalapeno pepper and fresh coriander very finely. Combine.
Add lime juice and olive oil.  Serve the soup with a generous dollop
of sour cream and another generous dollop of Mango Salsa on top of
the sour cream.
