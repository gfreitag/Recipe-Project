Title: Apple Fritters
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 12 servings
2 x  Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
2 x  Apples, chopped
1 c  Flour
1 t  Baking Powder
1/4 t  Cinnamon
Mix dry ingredients. In separate bowl, beat eggs, stir in milk and
apples. Combine with dry ingredients. Drop 1 Tablespoon at a time
into hot 375 deg F. fat. Fry until golden brown. Drain and serve
sprinkled with confectioners' sugar, and/or maple syrup. Makes 12-15.
