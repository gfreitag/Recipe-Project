Title: Cindy's Chocolate Chip Cookie
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 12 servings
3/4 c  Flour
1 c  Chocolate chips
1 c  Oats
1/2 t  Salt
1 ea Stick of butter
6 T  Brown sugar
6 T  White sugar
1/2 t  Vanilla
1 ea Egg
Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes. Can substitute 3 bags of instant
oatmeal instead of oats and salt.
