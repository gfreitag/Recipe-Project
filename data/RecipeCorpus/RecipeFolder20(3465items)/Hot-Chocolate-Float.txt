Title: Hot Chocolate Float
Categories: Chocolate, Dessert, Beverage
Yield: 2 servings
1 x  Ice Cream *
2 c  Warm Water
2 ea Env. Instant Cocoa Mix
*    Ice cream should be one of the following:
Chocolate-chip Mint or
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- In a 4-cup measure micro-cook water, uncovered, on
100% power for 4 to 5 minutes or till steaming hot. Stir in the cocoa
mix. Pour into two mugs. Top the hot chocolate mixture in each mug
with a small scoop of ice cream. Serve immediately.
