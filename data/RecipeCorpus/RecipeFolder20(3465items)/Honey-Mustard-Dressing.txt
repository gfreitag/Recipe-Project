Title: Honey-Mustard Dressing
Categories: Salad, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
3/4 c  Safflower oil
-(or other vegetable oil)
1 tb Dijon-style mustard
2 tb Honey
3 tb Lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
IN A BOWL, WHISK TOGETHER the mustard, honey, lemon juice, salt and
pepper. Gradually whisk in the oil to form a creamy dressing.

Makes 1 1/2 Cups
