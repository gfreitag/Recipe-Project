Title: Potato Filling (For Meats)
Categories: Penndutch, Stuffing
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Parsley, minced
2 T  Butter, melted
1 t  Salt
1/2 c  Celery, diced
2 c  Potato, mashed, hot
1 ea Egg, well beaten
1 ea Onion, minced
1/2 t  Poultry seasoning
1 pn Pepper
Mix together the potatoes and egg. Soak the bread in cold water and
squeeze dry. Add to the potato mixture, stir in the other
ingredients, and mix well. Use for stuffing fowl or meat.
