Title: One Shot Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 3 servings
1 T  Pepper
1 1/2 t  Dry mustard
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Tabasco
1 lb Butter
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
2 x  Cloves garlic, minced
1/2 c  Whiskey
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
Melt butter in a saucepan; add onion and garlic and cook slowly until
is soft. Add remaining ingredients and beat to mix.
