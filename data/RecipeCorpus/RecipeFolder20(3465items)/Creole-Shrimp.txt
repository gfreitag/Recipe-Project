Title: Creole Shrimp
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
1/4 ts Salt
1/3 ts Pepper
2 tb Orange Juice Concentrate
Thawed
3    Packets Equal
12 oz Cooked Shrimp
1 cn (15 Oz.) Tomato Puree
1/2 c  Water
3 tb White Vinegar
3 tb Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 ts Hot Sauce
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1 tb Mustard
In Saucepan, Combine Ingredients Except The Equal And Shrimp.Slowly
Bring To Boil, Stirring Occasionally.  Remove From Heat.  Stir in
Equal. Add Shrimp To Sauce And Serve.
Serve Over Rice.
