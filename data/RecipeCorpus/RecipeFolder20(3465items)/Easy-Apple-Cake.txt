Title: Easy Apple Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit, Dessert, Apples
Yield: 5 servings
1/2 t  Cinnamon
4 x  Apples, peeled and sliced
1/4 c  Sugar
MMMMM------------------------SECOND STEP-----------------------------
1/4 t  Vanilla
1 c  Flour
1 t  Baking Powder
1/2 c  Sugar
2 T  Soft Butter or Margarine
1 x  Egg
Preheat oven to 350 deg F.
Mix together 1/4 cup sugar and 1/2 t cinnamon. Place a layer of
apples in greased baking dish, sprinkle with half the sugar-cinnamon
mixture, cover with remaining apples and cover with remaining
sugar-cinnamon. Cream 1/2 cup sugar and butter. Add egg and vanilla
and mix well. Stir in flour and baking powder and spoon over apples,
spreading it as best as you can.
Bake 30 minutes
Best served warm, with vanilla ice cream.
