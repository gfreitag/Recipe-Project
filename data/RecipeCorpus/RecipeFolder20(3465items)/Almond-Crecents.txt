Title: Almond Crecents
Categories: German, Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 8 servings
2 1/2 c  Flour; unbleached
1 c  Almonds; ground
1 x  Confectioners' sugar
1 c  Butter or margarine
3/4 c  Sugar
1 t  Vanilla extract
1 1/2 t  Almond extract
Beat together butter and sugar until very light and fluffy. Blend in
extracts.  Mix in flour and almonds.
Using about 1 T of dough for each, shape into logs and bend into
crecents. Place on greased cookie sheet. Bake 12 to 15 minutes at 350
degrees F until light brown. While warm, roll crecents in
confectioners' sugar. Cool on racks and store in a tightly sealed
container. Makes 3 dozen cookies.
