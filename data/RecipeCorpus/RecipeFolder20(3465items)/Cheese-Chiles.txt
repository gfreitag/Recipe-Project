Title: Cheese Chiles
Categories: Appetizer, Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 12 servings
1 ea Cilantro Stems; Bunch, *
1 x  Paprika
1 c  Cheddar Cheese; Shredded
1 c  Colby Cheese; Shredded
1 t  Red Chiles; Ground
*  Cut the cilantro stems into 1/2-inch pieces.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Place all ingredients except cilantro and paprika
in food processor work bowl fitted with steel blade; cover and
process until smooth, about 1 minute.  Roll mixture by teaspoonfuls
into chile shapes. Insert cilantro pieces in wide ends of shape fro
stems. Sprinkle with paprika.
Cover and refrigerate until serving time.
