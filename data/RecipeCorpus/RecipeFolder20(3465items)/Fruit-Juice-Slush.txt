Title: Fruit Juice Slush
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 20 servings
1 cn Frozen orange juice (10 oz)
1 cn Frozen lemonade
2 c  Rum, gin, or vodka
2 c  Boiling water
4 ea GREEN tea bags
7 c  Water
2 c  Sugar
Put GREEN tea bags in 2 c. boiling water, let set and chill. Dissolve
7 c. water and 2 c. sugar together.
Mix all ingredients together and put in freezer for 24 hours or
longer. Stir before using.  Use 1/2 glass slush and 1/2 glass 7UP.
