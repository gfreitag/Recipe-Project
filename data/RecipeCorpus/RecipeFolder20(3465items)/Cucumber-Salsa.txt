Title: Cucumber Salsa
Categories: Tex/mexican, Vegetable, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1 t  Cumin; Ground
1/2 t  Salt
2 ea Cucumbers; Medium *
1 c  Dairy Sour Cream
1 c  Yogurt; Plain
1/4 c  Parsley; Snipped
1/4 c  Cilantro; Fresh, Snipped
*  Cucumbers should be pared, seeded and coarsely shredded.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Mix all ingredients.  Cover and refrigerate until
chilled, about 2 hours. Makes about 3 cups salsa.
