Title: Barbecued Pork Chops
Categories: Cajun/creol, Main dish, Pork
Yield: 8 servings
3 T  Brown sugar
4 T  Chili sauce
8 ea Pork chops
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Vinegar
2 T  Dry mustard
Combine ingredients and mix well.  Pour over seasoned chops in glass
baking dish, cover and bake at 400 F degrees for 1-1/2 hours,
uncovering last 30 minutes.
Water may be added to make gravy.  Delicious with ribs also. From
"Talk About Good" contributed by Susan Chandler Castille
