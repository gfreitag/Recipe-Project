Title: No Tenemos Rancho Applesauce Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 6 servings
1 t  Baking Powder
1/2 t  Salt
1 c  Nuts
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Oil
1 t  Vanilla
3 c  HOT Applesauce
2 c  Unbleached Flour
1 c  Sugar
1 T  Corn Starch
1 T  Cocoa
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Nutmeg
1/2 t  Cloves
2 t  Baking Soda
Sift together all the dry ingredients, add nuts and raisins. Stir in
the oil, vanilla and hot applesauce.
Pour into a 9 x 13-inch pan.  Bake at 350 degrees F. for 30 to 40
minutes or until done.
