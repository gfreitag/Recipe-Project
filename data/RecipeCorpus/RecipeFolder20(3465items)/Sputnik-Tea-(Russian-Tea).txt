Title: Sputnik Tea (Russian Tea)
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 10 servings
2 t  Cloves; Ground
2 t  Cinnamon
18 oz Tang Mix; (1 Jar)
3/4 c  Instant Tea /Lemon; Lipton's
1 1/2 c  Sugar
Mix all ingredients and keep in tightly sealed jar.
Use 2 t of the mix per cup of boiling water.  Can also be made and
then iced.
