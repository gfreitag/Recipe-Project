Title: China Moon Ten Spice Honey Dip
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chinese
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM--------------------------BARB DAY-------------------------------
- CM Pickled Ginger recipe
1/4 ts -China Moon Ten Spice; see
- recipe
1/2 c  Honey
1/4 c  Soy sauce; plus 1 tablespoon
1/4 c  Pickled Ginger juice; from
Makes 1 cup.
Combine all of the ingredients in a small non-aluminum saucepan.
Heat, stirring, over low heat until the honey dissolves, 3 to 4
minutes.  Store, refrigerated, in a clean container.  Warm over low
heat to a liquid consistency before using.

Source: China Moon Restaurant, San Francisco, Ca, Barbara Tropp
