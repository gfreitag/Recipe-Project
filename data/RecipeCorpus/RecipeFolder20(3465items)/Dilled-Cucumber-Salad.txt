Title: Dilled Cucumber Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Finely Chopped Green Onions
1/4 ts White Pepper
2 tb Minced Fresh Dill
8 oz Plain Low Fat Yogurt
2 sm Cucumbers, Peeled, Seeded
And Shredded
Spread Yogurt Thickly Over Several Layers Of Paper Towels; Cover With
Another Layer Of Paper Towels.  Let Stand 5 Min.  Scrape Yogurt Into A
Medium Bowl.  Add Cucumbers, Green Onions, & Pepper; Stirring Well,
Fold in Dill.  Cover & Chill 1 Hour.
