Title: Frankfuter Bohnensuppe (Bean Soup with Frankfu
Categories: German, Soup/stew
Yield: 4 servings
2 ea Onions; Small, Chopped
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper; White
6 ea Frankfurters, Sliced *
2 T  Parsley; Chopped
1 lb Navy Beans; Dried
8 c  Water
3 c  Beef Broth
1 ea Carrot; Chopped
1 ea Celery Stalk; Chopped
4 ea Bacon; Strips, Cubed
*  Note:  Use the real Frankfurters in this recipe and not the hot
dogs! +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++++++ Soak beans overnight.  In a 3-quart saucepan bring
beans, water and beef froth to a boil.
Cook for about 1 hour.  Add carrot and celery and continue cooking
for 30 minutes.  In a separate frypan cook the bacon until
transparent. Add the onions; cook until golden. Set aside. Mash soup
through a sieve or food mill.  Return to pan and add the bacon onion
mixture, salt and pepper. Add frankfurters; reheat about 5 minutes.
Sprinkle soup with chopped parsley and serve.
