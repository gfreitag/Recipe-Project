Title: Serendipity's Southern Chili
Categories: Chili
Yield: 8 servings
3    Garlic cloves,medium,crushed
1 T  Black pepper,freshly ground
1 T  Salt
1 T  Sugar
1 cn Tomatoes,whole(12oz ea)
4 cn Kidney beans,w/H2O(16oz ea)
4 T  Butter
1    Onion,large,coarsely chopped
4 lb Beef,lean,hamburger grind
8 T  Red chile,hot,ground
4 T  Red chile,mild,ground
3 T  Cumin,ground
1. Melt the butter in a large heavy pot over medium heat. Add the
onion and cook until it is translucent.~ 2. Combine the meat with the
ground chile, cumin, garlic, and pepper. Add this meat-and-spice
mixture to the pot. Break up any lumps with a fork and cook, stirring
occasionally, until the meat is evenly browned.~ 3. Stir in the
remainig ingredients. Bring to a boil, then lower the heat and
simmer, uncovered, for at least 4 hours but as many as 8 if possible.
Stir occasionally. Taste and adjust seasonings.~
