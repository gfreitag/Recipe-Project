Title: Rindfleisch-Eintopf (Beef Stew)
Categories: German, Beef, Main dish, Soup/stew, Vegetable
Yield: 6 servings
1 x  Pepper; To Taste
2 t  Mustard; Dry
1/2 t  Celery Seed
1/4 c  Water
1 lb Tomatoes; (1 can)
1/4 c  Shortening
3 lb Rump Roast; Boneless
2 c  Onions; Sliced
1/4 c  Unbleached Flour
2 T  Salt
2 T  Sugar
Melt shortening in a Dutch oven.  Add the meat and brown on all sides.
Place the onions on top of the meat. Mix the flour and seasonings
with 1/4 c water.
Blend with the tomatoes and add the misture to the dutch oven. Bake
at 325 degrees F about 2 hours, until the meat is fork-tender. Serve
with oven-browned potatoes.
