Title: Veal Loaf
Categories: Penndutch, Veal
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Bread crumbs
1 T  Parsley, chopped
1 t  Salt
1 T  Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Stock
3 lb Veal, lean, ground
1 ea Egg, well beaten
2 T  Butter, melted
1/8 t  Pepper
1/8 t  Nutmeg
Mix all the ingredients together and moisten with the stock. Pack
into a greased loaf pan. Dot with butter or other shortening and bake
at 350-F for 1 hour. Serve hot or cold as desired.
