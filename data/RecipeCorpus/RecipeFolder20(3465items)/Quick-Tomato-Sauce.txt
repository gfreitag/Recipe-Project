Title: Quick Tomato Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 t  Thyme
1/2 t  Basil
1 t  Garlic powder
1 pn Pepper
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Chicken bouillon
3 c  Chopped tomatoes
1 t  Apple juice concentrate
1/2 t  Oregano
Cook onions in bouillon until soft.  Add remaining ingredients. Bring
to boil, cover, simmer 30 to 45 min. Makes 3 1/2 cups. Cal: 10 per
1/2 c. Fat: trace.
