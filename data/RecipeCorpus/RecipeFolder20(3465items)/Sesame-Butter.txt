Title: Sesame Butter
Categories: Greek
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Garlic Salt
1 T  Toasted Sesame Seed; *
1/4 c  Margarine Or Butter;Softened
1 t  Worcestershire Sauce
*    To toast sesame seeds, spread out in a small pie tin and bake at
350 ~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Mix all ingredients together.
Spoon over hot patties.
