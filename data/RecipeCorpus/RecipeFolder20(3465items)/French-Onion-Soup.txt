Title: French Onion Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 2 servings
1/4 t  Salt
1/4 t  Cayenne pepper
6 ea Slice of French bread
1 c  Muenster cheese
3 ea Small onions
1/2 c  Butter
2 ea 10 1/2 oz. can beef broth
1 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Dry white wine
Add broth, water, wine, salt and pepper to the sauted onions. Cook to
boiling and simmer a minute or two.
Float french bread and Muenster cheese in the bowl.
