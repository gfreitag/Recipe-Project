Title: Basting Sauce for Roast (For Company)
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Beef
Yield: 12 servings
2 t  Salt
1 t  Pepper
2 T  Mayonnaise
2 T  Vinegar
2 T  Kitchen Bouquet
12 lb Chuck Or Arm Roast
2 t  Dry Mustard
1/2 t  Garlic salt
2 t  Onion Salt
2 t  Celery Salt
Place unseasoned Roast in Roaster with 1 inch water.
Cover and cook 3-4 hours depending on sixe of roast.
Add sauce and baste.  Continue to cook covered til tender. Thicken
liquid with flour.
