Title: Chicken Breasts with Rice
Categories: Main dish, Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Chopped Parsley
1 ea Jar (1 oz) Pine Nuts (opt.)
1 1/2 lb (2 Med.) Chicken Breasts *
1/2 t  Seasoned Salt
1 x  Paprika
1 ea Jar (2 1/2 oz) Dried Beef
2 ea Med. Stalks Celery, Chopped
1 ea Small Onion, chopped
1 T  Butter or Margarine
2 c  Cooked Rice
*    Have the butcher bone and cut each breast in to halves.
~------------------------------------------------------
~------------------ Snip beef into small pieces.
Cover and microwave beef, celery, onion and margarine in 2-Qt
casserole on high (100%) until onion is crisp tender, 3 to 4 minutes.
Stir in rice, parsley, and pine nuts. Arrange chicken breasts skin
sides up and thickest parts ot outside on rice mixture.  Sprinkle
with seasoned salt and paprika. Cover and microwave 5 minutes; turn
casserole one half turn. Microwave until chicken is done, 8 to 11
minutes.
