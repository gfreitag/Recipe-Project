Title: Tofu Au Gingembre
Categories: Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Sunflower oil
2/3 c  Tamari
1 lb Firm Tofu
1/3 c  Ginger root, grated
Cut the tofu in slices and place them side by side in a shallow
oven-proof dish. In a small bowl combine ginger, oil and tamari. Pour
over the tofu. Let marinate for 1 - 3 hours. Preheat oven to 350
degrees. Bake for 25 - 30 minutes. Serves 4. From The Gazette,
91/01/16
