Title: Muffin Mix
Categories: Misc/food, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
2/3 c  Sugar
1 tb Salt
8 c  Unbleached All-purpose Flour
1/3 c  Baking Powder
1 c  Vegetable Shortening
In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  With a
pastry blender, cut shortening into dry ingredients until evenly
destributed.  Put in a large airtight container. Lable as MUFFIN MIX
and stor in a cool, dry place.  Use whthin 10 to 12 weeks.  Makes
about 10 cups of MUFFIN MIX.
