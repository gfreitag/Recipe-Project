Title: Shrimp Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Seafood
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Drops of Tabasco Sauce (or4)
8 oz Cream Cheese, softened
3 T  Mayonnaise
1 t  Worcestershire Sauce
10 oz Canned Shrimp, drained
1/4 c  Dr. Pepper
2 t  Lemon Juice, fresh
1/8 t  Onion Salt
1 x  Dash Garlic Powder
Combine 1/2 the shrimp and the remaining ingredients in blender or
electric mixer; blend until fluffy and smooth. Coarsely chop
remaining shrimp; fold into mixture. Refrigerate. Source: Cookin'
with Dr. Pepper
