Title: Lemon Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie, Dessert
Yield: 24 servings
4 T  Flour
2 c  Sugar
6 T  REAL lemon juice
1 c  Butter (2 sticks)
2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Powdered sugar
4 ea Eggs
Cream 1 c. butter, 2c. flour and 1/2 c. powdered sugar together and
press into a 13"x9" pan to form crust.
Bake at 350* for 15-20 minutes. FILLING-  Mix together all other
ingredience to make filling.  Use real lemon juice. Pour over shell.
Bake again at 350* for 20-25 minutes. Cool completely, sprinkle with
powdered sugar.  Cut into small squares.
