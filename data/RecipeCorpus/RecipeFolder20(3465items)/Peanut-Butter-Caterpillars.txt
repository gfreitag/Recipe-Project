Title: Peanut Butter Caterpillars
Categories: Dessert, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 x  Grape
1 x  Banana
2 T  Peanut Butter
Peel and slice a banana. Sprad slices with peanut or sesame butter
and connect the slices. Add a grape up front for the head ("gluing"
with more peanut butter.) If desired, make this a game with the child
~ what veggie can we add to make antennas? etc. (maybe a strip of
celery.)
