Title: Kartoffelsalat Mit Biermarinade (Potato Salad/
Categories: German, Salad, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
2 T  Unbleached Flour
1/2 t  Mustard; Dry
1 T  Sugar
1 c  Beer; Any Brand
1/2 t  Tobasco Sauce
2 T  Parsley; Chopped Fresh
6 ea Potatoes; Medium
4 ea Bacon; Slices
1 T  Onion; Chopped
1 ea Celery; Stalk, Chopped
1 t  Salt
2 T  Butter
Boil potatoes in medium-size saucepan until just tender. Peel and
slice. Fry bacon until crisp.  Break into small pieces and mix with
onion, celery and salt; set aside. Stir melted butter and flour in a
small saucepan until blended.  Add mustard and sugar.
Slowly stir in beer and Tabasco sauce. Bring to boil, stirring
constantly. Pour over potatoes.  Sprinkle with parsley. Toss lightly
and let stand 1 hour.  Add bacon mixture; toss gently and serve.
