Title: Hunters Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Appetizer
Yield: 5 servings
5 ea Peppercorns
1 ea Bay leaf
2 c  Diced Polish sausage
1 c  Coarsely chopped bacon
1 ea Steamed potatoes
4 ea Dried mushroom
1/4 c  Water
2 lb Sauerkraut
1 ea Apple, peel, core, sliced
1 cn 20 oz Tomatoes
Soak the mushrooms in 1/4 cup water for 2 hours.
Bring to boil and simmer for 1/2 hour.  Slice.  Wash the sauerkraut
and squeeze it.  Add mushrooms and the liquid in which they were
cooked. Add the apples, the tomatoes, peppercorns, and bay leaf.
Cover and simmer for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Add the meat and the
bacon, simmer 1 hour longer. It is best reheated. Served with steamed
potatoes or rye bread.
