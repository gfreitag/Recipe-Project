Title: Green Tomato Relish
Categories: Relish, Can/preserv, Vegetable, Tomato
Yield: 6 servings
3 1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Cider Vinegar
1 c  Water
1 T  Mustard Seed
1 T  Ground Turmeric
2 t  Celery Seed
6 ea Red Sweet Peppers *
2 lb Green Tomatoes *
2 lb Onions *
1 ea Small Head Cabbage *
3 ea Sweet Green Peppers *
1/4 c  Pickling Salt
*    All vegetables are to be coarsely ground.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Mix vegetables and salt.  Cover and let stand 12
to 18 hours. Drain vegetables and rinse.
Mix vegetables and remaining ingredients.  Heat to boiling; reduce
heat. Simmer, uncovered, 3 minutes.
Pack simmering mixture in hot jars, leaving 1/8" headspace; seal.
Process 10 minutes in a boiling water bath. Makes 5 - 6 pints. NOTE:
~---- Vegetables can be chopped if you prefer.
