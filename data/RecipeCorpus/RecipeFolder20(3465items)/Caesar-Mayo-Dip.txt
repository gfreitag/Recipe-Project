Title: Caesar Mayo Dip
Categories: Appetizer, Cheese/eggs, Fish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 t  Black Pepper
2 tb Parmesan Cheese
1 tb Lemon Juice
2 ea Anchovy Fillets
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1 t  Dijon Mustard
1 t  Worcestershire Sauce
Chop and mash the anchovy fillets on a cutting board.
Put in a bowl and blend in the mayonnaise.  Add the remaining
ingredients and blend well. Cover and chill. Makes about 1 3/4 cups
of dip SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Seafood, Deli Roast Beef Or Turkey,
Radishes, Broccoli, Cauliflower, Snow Peas
