Title: Wonton Soup
Categories: Soup/stew, Chinese, Appetizer
Yield: 6 servings
25    Wontons
3    Scallions, thinly sliced
-- on the diagonal
1 1/2 c  Bok choy
1 tb Peanut oil
6 c  Vegetable stock
Salt & pepper
In a soup pot, saute the bok choy in the oil for 1 minute.  Add the
stock & bring to a boil.  Lower the heat & simmer till the bok choy
is just tender, about 5 minutes.  Season.

In a separate pot, bring about 2 quarts of water to a boil.  Drop the
wontons into the boiling water & cook til ltender.  Drain & add to the
soup.  Serve garnished with scallions.
