Title: Sweet Potatoes and Apples
Categories: Side dish, Vegetable
Yield: 5 servings
4 T  Butter
1/2 t  Salt
2 c  Sweet Potatoes   *
2 x  Apples, peeled
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
* boiled and skinned
Preheat oven to 350 deg F.
Slice cooked potatoes and apples 1/4"- 1/2" thick. Place half of
each in buttered baking dish. Sprinkle with half the sugar, salt, and
dot with half the butter. Repeat. Cover and bake 40 minutes.
Variations: Add 1/2 cup chopped nuts and/or tablespoon of grated
orange rind.
