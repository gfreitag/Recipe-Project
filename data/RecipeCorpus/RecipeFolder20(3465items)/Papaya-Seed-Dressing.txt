Title: Papaya-Seed Dressing
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Salt
1 c  Oil
1    Small Onion; minced
3 tb Papaya seeds
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Dry mustard
1 c  Tarragon vinegar
1 ds MSG (optional)
Place sugar, mustard, vinegar, MSG and salt in blender. Blend and
gradually add oil and onion. When thoroughly blended, stir in papaya
seeds. Makes 3 cups Created by: Sheraton Kauai Hotel, Hawaii (C) 1992
The Los Angeles Times
