Title: Chile Caribe
Categories: Chili
Yield: 1 servings
Chiles,dried
1. Rinse, dry, and remove the stems, then crush or tear the dried
chiles with your hands into little pieces (approximately 1/2 inch).
Chile caribe can contain a portion of the seeds of the pepper. Red
pepper flakes, sold in most supermarkets, have the look of chile
caribe but are usually the Italian type pepper and impart a different
flavor.~
