Title: Crab and Avocado Cocktail
Categories: Seafood, Appetizer, Tex/mexican
Yield: 6 servings
2 T  Cilantro; Fresh, Snipped
1 ea Clove Garlic; Finely Chopped
3/4 t  Salt
1 x  Pepper; Dash Of
1 1/2 c  Lettuce; Finely Chopped
1 x  Lemon Or Lime Wedges
1 c  Crab Meat; Cooked
2 ea Avocados; Peeled & Chopped
2 ea Jalapeno Chiles; *
1/4 c  Tomato; Chopped, 1 small
1/4 c  Lime Juice
2 T  Olive Or Vegetable Oil
2 T  Onion; Chopped
*  Jalapeno Chiles should be seeded and finely chopped.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Mix all ingredients except lettuce and lime
wedges. Place 1/4 cup of lettuce on ead of 6 serving dishes. Divide
the crabmeat mixture among the dishes.  Garnish with lime wedges.
