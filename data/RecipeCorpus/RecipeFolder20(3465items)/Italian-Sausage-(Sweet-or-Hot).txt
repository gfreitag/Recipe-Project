Title: Italian Sausage (Sweet or Hot)
Categories: Italian, Sausage, Pork
Yield: 1 servings
5 ea Cloves pressed garlic
2 T  Paprika
1 c  Cold water
5 lb Coarse Ground Pork Butt
1 1/3 T  Salt
1 1/3 T  Ground Coriander
3/16 T  Coarse Ground Black
pper Add 2 tsp crushed red peppers for HOT sausage.
Combine all ingredients, mix well and stuff into hog casing or make
patties.
