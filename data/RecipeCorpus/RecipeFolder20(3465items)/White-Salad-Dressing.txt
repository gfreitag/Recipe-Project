Title: White Salad Dressing
Categories: Penndutch, Dressing, Salad
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Cream
1/2 c  Vinegar
1 t  Mustard, dry
2 T  Olive oil
3 ea Egg yolk, well beaten
1 T  Flour
1 T  Sugar
1 t  Salt
Mix the dry ingredients together and slowly add the vinegar and oil.
Stir in the cream and beaten egg yolks and cook in top of double
boiler until thick.
