Title: Spiced Peaches
Categories: Penndutch, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
4 lb Brown sugar
1 t  Cloves, whole
1 t  Allspice, whole
7 lb Peach
1 qt Vinegar
1 T  Cinnamon, stick
Tie spices in a bag and add to the vinegar. Bring to a boil and stir
in the sugar. Scald and peel peaches leaving them whole. Drop
peaches, a few at a time, into spiced liquid and cook until soft.
Fill sterilized jars and seal.
