Title: Peanut Butter Chicken Wings
Categories: Poultry, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ts Durkees hot sauce
1/4 c  Peanut butter
1/2 ts Salt
4 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Soy sauce
2 1/2 lb Chicken wings; trimmed
2 ts Curry powder
1/2 ts Black pepper
2 tb Ginger; fresh minced
3    Garlic cloves; minced
Mix marinade ingredients in large bowl. Add wings, stir and
marinade for 2 hours at room temperature. Drain and reserve marinade.
Preheat oven boiler. Place on broiling rack and broil 15-20 minutes
or till browned. Turn after 10 minutes and baste with marinate (or
grill on BBQ).
