Title: Curried Mayonnaise
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Spread
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 ts Saffron Threads
1/4 ts Water
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Curry Paste, Red
2 ts Lime Juice
Mix saffron threads and water into a paste.  Combine all ingredients
and process until smooth.  Makes about 1 cup.  Will keep short
periods if refrigerated.  Serve with ham sandwiches, tuna salad, cold
turkey.
