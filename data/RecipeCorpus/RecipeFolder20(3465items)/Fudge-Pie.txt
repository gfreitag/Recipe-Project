Title: Fudge Pie
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
1 T  Flour
4 ea Eggs
1 x  Pinch of salt
2 ea Chocolate squares-unsweeten
1/4 lb Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1 t  Vanilla
Melt chocolate and butter together.  Add sugar and salt to beaten
eggs, add melted ingred. Stir in flour, add vanilla. Bake in greased
pie pan for 25 min. in 350 degree oven. Reduce to 250 degree and bake
15-20 min. longer.
