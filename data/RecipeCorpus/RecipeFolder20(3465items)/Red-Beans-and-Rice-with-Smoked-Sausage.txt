Title: Red Beans and Rice with Smoked Sausage
Categories: Pork, Main dish, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Smoked ham shanks
1/2 t  Sage
1 ea Large onion chopped
1 pn Cayenne pepper
1 x  Salt
1 x  Freshly cooked rice
1 lb Dried red beans
1 ea Garlic clove chopped
1 1/2 lb Smoked sausage cut
1 t  Dried thyme
1 x  Into chunks
1 t  Ground pepper
Place beans in Dutch oven and cover generously with water. Let soak 30
minutes. Add remaining ingredients to beans except salt and rice.
Bring to boil over  medium  high heat.
Reduce heat to medium low,  cover and  simmer until
beans  are tender,  adding more water if necessary (about 2 1/2
hours). Add  salt  to  taste.
Discard ham  bones.   Remove  about  3 tablespoons
of  beans from mixture and mash to paste;  return to
Dutch oven and stir.  Simmer 15 more minutes.  Serve over hot rice.
