Title: Black Bean Soup with Sour Cream and Mango Salsa
Categories: Soup/stew, Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Chicken stock
1    Salt and pepper
4    Bay leaves
1 tb Honey
2 c  Black turtle beans
1 c  Diced onion
1    Diced carrot
2    Diced celery stalks
3    Strips of bacon
Combine all the ingredients and add enough water to cover the beans.
Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer and cook until beans are
thoroughly cooked.  Add more water as required.  Remove bay leaves
and puree the soup in a blender.  Then pass the soup through a fine
sieve to remove the skins of the beans.
