Title: Old Time Cornbread
Categories: Bread, Londontowne
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Cornmeal
3 T  Lard, melted
1 c  Flour
2 ea Eggs
1 1/2 c  Buttermilk
1 t  Salt
3/4 t  Soda
Beat eggs, milk, and lard together. Add remaining ingredients and mix
well. Pour into greased 11" X 7" X 2" and bake in hot (400) oven
about 30 minutes.
