Title: Fettuccine with Scallops and Peas
Categories: Seafood, Pasta, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Unsalted Butter **
Saffron Butter Sauce ***
10 oz Frozen Peas
3/4 lb Fettuccine
1 1/4 lb Sea Scallops *
* Scallops should be rinsed and patted dry. ** Butter should be cut
into small bits. *** For Saffron Butter Sauce see recipe.
