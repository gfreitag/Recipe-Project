Title: Quick Applesauce Muffins
Categories: Muffin, Fruit, Apples
Yield: 12 servings
1/4 c  Milk
1 x  Egg
2 T  Cooking oil
2 c  Bisquick
1/4 c  Sugar
1 t  Cinnamon
1/2 c  Applesauce
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
2 T  Butter or margarine, melted
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 t  Cinnamon
Preheat oven to 350 deg F.
Combine Bisquick, 1/4 cup sugar, and 1 teaspoon cinnamon. Add
applesauce, milk, egg amd oil, and beat vigorously for 30 seconds.
Fill greased muffin pans 2/3 full and bake 12-15 minutes. Cool
slightly and remove from pans. Mix remaining sugar and cinnamon. Dip
tops of muffins in melted buter, then in sugar-cinnamon. Makes 12.
