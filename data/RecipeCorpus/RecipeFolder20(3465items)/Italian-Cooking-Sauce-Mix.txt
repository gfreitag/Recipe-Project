Title: Italian Cooking Sauce Mix
Categories: Misc/food, Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
4 cn (8 oz) Tomato Sauce
2 cn (6 oz) Tomato Paste
2 tb Parsley Flakes
2 tb Cornstarch
1 ts Instant Minced Garlic
1 1/2 ts Italian Seasoning
2 cn (14 1/2 oz) Stewed Tomatoes
2 c  Water
2 tb Instan Minced Onion
3 ts Salt
4 ts Green Pepper Flakes
3 ts Sugar
Puree Stewed tomatoes.  Combine all ingredients in a large kettle or
Dutch oven.  Simmer 15 minutes over medium-low heat.  Cool and put
into six 1-pt containers leaving 1/2-inch space at top.  Seal and
label containers Italian Cooking Sauce Mix.  Freeze and use within 6
months. Makes about 6 pints of mix.
