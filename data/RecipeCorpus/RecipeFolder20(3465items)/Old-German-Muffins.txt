Title: Old German Muffins
Categories: German, Bread, Dessert
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Cinnamon
2 t  Baking powder
2 1/4 c  Flour; unbleached
1/4 c  Almonds; ground
1 T  Orange rind; grated
1/4 c  Raisins; if desired
3/4 c  Butter or margarine
1/2 c  Sugar
2 ea Eggs; large
1 T  Rum
1 t  Vanilla extract
3 T  Milk
Cream butter and sugar.  Beat in eggs, rum, vanilla, and milk. Mix
cinnamon, baking powder, and flour.
Add flour mixture to butter mixture. Gently mix in almonds, orange
rind, and raisins.  Pour batter into greased muffin tins, filling
half full. Bake at 375 degrees F. for 25 to 30 minutes, or until
browned. Makes 18 muffins.
