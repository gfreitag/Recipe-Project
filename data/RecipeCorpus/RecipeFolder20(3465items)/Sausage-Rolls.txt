Title: Sausage Rolls
Categories: German
Yield: 1 servings
12    Sausages, about 6 in (16cm)
Long
FOR BRUSHING
A little canned milk
Cottage cheese and oil
Pastry (3)
FILLING
1/2    Egg white
roll out the pastry 1/8 in (3mm) thick; with a pastry wheel cut out 12
rectangles of 6x4 in (16x10 cm) and brush the edges with egg white.
roll up a sausage in each piece of pastry and lay on a greased baking
sheet. brush with milk. OVEN: pre-heat for 5 min. at very hot, bake
at moderately hot. BAKING TIME: 15-20 min.
