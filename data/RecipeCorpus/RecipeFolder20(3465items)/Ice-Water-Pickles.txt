Title: Ice-Water Pickles
Categories: Can/preserv, Vegetable
Yield: 5 servings
1 qt Cider Vinegar
1 c  Sugar
2 t  Pickling Salt
3 lb 4-inch Cucumbers
5 ea Med. Onions
1 T  Mustard Seed
Wash the cucumbers, quarter them lengthwise, and soak them in
ice-water for 2 hours.  Peel and slice the onions and pack them in
the bottoms of 5 pint jars.
Pack the cucumbers lengthwise in the jars.  Combine the mustard seed,
vinegar, sugar and salt, bring to a boil and boil for 1 minute. Fill
the jars to overflowing with the boiling-hot syrup and seal. Makes 5
pints.
