Title: Mousse in a Minute
Categories: Dessert
Yield: 5 servings
1 c  Thawed Cool Whip
1 pk Jello instant pudding (4 oz)
1 1/2 c  COLD milk
Prepare pudding mix as directed on package, using 1 1/2 cups milk.
Fold in whipped topping (Cool Whip) and spoon into dessert dishes.
Garnish with additional Cool Whip.  Great with any flavor of Jello
Instant Pudding.
