Title: Apricot Basting Sauce
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce, Tex/mexican
Yield: 2 servings
1 T  Honey
1 t  Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 c  Apricot Jam
1/4 c  Apricots; Dried, Fine Chop
1/4 c  Dry White Wine
Heat all ingredients over low head, stirring occasionally, until jam
is melted. Makes about 1 cup of sauce.
