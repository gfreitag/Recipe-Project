Title: Chocolate Pound Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 10 servings
8 oz Sour cream
4 ea Eggs
1 t  Vanilla
6 oz Nestle choc. chip
1 ea Duncan Hines Yellow mix
1 ea Small instant choc. pudding
3/4 c  Oil
Mix all together untill smooth-fold in bag of chocolate chips. Bake in
floured and greased tube pan, 300 degrees for 1 hour 15 minutes.
