Title: Plum Conserve
Categories: Penndutch, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
3 lb Sugar
1/2 lb Walnuts, chopped
3 lb Plum
1 lb Raisins
2 ea Orange
Wash oranges, remove seeds and grind. Pit the plums and cut in
quarters. Combine the oranges and plums and add the sugar and
raisins. Simmer about 1-1/2 hours. Add the walnuts and cook about 45
minutes longer. Pour into sterilized jars and seal.
