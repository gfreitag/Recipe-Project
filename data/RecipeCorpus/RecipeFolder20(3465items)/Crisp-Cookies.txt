Title: Crisp Cookies
Categories: Penndutch, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Milk
4 c  Flour
1 t  Cinnamon
1 c  Butter or other shortening
3 ea Egg, well beaten
1/2 t  Salt
2 t  Baking powder
1 t  Nutmeg
Cream the butter, add the sugar gradually, and cream mixture until
light. Add eggs and beat thoroughly. Sift 3 cups of the flour with
the salt, baking powder and spices and add alternately with the milk.
Add more flour to stiffen may require up to 5 cups flour). Chill for
several hours. Roll very thin and cut with cookie cutters. Put on
greased baking sheet and bake at 450-F about 7 minutes.
