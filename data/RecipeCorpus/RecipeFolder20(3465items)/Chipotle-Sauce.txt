Title: Chipotle Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetable, Tex/mexican, Chilies
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Carrot; Finely Chopped
1/4 c  Celery; Finely Chopped
1/4 c  Fresh Cilantro; Snipped
1/2 t  Salt
1/4 t  Pepper
2 ea Chipotle Chiles; *
2 ea Bacon; Slices, Finely Cut Up
1/4 c  Onion; Finely Chopped, 1 Sm
3 c  Tomatoes; Finely Chopped
1 c  Beef Broth
*  You can make this sauce as hot as you want by adding up to a total
of 4 dried Chipotle chiles.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- Cover chilies with warm water.  Let stand until
softened, about 1 hour. Drain and finely chop. Cook and stir bacon
and onion in a 2-quart saucepan until bacon is crisp; stir in chilies
and remaining ingredients. Makes about 4 cups sauce.
