Title: Irish Coffee
Categories: Beverage
Yield: 1 servings
1 x  Brown Sugar To Taste
1 x  Dessert Topping *
3/4 c  Warm Water
1 1/2 t  Instant Coffee Crystals
2 T  Irish Whiskey
*    Dessert Topping should be in a pressurized can.
~------------------------------------------------------
~----------------- In a nonmetal mug combine water and instant coffee
crystals. Micro-cook, uncovered, on 100% power about 1 1/2 minutes or
just till steaming hot. Stir in Irish whiskey and brown sugar.  Top
with pressurized dessert topping.
