Title: Fruit Pudding
Categories: Penndutch, Pudding
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Suet, chopped
1 c  Milk
1 t  Soda
1/4 c  Water, boiling
1/2 t  Nutmeg
1/4 t  Cloves
1 c  Raisins
1 c  Molasses
1 t  Salt
1 x  *dissolved in:
1/2 t  Cinnamon
1/2 t  Allspice
1 x  Flour
Combine and mix all the ingredients above, adding the dissolved soda
last. Pour into a well-greased mold, cover tightly and steam for 3
hours. Serve with Brown Sugar Pudding Sauce.
