Title: Buffalo Chicken Wings
Categories: Poultry, Snack, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Vinegar; optional
Vegetable oil
2 1/2 lb Chicken wings
1/4 c  Butter
3 tb Durkees hot sauce
Approx. Cook Time: :20
Fat grams    per serving:
Chill celery and Blue Cheese dressing if you are preparing them
with wings. Preheat oven broiler or BBQ. Trim off wing tips. Separate
wings into 2 sections. slowly melt butter in large saucepan. Add hot
sauce and vinegar and remove from heat.  Brush oil on both sides of
wings with vegetable oil and place on broiling rack. Broil 15- 20
minutes. Toss wings and sauce together.

less fattening than the traditional deep fried version

Source: _The Complete Book of Chicken Wings_by Joie Warner posted by
Anne MacLellan
