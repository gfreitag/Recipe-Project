Title: Cayenne Mayonnaise Dip
Categories: Appetizer
Yield: 4 servings
1 x  Cayenne Pepper; To Taste
1 1/2 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 c  Sour Cream
Blend all of the ingredients together until smooth.
Cover and chill. Makes about 2 cups of dip SUGGESTED DIPPERS: Seafood
Chunks, Pineapple, Cherry Tomatoes, Rye Or Cheese Crackers
