Title: Harvest Gold Relish
Categories: Relish
Yield: 2 servings
1/2    Jalapeno Pepper, Seeded &
Minced
1/4 c  Dilled Vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 c  Minced Fresh Dill
1 1/2 c  Whole Kernel Corn
2/3 c  Chopped Tomato
1/2 c  Chopped Gren Pepper
1/4 c  Finely Chopped Green
Onions
2 tb Brown Sugar
Combine First 7 Ingredients in A Heavy, Nonaluminum Saucepan; Bring
To A Boil.  Reduce Heat To Medium-Low & Simmer, Uncovered 20-25 Min.
OR Until Liquid Has Evaporated, Stirring Frequently.  Remove From
Heat; Stir in Salt.  Let Cool 5 Min.  Stir in Dill.  Spoon Into  A
Serving Bowl. Let Cool To Room Temperature.
