Title: Farmers Breakfast
Categories: German, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Salt
1 c  Ham; cooked, small cubes
2 ea Tomatoes; medium, peeled
1 T  Chives; chopped
4 ea Potatoes; medium
4 ea Bacon; strips, cubed
3 ea Eggs; large
3 T  Milk
Boil unpeeled potatoes 30 minutes.  Rinse under cold water, peel and
set aside to cool.  Slice potatoes.
In a large frypan cook bacon until transperent.  Add the potato
slices; cook until lightly borwned. Meanwhile blend eggs with milk
and salt. Stir in the cubed ham. Cut the tomatoes into thin wedges;
add to the egg mixture.  Pour the egg mixture over the potatoes in
the frypan. Cook until the eggs are set.
Sprinkle with chopped chives and serve at once.
