Title: Brussels Sprouts in Beer
Categories: German, Vegetable
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 t  Salt
2 T  Butter
1 lb Brussels sprouts; fresh
1 x  Beer; any brand, to cover
Trim and wash sprouts.  Place in a medium-size saucepan and pour
enough beer over them to cover.
Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes or until
tender. Add more beer if needed, as liquid evaporates. Drain; add
salt and butter. Serve hot.
