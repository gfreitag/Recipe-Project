Title: Cheese Cake
Categories: Penndutch, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 T  Flour
4 ea Egg
1 t  Vanilla
1 c  Sugar
1 lb Cream cheese
1 c  Half & half
1 x  *pastry
Line a deep pan with pastry. Mix flour and sugar together; add the
cream cheese and mix thoroughly. Beat eggs slightly, add the cream
and vanilla and combine with first mixture. Pour into pastry shell,
which has been baking at 400-F until starting to brown. Remove only
long enough to pour in filling. Reduce heat to 325-F and bake for 40
minutes.
