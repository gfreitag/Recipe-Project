Title: White Sauce
Categories: Penndutch, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 T  Flour
1/4 t  Salt
1 T  Butter
1 c  Milk
1/8 t  Pepper
Melt butter and add the flour and stir until blended. Slowly add the
milk and cook until mixture thickens, stirring constantly. Add
seasonings and cook 2 minutes longer.
