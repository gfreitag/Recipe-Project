Title: Overnight Layered Salad
Categories: Salad
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Shredded chedder cheese
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/2 t  Worcestershore sauce
1/4 t  Dry Mustard
2 T  Sliced green onions
3 c  Shredded lettuce
2 c  Shredded spinach
1/2 c  Sliced radishes
2 c  Cooked cubed chicken
1/2 c  Celery cut diagonally
In salad bowl, layer ingredients in following order; half lettuce,
half spinach, radishes, chicken, celery, remaining lettuce remaining
spinach and cheese.
Combine mayonnaise, worcestershire sauce and dry mustard. Spread
evenly over top of salad.  Cover and chill several hours or
overnight. Garnish with sliced green onions. Toss just before serving.
