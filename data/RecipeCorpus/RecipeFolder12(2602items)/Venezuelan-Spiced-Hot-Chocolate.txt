Title: Venezuelan Spiced Hot Chocolate
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 ga Milk
3    Whole star anise
2    Sticks cinnamon
Zest of one orange
5    Whole allspice berries
2 tb Brown sugar
8 oz Semisweet chocolate,
Coarsely chopped
1 c  Aged dark rum
Combine milk with spices and sugar in large heavy saucepan.  Cook over
medium heat, stirring to dissolve sugar, until milk just starts to
bubble around the edges (scalding).  Lower heat and cook 10 minutes.
Remove from heat and allow to steep 10 additional minutes, then
strain into another large heavy pot.  Heat gently, then add chocolate
and rum. Whisk briskly 5 minutes over low flame until chocolate is
completely melted.

Yield: 8 cups
