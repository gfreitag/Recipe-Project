Title: Teh Tarik
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 qt Boiling water
3 ts Tea powder
3 ts Condensed milk
4 ts Sugar
Pour the boiling water into large cup with tea powder. Stir well. Add
in milk and sugar. Stir well. Pour the tea into a big cup by pulling
one to another cup. Do the same method until the tea looks foamy and
creamy.

Yield: 1 serving
