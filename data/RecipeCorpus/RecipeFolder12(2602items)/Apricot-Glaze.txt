Title: Apricot Glaze
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Apricot jam
1 ts Water (approximately)
Place the apricot jam and water in a small saucepot and warm over
medium high heat until it dissolves. Just before boiling, remove from
heat. Dilute with a little more water if necessary until it is thin
enough to brush evenly. Apply immediately with a soft, flat pastry
brush.
