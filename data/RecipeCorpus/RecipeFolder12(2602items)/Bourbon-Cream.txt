Title: Bourbon Cream
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Heavy cream
1/4 c  Confectioner's sugar
1 tb Bourbon
In a large bowl whip the cream and sugar with an electric beater until
thick. Beat in the bourbon and continue to beat until soft peaks form.
Serve immediately.

Yield: 3 cups
