Title: Grilled Asparagus Wrapped In Prosciutto With Shaved Pecor
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 tb Capers
1 tb Finely chopped parsley
Freshly ground black pepper
12 md Asparagus spears, tough end
Removed
2 tb Extra virgin olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
12    Thin slices prosciutto
12    Thinly shaved pieces of
Pecorino Romano cheese
Whisk together the lemon juice and olive oil in a small bowl. Add the
capers and parsley and season with black pepper. Preheat grill. Grill
the asparagus, braised with olive oil. Toss the asparagus with the
olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste. Wrap a piece of
prosciutto around each spear and place on a platter. Drizzle with
lemoncaper vinaigrette and top with the cheese.
