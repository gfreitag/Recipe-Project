Title: Kailan In Oyster Sauce
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
14 oz Kailan (washed & cleaned)-
cn Substitute kale
2 tb Cooking oil
1 tb Garlic
6 tb Oyster sauce
Blanch the Kailan vegetable and keep warm. Heat up wok and add
cooking oil. Fry the garlic until light brown and stir in oyster
sauce. Pour the sauce on top of the vegetable. For decoration,
sprinkle with fried onions.

Yield: 4 servings
