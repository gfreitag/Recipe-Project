Title: Lumache Alla Marchigiana
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
5 lb Snails, purged overnight
With 1/2 pound cornmeal or 2
Cans
Escargot
6 tb Virgin olive oil
2 md Onions, thinly sliced
6    Cloves garlic, thinly
Sliced
2 tb Chopped fresh thyme leaves
1 c  Dry white wine
1 c  Black olives, with pits
1 c  Basic tomato sauce, recipe
Follows
If live, steam snails 1 hour, or until they leave their shells.
Remove and rinse.

In a 12 inch to 14 inch saute pan, heat olive oil until smoking. Add
onions and garlic and cook over medium heat until soft and lightly
brown. Add thyme leaves, snails, wine, olives and tomato and bring to
a boil. Lower heat and simmer 30 minutes. Season with salt and pepper
and serve.

Yield: 4 servings
