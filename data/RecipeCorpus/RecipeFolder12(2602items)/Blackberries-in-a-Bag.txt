Title: Blackberries in a Bag
Categories:
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------ESSENCE OF EMERIL #EE2192----------------------
2 qt Blackberries, about 8 cups
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Cassis
6    Sweet shortcakes, for
Serving
Place in a large bowl and mix to dissolve the sugar. Place 6 cups of
this mixture into a large baggie. Place the remaining mixture in the
food processor, and pulse until it is a smooth puree. Pour the puree
over the berries and seal the bag. Serve with shortcakes and sour
cream.
