Title: Wild Hickory Nut Waffles With Apple-Wild Blueberry Compot
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/4 tb Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg
1    Egg yolk
1 c  Buttermilk
3/4 c  Toasted, chopped wild
Hickory nuts
Sift together the dry ingredients. Lightly beat the egg, egg yolk and
buttermilk in a bowl. Stir into the dry ingredients. Stir in the
hickory nuts.
Spoon into a hot waffle iron and cook per manufacturerss instructions.

Serve with Apple-Wild Blueberry Compote

(courtesy Larry Forgione of An American Place/Beekman 1776 Tavern)
