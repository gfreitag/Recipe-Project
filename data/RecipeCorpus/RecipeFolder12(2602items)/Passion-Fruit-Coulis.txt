Title: Passion Fruit Coulis
Categories: Taste
Yield: 4 servings
5    Whole passion fruit, halved,
Pulp and juice scooped out
Or 1 cup
Unsweetened passion fruit
Juice
1 c  Sugar
In a small sauce pan add both ingredients. Bring to a boil, lower
heat to medium, cook until thickened, 5-10 minutes. Remove from heat.

If using pulp, strain through a fine sieve. Serve with plain cake.
