Title: Banana Coconut Cream With Chocolate Wafers
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 cn (14 ounces) coconut milk
2 oz Milk - to equal 1 pint
Total
2 oz Cornstarch
1    Egg
4    Egg yolks
4 oz Sugar
1 1/3 ts Vanilla
1 oz Butter
1 1/2 c  Heavy cream - whipped
2 1/2 ea Banana - peeled, cut in half
Lengthwise and chopped
1/2    Lemon - juiced
Dissolve the cornstarch in the milk. Add the egg and yolks and
incorporate. In a saucepan combine the milk and sugar and bring to a
boil. Temper into the yolk mixture. Return to a boil and whisk for 1
minute. Stir in vanilla and butter. Cool. When chilled whisk to
lighten and fold in whipped cream. Cut bananas and toss in lemon
juice. Stir in bananas.
