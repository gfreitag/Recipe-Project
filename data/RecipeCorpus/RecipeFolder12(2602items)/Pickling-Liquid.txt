Title: Pickling Liquid
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Distilled white vinegar
1 c  Water
2 1/2 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Salt
1 1/2 ts Whole allspice
1    (3-inch) cinnamon stick
1 1/2 ts Celery seeds
1 1/2 ts Mustard seeds
1 1/2 ts Whole black pepper corns
Place all of the ingredients in a nonreactive saucepan, over high
heat. Bring the mixture to a boil, reduce heat to medium and simmer
for 30 minutes. Remove from the heat and cool completely. Strain the
liquid and store in an airtight container. Yield: about 1 quart
