Title: Banana Colada
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
3    Ripe bananas, peeled and
Quartered
18 oz Unsweetened pineapple juice
4 oz Coconut milk
3 oz Dark rum
2 c  Ice
1/4 c  Toasted coconut
Place bananas and pineapple juice in a blender and blend until
smooth. Add coconut milk, rum and ice and mix until frothy. Garnish
with toasted coconut.
