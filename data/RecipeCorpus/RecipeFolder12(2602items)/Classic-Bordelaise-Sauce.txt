Title: Classic Bordelaise Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Olive oil
1/2    Stick (4 tablespoons)
Butter
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1 tb Chopped garlic
1 tb Chopped parsley
1/4 c  Dry red wine
In a saucepan over medium heat, warm the olive oil and butter. Add
the salt and pepper and simmer for about 4 minutes. Add the garlic
and parsley and cook for 30 seconds. Drizzle in the wine and simmer
for 3 minutes. Remove from heat and serve immediately.

Yield: 3/4 cup
