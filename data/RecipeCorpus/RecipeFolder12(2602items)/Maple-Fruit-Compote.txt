Title: Maple Fruit Compote
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
2 1/2 c  Pitted and sliced assorted
Stone fruit, such as peaches
Plums,
Apricots and/or cherries
3/4 c  Maple syrup
Combine the fruit and the maple syrup in a bowl and serve at room
temperature, or cover and refrigerate until an hour before serving.

Yield: About 3 cups
