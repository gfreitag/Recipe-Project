Title: Chia Lime Water
Categories: Hot
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Freshly squeezed lime juice
2 qt Water
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Chia seeds
Several sprigs of fresh sage
For garnish
Squeeze the limes with a Mexican lime squeezer. Stir together the
water, lime juice, and sugar until the sugar is dissolved. Stir in
the chia seeds and garnish with the sage sprigs.

Serve in tall glasses over ice.

Yield: 9 cups
