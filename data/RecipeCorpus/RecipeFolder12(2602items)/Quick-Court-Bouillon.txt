Title: Quick Court Bouillon
Categories:
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------TOO HOT TAMALES #TH6321-----------------------
1 qt Cold water
1    Onion, chopped coarsely
1    Bay leaf
10    Peppercorns
1 tb Salt
1/4 c  Vinegar;or 3x as much white
Wine
Bring all ingredients to a boil in a medium sauce pan, lower to a
simmer, and cook 1520 minutes. Strain hot courtbouillon over squid.
