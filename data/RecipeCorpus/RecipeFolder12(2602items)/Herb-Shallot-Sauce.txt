Title: Herb Shallot Sauce
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Unsalted butter
1/2 c  Shallots, minced
2 c  Beef stock
1    Sprig fresh thyme or 1
ts Dried
2 tb Snipped chives or parsley
Melt butter in a sauce pan over medium heat. Add shallots and cook
until softened. Add stock and thyme. and simmer 15 minutes. In a bowl
combine 1 tablespoon arrowroot with 2 tablespoons cold water. Add
arrowroot mixture to stock in a slow steady stream, and simmer until
thickened. Strain sauce and stir in chives or parsley.

Yield: 6 servings
