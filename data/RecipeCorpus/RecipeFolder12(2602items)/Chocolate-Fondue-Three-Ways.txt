Title: Chocolate Fondue Three Ways
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb High quality chocolate,
Finely chopped
2 1/4 c  Heavy cream
Splash of Creme de Mint
Splash of Nocello
Splash of Grand Marnier
Garnishes:
lb Cake, cut into bite-size
Pieces
Shortbread cookies
Biscotti
Coconut macaroons
lg Strawberries with stems
Bananas
Place 1 pound of chocolate in its own fondue pot. Turn the pot
setting to medium or 5. In a saucepan, heat the cream. When the cream
comes to a simmer, remove from the heat and pour 1/3 over each pot of
chocolate. Whisk each pot until smooth. Splash a different liquor
into each pot and whisk until smooth. Serve the fondues with the
various garnishes.

Yield: 12 servings
