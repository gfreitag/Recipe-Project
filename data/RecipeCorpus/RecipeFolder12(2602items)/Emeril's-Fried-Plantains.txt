Title: Emeril's Fried Plantains
Categories: Vegetables, Fruits/nuts, Side dish
Yield: 6 Servings
4 ea Ripe plantains
Oil for frying
Salt
Freshly ground pepper
Preheat the fryer. Peel the plantains and slice thinly on the bias.
Fry in batches until golden brown, about 2 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels. Season
with salt and pepper.
