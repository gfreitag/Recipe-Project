Title: Roasted Corn Guacamole
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
3    Avocado, peeled, seeded and
Coarsely chopped
2    Ears corn, roasted and
Kernels removed
1/2    Red onion, finely chopped
1/4 c  Coarsely chopped cilantro
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
2 tb Creme fraiche
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
Blue corn tortilla chips
Combine the avocados, corn, onion, cilantro, lime juice, and creme
fraiche in a bowl and mix well. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Serve with tortilla chips.

Yield: 4 servings
