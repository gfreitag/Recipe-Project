Title: Honeyed Carrots
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Carrots (12 to 16 medium),
Scraped and sliced into
1/2    -inch rounds
1/4 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Honey
4 tb Margarine or butter
Salt to taste
Combine all of the ingredients in a heavy saucepan and add 2
tablespoons of water. Cover and cook over very low heat for about 25
minutes, until the carrots are tender and the liquid is syrupy.

(Courtesy Joan Nathan)
