Title: Chicken Smothered In Rhubarb
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
Essence
1 tb Flour
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 lb Rhubarb, cut into 1 1/2-inch
Pieces
2 md Onions, julienned
1 tb Minced garlic
1    Bay leaf
Fresh thyme sprigs
1 c  White wine
3 tb Finely chopped parsley
Garnish: 2 cups cooked white
Rice, warm, 1 tablespoon
Finely chopped
Parsley
In a mixing bowl, toss the chickens with Essence and flour. In a large
heavy pot, heat the olive oil. When the oil is hot, brown the chicken
for 6 to 8 minutes on each side. Add the rhubarb and onions. Season
with salt and pepper. Stirring constantly, wilt and brown the onions,
scraping the bottom of the pot to loosen any brown particles, for
about 10 minutes. Add the garlic, herbs and wine, cover and reduce
the heat. Stir occasionally and cook for about 45 minutes or until
the chicken is tender. Stir in the parsley. Arrange the chicken on a
platter and garnish with Essence and parsley and serve with rice.

Yield: 6 servings
