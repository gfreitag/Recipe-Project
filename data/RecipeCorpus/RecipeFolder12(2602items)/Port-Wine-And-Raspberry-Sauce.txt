Title: Port Wine And Raspberry Sauce
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Fresh raspberries
1 1/2 c  Nonfat yogurt
1/4 c  Port wine
2 tb Sugar (optional)
In a food processor, blend all the ingredients until very smooth.
Strain, if necessary.

Yield: 2 1/2 cups
