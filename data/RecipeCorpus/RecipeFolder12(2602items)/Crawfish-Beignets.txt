Title: Crawfish Beignets
Categories: Cajun, Breads
Yield: 4 Servings
2 lb Crawfish tails, whole and
Chopped
2 c  Bacon Mirepoix
2 c  Pepper cheese, shredded
2 c  Cheddar/monterey jack cheese
Shredded
Buttermilk
6    Eggs
1 ts Seasoned salt
Mix first 4 ingredients together.  Scoop and form triangles.  Flour,
dip in batter, flour again and fry until golden.  Batter is 2 cups
buttermilk, 6 eggs and 1 teaspoon seasoned salt.
