Title: Glazed Grapefruit With Ginger
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
3    Grapefruit
6 ts Honey
3 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Crystallized ginger, finely
Chopped
1/3 c  Grated coconut (optional)
Preheat the broiler.

Cut each grapefruit in half and gently separate the sections from the
membrane by cutting around each section with a paring knife, keeping
the grapefruit intact. Place the grapefruit halves cut side up on a
baking sheet. Drizzle 1 teaspoon of honey over each half and sprinkle
evenly with the brown sugar.

Place grapefruit under broiler and brown well, about 3 minutes. Remove
grapefruit and sprinkle with the crystallized ginger and coconut if
desired. Serve warm.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings
