Title: Buffalo Chicken Wings 1
Categories: Taste
Yield: 4 servings
12    Chicken wings, halved
Vegetable oil
Salt and pepper
4 tb Butter
1    Bottle Frank's Louisiana Hot
Sauce
1 tb White vinegar
Blue cheese dressing:
1 c  Mayonnaise
3 tb Minced onion
1 1/2 ts Minced garlic
1/3 c  Minced parsley
1/2 c  Sour cream
1 tb Lemon juice
1 tb White vinegar
1/3 c  Crumbled blue cheese
Salt and pepper
Preheat oven to 350 degrees and preheat oil in deep fryer to 375
degrees. Reserve wing-tips for stock. Fry wings for 8 minutes.
Transfer to a tray with paper towels and let drain. Season with salt
and pepper.

In a saucepan melt butter. Add hot sauce, salt and pepper. Toss wings
in sauce. Roast 15 to 20 minutes.

In a bowl mix together mayonnaise, onion, garlic, parsley, sour cream,
lemon juice, vinegar and blue cheese. Transfer to serving bowl.
Arrange wings on serving platter. Garnish with flat leaf parsley.
Serve with dressing.

Yield: 4 servings
