Title: Curtis' Apple Butter
Categories: Try it, Gift, Condiment
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------PICK OF THE DAY #PD7711-----------------------
18    Apples, Golden delicious,
Peeled, cored and quartered
1 1/2 c  Apple cider
2    Cinnamon sticks
1 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/4 ts Cloves
1/4    Mace
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Lemon juice
Place all in pot. Cook for 2 1/2 hours, mashing apples occasionally,
until color turns deep brown.
