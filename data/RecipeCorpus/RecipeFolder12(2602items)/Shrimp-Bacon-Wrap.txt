Title: Shrimp Bacon Wrap
Categories: Shrimp
Yield: 4 Servings
24 lg Shrimp, peeled and deveined
12 sl Bacon
24    Wooden toothpicks
Fish fry
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Milk
Cooking oil for frying
Cut bacon slices in halves.  Wrap 1/2 slice of bacon around each
shrimp and secure with toothpick.  Dip in seasoned egg and milk
mixture then dip into fish fry.  Drop in hot oil until bacon is
crispy.
