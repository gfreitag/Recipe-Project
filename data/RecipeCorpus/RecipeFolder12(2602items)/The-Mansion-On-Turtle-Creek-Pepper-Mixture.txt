Title: The Mansion On Turtle Creek Pepper Mixture
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Ground black pepper
1/3 c  Ground white pepper
1 1/2 tb Ground cayenne pepper
Combine all ingredients. Cover tightly and store in a cool place. Use
to season red meats and game before cooking.
