Title: Moroccan Rub
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Paprika
1 ts Salt
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Coarsely ground black
Pepper
1/2 ts Ground ginger
1/2 ts Ground cardamom
1/2 ts Ground cumin
1/2 ts Ground fenugreek
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 ts Ground allspice
1/4 ts Cayenne
In small bowl, stir together all of the ingredients. Use immediately
or store in a tightly covered contianer for up to 1 month.

Rub onto the meat or poultry and let stand 15 minutes or refrigerate
up to 4 hours before grilling.

(recipe from "Marinades" by Melanie Barnard)
