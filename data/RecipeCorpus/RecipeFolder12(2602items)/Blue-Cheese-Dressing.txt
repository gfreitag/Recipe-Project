Title: Blue Cheese Dressing
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
2/3 c  Mayonnaise
1/3 c  Sour cream
1/3 c  Crumbled blue cheese
In a bowl combine well the mayonnaise, the sour cream and the blue
cheese.

Yield: Approximately 1 1/3 cups
