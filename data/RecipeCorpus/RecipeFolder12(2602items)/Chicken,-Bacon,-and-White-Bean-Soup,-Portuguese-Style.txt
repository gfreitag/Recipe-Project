Title: Chicken, Bacon, and White Bean Soup, Portuguese Style
Categories: Emeril, Chicken
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Chopped bacon
2 c  Chopped onions
1/2 c  Chopped celery
2 tb Minced shallots
1 tb Minced garlic
1 lb White beans, soaked
Overnight
2 qt Chicken stock
1    Bouquet garni
12 oz Small diced cooked chicken
1/4 ts Cayenne
Salt and black pepper
Romano cheese
sm Crusty bread loaf
In a stock pot, render the bacon until crispy, remove the bacon.
Saute the onions, celery, shallots, and garlic in the bacon fat for 2
to 3 minutes. Add the stock, beans and bouquet.  Bring up to a boil
and reduce to a simmer.  Allow to cook for about 2 hours or until the
beans are tender. Add the diced chicken meat.  Season with cayenne,
salt and black pepper. Garnish with Romano cheese and serve with
crusty bread.

Yield: 8-10 servings
