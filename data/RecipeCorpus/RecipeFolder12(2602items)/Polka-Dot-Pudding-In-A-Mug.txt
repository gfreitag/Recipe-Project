Title: Polka Dot Pudding In A Mug
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Cornstarch
2 tb Cocoa powder
1 1/2 tb Sugar
3/4 c  Milk
1/2 ts Vanilla extract
2 tb White chocolate chips
In a large mug (at least 12 ounces), dissolve the cornstarch, cocoa,
and sugar in the milk. Microwave on HIGH for 90 seconds, or until
mixture boils. Stir in the vanilla extract and chocolate chips. Chill
in the refrigerator at least 1 hour.

Yield: 1 serving
