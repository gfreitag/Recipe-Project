Title: Fresh Chili Soy Dip
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Fresh hot chili peppers, cut
Into small rings (keep
Seeds
Intact)
6 tb Dark soy sauce
2 tb Chinese red vinegar
2 tb Peanut oil
Put peppers, soy sauce, and vinegar in a serving bowl. Heat oil and
pour over mixture.

Yield: 6 ounces
