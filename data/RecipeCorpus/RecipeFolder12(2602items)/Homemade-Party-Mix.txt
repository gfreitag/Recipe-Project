Title: Homemade Party Mix
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Cashews
1/2 lb Hot roasted peanuts
2 c  Pretzel nuggets
8 c  Waffle cereal (such as
Chex)
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Worcestershire sauce
2 tb Creole spice
Preheat oven to 325 degrees F. In a large bowl combine nuts, pretzels
and cereal. Melt butter with Worcestershire sauce and drizzle over
nut mixture; sprinkle with Creole spice. Toss to coat completely,
pour onto a cookie sheet and bake until crisp and tasty, about 25
minutes.

Yield: approximately 12 cups
