Title: White Rice With Fried Plantains
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
3    Ripe plantains
4 tb Unsalted butter
6 c  Hot cooked white rice
Peel the plantains and cut into 1/4-inch slices on the diagonal. Melt
the butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Saute the plantains
until golden brown and soft, about 2 minutes per side. Heap the rice
on a platter and top with the browned plantains. Serve hot.

Yield: 6 servings for breakfast or as an accompaniment.
