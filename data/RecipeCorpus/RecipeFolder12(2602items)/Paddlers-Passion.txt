Title: Paddlers Passion
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
Mix in blender with shaved
Ice-
1 oz Vodka
1 oz Bacardi Amber Rum
1 1/2 oz Passion fruit juice
1 1/2 oz Orange juice
ds Coconut syrup
ds Grenadine
Blend and pour into a scooped-out whole pineapple, replace top and
serve with a straw. Garnish with a wedge of pineapple and cherry.
