Title: Ginger Crusted Onaga With Miso Sesame Vinaigrette
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2    Pieces Onaga ( 6-7 ounces)
1/4 c  Finely minced ginger
1/4 c  Green onion, minced by hand
1/4 c  Peanut oil
Few drops sesame oil
Salt to taste
Miso dressing:
2 tb Rice vinegar
2 tb Chicken stock
1 1/2 tb Miso
1 1/2 tb Sugar
Sesame Dressing:
1    Egg yolk
1 tb Chunky peanut butter
1/2 ts Ginger, fresh, peeled and
Minced
1 ts Dijon mustard
2 tb Rice vinegar
4 oz Vegetable oil
1 tb Sesame oil
1 ts White sesame seeds, toasted
Place green onions and ginger in non-aluminum bowl with high sides.
Season with salt to taste. Put peanut oil in heavy bottom pan and
heat until almost smoking. Carefully pour hot oil over green
onion/ginger mixture, oil will boil over the sides. Season with
sesame oil. Let cool.

Sear Onaga until brown. Apply ginger/onion mixture to surface. Bake
at 350 degrees for approximately 6 minutes or until cooked through.

Miso dressing: Mix all ingredients in bowl. Set aside.

Sesame dressing: Place egg yolk, peanut butter, ginger, Dijon and rice
vinegar in mixing bowl. Mix well. Slowly add vegetable oil in a steady
stream while mixing. When the oil is incorporated, add the sesame oil
and sesame seeds.

Yield: 2 servings
