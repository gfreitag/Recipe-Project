Title: Spicy Mango Ketchup
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1    Red onion, diced fine
1 tb Chopped garlic
2 tb Unsalted butter
1    Mango, diced
1/2 c  Rice wine vinegar
1/4 c  Ketchup
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1    Chipotle, pureed
1/2 tb Ground ginger
1/2 tb Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground cloves
In a saucepan, sweat the onions and garlic. Add the mangoes and
continue to cook for 3 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and bring
to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes. Puree and season to
taste with salt and pepper.
