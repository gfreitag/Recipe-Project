Title: Maytag Blue Sabayon Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
3 lg Egg yolks
2 ts Minced shallots
1/3 c  Maytag blue cheese,
Crumbled
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Freshly ground white pepper
1 ts Snipped fresh chives
Combine all of the ingredients in a small stainless-steel bowl. Set
the bowl over a pot of simmering water and whisk until the mixture
begins to thicken, about 2 minutes. Remove from heat.

Yield: about 3/4 cup
