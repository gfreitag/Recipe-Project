Title: Frozen Cappuccinos
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Freshly brewed espresso,
Cooled to room temperature
4 c  Ice cubes
1/2 c  Half-and-half or whole milk
1/4 c  Plus 2 tablespoons superfine
Sugar, or to taste
2 tb Cocoa powder
1/2 c  Kahlua or other
Coffee-flavored liqueur if
Desired
Garnish:
Cinnamon
In a blender blend all ingredients until smooth but still thick.
Divide mixture between 4 tall glasses and sprinkle with cinnamon.

Yield: 4 servings
