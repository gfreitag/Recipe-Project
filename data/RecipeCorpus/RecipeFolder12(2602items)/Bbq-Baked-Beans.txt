Title: Bbq Baked Beans
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Navy beans, soaked overnight
6 c  Water
6 oz Bacon, chopped
1    Onion, diced
1    Green pepper, diced
1    Red pepper, diced
1 c  Barbecue sauce
1 c  Cider vinegar
2 tb Molasses
2 tb Mustard
1 c  Any leftover smoked meat
In a saucepan cook beans with water for 2 hours.

In a saute pan, cook bacon over moderate heat.  Add onions and
peppers and cook until tender.  Add remaining ingredients, including
beans and bake at 250 degrees for 45 minutes.  Serve hot.
