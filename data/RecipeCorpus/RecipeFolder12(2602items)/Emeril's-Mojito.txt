Title: Emeril's Mojito
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 Drink
Juice from one fresh lime
1 ts Simple syrup
4    Sprigs of fresh spearmint
2 oz White rum
2 ds Angostura Bitters
Ice cubes
Club soda
In a tall chilled glass, combine the lime juice, simple syrup and
half of the mint leaves. Using the handle of a wooden spoon, mortar
the ingredients together. Add the rum, bitters and ice cubes. Top
with soda and garnish with the remaining mint leaves.
