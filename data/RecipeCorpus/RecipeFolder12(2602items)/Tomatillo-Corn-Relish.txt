Title: Tomatillo-Corn Relish
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
8 md Tomatillos, husked and
Coarsely chopped
1 tb Finely diced red onion
1 sm Jalapeno, finely minced
2 tb Fresh lime juice
2 tb Olive oil
3/4 c  Fresh corn kernels
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl.
