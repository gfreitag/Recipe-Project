Title: Creamy Cheddar Cheese Soup
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Apple smoked bacon, diced
2 tb Unsalted butter
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1/4 c  Celery, chopped
1/4 c  Flour
12 oz Beer, pale ale
1 qt Chicken stock
8 oz Maytag White Cheddar cheese,
Grated
Salt and pepper
Garnish:
Chopped parsley
1/2 c  Small diced toasted
Croutons
Brown the bacon in the soup pot. Remove excess grease. Add the butter,
onions, and celery to the pot with the bacon and cook for 5 minutes
over medium heat. Dust the flour over the vegetables and bacon, cook,
stirring for 3 minutes. Stir in the beer, and then the stock.

Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Add the
cheese and season with salt and pepper. Ladle into bowls and serve
with chopped parsley and croutons.

Yield: 4 servings
