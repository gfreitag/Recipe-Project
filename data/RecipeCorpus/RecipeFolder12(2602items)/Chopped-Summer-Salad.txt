Title: Chopped Summer Salad
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1    Head romaine lettuce, washed
And dried
5    Plum tomatoes
3 tb Lemon juice
1/4 c  Olive oil
1    Cucumber, peeled, halved,
Seeded and sliced
2 ts Capers
1 sm Red onion, quartered and
Sliced very thin
1 tb Chopped dill
Salt and pepper
Slice romaine leaves into 1/8-inch strips. Cut tomatoes in half
lengthwise, and each half into 6 pieces. Stir together lemon juice
and olive oil. Mix all other ingredients in a large bowl and add
vinaigrette to taste. Season with salt and pepper.
