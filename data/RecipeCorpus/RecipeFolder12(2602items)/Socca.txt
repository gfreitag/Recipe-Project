Title: Socca
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Lukewarm water
2 ts Salt
5 1/4 c  Chickpea flour
1/4 c  Extra virgin olive oil plus
2    Tablespoons
Preheat oven to 425 degrees F and preheat broiler.

Place water and salt in a large wooden bowl. Whisking constantly, add
checkpea flour until all is well blended. Stir in olive oil and let
stand 30 minutes. Smear a 14inch cast iron skillet with 2 tablespoons
oil and pour in batter. Place under broiler for 2 to 4 minutes, until
lightly blistering. Remove and place in oven and bake 2 to 4 minutes
until firm. Remove, cut into wedges.

Yield: 4 servings
