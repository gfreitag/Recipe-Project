Title: Apple-Wild Blueberry Compote
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Apple spoon fruit
1 c  Wild Blueberry spoon fruit
1/2 c  Dried wild blueberries
1/2 c  Roughly chopped wild hickory
Nuts, lightly toasted
2 ts Fresh lime juice
Stir all ingredients together. Serve at room temperature.

Yield: 6-8 waffles
