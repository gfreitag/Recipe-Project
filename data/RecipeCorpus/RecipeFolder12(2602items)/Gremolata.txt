Title: Gremolata
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Hazelnuts, coarsely chopped
2    Cloves garlic, finely
Chopped
1/4 c  Finely chopped parsley
2 tb Finely chopped lemon zest
Combine all ingredients in a medium bowl and season with salt and
pepper to taste.
