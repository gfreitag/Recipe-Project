Title: Classic Salsa
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
8    Italian tomatoes, seeded and
Diced
1 sm Red onion, diced
1 tb Minced jalapeno pepper
2 tb Chopped fresh cilantro
1    Lime-halved
Salt and pepper
In a bowl combine all the ingredients and taste. Adjust seasonings.
Serve with your favorite corn chips.

Yield: 2 cups salsa
