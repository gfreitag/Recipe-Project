Title: Berries Romanoff
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Grand Marnier
1 c  Sour cream
1/2 pt Vanilla bean ice cream
2 c  Sweetened whipped cream
1 pt Raspberries
1 pt Blueberries
1 pt Blackberries
1 pt Loganberries
1 pt Strawberries, halved
2 oz Chocolate curls
2 tb Chiffonade of mint
Powdered sugar in shaker
In a mixing bowl, whisk the Grand Marnier and sour cream together.
Stir in the ice cream. Fold in the whipped cream. Arrange the berries
on a platter. Pour the cream mixture over the top of the berries.
Garnish with the chocolate curls, mint and powdered sugar.

Yield: 6 servings
