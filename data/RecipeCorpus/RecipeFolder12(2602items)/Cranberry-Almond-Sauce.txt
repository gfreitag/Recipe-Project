Title: Cranberry-Almond Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Honey
1/2 c  Water
2 c  Fresh cranberries
1/4 c  Orange marmalade
1/4 c  Toasted sliced almonds
Nutmeg
In a sauce pot combine the sugar, honey, and water.  Bring to a boil,
stirring to incorporate the sugar and honey.  Boil for 6 minutes. Add
the cranberries, return to a boil, reduce the heat and simmer for 3
to 4 minutes, until the cranberries pop, stirring occasionally.
Remove from the heat Stir in the marmalade and almonds. Season with
nutmeg.

Yield: 8 servings
