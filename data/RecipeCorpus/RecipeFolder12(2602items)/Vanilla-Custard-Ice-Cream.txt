Title: Vanilla Custard Ice Cream
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1    Vanilla bean, finely
Chopped
3/4 c  Sugar
4    Egg yolks
1 c  Milk
1 1/2 c  Chilled heavy cream
In a food processor or blender grind vanilla bean with sugar until
mixture is combined well. In a metal bowl with an electric mixer beat
egg yolks, milk and vanilla sugar. Set bowl over a pan of simmering
water and beat for 8 to 10 minutes, or until thick and doubled in
volume. Remove bowl from heat and set it in a bowl of ice water. Beat
until cold. Add cream and beat until thickened and almost double in
volume. Transfer to an ice cream freezer and freeze according to
directions.

Yield: makes about 1 quart
