Title: Cream Filling
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1/2 c  Vegetable shortening
1/2 c  Evaporated milk
1/3 c  All purpose flour
ds Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Cream sugar and shortening until light and fluffy. In separate bowl,
mix milk and flour. Beat milk mix into sugar mix and beat until well
blended. Add salt and vanilla.
