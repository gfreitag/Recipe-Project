Title: Seared Bay Scallops With Fresh Sweet Corn And Truffles
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Olive oil
1/4 lb Bay scallops
Essence
1 tb Butter
2    Ears of sweet corn with
Kernels removed (about 1 cup
Kernels)
1/2 c  Heavy cream
Salt and white pepper
1    Black truffle
Drizzle of truffle oil
3    Long chives
1 tb Chopped chives
In a saute pan, heat the butter. Saut√© the corn for 2-3 minutes.
Season with salt and pepper. Add the cream and bring up to a boil.
Reduce to a simmer and cook for 2-3

minutes or until the cream thickens. Season with salt and pepper. In a
saute pan, heat the olive oil. Season the scallops with Essence. Sear
the scallops for 2-3 minutes or until the scallops have a nice sear
on each side. Spoon the sauce into a shallow bowl. Arrange the
scallops over the corn. Shave the truffles over the scallops and
drizzle with the truffle oil. Garnish with long chives and chopped
chives.

Yield: 2 servings
