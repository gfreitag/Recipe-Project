Title: Shrimp Verde
Categories: 2hot
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Cooked, cleaned shrimp,
Chopped in 1/4inch dice
1/4 c  Lime juice
1/4 lb Tomatillos, husks removed
And diced
1 bn Green onions, sliced
4    Jalapenos, seeded and
Chopped
1    Clove garlic, peeled
1 bn Cilantro leaves, chopped
1 sm Avocado, peeled and pit
Removed
1 ts Sea salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
Marinate the shrimp in the lime juice for one hour. Drain and reserve
the juice. In a large bowl combine the shrimp, tomatillos, green
onions, jalapenos, garlic, cilantro, avocados, salt and pepper and
toss to coat.

Serve immediately.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings
