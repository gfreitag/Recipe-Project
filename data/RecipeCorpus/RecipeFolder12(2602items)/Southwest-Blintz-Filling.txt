Title: Southwest Blintz Filling
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1 ts Ground chili or cumin
12 oz Cooked ground meat or
Chicken
Minced fresh seeded jalapeno
Peppers
Salt
1 tb Sour cream
Cook onions in vegetable oil.  When tender, add chili or cumin,
cooked meat and jalapeno peppers to taste; season with salt and add
sour cream.

Yield: enough for about 12 blintzes
