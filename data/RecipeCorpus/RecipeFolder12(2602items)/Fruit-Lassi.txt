Title: Fruit Lassi
Categories: Try it
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------COOKING MONDAY TO FRIDAY #MF---------------------
1    Ripe mango, banana, or
Papaya peeled and pureed
2 ts Honey; to taste
1/4 ts Saffron threads;steeped in
2 ts Hot water
1 c  Ice water
2 tb Juice, lemon; to taste
1 c  Yogurt
In a blender, puree the mango or other fruit with other ingredients;
chill for 2 hours before serving and stir before serving.

Yield: about 2 to 3 cups
