Title: Piri Piri Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Olive oil
4    Fresh jalapeno peppers,
Finely chopped, stems and
Seeds
2    Fresh poblano peppers,
Finely chopped, stems and
Seeds
1 tb Crushed red pepper
1 ts Salt
1 ts Ground black pepper
1 tb Minced garlic
1 tb Finely chopped fresh
Cilantro
1 tb Kosher salt
In a saucepan, combine the olive oil, peppers, crushed red pepper,
salt, and black pepper. Bring the liquid up to a boil and reduce the
heat. Simmer the sauce for 4 minutes and remove from the heat. Stir
in the garlic. With a hand held mixer, puree the sauce until smooth.
At this point, allow the sauce to sit for 1 week under refrigeration
before serving. Stir in the cilantro and kosher salt.
