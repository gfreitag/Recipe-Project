Title: Remonce
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb (1/2 stick) butter, softened
1/2 c  Sugar
1/4    To 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
(optional)
Cream the butter, sugar (and cinnamon, if added). Keep refrigerated.
Let soften at room temperature to spreadable consistency before using.

Yield: about 1/2 cup
