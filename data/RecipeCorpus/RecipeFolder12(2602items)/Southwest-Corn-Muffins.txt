Title: Southwest Corn Muffins
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Unsalted butter, softened
1/2 c  Confectioners sugar
2 lg Eggs
1/2 ts Salt
3 1/2 c  Cake flour
2 tb Baking powder
2 1/2 c  Coarsely ground cornmeal
2 c  Milk, approximately
1/2    Green bell pepper, seeded
And membranes removed,
Finely chopped
1/2    Red bell pepper, seeded and
Membranes removed, finely
Chopped
1/2    Yellow bell pepper, seeded
And membranes removed,
Finely chopped
1    Carrot, trimmed and finely
Grated
2    Ears corn, kernels only
1/4 c  Grated sharp yellow cheese
1/4 c  Grated Jalapeno Jack cheese
Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

In a mixing bowl, cream together butter and confectioners sugar.
Slowly add eggs. Combine dry ingredients and add alternately with
milk to make a stiff batter.

Toss together vegetables and cheese and fold in by hand until well
combined. Fill 2 ungreased muffin tins three-quarters full and bake in
preheated oven for approximately 30 to 35 minutes or until firm and
lightly browned. Serve warm. Yield: 2 dozen
