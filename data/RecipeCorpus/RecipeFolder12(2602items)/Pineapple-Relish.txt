Title: Pineapple Relish
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Mediumdiced fresh pineapple
1/4 c  Thinly sliced red onion
1/2    Jalapeno, minced
1 1/2 ts Coarsely chopped cilantro
1 tb Rice wine vinegar
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
Place the pineapple, onion, jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and
vinegar in a mixing bowl and mix well with a plastic spatula. Season
to taste with salt and pepper.

Yield: 1 1/2 cups
