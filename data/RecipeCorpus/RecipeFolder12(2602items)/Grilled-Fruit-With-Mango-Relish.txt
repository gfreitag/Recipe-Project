Title: Grilled Fruit With Mango Relish
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1    Banana, sliced on diagonal
1    Apple, cut into eighths
4 tb Preserved ginger syrup
2 tb Rice wine vinegar
2 tb Chopped cilantro
2 tb Olive oil
Mango relish:
1    Mango, diced
1 tb Chopped shallot
1 tb Chopped mint
1 ts Vegetable oil
Salt and pepper
Thread bananas and apples on soaked wooden skewers. Combine ginger
syrup, vinegar, cilantro and oil. Grill skewers, brushing them
frequently with ginger syrup mixture, until lightly browned and
crisp. Combine relish ingredients and place in a pretty bowl for
serving. Serve warm.
