Title: Poached Pears in Vanilla and Champagne
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Water
1    Vanilla bean
2 qt Champagne
4    To 6 pears, peeled
In a sauce pot combine the sugar and water together and boil for 2 to
3 minutes thus creating a simple syrup. Split the vanilla bean in
half, scraping the inside to remove the pulp. Add the champagne and
vanilla to the simple syrup. Bring to a simmer and add the pears.
Poach until tender-- about 6 to 8 minutes. Remove and cool.

Yield: 4 servings
