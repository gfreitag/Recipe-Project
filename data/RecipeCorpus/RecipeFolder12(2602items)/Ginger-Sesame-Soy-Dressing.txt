Title: Ginger Sesame Soy Dressing
Categories: Try it
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------GOURMET GETAWAYS IHILANI RES---------------------
1/2 c  Low sodium soy sauce
1/4 c  Cooking mirin (rice wine)
1/2 c  Water
1 oz Shredded fresh ginger
1 oz Sliced onion
1 oz Sliced celery
1 oz Shredded carrot
1 ds Sesame oil
2 tb Unsalted butter
Combine all ingredients except butter in saucepan. Boil for about 10
to 15 minutes to reduce the sauce, or until 2/3 cup of sauce remains.
Add butter, stirring until melted.
