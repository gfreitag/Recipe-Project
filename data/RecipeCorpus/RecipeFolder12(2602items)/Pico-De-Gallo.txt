Title: Pico De Gallo
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Seeded, diced tomatoes
1/4 c  Diced red onion
1 tb Diced jalapenos
1 tb Minced garlic
Juice of 2 limes
2 tb Cilantro, plus extra for
Garnish
Salt and pepper
In a bowl combine all ingredients.

Yield: About 2 cups
