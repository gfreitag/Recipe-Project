Title: Baked Bologna
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1    (10 pound) bologna
Salt and pepper
Preheat the oven to 200 degrees F. Using a sharp knife, score the top
of the bologna several times. Season the bologna with salt and
pepper. Place the bologna on a roasting pan. Bake the bologna for 6
to 7 hours, or until the outside of the bologna is completely
charred. Remove the bologna from the pan and place on a large
platter. Serve the bologna hot with rolls and whole-grain mustard.
Yield: about 24 servings
