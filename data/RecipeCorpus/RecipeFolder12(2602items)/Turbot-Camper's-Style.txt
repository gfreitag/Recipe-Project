Title: Turbot Camper's Style
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
12 oz Turbot fillet- cut into four
Pieces
Kosher salt to taste
Black pepper fresh ground
4    Sheets of aluminum foil- at
Least four times bigger than
Fish
Non-stick vegetable spray
4    Italian roma tomatoes-
Sliced lengthwise
8    Baby carrots, peeled and
Blanched
8    Baby zucchini, blanched and
Sliced in half lengthwise
8    Garlic cloves, peeled and
Blanched for 30 seconds
8    Baby leeks or similar onion,
Cleaned and roasted until
Soft
12    Basil leaves
4    Springs thyme
1/2 c  Parsley leaves
Season turbot on both sides with salt and pepper. Spray foil with
nonstick spray. Lay the fish on foil and place the vegetables and
herbs on top and all around the fish. Fold foil to seal and make a
tight package. Roast in 350 degree oven for about 20 minutes or put
on coals on grill for 15 minutes.

Yield: 4 servings
