Title: Fresh Tomato Salsa
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Vine-ripened red and/or
Orange tomatoes (about 5
Medium)
2    Fresh serrano or jalapeno
Chiles
1/4 md Onion (preferably white)
1/2 c  Fresh cilantro sprigs
1 ts Minced garlic
1 ts Sugar
1 1/2 tb Fresh lime juice
Salt and pepper to taste
Quarter and seed tomatoes. Cut tomatoes into 1/4-inch dice and
transfer to a bowl. Wearing rubber gloves, seed and finely chop
chiles. Finely chop enough onion to measure 1/4 cup and chop
cilantro. Stir chiles, onion, cilantro, and garlic into tomatoes with
sugar and lime juice and salt and pepper to taste. Salsa may be made
1 hour ahead and kept at cool temperature.

Yield: 2 1/2 cups
