Title: Apple Crostini With Fontina
Categories:
Yield: 4 servings
MMMMM--------------------MOLTO MARIO #MB5746-------------------------
2    Granny Smith apples  2
Golden Delicious apples
4 tb Butter
1/2 md Red onion, chopped into
1/8    Th dice
2 tb Poppy seeds
2 oz Grappa
4    Thick slices countrystyle
Bread
4    Thin slices Fontina cheese
Peel, core and cube apples into 1/4inch dice. In a 12inch to 14inch
sautJ pan, heat butter until foam subsides. Add onions and cook until
soft and golden brown, about 8 to 10 minutes. Add apples and poppy
seeds and cook until edges are softened, about 5 to 6 minutes. Add
grappa and lower heat. Meanwhile, grill bread on both sides and place
on platter. Place a cheese slice on each piece of bread. Spoon apples
over and serve.
