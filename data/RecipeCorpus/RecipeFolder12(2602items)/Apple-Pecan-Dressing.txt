Title: Apple-Pecan Dressing
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2 ts Olive oil
3/4 c  Diced bacon
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 c  Celery
1 1/2 c  Peeled and diced Granny
Smith apples
1/2 c  Pecan pieces
1/4 c  Chopped parsley
1/4 c  Chopped fresh sage
Salt and pepper to taste
4 c  Cubed day-old bread, cut
Into 1-inch cubes
Creole spice to taste-recipe
Enclosed
2 c  Chicken stock
In a large skillet heat oil and cook the bacon until it's crispy,
about 4 minutes. Add onions, celery, apples, pecans, parsley, sage,
and cook for 3 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Stir in
the bread cubes, Creole spice, and chicken stock. Cook until heated
through. Remove from heat and adjust the seasonings.
