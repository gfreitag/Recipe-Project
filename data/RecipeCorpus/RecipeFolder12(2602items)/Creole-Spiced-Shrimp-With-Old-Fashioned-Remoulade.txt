Title: Creole Spiced Shrimp With Old Fashioned Remoulade
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
2    Lemons, halved
4 qt Water
4    Bay leaves
3 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cayenne
2 tb Zatarain's Concentrated Crab
And Shrimp Boil
3 lb Large shrimp, peeled and
Deveined
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
3/4 c  Vegetable oil
1/2 c  Chopped onions
1/2 c  Chopped green onions
1/4 c  Chopped celery
2 tb Prepared horseradish
3 tb Creole or whole-grain
Mustard
3 tb Prepared yellow mustard
3 tb Ketchup
3 tb Chopped parsley
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1/8 ts Freshly ground black pepper
Squeeze the juice from the lemons into a large pot. Add the halves.
Add the water, bay leaves, salt cayenne, and crab boil. Bring to boil
and cook for 5 minutes. Add the shrimp. Remove from the heat, cover,
and let stand for 4 to 5 minutes. Drain. Serve either cooled or
chilled completely. Combine the remaining ingredients in a food
processor with a metal blade and process for 30 seconds. Use
immediately or store. Will keep for several days in an airtight
container in the refrigerator.

Yield: 4 to 6 servings
