Title: Cilantro Pesto
Categories: 2hot
Yield: 4 servings
6    Cloves garlic, peeled
1 bn Fresh parsley, stemmed
1 bn Cilantro, stemmed
2 tb Toasted almonds
4 tb Olive oil
Juice of 2 lemons
1/4 ts Sea salt
Freshly ground black pepper
Hand chop the garlic, parsley, cilantro, and almonds until fine.
Combine with the oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper until well mixed.
Store in a covered container in the refrigerator. Yield: about 1 cup
