Title: Dark Chocolate Ganache
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Heavy cream
2 tb Unsalted butter
18 oz Semisweet chocolate, chopped
Into 1/2 - ounce pieces
Heat the heavy cream and the butter in a 3-quart saucepan over medium
high heat. Bring to a boil. Place the semisweet chocolate in a
3-quart stainless steel bowl. Pour the boiling cream over the
chocolate and allow to stand for 5 minutes. Stir until smooth.
