Title: Polenta Taragna
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Polenta or course cornmeal,
Quick cooking, mixed with 1
Cup
Buckwheat flour
1/2 c  Butter plus 3 tablespoons
1/4 lb Crescenza cheese
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

Butter an oven proof dish with 3 tablespoons butter and set aside. In
a 6 quart sauce pan, heat 8 cups water to a boil. Whisking furiously,
slowly drizzle in mixed flours until all are in. Pull pan to front of
burner and switch to a wooden spoon. Cook until texture of molten
lava and add butter and cheese. Stir though and pour into baking
dish. Place in oven 8 to 10 minutes, remove and serve immediately.
