Title: Smoked Dry Rub Ribs
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
Dry rub #6
1 c  Salt
1 c  Paprika
1 c  Ground black pepper
1 c  Cumin
1 c  Ancho chile powder
Six 3-pound pork ribs
Jalapeno Dippin' Sauce,
Recipe follows
Mix the first 5 ingredients together well.  Rub ribs side to side and
top to bottom and place in the refrigerator overnight.  Allow ribs to
sit out at room temperature for 1 hour before smoking. Smoke at 200
degrees for 4 1/2 to 5 hours.  Allow to sit covered for
1/2    hour before eating.
