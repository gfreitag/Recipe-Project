Title: Agua De Melon
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1    Cantaloupe, peeled, seeded,
And cubed
1/2 c  Sugar, or to taste
6 c  Water
In a blender, place the cantaloupe and sugar with 2 cups of the water.
Blend until smooth. Strain into a large pitcher, add the remaining
water, and stir well. Add ice, and refrigerate until ready to serve.
Stir before serving.

Yield: 6 to 8 servings
