Title: Jicama Salad
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1 lg Jicama, sliced into
Julienned
2 lg Carrots, julienne
1 c  Red onion, finely sliced
1/2    Red pepper, julienned
1/2    Yellow pepper, julienned
1    Tomato, julienned
1/4 c  Fresh lime juice
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1 ts Honey
2 tb Olive oil
pn Cayenne
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
2 tb Chopped cilantro
Combine jicama, carrots, red onion, both peppers, tomato in a medium
bowl. Whisk together the lime juice, vinegar, honey, olive oil and
cayenne and season with salt and pepper to taste. Add the cilantro
and let sit for 30 minutes.

Yield: 4 servings
