Title: Chocolate Ganache
Categories: Hot
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Goodquality bittersweet
Chocolate
4 oz Heavy cream.
Chop chocolate fine and place in small bowl. Place cream in small pot
and bring to a simmer. Pour simmering cream over chopped chocolate
and stir until smooth. Cool to room temperature before glazing.

Yield: 1 cup
