Title: Ginger Dough
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/2 c  Granulated sugar
1 1/2 c  Shortening
1    Egg
12 oz Molasses
1 1/2 tb White vinegar
6 1/2 c  All purpose flour
2 1/4 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
3 tb Ground ginger
3    To 4 teaspoons ground
Cloves
Cream sugar and shortening. Add egg and beat. Beat in molasses and
vinegar. Sift together baking soda, salt, ginger, cloves, cinnamon.
Add to mix and cream. Add flour all at once and stir just until flour
is blended. Refrigerate until ready to use. This dough must be used
CHILLED.
