Title: Sweet And Sour Sauce
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Tomato sauce
2 tb Pineapple juice
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Cornstarch
In a saucepan combine vinegar, sugar, tomato sauce, pineapple juice
and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring. Whisk cornstarch with 2
tablespoons of water to a slurry and drizzle into sauce, whisking,
until it thickens. Pour into a pretty bowl for serving.
