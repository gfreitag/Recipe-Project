Title: Homemade Condensed Milk
Categories:
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------TOO HOT TAMALES #THT6107-----------------------
6 c  Nonfat milk
5 tb Sugar
In heavy saucepan pour milk and bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer
and cook for about 45 minutes, stirring occasionally. Stir in sugar
and continue simmering 10 to 15 minutes, or until milk has reduced to
3 cups. Strain. Milk can be refrigerated for up to one week.
