Title: Rum-Lime Punch
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Simple syrup
1 c  Dark rum
3/4 c  Fresh lime juice
5 tb Grenadine
1/4 ts Bitters
pn Ground nutmeg
Ice cubes
Lime slices
Combine equal amounts of sugar and water, bring to a boil and cook
until sugar is dissolved. Mix all punch ingredients together. Serve
over ice, garnish with lime slice.
