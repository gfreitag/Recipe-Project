Title: The Mansion On Turtle Creek Tortilla Soup
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Corn oil
4    Corn tortillas, cut into
Long strips
8    Garlic cloves, peeled
2 c  Fresh onion puree
4 c  Fresh tomato puree
5    Dried New Mexican chilies,
Fire roasted and seeded
2    Jalapenos, chopped
1 tb Cumin powder
1 tb Epazote, chopped
1 ts Ground coriander
1 lg Bay leaf
1 1/2 qt Chicken Stock
Salt to taste
Lemon to taste
Cayenne pepper to taste
1    Cooked whole chicken breast,
Skinless, boneless, and cut
Into thin strips
1 large avocado, peeled, seeded, and cut into small cubes
1 1/2    cups shredded cheddar cheese 4 corn tortillas, cut into
thin strips and fried crisp

Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add tortillas and
garlic and saute until tortillas are crisp and garlic is golden
brown, about 4 to 5 minutes. Add onion puree and cook for 5 minutes,
stirring occasionally until reduced by 1/2. Add tomato puree, roasted
chilies, jalapenos, cumin, coriander, epazote, bay leaf, and chicken
stock. Bring to a boil. Lower heat and simmer for approximately 40
minutes. Skim fat from surface, if necessary. Process through a food
mill to attain the perfect consistency or use a blender (soup may
become thick; thin out with additional chicken stock). Season to
taste with salt, lemon and cayenne (optional).

Yield: 10 to 12 servings
