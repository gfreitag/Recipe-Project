Title: Blackening Seasoning
Categories:
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------GRILLIN' AND CHILLIN' #GR361---------------------
3/4 c  Paprika
3/4 c  Cracked black pepper
1/4 c  Salt
2 tb Toasted crushed cumin seeds
2 tb Minced garlic
2 tb Minced onion
Combine all seasoning ingredients.
