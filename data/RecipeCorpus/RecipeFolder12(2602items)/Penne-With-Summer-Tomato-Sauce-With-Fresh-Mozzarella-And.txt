Title: Penne With Summer Tomato Sauce With Fresh Mozzarella And
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
4    Ripe tomatoes, seeded and
Diced
4    Cloves garlic, finely
Sliced
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 tb Balsamic vinegar
1/4 ts Red pepper flakes
1/4 c  Basil chiffonade
Salt
1 lb Penne, cooked al dente
1/2 lb Fresh mozzarella, cubed
In a large serving bowl, combine the tomatoes, garlic, olive oil,
vinegar, red pepper flakes and basil, season with salt. Add the pasta
and mozzarella and stir to combine.
