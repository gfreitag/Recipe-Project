Title: Cranberry Compote
Categories: Baker
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Cranberries
1 c  Orange juice
1/4 c  Brown sugar
Place all of the ingredients in a stainless steel sauce pan and simmer
until the cranberries pop and fruit cooks down a bit. Use as a sauce.

Yield: 6 servings
