Title: Lemon Creme Fraiche
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Heavy cream (room
Temperature)
2 tb Buttermilk
1 ts Lemon zest, grated
Mix cream and buttermilk together and store in 75 to 85 degrees F,
loosely covered for 18 to 24 hours. Refrigerate for up to 1 week.
Garnish with grated lemon zest.

Yield: 8 servings as appetizers
