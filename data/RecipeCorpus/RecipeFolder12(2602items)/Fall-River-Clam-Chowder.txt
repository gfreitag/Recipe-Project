Title: Fall River Clam Chowder
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  (about 4 ounces) diced bacon
1 c  Chopped leeks (bottom white
Part only)
2 c  Peeled 1/2-inch diced
Potatoes
1 c  Water
3 c  Half and half
1 lb Chopped clams
Salt and pepper
1/2 c  Sliced green onions
Creole seasoning
In a soup pot, cook the bacon until crisp; remove and reserve. To the
bacon fat, add the leeks and cook until tender, about 4 minutes. Add
the potatoes and water. Bring up to a boil, reduce the heat to a
simmer and add half-and-half. Cook until the potatoes are tender,
about 20 minutes. Add the clams and bacon and continue cooking for 3
minutes. Season with salt and white pepper. Garnish with green onions
and Creole seasoning, if desired.

Yield: 4 servings
