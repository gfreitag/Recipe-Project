Title: Creole Vegetable Jambalaya
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
3 c  Small diced onions
2 tb Minced shallots
1 c  Small diced bell peppers
2 c  Small diced eggplant
2 c  Small diced yellow squash
1 tb Minced garlic
3 c  Chopped tomatoes
3 ts Salt
2 ts Cayenne
3    Bay leaves
3 c  Long-grain rice
6 c  Water
1 c  Chopped green onions
Heat the oil in a large heavy pot over medium heat. Add the onions,
shallots, bell peppers, eggplant, squash, and garlic and saute until
tender about 3-5 minutes. Add the tomatoes. Season with salt and
cayenne. Add the bay leaves. Add the rice and stir for 2-3 minutes.
Add the water, stir and cover. Cook for 30-35 minutes, or until the
rice is tender and the liquid has been absorbed. Do not stir during
this cooking time. Remove from heat and let stand for 2-3 minutes.
Add the green onions and mix.

Yield: 8 to 10 servings
