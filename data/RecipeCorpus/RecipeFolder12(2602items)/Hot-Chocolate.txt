Title: Hot Chocolate
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Milk
1 c  Heavy cream
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Vanilla extract
3/4 lb Bitter chocolate (Ybarra or
Callebaut)
Place milk, cream, sugar and vanilla in a 4quart saucepan and bring
to a boil. Meanwhile, grate chocolate (using box cheese grater) to
small pieces. Add chocolate to milk mixture, remove from heat and
stir until melted, thick and smooth. Serve warm with churros.

Yield: 5 to 6 cups
