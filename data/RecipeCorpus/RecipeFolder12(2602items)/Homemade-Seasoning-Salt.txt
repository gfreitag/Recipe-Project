Title: Homemade Seasoning Salt
Categories: Live
Yield: 1 /3 cup
3 tb Kosher salt
2 ts Dried basil
1 ts Dried fennel
1 ts Paprika
1 ts Tumeric
1 ts Dried thyme
1/4 ts Allspice
1/4 ts Dry mustard
1/4 ts White pepper
Combine in a small bowl and stir until well combined. Store in an
air-tight container.

Yield: 1/3 cup

(recipe from "Marinades" by Melanie Barnard)
