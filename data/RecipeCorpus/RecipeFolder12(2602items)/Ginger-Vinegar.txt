Title: Ginger Vinegar
Categories:
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM-----------------ESSENCE OF EMERIL #EE2188----------------------
1/2 lb Ginger root
12 oz Rice wine vinegar
Peel the root, cut into small enough pieces to fit into the bottle.
Pour the vinegar over the ginger. Cork it up and allow to steep for 1
week before you use it.
