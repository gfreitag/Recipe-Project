Title: Red Sangria
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Simple syrup, equal parts
Sugar and water
2    Bottles red wine
3/4 c  Brandy
1/2 c  Triple sec
3/4 c  Orange juice
2    Oranges, sliced into thin
Round
2    Green apples, cored and
Sliced thin
2    Lemons, sliced into thin
Rounds
Boil sugar and water until sugar dissolves. Cool. Combine all
ingredients in a large pitcher and refrigerate, covered, 2 hours or
up to 2 days. Serve over ice.
