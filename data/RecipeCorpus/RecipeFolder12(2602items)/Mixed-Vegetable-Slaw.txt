Title: Mixed Vegetable Slaw
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Julienne zucchini
1/2 lb Julienne yellow squash
1/2 lb (about 4 carrots) julienne
Carrots
1    Julienne red onion
10    Cherry tomatoes, quartered
1/2 c  Olive oil
2 tb White wine vinegar
2 tb Chiffonnade fresh basil
Salt and black pepper
Combine and mix well. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
