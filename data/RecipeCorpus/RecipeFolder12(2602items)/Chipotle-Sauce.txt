Title: Chipotle Sauce
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2    Chipotle peppers in 1
TB adobo sauce
1 tb Chopped garlic
1 tb Chopped shallots
1/2 c  Cilantro, washed and roughly
Chopped
1/2 c  Seasoned rice vinegar
1    Lemon, juiced
2 c  Soy oil
Process all the ingredients, except for the soy oil. With the machine
running, add the oil in a slow stream until the sauce is emulsified.
Taste. Add more oil if necessary. Reserve.
