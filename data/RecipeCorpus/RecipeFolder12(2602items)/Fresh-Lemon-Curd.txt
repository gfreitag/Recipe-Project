Title: Fresh Lemon Curd
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Fresh lemon juice, about 16
Lemons
3/4 c  Sugar
8    Egg yolks
In a double boiler, over medium heat, whisk all the ingredients
together. Cook the sauce until thick, about 10 to 12 minutes,
stirring constantly. Remove from the heat and cool.

Yield: 1 1/2 to 2 cups
