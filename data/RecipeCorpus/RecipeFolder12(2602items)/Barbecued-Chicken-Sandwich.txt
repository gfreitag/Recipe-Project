Title: Barbecued Chicken Sandwich
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
Two 3 1/2-pound whole
Chickens
3 tb Dry rub #1
Barbecue sauce
Dry Rub #1---
1 tb Paprika, Hungarian
1/2 ts Celery salt
1/2 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Sage
1/2 ts Mustard
1/2 ts Chipotle powder
Mix all ingredients well and reserve in refrigerator, covered
tightly. Will keep for up to 2 weeks.
