Title: Tomato Salsa Cruda
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
4    Italian Roma tomatoes, cored
And chopped
1    To 2 serrano chilies,
Chopped
1/4 sm Onion, chopped
Juice of 1 lime
1 ts Sea salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
In a blender, place the tomatoes, chilies, onion, lime juice, salt,
and pepper. Blend briefly, making sure the salsa remains rough. Store
in a covered container, in the refrigerator, no more than 1 day.

Yield: about 1 cup
