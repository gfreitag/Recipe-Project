Title: Lemon Comfort Deluxe
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
2 oz Lemonade (fresh)
2 oz Southern Comfort
4 oz Champagne
Lemon wedge
Sugar to taste
Mineral water, optional
Pour lemonade into a glass. Add Southern Comfort, top off glass with
Champagne. Garnish with lemon wedge and sprinkle with sugar to taste.
Add mineral water if desired.
