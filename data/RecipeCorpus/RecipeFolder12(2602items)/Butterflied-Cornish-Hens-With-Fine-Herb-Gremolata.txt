Title: Butterflied Cornish Hens With Fine Herb Gremolata
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Finely minced garlic
1/4 c  Finely minced lemon zest
1/4 c  Finely chopped tarragon
1/4 c  Finely chopped parsley
1/4 c  Finely chopped chervil
1/4 c  Finely chopped chives
1 tb Olive oil
4    Rock Cornish game hens,
Butterflied
2 tb Olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients and mix well.  Preheat
grill. Brush the hens with olive oil and season with salt and pepper
to taste. Grill the hens over low heat until cooked through,
approximately 10 to 12 minutes per side.

Serve each person a whole hen and sprinkle with the gremolata.

Yield: 4 servings
