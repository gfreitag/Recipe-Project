Title: Anthony's Firecracker Shrimp
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 ts Oil
1 lb Large shrimp, shell-on,
Split down back and
Deveined
1 c  Heavy cream
Juice of 2 lemons
Tabasco
2 tb Butter
2 tb Chopped parsley
Salt and pepper
Heat oil in a large saute pan, add shrimp and toss and cook for 3
minutes. Add cream and lemon juice. Splash on as much Tabasco as you
can handle. Allow to reduce on high for 5 minutes; watch carefully,
it could overflow. Add butter and parsley. Season with salt and
pepper. Serve it up.
