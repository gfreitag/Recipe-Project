Title: Jumbo Lump Crab Salad With Plantain Chips And Avocado
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Jumbo lump crab meat, picked
Through for cartilage
1/4 c  Diced red, yellow and green
Pepper
2    Scallions, minced
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1/4 c  Ketchup
1    Tomato, peeled and diced
1    Lime juiced
Salt and pepper
Plantain chips
Sliced avocado
In a large bowl add all ingredients until blended, being careful not
to shred crab meat. Serve in a mold on a chilled plate, garnish with
plantain chips and sliced avocado.
