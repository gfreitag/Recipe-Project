Title: Chicken With Hazelnuts
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1    Whole chicken breast,
Halved
Salt and pepper
1 tb Flour
1 tb Oil
1 c  Chicken stock
1/2 c  Toasted hazelnuts
1/3 c  Softened butter
2 tb Sour cream
1 tb Chopped parsley
In a large skillet, heat oil. Season the chicken breasts with salt and
pepper and dust evenly with flour on both sides. Place chicken
breast, skin side down into the hot oil. Sear until golden brown and
turn. Sear for 1 minute and add the stock, and let reduce. In the
work bowl of your food processor, puree hazelnuts until finely
ground, then the butter and process until incorporated and smooth.

Remove the puree with a spatula and add to the skillet, a little at a
time, stirring constantly. Reduce heat, add sour cream, and adjust the
seasonings. Remove the chicken breasts and place on a serving
platter, top with the sauce. Garnish with chopped parsley.

Yield: 2 servings
