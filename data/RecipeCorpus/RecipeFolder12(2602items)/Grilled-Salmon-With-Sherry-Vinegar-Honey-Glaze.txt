Title: Grilled Salmon With Sherry Vinegar-Honey Glaze
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Sherry vinegar
2 tb Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Honey
1 tb Ancho chile powder
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
4    Salmon fillets, 6 ounces
Each
In a small saucepan over high heat, reduce the vinegar to 1/4 cup. In
a mixing bowl, combine the vinegar syrup with the mustard, honey, and
ancho chile powder and season to taste with salt and pepper. Let rest
30 minutes.

Preheat grill. Brush the salmon with the glaze and grill 3 minutes on
each side for medium. Serve with the relish.

Yield: 4 servings
