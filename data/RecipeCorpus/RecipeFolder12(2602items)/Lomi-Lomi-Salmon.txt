Title: Lomi Lomi Salmon
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Fresh salmon, salted
2 lb Tomatoes, finely chopped
8 oz Onions, finely chopped
1/3    Green onions, finely
Chopped
1/2 c  Chopped ice
Remove the salmon skin and cut into small dice. Place into a bowl and
add tomatoes and onions and chill. To serve place bowl on crushed ice.

Note: Lomi in Hawaiian means "to squeeze, crush, mash fine."

Recipes from Phillipe Padovani, Executive Chef, Manele Bay Hotel
