Title: Sugared Pecans
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Pecan halves
1 tb Egg whites
1 c  Sugar
Place pecans in a 9 by 13-inch pan. Pour the whites over the pecans
and rub them between your hands until coated. Using a slotted spoon
toss the pecans with sugar.

Bake the pecans in a preheated 325 degree oven for 30 minutes,
stirring every 5 minutes until sugar coating is even.
