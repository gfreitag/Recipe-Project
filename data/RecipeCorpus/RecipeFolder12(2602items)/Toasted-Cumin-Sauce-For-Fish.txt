Title: Toasted Cumin Sauce For Fish
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Olive oil
4 ts Cumin seeds
12    Cloves garlic, pureed or
Finely chopped
2 ts Dried oregano
2 ts Sea salt
1 ts Coarsely ground black pepper
Or to taste
Juice of 1 1/2 oranges
Juice of 1 1/2 limes
In a small pan, over a medium heat, warm the olive oil.  Add the cumin
seeds and toast them 1 to 2 minutes.  Add the garlic (cook a minute to
release oils) then add, oregano, salt and pepper and cook another 2
to 3 minutes.  Remove from the heat and add the orange and lime
juices. The sauce is best used immediately, but will keep for a few
days in the refrigerator.  Use as a finishing sauce for grilled
seafood, such as halibut or salmon.

Yield: about 1 1/2 cups
