Title: Gascon Clafoutis With Prunes
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Plus 6 tablespoons butter
1 lb Pitted prunes, soaked 4
Hours in 1/2 cup Armagnac,
Reserve 2
TB soaking
Liquid
3/4 c  Flour
1/2 c  Sugar
8    Eggs
4 c  Milk
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.

Use 1 tablespoon butter to grease a shallow 12 to 14inch quiche dish.
Melt the 6 tablespoons butter, browning lightly. Layer prunes in
pattern on quiche dish. Mix flour, sugar, eggs and melted brown
butter in a mixing bowl. Add the 2 tablespoons of reserved prune
soaking liquid. Stir in milk to form a thin batter. Pour batter over
prunes and bake in oven 35 to 40 minutes, until inserted knife exits
cleanly. Serve warm from dish.

Yield: 6 servings
