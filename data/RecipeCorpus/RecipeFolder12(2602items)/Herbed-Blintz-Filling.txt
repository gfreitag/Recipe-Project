Title: Herbed Blintz Filling
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Farmer's cheese
4 oz Cream cheese
1 c  Chopped herbs such as chives
Parsley, mint, basil
1    Egg yolk
Salt and pepper
In a mixing bowl combine any of the stuffings. Fill and fry as
described below.

Yield: enough for about 12 blintzes
