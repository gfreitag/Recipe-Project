Title: Salviata With Celery Ragu Di Trevi
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
6    Eggs
10    Sage leaves, roughly
Chopped
1/4 c  Grated Pecorino Romano
2 tb Virgin olive oil plus 4
Tablespoons
2    Scallions, thinly sliced
1 c  Celery stalks, tops and
Leaves
1/2 c  Basic tomato sauce. recipe
Follows
Beat eggs together with sage and Pecorino.

In a nonstick saute pan, heat 2 tablespoons olive oil over medium
heat. Pour in egg mixture and cook until set like an omelette,
flipping once.

Meanwhile, in a 12 inch to 14 inch saute pan, heat remaining oil until
smoking.  Add scallions and celery and saute until just softened,
about 4 to 5 minutes.  Add basic tomato sauce and simmer 5 to 6
minutes. Cut salivate into wedges and add spoonfuls of sauce. Serve
immediately.

Yield: 4 servings
