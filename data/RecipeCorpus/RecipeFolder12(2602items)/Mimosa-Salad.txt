Title: Mimosa Salad
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Dijon mustard
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground black pepper
1/4 c  Champagne vinegar
1 md Shallot, finely chopped
1/2 c  Extravirgin olive oil
2    Heads butter lettuce, pale
Inner leaves only, torn into
Bite sized pieces
2 lg Hardboiled eggs, whites cut
Into 1/4inch dice and yolks
Pressed
Through a sieve
In a glass bowl, whisk together the mustard, salt, pepper, vinegar,
and shallot. Drizzle the olive oil over in a slow, thin stream,
whisking all the time until the dressing is emulsified.

In a large bowl, combine the lettuce with enough dressing to coat
generously. Place a mound of the salad on each of 6 chilled plates and
garnish with some of the diced egg white and a dusting of the sieved
yolk.

Yield: 6 servings
