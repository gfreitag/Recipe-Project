Title: Garlic Aioli
Categories: 2hot
Yield: 4 servings
8    Cloves garlic, peeled
1/2 ts Salt
2    Egg yolks*
1 ts Red wine vinegar
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1 c  Olive oil
Mash the garlic with the salt, and combine with the egg yolks,
vinegar, and lemon juice in a bowl. Blend with a whisk. Gradually add
oil, a drop at a time, whisking constantly. As mixture begins to
thicken and look like mayonnaise, add oil more generously. Adjust
seasonings and store in refrigerator until ready to use.

Yield: 1 1/2 cups
