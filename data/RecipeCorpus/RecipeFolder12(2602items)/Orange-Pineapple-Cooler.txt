Title: Orange-Pineapple Cooler
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Freshly squeezed orange
Juice
3 c  Fresh pineapple juice
2 c  Sparkling water
Ice cubes
Orange slices, for garnish
Mix together the juices and soda and pour into tall icefilled glasses.
Garnish with orange slices.
