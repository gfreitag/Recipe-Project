Title: Spicy Dipping Sauce
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Soy sauce
2 tb Black vinegar, or Chinkiang
Vinegar
1 ts Chile paste
1/2 ts Sugar
1 tb Minced garlic
Combine ingredients in a small bowl and serve with Pork Dumplings.

Yield: 2/3 cup
