Title: Ranch Dressing
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1    Garlic clove, peeled
Salt and pepper
3/4 c  Buttermilk
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Minced parsley
2 ts Cider vinegar
1 ts Dry mustard
In a bowl, stir together the garlic and salt. Whisk in the buttermilk,
mayonnaise, parsley, cider vinegar, dry mustard, and salt and pepper
to taste. Dressing keeps, covered and chilled, 1 week.

Yield: 1 cup
