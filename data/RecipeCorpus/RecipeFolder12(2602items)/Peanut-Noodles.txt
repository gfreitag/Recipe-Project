Title: Peanut Noodles
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Smooth peanut butter
2 tb Toasted sesame oil
1 tb Light soy sauce
1 tb Rice wine vinegar
1/4 ts Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Dried hot red pepper flakes,
Optional
6 oz Cooked capellini
In a bowl, stir together peanut butter, sesame oil, soy sauce,
vinegar, sugar, salt and optional red pepper flakes. Toss with the
cooked noodles until well combined.
