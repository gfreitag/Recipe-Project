Title: Oatmeal Lace Cookies
Categories: Baker, Desserts
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 c  Sugar
1 c  Oats
1/4 c  Cream
1/4 c  Corn syrup
1 c  Melted butter
2 tb Vanilla extract
In a mixer with a paddle combine dry ingredients. In a separate bowl
stir together wet

ingredients then blend them into dry ingredients. Drop spoonfuls of
batter, spaced far apart, onto nonstick sheet pans and bake at 350
degrees.

Yield: 3 dozen
