Title: Grilled Sardines With Lemon-Pepper Vinaigrette
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
12    Fresh sardines
(approximately 1 pound)
Olive oil
Salt and freshly ground
Pepper
Preheat grill. Wash the sardines and soak in cold water for 10
minutes. Dry well. Grill the sardines for 3 to 5 minutes until just
cooked through.
