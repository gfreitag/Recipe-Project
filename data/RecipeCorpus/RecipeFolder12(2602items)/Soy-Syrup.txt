Title: Soy Syrup
Categories: Try it, Sauces
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM----------------CHEF DU JOUR MING TSAI #DJ93---------------------
2 c  Thin soy sauce
1/2 c  Brown sugar
1    Lime juiced
Combine all and reduce at a simmer until a syrup is achieved, 70-80
percent reduction.
