Title: Pork Pulled Barbecue With Corn Bread
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb Pork Shoulder
1    Jalapeno chile, minced
1/4 c  Tomato paste
Black pepper to taste
Combine all ingredients and bake covered in slow oven until meat is
falling apart. When cool enough to handle, pull into fine shreds.
