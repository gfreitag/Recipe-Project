Title: Fruit Smoothies
Categories:
Yield: 2 Smoothies
MMMMM--------------------EMERIL LIVE #EMIA41-------------------------
2 c  Whole milk
1 tb Honey
1    Banana, peeled and cut into
2    Inch pieces
1/2 pt Fresh strawberries
1/2 ts Vanilla
1    To 2 cups crushed ice
In a blender combine all the ingredients and puree until smooth.
Serve the smoothies in cold glasses.
