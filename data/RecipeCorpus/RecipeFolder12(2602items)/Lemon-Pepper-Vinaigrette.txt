Title: Lemon-Pepper Vinaigrette
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
1 sm Shallot, coarsely chopped
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 ts Coarse black pepper
1/4 ts Sea salt
Place lemon juice and shallot in a blender and blend until smooth.
Slowly add the olive oil and blend until emulsified. Add pepper and
salt and blend for 5 seconds.

Yield: 4 servings
