Title: Rustic Rub
Categories: Try it, Spices
Yield: 2 Cups
MMMMM--------------------EMERIL LIVE #EM1A38-------------------------
8 tb Paprika
3 tb Cayenne
5 tb Black pepper, ground
6 tb Garlic powder
3 tb Onion powder
6 tb Salt
2 tb Dried oregano
2 tb Dried thyme
Combine all the ingredients in a mixing bowl. Blend well. Can be
stored in an airtight ontainer in your spice cabinet for up to 3
months.
