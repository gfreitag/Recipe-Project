Title: Margarita Ice
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
1 qt Fresh lime juice
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Conintreau|
3 c  Very good Mexican Tequila
Whisk all of the ingredients together. Either freeze the liquid in a
air-tight container or use an ice cream machine, following
manufacturers Serve cold in Margarita glasses.
