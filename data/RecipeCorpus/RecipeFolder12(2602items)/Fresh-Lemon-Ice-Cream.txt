Title: Fresh Lemon Ice Cream
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Lemon zest
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1 c  Sugar
2 c  Light cream (or 1 cup heavy
Cream plus 1 cup milk)
1/8 ts Salt
Few drops of yellow food
Coloring (optional)
In a large bowl, combine the lemon zest, lemon juice, and sugar and
stir to combine well.

Gradually add the cream, salt, and, if desired, the food coloring,
stirring constantly. Continue to stir until sugar is dissolved.

Pour the mixture into ice cube trays with the dividers removed or
into an 8 x 8-inch metal baking pan. Cover tightly with foil and
freeze until the mixture is solid around the edges and mushy in the
middle. Stir well with a wooden spoon, cover with foil, and continue
to freeze until completely firm.

Yield: 2 servings
