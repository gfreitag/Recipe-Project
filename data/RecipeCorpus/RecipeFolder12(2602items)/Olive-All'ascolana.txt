Title: Olive All'ascolana
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Italian fennel salami,
Chopped and crumbled, or
Chopped in a food
Processor until quite fine
2    Eggs
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 c  Bread crumbs
2 c  Olive oil for frying
Heat oil to 375 degrees F.

Pit olives carefully.

Stuff 1 teaspoon chopped salami into each olive.

Set up breading station with eggs, cracked and beaten in one plate,
flour in a second plate and bread crumbs in a third. Roll olives in
egg, then flour and bread crumbs. Fry olives until golden brown.
Serve warm as an aperetivo.
