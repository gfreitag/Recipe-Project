Title: Flank Steak Columbian Style
Categories: Taste
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Beer
1 c  Minced onions
1 ts Powdered cumin
Salt to taste
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
1 c  Diced green pepper
2/3 c  Water
2 lb Lean flank steak
In a large pot, combine beer, onion, cumin, salt, cayenne pepper and
green pepper. Add the water, and bring to a boil. Add the meat, lower
heat, cover and braise the meat until it is very soft and
tender-about 1 1/2 to 2 hours, depending on thickness.

Drain the meat well, and grill it over a charcoal fire of under a
broiler just to heat it and impart a crispy texture.

Yield: 4 servings
