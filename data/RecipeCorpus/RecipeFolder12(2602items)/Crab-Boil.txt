Title: Crab Boil
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
Water
1    Bouquet garni
1/4 c  Crab boil
3    Lemons, cut in half
2    Garlic heads, slit in half
1    Blue Crab
1    Dungeness Crab
1    Green Shore Crab
1    Stone Crab
1    Common European Crab
2    Queen Crab claws
2    Snow Crab claws
2    King or Alaskan claws
Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add bouquet garni, crab boil,
lemons and garlic. Add the largest of the crabs first adding the
remainder of the crabs and removing the crabs as they become cooked.

In a sauce pan, melt 1/4 cup of butter with a few peeled garlic
cloves. Simmer the butter for 3-4 minutes in infuse the garlic into
the butter. Remove from the heat and discard the garlic cloves. Serve
immediately as a dipping sauce
