Title: Rompope
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
8 1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Sugar
2 sm Cinnamon sticks
8    Egg yolks
1 c  Brandy
Combine 8 cups of the milk, sugar and cinnamon in a saucepan and
bring to a boil. Simmer, stirring occasionally, 15 minutes.

In a bowl, combine the egg yolks with the remaining milk. Slowly pour
into the milk mixture and simmer, stirring frequently, until it
reaches the consistency of a thick sauce, about 10 minutes. Stir in
the brandy and remove from heat. Pour into a pitcher and chill.

Yield: 8 to 10 servings
