Title: Spinach Salad With Hot Bacon Dressing
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
5 sl Lean bacon, chopped fine
2 tb Minced shallot
1/2 c  Cider vinegar
1/2 ts Sugar
1 ts Dijon mustard
1 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper
1 lb Fresh young spinach, coarse
Stems discarded and leaves
Washed well
And spun dry
In a large skillet, cook bacon over moderate heat, turning it, until
it is crisp, transfer it to paper towel lined plate to drain, and
discard all but 2 tablespoons of the fat. In the remaining fat, cook
the shallot for 1 minute. Add the vinegar, sugar, mustard, oil, and
salt and pepper to taste. Bring to a boil, pour over spinach and
toss. Serve sprinkled with the bacon.

Yield: 4 servings
