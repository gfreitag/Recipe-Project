Title: Roasted Quail With Pomegranate Molasses And Port Glaze
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 ts Chopped fresh garlic
1 tb Chopped fresh shallot
1 tb Chopped fresh herbs
6    Semi-boneless quail
Mix all ingredients thoroughly and cover. Refrigerate for 4-6 hours or
overnight.
