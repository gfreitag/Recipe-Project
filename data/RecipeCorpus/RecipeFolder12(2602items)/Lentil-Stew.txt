Title: Lentil Stew
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Olive oil
1 tb Chopped garlic
1 1/2 qt Chicken stock
2 c  Brown lentils
1/4 c  Chopped tomatoes
1 lb Red skin potatoes, cut into
1/2    -inch dice
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 ts Chopped lemon zest
12 oz Spinach leaves, picked and
Cleaned
Salt and pepper
1/2 c  Chopped mint
1/2 c  Chopped parsley
1/4 c  Crumbled feta cheese
In a large pot heat oil over medium heat. Add the garlic, cook,
stirring for 30

seconds. Add the chicken stock, lentils, and tomatoes, and then bring
to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 10 minutes. Add the
potatoes, cook uncovered for 15 minutes , stirring occasionally. Add
the lemon juice, zest, and spinach. Simmer for 2 minutes or until the
spinach wilts. Fold in the mint and parsley. Adjust the seasonings.
Serve with crumbled feta.

Yield: 4 servings
