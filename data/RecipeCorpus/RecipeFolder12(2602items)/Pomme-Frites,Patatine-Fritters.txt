Title: Pomme Frites/Patatine Fritters
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Russet potatoes, peeled in
Water
4 qt Virgin olive oil
Salt and pepper
Cut potatoes into finger-sized slices of equal size and place in new
cold water. Heat oil to 385 F in a pot double the volume of oil. Add
potatoes one handful at a time and cook until golden brown. Remove
and drain on paper, season with salt and pepper and serve with
mayonnaise (French) or plain (Italian).

Yield: 4 servings
