Title: Hot Beets
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
6    To 8 cooked and peeled
Beets
2 tb Margarine
2 tb Chopped scallions, including
Some green tops
1 ts Sugar
1 tb White vinegar
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1 tb Chopped parsley
Thinly slice the beets. In a saucepan over low heat, toss the beets
with the margarine, scallions, sugar, vinegar, and salt and pepper.
Cook until tender. Just before serving, sprinkle with the parsley.
