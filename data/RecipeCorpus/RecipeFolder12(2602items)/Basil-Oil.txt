Title: Basil Oil
Categories: Try it, Gift, Condiment
Yield: 1 Cups
MMMMM--------------------COOKING LIVE #CL8954-------------------------
2 c  Tightly packed fresh basil
Leaves
1 c  Olive or vegetable oil
In a large sauce pan, blanch basil leaves for 5 seconds. Refresh in
an ice bath and squeeze out any excess liquid. Place leaves and oil
in a blender and puree.

First, strain the puree through a fine mesh sieve. Then line a small
strainer with several layers of cheesecloth and strain oil into a
glass jar. Flavored oil keeps, covered loosely and refrigerated at
all times, 1 month.
