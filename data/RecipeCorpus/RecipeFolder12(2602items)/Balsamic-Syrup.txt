Title: Balsamic Syrup
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Balsamic vinegar
In a medium-saucepan, over medium heat, add the balsamic vinegar.
Bring to a boil and reduce to a simmer. Simmer until the liquid
reduces by 3/4, about 30 minutes or until syrup-like consistency.
Remove from the heat and cool completely.

Yields: about 1/4 cup
