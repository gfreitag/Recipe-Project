Title: Broccoli with Garlic and Frascati
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
6 tb Virgin olive oil
4    Cloves garlic, thinly
Sliced
1 lb Broccoli, cut into spears,
Lengthwise
6 oz Dry white wine
1 tb Crushed red pepper flakes
Zest of 1 lemon and 1
Orange
In a 12 to 14 inch saute pan, heat olive oil and garlic until just
sizzling. Add broccoli and cook, tossing regularly for 8 to 10
minutes, until even the stem is tender. Use the wine to slow the pan
down if it gets hot enough to brown the garlic. When tender, add red
pepper flakes and zests and place on a platter. Serve as contorno to
braised or grilled meats.

Yield: 4 servings
