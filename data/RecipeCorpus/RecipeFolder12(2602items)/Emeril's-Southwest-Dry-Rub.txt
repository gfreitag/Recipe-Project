Title: Emeril's Southwest Dry Rub
Categories: Emeril
Yield: 4 servings
3 tb Chili powder
2 tb Paprika
1 tb Cayenne
1 tb Ground cumin
1 tb Ground coriander
1 tb Granulated garlic
1 tb Granulated onion
1 tb Kosher salt
1 tb Cracked black pepper
Combine all ingredients thoroughly.

Yield: About 2 cups
