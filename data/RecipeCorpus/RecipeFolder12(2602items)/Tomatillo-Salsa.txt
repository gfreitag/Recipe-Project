Title: Tomatillo Salsa
Categories: Toohot, Mexican
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Fresh tomatillos,
Husked, cut into quarters
3 lg Jalapeno chilies, stemmed,
Seeded (optional), chopped
1/2 c  Cold water
1/2 md Onion, cut in half
2 bn Fresh cilantro
2 ts Salt
Place tomatillos, jalapenos and water in blender. Coarsely chop. Add
onion, cilantro and salt. Puree until smooth, about 2 minutes.
Transfer to medium saucepan. Simmer until reduced to 2 cups, about 10
minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
