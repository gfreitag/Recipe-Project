Title: Avocado Salsa
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1    Avocado, diced
Juice of 1/2 lime
2 tb Minced onion
2 tb Minced cilantro
1/4 ts Sea salt
pn Cayenne pepper
In a small bowl, mix the avocado, lime juice, onion, cilantro, salt,
and pepper. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator no more
than 1 hour.

Yield: about 1 cup
