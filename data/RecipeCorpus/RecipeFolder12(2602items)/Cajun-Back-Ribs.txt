Title: Cajun Back Ribs
Categories: Cajun
Yield: 4 Servings
4 lb Pork back ribs
3 tb Paprika
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
1 tb Garlic powder
2 ts Oregano
2 ts Thyme
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts White pepper
1/2 ts Cumin
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Combine all seasoning and mix well.  Dust all surfaces of ribs with
marinade sauce.  Prepare medium hot coals and place in covered grill.
Grill ribs over indirect heat for 1-1/2 hours.  Turn every 15 minutes
until done.
