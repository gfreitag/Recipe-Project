Title: Beer Marinade
Categories: Toohot
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 c  Dark beer
1/4 c  Lemon juice
4    Cloves garlic, smashed
1 1/2 ts Sea salt
1 ts Freshly ground black pepper
2    Bay leaves
1 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Basil
1 ts Oregano
1 ts Thyme
To prepare the marinade, whisk together the oil, beer, and lemon
juice. Add the garlic, salt, pepper, bay leaves, mustard, basil,
oregano, and thyme. Mix well.
