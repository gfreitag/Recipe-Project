Title: Deep Fried Ice Cream
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
4    Scoops vanilla ice cream
Coating:
1/4 c  Bread crumbs
1/4 c  Almond flour
1    Egg
1/4 c  Flour
Garnish:
Confectioner's sugar
Mint leaves
Freeze scoops of vanilla ice cream

Beat egg. Combine bread crumbs and almond flour. Roll ice cream
scoops in flour, then beaten egg, and finally in almond/breadcrumb
mixture. Fry the ice cream in hot oil for 20 seconds. Put the fried
ice cream in bowl. Dust with confectioner's sugar and garnish with
mint leaf.

Yield: 4 servings
