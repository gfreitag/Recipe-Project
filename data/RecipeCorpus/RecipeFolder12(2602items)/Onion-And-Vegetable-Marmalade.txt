Title: Onion And Vegetable Marmalade
Categories: Molto
Yield: 4 servings
6 tb Extra virgin olive oil
6    Onions, chopped into 1/2
Inch dice
2 md Zucchini, cut into 1/2 inch
Dice
1 md Japanese eggplant, cut into
1/2    Inch dice
10    Cloves garlic, thinly
Sliced
1    Sprig each thyme, lavender,
Rosemary, tied together
2    Red bell peppers, seeded and
Cored, cut into 1/2 inch
Dice
1 tb Sugar
In a large heavy saute pan, heat oil over medium heat until smoking.
Add onions and cook until lightly browned, about 8 to 10 minutes. Add
zucchini, eggplant, herb bundle, bell peppers, and sugar and cook
until marmalade-like consistency, about 1 hour. Remove to cool and
store in a crock.

Serve with socca and fresh dried goat cheese.

Yield: 4 servings
