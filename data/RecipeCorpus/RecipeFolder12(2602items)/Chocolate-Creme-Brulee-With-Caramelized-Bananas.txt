Title: Chocolate Creme Brulee With Caramelized Bananas
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  And 2 tablespoons milk
3/4 c  Heavy cream
3 1/2 oz Bittersweet chocolate,
Chopped into small pieces
5    Egg yolks
1/3 c  And 1/2 tablespoon
Granulated sugar
1/3 c  And 1/2 tablespoon light
Brown sugar
4    Bananas
Preheat oven to 275 degrees.

Bring the milk and the cream mixed together to a boil. Add the chopped
chocolate into the cream and milk mixture, set aside for 5 minutes.

In a mixing bowl, beat the egg yolks with the granulated sugar. While
whipping the egg mixture, pour in the milk, cream and chocolate
mixture. Let cool.

Fill six ungreased ramekins with equal amounts of the chocolate brulee
mixture.

Poach in a water bath in the oven for about 40 minutes.

When the creme brulees are poached, transfer them to a sheet pan and
let cool. Decorate the top of each with 5 or 6 slices of bananas.
Sprinkle with brown sugar and broil the creme brulees under the
broiler until the sugar melts and turns golden color.

Yield: 6 servings
