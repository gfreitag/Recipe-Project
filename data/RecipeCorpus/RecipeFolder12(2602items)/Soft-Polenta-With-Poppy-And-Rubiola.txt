Title: Soft Polenta With Poppy And Rubiola
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Water
1 c  Polenta
2 tb Poppy seeds
8 oz Rubiola cheese
In a 4 quart saucepan, place cool water over high heat and bring
water to a boil. Season with salt and pepper. Add polenta in a thin
stream, whisking constantly. When all polenta is in, pull pot to
front of burner, remove whisk, and start stirring with a wooden
spoon. As polenta thickens, add poppy seeds and cheese and remove
from heat. Stir cheese through and serve immediately with oxtails.

Yield: 4 servings
