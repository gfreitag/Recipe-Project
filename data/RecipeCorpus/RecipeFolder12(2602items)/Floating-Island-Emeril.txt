Title: Floating Island-Emeril
Categories: Desserts, Emeril
Yield: 6 Servings
1 quart milk
4 egg whites
1/2    Cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2    Teaspoon cream of tartar
1 1/4    Cups creme anglaise
1/2    Cup caramel sauce
Mint for garnish
Bring the milk to a low simmer on the stove. Meanwhile, beat the egg
whites to soft peaks, add the sugar, vanilla, and cream of tartar,
and continue to beat until stiff peaks form. Using two large spoons,
form egg whites into football shapes. Individually drop the footballs
into the simmering milk. Gently turn them, allowing them to cook on
all sides. Cook for about 5 minutes. Remove the footballs from the
milk and place on plates, in a pool of creme anglaise. Drizzle the
top with caramel sauce. Garnish with mint.

Yield: dessert for 6
