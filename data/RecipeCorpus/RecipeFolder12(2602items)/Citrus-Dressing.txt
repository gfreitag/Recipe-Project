Title: Citrus Dressing
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Fresh orange juice
2 tb Lemon juice
1/4    To 1/3 cup vegetable oil
1/2 ts Grated orange zest
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper to taste
Process all the ingredients in a blender or food processor until
smooth. Store covered in the refrigerator for up to 1 week.

Yield: about 3/4 cup
