Title: Grilled Cheese Bruschetta
Categories: Grillin
Yield: 4 servings
8    (1/2-inch) thick slices of
Country bread
1/4 c  Olive oil mixed with 4
Cloves crushed garlic
1 c  Monterey Jack cheese, finely
Grated
8 oz Soft goat cheese
2 tb Coarsely ground black
Pepper
2 tb Finely chopped oregano
Preheat grill. Brush each slice of bread with the garlic oil. Grill,
oil-side-down until lightly golden brown. Turn each slice over and
top with 2 tablespoons of Monterey Jack, 1-ounce of goat cheese,
black pepper and oregano. Grill until cheese just begins to melt.

Yield: 4 servings
