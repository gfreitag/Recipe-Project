Title: Chlada Felfa Or Pepper Salad With Preserved Lemon
Categories: Tvfn
Yield: 4 servings
8 lg Bell peppers
2 tb Olive oil
2    Garlic cloves, minced
2 tb Preserved lemon rind, very
Finely diced
2 ts Ground cumin
Salt and freshly ground
Black pepper to taste
Fresh lemon juice to taste
Preheat the broiler. Place the peppers on a lightly greased baking
sheet about 3 inches from the heat source. Broil the peppers, turning
them carefully with thongs., until skins blacken evenly. Place them
in a paper or plastic bag and close it. When the peppers are cool
enough to handle, peel, seed, and finely dice them. Place them in a
colander to drain, then transfer them to a serving bowl and stir in
all the remaining ingredients. Chill until ready to serve.

Yield: 8 servings
