Title: Lemon Verbena Syrup
Categories: Live
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Lemon juice
1/3 c  Sugar
3 tb Water
1 tb Minced fresh lemon verbena
Or lemon thyme
Combine all ingredients in a small saucepan and bring to a boil until
sugar dissolves. Remove from the heat and let mixture steep for 20
minutes. Strain syrup to remove lemon verbena.
