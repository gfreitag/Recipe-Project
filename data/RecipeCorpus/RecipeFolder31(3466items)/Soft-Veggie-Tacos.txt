Title: Soft Veggie Tacos
Categories: Main dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1    Avocado
1/2    Lemon -- juice only
12    Flour tortilla (6")
2 c  Reserved Chili Succotash
4 oz Monterey jack cheese with
Peppers -- shredded
2 c  Reserved Bok Choy Cole Slaw
1 lg Tomato -- chopped
Cut the avocado lengthwise into 1/4's and discard the pit.  Strip off
the peel from each 1/4.  Dice the flesh into small pieces and toss
with the lemon juice.  Set aside.  Wrap the tortillas in a dampened
towel and warm in a microwave oven for 1 minute.  Heat the reserved
succotash in a heavy saucepan over medium heat, stirring frequently.
Serve the tortillas, succotash, shredded cheese, coleslaw and chopped
tomato for each person to assemble.
