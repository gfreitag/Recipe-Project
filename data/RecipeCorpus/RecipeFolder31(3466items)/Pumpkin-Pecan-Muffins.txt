Title: Pumpkin Pecan Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 24 muffins
4    Eggs
2 c  Sugar
3/4 c  Miracle Whip
16 oz Pumpkin
1 c  Pecans; chopped
2 3/4 c  Flour
2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Heat oven to 400.  Beat eggs and sugar with electric mixer on medium
speed until well blended.  Stir in miracle whip. Add pumpkin and
pecans; mix well.  Mix flour, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. Stir
flour mixture into pumpkin mixture.  Beat 2 minutes with electric
mixer on medium speed. Spoon batter into greased muffin cups, filling
cups full. Sprinkle batter with additional chopped pecans, if
desired. Bake 15 to 20 minutes. Cool 5 minutes; remove from pans.
