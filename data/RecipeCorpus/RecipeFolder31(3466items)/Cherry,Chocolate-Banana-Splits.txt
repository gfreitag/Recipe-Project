Title: Cherry/Chocolate Banana Splits
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Chocolate
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Hot fudge topping
4 tb Marshmallow creme: optional
2    Bananas
4    Vanilla ice cream scoops
1 c  Cherry pie filling
Granola cereal; optional
Microwave fudge topping and marshmallow creme until hot. Cut banana
lengthwise, then crosswise, making 4 pieces. Place 2 pieces in each
dessert dish.  Top with ice cream, cherry filling, hot fudge topping,
and marshmallow creme.  Garnish with granola.  Source: Comstock ad
