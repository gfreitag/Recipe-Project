Title: Health Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
6 c  Crispy rice cereal
1 1/2 c  Corn flakes
1 c  Shredded coconut
1 c  Salted peanuts
1 c  Chopped walnuts
1/2 c  Chocolate morsels --
(optional)
2 c  Sugar
2 c  White corn syrup
2 c  Whipping cream
Combine cereals, coconut, nuts and chocolate morsels; set aside. Boil
remaining ingredients to hard-ball stage; pour over cereal mixture;
mix well. Put into a buttered jelly-roll pan; pat down with wet
hands; cool; cut into squares.
