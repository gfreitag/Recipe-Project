Title: Watermelon-Lime Cooler
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
3 c  Watermelon Juice (see note)
1    Lime (Juice of one lime)
Sugar to taste
Note to make Juice: Take one fourth of a good sized watermelon,
remove all seeds and skin. Pound flesh or press through a food mill
to remove the juice. (this should make about 3 cups of juice)

Add the juice of one lime, sugar to taste.

Put a cube of watermelon and a slice of lime in each of four tall
glasses. Add ice cubes, and pour watermelon juice mixture over ice.
