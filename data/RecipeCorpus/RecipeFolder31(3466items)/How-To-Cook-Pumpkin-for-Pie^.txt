Title: How To Cook Pumpkin for Pie^
Categories: Pie/tart, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1    Text only
Slice, peel and then cut the pumpkin (or squash) into small pieces.
Put in a saucepan with a little water, cover and simmer until it's
tender. drain, let cool, and force through a strainer, or mast with a
potato masher.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
