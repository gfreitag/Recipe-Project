Title: Bacon And Pepper Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
GRANAROLI (XBRG76A)
1/3 lb Bacon
1    Green pepper -- diced
1    Red pepper -- diced
1    Onion -- chopped
20    Milliliters
1/3    Stick butter
4 tb Olive oil
10    Plum tomatoes -- chopped
1/4 c  White wine
1    Hot pepper halve -- seed
Garlic -- chopped
x   Salt and pepper  Saute bacon until almost crisp. Drain. Add
butter and oil. Saute onion and garlic. Add peppers and saute 5 min.
Add tomatoes and wine, cook 15 min. REMOVE HOT PEPPER. Season with
salt and pepper and serve over pasta. (wrv)
