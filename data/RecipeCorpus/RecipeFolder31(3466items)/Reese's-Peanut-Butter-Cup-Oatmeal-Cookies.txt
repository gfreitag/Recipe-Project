Title: Reese's Peanut Butter Cup Oatmeal Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 dozen
1/2 c  Margarine; softened
3/4 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Oats
48    Reeses miniature peanut
-butter cups
In large mixer bowl, beat margarine and peanut butter until light and
fluffy.  Add sugars, eggs and vanilla; beat well. Stir together flour,
baking soda and salt; blend into peanut butter mixture. Stir in oats.
Cover dough; refrigerate several hours or until firm enough to
handle. Heat oven to 350.  Shape dough into 1" balls. Place on
ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 9 to 11 minutes or until almost set;
cool 5 minutes. Remove wrappers from peanut butter cups and place one
on each cookie, pressing down gently. Remove from cookie sheet to
wire rack. Cool completely. Source: Hersheys wrapper
