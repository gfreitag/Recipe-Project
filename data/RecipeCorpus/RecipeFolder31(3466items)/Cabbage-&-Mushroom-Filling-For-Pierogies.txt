Title: Cabbage & Mushroom Filling For Pierogies
Categories: Appetizers, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
Cabbage
1/3 c  Water
1 ts Salt
1 lg Onion -- halved and sliced
1/4 ts Pepper
4 oz Mushrooms -- stems and
Pieces
2    Eggs; hardcooked -- chopped
2 tb Butter or margarine
Combine the cabbage, water, onion, mushrooms and butter in a large
saucepan. Cook covered over low heat till tender, about 30 min. Add
salt pepper and chopped eggs; mix well. Makes about 3 1/2 cups
