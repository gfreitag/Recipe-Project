Title: Sizzling Sausage Stir Fry
Categories: Main dish, Sausage
Yield: 6 servings
1 pk Hillshire Farm Kielbasa;
Sliced into bite size pieces
3 tb Oil
3 1/4 c  Stir fry type vegetables
3/4 c  Parmesan; grated
1 1/2 c  Rice
Salt and pepper to taste
Cook rice and keep warm.  Saute vegetables in oil until crisp and
tender. Add sausage and season to taste. Heat thoroughly. Mix in 1/2
cup parmesan cheese and serve immediately over warm rice sprinkling
with remaining cheese.  Source: Hillshire Farms ad
