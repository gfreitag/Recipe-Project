Title: Pineapple Salsa 3
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
8 oz Can crushed pineapple
1/4 c  Red bell pepper -- chop
1    Green onion -- slice
1 tb Lime juice
1 tb Soy sauce
1/4 ts Black & red pepper blend
Salsa: Combine pineapple, red bell pepper, green onion, lime juice,
soy sauce, and black and red pepper blend.
