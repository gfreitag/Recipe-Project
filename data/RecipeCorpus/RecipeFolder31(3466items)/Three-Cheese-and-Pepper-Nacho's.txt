Title: Three Cheese and Pepper Nacho's
Categories: Snacks, Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1    Bag (200 gram) nacho chips,
-triangle style
1 c  Grated medium cheddar
1 c  Grated swiss cheese
1/4 c  Grated parmesan
1/2 ts Garlic powder AND 1/2 tsp
-oregano
1/2 ea Green, red & yellow peppers,
-sliced into strips
Place 1/2 of nacho chips into baking dish. Arrange the peppers evenly
over top, sprinkle with the garlic powder and oregano.  Combine the
three cheeses together of use half.  Arrange other 1/2 of nacho's,
rest of the peppers and spices, and remaining 1/2 of cheese.  Bake in
400F oven for about 10-15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and just
starting to brown slightly.

Origin: Adapted from Kraft's 'What's Cooking' magazine, 12 th issue.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, May/95.
