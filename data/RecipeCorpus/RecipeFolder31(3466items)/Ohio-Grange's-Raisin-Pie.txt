Title: Ohio Grange's Raisin Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 2 servings
1 c  Raisins
2/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Nuts; chopped
1/2 c  Cider; boiled or sweet
Pickle vinegar
Cinnimon
Allspice
Salt
Boil a few minutes, cool and fill two crusts.

Source: Mrs. H. A. Atwood, Nelson Grange, Portage County, OH
