Title: 498838 Vaniliekranse
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Butter
1 1/3 c  Sugar
4 c  Flour
3    Eggs
1 tb Vanilla
Mix butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla, then add flour until blended.
Cool to use with cookie gun.  If using gun use star (smallest) to
dispense long coils of dough onto cookie sheet and cut to
approximately 2 1/2 inch lengths.  Join ends to form rings.  If no
gun is available, roll and cut with favorite cutters.  Bake in
350-400 degree oven until done (slightly golden color).  Recipe is
over 150 years old! Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   06-17-95
