Title: Blu-barb Pie
Categories: Pie/tart, Fruit
Yield: 1 pie
2    Sennebec pie crust
1/4 ts Salt
1 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Flour
2 c  Rhubarb, cut into chunks
2 c  Blueberries
1 tb Butter
Mix salt, sugar, and flour.  Add fruit and stir well.  Pour
:
into pie shell, dot with butter and cover with another crust.  Seal
and cut vent holes.  Bake 10 minutes at 450 , then lower temp to 400
and bake about 30 minutes more until crust is browned and filling
bubbly.
