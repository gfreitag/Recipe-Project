Title: Roasted pepper and tomato salad
Categories: Salads, Tomatoes
Yield: 8 servings
2 lg Red bell peppers
2 lg Green bell peppers
6    Tomatoes
6    Garlic cloves, minced
2 tb Olive oil
2 tb Fresh lemon juice
Char peppers over gas flame or in broiler until blackened on all
sides. Wrap in paper bag and let stand for 10 minutes. Peel, seed and
cut peppers into strips. Transfer to medium bowl.

Blanch tomatoes in a pot of boil water for 20 seconds. Drain and peel
tomatoes. Cut in half, squeeze out the seeds. Cut tomatoes into 1"
pieces. Add tomatoes and remaining ingredients to peppers and toss to
coat. Season to taste with salt & pepper.
