Title: Sausage Boats
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 3 servings
12    Pork sausage links
6    Eggs
1/3 c  Milk
ds Salt
6    Frankfurter buns
3 tb Water
Place links in water in skillet, cover, and cook for 5 minutes over
low heat.  Drain off liquid and finish cooking links until well
browned. Beat eggs and salt together and dip buns in mixture.  Grill
on both sides until browned.  Place sausages in buns; serve with
syrup. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   05-27-95
