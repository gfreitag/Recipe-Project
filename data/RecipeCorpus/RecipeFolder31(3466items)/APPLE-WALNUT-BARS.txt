Title: APPLE WALNUT BARS
Categories: Diabetic, Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 16 bars
1 c  Flour; all-purpose
1 1/2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Baking soda
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Margarine; calorie-reduced
--softened
2    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Sugar substitute; Splenda
1 lg Apple; grated, unpeeled
1/4 c  Applesauce; unsweetened
1/3 c  Walnuts; chopped, divided
Stir together flour, cinnamon, baking powder, baking soda and salt;
set aside.  Beat margarine until smooth.  Add eggs and vanilla and
beat well. Beat in sugar substitute for about 1 minute.  Add flour
mixture, apple and applesauce; beat until well-blended.  Stir in 1/4
cup walnuts.

Spread evenly in greased 8-inch square baking pan.  Sprinkle with
remaining nuts.  Bake at 350F for about 40 minutes or until tester
inserted in centre comes out clean.  Cool.

Cut into bars.

Per bar: Energy 97 cal Protein 2.1 g Fat 5.2 g Carbohydrates 10.6 g

Origin:  Splenda Low-Calorie Sweetener
