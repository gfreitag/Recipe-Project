Title: Cockie Leekie Soup (Aunt B's)
Categories: British isl, Soup/stew, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Fowl stock
8    Leeks
2 oz Rice
Salt and pepper
Make stock by putting an old fowl to boil in three quarts of water.
Add salt and skim as meccessary and cook steadily for about an hour
and a half.Frain off stock.Return this to pan with medium size
leeks,cut into 1 inch lengths and add to stock with pepper and washed
rice.Add more salt if required and boil for one hour longer.

Typed by G.Major    06\01\95 from a scottish cookbook from my Aunt B.
