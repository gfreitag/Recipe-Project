Title: Original Calzone
Categories: Italian, Pie/tart, Main dish
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Dough for Italian bread
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 sm Onion, chopped fine
1 ts Parsley chopped fine
1    8oz  can tomato sauce
1/2 c  Grated romano or parmesan
-cheese
1/4 c  Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1/4 c  Provolone cheese, shredded
-salt and pepper to taste
Mix ground beef with salt, pepper, onion, parsley, and grated cheese.
fry off until meat turns brown. After meat cools, mix in mozzarella
and provolone cheese and tomato sauce. Roll out dough in
approximately 6 inch circle. Put in 1/3 cup of meat mixture and fold
over like a apple turnover. Squeeze edges with fork and deep fry or
bake until golden brown.

(Recipe from members of the Little Italy Lodge, Order Sons of Italy in
America cookbook)
