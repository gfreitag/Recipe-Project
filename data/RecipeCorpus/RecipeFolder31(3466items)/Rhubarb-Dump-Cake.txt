Title: Rhubarb Dump Cake
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
5 c  Rhubarb; diced
3 c  Marshmallows; minature
1 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
1 pk Cake mix white
Water as needed for mix
3 oz Raspberry jello
Arrange rhubarb in bottom of 9x13 pan. Sprinkle with sugar and
gelatin. Cover with marshmallows. Prepare cake mix using 2 eggs and
required amount of water. Spread batter over the mixture. Bake at
350~F for 50-55 minutes. Serve warm with whipped cream.
