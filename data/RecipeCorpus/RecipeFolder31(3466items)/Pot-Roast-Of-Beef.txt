Title: Pot Roast Of Beef
Categories: Meats, Crockpot
Yield: 12 servings
3 md Potatoes -- quartered
2 lg Carrots -- sliced
1    Onion -- sliced
4    Cloves garlic -- chopped
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
3 lb Beef roast
1/2 c  Water
1/4 c  Red wine
Place vegetables & garlic in bottom of crock pot. Salt & pepper meat
& then put in pot. Pour liquid over top of roast. Cover & cook on low
approximately 10 to 12 hours or on high approximately 5 to 6 hours.

Note: I use 1/8 ts liquid crab boil instead of pepper.
