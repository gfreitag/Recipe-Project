Title: Nano-Nachos
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Grated cheddar cheese
Taco seasoning mix to taste
Tortilla chips
1    9 oz. can no-lard bean dip
Prepared guacamole Dip
16 oz Sour cream
Jalapeno peppers, seeded &
Thinly sliced
Melt cheese in medium saucepan over low heat. Stir in taco seasoning
and mix thoroughly. Spread tortilla chips with small amount of bean
dip and cheese mixture. If softer nacho is desired, microwave or
broil until softened.  Top with small amounts of guacamole dip and
sour cream. Garnish with sliced jalapenos.

F.A.T. REDUCER:  Use lowfat cheddar cheese and sour cream. * From
