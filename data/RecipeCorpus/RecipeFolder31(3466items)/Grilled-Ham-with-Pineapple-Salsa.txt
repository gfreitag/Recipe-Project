Title: Grilled Ham with Pineapple Salsa
Categories: Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
8 oz Pineapple;crushed,drained
2 tb Orange Marmalade
1 tb Cilantro;fresh,chopped
2 ts Jalapeno Pepper;fresh
-chopped
2 ts Lime Juice
1/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 lb Ham; 3/4 to 1 inch, fully
-cooked, center cut slice
Grill Directions:
Heat grill. In small bowl, combine all salsa ingrediants; mix
:
well.
When ready to grill, place ham on gas grill or on charcoal
:
grill 4 to 6 inches from medium coals. Cook 10 to 20 minutes until
thoroughly heated, turning 2 or 3 times. Serve with pineapple salsa.

Broiler Directions:
Prepare salsa as directed above. Place ham on broiler pan;
:
broil 4 to 6 inches from heat for 10 to 20 minutes or until
thoroughly heated, turning 2 or 3 times. Serve with pineapple salsa.
