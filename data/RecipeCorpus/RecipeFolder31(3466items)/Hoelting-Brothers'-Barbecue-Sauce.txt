Title: Hoelting Brothers' Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 32 servings
2 1/2 c  Water
18 oz Tomato paste
12 oz Molasses
1 1/2 c  Dark corn syrup
3/4 c  White vinegar
3/4 c  Honey
2 tb Paprika
2 tb Whiskey
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Liquid smoke
1 tb Hot pepper sauce
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Onion powder
1 ts Ground red pepper -- or to
Taste
2    Bay leaves
In a 4-quart dutch oven, combine all ingredients.  If necessary, use
a wire whisk to mix well.  Bring to boiling. Reduce heat and simmer,
uncovered, over medium-low heat for 30 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Remove bay leaves. Store in the refrigerator for up to
two weeks.  Makes 8 cups. Penny Halsey (ATBN65b). Note:  Recipe may
be halved, or extra sauce may be frozen.
