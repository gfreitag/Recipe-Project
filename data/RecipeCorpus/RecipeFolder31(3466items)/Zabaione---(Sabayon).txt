Title: Zabaione   (Sabayon)
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
30 g  Egg
600 g  Sugar
600 ml Marsala wine
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Beat eggs with sugar. Add marsala wine. Cook and stir sabayon sauce -
don't stop stirring. Sabayon is ready when it starts boiling. Serve
either hot or cold. Source: Given to share with the express
permission of Chef de Cuisine
