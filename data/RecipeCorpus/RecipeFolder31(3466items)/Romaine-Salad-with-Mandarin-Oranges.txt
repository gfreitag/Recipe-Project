Title: Romaine Salad with Mandarin Oranges
Categories: Diabetic, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
2 lg Heads romaine lettuce;
2/3 c  Green onions; chopped
1 cn (11-oz) mandarin oranges;
1/4 c  Italian salad dressing;
-FAT-FREE
1 pk (2-oz) toasted almonds;
-toasted
Rince lettuce and drain on paper towels.  Tear leaves into bite-sized
pieces, and place them in a salad bowl.  Clean and chop green onions,
including tops; add to the lettuce.  Drain liquid from oranges,
reserving liquid  for other uses.   Add orange slices to salad; toss
salad well so all ingredients are well mixed.  Garnish top of salad
with toasted almonds. Makes 4 servings = 1/4 recipe 1 serving. Food
Exchanges per serving: 1 VEGETABLE EXCHANGE + 1 FRUIT EXCHANGE + 1
1/2 FAT EXCHANGES; CAL: 154; FAT: 8g; FAT: 1g; CHO: 0mg; SOD: 454mg;
CAR: 18g; SUGARS: 9g; PRO: 6g; Source: Diabetes Forecast Magazine,
