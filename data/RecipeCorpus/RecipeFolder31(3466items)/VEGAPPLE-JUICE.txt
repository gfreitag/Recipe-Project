Title: VEGAPPLE JUICE
Categories: Beverages, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
4    Apples
4    Carrots
2    Parsnips
2    Yellow beets
1 c  Apple juice
Peel and core apples.  Peel and coarsely chop carrots, parsnips and
beets.

Place all ingredients in a blender or a food processor and process
until smooth.  (Add more apple juice if thinner consistency is
desired.)

Serve chilled, with ice and a sprig of mint in each glass.

Origin:  Treehouse Family Magazine, May/ June 1995
