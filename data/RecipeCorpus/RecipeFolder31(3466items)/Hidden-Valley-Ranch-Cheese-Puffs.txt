Title: Hidden Valley Ranch Cheese Puffs
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 10 slices
2 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
3/4 c  Mayonaise
1 tb Hidden Valley Ranch dressing
-mix
10 sl French bread; 1" thick
Mix the first three ingredients.  Spread mixture on bread slices.
Broil until puffed and golden.  (About 3 or 4 minutes) Source: Hidden
Valley ad Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   06-06-95
