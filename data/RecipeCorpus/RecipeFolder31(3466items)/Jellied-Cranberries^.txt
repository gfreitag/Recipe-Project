Title: Jellied Cranberries^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb Granulated gelatin
1/4 c  Cold water
1 c  Boiling water
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1/8 ts Salt
2/3 c  Celery, cut fine
1 c  Sweet cranberry sauce
Let gelatin stand in cold water till soft. Add boiling water, stir
till dissolved. Add lemon juice and salt; chill till thickened but
not set. Fold in celery and sauce. Dip custard cups in cold water,
pour in mixture. Chill to firm; unmold onto lettuce leaf; serve with
mayonnaise. good with chicken.
