Title: Tijuana Munch
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 12 servings
16 oz Van Camp's Pork and Beans
-- undrained, mashed
1/2 lb Pasteurized process cheese
-spread w/jalapeno peppers,
-- cubed
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
1 md Tomato; chopped
Taco or corn chips
Combine pork and beans, cheese spread and green onions; heat until
cheese spread is melted.  Stir in tomato.  Garnish with additional
green onions, if desired.  Serve warm with chips.

TWELVE 1/4 CUP SERVINGS

MICROWAVE DIRECTIONS: In 1-1/2-qt. microwaveable casserole, combine
pork and beans, cheese spread and green onions.  Cover with wax
paper; microwave at HIGH 4 to 5 minutes or until cheese spread is
melted, stirring after 2 minutes.  Stir in tomato.  Garnish with
additional green onions, if desired.  Serve warm with chips.

NUTRITIONAL ANALYSIS per serving: * calories 106 * carbohydrates 7 g *
protein 6 g * fat 6 g * calcium 133 mg * sodium 440 mg * cholesterol
20 mg * dietary fiber 3 g
