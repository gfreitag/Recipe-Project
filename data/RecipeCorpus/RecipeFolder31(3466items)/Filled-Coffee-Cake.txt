Title: Filled Coffee Cake
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 9 servings
MMMMM----------------------------CAKE---------------------------------
1/4 c  Butter
1/2 c  Granulated Sugar
2    Eggs -- separated
1 1/2 c  All-Purpose Flour
2 ts Baking Powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Milk
1 ts Vanilla Extract
Confectioner's Sugar
MMMMM--------------------------FILLING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Brown Sugar
1 tb Flour
1 ts Ground Cinnamon
1 tb Melted Butter
1/2 c  Nut Meats -- chopped
Preheat oven to 350 F.

For the cake: Cream butter, add sugar, and beat in egg yolks. Sift
together flour, baking powder, and salt, and add to yolk mixture,
alternating with milk. Add vanilla. Beat egg whites unitl stiff and
fold into batter.

For the filling: Combine all ingredients. Pour 2/3 of the batter into
a buttered 8-inch square pan. Spread on the filling, and pour the
rest of the batter over this. Bake for about 40 mins or until golden
on top and firm in the middle. Cool and sprinkle with powdered sugar.
Cut into squares and serve warm with butter.

Yield: 9 squares.
