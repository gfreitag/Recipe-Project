Title: Summer Lemonade
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 servings
4    Lemons
4 1/2 c  Water
1 1/2 c  Sugar
Squeeze juice from lemons.  Put juice in 1 1/2 quart pitcher. Add a
few lemon rinds.  Add remaining ingredients and ice as desired.
