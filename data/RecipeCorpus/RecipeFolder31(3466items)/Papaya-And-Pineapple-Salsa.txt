Title: Papaya And Pineapple Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
Siebergartner
1 sm Pineapple; peel, core --
1/2    " dice
1 ts Salt
1    Ripe papaya -- 1/2" dice
1 sm Red onion -- dice
1    Jalapeno; seed -- mince
1    Lime -- juice only
1 c  Cilantro -- chop
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
Place the pineapple chunks in a colander and sprinkle with the salt
and toss well. Allow to drain for 15-20 minutes or so, then press the
pineapple against the side of the colander to extract additional
juice. Discard juices. In a glass bowl, combine all ingredients and
mix well. Set aside at room temperature for at least 3 hours. Serve
at room temp with grilled fish.
