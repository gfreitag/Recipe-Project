Title: Amerigos' Primavera Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A
1/4 c  Butter
1 sm Onion -- chop
1    Carrot -- dice
3 oz Mushrooms -- slice
1/4 c  Broccoli florets
1/4 lb Cauliflower florest
3 c  Light cream
Pasta -- cook al dente
Parmesan -- grate
In a sauce pan heat butter. Saute onion, carrot, mushrooms and simmer
3 minutes. Add broccoli and caulflower; saute for 1 minute, then add
cream. Simmer for 5 minutes. Pour onto pasta and add parmigiano.
Source: Tony Cortese, Americo's Restaurant, NYC.
