Title: Cola Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
2    Sticks Oleo -- (margarine)
1 c  Coke -- (cola)
3 tb Cocoa
1/2 c  Buttermilk or sour milk
2    Eggs -- beaten
1 ts Baking Soda
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Oleo -- (margarine)
3 tb Cocoa
6 tb Coke
1 lb Powdered sugar -- (3 1/2 C
Sifted)
1 c  Chopped nuts
Preheat oven to 350~. In a large bowl combine flour and sugar. In a
saucepan heat oleo and coke; pour over flour mixture mixing well.
(use same pan for frosting)  Add buttermilk or sour milk, beaten
eggs, soda, vanilla and marshmallows, beating until combined.
(marshmallows will float to the top but will melt during cooking)
Pour into a 10 x 15 or 9 x 13-inch pan. Bake at 350~ for 35 minutes.

Frosting:  Start 10 minutes before cake is done. In the same pan heat
to boiling oleo, cocoa and coke. Remove from heat and add powdered
sugar beating with an electric mixer until well combined. Stir in
chopped nuts and spread on hot cake.
