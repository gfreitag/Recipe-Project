Title: Spam Lumpia
Categories: Spam
Yield: 2 dozen
1 c  Spam, chopped
1 ea Clove garlic, chopped
2 tb Oil
1 md Onion, chopped fine
1 c  Shrimp, chopped course
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Garbanzo beans, canned
1/2 c  Bean sprouts
1/2 c  Carrots, slivered
24    Lumpia wrappers
Saute garlic in oil until brown, then discard. In same pan saute
onions until soft. Add Spam, shrimp and 1/2 cup water. Simmer for
about 10 min or until meat is throughly cooked. Add beans, bean
sprouts and carrots. Cook for about 5 min. more until tender. If
liquid remains, drain from mixture. Turn onto a large platter to
cool. To make rolls, spread the wrapper with one corner towards you.
Place 2 or 3 tablespoons of mixture in the center. Fold bottem corner
over filling, then fold top corner down. Fold one side over, and roll
towards other corner. Moisten with egg white, or milk to seal top.
Fry in hot oil, turning until golder brown. Serve with sweet and sour
sauce Submitted By LISA BUTLER   On   05-30-95
