Title: Tangelo Chicken
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
4    Chicken breasts skin removed
2/3 c  Tangelo juice
1 tb Honey
1 tb Soy sauce
1 ts Grated ginger.
Combine marinade ingredients in a shallow dish. Add chicken and
marinade for several hours. Grill chicken until tender brushing with
marinade. Serve with a tossed salad.
