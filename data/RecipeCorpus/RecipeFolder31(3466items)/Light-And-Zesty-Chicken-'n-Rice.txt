Title: Light And Zesty Chicken 'n Rice
Categories: Main dish, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
4 ea Chicken Breast Halves
1/3 c  Italian Salad Dressing, no
-salt
2/3 c  Uncooked Reguar Rice
1 pk Broccoli, Carrots & Water
Chestnuts -- frozen, 1 lb
-pkg
1 cn French Fried Onions -- 2.6
-ounce
1 3/4 c  Low Sodium Chicken Broth
1/2 ts Italian Seasoning
Place chicken breasts in 8"x12" baking dish.  Pour salad dressing over
chicken.  Bake uncovered at 400 degrees for 20 minutes.  Pour off
liquid. Place rice, vegetables and 1/2 can french fried onions around
and under chicken.  Combine chicken broth and Italian dressing.  Heat
in micro-wave 3 minutes and pour over chicken and vegetables.  Bake
uncovered 25 minutes. Top with remaining onions; bake for 2-3 minutes
longer.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.
