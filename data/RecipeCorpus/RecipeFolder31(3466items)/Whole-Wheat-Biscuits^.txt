Title: Whole Wheat Biscuits^
Categories: Breads, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Whole wheat flour
4 ts Baking powder
1 sm Egg
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Shortening
1 c  Milk, about
Sift flour, salt and baking powder together. rub in shortening; add
egg and milk and mix to a light dough. roll out on a floured board,
cut into biscuits and bake about 15 minutes in a hot oven, about 425
degrees.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
