Title: Fiesta Southwestern Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  ReaLime Lime Juice from
Concentrate or ReaLemon
Lemon Juice from
Concentrate
2 md Tomatoes; seeded -- chopped
(about 2 cups)
15 oz Can black beans -- drained
Rinsed
1/2 c  White onion -- chopped
1/4 c  Cilantro -- chopped
1 tb To 2 T jalapeno pepper --
Chop
1    Clove garlic -- finely
Chopped
1 md Avocado; seeded; peeled
Chopped -- optional
In a medium bowl, combine all ingredients except avocado. Chill 4
hours or overnight.  Just before serving toss avocado with mixture,
if desired. Serve with LaFamous Tortilla Chips. Refrigerate
leftovers. Scource: Card Tearout from Magazine
