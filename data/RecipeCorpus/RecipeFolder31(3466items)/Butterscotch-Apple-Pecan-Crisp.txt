Title: Butterscotch Apple Pecan Crisp
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 10 servings
2 1/2 lb Apples;(tart)peeled & diced
12 oz Butterscotch chips
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Flour
1/2 ts Cinnamon
MMMMM--------------------------TOPPING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Flour
1/4 c  Brown sugar
1/4 c  Margarine
1 c  Pecans; chopped
3/4 c  Oats
Place apples in 13 x 9 baking pan.  In small bowl, combine
butterscotch morsels, brown sugar, flour and cinnamon; sprinkle over
apples. Bake in preheated 375 oven for 20 minutes. In medium bowl,
combine flour and brown sugar; cut in butter until crumbly.  Stir in
pecans and oats; sprinkle over apple mixture.  Bake for 30 to 40
minutes until apples are tender. Cool slightly.  Serve with ice cream
or cool whip.  Source: Nestle Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   06-10-95
