Title: Turkey Crunch Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Poultry
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
3 c  Diced, cooked turkey
2    Hard cooked eggs, chop
4 oz Sliced mushrooms
3/4 c  Diced celery
1/2 c  Slivered, blanched almonds
1 tb Chopped onion
3/4 c  Mayonnaise
Mix turkey, eggs, mushrooms, celery, almonds, and onion.  Stir soup
into mayonnaise and toss with turkey mixture.  Turn into a 2 quart
casserole. Sprinkle with chow mein noodles.  Bake at 350 degrees for
30 minutes. Randy Rigg
