Title: Steamer Almond Brussels Sprouts
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
10 oz Pkg brussels sprouts; frozen
1/4 c  Almonds; slivered
1 tb Butter or margarine
1/2 ts Chicken bouillon granules
Place brussels sprouts on steamer basket. Add 1 cup water to
reservoir, cover and steam for 19 to 22 minutes.

Place steamed brussels sprouts in rice bowl with remaining
ingredients. Add 1/4 cup water to reservoir, cover and steam 6 to 7
minutes

Serves 3 to 4

Total steaming time: 25 to 29 minutes.

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker booklet Typos By
