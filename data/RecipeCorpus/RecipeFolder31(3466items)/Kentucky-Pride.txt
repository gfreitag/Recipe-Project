Title: Kentucky Pride
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1 md Onion -- chunked
1/4 c  Bourbon
2 tb Brown Sugar
2 tb Ground Black Pepper
1 tb Canola Oil
Combine the paste ingredients in a food processor or blender and
process until the onion is finely chopped and a thick puree forms.
Refrigerate the paste, covered, for up to 2 weeks.
