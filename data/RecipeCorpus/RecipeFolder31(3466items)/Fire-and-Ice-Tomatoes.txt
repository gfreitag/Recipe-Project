Title: Fire and Ice Tomatoes
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish, Tomatoes
Yield: 4 servings
3/4 c  Vinegar
1 1/2 ts Celery salt
1 1/2 ts Mustard seed
1/2 ts Sugar
1/8 ts Red ground pepper
1/8 ts Black ground pepper
2 ts Salt (or 1 tsp salt and 1
Tsp of garlic salt)
1/4 c  Cold water
6    Large tomatoes
1    Large green pepper
1    Large red onion sliced in
Rings
Bring ingredients to a boil for one minute. While hot pour over the
tomatoes ( peeled and quartered), green pepper (sliced in strips),
and the red onion (sliced in rings). Cover and chill overnight. Add 1
sliced cucumber before serving. (OPTIONAL: sliced celery can also be
added) From: Stewart Hopper ~--
