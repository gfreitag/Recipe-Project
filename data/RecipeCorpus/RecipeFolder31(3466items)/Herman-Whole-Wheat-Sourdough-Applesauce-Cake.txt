Title: Herman Whole-Wheat Sourdough Applesauce Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Brown sugar, packed
1/3 c  Honey
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1/3 c  Shortening
1 1/2 ts Salt
2    Eggs
1 c  Herman sourdough starter
-see recipe
1 1/2 c  Applesauce
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Rum extract
1 1/3 c  All-purpose flour -- white
1/2 c  Whole wheat flour
3/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground allspice
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 c  Chopped walnuts -- optional
1 c  Raisins -- optional
Cream together the sugars, honey, butter, shortening and salt until
light and fluffly.  Add eggs, one at a time, mixing thoroughly.  Add
Herman starter, applesauce, vanilla and rum flavoring. In another
bowl, combine flours, spices, baking soda and baking powder. Mix
thoroughly with a wire whisk.  Fold into creamed mixture.  Fold in
chopped walnuts and raisins if using them.  Pour into a greased and
floured 10-inch tube pan or use a bundt pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for
75-80 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out
clean. Cool in pan a few minutes before removing.
