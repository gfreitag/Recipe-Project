Title: Kish
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
6    Eggs, beaten up
1 lb Sharp Cheddar cheese, shred
3 ds Louisiana hot sauce
Sliced black olives
Mix beaten eggs and shredded cheese and hot sauce together.  Pour
into 2 (9 inch) greased pie pans and top with sliced olives.  Bake at
350 degrees until cheese melts and eggs are done. Randy Rigg
