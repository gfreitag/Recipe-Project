Title: Wheat Free Pie Crust
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 pie crust
1 c  AM Barley Flour
-OR- other flour
1/4 c  Oil; preferably cold
3 tb Cold water
Mix all ingredients well.  Press into pie plate with fingers.  Prick
with fork.  For precooked crust, bake at 350 F. for 20 to 25 minutes.
