Title: Salmon Cuke Salad^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1    Head lettuce
2    Cucumbers, sliced
1    Onion, minced
Salt & pepper
1 c  Salmon, shredded
Boiled dressing
Break apart lettuce, wash and dry leaves and place on salad plates.
Put on cucumber slices, minced onion, slat and pepper. Beat together
salmon and boiled dressing; pour over vegetables.
