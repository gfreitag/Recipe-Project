Title: EASY BARBECUED VEGETABLE PATTIES
Categories: Bbq/grill, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Red lentils
3 c  Chicken stock,
Or vegetable stock or juice
1/4 c  Onions; finely diced
1/4 c  Celery; finely diced
1/4 c  Beets; finely diced
2 tb Garlic; finely chopped
3 tb Olive oil
1/2 c  Breadcrumbs; fresh
1/4 c  Flour
1 tb Dry mustard
2 tb Rosemary & cilantro; fresh
--chopped
2 ts Chili powder
Salt & pepper to taste
Tomatoes for garnish; chop
Cucumbers for garnish; chop
Cook lentils in stock until soft, approsimately 15 minutes.

Saute onions, celery, beets and garlic in oil until onions are
transparent, about 5 minutes.

Add lentil mixture, breadcrumbs, flour, mustard, herbs and spices to
vegetables and continue to cook until a dough is formed (about 5
minutes more).  Add more liquid if dough is too dry, or flour if
dough is too moist.

Form mixture into 8 patties and refrigerate for 1/2  hour.

Grill patties on barbecue over medium heat for approximately 8
minutes. Top with chopped tomatoes and cucumbers.

TIP:  Glaze patties with your favourite barbecue sauce while cooking,
or
add chopped chilies for a spicier version.
