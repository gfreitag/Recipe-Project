Title: Tomato-Basil Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Sherry vinegar
2 tb Dijon mustard
1 tb Red wine vinegar
1 tb Walnut oil
2 c  Tomatoes -- unpeeled and
Diced
1 c  Fresh basil -- chopped
2    Cloves garlic -- crushed
Combine first 4 ingredients in a bowl, stirring with a wire whisk. Add
remaining ingredients; stir gently.
