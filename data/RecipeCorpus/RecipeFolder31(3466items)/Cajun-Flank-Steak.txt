Title: Cajun Flank Steak
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Cajun/creol
Yield: 6 servings
2 ts Dried Basil
X salt to taste
1 tb Freshly Ground Black Pepper
1 ts Cayenne Pepper
2 ts Dried Thyme
2 ts Paprika
2 ea Garlic Cloves; crished
1 tb Dijon Mustard
1/4 c  Oil
1 1/2 lb Flank Steak
CAJUN FLANK STEAK

Flank steak is an inexpensive, low-fat cut that needs to be carved
against the grain for maximum tenderness. Homemade Cajun spices add
zip.

1. Whisk all ingredients together an dpour over steak. 2. Marinate 3
hours. 3. Broil or grill 4 to 5 minutes per side or until medium
rare. 4. Slice against grain into thin slices.
