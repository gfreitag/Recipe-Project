Title: Grape Juice`
Categories: Beverages, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Purple Grapes
1/2 c  Sugar
Boiling Water -- to cover
Combine the grapes and sugar in a quart jar. Cover with the boiling
water and process, covered, for 30 mins in a boiling-water bath. The
grapes can be left in for drinking or strained out--it's delicious
either way. Makes 1 quart.
