Title: Brenda Langton's Apple Or Pear Sauce~
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 4 servings
4 md Apples or pears -- peeled,
Cored and
Cut in 1-inch chunks (8
Cups)
1/2 c  Apple juice
2 tb Fresh lemon juice --
(optional)
1 tb Honey
1/4 ts Ground cinnamon
1/4 c  White wine -- (optional)
1.  Bring fruit and apple juice to a boil in a medium-size saucepan.
Reduce heat and simmer, covered, 20 to 25 minutes until fruit is very
soft.

2.  Add lemon juice, honey and cinnamon.  Puree fruit mixture in a
blender or food processor until smooth.

3.  If using wine, return sauce to saucepan and add wine.  Simmer for
3 to 5 minutes.  Serve warm or at room temperature.

Per 1/2 cup serving:  155 cal, 0 g pro, 40 g car, 1 g fat, 0 mg chol,
1 mg sod.
