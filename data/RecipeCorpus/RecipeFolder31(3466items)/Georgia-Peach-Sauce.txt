Title: Georgia Peach Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce, Meats, Game
Yield: 1 cup
4    Or 5 peaches, like Freestone
2 ts Lemon juice
2    Cinnamon sticks
10    Whole cloves
1 ts Sugar
Pinch of salt
Peel and slice the peaches and place in a nonreactive saucepan.  Add t
remaining ingredients and 1/2 cup of water.  Cook the suace over low
h for 15 mintues, stirring occasionally, Serve hot or at room temp.
Goo with ham, pork roast, or roast duck. Recipe from "The Melting Pot"
