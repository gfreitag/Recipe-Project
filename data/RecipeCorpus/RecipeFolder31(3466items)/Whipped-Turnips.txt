Title: Whipped Turnips
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 3 servings
4 c  Mashed cooked turnips
2 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/2 c  Melted butter
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
4    Eggs, slightly beaten
Combine turnips with bread crumbs.  Blend in remaining ingredients.
Place in a greased 2 qt. casserole and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
15 minutes or until set. Randy Rigg
