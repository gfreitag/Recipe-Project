Title: Coconut Chicken With Fresh Fruit
Categories: Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 8 servings
8 ea Boneless skinless chicken
-breast halves
2 tb Butter or margarine
1 tb Fresh ginger root --
-chopped
Salt -- to taste
1/3 c  Flaked coconut, toasted &
-divided
1 c  Whipping cream
3 ea Bananas -- quartered
1 lg Papaya, peeled, seeded &
-sliced
Melt butter in large skillet over medium-high heat; add chicken and
cook 4 minutes on each side or until lightly browned.  Sprinkle with
ginger, salt, and 3 T coconut; add whipping cream.  Cover and cook
over low heat 10 minutes.  Arrange chicken, bananas and papaya on a
large platter.  Pour warm sauce over chicken and sprinkle with
remaining coconut.  Garnish with lime wedges and Italian parsley.

NUTRITIONAL INFO:  294 cal; 102 mg sodium; 513 mg potassium.
