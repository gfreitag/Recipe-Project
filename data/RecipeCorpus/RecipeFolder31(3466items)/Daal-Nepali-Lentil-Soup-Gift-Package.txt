Title: Daal-Nepali Lentil Soup Gift Package
Categories: Soup/stew, Holiday
Yield: 1 gift pack
1-3 decorative containers or
Plastic bags
1 c  Orange lentils
2 ts Curry powder
1 ts Ground ginger
1 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Cumin
1 c  Basmati or texmati rice
Place lentils in decorative container.  In a small bowl, combine
spices well and place them in a jar or small plastic bag.  Seal
tightly.  Place the rice in another decorative jar or plastic bag.
Or, slide seasoning packet and rice packet into jar with lentils.
