Title: Arnaud Remoulade Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Prepared horseradish
1/4 c  Creole mustard
1/2 c  Tarragon vinegar
2 tb Catsup
1 tb Paprika
1 ts Salt
1 lg Clove Garlic
1 c  Salad oil
1/3 c  Green onions -- sliced
1/2 c  Celery -- sliced
1/2 ts Cayenne pepper
Place all ingredients in blender or processor; puree. Pour sauce over
boiled, peeled shrimp and allow to marinate at least 4 hours. Source:
Arnaud's Restaurant NOLA
