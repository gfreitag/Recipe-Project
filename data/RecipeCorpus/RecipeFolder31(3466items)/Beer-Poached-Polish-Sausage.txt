Title: Beer-Poached Polish Sausage
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill, Sausage
Yield: 4 servings
12 oz Beer
Kiebasa sausage, 1 1/4 lbs.
Vegetable oil
Juice of 1 lemon
Preheat the grill.  Place the beer in a skillet large enough to hold t
sausage.  Heat to boiling; reduce heat.  Prick the sausage and gently
poach it in the beer 4 minutes per side.  Drian.  If using presoaked
w chips or chunks or other flavorings, sprinkle them over the hot
coal o rock of a gas grill.  Brush the grid lightly with oil.  Brush
the saus lightly with oil. Grill over medium-hot heat for 5 mintues
per side. serve: spilt the sausage down the center or cut into thick
wedges. Sprinkle with lemon juice before serving.  Recipe from
"Cooking with F and Smoke". Submitted By TANANA REYNOLDS   On
05-23-95
