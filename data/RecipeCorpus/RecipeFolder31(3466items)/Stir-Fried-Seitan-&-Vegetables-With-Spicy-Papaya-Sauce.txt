Title: Stir-Fried Seitan & Vegetables With Spicy Papaya Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Wok, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM--------------------------STIR FRY-------------------------------
1 tb ;oil
1 lb Pork-style seitan; sliced
-into strips
4 md Zucchini or yellow summer
-squash; sliced
1 1/2 lb Snow peas; tips & strings
-removed
1/2 lb Carrots; thinly sliced
1 md Onion; diced
1/2    Green cabbage; diced -OR-
1    Head broccoli; cut into
-florettes
1    Red or green pepper; diced
MMMMM------------------------PAPAYA SAUCE-----------------------------
1 ts Dried coriander
1 ts Dried ginger or 1 inch cube
Fresh ginger; chopped
1 ts Chili powder or 1 fresh
Chili; chopped
1/2 md Onion; finely chopped
1 tb ;oil
2    Papayas; diced -OR-
4 c  Mexican papaya or honeydew
1/4 c  Brown sugar or honey
(optional)
1 lg Potato; white or sweet;
-cooked
2 c  ;water
Stir fry: Heat oil in a wok or frying pan.  Add onion and carrots and
stir fry until crisp-tender.  Then add the other vegetables and stir
fry until crisp-tender.  Add seitan last.

Place into a serving dish and pour hot sauce over the top.  Serve
immediately.

Papaya Sauce: Lightly saute' the spices and onion in oil.  Blend
remaining ingredients in blender until smooth.  Add to the pan and
simmer gently for 20 minutes.

Adapted from _Eating In: From Field to Kitchen in Biosphere 2_ by
Sally Silverstone.  The Biosphere Press.
