Title: Apple Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
3    Red delicious apples
2    Bananas
1/2 pk Pitted dates
1 cn Mandarin oranges, drained
1/2 lb Miniature marshmallows
Nuts, chopped
1 sm Container of sour cream
2 tb Sugar
1 md Size container of Cool Whip
Mix together and refrigerate. Randy Rigg
