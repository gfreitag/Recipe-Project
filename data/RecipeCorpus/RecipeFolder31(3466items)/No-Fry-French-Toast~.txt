Title: No-Fry French Toast~
Categories: Breakfast, Dips/spread
Yield: 4 servings
Nonstick spray coating
1    Egg -- sligthly beaten
1    Egg white -- slightly
Beaten
3/4 c  Skim milk
1 ts Vanilla
8 sl French bread -- 1/2" thick
MMMMM------------------------ORANGE SYRUP-----------------------------
1/4 ts Orange peel -- finely
Shredded
2/3 c  Orange juice
1 tb Honey
1 1/2 ts Cornstarch
1/8 ts Ground cinnamon
Cooking Time:  11 min.
Preparation Time:  10 min.

Spray a large baking sheet with nonstick spray. In a shallow bowl
combine egg, egg white, milk and vanilla. Dip bread slices in egg
mixture just long enough to coat both sides. Place on baking sheet.
Bake in a 450~ oven about 6 minutes or till bread is lightly browned.
Turn bread over and bake 5 to 8 minutes more or till golden.

ORANGE SYRUP:  In a small saucepan stir together orange peel, orange
jucie, honey, cornstarch, and cinnamon. Cook and stir till thickened
and bubbly. Cook and stir 2 minutes more. Serve toast with warm
orange syrup. Serves 4.

Per serving:  282 calories; 11g protein; 51g carbohydrate; 4g fat;
72mg cholesterol, 460mg sodium; 248mg potassium.
