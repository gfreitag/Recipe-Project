Title: Balsamic Vinegar Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Cider vinegar
1    Onion -- thinly sliced
3 tb Rosemary -- fresh
1 tb Italian parsley -- chopped
Juice of 1/2 lemon
1/2 c  Olive oil
2/3 c  Balsamic vinegar
1 tb Red peppercorns
Cook onion in cider vinegar until soft.  Remove from heat and add
remaining ingredients, mixing well.
