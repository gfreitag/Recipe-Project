Title: Tortellini Salad
Categories: Main dish, Salads, Pasta, Vegetables, Italian
Yield: 4 servings
4 c  Tortellini, cooked & cooled
3/4 c  Ripe Olives, sliced
1 c  Sauteed Mushrooms, sliced
1 tb Dried Toamtoes, minced
4 c  Broccoli, cooked & cooled
3    Green Onions, sliced
3 tb Fresh Basil, chopped
Pepper
3/4 c  Marinated Artichoke Hearts
2 ts Lemon Juice
Olive Oil
Combine all ingredients except artichoke marinade, lemon juice, and
olive oil. Combine lemon juice with artichoke marinade and add enough
olive oil to make 1/2 a cup. Drizzle marinade over salad. Toss
gently. Cover tightly and refrigerate overnight.
