Title: Fresh Tomato Fry^
Categories: Tomatoes, Tasteofhome, Depress/era
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1    Egg, beaten
1/3 c  Milk
6    Ripe firm tomatoes
Salt & pepper
Sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Add beaten egg; mix well.
Slice tomatoes rather thickly, sprinkle with salt and pepper and
drain. Dip tomato slices in batter, and fry in a hot greased skillet
until brown on both sides. Good for breakfast or lunch, served with
bacon.
