Title: Tic-tac-taco Mix
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 4 servings
1 1/4 c  Popped Popcorn
1/2 c  Bite-Sized Shredded Wheat
Biscuits
1/2 c  Round Toasted Oat Cereal
1 tb Margarine Or Butter
1/4 ts Chili Powder
ds Onion Salt; To Taste
ds Ground Cumin; To Taste
Equipment Needed: Dry Measures, Bowl, Spoon, Knife, Small Sauce Pan,
Measuring Spoons

In the bowl, combine the popcorn, wheat biscuits and oat cereal,
blending well.

In the small saucepan, stir the margarine, chili powder, onion salt,
and cumin.  Heat and stir until the margarine is melted and it is
well blended. Drizzle over the popcorn-cereal mixture and serve.
