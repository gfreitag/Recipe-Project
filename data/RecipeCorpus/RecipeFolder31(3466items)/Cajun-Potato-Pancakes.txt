Title: Cajun Potato Pancakes
Categories: Potatoes, Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
3 lg Raw white potatoes
1 tb Lemon juice
2 tb All-purpose flour
1 ts Sugar
1/2 md Onion -- grated
1 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/4 ts Ground cayenne pepper
1    Egg -- beaten
1/4 ts Salt
1 ds Black pepper
Peel and grate potatoes; toss with lemon juice and drain.  Combine
with remaining ingredients.  Measure out 1/4 to 1/3 cup mixture and
form into patties.  Place patties into hot oil in skillet; cook until
brown.  Turn and cook until brown.  Serve with eggs and favorite
breakfast meat.
