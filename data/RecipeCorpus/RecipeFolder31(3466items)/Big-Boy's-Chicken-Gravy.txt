Title: Big Boy's Chicken Gravy
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
28 oz Chicken broth
1/2 ts Onion powder
1 tb Chicken bouillon powder
2/3 c  Bisquick
In blender, combine broth, onion powder, bouillon powder and Bisquick.
Blend at high speed, about 1/2 minute or until smooth. Pour into
saucepan and stir constantly over medium high heat, about 4 or 5
minutes until if comes to a boil, is smooth and thickened a bit.
Serve at once. Can freeze up to 4 mons. Source: Gloria Pitzer's Make
Alike Recipes
