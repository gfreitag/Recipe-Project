Title: Sauerkraut #2
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
Canned Or Fresh Sauerkraut
Drain all liquid from the sauerkraut.  Barely cover with boiling
water, then cook, uncovered, 30 minutes or longer if desired.  Drain.
(Serve the drained liquid from the cooking as a juice cocktail.)  To
the hot sauerkraut, add caraway or celery seeds, or a dash of cloves,
and salt, pepper and butter or margarine to taste.
