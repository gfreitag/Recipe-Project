Title: Herman Croutons
Categories: Breads, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
LISA CRAWLEY
1/2    Loaf Herman Bread
-cut into 1/3" cubes
2 tb Olive Oil
2 tb Garlic Flavored Oil
3 tb Butter
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Garlic -- minced
2 tb Chives -- minced
2 tb Parsley -- finely chopped
Melt butter with oils in large skillet. When butter stops
"sputtering" add the garlic, herbs and salt; cook 1 min. Add bread
cubes, tossing gently until all the butter is absorbed.

On a baking sheet spread bread cubes in a single layer. Bake. Serve
warm or at room temperature on your favorite salad. Cool completely
before storing.

OVEN TEMP: 350  TIME: 10-15 min: or til golden brown.
