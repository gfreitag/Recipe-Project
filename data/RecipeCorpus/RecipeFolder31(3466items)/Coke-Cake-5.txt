Title: Coke Cake 5
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Flour
2 c  Sugar
2    Stick butter or margarine
3 ts Cocoa
1 c  Coca-Cola
1/2 c  Buttermilk
2    Eggs, beaten
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Baking Soda
1 1/2 c  Miniature marshmallows.
_______/Coca-Cola Cake\___________________________________

(With all the soft drink recipes, I'm including this one)

Combine flour and sugar in large bowl.  Heat margarine, cocoa and
Coca-Cola until it boils (WATCH IT!).  Pour over the sugar and flour,
mixing well. Add remaining ingredients and stir until well blended.
Bake in greased 9 x 13 pan.  Bake at 350 F for 30-40 minutes.  Frost
while hot.

Coca-Cola Icing

1 Stick margarine 3Tbsp Cocoa 6Tbsp Coca-Cola 1 Box Powdered sugar 1 C
Chopped pecans

Combine margarine, cocoa, and Coca-Cola and bring to boil.  Pour over
sugar and beat well.  Add nuts and spread on hot cake.
