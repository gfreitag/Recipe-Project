Title: Baked Onions^
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome, Depress/era
Yield: 1 servings
4 md Onions
1/4 c  Butter, melted
Salt & pepper
Put onions on a tin and bake as for potatoes. When done, remove
skins, pour on butter. Sprinkle with salt and pepper and serve hot.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
