Title: Rosy Fruit Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit, Vegetables, Appetizers
Yield: 4 cups
1 c  Ripe cantaloupe
-1/4" dice
1 c  Ripe watermelon
-1/4" dice
1 c  Peeled cucumber
-1/4" dice
4    Ripe plum tomatoes, seeded
-1/4" dice
1/3 c  Red onion(s),
-finely chopped
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Flat-leaf parsley
-coarsely chopped
1 ts Jalapeo chile
-seeded and minced
Mix all ingredients in a bowl and let rest at room temperature for up
to 1 hour before serving.
