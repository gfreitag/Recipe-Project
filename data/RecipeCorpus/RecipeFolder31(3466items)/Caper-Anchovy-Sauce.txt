Title: Caper-Anchovy Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
8    Anchovy fillets -- finely
Diced
1/4 c  Capers
1/4 c  Italian parsley -- chopped
1/2 c  Olive oil
1 tb Lemon juice
Salt and pepper
Mix anchovies, capers, parsley, olive oil, lemon juice and salt and
pepper to taste in bowl.  Set aside at least 1 hour before serving.
