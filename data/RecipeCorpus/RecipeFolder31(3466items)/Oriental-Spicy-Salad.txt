Title: Oriental Spicy Salad
Categories: Salads, Asian, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1/2    Head of cabbage, chopped
4    Scallions, chopped
2 tb Sugar
1 1/2 ts Pepper
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Sesame seed oil
6 ds Chinese hot oil
1 ts Salt
6 ts White vinegar
3 pk Ramen noodles, chicken flavr
2 ts Sesame seeds
2 oz Slivered almonds (toast for
15 minutes in 300 degree
Oven)
Mix sugar, pepper, vegetable oil, sesame seed oil, Chinese oil, salt,
and vinegar in a large bowl.  Add cabbage and scallions; toss and let
marinate for 1 to 2 hours.  Squeeze packages of uncooked Ramen
noodles to crush. Add to cabbage mixture, along with the sesame seeds
and slivered almonds. Add packs of noodle seasoning to suit your
taste. Randy Rigg Submitted By ROBERT MILES   On   06-24-95
