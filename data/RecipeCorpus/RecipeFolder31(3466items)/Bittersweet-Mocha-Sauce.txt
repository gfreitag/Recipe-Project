Title: Bittersweet Mocha Sauce
Categories: Desserts, Chocolate
Yield: 1 batch
2    Squares unsweetened
Chocolate, chopped
1 tb Instant coffee  dissolved in
1/4 c  Hot water
1/2 c  Sugar
ds Salt
3/4 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Light cream
Place all ingredients in blender.  Cover and blend at high speed until
smooth, about one minute, turning blender off and scraping down sides
after 30 seconds.

Serve over pudding, cake or ice cream.  Store any leftover sauce in
refrigerator.

Typed but not tested.
