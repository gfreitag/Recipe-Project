Title: Basil Pesto
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
6 ea A garlic cloves -- sliced
1/3 c  Pine nuts
2 c  Fresh basil leaves --
Packed
3/4 c  Parmesan cheese -- freshly
Gra
1/2 c  Light olive oil
Place all ingredients in food processor bowl and pulse until you get a
homogeneous sauce. Serve over linguine or your favorite pasta.
