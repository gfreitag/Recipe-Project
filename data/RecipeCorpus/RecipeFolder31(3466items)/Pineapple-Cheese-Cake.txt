Title: Pineapple Cheese Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
20    Graham crackers
1/2 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 lb Cottage cheese
2 c  (No.1 can) drained, crushed
-pineapple
Crumble crackers fine and mix with butter and sugar, and spread in
buttered loose-bottomed cake pan.  Bake in hot oven (425 F) for 15
minutes to 20 minutes.  Cool and spread with cottage cheese and then
with pineapple: garnish with rosettes of cheese and pieces of
pineapple.
