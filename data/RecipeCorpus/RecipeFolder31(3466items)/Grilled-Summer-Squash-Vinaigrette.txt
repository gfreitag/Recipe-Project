Title: Grilled Summer Squash Vinaigrette
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  Red wine vinegar
3 tb Lemon juice
1    Clove garlic -- minced
pn Cayenne
Salt and pepper
4    Zucchini -- sliced
Lengthwise
4    Yellow squash -- sliced
Lengthwise
Whisk together the oil, vinegar, lemon juice, garlic, cayenne, salt
and pepper.  Marinate the zucchini and yellow squash in this dressing
for 10 minutes.  Grill for 2 to 4 minutes per side over a hot fire
and toss with enough dressing to moisten.  Serve half.  Reserve half
for Ziti with Grilled Vegetables.
