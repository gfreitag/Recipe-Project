Title: Tutti-Frutti Punch
Categories: Beverages, Fruit, Low cal/fat
Yield: 1 quarts
16 oz Pkg frozen strawberries,thaw
6 oz Froz pineapple juice,thaw
3 oz Froz apple juice, thaw
3 oz Froz orange juice, thaw
1 1/2 c  Water
33 oz Bottle seltzer, chilled
Combine first 4 ingredients in container of blender; procees until
smooth. Combine strawberry mixture and water in a large pitcher;
cover and chill at least 3 hours. To serve, add seltzer to juice
mixture and stir gently. Serve punch immediately. 1/2 c. serving = 37
cal 0.1 g fat Submitted By CAROL VERGE   On   06-06-95
