Title: Poinsetta Salad^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
4    Tomatoes
1 sm Onion, minced
1    Stalk celery
1/4    Green pepper
Mayonnaise
Scald peel and chill tomatoes. Cut into sections almost down to the
base, forming a flower. Mix onion, celery and green pepper, put in
center of tomatoes and cover mixture with mayonnaise.
