Title: Chinese Chicken Salad
Categories: Main dish, Chinese, Poultry, Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1    Reserved Peking Chicken
5    Scallions, white part only
-thinly sliced
1/2    Red bell pepper -- sliced
2 c  Reserved Stir Fried Broccoli
-with Garlic
1 tb Honey
3 tb Rice vinegar
1 1/2 tb Dark sesame oil
1 1/2 tb Soy sauce, low sodium
3/4 ts Hot pepper sauce
1 1/2 tb Peanut oil
Cut the reserved chicken into 1" chunks.  You should have about 5
cups. Place in a microwave dish, cover with plastic, and warm in
microwave at full power for 2 minutes.  Meanwhile toss the scallions,
bell pepper and reserved broccoli in a large salad bowl.  Mix the
honey , vinegar, sesame oil, soy sauce,  hot pepper sauce, and peanut
oil in a small bowl.  Toss the chicken and vegetables with the
dressing and serve.
