Title: Harvest Ham Balls
Categories: Main dish, Meats
Yield: 3 servings
1    Egg, beaten
1/2 c  Soft bread crumbs
1/2 c  Milk
1 tb Brown sugar
1/8 ts Ground cloves
1 lb Ground, cooked ham
8    Canned peach halves
Celery tops or parsley
Mix egg, crumbs, milk, sugar, cloves and ham; shape into 8 balls.
Drain peaches, place in baking dish, and place a ball in each.  Bake
at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.  Garnish with celery tops. Randy Rigg
