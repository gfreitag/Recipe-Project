Title: Fried Squash
Categories: Stove top, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
3 lb Squash (white)
2    Eggs (beaten)
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Bread crumbs (buttered)
Salt (to taste)
Pepper (to taste)
Peel and slice squash into thin slices.  Salt and pepper to taste. Dip
squash into mixture of eggs and milk.  Roll in bread crumbs and dip
in milk a second time.  Roll squash in bread crumbs and drop into hot
grease until brown (approximately 5 minutes). Serves 4 to 6.
