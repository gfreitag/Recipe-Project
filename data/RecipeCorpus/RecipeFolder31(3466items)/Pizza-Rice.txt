Title: Pizza Rice
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 recipe
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 md Onion; chopped
4    Garlic cloves; minced
2    Tomatoes; chopped
6    Mushrooms; sliced
2 tb Parsley; chopped
1/2 ts Oregano
1/2 ts Basil
2 c  Quick Brown Rice
4 c  Water
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese
1/4 c  Olives, sliced
-- (green or black)
Saute' vegetables and seasonings in olive oil.  Stir rice and
vegetables into a rectangular casserole dish, and pour water on top.
Bake at 400 F. for 45 minutes; remove and sprinkle with cheese and
olives; cover and return to oven for an additional 10 minutes.
