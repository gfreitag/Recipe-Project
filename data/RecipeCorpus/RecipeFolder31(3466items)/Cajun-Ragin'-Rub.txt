Title: Cajun Ragin' Rub
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Celery Salt
1/4 c  Ground Black Pepper
1/4 c  White Pepper
1/4 c  Brown Sugar
2 tb Garlic Powder
1 tb Cayenne
1 tb Dried Thyme
2 ts Dried Sage
Mix the spices throughly in a bowl.  Store in a cool, dark pantry.
