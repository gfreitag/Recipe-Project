Title: Mock Tomato Soup
Categories: Low cal/fat, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb All-purpose flour
Cold water
1 c  Tomato juice, low sodium
Make a smooth paste of 1 T flour and a little water.  Add tomato juice
slowly and mix well. Cook until thickened.  You may season with herbs
& spices to taste (no salt).  Use as substitute for condensed tomato
soup. Makes 1 cup.
