Title: Pink Cloud Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Pecans
1 cn Cherry pie filling
1 cn Unsweetened crushed
Pineapple, drained
1 cn Milnot
13 oz Cool Whip
Mix all together except Cool Whip.  Then fold in Cool Whip until well
blended.  Refrigerate. Randy Rigg
