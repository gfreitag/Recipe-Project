Title: Frizzells
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 c  Light brown sugar
2    Eggs
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Pecans
1 pk Butterscotch chips
Blend shortening and sugar, stir in eggs.  Mix flour, baking powder,
and salt and stir in sugar mixture.  Stir in vanilla and butterscotch
chips. Spread on a well greased and floured jelly roll pan.  Press
nuts into top of mixture.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.  Do
not overbake. Cool and cut into serving pieces. Randy Rigg
