Title: Spanish Squash Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Main dish, Side dish, Vegetables, Nuts/grains
Yield: 1 recipe
1 pk Spanish Quick Brown Rice
-- (uncooked)
1 md Squash (of choice)
1 md Bell pepper; chopped
1 lb Stewed tomatoes; OR...
4    -Fresh tomatoes
Mix all ingredients in an oiled 9 x 13 baking dish.  Cover tightly
with aluminum foil or tight fitting baking dish lid.  Bake for 30
minutes at 350 F. or until squash is tender.
