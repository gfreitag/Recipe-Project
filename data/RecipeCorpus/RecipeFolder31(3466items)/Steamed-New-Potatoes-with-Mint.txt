Title: Steamed New Potatoes with Mint
Categories: Potatoes, Side dish
Yield: 2 servings
8 sm New potatoes
1 tb Mint, coarsely chopped
1 1/2 ts Butter
1/2 ts Kosher salt
Steam the potatoes over simmering water until done, about 30-40
minutes. Place potatoes in a bowl and add the mint, butter and salt.
Toss until the butter has melted and serve immediately.
