Title: Chili Dipping Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Chinese
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  White vinegar
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Ginger -- minced
1    Garlic clove -- minced
1 tb Asian chili sauce
1/2 ts Sesame oil
1 1/2 ts Cilantro -- chopped
Place water, vinegar, sugar, ginger and garlic in a nonreactive
saucepan over low heat.  Cook until sugar dissolves.  Remove from
heat and add remaining ingredients.
