Title: Sweet Potato Pie~
Categories: Potatoes, Pie/tart
Yield: 8 servings
2 c  Cooked, mashed sweet potato
1/4 ts Salt
2    Eggs -- well beaten
1/2 c  Sugar
1    Stick Margarine -- melted
1 c  Milk
1 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Ground cloves
1    Unbaked Pie crust
Whipped cream or topping
Mix all ingredients in order given and pour into unbaked pie crust.
Bake 10 minutes at 400~. Turn oven to 325~ and bake 30 minutes. Serve
with whipped cream or topping.
