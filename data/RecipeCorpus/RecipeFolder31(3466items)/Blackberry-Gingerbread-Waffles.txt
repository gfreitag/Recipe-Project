Title: Blackberry Gingerbread Waffles
Categories: Breads, Pcake/waffl, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Powdered ginger
3 ts Baking powder
3 ea Eggs
4 tb Butter, melted
4 tb Molasses
1 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Blackberries
Preheat waffle iron.  In a large bowl, mix flour, baking powder,
ginger. In a separate bowl, beat eggs, butter, buttermilk, and
molasses.

Source:  King's Orchard, Plantersville, Texas
