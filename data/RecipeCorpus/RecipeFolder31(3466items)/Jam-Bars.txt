Title: Jam Bars
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
1/2 ts Almond Extract
1    Egg
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking Powder
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Cloves
1/2 ts Salt
Mix everything except the jam together. Spread 1/2 of the dough in a
greased 8" square pan. Cover with jam, pour on the rest of the pastry
and bake at 400 degrees for 25 minutes.
