Title: Mustard Topper`
Categories: Breakfast, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Sour Cream
2 tb Mustard, Prepared
1 tb Lemon Juice
1 tb Minced Onion
1 ts Salt
1 ts Worcestershire Sauce
4 dr Hot Pepper Sauce
Combine all ingredients; blend well. Makes about 1 cup.
