Title: Primo Paste
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1    Whole Garlic Bulbs --
-peeled
6 tb Black Pepper --
-coarse-ground
6 tb Kosher Salt
1/4 ts Cayenne
6 tb Garlic-Flavored Olive Oil
Use a food processor combine the paste ingredients, mashing the
garlic with the pepper, salt, and cayenne.  Add the oil to form a
thick paste. Refrigerate the paste covered, for up to 2 weeks.
