Title: Tuna Sauce
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Salsa/sauce, Condiments
Yield: 10 servings
75 ml Olive oil
50 g  Garlic
75 g  Onion
700 g  Canned or fresh tuna
1    Glass white wine
400 ml Tomato sauce
50 g  Parsley
Salt and peppper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Fry garlic, onion with oil. Add the tuna and white wine, fry for 5
mins. Add tomato sauce and parsley, cook for 20 mins. Source: Given
to share with the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
