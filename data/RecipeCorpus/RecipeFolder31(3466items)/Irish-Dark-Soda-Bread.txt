Title: Irish Dark Soda Bread
Categories: Breads, Irish
Yield: 2 loaves
3 c  All purpose flour
2 c  Whole-wheat flour
2 ts Baking soda
1 tb Baking powder
2 tb Brown sugar
2 1/4 c  Buttermilk
Proceed as in recipe for Irish Soda Bread, beging very careful to
brea any lumps of brown sugar. Divide the dough into 2 loaves and
bake at 4 F. for 45 minutes, or until brown and crunchy. Cool on
racks. This one great with kids.  Recipe from the Frugal Gourmet.
