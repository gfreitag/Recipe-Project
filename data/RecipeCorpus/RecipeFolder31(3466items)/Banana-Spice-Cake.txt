Title: Banana Spice Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
2 1/4    Sifted cake flour
1 1/4 c  Sugar
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cloves
1 1/4 ts Cinnamon
1/2 ts Nutmeg
Add and beat 2 minutes
1/2 c  Shortening
1/2 c  Mashed bananas (1/3 - 1/2 C)
2    Eggs
Add and 1 minute
1 c  Mashed bananas
1 ts Vanilla
Bake in 2 - 8 inch round
-pans
375    For 25 minutes
Sift in large bowl:

Frost with Banana Butter Frosting
