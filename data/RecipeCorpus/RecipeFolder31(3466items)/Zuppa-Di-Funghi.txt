Title: Zuppa Di Funghi
Categories: Italian, Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 tb Margarine
2 lg Onions -- chopped
1 lb Mushrooms -- chopped
1/2 ts Salt
2    Cloves Garlic -- minced
3 c  Chicken Broth
1 c  Water
1 ts Italian Seasoning
3 tb Yogurt
Heat the margarine in a saucepan. Add onions and saute until golden.
Add mushrooms, salt, and garlic and cook for 3 mins, stirring
occasionally. Add broth and water and cook for 30 mins, stirring
occasionally. Before serving place 1/2 Tbs. yogurt in each soup
plate, pour soup over it and mix.
