Title: Walnut Wafers
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Flour
2 c  Brown sugar
1/2 ts Baking powder
1    Egg
1/2 ts Vanilla
1 c  Black walnuts, cut fine
pn Salt
Mix dry ingredients, add egg and beat, add vanilla and walnuts and mix
lightly.  Drop 2 inches apart on a greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350
degrees for about 8 minutes. Randy Rigg
