Title: Cream Sauce & Cream Soup Base
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Cashews
3 c  Water
1 tb Chicken-style seasoning
1 ts Onion powder
1/8 ts Garlic
2 tb Cornstarch
2 tb White flour
Blend, heat, stirring until smooth.   This sauce may be used as is
for a substitute for canned mushroom soup in recipes. Cream Soups:
Add any steamed vegetable you desire to the cream sauce, dilute the
sauce with water, and adjust seasonings. Chick Pea A-La-King:  Add
sauteed onions and mushrooms, green peas, pimentos, and garbanzo
beans (cooked), to cooked cream sauce.  Serve over noodles, biscuits,
or toast.
