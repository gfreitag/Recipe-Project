Title: Southern Sop
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 7 servings
4 c  Cider Vinegar
2 c  Water
6 tb Black Pepper -- ground
4 tb Salt
2 tb Worcestershire Sauce
2 tb Paprika
2 tb Cayenne
Combine the ingredients in a saucepan. Heat the mop and use it warm.
