Title: Cheese Piquantes
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1/4 c  Butter
2 dr Tabasco sauce
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Paprika
5 c  Rice cereal
3/4 c  Grated dry cheese
Melt butter in a large shallow pan.  Add seasonings.  Stir in cereal,
add grated cheese and mix.  Place in a 350 degree oven for 15
minutes, stirring occasionally.  Eat like popcorn.  You can use Rice
Chex, Rice Krispies or Puffed Rice cereals. Randy Rigg
