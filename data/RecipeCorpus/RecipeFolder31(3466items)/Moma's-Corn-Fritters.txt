Title: Moma's Corn Fritters
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Baking powder
3/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Paprika
2 c  Cut cooked corn
2    Eggs, seperated
Sift flour, baking powder, salt and paprika together. Add corn and
beaten egg yolks; fold in stiffly beaten egg whites. Fry in hot deep
fat (350F) until browned. Drain on absorbent paper. Serves 6 to 8.
