Title: Calzone
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian
Yield: 15 servings
2 1/2 kg Pizza dough
2 kg Mozzarella
1 1/2    Litres tomato sauce
800 g  Ham sliced
15    Eggs
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Spread dough to 30 cm size. Cover 1/2 the dough with tomato sauce,
then mozzarella then ham and 1 raw egg. Sprinkle with a little salt
and pepper. Seal dough. Brush lightly with tomato sauce. Cook and
serve. Source: Given to share with the express permission of Chef de
Cuisine
