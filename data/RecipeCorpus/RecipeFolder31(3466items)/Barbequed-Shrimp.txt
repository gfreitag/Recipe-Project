Title: Barbequed Shrimp
Categories: Seafood, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
5 lb Shrimp
1    Clove Garlic
1 1/2 ts Liquid Crab Boil
2 ts Dry BBQ seasoning
2 tb Worchestershire Sauce
4 1/2    Sticks Butter ( melted)
ds Tobasco
3/4 ts Red Pepper
3 ts Black Pepper
3 tb White Wine
1 sl Lemon
Wash and dehead (optional) the shrimp and place in a baking dish.
Combine all the other ingredients, and pour over the shrimp.  Bake 30
to 40 minutes at 350 degrees, basting the shrimp occasionally.  Turn
off oven and let stand 5 minutes before serving.

Serve with plenty of crusty French bread, for dipping into the sauce.
It's messy but wonderful.
