Title: Chow Chow (New England)
Categories: Relishes, Vegetables, Can/preserv
Yield: 8 servings
18 lg Tomatoes, peeled and
-chopped fine
1 sm Head of green cabbage
-chopped
2    Green peppers, chopped
1    Red pepper, chopped
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 c  Sugar
2 ts Ground cinnamon
1 ts Ground allspice
1 ts Ground cloves
6 tb Salt
1 lg Onion, peeled and
-chopped
Combine all ingredients.  Cover tightly and simmer until vegetables
are tender.  Spoon into hot sterilized jars; seal.  Makes 8 pints.
