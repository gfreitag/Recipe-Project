Title: Apple Cinnamon Pudding
Categories: Desserts, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 recipe
2 c  Apple juice
1 c  Water
1 pn Sea salt (optional)
1 c  AM Rice and Shine
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Roasted pecan or other nuts
-- (chopped)
1 ts Vanilla
Bring juice, water and salt to a boil in a large saucepan.  Stir in
Rice and Shine and cinnamon.  Turn heat to low.  Simmer 5 minutes.
Remove from heat.  Stir in nuts and vanilla.  Let set until slightly
cool.  Pour into a 9" square pan.  Refrigerate until set and cool.
Serve squares plain or with a little fruit syrup.
