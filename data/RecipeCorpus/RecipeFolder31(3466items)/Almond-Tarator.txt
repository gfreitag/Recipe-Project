Title: Almond Tarator
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 cups
1 c  Blanched slivered almonds
1 c  Packed white breadcrumbs
3/4 c  Water (approx)
3 tb Lemon juice
2    Garlic clove(s)
1/2 c  Olive oil
Finely grind almonds in food processor. Add breadcrumbs,  cup water,
lemon juice and garlic and process until thick and smooth, about 2
minutes. With machine running, gradually add olive oil through feed
tube. Mix in enough water to make thick creamy sauce. Season to taste
with salt. Serve with fish or shellfish, cooked vegetables, or as a
dip for pita bread.
