Title: Red Onion Marmalade
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 1 cup
Vegetable cooking spray
1 lg Red onion
-thinly sliced
1    Garlic clove(s), minced
1 c  Port wine
1 c  Red wine, pref Burgundy
1/2 c  Orange juice
1/3 c  Honey
1/4 c  Balsamic vinegar
1 ts Orange zest, finely grated
1/2 ts Salt
1. Lightly spray a medium non-stick skillet with cooking spray and
heat. Add the onion and cook over moderate heat until caramelized,
about 6 minutes. Add the garlic and stir for 1 minute. Add the port
and simmer, stirring, for 10 minutes.

2. Add the red wine, orange juice, honey, vinegar, orange zest, and
salt and simmer until the liquid is reduced and the onions are very
soft, about 15 minutes. Let cool, cover and refrigerate for up to 2
days. Return to room temperature before serving. Use to top
hamburgers or as an accompaniment to grilled meat or poultry.
