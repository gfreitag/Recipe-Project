Title: Config.Caesar Dressing
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 cup
3    Cloves garlic
Juice of 1 lemon
1/4 c  Olive oil
1/2 ts Coarsely ground black pepper
1    Egg yolk
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
Dash of Worcestershire
Sauce without anchovies
Salt to taste
In food processor or blender, blend all ingredients until creamy. If
thinner consistency is desired, blend in small amount of water. F.A.T.
REDUCER: Reduce olive oil to 2 tbs. *From "Quick Bytes: Computer
Lover's Cookbook" by Diane Pfeifer* ~Typed for you by Michelle Bruce
