Title: Chinese Wonder Salad
Categories: Salads, Chinese, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 cn French style green beans
1 cn Water chestnuts, sliced
1 cn Fancy Chinese vegetables
1    Onion, sliced thin
1 c  Celery, diced
1 c  Sugar
3/4 c  White vinegar
Salt and pepper
Drain all vegetables.  Mix all ingredients together and marinate
overnight. Will keep up to 2 weeks. Randy Rigg
