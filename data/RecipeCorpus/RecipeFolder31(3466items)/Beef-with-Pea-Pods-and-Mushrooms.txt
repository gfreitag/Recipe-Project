Title: Beef with Pea Pods and Mushrooms
Categories: Meats, Chinese, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/2 lb Lean beef sl thin, sm pcs
1/2 c  Sliced mushrooms
2 c  Pea Pods
2 tb Cooking oil
1 ts Salt
MMMMM--------------------------MARINADE-------------------------------
1 tb Wine
2 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Cornstarch
Marinate beef for 5 minutes.  Heat oil.  Add beef (including
marinade) and saute over high heat until beef changes color.  Add pea
pods and mushrooms. Cook for 2 minutes stirring constantly.  Serve
hot. Submitted By CAROLYN MCCOMAS   On   06-26-95
