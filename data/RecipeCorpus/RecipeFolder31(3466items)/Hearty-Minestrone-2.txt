Title: Hearty Minestrone 2
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 lb Italian sausage, mild
1 c  Onions -- chopped
2    Cloves garlic -- crushed
2 cn Beef broth (14.5 oz)
15 oz Garbanzo beans
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 c  Sauerkraut -- drained
1/2 c  Pasta, small
1 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Pepper
2 c  Zucchini -- sliced
Brown meat, onions and garlic in 3 quart saucepan.  Drain.  Stir in
remaining ingredients except zucchini.  Bring to boil.  Reduce heat
to low. Simmer 10 minutes.  Add zucchini.  Simmer 10 minutes or until
pasta is cooked.  Serve with crusty italian bread if desired.  305
calories and 6 grams fat.
