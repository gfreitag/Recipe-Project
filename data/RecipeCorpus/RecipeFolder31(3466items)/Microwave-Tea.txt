Title: Microwave Tea
Categories: Beverages, Microwave
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Water
4    Family size tea bags (Lipton
Preferred)
Lemon -- optional
Sugar -- optional
Formula: 1 tea bag per cup of water

Using a 4-cup glass measuring cup, fill with 4 cups water and 4
family size tea bags. Micro for 8 minutes. Let steep until it cools.
Remove tea bags and pour into a gallon pitcher and fill with water to
make 1 gallon. Serve over ice cubes with lemon and sugar, if desired.
Yield: 1 Gallon This makes good tea that doesn't get cloudy.
