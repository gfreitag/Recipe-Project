Title: Shrimp Pesto Pizza
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Pizza, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 15 servings
2 1/2 kg Pizza dough
1 1/2    Litres tomato sauce
1 1/2 kg Mozzarella
1 1/2 kg Shrimp, med size, pan fried
300 ml Pesto sauce
1 kg Fresh tomato sliced
500 g  Olives black and green
200 g  Sun dried tomato
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Spread the dough, top with tomato suace and mozzarella. Combine
shrimp and pesto sauce and top the pizza. Top with fresh tomato, sun
dried tomatoes and olives. Sprinkle with mozzarella. Cook and serve.
