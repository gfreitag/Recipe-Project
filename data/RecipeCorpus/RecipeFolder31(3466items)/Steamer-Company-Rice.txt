Title: Steamer Company Rice
Categories: Nuts/grains
Yield: 3 servings
10 1/2 oz Beef consomme
4 oz Mushrooms; sliced
2 tb Butter or margarine
-cut into pieces
1/2 c  Raw long grain rice
1/3 c  Onion; chopped
Combine all ingredients in rice bowl. Add 3 cups water to reservoir,
cover and steam for 45 to 50 minutes.

Serves 3

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker booklet Typos by
Dorothy Flatman 1995
