Title: Multi Bran Chex Raisin Muffins
Categories: Muffins, Low cal/fat
Yield: 12 muffins
1 3/4 c  Multi Bran Chex
1    Egg; beaten
1 c  Buttermilk
3 tb Oil
1 c  Flour
1/2 c  Brown sugar
2 1/2 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1/3 c  Raisins
Preheat oven to 400.  Grease 12 muffin tins.  In large bowl combine
cereal, egg, buttermilk, and oil.  Let stand 5 minutes. Stir to break
up cereal. In medium bowl combine flour, sugar, baking powder and
cinnamon. Add flour mixture to cereal mixture all at once, stirring
just until moistened. Stir in raisins. Batter will be thick. Divide
evenly among muffin cups. Bake 20 to 25 minutes.  Source: Multi Bran
