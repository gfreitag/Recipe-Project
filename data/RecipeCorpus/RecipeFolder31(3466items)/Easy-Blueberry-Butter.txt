Title: Easy Blueberry Butter
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
4    Sticks sweet butter, soft
1 c  Fresh blueberries, chopped
3 tb To 5 powdered sugar
Combine all ingredients in blender.  Whip until light.  This is great
on pancakes, waffles, toast or anything else.

Source:  King's Orchard, Plantersville, Texas
