Title: Cheese Squares
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
1    Loaf of cubed bread
3 oz Cream cheese
1/4 lb Sharp Cheddar cheese
1/2 c  Butter
2    Egg whites
Melt cheeses and butter in double boiler.  Beat egg whites until
stiff; fold in cheese mixture, dip cubes of bread in cheese.  Place
on buttered cookie sheet in an icebox overnight.  Bake at 400 degrees
for 3 to 5 minutes until slightly brown on edges. Randy Rigg
