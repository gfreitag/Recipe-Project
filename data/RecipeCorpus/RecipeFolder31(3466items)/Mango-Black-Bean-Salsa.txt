Title: Mango-Black Bean Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
6 c  Mangoes -- diced
2 c  Red onion -- finely diced
2 c  Black beans -- cooked
4    Jalapenos -- minced
1/4 c  Cilantro -- chopped
4    Garlic cloves -- minced
2 tb Olive oil
1/4 c  Lime juice
4 ts Cumin
Salt and pepper
Combine mangoes, onions, beans, jalapenos, cilantro, garlic, olive
oil, lime juice, cumin and salt and pepper to taste.  Chill.
