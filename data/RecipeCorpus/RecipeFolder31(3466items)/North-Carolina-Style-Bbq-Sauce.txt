Title: North Carolina-Style Bbq Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  White vinegar
1 c  Cider vinegar
1 tb Sugar
1 tb Red pepper -- crushed
Flakes
1 tb Tabasco sauce
Salt
Ground pepper -- to taste
Combine all ingredients in a noncorro- sive container and mix well.
Yield 2 cups. FROM:    CHUCK OZBURN (HBWK07A)
