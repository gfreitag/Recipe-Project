Title: Grandma R's Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
3    Cranberries, coarsely
Chopped
4 c  Sugar
3    Apples -- chopped
2    Cherry jell-o
1 c  Water -- hot
Mix Jell-o and hot water. Combine with all other ingredients and mix
well. Chill in refrigerator (overnight or longer), stirring
occasionally. Stir we to break up. Can be made a couple days ahead.
