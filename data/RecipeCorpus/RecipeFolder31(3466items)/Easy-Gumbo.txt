Title: Easy Gumbo
Categories: Soup/stew, Cajun/creol, Meats, Sausage
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 md Onion -- chopped
1/2 lb Shrimp -- peeled & deveined
24    Pieces reserved sausage from
-Meatballs & Sausage
1 c  Frozen okra -- sliced
1/2 c  Crushed tomatoes
1/2 c  Reserved Red Hot Red Pepper
-Sauce
Salt and pepper
3 c  Rice -- cooked
Heat the oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium high heat, add
the onion and cook until lightly browned, stirring constantly, about
3 minutes. Add the shrimp and toss for 30 seconds.  Add the sausage,
okra, tomatoes and pepper sauce.  Bring to a boil, reduce the heat,
season with salt and pepper, and simmer for 1 minute.  Serve over hot
rice.
