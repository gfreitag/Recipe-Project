Title: Rhaburber Kuchen`
Categories: Breakfast, Cheese/eggs, Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
2    Sticks Butter
1 1/2 c  Sugar
5    Or 6 Eggs
1 ts Vanilla Extract
2 1/4 c  (Scant) Flour
1 ts Salt -- scant
5 c  (To 6) Rhubarb, cut up
2 tb (Or 3) Sugar
Cream butter, sugar, and vanilla until fluffy. Add eggs, one at a
time; add flour and salt. Spread on cookie sheet and arrange cut-up
rhubarb on top. Sprinkle with sugar. Bake in 350 F oven 40 to 45
mins. Cool. Slice into rectangles. Serve with whipped cream.

As prepared at the Bernerhof Inn of Glen, NH.
