Title: Louisiana Cocktail Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Seafood
Yield: 1 cup
3/4 c  Catsup
Juice of 1 lemon
2 ts Prepared horseradish
1/4 ts Tabasco sauce or oth
Pinch of salt
Freshly ground peppe
Combine all the ingredients in a small stainless steel or other
nonreactive bowl and mix well.  Let stand at room temp for 30
mintues. Serve with crab cakes, boiled or fried shrimp, or oysters.
