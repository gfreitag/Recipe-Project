Title: Steamer Corn Combo
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1/3 c  Celery; chopped
1/3 c  Green pepper; chopped
10 oz Pkg whole kernel corn; thaw
8 oz Tomato sauce
4 oz Mushroom pieces; drained
1 ea Tomato; peel & chop
2 ts Brown sugar
1/2 ts Onion powder
1/4 ts Garlic salt
1 ds Black pepper
1 ds Liquid hot pepper sauce
1 tb Catsup
In rice bowl, combine celery and green pepper. Add 1/2 cup water to
reservoir, cover and steam for 10 to 12 minutes.

Stir in all remaining ingredients. Add 1-1/4 cup water to reservoir,
cover and steam for 24 to 26 minutes.

Serves 4

Total steaming time: 34 to 38 minutes.

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker Booklet Typos by
