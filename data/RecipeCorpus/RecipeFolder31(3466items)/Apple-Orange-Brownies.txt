Title: Apple-Orange Brownies
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 6 servings
6 tb Butter
1 c  Brown Sugar
1    Egg
1/2 c  Apple Sauce
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Baking Powder
1 1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking Soda
1/2 c  Chopped Nuts
2 tb Orange Juice
1 1/2 c  Powdered Sugar
1/2 ts Vanilla
Mix everything except the juice and powdered sugar and half of the
vanilla.

Pour into a greased 12*7*2 pan . Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes.
Mix remaining ingredients and pour over the hot brownies.
