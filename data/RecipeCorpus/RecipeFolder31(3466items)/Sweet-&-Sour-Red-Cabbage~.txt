Title: Sweet & Sour Red Cabbage~
Categories: Healthy, Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
1 md Red cabbage -- shredded
1 1/2 c  Water
2 tb Apple juice concentrate
2    Cloves Garlic -- whole
2 tb Lemon juice
1 tb White wine vinegar
1/2 ts Ground ginger
Simmer all ingredients in a deep saucepan, stirring occasionally,
until tender, about 35 minutes. Remove garlic cloves and serve hot or
cold.

Per serving: Calories: 17 Protein: 1g Carbohydrates: 4g Fat: 0g
Sodium: 8mg Cholesterol: 0mg
