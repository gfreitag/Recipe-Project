Title: Rhubarb Banana Jam
Categories: Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Cooked rhubarb
6 c  Sugar
2 lg Bananas, cut fine
Mix well and bring to boil and boil for 35 minutes or until thick.
Pour into hot jars and seal.
