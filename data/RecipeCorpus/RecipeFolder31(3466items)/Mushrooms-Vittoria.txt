Title: Mushrooms Vittoria
Categories: Vegetables, Side dish
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Butter
2 tb Olive oil
6 lg Garlic cloves, flattened
1 1/4 lb Mushrooms, halved or quarted
1 c  White wine
Italian parsley, chopped
Melt butter with the oli in a large skillet over med-high heat. Add
garlic and saute until beginning to brown, about 2 minutes. Using
slotted spoon, remove garlic and discard. Add mushrooms and saute
until brown and juices evaporate, about 15 minutes. Add wine and
simmer until reduced to a glaze on mushroooms, stirring occasionally,
about 10 minutes. Season to taste with salt & pepper, sprinkle with
parsley and serve.
