Title: Swiss Corn
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Fresh corn, cut from the cob
2/3 c  Milnot
1    Egg, beaten
2 tb Chopped onion
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Shredded Swiss cheese
1/2 c  Cracker crumbs
1 tb Melted butter
Cook corn in boiling salted water 2 to 3 minutes.  Drain and combine
with milk, egg, onion, salt, pepper, and Swiss cheese.  Place in
greased 10 x 6 x 1 1/2 inch casserole, or whatever you have that is
close. Toss cracker crumbs with butter.  Sprinkle over corn mixture.
Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes.  Let set a few minutes
before serving. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   06-05-95
