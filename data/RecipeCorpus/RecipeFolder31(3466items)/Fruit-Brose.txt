Title: Fruit Brose
Categories: Beverages, Fruit, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
2 oz Oatmeal
1/2 oz Brown sugar
1 ea Lemon -=OR=- orange
1/2 pt Boiling water
Mix together the oatmeal & sugar in a jug.  Add the grated rind &
juice of the lemon or orange.  Pour on the boiling water & leave
covered until it gets cold.

Strain, pressing out all the liquid.  Dilute to taste with water &
serve cold.

NOTE: Apparently, if you don't mind polluting your potent potable, it
is good heated up with a tot of whisky.

Mary Norwak, "Grains, Beans & Pulses"
