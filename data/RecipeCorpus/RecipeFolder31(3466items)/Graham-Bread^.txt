Title: Graham Bread^
Categories: Breads, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Graham flour
2 c  Sweet milk
1/2 c  Molasses
1 ts Salt
thoroughly mix all of the ingredients. Pour into a greased loaf pan
and bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes. Makes one loaf.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
