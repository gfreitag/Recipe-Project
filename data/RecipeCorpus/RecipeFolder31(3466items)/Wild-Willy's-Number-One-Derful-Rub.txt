Title: Wild Willy's Number One-Derful Rub
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 2 servings
3/4 c  Paprika
1/4 c  Black Pepper
1/4 c  Salt
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Chili Powder
2 tb Garlic Powder
2 tb Onion Powder
2 ts Cayenne
Mix the spices throughly in a bowl.  Store covered in a cool dark
pantry.
