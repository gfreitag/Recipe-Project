Title: Pineapple Topping
Categories: Ice cream, Fruit, Dips/spread, Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Margarine -- melted
1 c  Crushed pineapple
1 tb Brown sugar
1 ds Nutmeg
In a saucepan, melt margarine and add other ingredients. Cook until
thick, stirring constantly. Allow to cool slightly before pouring
over ice cream. Makes 1 1/4 cups.
