Title: Oat Flour Muffins
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 6 muffins
1 c  AM Oat Flour
1 ts Non-alum baking powder
1/2 ts Sea salt (optional)
2 tb Raw honey
1/3 c  Buttermilk
1/2 c  Raisins or dates
-OR- chopped nuts
1 tb AM Unrefined Vegetable Oil
1    Egg; beaten
-OR- egg replacer
Stir together dry ingredients.  Mix liquids and fruit or nuts
together and add to dry mixture, stirring lightly.  Fill 6 oiled
muffin tins 1/2 full and bake 15 minutes at 425 F.
