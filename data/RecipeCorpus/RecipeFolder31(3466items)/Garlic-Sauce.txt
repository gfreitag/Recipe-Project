Title: Garlic Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
4 tb Oil
8    Garlic cloves -- minced
Juice of 1 lime
1/2 ts Maggi liquid seasoning
1 ts Parsley -- minced
In small pan, heat oil and saute garlic lightly. Add lime juice, Maggi
seasoning and parsley.  Stir over medium heat about 3 minutes.

NOTE:  Maggi liquid seasoning is available at most supermarkets.
