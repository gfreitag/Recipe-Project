Title: Pecan Crunch Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Margarine
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Crushed Ruffles potato chips
1 c  Pecans
2 c  Flour
pn Salt
Cream margarine and sugar.  Add remaining ingredients and mix well
(expect it to be crumbly).  Make into balls the size of a walnut.
Flatten with a thick drinking glass dipped in sugar.  Bake for 10 to
12 minutes at 350 degrees on a ungreased cookie sheet. Randy Rigg
