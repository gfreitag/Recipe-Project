Title: Holiday Cherry Mincemeat Mold
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Pitted dark sweet cherries
2 sm Packages of cherry gelatin
2 c  Boiling water
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Prepared mincemeat
1/2 c  Diced apple
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Drain cherries and cut in half.  Reserve syrup.  Dissolve gelatin in
boiling water.  Add enough water to reserved syrup to make 1 3/4
cups. Stir into gelatin and add lemon juice.  Chill until partially
set. Fold in mincemeat, apple and nuts.  Pour into 6 1/2 cup mold.
Chill. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   06-25-95
