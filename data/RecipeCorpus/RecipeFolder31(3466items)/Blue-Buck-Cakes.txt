Title: Blue Buck Cakes
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  AM Blue Cornmeal
1 c  AM Buckwheat Flour
3/4 ts Sea salt (optional)
1 1/2 tb Non-alum baking powder
2 c  Milk or soymilk
1 1/2 ts Unrefined Vegetable Oil
1/2 c  Blueberries
Mix dry ingredients and liquids separately.  Stir mixtures together
just until lumps disappear.  Pour batter onto hot, oiled griddle,
turning once. Serve hot with your favorite sweetener.
