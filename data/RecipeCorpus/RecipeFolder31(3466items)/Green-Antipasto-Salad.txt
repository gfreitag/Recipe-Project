Title: Green Antipasto Salad
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
2 c  Cucumber slices
2 c  Broccoli flowerets
2 c  Cauliflower pieces
1/4 c  Green onion slices
1    Green pepper, cut in slices
8 oz Bottle of Italian dressing
Combine vegetables and toss lightly.  Marinate in dressing in
refrigerator overnight.  Drain, reserving marinade.  Serve in
lettuce-lined bowl with reserved marinade. Randy Rigg
