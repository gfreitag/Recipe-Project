Title: Jalapeno Pesto Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2    Jalapenos -- roasted/skinned
1 c  Basil leaves
1 1/2 c  Olive oil
Juice of 1 lime
1    Garlic clove -- roasted
1/4 c  Parmesan
1/4 c  Pumpkin seeds
Combine jalapeno, basil, oil, lime juice, garlic, Parmesan and pumpkin
seeds in blender.  Blend until smooth.
