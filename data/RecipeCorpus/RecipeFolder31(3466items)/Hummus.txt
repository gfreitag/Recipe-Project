Title: Hummus
Categories: Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 8 servings
1 lb Chickpeas
1 c  Tahini
1/4 c  Olive oil
2    Lemon -- juice only
3 tb Water
pn Cayenne pepper
ds Tabasco sauce
4    Cloves garlic
Salt to taste
1/2 c  Parsley -- chopped
1/4 ts Paprika
Soak the chickpeas in a large pot overnight in 10 cups of water.  Add
fresh water to cover by 2 inches, then boil for 2 hours or until
soft, adding water as necessary.  Drain.  Place all the ingredients
except the parsley and paprika in the bowl of a food processor.
Puree until smooth. Taste for seasoning.  Turn into a bowl and
sprinkle with the parsley and paprika. Serve with pita.
