Title: Baked Grits
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 3 servings
4 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Grits
1 ts Salt
2    Rolls of Kraft garlic cheese
1    Stick of margarine
2    Eggs
2/3 c  Milk
ds Tabasco sauce
Paprika
Cook grits in salted water.  Break the cheese and margarine into small
pieces and stir into hot grits until melted.  Slightly beat eggs, add
to milk; stir egg-milk mixture and Tabasco sauce into grits mixture.
Pour into buttered casserole, sprinkle with paprika and bake for 1
hour at 350 degrees. Randy Rigg
