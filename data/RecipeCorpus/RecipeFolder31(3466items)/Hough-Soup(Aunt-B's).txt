Title: Hough Soup(Aunt B's)
Categories: British isl, Meats, Soup/stew
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Hough
1 1/2 qt Water
1 lg Carrot
1 oz Sago
Piece of turnip
1    Onion
1 oz Drippings
Pepper and salt to taste
Wash meat and cut up into cubes.Prepare vegetables,dicing turnip and
carrot and slicing onion.Melt dripping in pan,put in meat and fry
until browned.Fraw meat to side of pan,put in vegetables and
brown.Sdd the water and seasoning and simmer for 3 hours.Strain
soup,return to pan,stir in sago and boul until sago is swelled and
cooked.Serve with snippets of toast.
