Title: Tomato Basil Dressing
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Olive oil
1 lg Tomato -- chopped
1 bn Basil -- chopped
1/4 c  Parsley -- chopped
Salt
1 ts Pepper
Put the ingredients into a blender, cover, and blend on high speed
for 30 seconds.  Taste for seasoning.
