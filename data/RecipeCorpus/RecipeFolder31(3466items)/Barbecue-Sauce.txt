Title: Barbecue Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 28 servings
1/2 sm Onion -- minced
1    Garlic clove -- minced
2 tb Butter
1 c  Ketchup
1/4 c  Bourbon
2 tb Maple syrup
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Mustard powder
1 ts Salt
1 ts Chili powder
Saute onions and garlic in butter in a heavy saucepan.  Add remaining
ingredients.  Bring to a boil and simmer 10 minutes. Use to paste
meats for grilling. Simmer 10-15 minutes longer to reduce to a
thicker sauce for serving with cooked meats. Especially good for ribs
or pork chops. Makes a little less than 2 cups, about 28
one-tablespoon servings.
