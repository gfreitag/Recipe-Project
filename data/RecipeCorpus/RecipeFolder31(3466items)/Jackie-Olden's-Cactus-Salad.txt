Title: Jackie Olden's Cactus Salad
Categories: Salads, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 nice folks
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
1 c  Nopalitos;* (cactus leaves,
-available in glass jars in
-Mexican food section of
-supermarkets)chopped
1/4 c  Cilantro; chopped
2    Whole avocados; peeled and
-cubed
1 c  Garbanzo beans; drained
3 cl Whole garlic; chopped
4    Green onions; chopped
1 sm Can ripe olives; sliced
-drained
1/2 lb Monterrey Jack cheese; cubed
Salt and pepper to taste;
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Oil;
3 tb Wine vinegar;
1 tb Dijon mustard;
ds Of tabasco
->OR<-
ds Other hot sauce;
Salt and pepper taste
Mix all salad ingredients together and chill until serving. Combine
dressing ingredients, mix well, and pour over salad.

*If all else fails, come and visit our neighbors. They have hugh
plant in their back yard. I' m sure they will let a body pick some....
