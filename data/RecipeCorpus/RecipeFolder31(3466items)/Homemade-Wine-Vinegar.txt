Title: Homemade Wine Vinegar
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Red/white wine
2 c  Red/white wine vinegar
lg Dark colored wine bottle
Piece of vinegar mother -
Starter
Combine the wine, wine vinegar and optional 'mother' in a large wine
or vinegar bottle. Cover the opening with cheesecloth and then let
the mixture stand in a cool, dark place for 6-8 weeks before using.

To keep the process going, withdraw no more than 1/10 at any one time.
Replace portion used with more wine. Allow to stand a week or so
before withdrawing more.
