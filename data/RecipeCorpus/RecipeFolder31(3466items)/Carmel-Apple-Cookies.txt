Title: Carmel Apple Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1/2 c  Shortening
1 1/3 c  Brown sugar, firmly packed
1    Egg
2 1/4 c  Sifted flour
1 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Ground cloves
1/2 ts Nutmeg
2 c  Grated peeled apples
2 c  Light raisins
1/2 c  Apple juice
2 c  Chopped walnuts
Carmel icing
Cream shortening, sugar and egg.  Sift dry ingredients together and
add to creamed mixture.  Stir in remaining ingredients, except icing.
Drop 3 inches apart on a greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees
for about 12 minutes.  Cool, spread with icing.

Carmel Icing 1/4 c butter 1/4 c brown sugar, packed 1 1/2 c powdered
sugar 1/4 ts salt
2 1/2 tb light cream

Mix butter and brown sugar in a saucepan and cook until sugar is
dissolved. Add sifted powdered sugar, salt and cream.  Beat until
smooth. If too thick, add a little more cream. Randy Rigg
