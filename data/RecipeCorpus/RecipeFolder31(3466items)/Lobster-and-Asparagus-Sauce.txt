Title: Lobster and Asparagus Sauce
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Salsa/sauce, Condiments
Yield: 10 servings
1 13/16 kg Lobster meat
50 g  Onion
50 ml Olive oil
1    Glass white wine
500 g  Fresh blanched asparagus (1"
-pieces), if canned, put in
-only at
The last minute and stir
-very gently
500 ml Tomato sauce
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Same method as seafood sauce. Source: Given to share with the express
permission of Chef de Cuisine
