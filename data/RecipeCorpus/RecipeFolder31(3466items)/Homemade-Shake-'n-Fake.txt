Title: Homemade Shake 'n Fake
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 1 servings
4 c  Flour
4 c  Ground cheap crackers
4 tb Salt
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Garlic powder
2 ts Onion powder
3 tb Paprika
1/4 c  Vegetable oil
Mix well, store in fridge in covered container. Makes the equivalent
of about 20 of them fancy store-bought packets, but at a quarter of
the price. Submitted By MARK MIKLAS   On   05-25-95
