Title: Pistachio-Mint Brownies^
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 16 squares
Southern Living 2/94
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
2 oz Semisweet chocolate
1/3 c  Butter or margarine
1 c  Sugar
2 lg Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
3/4 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1/2 c  Chopped pistachios or
Pecans
1 pk (8 3/4oz) chocolate mint
Patties, unwrapped
2 tb Chopped pistachios or
Pecans
Combine chocolate and butter in a heavy saucepan; cook over low heat,
stirring often until chocolate melts. Remove from heat.

Stir in sugar, eggs and vanilla. Add flour and salt, stirring until
blended. Stir in 1/2 cup pistachios. Spoon into  a greased 8" square
pan.

Bake at 350F. for 22 minutes. Top with mint patties. Bake an
additional 2 minutes.

Cover with foil and let stand 5 minutes. Uncover and evenly spread
melted mint patties over top. Sprinkle evenly with 2 tablespoons nuts
and cool on a wire rack. Cut into squares.makes 16 squares.
