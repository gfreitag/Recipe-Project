Title: Pastry Trimmin's Treats^
Categories: Snacks, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
Pastry trimmings
Sugar
Cinnamon
Place assorted shapes of trimmings from your pie on a cookie tin.
Sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon and bake about 15 minutes. serve
warm. the kids will eat these all up in a hurry, so get yours first!

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
