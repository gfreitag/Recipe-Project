Title: Blackened Fish
Categories: Bbq/grill, Fish, Herbs/spice
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Paprika
2 tb Cayenne pepper
2 tb White pepper
3/4 c  Creole seasoning
1/4 c  Blackening seasoning
Fish fillets; 4 to 12
1/8 c  Butter (1/4 stick)
Mix the spices.  Coat the fillets with the spice mixture.

Melt the butter in an iron skillet over a hot cooking fire.  Sear the
fish for 1 to 2 minutes on each side, or until they smoke and appear
"blackened".

Remove the skillet from the fire and cover.  The heat in the pan will
complete the cooking in about 10 minutes.  The spices can be mixed at
home and stored in an airtight container.

Nutritional information: per serving: 321 calories; 13g fat; 1261mg
sodium; 137mg cholesterol
