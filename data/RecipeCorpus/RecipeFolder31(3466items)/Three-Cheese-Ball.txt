Title: Three Cheese Ball
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 3 servings
8 oz Cream cheese
4 oz Cheddar cheese
4 oz Shredded blue cheese
1 tb Minced onion
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
Leave cheese at room temperature for two hours.  Place all ingredients
except nuts in mixing bowl and whip with mixer.  Shape into ball and
roll in nuts.  Wrap in wax paper and chill. Randy Rigg
