Title: Chicken With Rice
Categories: Poultry, Crockpot, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
1 lg Chicken, 2-5 lbs, cut in
-pieces
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Paprika
1/8 ts Pepper
1/2 lg Onion, chopped
1 lg Tomato, peeled and chopped
1 tb Minced parsley
1/8 ts Saffron
1 1/2 c  Water
1 c  Uncooked rice
1    Clove Garlic, crushed
Season chicken with salt, paprika, and pepper. Put in crock pot. Add
other ingedients, except rice. Cook 6 - 8 hours. Add rice and stir.
Cook on high 1 hour.
