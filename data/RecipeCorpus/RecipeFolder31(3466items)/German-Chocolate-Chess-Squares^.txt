Title: German Chocolate Chess Squares^
Categories: Chocolate, Cake/cookie
Yield: 4 dozen
Southern Living 2/94
Carolyn Shaw 6-95
1 pk (18.25oz) German chocolate
Cake mix with pudding
1 lg Egg, lightly beaten
1/2 c  Butter or amrgarine, melted
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 pk (8oz) cream cheese,
Softened
2 lg Eggs
1 pk (16oz) powdered sugar,
Sifted
Combine cake mix, egg, butter and pecans, stirring until moistened.
Press into bottom of a greased 13x9x2" pan; set aside.

Combine cream cheese, eggs and 1 cup powdered sugar; beat at medium
speed with an electric mixer until blended. Gradually add remaining
powdered sugar, beating well after each addition. Pour over chocolate
layer, spreading evenly.

Bake at 350F. for 40 minutes. Cool on a wire rack and cut into
squares. Makes 4 dozen.
