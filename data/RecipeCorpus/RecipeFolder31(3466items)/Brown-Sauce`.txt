Title: Brown Sauce`
Categories: Main dish, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Butter
2 tb Cornstarch
1 sm Onion -- chopped
1 tb Dijon Mustard
1 cn Beef Broth (10 1/2 Oz Can)
Melt butter. Stir in cornstarch; add onion and cook until tender.
Stir in mustard and beef broth. Cook, stirring constantly, about 5
mins or until thickened. Makes 1 1/2 cups.
