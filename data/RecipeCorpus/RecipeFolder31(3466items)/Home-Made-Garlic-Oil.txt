Title: Home Made Garlic Oil
Categories: Tasteofhome, Condiments, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
2 tb Garlic; minced
Prepare this oil in advance and keep stored in covered jar in the
refrigerator.

Heat vegetable oil in a small saucepan over medium high heat. When
oil is hot, add minced garlic and saute until lightly brown. Remove
from heat, set aside until cool. Pour into covered jar and store in
refrigerator. Makes about 1/2 cup.
