Title: Baked Indian Pudding`
Categories: Native amer, Puddings
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Yellow Cornmeal, Granulated
1/2 c  Black Molasses
1/4 c  Granulated Sugar
1/4 c  Lard Or Butter
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Baking Soda
2    Eggs
1 1/2 qt Hot Milk
Mix ingredients thoroughly with half of the milk and bake in a very
hot oven until mixture boils. Then stir in the remaining hot milk and
bake in a well-greased stone crock at a low temperature for 5 to 7
hours. Makes 1/2 gallon.

As prepared at The Griswold Inn of Essex, CT.
