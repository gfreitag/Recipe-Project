Title: Swedish Cabbage Rolls (Slow Cooker)
Categories: Crockpot, Meats, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------------CABBAGE ROLLS----------------------------
6 ea Large cabbage leaves
1 ea Egg -- beaten
1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Onion -- minced
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
1 lb Ground beef
1 c  Rice -- cooked
MMMMM---------------------------SAUCE--------------------------------
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 tb Brown sugar
1 tb Lemon juice
1 ts Worchestershire sauce
CABBAGE ROLLS: Boil cabbage leaves until limp. Combine egg, milk,
onion, salt, pepper, beef and cooked rice. Divide into 6 portions.
Place each portion into a cabbage leaf, fold in sides and roll over.
Place in slow cooker pot. SAUCE: Combine tomato sauce, brown sugar,
lemon juice and worchestershire sauce. Pour over cabbage rolls in
slow cooker pot. Cover and cook on low 7 to 9 hours.
