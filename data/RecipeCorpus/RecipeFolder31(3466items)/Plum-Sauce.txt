Title: Plum Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Asian
Yield: 1 cup
2 lb Canned purple plums, drained
-reserve 3/4 cup juice)
1/2 c  Apricot preserves
1 tb Minced garlic *
2 ts Fresh ginger, grated
1 tb Red rice vinegar or cider
1 ts Brown sugar
1/2 ts Soy sauce
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper, fresh
pn Cayenne pepper
Remove and discard the pits and skins from the plums.  Puree the
plums a food processor until smooth.  Place the puree in a smal heavy
suacep and add the remaining ingredients. Add enough of the reserved
juice to make the mixture the consistency of applesauce.  Bring the
mixture to boil and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring often.  Remove
the garlic.  I silky smooth sauce is desired, strain. Serve at room
temp.

*NOTE: It is preferable to put the minced garlic in a tea straner for
of removal.
