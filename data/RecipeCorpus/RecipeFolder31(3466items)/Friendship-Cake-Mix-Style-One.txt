Title: Friendship Cake Mix Style One
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk 3 oz Instant vanilla pudding
Mix
2/3 c  Oil
4    Eggs
1 pk Yellow cake mix
1 1/2 c  Brandied fruit -- well
Drained *
1/2 c  To 1 cup Pecans -- chopped
1 c  Raisins
* See recipe, Brandied Fruit Starter for Friendship Cake.

Combine pudding, oil, eggs and cake mix.  Beat 3 minutes at medium
speed. With spoon, blend fruit, floured nuts and raisins into batter.
Pour or spoon into greased and floured Bundt pan. Bake at 350~ for
40-50 minutes.
