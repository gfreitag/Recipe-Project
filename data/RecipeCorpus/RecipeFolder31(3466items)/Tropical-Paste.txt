Title: Tropical Paste
Categories: Condiments, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 cup
8 md Scallions
-coarsely chopped
1    1" piece ginger
-coarsely chopped
1 sm Fresh or dried
-hot green or red chile
-stemmed and seeded
1/4 c  Brown sugar
2 tb Tamarind concentrate
-or paste
2 tb Lime juice
2 tb Coarse salt
1 tb Tomato paste
2 ts Curry powder
1 ts Ground allspice
In blender or food processor, mince scallions, ginger and chile.
Scrape down the sides of the processor. Add remaining ingredients and
process until a paste forms. Will keep for up to 2 days, refrigerated
in an airtight container. Use with shrimp, salmon fillet, whole
chicken, center-cut pork loin, pork butt and beef brisket.

Food and Wine June 1995
