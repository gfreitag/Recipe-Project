Title: Enchil-Load-DOS
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 10 servings
1    Recipe "Hacker Helper"
2 ts Cumin
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper or to taste
2 c  Prepared salsa, divided
10 md Sized soft flour tortillas
1 1/2 c  Jack or cheddar cheese,
Grated
Chopped cilantro leaves for
Garnish
In large skillet, prepare or reheat "Hacker Helper". Stir in spices
and 1/2 c salsa. Simmer until heated through. Fill tortillas one at a
time with 2-3 tbs wheat mixture and sprinkle with 2 tbs cheese. Roll
up and place seam side down into a large shallow baking dish. Top
with remaining salsa and cheese. Bake at 350F for 20 minutes. Garnish
w/ cilantro. F.A.T. REDUCER: use lowfat cheese *From "Qucik Bytes:
Computer Lover's Cookbook" by Diane Pfeifer* ~Typed for you by
