Title: Oven Baked Pork Chops
Categories: Meats
Yield: 8 servings
1    Envelope Lipton Golden Onion
Recipe Soup Mix
2    Eggs -- well beaten
2/3 c  Plain dry bread crumbs
8    1/2" thick
Pork chops
Preheat oven to 350~. Combine soup mix with bread crumbs. Dip chops in
eggs, then soup mixture, coating well. Place in lightly greased large
shallow baking pan and drizzle, if desired, with melted butter. Bake,
turning once, 1 hour or until tender.
