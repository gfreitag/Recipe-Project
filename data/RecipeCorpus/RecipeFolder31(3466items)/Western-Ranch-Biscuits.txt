Title: Western Ranch Biscuits
Categories: Pets
Yield: 3 pounds
1 pk Dry yeast
1/2 c  Warm water
2 c  Warm chicken or beef broth
1/2 c  Powdered milk
1/4 c  Margarine or hamburger greas
1/4 c  Honey
1 ea Egg
1/4 ts Salt
2 c  All purpose flour
1 c  Wheat germ
1 c  Corn meal
2 c  Bulgur (cracked wheat)
4 c  Whole wheat flour
In small bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water.  In large bowl, combine
broth, powdered milk, maragrine/grease, honey, egg, and salt.  Add
yeast and mix well.  Stir in flour, corn meal, wheat germ and cracked
wheat. Nix well, add whole wheat flour, 1/2 cup at a time, mixing
well. Knead in final amounts of flour by hand, and continue kneading
for 5 minutes or until dough is not sticky.  Roll to 1/2 inch, cut
into shapes. Place on greased baking sheet, cover lightly and let
rise 20 minutes. Bake at 300 for 1 hour.  Turn off heat and let sit
in oven for at least an hour.

**You can add bacon bits or cooked liver to these biscuits to give it
a
more meaty western flavor**
