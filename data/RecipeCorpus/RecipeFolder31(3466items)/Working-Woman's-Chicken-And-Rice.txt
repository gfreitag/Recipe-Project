Title: Working Woman's Chicken And Rice
Categories: Poultry, Crockpot, Rice
Yield: 1 servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
6    Boneless chicken breasts --
-skinned
2 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
Salt and pepper to taste
1 pk Rice-A-Roni (Chicken
Flavor)
MMMMM-------------------------DIRECTIONS------------------------------
In slow cooker put chicken breasts with canned soups, salt and
pepper. Cook all day on LOW (approx. 10 hrs. or until chicken is
tender). Fix Rice-A-Roni per directions on box.  Put on plate and
place chicken and gravy on top. From the files of Earl Shelsby
Reposted by Dale/Gail Shipp
