Title: Big Bucket in the Sky Chicken 1
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Self rising flour
2 pk Cup of soup, tomato OR
Knorr tomato with basil
1 tb Paprika
1 ts Salt
2 pk Good Seasons Italian
-Seasoning
14 lb Butter in a pan
Mix above ingredients. Put in plastic bag & shake. Put in an oiled 10
x 13 baking dish. Brush butter over coated chicken pieces. Bake at
375 degrees for 20 - 40 minutes til juices run clear. Do not cook til
dry.
