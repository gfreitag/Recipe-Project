Title: Grama's Good Pie Crust
Categories: Pie/tart, Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Flour, sifted
1/4 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts To 1 salt
2/3 c  Lard or shortening
3 tb Sugar, if desired
1/3 c  Cold water, about
Sift together dry ingredients. Add fat and cut with tow knives or
pastry blender until mealy. Stir in water until pastry leaves the
sides of bowl. Divide into 2 balls. roll one to fit the pie tin, use
the other for the top of the pie.
