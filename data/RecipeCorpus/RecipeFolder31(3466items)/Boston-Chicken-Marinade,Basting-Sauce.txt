Title: Boston Chicken Marinade/Basting Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Canola oil
1 tb Honey
1 tb Lime juice
1/4 ts Paprika
Mix all ingredients well and apply to skin-side-up chicken in a single
layer in greased baking dish or pan. Bake at 400 F for 35-40 minutes.
Baste every 8-10 minutes during the baking time. Source: Gloria Pitzer
