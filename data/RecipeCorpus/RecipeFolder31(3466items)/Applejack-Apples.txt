Title: Applejack Apples
Categories: Fruit, Alcohol
Yield: 4 servings
6    Apples -- medium size
3 tb Lemon juice
Applejack -- * see note
2 tb Unsalted butter
Apple cider
* Use Calvados, a French apple brandy, or regular brandy if
preferred. Red wine may also be used with excellent results.

1. Arrange the apple slices in a glass baking dish. Sprinkle with
lemon juice and Applejack or your choice of brandies or wine. Dot
with the butter. Add enough apple cider to fill the dish to a depth
of 1 inch. 2. Bake in a preheated 375-degree oven for 20 minutes.

Serving Ideas:  Serave grilled, or warm with vanilla ice cream.
