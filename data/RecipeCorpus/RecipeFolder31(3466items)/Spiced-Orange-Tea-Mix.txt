Title: Spiced Orange Tea Mix
Categories: Beverages, Fruit, Herbs/spice
Yield: 32 servings
1 c  Powdered orange breakfast
-drink mix (ie, tang)
1 c  Powdered unsweetened instant
-tea mix
1/2 c  White sugar
2 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Ground cloves
Combine all ingredients; store in a tightly covered container.  Makes
about 2 cups dry mix, 32 servings.

TO SERVE:  Spoon approx 2-3 tsp mix in a cup, more or less to taste.
Add 6 ounces boiling water, stir well.

VARIATION:  Substitute apple juice, or cranberry juice for the water.

Origin: Watkins Food Gifts Cookbook. Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
Sept/94.
