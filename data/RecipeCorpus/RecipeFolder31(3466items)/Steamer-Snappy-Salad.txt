Title: Steamer Snappy Salad
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Cauliflower; bite size
1 c  Broccoli; bite size
1 c  Carrot or green beans
-bite size
Low calorie Italian dressing
Make a crunchy, colorful low calorie salad. Steam bite sized pieces of
cauliflower, broccoli, carrots or green beans just until crisp
tender. Toss with a low calorie Italian salad dressing ...
refrigerate several hours ... enjoy with a clear conscience!

Place bite size vegetables in steamer basket. Add 1 cup water to
reservoir, cover and steam for approximately 20 minutes or until just
crisp tender. Immediately remove from steamer and plunge into ice
water to stop cooking process. Refrigerate.

Serves 4
