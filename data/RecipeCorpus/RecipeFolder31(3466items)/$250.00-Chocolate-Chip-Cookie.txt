Title: $250.00 Chocolate Chip Cookie
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate
Yield: 112 cookies
2 c  Butter
2 c  Sugar
2 c  Brown Sugar
4 ea Eggs
2 ts Vanilla
4 c  Flour
5 c  Oatmeal
1 ts Salt
2 ts Baking powder
24 oz Chocolate chips
1 ea Hershey bar, 8 ounce
3 c  Nuts, chopped, choice
Cream the butter and both sugars.  Add eggs and vanilla.  Mix with
flour, oatmeal blended to a fine powder, and soda.  Add chocolate
chips, grated Hershey bar, and nuts.  Roll into balls and place 2
inches apart on a cookie sheet.  Bake 10 minutes at 375 degrees.
YIELDS 112 COOKIES
