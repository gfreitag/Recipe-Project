Title: Hacker Helper
Categories: Vegetables, Nuts/grains, Side dish
Yield: 2 cups
1 md Yellow onion, diced
3    Cloves garlic, minced
2 tb Oil
1 c  Bulghur (cracked) wheat
2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
In large skillet, saute onion and garlic in oil over low heat until
onion is translucent. Add bulghur. Saute briefly, stirring to coat
well. Stir in 2 c water and the salt. Cover and let simmer, stirring
occasionally. Cook for 10-15 minutes until mixture is slightly chewy,
adding more water if necessary. *From "Quick Bytes: Computer Lover's
Cookbook" by Diane Pfeifer* ~Typed for you by Michelle Bruce
