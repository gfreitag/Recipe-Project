Title: Dutch Bouncer`
Categories: Meats, Breakfast
Sm Sirloin Tip Of Beef
Oil
Black Pepper -- crushed
Pumpernickel Bread
Yield: 1 servings
:
:
:
2  Eggs -- fried
Au Jus
:

Trim off all excess fat and membranes from meat; rub the meat with
oil and crushed black pepper, and marinate for a few hours. Serve in
a hot saute pan and roast in a hot oven, turning often until meat is
medium-rare. Slice meat across the grain. Serve in fresh-baked dark
pumpernickel bread, topped with 2 fried eggs with au jus served on
the side.

As prepared at The Dearborn Inn of Dearborn, MI.
