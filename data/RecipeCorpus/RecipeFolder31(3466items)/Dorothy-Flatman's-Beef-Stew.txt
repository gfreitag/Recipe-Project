Title: Dorothy Flatman's Beef Stew
Categories: Soup/stew, Meats, Vegetables, Crockpot
Yield: 4 servings
7 oz Beef, chunked
3 c  Water
4 c  Potatoes, cubed
1 c  Celery, sliced
1 c  Carrots, sliced
1 c  Onion, chopped
1 ea Pkg beef stew seasoning mix
MMMMM-------------------OR: HOMEMADE SEASONING------------------------
2 ts Beef bullion
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ea Bay leaf
1 tb Flour
Combine all in slow cooking pot.  Cover and cook on low all day or
until vegetables are tender.  Thicken if desired.

Created by Dorothy Flatman Adapted from Schilling Beef Stew seasoning
mix package
