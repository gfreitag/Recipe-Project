Title: Stoved Potatoes(Stovies)Aunt B's
Categories: British isl, Potatoes
Yield: 4 servings
10    Potatoes
2 md Onions
Drippings or butter
Salt and pepper to taste
Use good roast beef dripping if available or scraps of lamb or
mutton.Peel and slice potatoes and put into pan with just enough
water to cover base and keep potatoes from burning.Add the sliced
onions and season well with pepper and salt.Cut in about 4oz dripping
or butter or a proportionate quantity of meat scraps.Cover pan
closely and simmer for about an hour.
