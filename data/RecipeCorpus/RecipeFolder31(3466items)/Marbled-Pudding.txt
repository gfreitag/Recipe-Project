Title: Marbled Pudding
Categories: Puddings
Yield: 6 servings
2 c  Cold milk
1    3 1/2oz pkg instant
-pudding mix, any flavor
1 c  Cool whip
Pour milk into small mixer bowl. Add pudding mix. Beat at lowest
speed til well blended, 1-2 mins. Fold cool whip into pudding for a
marbled effect. Garnish with additional cool whip. Makes 6 servings.
