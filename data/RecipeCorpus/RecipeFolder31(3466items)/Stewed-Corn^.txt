Title: Stewed Corn^
Categories: Vegetables, Tasteofhome, Depress/era
Yield: 1 servings
4    Or 6 cobs of corn
Salt and pepper
Sugar
Milk
Draw a sharp knife down the center of each row of corn; press out the
pulp with the back of the knife. To each pint, add 1/2 ts each salt
and sugar, dash of pepper and 1/2 c milk.Heat and simmer 10 minutes.
