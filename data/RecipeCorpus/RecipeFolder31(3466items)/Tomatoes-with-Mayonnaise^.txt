Title: Tomatoes with Mayonnaise^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 lg Tomatoes
Mayonnaise
Sugar
Peel tomatoes and cut in half. Put about 2 tablespoons mayonnaise on
top of each one, sprinkle with about 1 teaspoon sugar. that's it and
it's very good.

Many of the recipes in this collection did not contain amounts or oven
temperatures. I have typed them in as they appear in the book, typos
and all.
