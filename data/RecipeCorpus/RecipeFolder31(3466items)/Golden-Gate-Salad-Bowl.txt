Title: Golden Gate Salad Bowl
Categories: Salads, Seafood
Yield: 6 servings
MMMMM-----------------------CREAM DRESSING----------------------------
1 c  Cottage cheese
1/4 c  Dairy sour cream
1 ts Grated lemon peel
1/4 ts Salt
Milk
MMMMM---------------------------SALAD--------------------------------
2 c  Torn iceberg lettuce
1 c  Torn romaine
1/2 c  Thinly sliced celery
1/4 c  Sliced green onions
1/2 lb Cooked lobster, shrimp,
-crabmeat or tuna
3/4 c  Halved cherry tomatoes
1    Grapefruit, sectioned
1    Avocado, sliced
5    Pitted ripe olives, sliced
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
To prepare dressing: Beat cottage cheese in a small mixing bowl until
fairly smooth.  Add sour cream, lemon peel and salt.  Add milk to
thin, if desired.  Cover and chill.  (Will make 1-1/4 cups.)

To prepare salad: Toss lettuce, romaine, celery and onions in a large
salad bowl.  Arrange lobster, tomatoes, grapefruit, avocado and
olives on greens. Sprinkle lemon juice over all; cover and chill.
Just before serving, toss dressing with salad.

The Birmingham News Wednesday, June 1st, 1994
