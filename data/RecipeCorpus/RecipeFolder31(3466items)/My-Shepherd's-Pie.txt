Title: My Shepherd's Pie
Categories: Main dish, Meats, Pie/tart, Appetizers
Yield: 4 servings
2 lb Left over roast beef
1 lg Onion
2 pk Bovril powder
1/2 c  Water
Mashed potatoes
Grind the roast beef.  Saute the onion and add bovril, water, and
beef. Cook for a short time.  Put in a casserole.  Top with mashed
potatoes and put in 375 F oven for about 30 minutes.  Brown under
broiler.
