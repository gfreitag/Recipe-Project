Title: Fruit Parfait Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
Fold together gently:
1 lg Can of crushed pineapple,
Drain well
1 cn Cherry pie filling
1 cn Milnot
1 lg Carton of Cool Whip
May be eaten soft or slightly frozen, but in my opinion it tastes
better slightly frozen.  You can also susbstitute other pie fillings
such as blackberry, peach, blueberry, etc. Randy Rigg
