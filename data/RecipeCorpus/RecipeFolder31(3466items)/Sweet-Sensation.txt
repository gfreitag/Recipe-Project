Title: Sweet Sensation
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Ground Allspice
1/4 c  Brown Sugar
1/4 c  Onion Powder
2 tb Salt
2 ts Ground Nutmeg
2 ts Ground Cinnamon
2 ts Dried Thyme
Mix the spices throughly in a bowl.  Store in a cool, dark pantry.
