Title: Fruity Cake
Categories: Cake/cookie, Holiday
Yield: 1 9"x5" cake
1    Box AM Muffin Mix
--(Apple Spice Oat Bran)
1/2 c  Pitted and chopped dates
1/2 c  Diced dried papaya
1/2 c  Diced dried pear
1/2 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
3 tb Grated orange rind
2 tb AM Unrefined Sesame Oil
1/4 c  Honey
1 ts Vanilla
1 1/4 c  Water
1    Egg; beaten (optional)
Preheat oven to 350 F.

Combine the liquid ingredients.  In a separate bowl stir the mix into
the fruit and nuts.  Combine the two mixtures and add the orange
rind.  Bake in a 9" x 5" pan for 45 minutes.
