Title: Name-Your-Herb-Paste
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 servings
3 c  Fresh Herbs (Mint, Basil,
Parsley, Etc)
12    Cloves Garlic
1 ts Salt
1 c  Olive Oil
In a food processor, combine the herbs, garlic, and salt. Process
until the herbs are finely chopped.  Add the oil in a slow stream,
mixing throughly. The paste should be refrigerated and used within a
day or two.
