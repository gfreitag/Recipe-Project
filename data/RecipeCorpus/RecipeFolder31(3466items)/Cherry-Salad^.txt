Title: Cherry Salad^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Cherries, pitted
1/2 c  Almonds, blanched
French fruit dressing
Lettuce leaves
Pit your favorite variety of ripe cherries. Shred almonds, mix with
cherries, moisten with French fruit dressing and serve on lettuce
leaves. Delicious with cold meat, broiled chops, or chicken for a
luncheon.
