Title: No-Bake Blackberry Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Lemonade
1/8 ts Salt (optional)
4 c  Blackberries
1 c  Or more Whipped Topping
1 1/2 c  Sugar
1    9" prebaked pie crust
5 tb Cornstarch (heaping)
Mix the lemonade, sugar, salt and corn starch together in a medium
saucepan; heat to a boil or until thick.  Add blackberries, pour
mixture into cool pie shell; chill 3-4 hours.  Top with whipped
topping.

Source:  King's Orchard, Plantersville, Texas
