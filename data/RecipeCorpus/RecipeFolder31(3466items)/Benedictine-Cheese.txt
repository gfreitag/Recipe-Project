Title: Benedictine Cheese
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 3 servings
8 oz Cream cheese
1 sl Cucumber, 1 inch size
1 sm Onion
Mayonnaise
Chop onion and cucumber and add to cheese.  Thin with mayonnaise.
Color with green food coloring.  Spread on crackers or bread.  If
using for dip, thin to the desired consistency with milk or cream.
