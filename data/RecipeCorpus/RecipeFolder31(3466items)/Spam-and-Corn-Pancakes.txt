Title: Spam and Corn Pancakes
Categories: Spam
Yield: 12 servings
1 cn Spam, chopped
2 ea Eggs
1/2 c  Milk
2 c  Corn, cooked
2 c  Flour
Salt
Pepper
Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Heat griddle and ladel one scoop
on the pre-heated surface. Turn when bubbles appear on top
