Title: Barbecued Appetizer Meatballs
Categories: Meats, Appetizers, Bbq/grill
Yield: 48 meatballs
3 lb Lean ground beef
2 c  Quick oatmeal
13 oz Evaporated milk
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
1 c  Onion chopped
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 ts Chili Powder
Mix ingredients together & shape into walnut-sized balls.  Place in 2
(two) 9 x 13-inch baking dishes.

Sauce: 4 C. Catsup 2 C. Brown Sugar 3 T. Liquid Smoke 1 t. Garlic
Powder 1 C. Chopped Onion

Mix ingredients in bowl until brown sugar is dissolved.  Pour over
meatballs.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.

If you make the meatballs larger, this makes great meatball
sandwiches. Submitted By VIRGINIA KETTLER   On   05-26-95
