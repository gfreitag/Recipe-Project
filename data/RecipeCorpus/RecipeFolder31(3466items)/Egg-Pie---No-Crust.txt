Title: Egg Pie - No Crust
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 2 servings
4    Eggs
2 c  Milk
1 c  Sugar
5 tb Flour
6 sm Chunks of butter
ds Salt
Nutmeg
Vanilla
Cinnamon
Add flour and sugar together with other ingredients.  Bake at 375
degrees for 45 minutes. Randy Rigg
