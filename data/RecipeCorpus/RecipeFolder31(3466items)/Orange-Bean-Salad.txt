Title: Orange Bean Salad
Categories: Vegetables, Low cal/fat, Salads
Yield: 5 servings
3 c  Firmly cooked, drained and
. rinsed white beans
1 c  Diced celery
1 c  Chopped red onions
2 tb White wine vinegar
2 tb Water
6 tb Orange juice concentrate
1/2 c  Chopped fresh mint
1/2 ts Finely minced jalapeno
. pepper (optional)
MMMMM------------------------PER SERVING-----------------------------
207    Calories
10 2/3 g  Protein (10.7g)
41 g  Carbohydrates
5 g  Fiber
2/3 g  Fat (0.7g [0.2g saturated])
26 mg Sodium
Combine all.  Serve chilled or at room temperature.

From USA WEEKEND, May 5-7, 1995.
