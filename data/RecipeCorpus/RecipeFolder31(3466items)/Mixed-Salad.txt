Title: Mixed Salad
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
1 cl Garlic, halved
1 sm Head lettuce
3    Tomatoes, cut in eighths
6    Radishes, sliced
1/2    Cucumber, sliced
1 sm Bunch green onions
1 tb Parsley
1/2 c  French dressing
Have salad bowl very cold. Rub with cut garlic. Break crisp, dry
lettuce into bowl. Next, arrange tomatoes on top, then radishes. Add
cucumber slices and onions, sliced thin. Sprinkle with parsley and
chill at least a half hour before serving. At the table, pour on
French dressing and toss gently using 2 forks, lifting from the
bottom until well-coated.
