Title: Diabetic Pumpkin Cookies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Vegetables, Diabetic
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Shortening
1 c  Cooked pumpkin
1    Egg
1 ts Vanilla
2 c  Flour
1/4 ts Allspice
1/2 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Baking powder
1 ts Cinnamon
1 c  Raisins
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
Soak raisins in hot water for 5 minutes.  Drain.  Cream shortening.
Add pumpkin, egg and vanilla.  Beat well.  Mix dry ingredients. Add
to creamed mixture.  Mix well.  Add raisins and nuts. Drop on greased
cookie sheets and press with a fork.  Bake at 350 degrees for 12
minutes. Makes 3 to 4 dozen. *Contributor writes, "I freeze these;
otherwise, they get moldy. They can be eaten right out of the
freezer."
