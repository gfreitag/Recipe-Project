Title: Frosted Cheese and Fruit^
Categories: Depress/era, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 servings
3 oz Cream cheese
3 tb Mayonnaise
2 tb Lemon juice
1/2 c  Evaporated milk, cold
1/2 c  Dates, chopped
1/2 c  Pineapple, crushed
Mash cream cheese, adding mayonnaise a little at a time. Add lemon
juice to milk and whip hard till thick. Mix cheese and milk mixtures,
fold in dates and drained pineapple. Pour into a mold, pack in ice
and salt for 4 hours. Or freeze.
