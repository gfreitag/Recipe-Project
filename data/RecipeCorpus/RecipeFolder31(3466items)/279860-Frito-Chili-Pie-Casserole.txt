Title: 279860 Frito Chili Pie Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Chili, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Fritos brand corn chips
1 lg Onion, chopped
1 cn (19 oz.) chili
1 c  Grated American cheese
Place 2 cups of Fritos brand corn chips in a baking dish.  Arrange
chopped onion and half of grated cheese on the corn chips.  Pour
chili over onion and cheese.  Top with the remaining corn chips and
grated cheese. Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes.  Makes 4-6
servings. Submitted By BARRY WEINSTEIN   On   06-17-95
