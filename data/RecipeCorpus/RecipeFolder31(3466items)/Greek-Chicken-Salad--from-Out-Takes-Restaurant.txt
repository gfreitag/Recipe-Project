Title: Greek Chicken Salad  from Out Takes Restaurant
Categories: Poultry, Salads, Greek
Yield: 6 servings
6 ea 5-oz skinless chicken
-breasts; cooked and diced
1 ea Tomato; diced
1 sm Cucumber; peeled, seeded, &
-diced
2 ea Green onions; chopped
1 sm Green pepper; diced
MMMMM--------------------------DRESSING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Extra virgin olive oil
1/4 c  Fresh lemon juice
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 cl Finely minced garlic
1 ts Oregano
Combine salad ingredients.
Whisk dressing ingredients together and pour over salad ingredients.

Recipe from "Out Takes" restaurant in Birmingham, Alabama. Recipe
printed in "The Birmingham News" October 12, 1994. Typos by Jeff
