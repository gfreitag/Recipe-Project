Title: Spinach-stuffed Flank Steak
Categories: Meats
Yield: 6 servings
2 tb Oil
1/2 lb Mushrooms; chopped
1/4 lb Spinach; chopped
1 1/2 c  Bread crumbs; dry
2    Egg whites; slightly beaten
3 tb Parsley; fresh, chopped
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ts Rosemary; dry, crushed
1/2 ts Pepper
2 lb Flank steak
String; to tie steak roll-up
1 c  Beef broth
6    Carrots; cut in chunks
Preheat oven to 350.

In a skillet heat oil over medium and fry mushrooms 3 min. Add
spinach and stir fry 2 more min. Transfer to a bowl; cool; and add
next 6 ingreds. Lay steak on a work surface; cutting horizontally,
make a pocket. Fill with the spinach stuffing. Starting with the open
pocket side, roll up the meat into a log. Tie with string.

Place in a roasting pan and add broth and carrots. Roast one hr.
Remove from pan; let stand 10 min before carving. Meanwhile boil pan
juices until reduced and thickened and pour over the steak log slices.
