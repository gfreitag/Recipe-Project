Title: Shrimp Sauce and Pepper
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Salsa/sauce, Condiments
Yield: 10 servings
75 g  Butter
75 g  Onion
1 3/16 kg Shrimp
1    Glass white wine
400 g  Tomato sauce
50 g  Parsley
500 g  Red and yellow peppers
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Fry onion with butter. Add the shrimp, white wine salt and pepper,
cook for 5 mins. Add the tomato sauce, cream and parsley. Cook for 20
mins. Source: Given to share with the express permission of Chef de
Cuisine
