Title: Joggin In a Jar
Categories: Beverages, Fruit
Yield: 3 cups
1 c  Grape Juice
1 c  Apple Juice
1 c  Cranberry Juice
2 tb Apple Cider Vinegar
2 tb Honey
Mix all ingredients, mix well and chill.

Drink one glass every morning.
