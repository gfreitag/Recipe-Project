Title: SFOGATO (Zucchini SoufflÂ‚)
Categories: Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 6 servings
1 lb Lamb, ground
3    Onion; finely chopped
4 tb Butter
1 lb Zucchini, small; cubed
1 ds Pepper
2 ts Parsley; chopped
1 ts Salt
1 c  ;Water
8    Eggs
Tomato sauce
Saute lamb and onions in butter until meat is browned. Add zucchini,
pepper, parsley, salt, and water and cook over low heat, stirring
occasionally, until meat and vegetables are tender. Cool slightly.
Beat eggs until frothy. Stir into cooked mixture. Pour into a 1 qt.
baking dish or souffle dish and bake in a 375-deg. oven for 30 min.
or until set. Cut into squares and serve hot with tomato sauce. 6
servings. Submitted By SAM WARING   On   05-23-95
