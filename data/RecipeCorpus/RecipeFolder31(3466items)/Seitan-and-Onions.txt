Title: Seitan and Onions
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 3 portions
3    4-oz. pieces cooked Seitan
3 tb Vegetable oil
3 c  Half moon sliced onions
1/4 ts Ground black pepper (opt.)
2 tb Tamari soy sauce
1 1/4 c  Vegetable stock or water
Prep time: 15 to 25 minutes

Slice onions and set aside.  Saute Seitan in oil at high heat to
brown. When the Seitan starts to brown, add the onions and saute a
few minutes, then add the rest of the ingredients, cover and cook
until liquid is reduced to almost nothing.  Serve while hot as is
with a grain or bread of your choice.

This recipe has been developed by Bro. Ron Pickarski as part of his
work toward being a participant and finalist in the Culinary Olympics
held in Frankfurt, Germany.
