Title: Lemon Lime Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Lemon Jello
1 pk Lime Jello
1 c  Hot water
1 sm Can of crushed pineapple
3 tb Mayonnaise
1 cn Eagle brand milk
1 ct Cottage cheese
Dissolve jellos in hot water and mix well.  While mixture is hot add
remaining ingredients and place in refrigerator until set. Randy Rigg
