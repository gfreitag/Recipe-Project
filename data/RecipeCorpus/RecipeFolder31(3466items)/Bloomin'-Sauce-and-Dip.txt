Title: Bloomin' Sauce and Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Salsa/sauce, Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
8 oz Sour cream
8 oz Picante sauce (Pace medium
-or hot)
8 oz Cream cheese (room
-temperature so it is soft)
Garlic (to taste)
Lawry's Seasoned Salt (to
-taste)
Mix and put in refrigerator to thicken for a day.
