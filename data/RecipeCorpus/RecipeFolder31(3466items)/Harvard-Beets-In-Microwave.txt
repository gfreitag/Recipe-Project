Title: Harvard Beets In Microwave
Categories: Vegetables, Microwave
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Can Diced or sliced beets
1 tb Cornstarch
3 tb Sugar
1/4 ts Salt
3 tb Vinegar
2    Whole Cloves
1 tb Butter or margarine
Drain beets, reserving 1/3 cup liquid; set aside. In a 1-quart
casserole combine cornstarch, sugar and salt. Stir in reserved liquid
and vinegar. Add beets and cloves; mix gently. Cover with waxed
paper. Microwave on HIGH for 6 minutes, stirring gently every 2
minutes, or until sauce thickens; stir in butter. Let stand, covered,
5 minutes before serving.

Serves 4.
