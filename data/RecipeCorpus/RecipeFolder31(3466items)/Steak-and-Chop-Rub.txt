Title: Steak and Chop Rub
Categories: Herbs/spice
Yield: 1 cup
2 tb Pepper, coarsely ground
-or cracked
3 tb Onion powder
2 1/2 tb Coarse salt
2 tb Garlic powder
1 tb Worcestershire powder
-look for Le Chateau brand
2 ts Ground ginger
2 ts Dry mustard
1 ts Sugar
Mix all the spices together and store in an airtight container for up
to 8 weeks. Tailored for tender cuts of meats.

Food and Wine June 1995
