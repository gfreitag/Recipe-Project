Title: Southern Bourbon-Mustard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  Dijon mustard
1/4 c  Plus 1 ounce bourbon
1/4 c  Soy sauce
1/2 c  Dark brown sugar
3 tb Worcestershire
1/3 c  Green onions -- minced
3    Garlic cloves -- minced
Mix all ingredients together to make the sauce.

Can be used as either a marinade or a basting sauce for shellfish,
fish, beef or chicken.
