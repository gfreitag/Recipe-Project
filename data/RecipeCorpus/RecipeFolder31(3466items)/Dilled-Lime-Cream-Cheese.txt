Title: Dilled Lime Cream Cheese
Categories: Appetizers, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 batch
1/2 lb Cream cheese, softened
3 lg Scallions
-finely chopped
1 1/2 tb Dill, finely chopped
-plus extra sprigs
-for garnish
1 1/2 ts Lime juice
1/2 ts Lime zest, finely grated
1/4 ts Pepper
In a medium bowl, whip the cream cheese until fluffy. Add the
scallions, chopped dill, lime juice, lime zest and pepper and beat
until combined.
