Title: Guaca-Modem Dip
Categories: Appetizers, Dips/spread
Yield: 3 cups
3    Ripe avocados, peeled and
Seeded
1/2 sm Onion, finely chopped
2 tb Fresh lime juice
1    Clove garlic, crushed
1 md Tomato, diced
Jalapeno pepper to taste,
Seeded & finely chopped
1/4 c  Chopped cilantro leaves
Salt to taste
Chili powder to taste
Tortilla chips for dipping
In medium bowl, roughly mash avocado with a fork. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Set aside for a few minutes to enhance flavors. Serve
with tortilla chips. *From "Quick Bytes: Computer Lover's Cookbook"
by Diane Pfeifer* ~Typed for you by Michelle Bruce
