Title: Mock Condensed Tomato Soup
Categories: Low cal/fat, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 tb All-purpose flour
-cold water
1 c  Tomato juice, low sodium
Make a smooth paste of 1 T flour and a little water.  Add tomato juice
slowly and mix well. Cook until thickened.  You may season with any,
or combination, of the following herbs & spices to taste (no salt):
basil, bay leaf, garlic, marjoram, oregano, parsley, rosemary and
thyme, some or all of which can be found in most commercial condensed
tomato soup . Makes 1 cup.
