Title: Seafood Scampi~
Categories: Fish, Seafood, Main dish
Yield: 4 servings
1/4 c  Margarine
1 md Onion -- cut in wedges
1    Green Pepper
1    Clove Garlic -- finely
Chopped
1 pk Louis Kemp Crab Delights
Flakes
4 oz Mushrooms -- sliced
1 tb Lemon Juice
1 ts Parsley Flakes
1/2 ts Lemon Pepper
6 oz Fettuccini Noodles --
Cooked
Melt margarine in a skillet on med-high.  Saute onion, green pepper
and garlic 3 minutes.  Add remaining Scampi ingredients and stir 3
minutes. Add fettuccini; toss and heat through.

Source:  An ad for Louis Kemp Seafood

Per serving:  Calories 315; fat 12.7 g; cholesterol 30 mg;
carbohydrate 36.8 g; fiber 2.3 g; protein 13.7 g; sodium 431 mg;
potassium 399 mg; calcium 72 mg. Good source of vitamin A.
