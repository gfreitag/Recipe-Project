Title: Grilled Caesar Shrimp Salad
Categories: Salads, Seafood, Vegetables
Yield: 1 servings
Marinade 1 lb large, peeled
And deveined shrimp in
About 1/2 cup of Heinz
Creamy Caesar Salad Dressing
For about 30 minutes.
Thread onto soaked
Bamboo skewers and grill
Using drained dressing as a
Baste.  Toss together
1 head romaine lettuce
With some sliced red
And yellow peppers.
Arrange grilled shrimp on
Top of lettuce.  Garnish
With croutons.  Serve
With fresh dressing on side
Origin: Adapted from Kraft's 'What's Cooking' magazine, 12th issue.
