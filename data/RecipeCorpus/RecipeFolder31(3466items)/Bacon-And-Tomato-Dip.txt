Title: Bacon And Tomato Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
1 pk Cream cheese (8oz) --
Softened
3 tb Milk
2 ts Prepared mustard
6 sl Bacon, crisply cooked --
Crumbled
1 md Tomato, seeded -- finely
Chopped
Beat cream cheese, milk and mustard in med. bowl til well blended.
Stir in bacon and tomato. Cover and refrigerate at least 30 mins.
Yield: about 2 cups
