Title: Paul And Linda Mccartney's Green Pea Soup
Categories: Soup/stew
Yield: 10 servings
1/2 lb Green split peas
1/4 lb Orange lentils
1 1/2 lg Onions -- quartered
4    Stalks celery -- including
-leaves -- rinsed & chopped
2    Tomatoes -- peeled
2    Leeks; white part only --
-cut into 1-inch chunks
5 c  Water (about)
1/4 lb Butter or soy margarine
Crushed black peppercorn
Sea salt
Place split peas, lentils, onions, celery, tomatoes and leeks in
large pot and cover with water.

Bring to boil, lower heat and simmer about 1 1/2 hours, until split
peas and lentils are soft. Add butter and stir until melted. Add
peppercorns and sea salt to taste.

Each serving contains about: 222 calories; 148 mg sodium; 25 mg
cholesterol; 10 grams fat; 26 grams carbohydrates; 10 grams protein;
2.07 grams fiber.

Source: Famous Vegetarians and Their Favorite Recipes by Ryan Berry
(Pythagorean Publishers).

Presented by: Barbara Hansen, Times Staff Writer, L.A. Times article,
"Gentle Beans", 10/20/94, page H29.
