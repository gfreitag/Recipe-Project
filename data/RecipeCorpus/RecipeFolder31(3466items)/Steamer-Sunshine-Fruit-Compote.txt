Title: Steamer Sunshine Fruit Compote
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
6 oz Dry mixed fruit
6 oz Dry apricots
16 oz Can peaches; drained
-in heavy syrup
1/3 c  Orange juice
2 tb Honey
Combine all ingredients in rice bowl. Add 1 cup water to reservoir,
cover and steam 19 to 22 minutes.

Serves 6 to 8

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker Booklet Typos by
Dorothy Flatman 1995
