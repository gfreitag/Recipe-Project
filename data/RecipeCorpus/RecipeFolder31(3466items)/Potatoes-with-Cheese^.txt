Title: Potatoes with Cheese^
Categories: Potatoes, Tasteofhome, Depress/era, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 servings
6 md Potatoes
1 ts Salt
2 tb Flour
1/2 c  Grated cheese
4 tb Butter
Pepper
2    Egg yolks, beaten
2 1/2 c  Milk
Pare potatoes and cut in very thin slices. Cook 5 minutes; drain. Put
about 1/4 of the potatoes in a baking dish, and sprinkle on about 1/4
of the salt, flour, cheese, butter and a little pepper. continue for
3 more layers. Mix the yolks with the milk and pour over all. Bake
for an hour until potatoes are tender and crust is set. Bake at 350
degrees.
