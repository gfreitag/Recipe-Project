Title: Herb-Seasoned Popcorn
Categories: Snacks, Low cal/fat
Yield: 6 servings
6 c  Popped popcorn
2 tb Margarine or butter, melted
1/2 ts Dried basil leaves
1/4 ts Dried oregano leaves
ds To 1/8 ts cayenne pepper
1 tb Grated Parmesan cheese
Place popcorn in large bowl.  In small bowl, combine margarine, basil,
oregano and cayenne; pour evenly over popcorn.  Sprinkle with Parmesan
cheese; toss gently.
