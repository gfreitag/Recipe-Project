Title: Sugarless Blueberry Grunt
Categories: Fruit, Diabetic, Vegetarian
Yield: 3 servings
1 qt Blueberries
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Apple juice concentrate
MMMMM--------------------------DUMPLING-------------------------------
2 c  Sifted flour
3 ts Baking powder
1 ts Salt (scant)
4 tb Shortening
2/3 c  Milk
2    Eggs, beaten
Stew berries, water and apple juice in a saucepan until there is
plenty of juice.

DUMPLING Sift together flour, baking powder and salt. Cut in
shortening. Add milk and beaten eggs.  Drop by tbsp over stewed
berries. Cover tightly and cook 12 minutes.

Source: My adaptation of Dutch Oven original Adapter: Shirley Goos
