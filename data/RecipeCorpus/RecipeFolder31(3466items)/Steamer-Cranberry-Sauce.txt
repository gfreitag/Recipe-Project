Title: Steamer Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Cranberries
2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Water
Combine all ingredients in rice bowl. Add 1-1/2 cups water to
reservoir, cover and steam 26 to 30 minutes.

Makes 1 batch

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker Booklet Typos by
Dorothy Flatman 1995
