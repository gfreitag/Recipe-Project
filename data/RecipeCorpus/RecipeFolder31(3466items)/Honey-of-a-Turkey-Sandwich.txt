Title: Honey of a Turkey Sandwich
Categories: Sandwich
Yield: 4 servings
2 c  Turkey; cooked & cubed
1/2 c  Marie's Honey Mustard
-Dressing and Dip
1    Celery stalk; chopped
1 lg Green onion; chopped
8 sl Dark pumpernickel bread
In medium bowl, combine turkey, dressing, celery and onion. Divide
mixture evenly among 4 bread slices. Top with remaining bread slice.
