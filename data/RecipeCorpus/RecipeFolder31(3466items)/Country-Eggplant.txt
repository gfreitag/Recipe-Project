Title: Country Eggplant
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
1 md Eggplant, pared and cubed
3 tb Bacon grease
1/2 c  Diced onion
8 oz Tomatoes, chopped
1 tb Parsley, cut fine
1 ts Sugar
1/2 ts Chili powder
2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
1 c  Buttered coarse bread crumbs
1/2 c  Grated cheese
Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Grease a 2 quart baking dish.  Saute
eggplant in grease for 5 minutes.  Pour into dish.  Saute onions 2
minutes, stir in all ingredients except crumbs and cheese.  Bring to
a boil and pour over eggplant.  Sprinkle with crumbs and cheese. Bake
for 45 minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   06-05-95
