Title: Jane's Bread Machine Soft Rye
Categories: Breadmaker, Breads
Yield: 1 servings
2 c  Bread flour
2 tb Molasses
1/2 c  Rye flour
1 tb Butter
1 ts Salt
1 c  Water
1 tb Dry milk
1 1/2 ts Yeast
1 ts Caraway seed
Place ingredients in machine according to machine directions. Process
at full bake (4 hours).
