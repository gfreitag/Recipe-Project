Title: Dorothy Flatman's Lasagne
Categories: Pasta, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
12 ea Lasagne noodles; cooked
2 c  Cottage cheese (16 oz)
1/2 c  Parmesan cheese; grated
2 ts Parsley flakes
1 lb Cheeses; sliced
3 c  Spaghetti sauce
Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese and parsley flakes. Divide all
ingredients in half to make 2 each 9x9 baking dishes. In each dish:
Layer noodles, 1/2 of sliced cheese, noodles, all of divided cheese
mixture, noodles, 1/2 of sauce, rest of noodles and sliced cheese.
Top with rest of sauce. Bake at 350 degrees 1 hour.
