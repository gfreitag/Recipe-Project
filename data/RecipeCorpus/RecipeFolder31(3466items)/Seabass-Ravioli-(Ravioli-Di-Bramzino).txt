Title: Seabass Ravioli (Ravioli Di Bramzino)
Categories: Fish, Masterchefs, Italian
Yield: 10 servings
600 g  Pasta dough
500 g  Seabass, meat
250 g  White bread
150 ml Milk
100 g  Parmesan cheese
10 g  Dry marjoram
3    Egg yolks
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Blend seabass with bread, parmesan, marjoram and milk, to a farce.
Add in egg yolks. Salt and pepper to season. Prepare ravioli as in
the traditional manner.
