Title: Quick Hummus
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 recipe
1 c  AM Garbanzo Flour
1 1/2 c  Cold water
4 tb Lemon juice
4 tb AM Sesame Tahini
3/4 ts Sea salt (optional)
1/4 ts Garlic powder; OR...
1 lg -Garlic clove, pressed
2 tb AM Unrefined Olive Oil
In a saucepan stir garbanzo flour into the cold water until smooth.
Heat mixture until bubbly, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat.
Add lemon juice, tahini, garlic and salt if used.  Mix well; cool.
If mixture appears to be too thick, add more water before serving.
Pour oil over hummus, or stir into the mixture.
