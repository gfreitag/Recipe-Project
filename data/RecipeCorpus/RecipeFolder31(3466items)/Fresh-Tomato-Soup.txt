Title: Fresh Tomato Soup
Categories: Italian, Soup/stew, Tomatoes
Yield: 6 servings
6 md Tomatoes -- coarsely
Chopped
1 lg Onion -- chopped
2    Stalks Celery -- chopped
2 c  Chicken Broth
1 tb Tomato Paste
1/2 ts Basil
1/2 ts Oregano
1/4 ts Sage
1/4 ts Pepper -- freshly ground
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Whipped Cream Or Sour Cream
2 tb Chives -- minced
Place all the ingredients except cream and chives in a saucepan.
Simmer uncovered for 40 mins, stirring occasionally. Strain through a
fine sieve. Adjust seasoning. Reheat soup and serve topped with a
spoonful of cream and minced chives.
