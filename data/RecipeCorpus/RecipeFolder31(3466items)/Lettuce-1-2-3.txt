Title: Lettuce 1-2-3
Categories: Salads
Yield: 4 servings
1    Head romaine lettuce, washed
And dried
2 tb Lemon juice
3 tb Olive oil
Salt and freshly ground,
Pepper to taste
Dash garlic powder
Grated Parmesan cheese
Croutons
Tear lettuce into small pieces and place in large serving bowl. In
small bowl, blend lemon juice, oil, salt, pepper and garlic powder.
Toss into salad. Top each serving with Parmesan cheese and croutons.
