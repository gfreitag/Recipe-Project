Title: Just White Beans
Categories: Salads, Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Dry white pea beans
3 tb Olive oil
2 tb Red wine vinegar
1 ts Salt
2 md Garlic cloves, minced
Black pepper
1/2 ts Basil
1/4 c  Parsley, minced
2 ts Dillweed
Pickled red onions, see
-- recipe
Place beans in a medium sized saucepan & cover with water.  Bring to a
boil, reduce heat & cook, partially covered until tender.  About 1
hour. Drain well & transfer to a medium-sized bowl.

Add remaining ingredients, mix gently & taste to correct seasonings if
necessary.  Cover tightly & chill until ready to serve.  Serve topped
with pickled red onions.

Mollie Katzen, "Moosewood Cookbook"
