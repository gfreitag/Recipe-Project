Title: Sweet and Sour Vidalia Onions
Categories: Vegetables, Salads
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Onions (Vidalia, Texas 1015,
. or Walla Walla), sliced
3 c  Cucumbers, peeled, seeded,
. thinly sliced
1/2 c  Carrots, grated
1/2 c  White wine vinegar
1/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Fresh dill, minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Black pepper
Toss onions, cucumbers and carrots together in a bowl.

Combine remaining ingredients and pour over vegetables.  Toss to coat.

Refrigerate for at least two hours before serving.
