Title: California-Style Barbecue Sauce`
Categories: Bbq/grill, Cowboy, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Granulated Sugar
1/2 c  Japanese Soy Sauce
1/2 c  Tomato Catsup
1/2 c  Dry Sherry
3    Cloves Garlic -- crushed
1 ts Ground Ginger
1/2 ts Pepper -- freshly ground
1. In a medium bowl, stir all ingredients until combined. Ccver and
refrigerate until ready to use. (The sauce can be prepared up to 3
days ahead, covered, and refrigerated.) Note: This sauce shows the
influence of Asian ranch cooks.

Makes about 1 1/2 cups from Hearst Ranches of San Simeon, CA.
