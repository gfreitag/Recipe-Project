Title: Creamy Cocoa Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 batch
1 c  AM Whole Wheat Pastry Flour
1/2 c  AM Oat Bran
1/2 c  AM Oat Flakes
1/2 c  Slivered almonds
3/4 c  AM Sesame Tahini (soft)
2 tb Rice syrup; mixed with...
2 tb Water
1 c  Tofu
1/2 c  Ricotta cheese
-OR- low-fat cottage cheese
1/3 c  Cocoa
1/2 c  Maple syrup
1 ts Vanilla; OR...
1/2 ts -Almond extract
Combine first six ingredients to form a crumb mixture.  Set aside (if
crumb mixture does not stick together, add a little oil).  Beat
together remaining ingredients.  Place half of crumb mixture in an
oiled 9" x 12" pan, press down well; cover with cocoa mixture, then
top with remaining crumb mixture.  Bake at 350 F. for 30-35 minutes
or until golden.  Cool and cut into squares.
