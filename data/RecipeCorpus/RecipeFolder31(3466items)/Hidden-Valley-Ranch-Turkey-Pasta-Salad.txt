Title: Hidden Valley Ranch Turkey Pasta Salad
Categories: Salads, Pasta, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Corkscrew pasta; cooked and
-drained
1 c  Turkey; cut into strips
4 c  Raw vegetables; chopped
1/4 c  Parmesan; grated
2 c  Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing
Toss all ingredients in a bowl.  Chill until ready to serve.
