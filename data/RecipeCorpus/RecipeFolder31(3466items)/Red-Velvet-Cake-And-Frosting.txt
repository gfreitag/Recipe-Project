Title: Red Velvet Cake And Frosting
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 1 servings
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2    Eggs
2 1/2 c  Cake flour
1 1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Vanilla
1 ts Baking soda
1 ts Vinegar
5/8 oz Red food coloring
MMMMM--------------------------FROSTING-------------------------------
1 lb Powdered sugar
8 oz Cream cheese
1    Stick butter
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Chopped pecans
Cream eggs, sugar, oil and vinegar.  Sift cake flour and soda
together. Add flour while beating.  Add milk slowly.  While still
beating, add vanilla and food coloring.  Bake in 3 layers at 350
degrees for 25 minutes. Press lightly to check if layers are spongy
and done.

FROSTING: Melt cream cheese and butter on very low heat. Add sugar,
pecans and vanilla.  Mix well and spread over cooled layers, top and
sides.  If mixture becomes too thick, add a little milk.

Makes:  1 3-layer cake
