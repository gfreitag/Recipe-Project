Title: Hard Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  Butter, at room temp
3/4 c  Sugar
2 tb Heavy cream
4 tb Brandy, bourbon, or rum
Beat the butter until very light. Slowly add the sugar. Then beat in
the cream first, then the liquor.

SOURCE: Biscuits, Spoonbread, and Sweet Potato Pie (pg. 142)
