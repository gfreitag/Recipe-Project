Title: Three Bean Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
8 sl Bacon
1 c  Finely diced celery
1 1/2 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Water
6 oz Tomato paste
1/2 c  Packed brown sugar
1    Envelope spaghetti sauce mix
2 tb Prepared mustard
1 ts Garlic salt
2 tb Vinegar
2 cn Large dried lima beans,
Drained
1 cn Kidney beans, drained
1 cn Pork & beans in tomato sauce
Cook bacon until crisp and drain on paper towels.  Pour off all but 2
tablespoons of fat from the skillet.  Add celery and onion to skillet
and saute about 5 minutes.  Add water, tomato paste, brown sugar,
spaghetti sauce mix, mustard, garlic salt and vinegar and bring to a
boil. In a 3 quart casserole combine the beans.  Add the crumbled
bacon, tomato mixture and blend well.  Bake uncovered at 350 degrees
for 1 hour. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   06-02-95
