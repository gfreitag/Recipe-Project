Title: Make A Dream Pie
Categories: Pie/tart
Yield: 1 servings
2    Envelopes Dream Whip
-whipped topping mix
2 3/4 c  Cold milk
1 ts Vanilla
2 pk Jello instant pudding and
Pie filling -- any flavor
(4 serving size)
1    Baked 9-inch pie shell,
-cooled, --OR--
1    Premade graham cracker pie
-crust
--OR--
1    Chocolate flavor crust
Beat whipped topping mix with 1 cup milk and the vanilla, using large
mixer bowl; beat at high with electric mixer about 6 minutes or until
topping thickens and forms peaks. Add remaining milk and pudding mix;
blend at low. Beat at high for 2 minutes, scraping bowl,
occasionally. Spoon into pie shell. Chill at least 4 hours.
