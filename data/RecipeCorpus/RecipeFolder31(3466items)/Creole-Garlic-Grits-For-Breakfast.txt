Title: Creole Garlic Grits For Breakfast
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
1 c  5-MINUTE GRITS
4 c  WATER
6    STRIPS BACON
4    CLOVES GARLIC,MINCED
1    STICK MARGARINE
8    EGGS
SALT & PEPPER TO TASTE
Cook grits, seasoned with 1 teaspoon salt for 5 minutes; add minced
garlic & stir. Fry bacon slices until crisp & crumble well on small
plate. Fry eggs in 1/2 stick margarine,your choice, over or up.

Place 1/4 of grits in center of serving plate & add pat of margarine.
Sprinkle with crumbled bacon & top with two eggs. Season to taste.
Serve with hot biscuits & coffee. (Serves 4)
