Title: Strawberry Jam #2
Categories: Fruit, Low cal/fat
Yield: 3 jelly jar
MMMMM---------------------FROM CHEF FREDDY'S--------------------------
1 c  Strawberrys* [sliced]
3/4 c  Diet soda (or club soda)
1 pk Lo-cal gelatin desert
3 pk (packets) EQUAL sugar sub-
-situte
* may subsitute any fresh
-fruit for strwaberrys.
In a sauce pan combine the berries and the soda and bring to a
1)
boil, cooking for 1 min... remove and stir in the gelatin dessert mix
until it is totally dissolved... 2) Stir in the sugar subsitute, and
pour into jars... *note* I would assume that the jars would have to
be covered and that the jam would have to be refrigerated when not in
use...
