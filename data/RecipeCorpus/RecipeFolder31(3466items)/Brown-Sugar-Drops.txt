Title: Brown Sugar Drops
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1    Egg, well beaten
1/2 c  Unsifted flour
1/4 ts Soda
1 c  Brown sugar, packed
2 c  English walnuts, chop medium
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
Stir together beaten egg, brown sugar, and vanilla.  Stir in flour,
soda, and salt.  Stir in walnuts.  Mix well.  Drop by teaspoons on
greased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 7 to 10 minutes or
until they start to brown on the edges.  Do not overbake.  Remove
immediately and cool. Randy Rigg
