Title: Men's Delight Salad
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
Dissolve:
3 oz Lime gelatin
15 lg Marshmallows
1 c  Boiling water
Keep over low heat until
Marshmallows melt.  Cool
Until partially thickened
And then beat with mixer.
Add:
1 c  Drained crushed pineapple
1 c  Cottage cheese
1 c  Whipped cream
1/4 c  Chopped nuts
Chill until firm. Randy Rigg
