Title: Smooth and Tasty Kidney Bean Dip
Categories: Dips/spread, Appetizers
Yield: 1 recipe
3 c  Cooked AM Kidney Beans
-- (1-1/2 cups dried)
1    Onion; chopped
8 oz Cream cheese; OR...
1 c  -Creamy tofu
1/2 c  Mild picante sauce
2 tb Tamari sauce (optional)
1 tb Chili powder
1/2 c  Green enchilada sauce
1 ts Garlic powder
Blend (in blender or food processor) all ingredients.  Serve with
fresh vegetable pieces or tortilla chips.
