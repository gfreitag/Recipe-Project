Title: Frozen Yogurt Sandwiches
Categories: Fruit, Desserts
Yield: 9 servings
1 c  Plain yogurt; to 2c
1 c  Unsweetened fruit juice
-or 6 oz froz concentrate
-thawed <orange, apple,
-grape or pineapple>
1 ts Vanilla
1 tb Honey
MMMMM--------------------------OPTIONAL-------------------------------
1 ea Ripe banana
10 ea Ripe strawberries
1 tb Unflavored gelatin
1/4 c  Cold water; to 1/2c
Graham crackers
Yogurt and oj make nice popsicles.

If using whole fruit, puree everything in blender or processor.

Gelatin in mixture retards melting on a hot day. Soften in water, heat
slightly to dissolve, blend with yogurt mixture. Pour into molds or
small paper cups

OR

After blending, pour mixture about 1 inch thick in pan lined with
graham crackers.  Top with more crackers.  Freeze until firm.
