Title: Avocado Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
2    Ripe avocados
1    Jalapeno, roasted -- minced
1/2    Red onion -- minced
3    Green onions -- minced
3 tb Cilantro -- roughly chopped
2 tb Rice wine vinegar
6 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper
Cut each avocado in half.  Remove seed, make dice sized criss-crosses
in avocado flesh with a knife, and scoop out dice with a spoon. Place
in a mixing bowl and add chili, onion, green onions and chopped
cilantro. Whisk together vinegar and oil in a separate bowl, add salt
and pepper to taste and pour over avocado mixture. Mix gently, being
careful not to mash avocado or salsa will resemble guacamole and
taste muddy.
