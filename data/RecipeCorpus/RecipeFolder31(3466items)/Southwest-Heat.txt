Title: Southwest Heat
Categories: Bbq/grill, Herbs/spice
Yield: 3 servings
1 c  Dried New Mexico Red Chile
1 c  Ground Dried Ancho Chile
6 tb Salt
6 tb Ground Cumin
1 tb Dried Oregano
Mix spices throughly in a bowl.  Store in a cool, dark pantry.
