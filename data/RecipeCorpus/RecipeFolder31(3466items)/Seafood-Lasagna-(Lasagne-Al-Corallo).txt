Title: Seafood Lasagna (Lasagne Al Corallo)
Categories: Pasta, Masterchefs, Italian
Yield: 10 servings
600 g  Lasagna (dough)
1 1/2    Litres seafood sauce
600 ml Bechamel
150 g  Butter
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Cook pasta until al dente. Mix in seafood sauce, bechamel and butter.
