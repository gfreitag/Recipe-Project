Title: Herb Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 8 servings
1 c  Reduced-calorie margarine,
-melted
1 tb Fresh basil --
Finely chopped
1    Clove garlic -- crushed
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl, and stir well.  Serve sauce
immediately.

Serving Ideas : Alternative to plain butter for lobster.

NOTES : 51 calories per serving of one tablespoon. Higher than the
standard 30% calories from fat, but it contains only half the fat
: Cooking Light, Jul/Aug 1994, p. 92
