Title: Ziti With Grilled Vegetables
Categories: Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
1 lb Ziti pasta
Reserved Grilled Summer
-Squash
1    Roasted red peppers --
-jarred or homemade
1    Clove garlic -- minced
2 tb Onion -- minced
1 tb Olive oil
Salt and pepper
1/4 c  Spicy Tomato Butter
1/3 c  Parmesan cheese -- freshly
-grated
2 tb Basil leaves -- chopped
2 tb Parsley -- chopped
Boil the ziti in a large pot of lightly salted boiling water under
tender, about 12 minutes.  While the pasta is cooking, dice the
reserved squash and the roasted pepper.  Saute the garlic and onion
in the olive oil until softened, about 10 seconds.  Do not allow them
to brown.  Add the squash and roasted pepper and heat through.
Season liberally with salt and pepper.   When the pasta is ready,
drain well and toss with the vegetables, tomato butter, cheese, basil
and parsley.  Season with more salt and pepper if needed and serve.
