Title: Hot Pepper-Lemon Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 24 servings
2 c  Red onion -- minced
1/2 c  Fresh lemon juice
5 lg Jalapeno peppers -- minced
1/2 c  Fresh cilantro -- minced
2    Garlic cloves -- minced
SAUCE: Combine all ingredients in a bowl; stir well.  Cover and
marinate in refrigerator at least 8 hours.  Serve with pork and
feijoada (black beans and sausage Brazilian traditional dish.)
