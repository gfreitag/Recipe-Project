Title: Shiitake Mushroom, Sweet Potato & Hazelnut Sauce
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 quart
2 c  Onions, diced
2 tb Olive oil
1/4 ts Salt
3/4 ts Rosemary, fresh & minced
2 c  Sweet potatoes, diced
1 1/2 c  Vegetable stock
2 tb Water
1 c  Rice milk -=OR=-
-- soy milk
2 tb Hazelnut butter
1 1/2 c  Shiitake mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 tb Tamari
1 pn Black pepper
Saute onions in oil until lightly browned.

In a large pot, combine half of the onions with the salt, 1/2 ts
rosemary, sweet potatoes & 1 1/2 c stock.  Cook until potatoes are
tender. Cool to room temperature & blend with milk & hazelnut butter
until very smooth. Set aside.

Combine remaining onions, mushrooms, tamari, pepper, remaining
rosemary & 2 tb water.  Cook for 5 minutes.  Add to sweet potato
mixture & simmer for 5 minutes.  Serve hot over fresh pasta.

NOTE Only use fresh shiitake mushrooms.
