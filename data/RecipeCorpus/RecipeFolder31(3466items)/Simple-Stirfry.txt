Title: Simple Stirfry
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 pk Green Giant American
-Mixtures San Francisco
-style frozen veggies
1 md Onion; sliced
1 tb Oil
1/3 c  Stir fry sauce; purchased
2 c  Cooked turkey or chicken
-breast strips
In a large skillet saute veggies and onion in oil for 6-8 mins until
crisp tender.

Combine stir fry sauce and turkey.  Add to vegetable mixture, heat
thoroughly.

Serve over rice, if desired.
