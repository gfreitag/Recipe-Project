Title: Fudgy Chip Bars
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 2 dozen
30    Oreo cookies; coarsely
-crushed
3/4 c  Flour
1 c  Coconut
1 c  Chocolate chips
1/4 c  Margarine; melted
14 oz Sweetened condensed milk
1 ts Vanilla
Powdered sugar
In medium bowl, combine cookies, flour, coconut and chocolate chips.
Stir in margarine, condensed milk and vanilla. Spread into greased 8"
square baking pan. Bake at 375 for 25 to 30 minutes. Cool, sprinkle
with powdered sugar.  Cut into bars.  Source: Blue Bonnet
