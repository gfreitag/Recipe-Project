Title: Hot Cakes (Very Good)
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 1 servings
4    Eggs -- well beaten
1 qt Milk
2    Sticks Butter or margarine
Melted
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Salt
4 ts Baking powder
1 1/2 c  Flour
1 tb Vanilla
Add eggs to milk and vanilla. Add butter or margarine, cooled to this
mixture; sugar and salt mixed with flour and baking powder. Mix well,
fry on hot skillet or griddle. Makes very thin batter. Serve with
your favorite syrup.
