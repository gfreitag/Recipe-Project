Title: Butterscotch Squares
Categories: Cake/cookie
Yield: 3 servings
1 lb Brown sugar
1 c  Butter
2    Eggs
2 c  Flour
1 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Walnuts, chopped
Cook sugar and butter in top of double boiler until sugar dissolves.
Cool; add eggs one at a time, beating well after each.  Stir in other
ingredients.  Spread in jelly roll pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25
minutes.  Cut into squares while hot. Randy Rigg
