Title: Steamer Carrot Medley
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 servings
4 c  Carrots; pared & sliced thin
1 sm Onion; thin sliced and
-separated into rings
2 ts Dried parsley flakes
1 ts Sugar
1 ds Salt
1 ts Chicken bouillon granules
Combine all ingredients in rice bowl. Add 1-1/2 cups water to
reservoir, cover and steam for 26 to 30 minutes.

Serves 8

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker Booklet Typos by
Dorothy Flatman 1995
