Title: Beef Jerky #2 (My Favorite)
Categories: Meats, Can/preserv
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 ts Pepper (MORE FOR HOT)
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1/2 ts Onion Salt
1 lb Beef Roast
1/2 c  Soysauce
1/2 ts Garlic Salt
1/2 ts Lemmon Pepper
Marinate 1 hour or overnight.  Bake in oven 150 to 170, overnightfor
10 - 12 hours.
