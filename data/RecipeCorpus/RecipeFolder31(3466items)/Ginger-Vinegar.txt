Title: Ginger Vinegar
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 2 cups vineg
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: BETTY CROCKER'S COOK---------------------
2 c  White wine or white vinegar
1/2 c  Peeled chopped gingerroot
Shake vinegar and gingerroot in tightly covered glass jar or bottle.
Store in cool, dry place 10 days. Strain vinegar.
