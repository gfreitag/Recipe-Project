Title: Tabasco Sauce
Categories: Can/preserv, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3 ea Dozen tabasco or other
-long, hot red peppers
1 ea Clove garlic
1 tb Sugar
1/2 ts Salt
1 ts Horseradish
1 c  Vinegar
Add 1 cup water to the peppers and garlic.  Cook until tender, then
press through fine sieve.  Add other ingredients.  Simmer until
blended. Pour into hot Ball jars; seal at once. The sauce may be
thinned, as used, with either vinegar or salad oil. From: The Ball
Blue Book 1947 Shared By: Pat Stockett
