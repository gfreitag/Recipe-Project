Title: Steamer Candied Apples
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
8 md Cooking apples; peeled,
-cored and chopped
1/4 c  Red hot candies
1/3 c  Brown sugar
1 ds Nutmeg
Place apples into rice bowl. Add 1 cup water to reservoir, cover and
steam 19 to 22 minutes.

Stir in red hots, brown sugar and nutmeg. Add 2/3 cup water to
reservoir, cover and steam 14 to 16 minutes.

Serves 6

Total steaming time: 33 to 38 minutes

Source: Rival Automaatic Steamer And Rice Cooker booklet Typos by
Dorothy Flatman 1995
