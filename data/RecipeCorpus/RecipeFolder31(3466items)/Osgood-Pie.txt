Title: Osgood Pie
Categories: Desserts, Pie/tart, Tasteofhome
Yield: 1 pie (9")
3    Eggs; beaten
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Cinnamon, ground
1/2 ts Cloves, ground
1 1/2 ts Vinegar
1 c  Raisins
2/3 c  Pecans, chopped
1    Pastry shell (9"); unbaked
Combine eggs and sugar; beat well.  Add spices, vinegar, raisins, and
pecans; pour into pastry whell.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
