Title: Creamy Macaroni And Cheese  (April 1975)
Categories: Pasta, Casseroles
Yield: 4 servings
1    Egg
2 c  Milk
2 c  (8oz) Elbow macaroni,
-cooked and drained
8 sl Processed American cheese --
Diced (about 6oz)
1/2 ts Salt -- or to taste
Freshly ground pepper -- to
Taste
Minced green onion --
(optional)
In a large saucepan beat egg and milk until well blended. Stir in
macaroni and cheese and cook and stir over very low heat until cheese
is melted and sauce is thickened. (Do not boil) Stir in salt and
pepper. Pour into serving dish and garnish with onion. Makes 4
servings.
