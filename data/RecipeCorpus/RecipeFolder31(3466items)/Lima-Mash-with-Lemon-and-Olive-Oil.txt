Title: Lima Mash with Lemon and Olive Oil
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
2 c  Lima beans
-fresh or frozen
2    Garlic clove(s), sliced
1/2 c  Salted water
3 tb Olive oil
Lemon juice to taste
Salt and pepper to taste
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Olive oil
Lemon zest
Put lima beans, garlic and salted water in a medium saucepan. Bring
to a boil over medium-high heat. Cover, lower heat, and simmer for
about 15 minutes or until beans are very tender. Coarsely pure beans
and garlic in a food processor. Transfer to a small bowl and
gradually beat in 3 tbs olive oil. Add lemon juice to taste, then
season with salt and pepper. SErve on country-style bread. Drizzle
with a little olive oil and garnish with lemon zest.
