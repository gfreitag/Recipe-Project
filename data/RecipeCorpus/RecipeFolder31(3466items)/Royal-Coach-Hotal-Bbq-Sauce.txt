Title: Royal Coach Hotal Bbq Sauce
Categories: Bbq/grill, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
Chuck Ozburn HBWK07A
1 c  Dry red wine
6 oz Can tomato paste
1/4 c  Olive oil
1 1/2 ts Dried oregano - crushed
1 ts Garlic powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
Mix all ingredients  in a saucepan  and heat to simmering. Brush on
steaks or   burgers,   as desired,  during cooking. Heat  remaining
sauce and pass with the meat. Makes about 1 3/4 cups.
