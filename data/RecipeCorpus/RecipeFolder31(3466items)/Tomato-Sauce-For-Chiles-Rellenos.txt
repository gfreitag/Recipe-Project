Title: Tomato Sauce For Chiles Rellenos
Categories: Tex/mexican, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 2 servings
3 tb Onion -- finely chopped
2 ts Garlic, minced
1 tb Butter
1 cn (15oz) Spanish-style tomato
-sauce
1/3 c  Water
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Crumbled oregano
Saute onion and garlic in butter til golden.  Stir in tomato sauce,
water, salt, and oregano. Simmer for 15 minutes. Serve hot. Makes 2
1/2 cups.
