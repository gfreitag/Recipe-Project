Title: Berry Vinegar
Categories: Salads, Dress/stuff
Yield: 2 cups vineg
MMMMM----------------SOURCE: BETTY CROCKER'S COOK---------------------
2 c  White wine or white vinegar
2 c  Berries, crushed
Shake vinegar and berries in tightly covered glass jar or bottle.
Store in cool, dry place 10 days. Strain vinegar. Place a few berries
in jar to identify, if desired.
