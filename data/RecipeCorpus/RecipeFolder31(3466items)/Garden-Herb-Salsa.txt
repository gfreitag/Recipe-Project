Title: Garden Herb Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 10 servings
1 1/2 c  Diced plum tomato
1/2 c  Red bell pepper -- diced
1/2 c  Yellow bell pepper -- diced
1/4 c  Shallots -- minced
1/4 c  Cilantro -- chopped
1 tb Jalapeno peppers -- minced
1 tb Fresh tarragon -- chopped
1/8 tb Balsamic vinegar -- or
Sherry vinegar
2    Cloves garlic -- crushed
Combine all ingredients in a bowl, and stir well. Cover and chill at
least 30 minutes.
