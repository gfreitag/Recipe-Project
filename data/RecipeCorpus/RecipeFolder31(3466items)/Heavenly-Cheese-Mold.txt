Title: Heavenly Cheese Mold
Categories: Salads
Yield: 3 servings
1 pk Lemon Jello
1 c  Boiling water
3/4 c  Syrup from pineapple
1 c  Mayonnaise
1 1/2 c  Drained, crushed pineapple
1 c  Sharp Cheddar cheese, shred
Dissolve gelatin in boiling water; add syrup.  Gradually add to
mayonnaise and mix until blended.  Chill until slightly thickened.
Fold in pineapple and cheese; pour into a 1 1/2 quart mold.  Chill
until firm; unmold. Randy Rigg
