Title: Chunky Salsa
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
4    Green onions -- chopped
1 lg Tomato -- finely chopped
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1/4 c  + 2 T green olives --
Chopped
1 1/2 tb Dried cilantro
2 tb Lime juice
2 1/4 ts Olive oil
2 ts Minced jalepeno pepper
3    Cloves garlic -- chopped
1/4 ts Salt
1/8 ts Cracked blk pepper
Combine all. Cover and refridgerate 6 -8 hours. Formatted by Olivia
Lieberman
