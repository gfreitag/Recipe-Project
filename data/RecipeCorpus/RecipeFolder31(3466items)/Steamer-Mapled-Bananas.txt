Title: Steamer Mapled Bananas
Categories: Fruit
Yield: 4 servings
2 tb Butter or margarine
3 tb Maple syrup
5 ea Bananas; 1/2" slices
1 tb Lemon juice
Place butter in rice bowl. Add 1/4 cup water to reservoir, cover and
steam 6 to 7 minutes.

Stir in syrup. Add bananas. Stir to coat bananas with syrup mixture.
Add 1/2 cup water to reservoir, cover and steam 10 to 12 minutes.

Sprinkle with lemon juice. Serve warm.

Serves 4

Total steaming time: 16 to 19 minutes.
