Title: Kohlrabi Kebabs
Categories: Main dish, Bbq/grill, Vegetables
Yield: 4 servings
12 sm Kohlrabi bulbs, peeled
1 tb Sesame oil
1 tb Brown rice syrup
2 tb Water
2 tb Sherry
1/2 c  Black bean paste
Preheat the broiler.

Fill a large pot with water & bring to a boil.  Parboil the kohlrabies
until heated through, about 5 minutes.  Drain.

Place 3 bulbs on each of 4 skewers & brush with oil.  Place the
skewers under a broiler until tender, about 8 minutes, rotating every
two minutes. Remove & set aside.

In a small bowl, combine syrup, water, sherry & black bean paste.
Brush liberally over the kohlrabies & return to the broiler.  Remove
when the paste is dry & has formed a glaze.  Serve over boiled rice,
sprinkled with slivered almonds.
