Title: Veal Chops With Mushroom Sauce
Categories: Meats, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 6 servings
1/3 c  Flour
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
6    Veal shoulder chops
4 tb Oil
1    Clove Garlic -- chopped
2    10.5oz cans cream of
-mushroom soup, condensed
1 tb Paprika
3/4 ts Salt
1 ds Marjoram
6    Button mushrooms (optional)
Parsley -- chopped
(optional)
Combine flour, salt and pepper in a paper bag. Drop chops in and shake
until each chop is coated with flour. Heat oil in a heavy skillet with
garlic. Add chops and brown on both sides. Drain off fat. Mix mushroom
soups with paprika, salt and majoram and pour over chops. Cover and
cook, stirring occasionally, over low heat about 20 minutes. Place
chops on a serving plate. Pour soup over chops. Garnish each chop
with a button mushroom with a dab of chopped parsley on top, if
desired.

Serving ideas:  French fries, brussel sprouts, hot rolls, jellied
fruit salad, and brownies.
