Title: Low Fat Berry Bavarian
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1 1/2 c  Boiling water
1 pk 8 serv size or 2 pk 4 serv
-size Jello brand raspberry
-or strawberry sugar free
-low calorie gelatin dessert
1 c  Cold water
Ice cubes
2 3/4 c  Thawed cool whip lite
-whipped topping
Stir boiling water into gelatin in large bowl 2 mins or until
completely dissolved.  Mix cold water and ice cubes to make 2 cups.
Add to gelatin, stirring until slightly thickened.  Remove any
remaining ice.

Stir in whipped topping with wire whisk until smooth.  Spoon into 8
dessert dishes.

Refrigerate 2 hours or until firm.  Garnish with additional whipped
topping, raspberies and mint, if desired.  Store leftover dessert in
refrigerator.

Per serving contains 60 calories and 3 grams of fat. From Jello ad
