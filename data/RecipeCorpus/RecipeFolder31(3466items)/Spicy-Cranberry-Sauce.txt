Title: Spicy Cranberry Sauce
Categories: Fruit, Salsa/sauce
Yield: 16 servings
1 cn Cranberry sauce -- jellied
2 tb Prepared horseradish
2 tb Honey
1 tb Lemon juice
2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Ground red pepper
1 sm Garlic clove -- minced
Combine all ingredients in a saucepan; stir well.  Bring to a boil;
cover, reduce heat, and simmer 5 minutes.  Serve warm.  Yield: 2 cups.

Serving Ideas:  Serve with pork, shrimp, or chicken

NOTES:  28 CALORIES per tablespoon Recipe By
