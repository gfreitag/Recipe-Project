Title: Country Style Nacho's
Categories: Snacks, Tex/mexican, Cheese/eggs, Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1    Bag (200 gram) nacho chips,
-round style
1 c  Grated colby cheese
1 c  Seeded, chopped tomato
1 c  Kernel corn
1 c  Canned kidney beans, rinsed,
-drained and blotted dry
1 ts Chili powder
1/4 c  Chopped green onions
Place 1/2 of chips into baking dish.  Arrange 1/2 of remaining
ingredients over top.  Cover with other 1/2 of nacho's and top with
remaining ingredients.  Bake in a 400 F oven for about 10 minutes.

Origin: Adapted from Kraft 'What's Cooking' magazine, 12 th issue.
Shared by: Sharon Stevens, May/95.
