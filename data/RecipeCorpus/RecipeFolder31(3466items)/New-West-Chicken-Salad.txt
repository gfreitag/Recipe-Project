Title: New West Chicken Salad
Categories: Poultry, Salads
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Lowfat mayonnaise
1/2 ts Ground ginger -- optional
1/2 ts Salt
3 c  Cubed cooked chicken
1 1/2 c  Red seedless grapes
1 c  Sliced celery
1/3 c  Sliced green onions
1/2 c  Broken walnuts or pecans
In a large bowl combine first 3 ingredients. Stir in remaining
ingredients. Cover; chill. Makes 5 1/2 cups.
