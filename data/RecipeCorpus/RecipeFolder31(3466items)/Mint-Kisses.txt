Title: Mint Kisses
Categories: Herbs/spice, Candies
Yield: 5 dozen
4    Egg whites
1/8 ts Cream of tartar
1 1/2 c  Confectioners' sugar
1 dr Green food coloring
1/4 c  Finely chopped fresh mint
-leaves
Preheat oven to 225F.  Beat the egg whites and cream of tartar to a
stiff froth.  Add the confectioners' sugar and coloring and whip 3 to
5 minutes, or until the mixture forms a stiff paste.  Fold in the
mint.

Drop the paste from a spoon, swirling the top, onto a cookie sheet
lined with parchment paper.  Bake 40 to 45 minutes, or until the
kisses are completely dry and have small cracks in the top.  Cool
completely and store, covered, at room temperature.
