Title: Snow Peas Oriental
Categories: Vegetables, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
x  Vegetable cooking spray
1 ts Light sesame oil
1/2 lb Fresh snow pea pods, trimmed
1/2 c  Diagonally sliced carrot
1/4 c  Sliced water chestnuts
1/2 c  Canned no-salt-added chicken
-broth, undiluted
1 ts Low-sodium soy sauce
1 ts Cornstarch
Coat a nonstick skillet with cooking spray.  Add oil, and place over
medium-high heat until hot.  Add snow peas and carrot.  Saute 2
minutes. Add water chestnuts and broth.  Bring to a boil.  Cover,
reduce heat, and simmer 5 minutes or until vegetables are
crisp-tender.

Combine soy sauce and cornstarch, stirring until cornstarch
dissolves. Add to vegetale mixture.  Cook over medium heat, stirring
constantly, until suace th8ickens.  Serve immediately.  (Serving
size:  1/2 cup.)

Calories 51 / Protein 2.1 / Fat 1.4 / Carbohydrate 7.3 / Cholesterol
0 / Iron 1.3 / Sodium 41 / Calcium 28
