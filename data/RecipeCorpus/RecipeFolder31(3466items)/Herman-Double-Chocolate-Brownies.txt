Title: Herman Double Chocolate Brownies
Categories: Cake/cookie, Chocolate, Herman, Breads, Sourdough
Yield: 1 servings
3/4 c  Butter, room temperature
1 c  Granulated sugar
1 c  Packed brown sugar
4 x  Eggs
1/2 c  Unsweetened cocoa
1 1/2 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 c  Herman Sourdough Starter
1 tb Vanilla
3/4 c  Semisweet chocolate pieces
1/2 c  Chopped nuts
In bowl of electric mixer, cream butter and sugars. Add eggs one at a
time, beating well after each. Stir dry ingredients together. Combine
starter and vanilla; add alternately with dry ingredients to sugar
mixture. Spread in greased 11 1/2 x 7 1/2 x 1 1/2" pan. Sprinkle
chocolate and nuts on top. Bake in preheated 350F oven 30 min.
