Title: Black Ink and Cuttlefish Sauce
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Salsa/sauce, Condiments
Yield: 1 servings
100 g  Cuttlefish
30 ml Squid ink
70 g  Onion
10 ml Olive oil
1    Glass wine
100 g  Parmesan cheese
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Fry onion in oil. Add cuttlefish and saute for a few minutes. Add
wine and reduce by 1/2. Add desired pasta, stir in squid ink, add
parmesan. Source: Given to share with the express permission of Chef
de Cuisine
