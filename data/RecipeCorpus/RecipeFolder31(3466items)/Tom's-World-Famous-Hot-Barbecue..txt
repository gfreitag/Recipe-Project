Title: Tom's World Famous Hot Barbecue.
Categories: Meats, Bbq/grill, Stove top
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Round bottom roast
MMMMM----------------------BBQ INGREDIENTS---------------------------
1/2 c  White vinegar
1/2 c  Catsup
1/4 c  Water
1 sm Onion, diced, cut into small
-pieces
1 1/2 ts Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Worcestershire sauce
1 1/2 ts Prepared mustard (like
-French's)
1/4 ts Salt (I leave this out as
-the Worcestershire sauce is
-salty)
1/8 ts Pepper
Touch of crushed red peppers
(depending on how hot you
-want it..it's the same
-stuff you put on pizza if
-you have a mind to)
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl. Take your roast, slice it raw,
place it in a pan, add the above and simmer till it's easily sliced
with a spoon or however you like it. You may have to double up on the
above. Depends on how long you cook it.
