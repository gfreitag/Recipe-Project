Title: Steamer Italian Extravaganza
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 6 servings
1/2 lb Broccoli flowerettes
1/2 lb Cauliflower flowerettes
1 ea Zucchini; sliced 1/2" thick
8 oz Mushrooms
1 c  Cherry tomatoes
1/2 ts Garlic salt
3 tb Parmesan cheese; grated
Arrange vegetables except tomatoes in steamer basket. Add 3/4 cup
water to reservoir, cover and steam 16 to 17 minutes.

Add cherry tomatoes. Sprinkle all with salt and cheese. Add 1/4 cup
water to reservoir, cover and steam 6 to 7 minutes.

Serves 4 to 6

Total steaming time: 22 to 24 minutes.

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker booklet Typos by
