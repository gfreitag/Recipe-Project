Title: Tangy Chicken Salad
Categories: Salads, Poultry
Yield: 4 servings
1 c  Pineapple, chunked & drained
1/2 c  Plain yogurt
2 c  Chopped chicken or turkey
1/2 ts Curry powder
3/4 c  Red grapes, halved
1/3 c  Oats, uncooked
1/2 c  Sliced celery
1/3 c  Dry roasted peanuts
Stir curry into yogurt and add all ingredients except oats and
peanuts. Add those immediately before serving and mix. Makes 4
servings at 275 calories each.
