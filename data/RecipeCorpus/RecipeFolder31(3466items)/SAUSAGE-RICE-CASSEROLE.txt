Title: SAUSAGE RICE CASSEROLE
Categories: Main dish, Sausage, Rice, Casseroles
Yield: 1 servings
1 lb Bulk hot sausage
1 c  Chopped celery
1 c  Chopped onion
1 c  Chopped green peppers
2 cn Chicken broth
1 c  Uncooked regular rice
1 1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
Brown sausage in heavy skillet, add celery, onion, & green pepper and
simmer until tender.  Add broth, ricce & seasonings, stir to mix.
Pour into 2 qt. casserole, cover & bake at 325 for one hour.
