Title: Chilaquiles Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Tex/mexican
Yield: 1 servings
3 c  Tomatillo salsa
1 c  Whipping cream
Salt and pepper
Combine Tomatillo Salsa, whipping cream and salt and pepper to taste.
Mix well.  Makes 1 quart.
