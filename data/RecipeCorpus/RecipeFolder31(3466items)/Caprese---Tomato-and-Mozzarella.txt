Title: Caprese - Tomato and Mozzarella
Categories: Masterchefs, Italian, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 6 servings
1 kg Mozzarella sliced
1 kg Fresh tomato
200 ml Olive oil
40 g  Dry oregano
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Put sliced mozzarella and fresh tomato on the plate one by one (like a
circle). Sprinkle with oregano and salt.
