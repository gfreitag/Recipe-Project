Title: J&Js Outdoor Style Hickory Bbq Sauce
Categories: Salsa/sauce, Bbq/grill
Yield: 1 servings
1/2 c  White or cider vinegar
1 c  Tomato ketchup
4 tb Water
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Soy sauce
2 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Mrs. Dash seasoning
1/2 ts Garlic sauce
2 ts Tomato paste -- heaping
1 ts Colgins hickory liquid
Smoke
1 ts Kitchen bouquet browning
And seasoning sauce
1/4 sm Onion -- chopped finely
Stir until brown sugar dissolves. Make the night before and let stand
in the refrigerator.  Put it on and Enjoy...... Formatted by Olivia
Liebermann.
