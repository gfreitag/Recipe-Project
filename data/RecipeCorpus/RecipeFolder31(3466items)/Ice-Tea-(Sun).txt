Title: Ice Tea (Sun)
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 4 servings
4    Tea bags
1 1/2 qt Cold water
In a two quart clear glass container, place tea bags.  Add 1 1/2
quarts of cold water.  Cover.  Let stand in full sun 2 to 3 hours or
until desired strength.  Remove tea bags, serve over ice.

Store this tea in refrigerator.

NOTE

You don't need the sun to brew Sun Tea.  Simply prepare as directed
above, except let stand at room temperature several hours or until
desired strength.
