Title: Cucumber Dip
Categories: Dips/spread
Yield: 1 servings
2    8 oz pkgs Philadelphia
-Cream Cheese (1 lb)
1 md Cucumber, seeded -- coarsely
Shredded
4    Green onions -- minced or
Chopped
3    Squirts Tabasco sauce
2 tb Mayonaise
Salt and pepper to taste
Mix all ingredients in blender. Chill.
