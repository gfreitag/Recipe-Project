Title: Low-cal Blueberry Ice Cream
Categories: Desserts, Fruit, Ice cream
Yield: 1 gallon
1/2 ga 2% Low fat milk
1/2 c  Almonds, blanched, skinned,
- chopped, roasted 10 min
- at 350
20    Envelopes Equal
1 tb Vanilla
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding
2    Envelopes Dream Whip
1 c  Blueberries
Prepare Dream Whip according to directions on package, using 1 cup
milk (omit vanilla).  Cut blueberries and mash with fork.  Mix with 3
envelopes Equal.  Blanch, skin, chop and roast almonds for 10 minutes
at 350. Stir together 1 cup milk, 17 envelopes Equal and 1 Tablespoon
vanilla until dissolved.  Mix vanilla pudding with 2 cups milk. Put
in 1 gallon freezer can, then pour remaining milk of the 1/2 gallon 2
percent. Freeze. Delectable!  Makes 16 servings, 161 calories per
serving.

Source:  King's Orchard, Plantersville, Texas
