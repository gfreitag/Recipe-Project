Title: Cheese And Carrot Corps
Categories: Kids, Snacks
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Whipped Cream Cheese
1/4 c  Peanut Butter
2 tb Carrot; Shredded
Blend the cream cheese and peanut butter together and mix well.  Add
the shredded carrot, mixing well.  Serve on toasted raisin bread or
toasted bagels.
