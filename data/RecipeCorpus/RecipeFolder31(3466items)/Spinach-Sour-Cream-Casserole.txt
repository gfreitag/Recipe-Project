Title: Spinach Sour Cream Casserole
Categories: Casseroles, Vegetables
Yield: 3 servings
3    Pkgs frozen spinach, chopped
8 oz Sour cream
1/2    Envelope dry onion soup
Grated Cheddar cheese
Bread crumbs
Cook spinach according to directions on package, drain.  Mix spinach,
sour cream, and onion soup mix.  Place in casserole, sprinkle top
with bread crumbs and cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 30
minutes. Randy Rigg Submitted By RANDY RIGG   On   06-01-95
