Title: Steamer Trout With Herb Stuffing
Categories: Seafood
Yield: 3 servings
3 ea Pan-dressed trout
-fresh or thawed
1/2 ts Salt
2 c  Herb stuffing
1/3 c  Butter or marg; melted
1/2 c  Fresh mushrooms; sliced
1/3 c  Green onions; sliced
1 tb Parsley; chopped
1 tb Pimento; chopped
2 ts Lemon juice
1/4 ts Margarine
Wash and dry trout. In mixing bowl, combine remaining ingredients.
Stuff fish and place in steamer basket. Add 1-1/2 cups water to
reservoir, cover and steam from 26 to 30 minutes.

Serves 3

Source: Rival Automatic Steamer And Rice Cooker booklet Typos by
Dorothy Flatman 1995
