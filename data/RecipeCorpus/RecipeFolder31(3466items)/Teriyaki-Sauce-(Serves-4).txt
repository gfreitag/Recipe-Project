Title: Teriyaki Sauce (Serves 4)
Categories: Salsa/sauce
Yield: 1 servings
3 tb Soy sauce
3 tb Water
2 tb Sugar
3 tb Sherry or bourbon
1 pn Monosodium glutamate (MSG)
2 ts Tarragon vinegar
2    Cloves garlic, split
2    Green onions, split
1/2 ts Sesame seed, crushed
Combine soy sauce, water, sugar, tarragon vinegar, garlic and onions
in saucepan.  Simmer to cook garlic and onion.  Then remove and add
sherry, MSG and sesame seed.
