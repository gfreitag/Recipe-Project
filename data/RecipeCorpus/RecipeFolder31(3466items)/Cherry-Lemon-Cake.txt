Title: Cherry Lemon Cake
Categories: Desserts, Cake/cookie
Yield: 12 servings
1    Lemon cake mix
3    Eggs
1/2 c  Graham cracker crumbs
21 oz Cherry pie filling
Powdered sugar
Preheat oven to 375.  In large mixer bowl beat cake mix and eggs on
medium speed for 1 minute.  Spoon into a greased 13 x 9 baking pan
and spread evenly; sprinkle with crumbs. Spoon pie filling over
batter. Bake 35 to 40 minutes.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar before
serving. Source: Comstock ad Submitted By MEG ANTCZAK   On   06-02-95
