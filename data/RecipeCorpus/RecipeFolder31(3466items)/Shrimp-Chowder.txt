Title: Shrimp Chowder
Categories: Soup/stew, Seafood
Yield: 5 servings
2 c  Diced potatoes
3/4 c  Chopped onions
1/4 c  Finely chopped celery
1 1/2 c  Water
1 ts Salt
Freshly ground pepper
2 c  Half-and-half or evaporated
Milk
8 oz Uncooked shelled shrimp
1 tb Minced parsley
Bring potatoes, onion, celery, water, salt and pepper to a boil in a
large saucepan.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer about 10 minutes.  Add
half-and-half or evaporated milk and shrimp and continue to simmer to
a few minutes longer, or until shrimp turns pink.  Ladle into soup
bowls; sprinkle with parsley.
