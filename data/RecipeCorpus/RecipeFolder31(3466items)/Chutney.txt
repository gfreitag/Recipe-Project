Title: Chutney
Categories: Condiments, Relishes, Fruit
Yield: 8 servings
1/4 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Lemon rind; chopped
5    Fresh figs; diced (or dried
-& soaked overnight)
2    Papayas; diced -OR-
4 c  Mexican papaya or honeydew
1/4 c  Onion; finely diced
1 c  Green pepper or green
-cabbage; diced
2 md Bananas; thinly sliced
Pour lemon juice over figs and papayas and let sit 1 hour.  Mix in
remaining ingredients and pack tightly into a casserole dish.  Bake
in a 350 degree F oven for 20 minutes or microwave for 5 minutes.
Chill before serving.

Cal. 86, Protein 1 g, Fat 0.5 g, Chol. 0

From _Eating In: From Field to Kitchen in Biosphere 2_ by Sally
Silverstone.  The Biosphere Press.
