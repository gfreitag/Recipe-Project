Title: Citrus Iced Tea ala Mode
Categories: Beverages, Fruit, Ice cream
Yield: 6 servings
3 c  Double strength cold tea
1/2 c  Chilled orange juice
1 pt Vanila ice cream
Blend ingredients until smooth and pour into a tall glass.

Origin: Women's Circle, Savory Cooking Shared by: Sharon Stevens,
May/94.
