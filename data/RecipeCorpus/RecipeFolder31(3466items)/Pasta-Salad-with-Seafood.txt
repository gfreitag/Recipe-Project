Title: Pasta Salad with Seafood
Categories: Salads, Seafood, Masterchefs, Italian
Yield: 6 servings
600 g  Pasta, any kind
300 g  Squid cooked and sliced
300 g  Cuttlefish, cooked and
-sliced
300 g  Shrimp
200 g  Clams
200 g  Mussels
200 ml Olive oil
50 ml Lemon juice
70 g  Parsley
Salt and pepper
Giuliano Gasali of the Grand Formosa Regent Hotel Taipei.

Cook the pasta 'al dente' leave it cold. Mix all together. Source:
Given to share with the express permission of Chef de Cuisine
