Title: Grilled Sweet Peppers
Categories: Side dish, Vegetables, Bbq/grill, Low cal/fat
Yield: 4 servings
4 tb Red wine vinegar
1 tb Dijon mustard
2 tb Red onion, minced
4 ea Garlic cloves, minced
Pepper, to taste
2 lg Red bell peppers, quartered
2 lg Green bell peppers, quarter'
Preheat broiler or grill.  In a small bowl, combine the mustard,
onion, garlic & pepper.

Place bell peppers on the grill or broiler & brush with the mustard
mixture.  Cook until lightly browned, then turn.  Brush the other
side & cook until browned & slightly softened.  Serve hot or cold
with what is left of the marinade.

"Vegetarian Times" August, 1994
