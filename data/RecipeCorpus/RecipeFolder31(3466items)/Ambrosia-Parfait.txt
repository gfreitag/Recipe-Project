Title: Ambrosia Parfait
Categories: Desserts, Fruit
Yield: 6 servings
1 1/2 c  Milk; 2%
8 oz Pineapple, crushed; drain &
-reserve juice
1 pk Vanilla sugarfree pudding;
-4 serving size
1 sm Banana; diced
1 c  Strawberries; sliced
1/4 c  Coconut
Pour milk and reserved juice from pineapple into medium bowl. Add
pudding mix.  Beat with wire whisk 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in banana.
Spoon 1/2 of the pudding mixture evenly in 6 dessert glasses.  Layer
with strawberries, pineapple and coconut.  Spoon remaining pudding
mixture over fruit. Refrigerate until ready to serve. 110 calories
and 2.5 grams fat. Source: Kraft ad
