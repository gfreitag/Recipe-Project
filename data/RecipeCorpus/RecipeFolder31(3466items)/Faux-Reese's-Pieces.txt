Title: Faux Reese's Pieces
Categories: Candies, Desserts
Yield: 1 servings
1/4 c  Margarine
1/4 c  Peanut butter
3/4 c  Powdered sugar
1/3 c  Graham cracker crumbs
1/2 c  Chocolate chips
1.  Heat margarine and peanut butter in small saucepan over low heat
until melted.

2.  In a medium bowl, stir sugar and graham cracker crumbs.

3.  Add margarine and peanut butter mixture.  Stir until blended.

4.  Press into 9 x 9 pan.

5.  Melt chocolate chips in microwave, then spread onto pressed
mixture.

6.  Chill.

7.  Cut to desired size.
