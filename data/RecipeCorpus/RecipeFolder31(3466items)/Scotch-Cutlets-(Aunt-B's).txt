Title: Scotch Cutlets (Aunt B's)
Categories: British isl, Vegetarian
Yield: 4 servings
4 oz Oatmeal
1 oz Flour
1/2 ts Salt
2 oz Margarine
1    Dessertspoonful chopped leek
Parsley
Milk
Egg and breadcrumbs
Mix oatmeal with flour,leeks and salt.Stir in half a teaspoonful
chopped parsley if likedk.Melt margarine and add with milk to make a
soft mixture.Leave to swell and become firm. Shape into cutlets and
coat with egg and breadcrumbs.Fry in smoking hot fat until golden
brown.Drain and serve on a mound of mashed potatoes with mixed
vegetables.Serve very hot.
