Title: Orange Bergamot Ice Ring
Categories: Beverages
Yield: 1 batch
Orange slices
Orange bergamot leaves
Cold iced tea
-- enough to fill punch bowl
Ice cubes
In the bottom of a ring mold, place orange slices and orange bergamot
leaves in a pleasing design.  Cover with 1/4" of water and freeze.
Fill mold with ice cubes (to hold fruit and leaves at the bottom of
the mold), and add very cold water to fill remaining spaces.  Freeze
mold until solid, preferably overnight.  Unmold into tea-filled punch
bowl with fruit and leaves up.

The editors wrote: "This elegant and festive addition to a summer
punch bowl is ridiculously easy to make."

From Marge Patrick/Clovis, CA in "The Kitchen Table: Where Herbs and
Spices Make a Difference" column in "The Herb Companion."  Aug./Sept.
