Title: Bran Muffins
Categories: Muffins
Yield: 12 servings
2 c  Bran
1 ts Salt
1/2 c  Molasses
1 c  Raisins (optional)
1 c  White flour
1/4 c  Cornmeal
1 1/4 c  Skim milk
1 ts Baking soda, dissolved in wa
Mix all ingredients together and pour into a muffin tin, using either
nonstick pan or paper liners.  Bake 325F for 25 min.
