Title: Pecan Pie 1
Categories: Desserts
Yield: 8 servings
3 ea Egg
1 c  Karo; white
1 ts Vanilla
1 c  Pecans; chopped
1/2 ea Butter; stick
1 c  Sugar
3 tb Corn meal
1 ea Salt; pinch
1 ea Pie shell
per serving: 250 Fat grams per serving: 30 Approx. Cook Time:
Calories
1:30 Cholesterol per serving: 20 Add all ingredients together in bowl, and
mix well.  Pour into pie shell, and bake 1 hour at 300 degrees. Allow to
cool and serve.
