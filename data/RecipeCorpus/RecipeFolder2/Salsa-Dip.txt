Title: Salsa Dip
Categories: Dips, Mexican, Salsa
Yield: 4 servings
1 tb Olive oil
1 tb Vinegar
1 tb Garlic salt
4 ea Tomatoes (chopped/drained)
2 pk Green onions (chopped)
1 cn Black olives (chopped)
1 cn Green chilies (chopped)
Mix; refrigerate at least 1 hour (better overnight).  Serve with
tortilla chips.
