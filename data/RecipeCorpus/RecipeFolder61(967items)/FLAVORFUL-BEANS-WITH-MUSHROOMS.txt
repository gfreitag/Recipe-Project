Title: FLAVORFUL BEANS WITH MUSHROOMS
Categories: Vegetarian, Main dish, Easy
Yield: 4 Servings
12 oz Mushrooms; finely chopped
12 oz Sacallions; finely chopped
12 oz Aduki beans; cooked
3 tb Olive oil
2 ts Tarragon
1/2 ts Dill
1/2 ts Salt
Preheat oven to300 degrees. Lighly grase a 4 x 8 baking dish with
sesame oil. Combine all ingredients and place in the baking dish.
Bake for 25 minutes with a cover.
