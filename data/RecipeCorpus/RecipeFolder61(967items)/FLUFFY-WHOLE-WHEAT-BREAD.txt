Title: FLUFFY WHOLE WHEAT BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 24 Ounces
1/2 c  Milk
1/2 tb Oil
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 tb Syrup, corn
2 ts Yeast
2 1/2 c  Flour, whole wheat
2 c  Rice, cooked
1/2 c  Water; warm
Scald milk; add oil, salt, and corn syrup.  When lukewarm, add ALL
ingredients to breadmachine.  Set "baking control" at 11 o'clock.
Select "white bread" and push Start.
