Title: FIESTA APPETIZERS^
Categories: Crs, Totest, Post, Chopmeat
Yield: 48 Servings
1 lb Ground beef
1 lb Bulk sausaage
1 md Onion, chopped
1 lb Mexican style Velveets,
Cubed
1 tb Worcestershire
1 ts Dried oregano
Salt and pepper to taste
Party rye bread
Brown beef, pork and onion until onion is tender; drain. Stir in
remaining ingredients except bread.

Spread rye bread with about 1 tablespoon mixture each and broil until
hot and bubbly. May be frozen and broiled without defrosting.
