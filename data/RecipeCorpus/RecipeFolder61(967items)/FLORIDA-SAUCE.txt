Title: FLORIDA SAUCE
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Butter
1/2 c  Brown sugar -- packed
1 c  Tomato  puree
1/2 c  Vinegar
6 oz Horseradish
1/2 c  Lime juice
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
2 ts Tabasco sauce
In a large saucepan melt butter and add remaining ingredients. Simmer
slowly for 20-25 min., stir frequently to prevent burning. Sauce can
be used immediately. Use this sauce the same day becauce flavors fade
quickly.
