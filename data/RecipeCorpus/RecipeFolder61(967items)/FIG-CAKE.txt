Title: FIG CAKE
Categories: Cakes
Yield: 1 Servings
3/4 c  Margarine
2 c  Sugar
1 c  Buttermilk
1 ts Soda
1/2 ts Salt
4    Eggs
1 ts Cinnamon
1 ts Allspice
1 ts Vanilla
3 c  Flour
1 pt Figs
1 c  Nuts, chopped
Mix figs well in mixer.  Add eggs, sugar and margarine. Add the other
ingredients and mix well. Pour into well greased glass dish. Bake at
350 degrees for 1 hour or until done. Randy Rigg
