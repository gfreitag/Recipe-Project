Title: FRED DREXEL'S  1981 WINNING RECIPE CHILI
Categories: Chili
Yield: 4 Servings
2 1/2 lb Beef brisket, 1" cubes
1 lb Lean pork, ground
1 ea Large onion, chopped finely
2 tb Wesson oil
1 x  Salt and pepper to taste
3 ea Cloves garlic, minced
2 tb Diced green chilies
8 oz Tomato sauce
1 ea Beef bouillon cube
12 oz Budweiser beer
1 1/4 c  Water
4 tb Chili powder (or 6 T if desi
2 1/2 tb Ground cumin
1/8 ts Dry mustard
1/8 ts Brown sugar
1 pn Oregano
In a large kettle or Dutch oven, brown the beef, pork and onions in
hot Wesson oil.  Add salt and pepper to taste. Add remaining
ingredients. Stir well.
Cover and simmer 3 to 4 hours, until meat is tender and chili is
thick and bubbly.  Stir occasionally. Serves 4
