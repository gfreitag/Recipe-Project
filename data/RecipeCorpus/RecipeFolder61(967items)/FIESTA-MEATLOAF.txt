Title: FIESTA MEATLOAF
Categories: Hamburger, Main dish, Spicy, Meat loaf
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Onion, chopped
1/2 c  Celery, chopped
1/4 c  Green pepper, chopped
1 tb Vegetable oil
1 cn Chili sauce,heinz 12 oz
1 1/2 lb Ground beef, very lean
1 c  Bread crumbs
2 tb Worcestershire sauce
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
Saute onion and celery and green pepper in oil until vegetables are
just soft. remove from heat.  stir into chili sauce, combine 1/2
vegetable sauce mix with ground beef, bread crumbs, egg,
worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper.  shape into a loaf on a
shallow oven proof dish. bake 350 1 hour. let stand 5 minutes. heat
remaining sauce. slice meat loaf, pour remaining sauce over slices.
