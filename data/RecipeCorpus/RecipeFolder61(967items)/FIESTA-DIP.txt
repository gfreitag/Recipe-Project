Title: FIESTA DIP
Categories: Dips
Yield: 12 Servings
1/2 c  Refried beans
1/2 c  Cheese, cheddar; shredded
1/4 c  Salsa
1/2    Green chili pepper, chopped
Combine all ingredients and place in crock pot. Cover and heat 30 to
60 minutes until cheese is melted. Serve with tortilla chips or corn
chips.
