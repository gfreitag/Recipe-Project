Title: FOCACCIA (EMERIL)
Categories: New, Text, Import
1    batch focaccia dough,
-proofed (recipe follows)
Yield: 4 Servings
:
2 TB olive oil
2 TB mixed chopped herbs
1/4 c  grated Parmesan cheese
Salt and pepper
:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spread the dough out on a oiled sheet
tray and press "dimples" into it with your fingertips. Drizzle with
the oil, herbs and cheese and season. Bake until browned and cooked
through and let cool.
