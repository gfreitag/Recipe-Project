Title: FONDANT PATTIES
Categories: Candies
Yield: 6 Servings
Text Only
Tint fondant with any desired food coloring.  Flavor to suit the
taste. Knead until coloring and flavoring are well blended with
fondant. Form into patties. Press nuts, raisins, candied cherries or
other fruits in the top. If desired, dip into melted chocolate. The
