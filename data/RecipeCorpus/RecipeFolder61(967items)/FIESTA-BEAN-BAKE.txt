Title: FIESTA BEAN BAKE
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Cooked Idaho Beans
-- Small Red, Pinto
-- or Pink Beans
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1    Clove garlic; minced
6 tb Vegetable oil, divided
1/4 c  Milk
1 c  Whole kernel corn, drained
1 c  Grated Monterey Jack
-OR- Cheddar cheese
Drain beans well. In a large kettle, saute onion and garlic in 2
tablespoons oil until soft. Blend in flour. Stir in tomatoes, chilies
and beans, breaking up tomatoes. Cook and stir until boiling. Set
aside. Combine 1/4 cup oil with remaining ingredients, except cheese,
to make batter. Pour two-thirds of the batter into a greased 2-quart
casserole. Sprinkle with two-thirds of the cheese. Top with bean
mixture. Spoon remaining batter around edge. Sprinkle with remaining
cheese. Bake in preheated 350-degree F oven for 30 to 35 minutes or
until cornbread is done. Makes 6 servings.
