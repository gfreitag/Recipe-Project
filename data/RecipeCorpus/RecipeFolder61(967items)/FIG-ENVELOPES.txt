Title: FIG ENVELOPES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Flour
3 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
2 tb Sugar
2 ts Shortening
3/4 c  Milk
1 c  Cooked figs, chopped and
-well drained
1    Egg, well beaten
Sift flour, measure, and sift with baking powder, salt, and sugar.
Cut in shortening with 2 spatulas. Slowly add enough milk to form a
soft dough. Turn onto lightly floured board. Roll in sheet 1/4 inch
thick. Cut in 4-inch squares.  Place 2 tablespoons chopped figs on
1/2 of each. Moisten edges with cold milk. Fold. Press edges
together. Brush tops with egg mixed with 1 tablespoon milk and 1
teaspoon sugar.  Place on slightly oiled baking sheet.  Bake in hot
oven (450 F) 20 minutes. 6 servings. The Household Searchlight
