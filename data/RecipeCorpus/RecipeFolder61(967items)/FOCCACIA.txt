Title: FOCCACIA
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Pizza dough
2 oz Olive oil
1 pn Salt
1 oz Garlic -- minced
1 1/2 oz Kalamata olives -- pitted
And chopped
1 pn Rosemary -- chopped
Roll out pizza dough flat without a rim, approximately 10-inch circle.
Cover entire surface with oil. Evenly spread garlic and olives across
dough. Sprinkle rosemary and salt. Bake in oven on pizza stone until
golden brown. Allow to cool and serve.
