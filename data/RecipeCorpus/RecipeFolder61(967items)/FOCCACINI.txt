Title: FOCCACINI
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Pizza dough
Olive oil
Salt
Toppings -- * see note
Roll out pizza dough until very thin.  Cut into 3-inch circles.
Remove and discard excess dough. Brush with olive oil and sprinkle
with salt and toppings. Bake in oven on pizza stone until golden
brown. Allow to cool and serve.
