Title: FISH CHOWDER
Categories: Soups
Yield: 4 Servings
1 c  Water
1 c  Diced potatoes
3    Slices of bacon
1    Med. onion
3/4 lb Fish fillets
1/8 ts Thyme
1 c  Half and half
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2 tb Fresh parsley
Bring water to a boil in a Dutch oven; add potatoes.
Cover and cook 10 minutes.  Fry bacon until transparent; add onion,
and cook until onion is soft and bacon is lightly browned. Add bacon,
onion , bacon drippings, fish fillets, and thyme to potatoes.
Simmer 10 minutes or until potatoes are tender. Stir in half and
half , salt, pepper, simmer 5 minutes. Sprinkle with parsley.
