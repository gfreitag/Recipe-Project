Title: FISH DISH
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 lb Turbot, or other lean fish
3/4 c  Butter, melted
1 ea Lemon, juiced
1/2 c  Grated Parmesan cheese
1 c  Fine bread crumbs
Cut fish into steaks.  Combine melted butter and lemon. In a separate
bowl, mix Parmesan cheese and bread crumbs. Dip each piece of fish
into melted butter and then roll in crumb mixture.  Bake at 350
degrees for 15- 20 minutes.  Serves 4.
