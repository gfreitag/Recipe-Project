Title: FLAVORED MOCHA MIX
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 2 Servings
1 1/2 c  Nondairy Powdered Creamer
1 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Instant Coffee Granules OR
1/2 c  Coffee Crystals
1/2 c  Unsweetened Cocoa
1 ds Salt
1/4 ts Vanilla Extract
1/4 ts Almond Extract
Boiling Water
Whipped Cream -- if desired
In lrg. bowl, combine all ingredients except extracts, boiling water
and whipped cream; mix well.  Place 1 1/2 cups of mixture in sm.
bowl. Add vanilla extract; mix well. Add almond extract to remaining
mixture in large bowl; mix well.  Store each in tightly covered
container. To serve: Combine 3 tbsp. of desired mix with 1 cup
boiling water; stir to blend. Garnish with whipped cream. Makes 1 1/2
cups of each flavor of mix. "This versatile mix includes almond and
vanilla-flavored choices. Give a pkg. of the mix with a holiday mug.
Be sure to include a copy of the recipe. Could also include with
chocolate gift basket. Pillsbury Holiday" cookbook, Classic #94.
