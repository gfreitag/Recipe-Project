Title: FIERY SALSA
Categories: Appetizers, Mexican, Sauces, Dips
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 lg Onion
8 ea Tomatoes
1/2 sm Bunch cilantro
10 ea Jalapeno peppers
Juice of 1 lime
Juice of 1 lemon
2 tb Vinegar
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 ts Garlic salt
1 ts Oregano
Dice the onions, tomatoes, jalapenos & cilantro as small as possible.
Combine the rest of the ingredients. Chill overnight.
