Title: FLAVORED MUSTARDS
Categories: Info/tips
***** NONE *****
Yield: 1 Servings  :

Create your own flavored mustard - Lemon mustard, a perfect
complement for chicken, stir 1 teaspoon grated lemon rind and 1
tablespoon fresh lemon juice into 1/2 cup Dijon mustard; - Green
peppercorn mustard, a great beef booster, mix 1 tablespoon freshly
ground green peppercorns and 1 minced shallot into 1/2 cup Dijon
mustard; - Honey mustard, to perk up ham and pork, combine equal
parts honey and mustard and whisk until smooth; - Chili mustard,
adding a certain sizzle to Southwestern dishes, combine 2 tablespoons
diced, pickled jalapeno chilies with 1/2 cup Dijon mustard; -
Horseradish mustard, a dip to dazzle your palate, combine 1
tablespoon grated horseradish, a dash of Tabasco and 2 tablespoons
nonfat mayo with 1/2 cup Dijon mustard. [mcrecipe archive: 15 Oc 96
