Title: FOOLPROOF TART DOUGH
Categories: Desserts, Pies
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  All-purpose flour
1 ds Salt
1 ts Sugar
1    Stick of butter, COLD
6 tb Cold heavy cream or
Whipping cream
This recipe can be used for pies, tarts, cheesecake or quiche. It
makes a nice flaky shell. Place 1 cup of flour in a bowl with a
little salt and 1 tsp sugar. Cut in one stick of cold butter on top
and mix until it is the texture of golden meal. If ambient
temperature in kitchen is high, then chill flour and bowl in freezer
for up to 1 hour. Add 6 Tbs of cold heavy cream (or whipping cream).
Process in food processor until dough forms a ball. Turn out on a
board or counter, dust lightly with flour and roll to shape. If in
doubt about whether you are using enough flour when rolling out
dough, use a lot and brush off excess.
