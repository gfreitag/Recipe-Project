Title: FIVE SPICE POWDER
Categories: Spices, Chinese
Yield: 1 Servings
5 ts Ground anise (aniseed)
5 ts Star anise
2 tb Whole cloves
1 ea Cinnamon stick (5") in
-cassia bark
7 ts Fennel seeds
Using a blender, process all ingredients until finely ground. Store
in an airtight jar up to 3 months.

Date: 03-19-93
From: Richard Davis
