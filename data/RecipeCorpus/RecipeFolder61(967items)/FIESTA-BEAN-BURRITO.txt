Title: FIESTA BEAN BURRITO
Categories: Sandwiches, Mexican, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Vegetarian beans
1 tb Salsa
1 ts Chopped cilantro,optional
1    Whole wheat tortilla
Spread beans over tortilla.Sprinkle on remaining ingredients.Heat in
microwave until warm,about 40 seconds.Roll tortilla and mixture into a
burrito.
