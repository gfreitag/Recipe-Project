Title: FINNISH BREAD
Categories: Breads, Finnish
Yield: 12 Servings
1 1/8 c  Butter
1/2 c  Sugar
3 1/2 c  Flour
Mix well and let stand in cold place for 1 hour.  Roll into strips
about 2/3-inch in diameter and cut into small pieces. Brush with egg
and sprinkle with sugar and chopped nuts. Bake in a moderate oven
(350) till golden brown.
