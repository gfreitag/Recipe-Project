Title: FOOD PROCESSOR CAROB BROWNIES
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 1 Servings
6 tb Carob powder
1 c  Flour
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Baking powder
2    Eggs
3/4 c  Honey
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Butter -- cut in chunks
1/2 c  Walnuts
Sift together carob, flour, salt and baking powder. In food processor,
cream eggs, honey and vanilla. Add dry ingredients to processor, plus
butter and walnuts.  Pour into 9x9 greased pan. Bake at 350F for 30
minutes.
