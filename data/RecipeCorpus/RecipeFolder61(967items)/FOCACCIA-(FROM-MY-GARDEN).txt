Title: FOCACCIA (FROM MY GARDEN)
Categories: Wasnone
1    onion -- chopped
Yield: 1 Servings
1    tomato -- diced
1/4 ts chopped herbs: rosemary --
basil, oregano
:
1/2    recipe pizza dough
1 TB oil
1/4    stick butter -- melted

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Combine onion and tomato and herbs with
dough. Knead together gently. Once combined, roll the dough flat.
Place dough on an oiled baking sheet. Bake for 12 minutes, or until
golden brown. Brush the dough with butter a few minutes before
removing from the oven. Serve.
