Title: FREEZER SLAW
Categories: Salads
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Head of cabbage
1    Carrot
1    Green pepper
1    Onion, chopped
1 ts Salt
1 c  Vinegar
1 c  Sugar
1 ts Celery seed
1 ts Dry mustard
Chop up cabbage, carrot, pepper, onion and add salt. Let stand 1
hour. Boil together vinegar, sugar, celery seed and mustard. Cool and
pour over slaw. Put in containers and freeze or use. Randy Rigg
