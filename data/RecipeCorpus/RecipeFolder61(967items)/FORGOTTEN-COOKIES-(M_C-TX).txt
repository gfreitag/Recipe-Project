Title: FORGOTTEN COOKIES (M_C-TX)
Categories: Cookies, Desserts, Snacks
Yield: 15 Servings
2    Egg whites (room
-temperature)
2/3 c  Sugar
1 c  Chopped nuts
1 c  Chocolate chips
Pinch salt
Beat egg whites until thick and foamy. Add sugar and salt; beat until
stiff. Add pecans and chocolate chips (stir in). Place on foil lined
cookie sheet. Preheat oven to 350 ; turn off oven when you put
cookies in and leave overnight.
