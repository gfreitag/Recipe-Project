Title: FOOTY'S PERFECT PASTA
Categories: Pasta, Seafood, Vegetables, Cheese
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Fettuccine
1 lb Cooked shrimp, chopped into
-bite-size pieces
1 1/2 c  Half-and-half or heavy cream
1 c  Frozen peas, thawed
3/4 c  Parmesan cheese, grated
Bring 4 quarts of water to a rolling boil.  Add fettuccine and cook
about 7 minutes.  Drain well; return to pot and add cream, peas and
cheese over low heat, heat through.

Note: Footy is a radio personality in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida on Y-100
