Title: FOUNDATION SPONGE
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Lukewarm water
1/2    To 1 cake dry yeast
-Flour
Crumble yeast in water.  Allow to stand 20 minutes. Add flour to make
a stiff drop batter.  Beat until smooth. Cover and let stand in warm
place overnight until light and bubbly. The greater amount of yeast
makes the sponge work more rapidly. The Household Searchlight - 1941
