Title: FRANGELICO CHEESECAKE
Categories: Cheesecakes, Liquor, Creamcheese, Cakes
Yield: 10 Servings
32 oz Cream cheese
3    Eggs
3/4 c  Sugar
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Frangelico
Graham Cracker Crust
Blend all ingredients well. Pour into pie crust or a graham cracker
crust pressed into a spring form. Preheat oven to 350~ and bake 40
~50 minutes. Chill before serving.
