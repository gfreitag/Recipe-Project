Title: FREEZER ICE CREAM - SUGAR FREE
Categories: Diabetic, Desserts
Yield: 16 Servings
MMMMM------------------------INGREDIENTS-----------------------------
8 c  PET Lite milk
1/2 c  Sugar Subst. equivalent
4 c  Strawberries
8    Pineapple, slices, canned
16 tb Pineapple Juice, from can
2 c  Orange Juice
Directions: Put all ingredients in a home freezer and freeze
according to instructions.
Makes 16 1-Cup Servings.
Exchanges: Each Serving equals 1 Milk and 3/4 Fruit.
Calories:  125 From Files of A.Broaddus 6/24/93
