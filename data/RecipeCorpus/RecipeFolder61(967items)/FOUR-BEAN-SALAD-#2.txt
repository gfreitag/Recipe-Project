Title: FOUR BEAN SALAD #2
Categories: Beans, Salad dress, Salads, Honey
Yield: 1 Servings
1 cn Red kidney beans
1 cn Green beans
1 cn Yellow beans
1 cn Garbanzo beans
Dressing-----
1/2 ts Celery seed
1 lg Onion -- sliced
3/4 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Salad oil
1    Green pepper -- chopped
3/4 c  Honey
Heat the dressing ingredients together just to blend. Mix the beans
together and pour dressing over it. Best if refrigerated at least 3
hours before serving.
