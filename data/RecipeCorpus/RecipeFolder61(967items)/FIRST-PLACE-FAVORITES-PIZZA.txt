Title: FIRST PLACE FAVORITES PIZZA
Categories: Main dish, Low-cal
Yield: 8 Servings
4 oz Cans chopped mushrooms
1 c  Soft bread crumbs(2 slices)
1    Egg
1/2 ts Salt
Dash pepper
1 lb Ground chuck
1/2 ts Oregano
2 oz Low-fat mozzarella cheese
5 1/2 oz Can pizza sauce
1/4 c  Chopped onion
1/4 c  Green pepper
Drain mushrooms, reserve liquid, add water if necessary to equal 1/2
cup. Add bread crumbs, egg(slightly beaten), salt and pepper. Let
Stand 5 minutes.  Add ground chuck, 1/2 tsp. oregano, half mushroom,
mix lightly. in 9" pie plate form a meat crust. Cut 2 slices cheese
in 8 triangles, ;ayer pver meat. Top with pizza sauce, onion, green
pepper and remaining mushrooms.   Sprinkle with extra oregano, if
desired. Bake at 350 degrees for 55 minutes.  Top with remaining
cheese; bake 5 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.

exchanges:2 meat, 1/2 bread source: First Place Favorites
