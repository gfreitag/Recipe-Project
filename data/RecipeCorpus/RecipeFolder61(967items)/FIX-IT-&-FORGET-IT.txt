Title: FIX IT & FORGET IT
Categories: Ground turk, Healthy and, Pudding, Indian, Turkish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 md Potatoes -- peeled & Sliced
1 lb Ground turkey
1 cn Cream of mushroom soup
1 cn Vegetable beef soup
1 md Onion -- sliced
Put potatoes in casserole or dutch oven. Crumble raw meat over the
top, add the soups and onion.  Cover and bake 1 1/2 hours or until
potatoes are done at 350.
