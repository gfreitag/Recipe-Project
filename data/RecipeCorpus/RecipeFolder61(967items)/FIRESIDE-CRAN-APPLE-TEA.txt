Title: FIRESIDE CRAN-APPLE TEA
Categories: Digest, Oct.
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Cranberry Juice
1 1/2 c  Water
4    Herbal Tea -- bags, cinnamon
Apple
4    Cinnamon Sticks
1/3 c  Brown Sugar
In med saucepan, bring cranberry juice and water to boil. Add tea
bags and cinnamon sticks.  Remove from heat; cover and steep for 5
min. Place cinnamon sticks in preheated mugs. Add brown sugar to tea
mixture. Stir until dissolved.  Pour hot tea into mugs.
