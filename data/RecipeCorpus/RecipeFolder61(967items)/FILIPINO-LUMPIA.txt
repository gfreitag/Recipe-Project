Title: FILIPINO LUMPIA
Categories: Beef/veal, Asian, Phillipines
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Ground Beef
3    Garlic Cloves; finely minced
Carrot; finely diced
1 ts Salt
1/2    Bunch String Beans *
1/4 ts Pepper
1/2 md Onion; finely diced
2 pk Egg Roll Wrappers
Potato **
Fat for Deep Frying
* Partially cooked, then blanched ** Partially cooked, peeled then
diced Mix the ground beef with the carrot, beans, onion, potato,
garlic, salt and pepper. Cut egg roll wrappers in half. PlaceH: 3 1 T
filling in center of each wrapper. Roll up halfway, tightly, and fold
ends inward. Seal last third with water to make edges stick. Deep fry
until golden. Serve with sweet-sour sauce. Recipe courtesy of: Alvin
Rivera, 06 Jan 93 18:55:51
