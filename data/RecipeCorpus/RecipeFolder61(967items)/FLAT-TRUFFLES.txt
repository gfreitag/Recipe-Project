Title: FLAT TRUFFLES
Categories: None
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Margarine/butter
1/3 c  Cocoa
3/4 c  Icing (confectioner's) sugar
Crushed nuts.
Cream butter til soft and pliable.  Sift cocoa.  Blend well. Sift
icing sugar and add.  Mix well, adding more icing sugar if too soft
to roll. (At least 1 or 2 spoonfuls). Shape into a roll. Roll in
crushed nuts. Chill for several hours.  Serve in slices.
