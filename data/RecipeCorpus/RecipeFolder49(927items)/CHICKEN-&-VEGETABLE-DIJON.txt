Title: CHICKEN & VEGETABLE DIJON
Categories: Chicken
Yield: 1 Servings
4 ea Chicken Breast, bone/skinles
1 1/2 c  Broccoli flowerets
1 1/2 c  Mushrooms, sliced
1 sm Green pepper, sliced
1 sm Red pepper, sliced
1 md Onion, sliced
1 cn Broccoli soup
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1/2 c  Milk
2 tb Dijon mustard
2 tb Margarine
In skillet in 1 tbsp margarine, cook chicken 10 minutes, or until
browned. Remove from pan. In 1 tbsp margarine cook veggies until
tender. Stir often. Stir in soups, milk, and mustard. Bring to boil.
Return chicken. Simmer until ready to eat. Serve over pasta.
