Title: CHICKEN & BROCCOLI AU GRATIN
Categories: Chicken, Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1/4 c  Onion, chpd
1/4 c  Flour
1 ts Salt
1 ts Pepper
1 cn Evaporated milk, lg
1 pk Froz broccoli spears, cooked
1    Chicken, cooked, cubed
1 c  Cheese, shredded
Saute onion in margarine; stir in flour, salt and pepper. Drain
mushrooms, reserving liquid; add water to make 1/2 cup liquid.
Gradually stir into flour mixture in skillet. Blend in milk until
smooth. Add mushrooms. Cook and stir over medium heat until sauce
begins to thicken. Arrange a layer of chicken with the broccoli
spears on top in a 9x13 baking dish. Pour sauce over chicken and
broccoli. Top with cheese. Bake 375 for 20 min. Let sit 15 min before
serving.
