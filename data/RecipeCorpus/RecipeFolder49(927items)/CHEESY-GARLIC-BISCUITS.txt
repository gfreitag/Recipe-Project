Title: CHEESY GARLIC BISCUITS
Categories: Petfood, Misc.
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Whole-wheat flour
1 c  Cheddar cheese, grated
2 tb Margarine or shortening
3/4 c  Milk
Garlic powder
Preheat oven to 375Ã¸F.  Combine flour and cheese, and cut in
margarine or shortening.  Add milk and mix well. Knead lightly.
Divide dough into two parts, and roll out to 1 inch thickness on a
floured board. Cut into desired shapes, and place biscuits on a
ungreased cookie sheet. Prick with a fork, and sprinkle with garlic
powder. Bake 12 to 15 minutes, or until lightly browned.  Cool and
serve.  Refrigerate to store. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com
