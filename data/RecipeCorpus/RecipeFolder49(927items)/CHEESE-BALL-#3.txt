Title: CHEESE BALL #3
Categories: Cheese, Holiday
Yield: 1 Servings
16 oz Cream cheese
1 pk Dried chipped beef
1 ts Caraway seed (optional)
1 tb Minced onion
Shred the chipped beef, then stir it into the cheese. Form into a
ball and chill.  No need to remove cheese ball from the refrigerator
early because it spreads easily right from the start.
