Title: CHEESY GREEN BEANS
Categories: Main dish, Vegetables
Yield: 10 Servings
10    Slices bacon
2 pk (16oz) cut green beans
1 lb Mushrooms, sliced
3/4 c  Chopped onion
3/4 ts Ground black pepper
1    (160z)jar pastuerized chees
Spread
Cook bacon until crisp. Drain bacon, reserving 1/4 cup drippings.
Crumble bacon; reserve 2 tablespoons. Microwave beans as directed on
package; drain well. Add mushrooms and onion to reserved 1/4 cup
drippings in skillet. Cook and stir on medium-high heat 10 minutes;
drain. Add bacon, hot cooked green beans and pepper; mix lightly.
Spoon into 2-quart serving dish. Microwave process cheese spread as
directed on label. Pour over green bean mixture. Sprinkle with
reserved 2 tablespoons crumbled bacon.
