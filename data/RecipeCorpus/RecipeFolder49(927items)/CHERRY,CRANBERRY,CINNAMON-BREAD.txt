Title: CHERRY/CRANBERRY/CINNAMON BREAD
Categories: Abm, Posted-mm
Yield: 15 Servings
2 c  Bread flour
1 1/4 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
1 ts Cinnamon -- ground
1 tb Oil
1 c  Milk -- 1%
1 tb Lemon peel -- grated
1 1/4 ts Yeast
1/8 c  Cranberries -- dried
1/8 c  Cherries -- dried
Orange peel may be substituted for the lemon peel. Place all of the
ingredient into the breadmachine and press start. If you prefer the
berries in larger pieces add them AFTER the initial rise. This bread
is wonderful lightly toasted and spread with cream cheese.
