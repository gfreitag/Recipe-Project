Title: CHESS PIE
Categories: Pies
Yield: 8 Servings
1 1/2 c  SUGAR
1/2 c  BUTTER
1 tb PLAIN CORN MEAL
3 ea LARGE EGGS
1 ts VANILLA
1 ts VINEGAR
5 tb LIGHT CREAM
1 ea UNBAKED 9 INCH PIE SHELL
Cream butter and sugar.  Beat eggs together well and add
remaining ingredients.  Fold egg mixture into butter/sugar
mixture.  Pour into pie shell.  Bake at 400 degrees F for 10
minutes and then lower the heat to 325 degrees F and bake for an
additional 25 minutes or until set.
