Title: CHICKEN & HERB DUMPLINGS
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 5 Servings
2 1/2 lb Chicken -- cut up
1 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
2    Whole cloves
8 ea Small white onions
1    Clove garlic -- minced
1/4 ts Powdered marjoram
1/4 ts Powdered thyme
1 ea Bay leaf
1/2 c  Dry white wine
1 c  Dairy sour cream
1 c  Packaged biscuit mix
1 tb Parsley -- chopped
6 tb Milk
Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper; place in slow-cooking pot.
Insert cloves in one onion.  Put all onions in pot. Add garlic,
marjoram, thyme, bay leaf, and wine. Cover and cook on low 5 to 6
hours. Remove bay leaf and cloves.  Stir in sour cream.  Turn control
to high. Combine biscuit mix with parsley; stir milk into biscuit mix
with fork until well moistened. Drop dumplings from teaspoon around
edge of pot. Cover and cook on high for 30 minutes.
