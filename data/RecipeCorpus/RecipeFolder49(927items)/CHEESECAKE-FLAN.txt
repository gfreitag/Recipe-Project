Title: CHEESECAKE FLAN
Categories: Desserts, Cheesecakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
3 tb Water
8 oz Cream cheese; softened
3    Eggs
1 ts Vanilla
1 cn Sweetened condensed milk
13 oz Evaporated milk
Caramelize the sugar and water.  When caramelized, pour into a 9-inch
round pan. Preheat oven to 350.

Put cream cheese, eggs, and vanilla in blender.  Blend until smooth.
Add condensed milk and blend again. Add evaporated milk and blend
again. Pour over caramelized sugar in pan and set pan in another pan
with has about one inch of water in it.  Bake for one hour. Cool and
invert on a plate. Chill.
