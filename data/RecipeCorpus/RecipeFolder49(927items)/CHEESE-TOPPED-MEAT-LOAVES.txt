Title: CHEESE TOPPED MEAT LOAVES
Categories: Meats
Yield: 1 Servings
2 lb Ground beef
2 c  Soft bread crumbs
3/4 c  Chopped onion
2/3 c  Milnot milk
2    Eggs
1 1/2 ts Salt
Mix all of the above in one large bowl.  Shape into 8 loaves. Place in
ungreased cake pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Top each
loaf with American cheese and bake until cheese is melted.
