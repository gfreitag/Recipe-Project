Title: CHEEESE FILLING FOR NUT & POPPY SEED ROLLS
Categories: Kosher, Desserts
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Cottage cheese (scant lb.)
3    Egg yolks
1/2 c  Sugar
Lemon flavoring
Put cheese thru sieve and add rest of ingredients and blend well.
Prepare dough for Nut & Poppyseed Rolls (#164). Cut dough into
squares and press into bottoms of cupcake tins. Fill each cake with a
little cheese and fold over corners of dough toward the center of the
cake Bake at 350 F. until brown.
