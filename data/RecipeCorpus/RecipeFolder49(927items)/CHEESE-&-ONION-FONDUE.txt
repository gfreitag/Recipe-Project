Title: CHEESE & ONION FONDUE
Categories: Fondue
Yield: 1 Servings
1 oz Butter
1 lg Onion -- finely chopped
2 ts Plain flour
5    Fluid ounces  thick sour
Cream
8 oz Gruyere cheese -- grated
8 oz Cheddar cheese -- grated
1 tb Chives -- chopped
Pepper
sm Cooked potatoes -- to serve
sm Cooked sausages -- to serve
Melt butter in saucepan, cook onion for 4-5 minutes until soft. Stir
in flour, then add cream, cook for 2 minutes. Continue to cook whilst
adding cheeses and heat until mixture is smooth, stirring frequently.
Add chives and season with pepper. Pour into fondue pot and serve
with the potatoes and sausages.
