Title: CHEESEBURGER CASSEROLE
Categories: Casseroles, Main dish, Beef
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb GROUND BEEF
1/4 c  CHOPPED ONION
8 oz CAN TOMATO SAUCE
1/4 c  CATSUP
1/8 ts PEPPER
1/2 lb AMERICAN CHEESE SLICES
7 oz CAN REFRIGERATOR BISCUITS
BROWN THE GROUND BEEF AND ONIONS IN A SKILLET. DRAIN OFF THE FAT. ADD
TOMATO SAUCE, CATSUP AND PEPPER. HEAT MIXTURE. ALTERNATE MEAT MIXTURE
AND CHEESE SLICES IN AN UNGREASED 1 1/2 QUART CASSEROLE. ARRANGE
BISCUITS AROUND EDGE OF CASSEROLE. BAKE AT 400 F. FOR ABOUT 20
MINUTES OR TIL BISCUITS ARE GOLDEN BROWN.
