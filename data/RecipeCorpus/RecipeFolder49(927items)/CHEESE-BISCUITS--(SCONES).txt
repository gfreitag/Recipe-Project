Title: CHEESE BISCUITS  (SCONES)
Categories: Breads, Quick
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Bisquick
1/2 c  Cold water
3/4 c  Sharp grated cheddar cheese
2 tb Butter -- melted with
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Parsley flakes
1/4 ts Italian seasonings
Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Mix bisquick, water and cheese. Drop by
large spoonfuls onto greased baking sheet. Bake for 8-10 minutes.
After baking, while still hot, brush on melted butter mixed with
garlic powder, parsley flakes and Italian seasoning. Serve hot. You
may also substitute soda water or ginger ale for the water.
