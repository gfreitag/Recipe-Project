Title: CHEESE BRIOCHE 2
Categories: Breads
Yield: 4 Servings
1 x  SEE "CHEESE BRIOCHE 1" FOR I
Bake in a 375 F oven for 30-35 minutes until light golden brown
and puffy. Let cool 10 minutes. Brioche will deflate. Remove from
pan. Slice into 1-2 inch slices. Serve hot or at room-temperature
on a wooden cutting board.
Yields 16 slices.
