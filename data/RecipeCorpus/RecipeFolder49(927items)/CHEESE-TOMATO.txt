Title: CHEESE TOMATO
Categories: Vegetables, Diabetic, Cheese/eggs
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Tomato(thickly sliced)
1 ds Celery salt
1 ds Garlic salt
1 ds Pepper
1 oz American cheese(grated)
Place tomato slices on broiler pan coated with vegetable cooking
spray. Sprinkle with seasonings. Top with cheese. Broil 5-6 inches
from heat until cheese is melted. 1 serving = 1 vegetable, 1 high-fat
meat calories = 140
