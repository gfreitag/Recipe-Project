Title: CHICKEN & GRAVY
Categories: Gravy, Chicken, Crock
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Chicken
1 cn Cream mushroom soup
1/4 c  Wine, dry white
4 oz Mushrooms
Season chicken with salt and pepper to taste.  Place in crockpot. Mix
wine and soup and pour over chicken. Pour mushrooms over top of
chicken and cook on low 7-9 hours or on high for 3-4 hours. Sliced
onions may be added for a different flavor. Servings: 4
