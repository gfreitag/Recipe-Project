Title: CHEESE DUMPLINGS
Categories: Breads, Casseroles
Yield: 4 Servings
3 tb Shortening
1 1/2 c  Flour
2 ts Baking powder
1/2 ts Salt
1/4 c  Shredded sharp cheddar
3/4 c  Milk
Cut shortening into flour; baking powder and salt in medium bowl until
mixture resembles fine crumbs.  Stir in cheese and milk until
blended. Drop dough by 10 to 12 spoonfuls onto casserole/stew and
cook uncovered for 10 minutes.  Cover and cook 10 minutes longer.
