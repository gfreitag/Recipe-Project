Title: CHICKEN & CHILIES CASSEROLE
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 8 Servings
8    Chicken breast halves --
Skinn
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Pepper
1    8 oz pkg shredded
Mozzarella
1    4 oz can sliced mushrooms --
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
2    Tomatoes -- peeled and
Diced
1    4 oz can chopped green
Chili
Recipe by: Southern Living Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper and
place in greased 11 x 7 x 1/2" baking dish. Combine cheese and next 3
ingredients and pour over chicken. Sprinkle with chilies. Bake
uncovered at 350 for 35 minutes.  Serve over rice.
