Title: CHEESECAKE CREME DIP
Categories: Appetizers, Cheesecakes
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Cream cheese, softened
1    Jar (7oz) marshmallow creme
Mix cream cheese and marshmallow creme with electric mixer on medium
speed until well blended. Refrigerate. Serve with assorted cut-up
fresh fruit or pound cake cubes. Garnish, if desired. Makes 1 3/4
cups. Prep.time: 5 minutes + refrigerating.
