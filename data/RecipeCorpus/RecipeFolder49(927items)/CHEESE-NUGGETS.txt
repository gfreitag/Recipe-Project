Title: CHEESE NUGGETS
Categories: Appetizers
Yield: 36 Servings
1/2 c  Butter
1 c  All-purpose flour
2 c  Grated sharp cheddar cheese
1/4 ts Cayenne pepper
Mix butter and flour together well. Add cheese and work in well.
Shape into small marble-sized balls. Place on ungreased baking sheet.
Bake in 375F (190C) oven for about 10 minutes. Makes 36.
