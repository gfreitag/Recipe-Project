Title: CHEESE PEPPER BREAD
Categories: Dairy, Breadmaker
Yield: 12 Servings
-judy Garnett PJXG05A
1    Yeast
1 ts Sugar
1 tb Vegetable oil
3 c  Bread flour
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Pepper; freshly ground
1 c  Swiss cheese; grated
1 c  Water; warm
Place ingreds. in order above in bread pan of B/M. Set for white
bread or French Bread.
