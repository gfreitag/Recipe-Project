Title: CHEESE-ONION BAKE
Categories: Side dish, Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
5 md Onions
1/4 c  Butter (or marg.)
1/4 c  Flour, all-purpose
2 c  Milk
1/2 ts Salt
1/8 ts Pepper
2 c  American cheese; shredded
Cut onions into thin slices; separate into reings. Place onion rings
in an ungreased 1-1/2 quart casserole. Melt butter in saucepan; blend
in flour, and gradually stir in milk.  Cook, stirring constantly,
until thickened.

Add salt, pepper, and cheese; stir until cheese has melted. Pour
sauce over onions.  Bake, uncovered, at
325    degrees about 45 minutes.

SOURCE: Southern Living Magazine, June, 1974. Typed for you by Nancy
Coleman.
