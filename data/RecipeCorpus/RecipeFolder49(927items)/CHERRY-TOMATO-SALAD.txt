Title: CHERRY TOMATO SALAD
Categories: Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Servings
1 pt Cherry tomatoes
6    Bacon slices
1/4 c  Mayonnaise
1 tb Tarragon vinegar
2 tb Olive oil
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Fresh basil -- chopped
6    Leaves romaine lettuce
2 tb Chives -- chopped
Wash and stem tomatoes. Dry completely. Cook bacon until very crisp;
drain thoroughly on paper towels. Mix mayonnaise, vinegar, olive oil,
salt, and basil. Pour over tomatoes, crumble bacon over all, and stir
to coat tomatoes with dressing. Serve on lettuce leaves, sprinkled
with chives.

Serve at once or refrigerate until ready to serve.

: Elizabeth Powell
