Title: CHICK PEAS IN OLIVE-TAHINI SAUCE
Categories: Ethnic, Vegetarian, Sauces
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tb Olive oil
2 ea Garlic cloves, minced
1 sm Onion, chopped
3 lg Celery stalks, chopped
1 md Red or green bell pepper,
-- chopped
Olive-tahini sauce -- see
-- recipe
3 c  Cooked chick peas
1/4 c  Wheat germ
1 tb Soy sauce
1/2 ts Marjoram
1/4 ts Coriander
Black pepper
Heat oil in large skillet.  When hot, add garlic, onion & celery &
saute till onion is translucent.  Add bell pepper & saute till
vegetables are lightly browned. Remove from heat & pour olive-tahini
sauce into the skillet along with the remaining ingredients. Return
to low heat & simmer 5 to 7 minutes.  Serve over rice or other grain.

Nava Atlas, "Vegetariana"
