Title: CHEESY MEAT LOAF
Categories: Beef, Cheese
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Tomato sauce
1/2 c  Dry bread crumbs
1/2 c  Cheddar cheese, diced
1 ts Salt
1 1/2 lb Ground beef
1/4 c  Onions, diced
1    Egg, slightly beaten
Pepper
Mix all ingredients together in a larger bowl. Shape into a loaf pan
and bake 350F for 1 hr. Cover with any remaining tomato sauce or
ketchup and serve with mash potatoes
