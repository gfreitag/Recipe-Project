Title: CHEERIO BARS
Categories: Snacks, Kids
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 c  Peanut butter
1/2 c  Sugar
1/2 c  Honey
3 c  Cheerios
Salted peanuts
Bring sugar and honey to a boil in 2 qt saucepan. Remove from
heat,stir in peanut butter until blended. Pour in cheerios and mix
until well coated. Spread in buttered 9 x 13 pan and let cool. cut in
squares.

My kids loved these when they were growing up.

They used to like me to add chocolate chips, of course...
