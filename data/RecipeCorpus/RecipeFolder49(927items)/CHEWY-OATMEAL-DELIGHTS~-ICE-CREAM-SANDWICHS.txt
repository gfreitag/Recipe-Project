Title: CHEWY OATMEAL DELIGHTS~ ICE CREAM SANDWICHS
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM--------------------FROM JONI'S KITCHEN-------------------------
1 c  Flour
1 c  Brown sugar
1/2 c  Butter
2 tb -boiling water
1 tb Corn syrup
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1 ts Vanilla
1/2 c  Raisins
1 pt Ice Cream
Toss together oats, sugar, and flour. Stir in butter. In a smallbowl
combine water, corn syrup, baking soda, vanilla, and raisins. Add to
oat mixture and knead lightly until dough comes together. Shape into
18 balls about 1 1/2 in. Flatten slightly on greased sheets, allow
aninch for expansion. Bake at 350 for 10 min or until golden.Makes 9
ice cream sandwiches. L.Jordanejordan@nero.uvic.ca.
