Title: CHEESE BALL #1
Categories: Cheese, Holiday
Yield: 1 Servings
1 ct Wispride Cheddar Cheese
With Port Wine
4 oz Blue cheese
8 oz Cream cheese
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 tb Finely minced onion
MMMMM--------------------------GARNISH-------------------------------
Walnuts, pecans, or parsley
Let cheeses soften a little; mix everything (except your choice of
garnish) together using electric mixer; form ball; chill for several
hours (2 - 24); take from refrigerator 30 mins before serving; cover
with pecans, walnuts (halves or pieces) or parsley. Surround with
crackers.
