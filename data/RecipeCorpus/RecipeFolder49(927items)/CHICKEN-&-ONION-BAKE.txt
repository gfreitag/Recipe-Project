Title: CHICKEN & ONION BAKE
Categories: Casseroles, Chicken, Poultry, Main dish
Yield: 4 Servings
3 lb Chicken, cut up
1 cn Condensed cream of chicken
-soup (10 3/4-oz.)
1 tb Snipped parsley
1/2 ts Poultry seasoning
1 1/2 c  Frozen small whole onions
Paprika
Place chicken pieces, skin side up, in a 12x7 1/2x2-inch baking dish.
Stir together soup, parsley, and poultry seasoning; stir in onions.
Spoon over chicken. Bake, covered, at 350 till chicken is tender,
about 1-hour. Stir sauce, sprinkle with paprika. Serve with mashed
potatoes or hot cooked rice, if desired.
