Title: CHEESECAKE - CAROL ORANGE
Categories: Cheesecakes
Yield: 1 Servings
100 g  Flour
50 g  Sugar
5 ml Lemon rind
1 1/2 ml Vanilla
1    Egg yolk
50 g  Softened butter
1 kg Cream cheese
300 g  Sugar
20 g  Flour
7 ml Orange rind
7 ml Lemon rind
1 1/2 ml Vanilla
5    Eggs
2    Egg yolks
50 ml Cream
1.  Mix crust ingredients; press into a baking pan. Bake 10 minutes
at 200 øC

2.  Combine filling ingredients; mix well, and pour into baked crust.

3.  Bake at 225 øC for 10-12 minutes.

4.  Reduce to 100 øC for 1 hour.

Difficulty    : easy to moderate. Precision
: measure the ingredients.
