1.  Put a small amount of olive oil in a frying skillet over med.-high
heat. Add chicken and sprinkle first portions of pepper and oregano
over the chicken.

2.  When the chicken is almost done cooking, add the pepperoni. After
pepperoni becomes hot, remove from heat and drain excess oil.

3.  Replace skillet at low heat, add tomato sauce and remaining
spices.

4.  Serve hot over pasta, sprinkling shredded mozzarella over the
sauce if desired.
