Title: CHERRY PINEAPPLE JAM
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
4 qt Pitted cherries
2 c  Crushed pineapple
Sugar
Combine fruits and add an equal weight of sugar.  Heat slowly to
boiling. Simmer 3/4 hour.  Pour into platters. Cover with glass and
set in the sun until desired consistency is reached. Florence Taft
Eaton, Concord, MA.
