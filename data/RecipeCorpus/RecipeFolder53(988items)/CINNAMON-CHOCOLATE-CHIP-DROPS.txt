Title: CINNAMON CHOCOLATE CHIP DROPS
Categories: Mixes
Yield: 36 Servings
3 c  Basic cookie mix
1 ts Cinnamon
1/4 c  Milk
1    Egg -- lightly beaten
1 c  Semi-sweet chocolate mini*
3/4 c  Nuts -- chopped
Combine all ingredients except Mini Chips
* Hershey's mini chips
and nuts, in a large bowl. Stir in Mini Chips and nuts. Drop by
teaspoonfuls onto a greased cookie sheet. Bake at 375 F. for 10 to 12
minutes. Remove from cookie sheet; cool on a wire rack. About 3 dozen.
