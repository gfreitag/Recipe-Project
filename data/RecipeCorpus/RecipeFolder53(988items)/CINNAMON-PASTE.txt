Title: CINNAMON PASTE
Categories: Low-fat, Sauces, Mcdougall
Yield: 8 Servings
6 tb Honey
3 tb Ground cinnamon
Recipe by: The New McDougall Cookbook
Blend the honey and cinnamon thoroughly.
