Title: CHOW MEIN #1 - MEAT
Categories: Asian, Chow mein #, Noodles
Yield: 4 Servings
1    Thick Lean Porkchop
1/2 ts Soy Sauce
1/2 ts Sesame Oil
1/2 ts Hot Pepper Oil
1/2 ts Garlic Powder
1/2 c  White Wine
1/4 c  Water
1/8 ts Chinese Five Spice Powder
Slice porkchop paper thin. Combine in pot with other ingredients.
Simmer for 20 minutes. Remove pork from broth. Reserve cooking broth.
