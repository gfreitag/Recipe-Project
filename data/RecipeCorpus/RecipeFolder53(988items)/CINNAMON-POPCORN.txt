Title: CINNAMON POPCORN
Categories: Snacks, Party, Popcorn
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 qt (6 cups) popped popcorn
3 tb Margarine or butter, melted
2 tb Brown sugar
1/2 ts Cinnamon
Place popcorn in large bowl or container.  Drizzle melted margarine
over popcorn.  In small bowl, combine brown sugar and cinnamon. Place
popcorn and sugar-cinnamon mixture in large paper bag. Close bag
tightly; shake until popcorn is evenly coated.

Source:  All New Party Cookbook
