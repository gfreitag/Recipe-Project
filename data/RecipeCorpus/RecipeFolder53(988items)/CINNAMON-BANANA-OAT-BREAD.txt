Title: CINNAMON BANANA OAT BREAD
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 c  Mashed banana
1    Egg
2 tb Vegetable oil
3 tb Molasses
1 ts Salt
1 tb Cinnamon
3/4 c  Quick-cooking oatmeal
3/4 c  Whole-wheat flour
1 1/2 c  Unbleached white flour
1 1/2 ts Yeast
Place all ingredients in the machine in the order suggested by the
manufacturer.  Program for white bread or bread and press start.
Makes 1 lb loaf. Submitted By RHOMMEL <RHOMMEL@IX.NETCOM.COM> On MON,
