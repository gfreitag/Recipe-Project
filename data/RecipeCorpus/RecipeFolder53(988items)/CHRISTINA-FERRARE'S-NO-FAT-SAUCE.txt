Title: CHRISTINA FERRARE'S NO-FAT SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Celebrity, Wrv
Yield: 1 Recipe
MMMMM-----------------WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A----------------------
3 tb Shallots; mince
1/4 c  Red wine
2 tb Dijon mustard
1/2 c  Chicken stock
2 tb Chives; chop
Spray pan with cooking spray. Saute shallots for 1 minute. Add wine,
mustard and stock. Cook 5 minutes to thicken. Add chives. Source:
Home and Family. MM Waldine Van Geffen vghc42a@prodigy.com.
