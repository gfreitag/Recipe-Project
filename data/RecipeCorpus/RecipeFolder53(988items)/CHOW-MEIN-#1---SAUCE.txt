Title: CHOW MEIN #1 - SAUCE
Categories: Asian, Chow mein #, Noodles
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Broth from Chow Mein #1 -
Meat
1 ts Sesame Oil
1/2 ts Hot Chili Oil
1/4 ts Freshly Milled Black Pepper
1/4 ts Garlic Powder
4 tb Soy Sauce
1/4 c  Oil
1/4    Chicken Broth
1 ts Sugar
Combine all ingredients and mix well. Set aside.
