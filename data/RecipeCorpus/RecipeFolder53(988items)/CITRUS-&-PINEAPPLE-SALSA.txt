Title: CITRUS & PINEAPPLE SALSA
Categories: Sauces, Appetizers
Yield: 2 Cups
2 md Oranges, peeled, sectioned,
And chopped
1 c  Fresh pineapple, chopped
1 md Jalapeno pepper, seeded and
Finely chopped
2 tb Thinly sliced green onion
1 tb Cilantro, snipped
1 ts Lime peel, shredded
Stir together oranges, pineapple, jalapeno pepper, green onion,
cilantro, lime peel and dash salt in a bowl. Cover and chill for
about 4 hours.
