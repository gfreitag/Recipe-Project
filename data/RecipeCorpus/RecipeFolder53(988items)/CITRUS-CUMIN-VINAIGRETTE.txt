Title: CITRUS CUMIN VINAIGRETTE
Categories: Miamiherald, Salad dress
Yield: 2 Cups
1 c  Freh orange juice
1/3 c  Cider vinegar
1/3 c  Canola oil
1 tb Fresh lemon juice
2 ts Ground cumin
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Freshly ground pepper
1/4 ts Grated orange zest
1/4 ts Grated lemon zest
1/4 ts Grated lime zest
Whisk all ingredients together in a small bowl. Refrigerate.

Nutritional info per tablespoon: 24 cal; .1g pro, 1g carb, 2g fat
(82%)
