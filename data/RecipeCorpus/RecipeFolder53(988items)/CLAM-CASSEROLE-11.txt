Title: CLAM CASSEROLE 11
Categories: Casseroles
Yield: 12 Servings
20    Crackers crushed
1 cn Clams & juice
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Melted margarine
1    Onion chopped
2    Eggs
mix all ingredients to-gether and let sit for 5-10 minutes. Pour into
greased casserole and bake for 35 -
45    minutes. Oven 350 F.
