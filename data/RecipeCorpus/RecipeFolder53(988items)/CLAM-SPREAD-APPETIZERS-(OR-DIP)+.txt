Title: CLAM SPREAD APPETIZERS (OR DIP)+
Categories: Appetizers, Shellfish, Dairy
Yield: 1 Recipe
1 cn Diced clams
2 tb Diced onions
ds Tabasco
ds Worcestershire
8 oz Cream cheese
1 ea Loaf thinly sliced cocktail
-rye bread
Combine ingredients and spread, rather thickly, on bread. Broil till
brown and bubbly. Alternative: Thin out with milk and use as a dip.
Mary Riemerman
