Title: CHRISTMAS COCKTAIL
Categories: Beverages, Christmas
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Sugar
2/3 c  Water
2/3 c  Unsweetened pineapple juice
2/3 c  Lemon juice
2 tb Fresh or bottled lime juice
2    Unbeaten egg whites
4 c  Finely crushed ice
Maraschino cherries
Cook, sugar and water 5 minutes; chill.  Shake all ingredients except
cherries in jar until light and frothy. Top with a cherry. Serve
immediately. Source: Quick & Easy. ch
