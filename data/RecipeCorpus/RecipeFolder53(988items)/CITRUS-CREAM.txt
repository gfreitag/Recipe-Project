Title: CITRUS CREAM
Categories: Non-alcohol, Cocktail, Beverages, Alcohol
Yield: 4 Servings
1 1/2 c  Orange juice
1/2 c  Grapefruit juice
1/2 c  Simple syrup*
1/2 c  Grenadine syrup
1 c  Heavy cream
1 1/2 c  Ice cubes
* See recipe for "Simple Syrup" Put all ingredients in a blender and
blend until smooth.  Pour into 4 stemmed cocktail glasses.

Source:  Woman's Day 12/17/96
