Title: CILANTRO OIL
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Packed cilantro leaves
1 c  Lighttasting olive oil
Bring a large saucepan of water to a boil. Add the cilantro, making
sure to push all the leaves under the boiling water. Blanch the
cilantro for 5 seconds. Drain and immediately plunge into a bowl of
ice water. Drain well and squeeze out all liquid . In a blender,
puree the cilantro with the olive oil. Strain the puree through 6
layers of cheesecloth or a paper coffee filter and put in a
sterilized glass bottle, tightly capped. Store in the refrigerator
for up to 2 weeks. Yield: about 3/4 cup Copyright, 1996, TV FOOD
