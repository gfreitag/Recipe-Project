Title: CHOKECHERRY SYRUP WITH CORN SYRUP
Categories: Syrup, Chokecherry
Yield: 4 Servings
3 c  Chokecherry juice
1 c  White corn syrup
2 c  Sugar
Boil together 10 minutes.  Put in  hot, sterilized jars. Seal and
process 8 minutes in water bath.
