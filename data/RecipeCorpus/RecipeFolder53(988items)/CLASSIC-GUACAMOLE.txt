Title: CLASSIC GUACAMOLE
Categories: Appetizers, Dips
4    avocados  -- seed,peel
Yield: 1 Servings
2 TB lemon juice
1    clove garlic  -- crushed
1    tomato  -- finely chopped
1/4 c  onion  -- finely chopped
1/4 ts ground cumin
4 dr hot pepper sauce
Tortilla chips
:

Using a fork, coarsely mash avocado with lemon juice and garlic. Stir
in remaining ingredient to blend. Garnish as desired and serve with
tortilla chips.
