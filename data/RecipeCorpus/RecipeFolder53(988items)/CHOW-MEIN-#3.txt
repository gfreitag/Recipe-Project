Title: CHOW MEIN #3
Categories: China, Game
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Veal steak cut in 1/2 inch
-squares
2 tb Butter/margarine
2 c  Celery in 1/2 inch cubes
1 c  Sliced onion
1 cn Tomato soup
1 tb Worcestershire sauce
1 ds Of tabasco sauce
1 ds Of paprika
1 ds Of pepper and salt
1 ts Celery salt
1 tb Brown sugar or chinese sweet
-sauce
1 cn (16-oz) bean sprouts or chow
-mein vegetables
Chow mein noodles
Brown veal in butter, put in celery and onions and when golden, add
soup, worcestershire sauce, tabasco, paprika, pepper, salt, celery
salt, and brown sugar. Place in casserole and bake in 350 degree oven
for 1 hour. Mix in bean sprouts and return to oven and heat through.
Serve over heated chow mein noodles.
