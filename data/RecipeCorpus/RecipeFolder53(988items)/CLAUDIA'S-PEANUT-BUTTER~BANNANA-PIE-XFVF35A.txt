Title: CLAUDIA'S PEANUT BUTTER~BANNANA PIE XFVF35A
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 8 Servings
1 x  Graham Cracker Pie Shell
1/2 c  Milk
8 oz Cream Cheese Softened
1 c  Powdered Sugar
1/2 c  Peanut Butter
8 oz Carton Cool Whip
1 x  Chopped walnuts
2 x  Bannanas
Whip Cream cheese untill light ,blend in powdered sugar,add
milk,beating constantly ,fold in whipped toping and mix well ,divide
mixture in half add peanut butter to 1/2 and bannanas to other 1/2 of
mixture.layer in pie shell and garnish with chopped walnuts freeze
untill firm.
