Title: CISSIE'S WINE CAKE
Categories: Desserts, Cakes
Yield: 12 Servings
1 pk Duncan hines yellow cake mix
1 pk Instant vanilla pudding
4    Eggs
1/3 c  Crisco oil
1/3 c  Medium or dry cream sherry
1 ts Nutmeg
1 ts Almond extract
1/2 c  Nuts
Combine all ingredients except nuts and beat for 5 minutes. Stir in
nuts.
Pour in well-greased bundt or angel pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45
minutes.  Cool for 5 or 10 minutes before turning out.

Cissie McLeod (Sen. Bill McLeod)
