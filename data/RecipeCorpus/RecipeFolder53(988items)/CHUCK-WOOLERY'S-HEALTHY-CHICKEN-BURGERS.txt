Title: CHUCK WOOLERY'S HEALTHY CHICKEN BURGERS
Categories: Sandwiches, Chicken, Celebrity, Low-fat, Wrv
Yield: 4 Sandwiches
MMMMM-----------------WALDINE VAN GEFFEN VGHC42A----------------------
1 lb Ground chicken or turkey
1/2 c  Plain bread crumbs
1/4 c  Apple sauce
1/4 c  Yellow onion; mince
1/4 c  Parsley; chop
1 ts Lea & Perrin
1 ts Lemon juice
Salt and pepper
Combine all ingredients and shape into patties. Grill, broil or saute
about 4-5 minutes per side. Serve with Christina Ferrare's No-Fat
Sauce. Source: Home and Family. MM Waldine Van Geffen
vghc42a@prodigy.com.
