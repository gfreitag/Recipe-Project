Title: CITRUS SPREAD.
Categories: Spreads
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Lemon
100 g  3,5 oz medium fat cream
Cheese
25 g  1 oz caster sugar.
Finely grate the rind from the lemon and mix with the cheese. Squeeze
the juice from the lemon. Add 15 ml or 1 tbsp of the lemon juice and
the sugar to the cheese. Keep in the fridge.
