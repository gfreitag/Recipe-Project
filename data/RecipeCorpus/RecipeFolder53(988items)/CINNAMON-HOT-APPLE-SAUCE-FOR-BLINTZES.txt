Title: CINNAMON HOT APPLE SAUCE FOR BLINTZES
Categories: Other sauce
Yield: 1 Servings
2 tb Unsalted butter
2 tb Heavy cream
1/2 c  Sugar
3/4 ts Cornstarch
2 tb Grenadine syrup
1 tb Calvados or apple brandy
2 c  Tart cooking apples --
Sliced
2    Tablespoons   Sour cream
Melt the butter in a medium skillet over moderate heat. Add the sugar,
syrup, and apple slices.  Stir to coat the slices. Cover and cook,
stirring once, until the apples are translucent, about 5 minutes.

Stir together the sour cream, heavy cream, and cornstarch. Stir the
mixture into the apples.  Bring to the boil, cook for 1 minute, and
remove from the heat.  Stir in the Calvados.

Makes 1-1/4 cups.

From The Brooklyn Cookbook by Stallworth and Kennedy
