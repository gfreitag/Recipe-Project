Title: CITRUS CHICKEN & RICE TERIYAKI
Categories: Poultry, Grains, Oriental
Yield: 4 Servings
4    Chicken breast halves
1/3 c  Teriyaki sauce,low-sodium
3 tb Teriyaki sauce,low-sodium
2 tb Peanut oil
1/2 ts Sesame seeds
1/2 ts Ginger,ground
1/2 c  Walnuts,chopped
1 c  Rice,long-grain,cooked
1    Orange,peeled & sectioned
1/2    Lemon,sliced
1. Combine chicken and 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce in bowl. Refrigerate
for 1-2 hours. 2. Drain chicken; slice into thin strips. Stir-fry
chicken in oil in large skillet over medium-high heat 5 minutes until
cooked through. Add sesame seeds, ginger, walnuts, 3 tablespoons
teriyaki sauce and rice; stir-fry to heat through. Add orange and
lemon. Serve. (Pam Pearson, Pawtucket RI)
