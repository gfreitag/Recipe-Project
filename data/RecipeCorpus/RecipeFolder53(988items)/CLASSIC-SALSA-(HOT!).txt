Title: CLASSIC SALSA (HOT!)
Categories: Vegetarian, Sauces
Yield: 1 Servings
2 c  Tomatoes; peeled, chopped
2 tb Cilantro; minced
2 tb Onion, red; minced
1 ts Chilies, hot; minced
ds Honey or alternative
Chili powder
Salt
Wine vinegar or lemon juice
Combine tomatoes, cilantro, onion and hot pepper. Season to taste
with sweetener, chili powder, salt, and wine vinegar. Yields 2 1/2
cups.
