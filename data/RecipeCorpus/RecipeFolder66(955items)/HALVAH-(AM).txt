Title: HALVAH (AM)
Categories: Biscuits
Yield: 1 Batch
1/2 c  AM Hulled Sesame Seeds
2 tb AM Hulled Sesame Seeds
3 tb Raw honey
1/4 c  AM Sesame Tahini
-- (use the driest part
-- of the jar)
1/8 ts Almond extract
Grind 1/2 cup seeds in a blender.  Mix ground seeds, whole seeds,
tahini, honey and extract in a bowl.  Mix all together until
thoroughly blended. Roll into small balls or into a long roll.
Refrigerate.
