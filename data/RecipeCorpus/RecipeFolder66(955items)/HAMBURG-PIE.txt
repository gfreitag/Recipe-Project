Title: HAMBURG PIE
Categories: Meats, Casseroles
Yield: 6 Servings
1 lb Hamburger
1 c  Minced onion
1/2 ts Salt
1 c  Cheddar cheese grated
1 c  Milk
1/2 c  Biscuit mix
2 ea Eggs
cook hamburger and onion to-gether, drain off fat,add the salt and
stir well, put in greased 9 inch pan and sprinkle cheese on top. Mix
the eggs milk and biscuit to-gether well and pour over above mixture,
bake in
400    oven 25 minutes.
