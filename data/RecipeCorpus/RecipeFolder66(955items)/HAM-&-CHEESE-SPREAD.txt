Title: HAM & CHEESE SPREAD
Categories: Pork, Salads, Sandwiches, Appetizers
Yield: 6 Servings
1 c  1% LOW-FAT COTTAGE CHEESE
2 tb GRATED PARMESAN CHEESE
1 ts DIJON MUSTARD
2 oz LOW-FAT SWISS CHEESE, FINELY
SHREDDED, (1/2 CUP)
6 oz PITA BREAD TRIANGLES,
FRENCH BREAD SLICES, OR
MELBA TOAST ROUNDS
IN MED-SIZE BOWL, COMBINE COTTAGE CHEESE, PARMESAN CHEESE, AND
MUSTARD; ADD HAM AND SWISS CHEESE. WITH WOODEN SPOON, MIX UNTIL
THOROUGHLY BLENDED. TO SERVE, SPREAD ON PITA TRIANGLES OR TOAST.

PER SERVING: (1/4C AND 1OZ BREAD) 230 CAL., 15GR. PRO., 31GR. CARB.,
4.5GR. FAT, 19% CAL FROM FAT, 15MG. CHOL., 626MG SOD.
