Title: GROUND MEAT MIX
Categories: Ground, Chicken, Breast
Yield: 20 Servings
3    Egg whites -- whipped
2 c  Bread crumbs
1 c  Celery -- chopped
1 c  Onions -- chopped
1 c  Bell peppers
1 c  Carrots -- shredded
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Olive oil
3 lb Ground chicken breast,
Skinless -- cooked
In a mixing bowl, combine egg whites, bread crumbs, celery, onions,
bell peppers, carrots, and salt. Add chicken and mix well. In a
skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Add chicken mixture. Cook until
chicken is no longer pink. Cool.
