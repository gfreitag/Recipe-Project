Title: GUERRINI CHICKEN WINGS**
Categories: Chicken, Appetizers, Bar-b-q
Yield: 6 Servings
2 lb To 3 lb chicken wings
1 1/2 ts Salt
7 tb Cornstarch
2    Cloves garlic, minced
4 tb Flour
2    Eggs
5 tb Soy sauce
4 tb Sugar
1 tb Toasted sesame seeds
2    Green onions, chopped
Oil for frying
Mix all ingredients except oil and chicken.  Add chicken to mixture
and marinate overnight in the refrigerator, turning occasionally to
coat. Deep fry until golden brown.
