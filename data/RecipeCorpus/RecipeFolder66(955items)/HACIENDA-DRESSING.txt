Title: HACIENDA DRESSING
Categories: Salads, Dressings
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 c  Pace Picante Sauce
1/2 c  Ranch, creamy garlic, creamy
-cucumber or creamy Italian
-dressing
Combine ingredients; mix well. Toss with mixed green salads, bean
salads or pasta salads, or drizzle over sliced tomatoes, cucumbers or
avacados.

Makes about 1 cup dressing.
