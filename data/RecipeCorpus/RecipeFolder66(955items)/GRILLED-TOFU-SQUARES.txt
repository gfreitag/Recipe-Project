Title: GRILLED TOFU SQUARES
Categories: Vegetables, Sept.
Yield: 1 Servings
1 pk Prefried tofu squares
1    Cucumber : juliened
1    Carrot
x  Some bean sprouts : blanced
x  Pineapples : sliced
Thinly/strips
: juliened
1) grill the tofu squares lightly then cut the sides open then stuff
the julienned vegs.

2) EAT with sauce : 1 part chilli sauce   1 part lemon juice sugar to
taste

Hope that you'll all try this quick finger food and for the kids eat
with plum sauce!
