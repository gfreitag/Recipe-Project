Title: HAROSETH FOR PASSOVER
Categories: Pileggi, Not exp, Try it, Passover
Yield: 3 Cups
MMMMM------------------SEPHARDIC COOKING; MARK-----------------------
~Jewish, Uzbek
1 c  Walnuts
1 c  Raisins
1 lg Apple, peeled and cored
1/2 c  Red Passover wine

Grind everything together to mix thoroughly but leaving some texture.
Do not grind too smoothly.

Every Jewish community has its own version of Haroseth, that
indispensable ritual food for the Passover Seder table. Here is the
Bukharan version.
