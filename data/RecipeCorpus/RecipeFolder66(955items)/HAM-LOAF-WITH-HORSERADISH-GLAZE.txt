Title: HAM LOAF WITH HORSERADISH GLAZE
Categories: Meats, Main dish, Ground beef
Yield: 12 Servings
---Ham Loaf---
1 1/2 lb Ground ham
1 1/2 lb Ground pork
1/2 lb Ground beef
1 1/2 c  Oats
4    Eggs; beaten
1/2 c  Chopped onion
1/2 ts Pepper
2 c  Tomato juice
---Horseradish Glaze---
1 1/2 c  Sugar
2 tb Flour
2 ts Dry mustard
8 tb Horseradish
Combine all ham loaf ingredients thoroughly and pack in 9x13" pan.
Bake in 350 degree oven 45 minutes.
Mix together Horseradish Glaze ingredients and spread on Ham Loaf
mixture. Continue baking 30 minutes more.

Source: Betty J. Shaffer, Stamford (Nebr.) Centennial Cookbook
