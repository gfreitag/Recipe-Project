Title: HARBYS BARBEQUE SAUCE
Categories: Bbq sauces
Yield: 16 Servings
1 c  Water
1/3 c  Vinegar, white
3 tb Cornstarch
6 tb Sugar
1/2 c  Ketchup
1 tb Soy sauce
2 ts Onion powder
1 1/2 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Garlic salt
1/2 ts White pepper
Mix very well.  Heat in 2 quart saucepan, stirring constantly until
thickened and clear.
