Title: HAM SALAD WITH CHEDDAR DRESSING
Categories: Cheese/eggs, Meats, Main dish
Yield: 6 Servings
3 c  Bite-size pieces salad green
1 1/2 c  Frozen green peas; thawed
-and drained
3/4 lb Fully cooked smoked ham; cut
-into 1/2 inch cubes (about
-2 cups)
1/2    Cauliflower head; broken in
-to flowerets
1/2 sm Red onion; thinly sliced
H
Cheddar Dressing
1 c  Cheddar cheese; shredded
-(4 ounces)
1/4 c  Milk
2 tb Wine vinegar
1/4 ts Salt
3 oz Cream cheese; softened
1    Garlic clove
Toss all ingredients except Cheddar Dressing.  Serve with dressing
and freshly ground pepper if desired. Cheddar Dressing:
Place all ingredients in workbowl of food processor fitted with
steel blade or in blender container. Cover and process, stopping once
to scrape down sides, until mixture is of uniform consistency, about
30 seconds.
