Title: HAU TOEI CHAO FAN B1
Categories: Eggs, Chinese, Side dish, Grains
Yield: 6 Servings
1/4 c  Peanut or other salad oil
4    Eggs; well-beaten
1 c  Chopped onion
10 oz Frozen peas & carrots
-- thawed
2 ts Salt
4 c  Cold cooked rice
3 tb Soy sauce
1/2 c  Diced cooked ham
2    Scallions; finely chopped
Heat 2 tbs. oil in wok or large skillet over high heat until very
hot. Add eggs, stir-fry 1/2 munite until firm (same as scrambled
eggs). Remove, cut into small pieces and set aside set aside. Heat
remaining oil over high heat; lightly brown onion. Add peas and
carrots and salt; stir-fry 1 minute. Add cold rice, stir constandly
for 2 minutes. Add soy sauce, ham, scallions and egg; mix well and
serve hot.
