Title: HASTY PUDDING (MRS. BEETON'S)
Categories: Desserts, Mrs. beeton
Yield: 3 Servings
*INGREDIENTS*
1 pt Milk
Sugar
Flour, tapioca or sago
Salt
Serves 3

*DIRECTIONS*

Boil up the milk; with the left hand sprinkle in sufficient flour,
sago, or tapioca to thicken the milk, stirring briskly meanwhile. Add
a little salt and sugar to taste, stir and cook for about 10 minutes,
and serve with cream and sugar, jam or treacle.

Time: About 20 minutes. Sufficient for 3 or 4 persons.

From: Mrs. Beeton's All About Cookery, Ward, Lock & Co., Ltd., Date
unknown (c. 1890's)

Shared by June Hoffman, 6/93
