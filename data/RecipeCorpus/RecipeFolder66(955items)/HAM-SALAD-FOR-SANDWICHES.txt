Title: HAM SALAD FOR SANDWICHES
Categories: Sandwiches, Pork
Yield: 1 Salad
1 lb Ham (Pork, Veal or Turkey)
4    Or 5 pickles (cucumbers)
Salt and Pepper
1/2 c  Mayonaisse
1/2 c  Cream
Bread in Slices for to make
The sandwiches
Cut the ham and little cucumbers in very thiny pieces, mix with the
cream, mayonaisse, salt and pepper. Fill the bread. Patricia Wriedt
