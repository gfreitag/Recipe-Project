Title: HAZELNUT & MUSHROOM SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
1/2 lb Mushrooms, sliced
3/4 c  Salad oil
1/2 c  Wine vinegar
2 tb Water
3/4 ts Salt
3/4 ts Savory
1/4 ts Onion powder
1/8 ts Pepper
1 sm Head romaine
-torn into bite sized pieces
3/4 c  Roasted & chopped hazelnuts
-(Oregon)
Combine mushrooms, oil, vinegar, water and seasonings. Chill about 1
hour. Toss undrained mushrooms with romaine and hazelnuts in salad
bowl.

Yield: 6 to 8 servings.
