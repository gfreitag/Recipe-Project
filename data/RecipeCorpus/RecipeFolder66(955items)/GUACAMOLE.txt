Title: GUACAMOLE
Categories: Mexican
Yield: 4 Servings
2 ea Avocado, pitted & peeled
1 ea Onion, minced
1 ts Chili powder
1 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper, ground
1 ea Tomato, chopped
Mash avocados with silver spoon. Beat in the other ingredients until
creamy.  Refrigerate. You can substitute 2* green finely chopped chili
peppers instead of chili powder. * For standard serving size.
