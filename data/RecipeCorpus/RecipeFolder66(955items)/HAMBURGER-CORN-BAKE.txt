Title: HAMBURGER CORN BAKE
Categories: Hamburger
Yield: 6 Servings
1 1/2 lb Hamburger
1 ea Chopped onion
1 cn Kernel corn
1 cn Cream of chicken soup
1 cn Mushroom soup
1 c  Dairy sour cream
1/4 c  Chopped can pimento
3/4 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
In large fry pan saute hamburger & onion till meat is cooked then add
rest of ingredients. Stir in 3 cups of medium broad noodles which
have been cooked and drained. Turn into casserole and top with chow
mein noodles, dot with margarine and bake 45 minutes in 350 F oven.
