Title: HARVEST RICE
Categories: Side dish, Vegetarian
Yield: 6 Servings
3 tb Vegetable oil
1 c  Thinly sliced carrots
1 c  Sliced green onions
-- including tops
2 c  Cooked brown rice
1/2 c  Seedless raisins
1 tb Sesame seed
1/2 ts Salt
Cook carrots in oil in large skillet over medium-high heat until
tender crisp.  Add onions and apples; cook 5 minutes. Stir in rice,
raisins, sesame seed, and salt. Cook, stirring until thoroughly
heated.

Each serving provides: * 234 calories * 3.1 grams protein * 8.3 grams
fat * 38.8 grams carbohydrate * 189 milligrams sodium * 0 milligrams
cholesterol
