Title: HAMBURGER & BARLEY SOUP
Categories: Soups
Yield: 8 Servings
1 lb Ground beef preferable lean
1 c  Celery  diced
1 c  Carrots, diced
1 c  Cabbage, sliced
1    Onion, small, diced
4 c  Beef broth
1 1/2 c  Tomatoes, canned plum, undra
1/3 c  Barley, uncooked
1 ts Thyme, dried
1/2 ts Basil
1 c  Garlic, chopped
1 1/2 ts Brown sugar
1 1/2 ts Balsamic vinegar
1 ts Worcestershire sauce
Salt & pepper
Brown beef in a large saucepan. Drain off any fat then add celery,
carrots, cabbage, onion, beef broth, tomatoes and juice, barley,
thyme, basil and garlic. Cover, bring to a boil then reduce heat and
simmer 45 minutes to an hour or until vegetables and barley are just
tender. Remove from heat, stir in brown sugar, vinegar,
Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper. Check seasoning and serve with
plenty of crusty bread or rolls.
