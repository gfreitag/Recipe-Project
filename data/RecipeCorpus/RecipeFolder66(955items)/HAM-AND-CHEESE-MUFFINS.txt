Title: HAM-AND-CHEESE MUFFINS
Categories: Breads, Muffins
Yield: 12 Servings
MMMMM-------------------LISA CRAWLEY  TSPN00B------------------------
1 3/4 c  Flour
1/3 c  Rye Flour
2 ts Baking Powder
1/4 ts Salt
1 tb Light Brown Sugar
1/3 c  Cooked Ham; finely chopped
1/2 c  Swiss Cheese; shredded
1 lg Egg; lightly beaten
1 c  Milk
1/4 c  Vegetable Oil
3/4 ts Spicy Brown Mustard
1/2 ts Worcestershire Sauce
3    Drops Hot Sauce
Combine first 5 ingredients in a lg. bowl; stir in ham and cheese.
Make a well in center of mixture. Combine egg and next 5 ingred.; add
to dry ingred., stirring just until moistened. Spoon into greased
muffin pans, filling 2/3 full.  Bake at 400 for 22-25 min. Remove
from pans immediately. Yield: 1 dozen.
