Title: HAM 'N EGGS CRESCENT BRUNCH PIZZA
Categories: Cheese, Pork
Yield: 6 Servings
8 oz Pillsbury quick crescent din
4    Eggs
1/2 ts Italian seasoning
1/2 ts Salt; if desired
1    Cooked ham; cubed
4    Mozzarella cheese
9 oz Frozen cut broccoli; thawed
4 oz Sliced mushrooms; drained
2 tb Green pepper; chopped
1 lg Tomato, peeled, seeded & dic
2 tb Grated parmesan cheese
Recipe by: skthom@ccmail.monsanto.com ("Sheri K. Thomasson") Heat
oven to 350 degrees.  Seperate dough into 8 triangles. Place
triangles in ungreased 12-inch pizza pan or 13X9-inch pan; press over
bottom and 1/2 inch up sides to form crust.  Seal perforations. Beat
eggs with Italian seasoning and salt; pour into doughlined pan.  Top
with ham, mozzarella cheese, broccoli, mushrooms, green peppers and
tomato;sprinkle with parmesan cheese.

Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes or until edges are set and
crust golden brown.  6 to 8 servings.
