Title: HAWAIIAN  CORN  BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 1 Servings
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs
2    Cubes melted butter
3/4 c  Sugar
2 c  Bisquick
1/2 ts Baking soda
2 tb Cornmeal
Combine all ingredients until smooth.  Pour in greased 8 x 8 inch
pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. (You may want to half this
recipe.)
