Title: HANDMADE SHAKE & BAKE CHICKEN
Categories: Poultry
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 c  Unbleached flour
1 ts Paprika
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Garlic powder
1/2 ts Thyme
1/4 ts Black pepper
4    Skinless boneless chicken
Breast
1/2 c  Skim milk -- at room
Temperature
Preheat oven at 375.  Prepare pan with foil, sprayed with cooking
spray. Combine flour, paprika, salt, garlic powder, thyme, and pepper
in a plastic bag. Dip breasts in milk; shake off excess. Add to bag
and shake to coat evenly.  Place chicken on a prepared baking dish.
Bake 40 minutes, until chicken is tender and coating crisp.
