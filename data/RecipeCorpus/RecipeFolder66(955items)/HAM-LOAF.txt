Title: HAM LOAF
Categories: Main dish
Yield: 1 Servings
1 lb Ground ham
1 lb Ground pork
1 c  Bread crumbs
1 c  Milk
2    Eggs, slightly beaten
Topping
1/4 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Water
1 c  Brown sugar
1 ts Mustard
1/2 c  Catsup
Combine loaf ingredients together and shape into a loaf. Mix the
topping ingredients and pour over meat before baking. Bake at 350
degrees for 1 hour. Randy Rigg
