Title: HAWAIIAN MARTINI
Categories: Alcoholic, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Serving
1 1/2 oz Vodka
3/4 oz Blue curacao
Splash of orange juice
1    Maraschino cherry
Combine and shake until frost forms on outside of shaker (10-15
shakes). Strain into martini glass. Drop cherry into glass (instead
of the customary olive). Serves 1.

Per serving: 125 calories, 0 grams fat (0 percent fat from calories),
0 milligrams cholesterol, 0 grams protein, 4 grams carbohydrate, 1
milligrams sodium.

SOURCE: The Sting-Ray Room, Raleigh, NC. Recipe appeared in the
