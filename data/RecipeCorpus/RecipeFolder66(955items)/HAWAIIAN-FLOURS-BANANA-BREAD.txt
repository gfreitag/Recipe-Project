Title: HAWAIIAN FLOURS BANANA BREAD
Categories: Breads
Yield: 9 Servings
1 c  Banana Cake Mix
1/2 c  Whole Wheat Flour
1 c  Bread Flour
1 c  All Purpose Flour
2 ts Yeast
2 tb Oil
3 tb Gluten
1 tb Brown sugar
1 1/4 c  Water (boiling)
1/2 c  Oats
1/4 c  Chopped nuts (optional)
(Walnut or Pecan)
Boil water then add oats.  Let steep for 10 minutes. Add ingredients
according to your ABM requirements.

Makes a small loaf--9 slices plus heel.
