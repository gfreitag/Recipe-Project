Title: Homemade Croutons
Categories: Salads
Servings:  2
1 T  Butter
5 T  Light Oil
2 ea Garlic cloves minced
1 t  Oregano
1 t  Thyme
5 ea Day old bread
Melt butter in skillet then add oil.  Mix in remaining
ingredients.  Cut 5 slices of day old bread and remove
crusts.  Cut into 1/2" cubes.  Fry slowly until
lightly brown.
