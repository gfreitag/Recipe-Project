Title: Date Nut Bars
Categories: Cakes
Servings: 10
1 lb Brown sugar
1/3 c  White sugar
1 1/2 ea Stick of butter
3 ea Eggs
2 1/2 c  Flour-plain
1/2 t  Salt
1 T  Vanilla
1 c  Chopped pecans
1 pk Dates
Mix all together - put in greased cookie sheet which
has edge. bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.  Cut
while warm.  Makes about 4 dozen. Caot dates with a
little flour before mixing in.
