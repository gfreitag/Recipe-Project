Title: Salsa, Low Cal.
Categories: Relishes
Servings:  1
3 T  Instant Minced Onion
3 T  Water
1 ea Tomatoes, 16 oz. can, crush
1/4 c  Chopped green pepper
1 T  Chili powder
1/4    arlic powder
1 ds Red pepper, ground
In medium sized bowl, combine all ingredients; mix
well.  Place in covered container; chill until ready
to serve.
