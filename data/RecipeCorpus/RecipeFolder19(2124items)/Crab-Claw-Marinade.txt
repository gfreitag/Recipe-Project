Title: Crab Claw Marinade
Categories: Appetizers
Servings:  6
1 c  Olive oil
1/2 c  Vinegar
1/4 c  Lemon juice
1 t  Tarragon
3/4 t  Black pepper
3/4 t  Salt
3/4 t  Sugar
----------------------FOOD PROCESSOR----------------------
1 c  Parsley
1 c  Celery
1 c  Scallions
4 ea Cloves of garlic (4-10)
Mix all together, pour over claws and serve.
