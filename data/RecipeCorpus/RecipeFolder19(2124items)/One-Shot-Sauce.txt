Title: One Shot Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Servings:  3
1 lb Butter
1/2 c  Finely chopped onion
2 x  Cloves garlic, minced
1/2 c  Whiskey
1/4 c  Worcestershire sauce
1 T  Pepper
1 1/2 t  Dry mustard
1 t  Salt
1/4 t  Tabasco
Melt butter in a saucepan; add onion and garlic and
cook slowly until is soft. Add remaining ingredients
and beat to mix.
