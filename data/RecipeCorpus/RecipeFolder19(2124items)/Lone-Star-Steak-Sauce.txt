Title: Lone Star Steak Sauce
Categories: Sauces
Servings:  4
1/2 c  Butter
2 T  Worcestershire Sauce
3/4 t  Black Pepper
2 ea Drops Tabasco
1/2 c  Lemon Juice
1 ea Sm. Clove Garlic, Minced
1/2 t  Dry Mustard
1 x  Salt To Taste
Combine all ingredients, heat until butter melts.
Broiler juices may be added.  Serve with Steak Salt.
