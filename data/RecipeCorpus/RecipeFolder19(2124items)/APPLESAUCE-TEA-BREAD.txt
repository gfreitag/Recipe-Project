Title: APPLESAUCE TEA BREAD
Categories: Breads
Servings: 20
2 1/2 c  White flour
2 t  Baking powder
1/2 t  Salt
1/2 c  Sugar
1 T  Cinnamon
8 oz Applesauce
1 ea Egg
1 c  Skim milk
Mix the dry ingredients together and add the
applesauce, then the egg and milk.  Pour into nonstick
loaf pan and bake at 350F for 50 min.  Remove and
cool.  Cal: 77 (slice); Fat: 1/10g.
