Title: Cheese Log
Categories: Appetizers Cheese/eggs
Servings: 10
1/2 lb New York Sharp Cheese
1/2 lb Pimento cheese
1/2 lb Cream cheese
2 ea Garlic cloves
2 t  Worcestershire sauce
1/4 t  Red pepper
2 T  Mayonnaise
1/4 t  Salt
Grate cheese.  Add all ingredients, chill a little
and divide into thirds.  Roll in paprika.  Can be
frozen.
