Title: GRANNY SMITH CHOCOLATE OATMEAL PIE
Categories: Pies, Fruits
Yield: 8 Servings
4    Eggs
1 c  Sugar
2 tb Flour
1 ts Cinnamon, ground
1/8 ts Salt
2 tb Butter; melted
1/2 c  Light corn syrup
1 ts Vanilla extract
1 c  Old-fashioned rolled oats
2    Granny Smith apples; peeled/
-cored/chopped
1/2 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1    9" unbaked pie shell
1. Beat eggs in a large bowl until frothy.

2. Mix together sugar, flour, cinnamon and salt in a small bowl. Stir
into eggs until blended. Add butter, corn syrup and vanilla; mix
well. Fold in oats, apples and chocolate chips. Spoon filling into
pie shell.

3. Bake in a preheated 350-degree oven until pie is set, about 1 hour
and 10 minutes. Let cool before cutting.

Source: Chicago Sun Times, November 6, 1996
