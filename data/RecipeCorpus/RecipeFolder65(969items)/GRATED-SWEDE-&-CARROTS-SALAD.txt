Title: GRATED SWEDE & CARROTS SALAD
Categories: Britain, Vegetables, Sweden, Salads
Yield: 4 Servings
MMMMM--------------------------SERVES 4-------------------------------
1 lb Carrots, peeled and grated
4    Ozs swede, peeled and grated
2    Ozs raisins
MMMMM----------------------FOR THE DRESSING---------------------------
5    Fl oz carton natural yogurt
3    Level Tablespoons mayonnaise
2    Level teaspoons English
-made-mustard
2-3 level Tablespoons
-mango chutney
1/2 oz Flaked almonds, toasted
Place the carrots, swede and raisins in a bowl. Mix the yogurt,
mayonnaise, mustard and chutney together and toss with the grated
vegetables and raisins. Sprinkle over the flaked almonds before
serving. Source: Magazine ~ possibly Woman's Weekly - date unknown
