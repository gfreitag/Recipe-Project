Title: GREEN IRISH WHISKEY SOUR JELLY FOR ST. PATRIC
Categories: Alcohol, Beverages
Yield: 1 Servings
2 sm Boxes Lemon-Lime Flavor
-Gelatine, * see note
2 c  Boiling Water
1 1/2 c  Cold Water
1/2 c  Irish Whiskey
* Two small boxes of Nabisco "Royal"-brand gelatine, LEMON-LIME
flavor. (General Foods Jell-O brand doesn't come in this flavor, but
you could probably substitute one small Lemon Jell-O and one small
Lime Jell-O.)

Combine in the usual way..
