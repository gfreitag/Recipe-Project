Title: GRATED APPLE KUGEL
Categories: Kosher, Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 1 Servings
6    Large, juicy apples,grated
1/2 c  Sifted matzo meal
Grated peel of 1 lemon
1/2 c  Sugar
Blanched almonds
8    Eggs, separated
Stir the sugar and yolks well; add the grated apples, and mix well.
Then stir in the matzo meal and the grated lemon peel. Last add the
stiff bveaten whites of the 8 eggs. Bake in an ungreased spring form
pan. Sprinkle a handful of blanched almonds on top just before
baking. Bake at 325 F. approx. 1 hr.
