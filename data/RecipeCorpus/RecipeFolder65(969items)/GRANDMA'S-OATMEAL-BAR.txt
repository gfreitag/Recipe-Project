Title: GRANDMA'S OATMEAL BAR
Categories: Cookies
Yield: 24 Bars
1/4 c  Margarine, (unsalted)
1/4 c  Sugar
1/4 c  Molasses
1/4 c  Egg substitute
6 oz Can frozen orange juice,
- concentrate, thawed
1/2 c  Rolled oats
1/4 c  Raisins
1/4 c  Walnuts
2 c  All purpose flour
1 ts Baking flour
1/2 ts Ginger
1/2 ts Cinnamon
1/4 ts Salt
1/4 ts Cloves
1/4 ts Nutmeg
Cream margarine and sugar until fluffy.  Stir in molasses, egg
substitute and orange juice.  Mix in reamining ingredients. Spread in
13 by 9 by 2-inch pan sprayed with vegetable spray. Bake in a
325-degree oven for 25 to 30 minutes.  Cool and cut into 24 bars.
