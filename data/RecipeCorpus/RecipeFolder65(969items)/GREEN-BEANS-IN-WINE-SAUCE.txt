Title: GREEN BEANS IN WINE SAUCE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 8 Servings
4 c  Green beans, cooked
3 sl Bacon -- diced
1 md Onion -- sliced
3 tb Sugar
2 ts Cornstarch
3 tb Tarragon vinegar
1/2 c  Sherry
Saute bacon and onion.  Add sugar, cornstarch, vinegar and wine. Cook
until thickened, stirring constantly.  Pour over hot beans; serve.
