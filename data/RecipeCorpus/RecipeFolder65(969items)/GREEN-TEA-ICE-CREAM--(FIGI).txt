Title: GREEN TEA ICE CREAM  (FIGI)
Categories: Asian, Beverages, Desserts, Frozen
Yield: 4 Servings
1/2 ga Vanilla ice cream
1/2 oz Powdered green tea
Soften the ice cream and add the powder in small amounts, until the
ice cream has the strength and flavor you desire. Then refreeze the
ice cream until it is ready to be served.

: Sunshine Magazine (9/17/95)
