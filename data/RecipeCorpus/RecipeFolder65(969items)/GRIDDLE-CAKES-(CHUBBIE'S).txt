Title: GRIDDLE CAKES (CHUBBIE'S)
Categories: Breakfast
Yield: 4 Servings
2 c  Flour
2 tb Sugar
3/4 ts Salt
3 ts Baking powder
2 ea Eggs
1 1/4 c  Milk
1/4 c  Melted shortening or oil
Stir to-gether flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Beat eggs and
milk. Combine with dry ingredients mixing until smooth, add oil or
shortening.
