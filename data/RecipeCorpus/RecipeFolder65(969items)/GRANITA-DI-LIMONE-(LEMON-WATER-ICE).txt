Title: GRANITA DI LIMONE (LEMON WATER ICE)
Categories: Desserts, Fruits
Yield: 3 Servings
4 lg Lemons
1/2 c  Sugar
2 c  Water
Wash one lemon and pare the rind thinly with a potato peeler. Heat the
sugar and water gently until the sugar has completely dissolved, then
add the rind and boil for 5 minutes exactly. Strain and leave until
cold. Squeeze the juice from all 4 lemons, strain into the syrup and
mix. Pour into an ice cube tray and freeze until firm.
