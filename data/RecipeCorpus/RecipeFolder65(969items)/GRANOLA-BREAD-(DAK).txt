Title: GRANOLA BREAD (DAK)
Categories: Breadmaker
Yield: 1 Servings
MMMMM------------------MICHAEL HATALA  RJHP21A-----------------------
MMMMM---------------------FOR 1-1/2 LB. LOAF--------------------------
1 pk Yeast
3/4 c  Whole wheat flour
2 c  Bread flour
1 c  Granola; grind finely in
-blender
3/4 ts Salt
1 1/2 ts Sugar
3/4 c  Warm water
1/2 c  Buttermilk
2 tb Sweet butter; warm
2 tb Honey
1    Egg
Add all ingredients, select white bread and push start.

From Loafing It by DAK
