Title: GRANOLA #3
Categories: Breakfast, Grain/pasta, Vegetarian, Diabetic
Yield: 11 Servings
2 c  Wheat flakes
2 c  Rye flakes
1 c  Barley flakes
3 c  Oat flakes
1 c  Shredded coconut
1 c  Nuts, chopped
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds
1/2 c  Sesame seeds
MMMMM--------------------------COATING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Oil
1/2 c  Honey**
2 ts Vanilla
1 ts Salt
**PERSONAL NOTE from Ursula
-Taylor for use by diabetic-
-why not use Cary's SF syrup
Mix all the cereal ingredients together in a big bowl.
Spread out on shallow pans.  Pour the coating over the cereal
mixture. Bake in 275 oven for 1 hour.  Mix the cereal every 15
minutes to coat all the flakes. Cool and store in airtight
containers. Makes about 11-12 cups of granola.
