Title: GREEN PEA GUACAMOLE
Categories: Mexican, Appetizers, Vegetables, Low fat
Yield: 1 1/2 cups
20 oz Frozen peas, rinsed;
-defrosted and patted dry
1 c  Onion; chopped
2 lg Cloves of garlic; peeled and
- minced
2 ts Lemon juice
1/4 c  Fresh cilantro; chopped
1 ea Salt and ground pepper; to
-taste
1 ds Hot pepper sauce; optional
Place peas, onion and garlic in food processor fitted with steel
blade. Process until smooth.  Add lemon juice and pulse motor until
blended. Stir in cilantro and season to taste with salt, pepper and
hot pepper sauce. Scrape into bowl, cover and chill until needed.

Nutritional analysis per serving:
116    calories,
0.5    grams fat, no cholesterol,
112    milligrams sodium
