Title: GREEN-BEAN SALAD
Categories: German, Salads, Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 lb Green beans; fresh *
1 x  ;boiling salted water
1/4 c  Stock; **
3 tb Vinegar
3 tb Vegetable oil
2 ea Onions; med., thinly sliced
1/2 ts Dried dillseed
1 ts Sugar
*    Green beans should be sliced lengthwise (French Cut). ** Stock
is the water that the green beans were cooked in. (Not regular
+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
++++++++++++++ ++++ Cook beans in boiling salted water until just
tender. Reserve 1/4 cup of the cooking liquid and drain off the rest.
Prepare sauce by combining vinegar, oil, reserved vegetable stock,
onions, dill, and sugar; stir until blended.  Pour mixture over
beans; marinate several hours before serving.
