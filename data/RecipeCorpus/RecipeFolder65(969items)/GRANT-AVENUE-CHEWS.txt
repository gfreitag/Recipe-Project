Title: GRANT AVENUE CHEWS
Categories: Cookies, Bars
Yield: 24 Squares
1 c  C and H Golden Brown Sugar
-- firmly packed
2    Eggs
3/4 c  All-purpose flour
1 ts Baking powder
14 ts Salt
1 c  Chopped dates
1 c  Chopped walnuts
C and H Powdered Sugar
Mix together sugar and eggs.  Add flour, baking powder and salt. Mix
well. Fold in dates and nuts.  Pour into greased 9-inch square pan.
Bake in 350 degree oven 30 minutes. Cool 10 minutes. Cut into 24
squares. Roll in powdered sugar.
