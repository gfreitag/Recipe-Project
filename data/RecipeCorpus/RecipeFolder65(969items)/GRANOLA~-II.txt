Title: GRANOLA~ II
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 10 Cups
6 c  Reg. oats, uncooked
1 1/2 c  Coconut
3/4 c  Honey
1/2 c  Vegetable oil
1 ts Cinnamon
1 1/2 c  Chopped pecans
1 c  Wheat germ
1/4 c  Water
1 tb Vanilla flavor
1 ts Nutmeg
1/2 ts Salt
Combine oats, pecans, coconut and wheat germ. Combine honey and
water in small saucepan; heat, stirring frequently until mixture
thins and is thoroughly heated.  Stir in oil and vanilla; pour over
oat mixture and mix well.  Stir in spices and salt. Pour into 2
greased 13 x 9 x 2 -inch baking pans. Bake at 250 F. for 1 hr.,
stirring abt. every 15 min. Store in airtight container.
