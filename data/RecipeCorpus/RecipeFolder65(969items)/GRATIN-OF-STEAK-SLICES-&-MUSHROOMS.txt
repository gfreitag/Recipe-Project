Title: GRATIN OF STEAK SLICES & MUSHROOMS
Categories: Beef
Yield: 3 Servings
3/4 lb Mushrooms -- chopped fine
3    Cloves garlic -- minced
Fine
6    Shallots -- chopped fine
OR scallion bulbs
1/4 c  Fresh parsley -- chopped
2 tb Fresh herbs -- chopped
Such as: thyme,savory, or
Tarragon -- or equvalent
Dried
3/4 c  Fresh bread crumbs
Salt
Fresh ground pepper
1/4 c  Olive oil
8 sl Flank steak, broiled -- thin
Rare
2 tb Red wine
Mix the mushrooms, garlic, shallots, parsley, and herbs with 1/2 cup
bread crumbs.  Add salt and pepper to taste, then mix in 1 tablespoon
olive oil. Rub the bottom of a shallow casserole with a little olive
oil. Distribute half the mushroom-crumb mixture over the bottom in an
even layer. Lay the steak slices in a single layer on top; season
with salt and pepper and drizzle the wine over.  Spread the remaining
mushroom mixture over the beef slices, and top with the last of the
bread crumbs. Drizzle the remaining olive oil on top, and bake,
uncovered in a preheated 375-degree oven for 25 minutes.

: L.L.Bean Book of New England Cookery
