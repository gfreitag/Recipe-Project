Title: GREEN OLIVE PESTO
Categories: New, Text, Import
Yield: 4 Servings    1 1/2 c  lg or jumbo green olives,
such as ascolane, pitted
:
1/2    red onion, finely chopped
1/4 c  pine nuts
1    clove garlic, thinly sliced
1/2 c  extra virgin olive oil, --
give or take 1 ounce
:

In a food processor, combine olives, onion, pine nuts and garlic and
blend I minute. With motor running, slowly pour in olive oil until a
thick, smooth paste is formed. texturally resembling rough bechamel.
Allow to stand 1/2 hour before using.

Yield: 2 1/2 cups
