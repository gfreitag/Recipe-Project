Title: GRANDMA'S PEACH PIE
Categories: Pies, Desserts
Yield: 6 Servings
6 tb Butter, melted
1/3 c  + 2 T butter
1 1/4 c  Sugar
1 ea Graham cracker crust
6 ea Peaches, sliced
1 ea Cinnamon
Mix the melted butter, butter and sugar.  Sprinkle half of the
mixture in a 9-inch unbaked graham crust. Arrange sliced fresh
peaches in the pie shell until crust is well-filled. Spread rest of
sugar mixture on top of peaches. Sprinkle with cinnamon.  Bake at 400
degrees until bubbly, about 20-30 minutes.  Serves 6
