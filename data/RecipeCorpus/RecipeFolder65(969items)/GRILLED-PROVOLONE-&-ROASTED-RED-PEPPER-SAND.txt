Title: GRILLED PROVOLONE & ROASTED RED PEPPER SAND
Categories: Appetizers, Snacks, Vegetables, Easy
Yield: 2 Sandwiches
4 sl Multi-grain bread
4    (3/4 oz.) slices Provolone
-cheese (from deli)
2    Roasted red bell peppers
-from jar, drained, cut into
-1 inch strips
1 tb Margarine or butter,softened
Top each of 2 bread slices with 1 cheese slice, half of bell pepper
strips and another cheese slice.  Top with remaining bread slices.
Spread margarine on both sides of sandwiches.
