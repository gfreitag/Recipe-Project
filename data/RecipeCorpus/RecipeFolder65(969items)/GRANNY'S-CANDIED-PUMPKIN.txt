Title: GRANNY'S CANDIED PUMPKIN
Categories: Vegetables, Holiday
Yield: 1 Servings
10 c  Thinly cubed pumpkin
2 c  Sugar
6 sl Bacon
Place pumpkin in a large casserole dish.  Cover with sugar. Bake
in a 350F oven until pumpkin starts to get soft. Stir pumpkin and
place bacon on top. Bake until liquid is syrupy.
