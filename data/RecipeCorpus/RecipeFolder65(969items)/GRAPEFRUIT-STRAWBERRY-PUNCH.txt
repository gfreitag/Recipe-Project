Title: GRAPEFRUIT-STRAWBERRY PUNCH
Categories: Beverages, Fruits
Yield: 15 Servings
8 oz Grapefruit frozen
-concentrate
2 lb Strawberries, frozen; thawed
2 qt Ginger ale
Ice cubes
Crush strawberries. Place in bowl. Reconstitute grapefruit juice.
Pour over strawberries; add ice and ginger ale.
