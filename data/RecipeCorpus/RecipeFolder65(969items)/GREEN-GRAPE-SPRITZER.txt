Title: GREEN GRAPE SPRITZER
Categories: Beverages, Diabetic
Yield: 6 Servings
2 c  Grapes, green
1 c  Pineapple juice, chilled
1 c  Mineral water, chilled
In blender container, combine grapes and pineapple juice. Cover; blend
until smooth. Strain to remove grape skins. Return liquid to blender
container. Add mineral water, blend 10 seconds to combine. Serve
S: FAST & HEALTHY
immediately.

60    calories 0 g fat
