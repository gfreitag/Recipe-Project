Title: GREEK MASHED POTATOES WITH GARLIC
Categories: Vegetables, Ethnic
Yield: 8 Servings
3 lb Potatoes
1 c  Vinegar
1    Egg yolk
5    Cloves garlic
2 c  Olive oil
1 tb Salt
1 c  Cooking water from potatoes
Boil potaotes and mash and cool. Add minced garlic, oil, vinegar,
salt. Blend in egg yolk. Add hot cooking water from potatoes and mix
well.
