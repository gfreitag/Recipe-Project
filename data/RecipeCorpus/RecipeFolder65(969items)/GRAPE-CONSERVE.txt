Title: GRAPE CONSERVE
Categories: Condiments
Yield: 6 Servings
3 lb Ripe grapes
2 c  Chopped nuts
2 1/2 lb Sugar
1/2    Lemon
Wash grapes.  Separate skins and pulp.  Simmer pulp until soft. Remove
seeds.  Combine skins, pulp, sugar, and lemon which has been finely
chopped.  Simmer slowly, stirring constantly, until thick. Add nuts
and cook 5 minutes. The Household Searchlight
