Title: GREEN MEATBALLS (MEATLESS)
Categories: Appetizers, Main dishes, Mc
2 pk frozen chopped spinach --
thawed and squeezed
Yield: 1 Servings
:
1/2 c  seasoned bread crumbs
1/4 c  Parmesan
3    egg whites
2    cloves garlic -- minced
1 sm onion -- minced
1/2 ts oregano

Combine all ingredients, mixing by hand.
Preheat oven to 350B0F.
Mixture should be the consistency to form meatballs. If too soft, add
breadcrumbs.  Form into small balls. Bake on a Pam-sprayed cookie
sheet 30-35 minutes.
