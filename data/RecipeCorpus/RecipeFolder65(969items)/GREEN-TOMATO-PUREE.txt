Title: GREEN TOMATO PUREE
Categories: Main dish, Pasta, Sauces, Vegetables
Yield: 6 Cups
3 lb Green tomatoes
1/2 c  Water
2 tb Sugar
Core tomatoes and cut into 1" chunks.  Place in a stainless steel or
enamelware saucepan.  Add water and sugar. Bring to a boil over medium
heat.  Lower heat to a simmer. Cover. Cook 2 hours or until tomato
bits are very soft.  Place mixture, 1/3 at a time, in blender or food
processor. Process until smooth. Mixture can be frozen. Serve tossed
with pasta.
