Title: GREEK TOMATO SAUCE
Categories: Sauces, Greek, Lamb
Yield: 1 Servings
3 tb Olive oil
2 ts Oregano
1    Onion; chopped
1 c  Red wine
1    Clove garlic; chopped
8 oz Can tomatoes
5    Very ripe tomatoes
(coarsely chopped)
2 tb Parsley; chopped
1 pn Allspice
Salt & pepper to taste
1. Heat fry pan and add olive oil. Saute onion and garlic until
clear. Add tomatoes, parsley and oregano. Simmer, covered until the
tomatoes are very tender, about 25 min. Add remaining ingredients and
cook 20 more minutes.
