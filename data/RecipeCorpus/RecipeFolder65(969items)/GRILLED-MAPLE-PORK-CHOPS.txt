Title: GRILLED MAPLE PORK CHOPS
Categories: Pork
Yield: 1 Servings
1/4 c  Butter
1 c  Maple syrup
1    Egg
1 c  Flour
1/4 ts Salt
1 ts Vanilla
1/4 c  Chopped nuts; (walnuts)
Recipe by: Wisconsin Maple Producers Council - Aniwa, WI. 54408 Cream
butter and maple syrup. Add flour, egg baking powder, salt and
vanilla. Mix and stir in walnuts. Spread in 9-inch cake pan. Bake at
350 F for 25 minutes. Cool. Cut into squares.
