Title: GREEN BEANS~ NEW POTATOES~ HAM
Categories: Improv, Kohl, Vegetables, Meats
Yield: 1 Recipe
Fresh green beans, cleaned
-and snapped
Smoked ham, cut into small
-chunks
Small new potatoes
Combine beans and ham into a large sauce pan. Cover with water. Bring
to a boil, then simmer until beans are nearly done, adding water as
necessary. Peel potatoes and put them (whole) in with the beans and
ham. Continue cooking until potatoes are done.

OPTIONAL: Substitute Bacon for Ham

From: IT NEVER TURNS OUT THE SAME WAY COOKBOOK A Collection of
Recipes from the Kitchen of Joyce & Clem Kohl
