Title: GRILLED PORTABELLO BURGERS
Categories: Vegetables, Vegetarian
Yield: 1 Servings
8 md Portabello mushrooms
1 c  Olive oil
1/3 c  Fresh lemon juice
5    Cloves fresh minced garlic
2 bn Fresh basil, chopped
1 bn Fresh oregano, chopped
3 tb Fresh parsley, chopped
1 ts Fresh thyme, chopped
Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste 8 slices mozzarella
cheese 8 kaiser rolls (or 2 loaves of Italian Bread) 8 romaine
lettuce leaves 8 slices large, fresh tomato Peel the mushrooms; brush
away any dirt before you cut away the stems. Combine the oil, lemon
juice, garlic, fresh herbs, salt and pepper to make a marinade. Add
the mushrooms and the sliced mozzarella; marinate at room temperature
for about an hour. Grill the mushrooms over hot coals for about 2
minutes on each side. Remove from the grill and allow to rest for 5
minutes. Serve the mushrooms on lightly toasted kaiser rolls with the
lettuce, sliced tomatoes and marinated mozzarella cheese.  Cut each
roll into quarters and serve. Makes 8 burgers From: Asbury Park Press
