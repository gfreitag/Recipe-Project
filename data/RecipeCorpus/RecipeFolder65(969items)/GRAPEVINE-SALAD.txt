Title: GRAPEVINE SALAD
Categories: Salads
Yield: 6 Servings
2    Eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 c  Orange juice
2 c  Any type seedless grapes,
-cut in half
1/4 c  Maraschino cherries, cut
-in half
1/2 c  Chopped walnuts
1 cn (20 oz.) pineapple chunks,
-drained
2    Bananas
In a small saucepan, cook eggs and orange juice over very low heat
until slightly thickened, stirring constantly; set aside to cool.
Meanwhile, in a medium bowl, combine remaining ingredients. Fold in
the egg mixture until evenly combined and refrigerate overnight to
"marry" the flavors. Typed in MMFormat by cjhartlin@msn.com  Source:
