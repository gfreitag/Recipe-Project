Title: GREEN  TOMATO  JELLY
Categories: Jelly, Canning
Yield: 6 Servings
5 c  Green tomatoes,
Cut into sm. pieces
4 c  Sugar
6 oz Pkg. raspberry Jello
Preparation : Boil tomatoes and sugar together for 15 minutes. Remove
from heat.  Add Jello and stir well. Put into blender. Blend well.
Pour into jars and seal. Walt MM
