Title: GREEN TOMATO MINCEMEAT - CAPE BRETON
Categories: Preserves, Holiday, Pastry*pies, Vegetables
Yield: 4 12-oz jars
6 c  Green tomatoes; chopped
6 c  Apples; choppped
2 c  Suet; chopped
1 c  Vinegar
1/2 c  Water
4 c  Brown sugar
1    Orange; chopped
1 tb Cinnamon
1 tb Cloves
1 tb Allspice
1 tb Mace
2 lb Raisins; chopped
Combine all ingredients in saucepan and cook slowly for 2 1/2 - 3
hours
