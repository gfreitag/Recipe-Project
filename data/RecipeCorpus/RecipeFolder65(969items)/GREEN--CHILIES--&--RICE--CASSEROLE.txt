Title: GREEN  CHILIES  &  RICE  CASSEROLE
Categories: Kaz
Yield: 6 Servings
3/4 c  Long-grain white rice --
Uncooked
1 tb Oil
1 1/2 c  Water -- boiling
1/2    To 1 tsp. salt (optional)
2 c  Dairy sour cream
1/4 lb (4 oz.) Monterey Jack cheese
Cut in sm. cu
1    (6 oz.) can diced green
Chili peppers, rin
3/4 c  Grated Monterey Jack cheese
Butter
In medium saucepan, heat oil over medium-high heat; add rice and stir
until rice is coated with oil and heated through. Add boiling water
and salt, if desired. (Note: Cheese and chilies are salty.) Bring to
boil over high heat; cover and reduce heat to low. Cook 20 to 25
minutes or until water is absorbed.

cooked rice with the sour cream.  Spread 1/2 of rice mixture on
