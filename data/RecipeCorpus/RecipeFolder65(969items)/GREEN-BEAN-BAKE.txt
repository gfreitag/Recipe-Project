Title: GREEN BEAN BAKE
Categories: Vegetables
Yield: 4 Servings
1 cn Mushroom soup
1 cn Green beans
1 cn French fried onions
1 ts Soy sauce
drain beans and reserve 1/4 cup of the liquid. Alternate layers of
beans & onions in baking dish. Mix soup, bean liquid and soy sauce
and pour over vegetables. Sprinkle with 1/2 cup grated cheese and
bake 20-30 min. in 350 oven. Double or triple if need be.
