Title: GREEK-STYLE GREEN BEANS
Categories: Dupree, Vegetable
2 lb green beans --  tipped and
tailed
Yield: 6 Servings
:
3 TB butter
2    garlic cloves -- peeled and
chopped
:
1    red bell pepper -- seeded
and sliced
:
14    Greek-style olives -- drain
pit and halve
:
1 TB chopped fresh oregano
2 TB fresh lemon juice
Salt and pepper
:

Bring a large quantity of salted water to the boil in a large
saucepan. Add the beans and blanch 5 to 7 minutes. Drain, refresh
under cold water, and drain again. This can be done3 or 4 days in
advance. In a skillet, heat the butter until it sings . Add the
garlic and red pepper and cook until the garlic turns golden, being
careful not to let it burn. This will only take a minute or two. Add
the blanched beans, olives, oregano, lemon juice, and salt and pepper
to taste. Toss for about 2 minutes, until the beans are heated
through. Yield: 6 to 8 servings
