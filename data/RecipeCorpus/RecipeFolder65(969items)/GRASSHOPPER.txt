Title: GRASSHOPPER
Categories: Mixed, Drinks, Alcohol
Yield: 1 Servings
1/2 oz Creme de menthe
1/2 oz White creme de cacao
2 oz Heavy cream
Cracked ice
Fill a mixing glass with cracked ice. Add creme de menthe, creme de
cacao, and cream. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
Garnish with chocolate shavings.
