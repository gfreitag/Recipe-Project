Title: GRANOLA-PEANUT BUTTER BALLS
Categories: Candies
Yield: 16 Pieces
1/4 c  Creamy peanut butter or
Chunky peanut butter
2 tb ;water
1/4 c  Nonfat dry milk
1/4 c  Honey
2 c  Cinnamon-and-raisin granola
1/4 c  Semisweet chocolate chips
1/4 c  Flaked coconut
In a 2-quart saucepan combine peanut butter, water, dry milk, and
honey. Cook slowly over low heat, stirring constantly, until mixture
thickens. Remove from heat.  Stir in granola, then chocolate chips.
Let cool 1-2 minutes or until mixture is cool enough to handle. Place
coconut in shallow pie plate.  With buttered hands shape mixture into
16 balls. Roll each ball in coconut; place in covered container using
wax paper between layers. Store in refrigerator.  This makes 16 balls.
