Title: GRAPES WITH YOGURT SAUCE
Categories:
Yield: 1 Servings
1    Ripe small banana -- peeled
And sliced
1/2 c  Plain whole milk yogurt
Superfine sugar to taste
1 ds Rum
1 1/2 c  Red seedless grapes --
Rinsed and halved
Mint leaves -- or chopped
Pistachio nuts for garnish
Puree banana with yogurt and season with some superfine sugar and a
dash of rum. Spoon into the bottom of wine glasses and spoon grapes
over the top; garnish with mint or pistachios.

Yield: 2 servings
