Title: GRAPE NUTS BREAD - MACHINE
Categories:
Yield: 1 Loaf
1 1/3 c  Water
2 2/3 tb Vegetable oil
1 1/3 tb Sugar
2 ts Salt
2/3 c  Grape Nut cereal
-(not flakes)
2 2/3 c  Bread flour
2 1/2 ts Yeast
Add to bread machine in order specified in your manual. White bread,
medium bake setting.  This is a flavorful crunchy bread. It is a lot
like the old cracked wheat bread.
