Title: GREEN CHILI SALSA
Categories: Salsa, Vegetables
Yield: 1 Servings
1 tb Oil
1    Chopped Onion
1 cn Chopped Green Chili
1    Clove Chopped Garlic
5    Tomatoes
Heat oil, add onion and simmer for a couple of minutes. Add chili and
simm a couple of more minutes. Add garlic and tomatoes, simmer 10
more minutes. Add salt and pepper to your taste. Now blend if you
used natural chilies. Blend anyways LA RA HOT
