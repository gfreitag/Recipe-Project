Title: GRILL CHICKEN WITH HAVANA SAUCE
Categories: Barbecue, Main dish, Chicken
Yield: 8 Servings
28 oz Plum tomatoes; drained and
1/3 c  Olive oil
1/4 c  White wine
1 tb White vinegar
3    Green onions ; chopped
4 c  Garlic; minced
1/2 ts Salt
1/2 ts Pepper
2 ts Celentro; minced
8    Chicken ; breasts , skin re
Ground pepper
In lg. bowl combine all the ingredents for the sauce. Mix well, cover
and refrigerate over night. Heat an outside grill and let the sauce>
come to room temp. Sprinkel the chicken with lime juice and with salt
and pepper, as you like.  Place on grilland cook for about 6 min per
side or until brown.  brush the sauce on the chicken throughout the
grilling and top each with more sauce when serving.
