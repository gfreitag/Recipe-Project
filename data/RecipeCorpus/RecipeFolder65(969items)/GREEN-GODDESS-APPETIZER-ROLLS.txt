Title: GREEN GODDESS APPETIZER ROLLS
Categories: Breads, Appetizers
Yield: 8 Servings
8    Fresh hard rolls
1 1/2 c  Shredded mozarrela
1/3 c  Or more of bottled green
Goddess dressing
Or homemade dressing
1/4 c  Butter softened
3 tb Chopped chives
Minced fresh garlic to taste
Slice rolls in half. Butter each half.Spread on green goddess
dressing. sprinkle with some of the shredded cheese and chives and
minced garlic to taste.Broil 4 to 5 inches from heat source until
browned and bubbly.Yum yum!
