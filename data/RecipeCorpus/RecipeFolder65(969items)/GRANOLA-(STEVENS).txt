Title: GRANOLA (STEVENS)
Categories: Snacks
Yield: 11 Servings
2 c  Wheat flakes
2 c  Rye flakes
1 c  Barley flakes
3 c  Oat flakes
1 c  Shredded coconut
1 c  Nuts, chopped (your choice,
-almonds, peanuts, etc)
1/2 c  Sunflower seeds
1/2 c  Sesame seeds
MMMMM--------------------------COATING-------------------------------
1/2 c  Water
1/2 c  Oil
1/2 c  -To:
3/4 c  Honey
2 ts Vanilla
1 ts Salt
Mix all the cereal ingredients together in a big bowl. Spread out on
shallow pans.  Pour the coating over the cereal mixture. Bake in 275
oven for 1 hour.  Mix the cereal every 15 minutes to coat all the
flakes. Cool and store in airtight containers. Makes about 11-12 cups
of granola.
